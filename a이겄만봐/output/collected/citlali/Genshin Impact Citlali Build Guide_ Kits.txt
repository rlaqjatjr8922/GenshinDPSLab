Genshin Impact Citlali Guide: Kit Explained
Citlali
’s gameplay centers around her
Elemental Skill
, which features the
Nightsoul
mechanic, a signature trait shared by other characters from
Natlan
. This mechanic allows her to fly mid-air while also additional buffs to herself.
Genshin Impact Citali (Image via HoYoverse)
Normal Attack: Shadow-Stealing Spirit Vessel
Normal and Charged Attack deals
Cryo DMG
while Plunging Attack deals
AoE Cryo DMG
Elemental Skill: Dawnfrost Darkstar
She summons an
Opal Shield
that scales with her Elemental Mastery and absorbs Cryo DMG with increased efficiency. At the same time, she calls forth “
Obsidian Tzitzimitl: Itzpapa,
” a creature that delivers
AoE Cryo DMG
. Activating this skill grants Citlali
24
Nightsoul points
and places her in the Nightsoul’s Blessing state, which persists while Itzpapa remains on the field.
If Citlali has 50 or more Nightsoul points, Itzpapa enters the Opal Fire state, consuming Nightsoul points continuously to create a Frostfall Storm that deals with constant
AoE Cryo DMG
. This effect ends when her Nightsoul points are fully depleted, emphasizing the importance of careful resource management to maximize her damage potential.
Elemental Burst: Edict of Entwined Splendor
Citlali calls upon celestial allies to summon a massive ice storm that bombards the area, dealing
Nightsoul-aligned Cryo DMG.
This ability also restores a set amount of Nightsoul points and spawns
Spiritvessel Skulls
near up to three enemies within the AoE.
These skulls explode after a short time, dealing additional AoE Cryo DMG and further restoring
Nightsoul points
for Citlali. This Burst not only provides immense damage but also enhances her ability to maintain Nightsoul points for extended periods, allowing her to keep up the pressure in battle.
Citlali’s Passives
Citali in Genshin Impact (Image via HoYoverse)
Mamaloaco’s Frigid Rain
Reduces Pyro and Hydro RES
of enemies affected by
Frozen or Melt reactions by 20% for 12 seconds.
This allows her team to capitalize on increased damage from those reactions. Additionally, Citlali regains
16 Nightsoul points
whenever these reactions are triggered, with a cooldown of
8 seconds
for the restoration effect.
This mechanic makes her highly effective in teams that can consistently trigger Frozen or Melt, as it boosts team damage while maintaining her
Nightsoul resource.
Itzpapalotl’s Star Garments
Scales Itzpapa’s and Citlali’s Burst damage directly with her
Elemental Mastery,
significantly boosting her DPS potential. During her Elemental Skill,
Itzpapa’s Frostfall Storm
damage increases by
90% of her Elemental Mastery
, while her Elemental Burst’s Ice Storm damage gets an even greater boost, scaling at
1200% of her Elemental Mastery.
Furthermore, she regains 4 Nightsoul points when allies trigger
Nightsoul Bursts,
adding more ways to replenish her critical resource. These effects make Elemental Mastery an essential stat for Citlali, reinforcing her synergy with reaction-heavy teams and boosting her output in both sustained damage and burst windows.
Night Realm’s Gift: Smoke, Mirrors, and the Flowing Winds
Introduces exploration and mobility mechanics tied to
Natlan’s Phlogiston systems
. When in areas with Phlogiston Mechanics, Citlali can perform “
Nightsoul Transmission,”
allowing her to leap into the air and deploy
“White Obsidian Tzitzimitl: Citlalin,
” floating in the sky.
This enhances her mobility and vertical reach in specific regions, enabling unique interactions with the environment. While in mid-air, she can hold her
Normal Attack
to enter
Aiming Mode
, prioritizing Phlogiston over stamina consumption for
Charged Attacks.
Genshin Impact Citlali Build Guide: Constellations
In this section, we will break down the constellations for Citlali in Genshin Impact.
C1
This constellation gives Citlali a buff called
Opalstar Vestments
when she uses her Elemental Skill. It provides
10 Stellar Blade stacks
, which are consumed whenever your other characters deal damage. Each stack boosts their damage by a massive amount based on
Citlali’s Elemental Mastery.
Additionally, when Frozen or Melt reactions happen, Citlali gets more stacks, but only every
8 seconds.
This makes her amazing in reaction-heavy teams since she not only deals great Cryo damage but also boosts her allies’ attacks. Plus, it helps her move better by lowering the energy (
Phlogiston
) she uses during her mid-air abilities.
C2
C2 turns Citlali into an incredible team player. It gives her a bonus of
125 Elemental Mastery
and also boosts her teammates’ Elemental Mastery by 250 if they’re shielded by her
Opal Shield
or have
Itzpapa
following them. This makes reaction-based damage like Melt and Frozen much stronger for your whole team. On top of that, it improves her passive skill
Mamaloaco’s Frigid Rain,
further reducing enemy Pyro and Hydro resistance when reactions like Frozen or Melt are triggered.
C3
Elemental skill increased by 3 levels.
C4
C4 is where Citlali starts to shine even more. When
Itzpapa’s Frostfall Storm
hits enemies, it summons an extra
Spiritvessel Skull
. These Skulls explode and deal massive
AoE Cryo damage
based on
Citlali’s Elemental Mastery
. On top of that, each explosion restores Nightsoul points and Energy for Citlali, helping her maintain her skills for longer fights. This effect can trigger
every 8 seconds
, so it’s a consistent way to increase her damage and sustain her abilities.
C5
Elemental burst increased by 3 levels.
C6
C6 completely changes how Citlali’s Elemental Skill works. When you summon
Itzpapa
, it immediately enters its most powerful state,
O
pal Fire
, and stays there permanently, even when Nightsoul points run out. Instead of ending, Itzpapa keeps consuming Nightsoul points, which in turn gives Citlali special
Cifra of the Secret Law
points. These points boost her damage and also increase the
Pyro and Hydro damage of the entire team
. However, when Itzpapa leaves or when you use her Elemental Skill again, the
Cifra
points disappear. This constellation makes Citlali incredibly powerful, turning her into a team damage booster while maximizing her personal Cryo damage.
As far as Constellations go, Citlali’s constellations are pretty average at best. The only game-change worthy is undeniably her C6. In order of best to least best constellation:
C1>C6>C2>C4>C5>C3

Best Team Comps for Citlali in Genshin Impact
Citlali works best in
Melt
or
Freeze team comp
s, where her abilities shine by supporting
Pyro
or
Hydro DPS
characters. Her
passive talent reduces the Pyro and Hydro resistance
of enemies, making it easier for those elements to deal more damage.
This makes her an excellent enabler for teams focused on reactions like Melt or Freeze. However, Citlali doesn’t reduce Cryo resistance with her kit, meaning
Cryo DPS
characters won’t benefit as much from her support compared to other units.

Final Thoughts
Citlali
is a remarkable
Cryo support
and
sub-DPS
with a complex and versatile kit that excels in Melt and
Freeze
team compositions. She brings strong team utility through shields, buffs, and consistent Cryo application, which sets her apart as a flexible and impactful unit. However, Citlali’s gameplay has some limitations. Her inability to reduce
Cryo resistance
means she provides less benefit to Cryo main
DPS units.

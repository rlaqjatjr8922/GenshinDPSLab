Genshin Impact Character Guide: How to Build the Best Citlali
Date: 2025-01-07 09:14
To effectively build and utilize Citlali in Genshin Impact, it is crucial to understand her role within team compositions and how she operates both on and off the field. As a 5-star Cryo character who uses a catalyst, Citlali was introduced in Version 5.3 alongside Mavuika. Her specialization lies in providing protective shields for allies and applying Cryo to enemies, which can then be used for various elemental reactions.
Citlali's Elemental Skill automatically creates an Opal Shield around her and summons Itzpapa, which executes an AoE Frostfall Storm once at least 50 Nightsoul points are accumulated. Her Elemental Burst not only deals AoE Cryo damage but also restores Nightsoul points and summons Spiritvessel Skulls that explode, dealing additional damage and restoring more Nightsoul points. Once you have gathered Citlali’s materials and ascended her, you can start focusing on optimizing her build to enhance these abilities.
The Best Weapon for Citlali
The foundation of Citlali's optimal build begins with finding the right catalyst. Starcaller’s Watch, her signature weapon, is the best 5-star option available. This weapon has a lower base ATK (542 at level 90) but compensates with a significant Elemental Mastery sub-stat (265 at level 90) and grants Citlali an extra 100 Elemental Mastery. The Mirror of Night effect, activated after creating a shield, boosts the active party member's damage by 28% against nearby opponents every 14 seconds. While Starcaller’s Watch is ideal due to its enhancement of Elemental Mastery, A Thousand Floating Dreams serves as an excellent alternative. This weapon provides similar base ATK and Elemental Mastery stats but introduces different buffs based on party composition, increasing Elemental Mastery or DMG Bonus depending on allies' elements. If neither premium weapon is accessible, there are several reliable 4-star options like Wandering Evenstar, Sacrificial Fragments, and Mappa Mare, each offering unique benefits that can complement Citlali's playstyle.
The Best Artifact Sets for Citlali
When selecting artifacts for Citlali, two sets stand out as particularly beneficial: Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City and Gilded Dreams. The Scroll set enhances Citlali's ability to recover Elemental Energy when an ally triggers a Nightsoul Burst and significantly boosts allied Elemental DMG upon triggering a reaction, especially if Citlali is in the Nightsoul's Blessing state. Meanwhile, the Gilded Dreams set increases Citlali's Elemental Mastery and provides buffs based on the elements of her allies, enhancing her support capabilities.
Farming for either artifact set should prioritize increasing Elemental Mastery, as this stat amplifies the strength of her shield and the potency of elemental reactions. Energy Recharge is also critical for maintaining her Nightsoul points, aiding in damage output. While boosting CRIT rates and Cryo DMG can improve her offensive presence, focusing on Elemental Mastery is key to maximizing Citlali's effectiveness.
The Best Team Compositions for Citlali
Citlali demonstrates versatility as a Cryo shielder, suitable for a variety of team compositions. However, she particularly excels when integrated into Freeze or Melt-focused teams. After an ally triggers the Frozen or Melt reactions, Citlali can reduce enemy resistance to Pyro and Hydro by 20% for 12 seconds, making her an invaluable support character. Pairing her with characters like Neuvillette, Mualani, Arlecchino, or Mavuika in Genshin Impact maximizes this advantage, enhancing the effectiveness of these elemental strategies.
1.Melt Team
Citlali (Cryo Shielder & Support) + Mavuika (Pyro DPS) or Arlecchino (Pyro DPS) + Rosaria (Cryo sub-DPS & Support) or Xilonen (Geo Support) + Bennett (Pyro Support & Healer)
2.Permafreeze Team
Citlali (Cryo Shielder & Support) + Neuvillette (Hydro DPS) or Mualani (Hydro DPS) + Rosaria (Cryo sub-DPS & Support) + Kokomi (Hydro Healer & Support) or Barbara (Hydro Healer & Support)
3.F2P Team
Citlali (Cryo Shielder & Support) + Kaeya (Cryo DPS) or Gaming (Pyro DPS) + Xingqiu (Hydro sub-DPS) + Bennett (Pyro Support & Healer)

Skill Priority for Citlali
Due to resource limitations, prioritizing skill upgrades is essential. Citlali's Elemental Skill should take precedence, as it generates the shield and delivers consistent off-field Cryo damage. Following closely is her Elemental Burst, which aids in charging her skill's damage. Normal Attacks can be neglected since they do not align with her primary role.
Citlali's build is relatively straightforward, requiring few different stats to perform optimally. While lacking her signature weapon may complicate the process, there are still viable F2P options. Despite some limitations, Citlali remains a formidable support character for Freeze and Melt team compositions in Genshin Impact, capable of significantly enhancing team performance through her unique skillset and strategic application of elemental reactions.

Chongyun Is Best Used As Quickswap Burst DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ National Team
- A classic team for Chongyun.
- Consists of only 4 star characters, some can be changed for 5 star equivalents.
- Comfortable to use even in high difficulty challenges like Spiral Abyss.
2nd
▼ Freeze Team
- Ayaka is the Cryo DPS that Chongyun supports.
- Xilonen & Furina can further increase the damage dealt while Chongyun can give ATK SPD increase to Ayaka and also deal substantial damage with his Burst.
3rd
▼ Burnmelt DPS
- Reliant on a Dendro sub-DPS to extend the Pyro aura on the enemies so Chongyun can Melt more often.
- As the current best Burning sub-DPS, Emilie is used along with Mavuika who can hold Scroll set to buff the Elemental DMG of the team.
- Bennett for healing and ATK buff on Chongyun, Emilie and Mavuika.
Chongyun's Burst has high damage multipliers with a low Energy cost of 40. This makes him really good for quickswap teams like the National team. Aside from his Burst's damage, Chongyun can be used as a support for Cryo DPS since he can increase the Cryo DMG taken by enemies.

The National Team is a quickswap type of team where three (3) Sub-DPS characters are used with Bennett being the main support who heals and buffs their damage. Vaporize, Freeze, and Melt are the main damaging reactions in this team.
Rotation Of National Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett and use his Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Xiangling and use her Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Xingqiu then use his Burst and Skill
4
Switch to Chongyun and do one Normal Attack
5
Use Chongyun's Skill then Burst
6
Switch back to Xiangling and use her fast Normal Attacks to apply Cryo
7
Repeat from 1
There's no strict rotation order in National Team, you only need to use Bennett's Burst before everyone else so they can get buffed.

Chongyun can activate Cryo resonance to reduce the CRIT rate requirement. He can also help funnel Energy to Ayaka. Ayaka's damage mainly comes from her Burst but her Normal/Charged Attack are also used in her rotations, which can benefit from Chongyun's ATK SPD buff.
Rotation Of Freeze Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Chongyun and use his Skill
3
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies with Normal Attack twice
4
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
5
Switch back to Chongyun and use his Burst
6
Switch to Ayaka and use her Burst then Skill
7
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage and refresh her infusion with her Dash.
8
Repeat from 1

No.

5

6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

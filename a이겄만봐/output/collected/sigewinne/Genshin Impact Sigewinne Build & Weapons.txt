Welcome to the ultimate guide for Sigewinne’s builds and weapons in
Genshin Impact
, where we delve into crafting the most effective setups to enhance her role as both a healer and a support for off-field damage dealers.
Who’s Sigewinne?
Sigewinne in
Genshin Impact
functions as a versatile healer and support character. Her Elemental Skill heals the entire team, while her passives boost Hydro Damage Bonus and Skill Damage. She can also deal significant damage with her Elemental Burst, making her effective as both a healer and a burst DPS support.
Sigewinne Best Build
To get the most out of Sigewinne’s healing and boost her Elemental Skill damage, she needs around 65,000 HP. Since there aren’t many HP-focused bows, you can also consider using bows that boost Energy Recharge or have other support features. If you have her signature weapon, you might not need to use sets like Ocean-Hued Clam.
Sigewinne Best Weapon
Silvershower Heartstrings stands out as an excellent weapon for Sigewinne, offering up to 66.2% HP increase through stacks obtained via her Skill or healing. Each stack boosts Max HP by 12/24/40%, and at three stacks, adds +28% CRIT Rate briefly. For players without all constellations, focusing on HP% from artifact sets and Ascension is usually sufficient, making the additional stack benefits less necessary.
Sigewinne Artifact
For
Sigewinne
in Genshin Impact, the best artifact set is the 4-piece Ocean-Hued Clam for healing and DPS. If focusing purely on healing, use the 4-piece Maiden Beloved. You can also mix two 2-piece HP +20% sets or combine 2-piece HP +20% with 2-piece Healing Bonus +15%. For a damage focus, mix two 2-piece sets from Hydro DMG Bonus +15%, Noblesse Oblige, HP +20%, or Emblem of Severed Fate. Main stats should be HP/HP/HP or HP/HP/Healing Bonus, and for Burst damage, consider HP or Energy Recharge/Hydro DMG Bonus/Crit or HP.
Sigewinne Best Teams
Sigewinne finds herself in a unique role: she enhances the damage of off-field characters who rely on their Skills, while mainly focusing on healing. Characters like Fischl and Furina, who frequently use their Skills from afar, benefit greatly from her buffs. This setup allows your off-field DPS to shine as the team’s primary damage dealer. Alternatively, if you prefer a straightforward approach, Sigewinne can serve as a reliable healer for any team composition needing support.
Conclusion
In conclusion, Sigewinne stands as a versatile character in
Genshin Impact
, offering potent healing capabilities and strategic buffs that can significantly amplify the effectiveness of off-field damage dealers or stabilize any team in need of reliable support. Whether focusing on maximizing her healing prowess or boosting team DPS, Sigewinne proves invaluable in diverse gameplay scenarios.

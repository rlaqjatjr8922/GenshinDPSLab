Sigewinne joins the roster as one of the last Fontainian characters as the game is heading towards the Natlan expansion, in
Genshin Impact
. She offers a unique blend of healing, support, and off-field Hydro application. Her ability to heal and buff allies, combined with her potential to apply Hydro effects off-field, makes her a valuable asset for players seeking to diversify their strategies. While she joins the ranks of
other Hydro healers like Barbara and Kokomi
, Sigewinne’s abilities offer distinct advantages that can be leveraged by certain characters to enhance team's DPS performance.

As a healer, Sigewinne can fit into any team that needs healing. However, she truly shines in Hydro-centric setups or those requiring consistent Hydro-related elemental reactions and healing. For teams focused on maximizing elemental reactions, such as Bloom or Tazer teams, Sigewinne’s off-field Hydro application is particularly valuable. Additionally, her supportive abilities are beneficial in sustaining the team during self-damage-inflicting reactions like Bloom, making her an excellent choice for players looking to optimize both offensive and defensive aspects of their gameplay. Sigewinne also increases the Elemental Skill DMG of characters, which, combined with her healing,
makes Furina her most synergistic team member
. Here are some other characters she works well with in
Genshin Impact
.
Sigewinne + Furina + Nilou + Nahida
Sigewinne:
Healer, Buffer, Hydro-Enabler.
Furina:
Hydro-Enabler, Sub-DPS, Buffer
Nilou:
Bountiful Core enabler
Nahida:
Sub-DPS, Dendro-Enabler, Buffer
A Bloom team featuring Sigewinne, Furina, Nilou, and Nahida is Sigewinne's best team composition. Sigewinne serves as the primary healer and Hydro applicator in this setup. Her ability to heal and apply Hydro off-field ensures continuous support and sustains the team during intense battles. Additionally, her Elemental Skill DMG buff enhances the overall damage output of the team, especially for Furina and Nahida.

Furina complements Sigewinne's abilities by providing additional Hydro application and further enhancing the team's offensive potential with her buffs. Her presence also synergizes well with Sigewinne's healing, as she'll drain the teams HP, while she benefits from constant HP drain and gain mechanics for increasing team's DMG potential. Due to her ability to convert Dendro
Cores into Bountiful Cores, Nilou is the key to maximizing Bloom reactions
in this team, as otherwise the Bloom reaction itself isn't as strong. Nahida as the sole Dendro user in the team, plays a crucial role in triggering Bloom reactions. Her Elemental Skill can mark multiple enemies, ensuring that Dendro application is consistent and widespread. Being the best off-field Dendro applicator in the game currently, its not recommended to play this comp, if players don't have access to her.
Sigewinne + Furina + Yae Miko + Kazuha
Sigewinne:
Healer, Buffer, Hydro-Enabler.
Furina:
Hydro-Enabler, Sub-DPS, Buffer
Yae Miko:
Electro-Enabler, Sub-DPS
Kazuha:
Buffer, Debuffer (Viridescent Venerer), Crowd Control
In this Taser team composition, Sigewinne and Furina's roles remain the same as above, with additional characters like Yae Miko and Kazuha providing consistent elemental application, and further benefits for the team while no particular team member is the main carry. As such, it is a team composition suitable for players that are interested in teams where they can quickly swap between team members, while not needing to rely on a selfish on-field main DPS. Nevertheless, its recommended that if players are using Golden Troupe on Furina and Yae Miko, they mostly remain off-field to be able to benefit from the 4-piece set bonus of this set.

Electro-charged team is rather underrated in Genshin Impact, but it packs a lot of potentials if used right.

Yae Miko serves as the primary Electro DPS, capable of dealing significant damage with her Elemental Skill and Burst. Her elemental skill provides off-field consistent Electro DPS, pairing excellently with the Hydro application from Sigewinne and Furina. This setup enables consistent Electro-Charged reactions, while she can also benefit from Sigewinne's Elemental Skill buff. Players can also consider Fischl or Raiden Shogun instead, if they don't have access to Yae Miko for a similar team composition. Kazuha on the other hand, enhances the team's elemental damage and crowd control capabilities. His ability to group enemies together with his Anemo abilities and provide elemental damage buffs through his Swirl reactions (Viridescent Venerer) as well as his own kit amplifies the damage output, and consistent application of Hydro and Electro elements.
For players with Sigewinne's C2 and seeking a more dedicated on-field DPS option, Neuvillette can replace Kazuha. His abilities synergize well with Sigewinne's Hydro application and healing. With Sigewinne's C2 reducing Hydro resistance of the enemies
Neuvillette's damage potential is significantly
increased. Otherwise, Neuvillette doesn't benefit from the Elemental Skill buff Sigewinne provides, unlike Yae Miko or Furina.
Sigewinne + Clorinde + Fischl + Sucrose
Sigewinne:
Healer, Buffer, Hydro-Enabler.
Clorinde:
Main DPS, Electro-Enabler
Fischl:
Electro-Enabler, Sub-DPS, Battery
Sucrose:
Buffer, Debuffer (Viridescent Venerer), Crowd Control, Battery
A team featuring Sigewinne, Clorinde, Fischl, and Sucrose is another Taser team possibility, with less reliance on 5-star characters. Sigewinne's role remains the same, while Clorinde
serves as the main DPS and Electro applier
. It's also worth noting that Sigewinne and Clorinde share a Bond of Life synergy, with both being able to gain Bond of Life. However, this is not particularly important, as it primarily contributes to Sigewinne's energy gain, and her Elemental Burst is not as prominent in her kit as her Elemental Skill. Furthermore, having sucrose and Fischl on the team also provides battery characters, making it quite redundant.
The rest of the team is F2P-friendly, which makes it quite ideal for players that have recently pulled Clorinde and Sigewinne in their debut version update. Fischl adds consistent off-field Electro damage, while also beneffiting from the Elemental Skill buff. Sucrose provides crowd control and boosts elemental damage. Overall, it is similar to how Kazuha and Yae Miko team would perform, albeit with a F2P possibility.

SUBSCRIPTION
DIGITAL
Step into Teyvat, a vast world teeming with life and flowing with elemental energy.
You and your sibling arrived here from another world. Separated by an unknown god, stripped of your powers, and cast into a deep slumber, you now awake to a world very different from when you first arrived.
Thus begins your journey across Teyvat to seek answers from The Seven — the gods of each element. Along the way, prepare to explore every inch of this wondrous world, join forces with a diverse range of characters, and unravel the countless mysteries that Teyvat holds...
MASSIVE OPEN WORLD
Climb any mountain, swim across any river, and glide over the world below, taking in the jaw-dropping scenery each step of the way. And if you stop to investigate a wandering Seelie or strange mechanism, who knows what you might discover?
ELEMENTAL COMBAT SYSTEM
Harness the seven elements to unleash elemental reactions. Anemo, Electro, Hydro, Pyro, Cryo, Dendro, and Geo interact in all sorts of ways, and Vision wielders have the power to turn this to their advantage.
Will you vaporize Hydro with Pyro, electro-charge it with Electro, or freeze it with Cryo? Your mastery of the elements will give you the upper hand in battle and exploration.
BEAUTIFUL VISUALS
Feast your eyes on the world around you, with a stunning art style, real-time rendering, and finely tuned character animations delivering you a truly immersive visual experience. Lighting and weather all change naturally over time, bringing every detail of this world to life.
SOOTHING SOUNDTRACK
Let the beautiful sounds of Teyvat draw you in as you explore the expansive world around you. Performed by the London Philharmonic Orchestra, the soundtrack changes seamlessly with the time and gameplay to match the mood.
BUILD YOUR DREAM TEAM
Team up with a diverse cast of characters in Teyvat, each with their own unique personalities, stories, and abilities. Discover your favorite party combinations and level up your characters to help you conquer even the most daunting of enemies and domains.
JOURNEY WITH FRIENDS
Team up with friends across various platforms to trigger more elemental action, tackle tricky boss fights, and conquer challenging domains together to reap rich rewards.
As you stand atop the peaks of Jueyun Karst and take in the rolling clouds and vast terrain stretching out before you, you may wish to stay in Teyvat a little longer... But until you are reunited with your lost sibling, how can you rest? Go forth, Traveler, and begin your adventure!

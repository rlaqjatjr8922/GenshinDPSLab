Genshin Impact Sigewinne Guide: Kit Explained
Sigewinne’s playstyle is based on the
Bond of Life mechanic
. When she uses her skill, she generates
Sourcewater Droplets
that provide her with a Bond of Life, which temporarily
increases her max HP
.
Genshin Impact Sigewinne (Image via HoYoverse)
Normal Attack: Targeted Treatment
Her normal attacks are fairly simple, allowing her to shoot up to three consecutive arrows. Her charged shot is where things start to get more interesting. When fully charged, she continuously fires slow-moving Hydro bubbles at enemies, making her a unique ranged Hydro applicator.
This means she can keep applying Hydro status from a distance, setting up elemental reactions effectively. If you hold her charged shot long enough, the final arrow will deal a stronger burst of Hydro damage, making it useful for precision attacks.
Elemental Skill: Rebound Hydrotherapy
Her Elemental Skill is the core of her kit. She shoots out a bouncing bubble that deals Hydro damage based on her max HP and heals the team, except for herself, every time it bounces. The more it bounces, the more healing it provides. However, after five bounces, the bubble disappears, and she finally heals herself. This means Sigewinne can keep the party alive while remaining somewhat vulnerable until the effect ends.
If you hold her skill instead of tapping it, she creates a larger bubble that does more damage and heals more effectively. This is especially useful for dealing with weaker enemies, as they get trapped inside the big bubble, making them easy targets.
Additionally, when she uses her skill, she generates
Sourcewater Droplets
that provide her with a Bond of Life, a special effect that temporarily increases her max HP. When this bond disappears, she regenerates energy, which helps her use her Elemental Burst more often.
Elemental Burst: Super Saturated Syringing
Her Elemental Burst,
Super Saturated Syringing
, is a simple but powerful ability. She unleashes a large Hydro AoE attack that scales with her max HP. Additionally, it absorbs nearby
Sourcewater Droplets
, further fueling her Bond of Life mechanic.
This burst makes her a strong Hydro applicator while providing an extra source of energy generation. One of her key traits is her Arkhe:
Ousia alignment
, which causes her bubbles to occasionally summon Surging Blades that deal additional Hydro damage. This makes her even better at maintaining Hydro application over time.
Sigewinne’s Passives
Genshin Impact Sigewinne Guide (Image via HoYoverse)
Requires Appropriate Rest
Her first passive,
Requires Appropriate Rest
, activates after she uses her Elemental Skill. For 18 seconds, she gains a
Hydro DMG
Bonus and receives 10 stacks of
Convalescence
. These stacks interact with her team’s off-field Elemental Skills, boosting their damage whenever they hit an enemy. The bonus scales with Sigewinne’s max HP, meaning the more HP she has, the stronger the boost.
If she has over
30,000 HP
, each stack adds extra damage, with a cap of
2,800 bonus damage
per hit. This passive encourages her to be paired with characters who deal
Elemental Skill
damage while off-field, making her an ideal partner for sub-DPS units who frequently trigger reactions.
Detailed Diagnosis, Thorough Treatment
Her second passive,
Detailed Diagnosis
, Thorough Treatment, improves her healing output based on the Bond of Life mechanic. The more Bond of Life HP stored across the party, the stronger her healing becomes.
For every
1,000 HP
stored in Bonds of Life, her
healing increases by 3%
, up to a
maximum of 30%
. This means that as she gathers and clears Bonds of Life, her healing potential scales up, making her especially useful in prolonged fights where healing needs to be at its peak.
Emergency Dose
Her final passive, Emergency Dose, activates exclusively while exploring underwater. If the active character’s HP falls below
50%
, Sigewinne automatically heals them over
2.5 seconds
, restoring
50% of their max HP
. However, the trade-off is that the character’s Elemental and
Physical Resistance
will be reduced for
10 seconds
.
Since underwater combat in Fontaine relies on dodging rather than traditional damage mitigation, this passive is a strong survival tool, ensuring that players can stay in action longer without needing to switch to a healer. However, the resistance drop means players will need to be more careful after the heal is triggered.
Genshin Impact Sigewinne Build Guide: Constellations
In this section, we will break down the constellations for Sigewinne in Genshin Impact.
C1
This constellation improves her Elemental Skill significantly by allowing her
Bolstering Bubblebalm
to bounce 3 extra times. This means more healing for the team, more Hydro application, and a longer duration of crowd control for weaker enemies. Additionally, the first 3 bounces won’t shrink the big
Bubblebalms
, letting them deal their highest damage for longer.
On top of that, her passive
Requires Appropriate Rest
gets a buff—each bubble bounce now generates
Convalescence
stacks, further increasing teamwide Elemental Skill damage. The bonus scaling is also increased, allowing up to
3,500 extra damage
per Elemental Skill hit instead of the original 2,800.
C2
Sigewinne gains a
Bubbly Shield
worth
30% of her Max HP
, which is especially effective against Hydro damage, absorbing it with 250% efficiency. Since her kit naturally scales with HP, this shield will be quite strong, improving her survivability.
Additionally, whenever her Skill or Burst hits an enemy, it reduces their
Hydro Resistance by 35% for 8 seconds
, making it easier for Hydro DPS units.
C3
Elemental skill increased by 3 levels.
C4
This extends the duration of her Elemental Burst by 3 seconds, allowing it to apply Hydro for longer and deal more damage overall.
C5
Elemental burst increased by 3 levels.
C6
This is Sigewinne’s biggest offensive upgrade. Whenever she heals, her
Super Saturated Syringing (Burst) gains CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG based on her Max HP
. Every 1,000 HP increases CRIT Rate by 0.4% and CRIT DMG by 2.2%, up to a maximum of
20% CRIT Rate and 110% CRIT DMG
.
This turns her Burst into a
high-damage nuke
, making her not just a healer but also a secondary DPS. Since her healing is frequent, she will almost always have this buff active when using her Burst.
As far as Constellations go, Sigewinne’s constellations are pretty average at best. If you’re

Best Team Comps for Sigewinne in Genshin Impact
Sigewinne is a
Hydro healer
whose main strength lies in
buffing Elemental Skill damage

off-field skill damage

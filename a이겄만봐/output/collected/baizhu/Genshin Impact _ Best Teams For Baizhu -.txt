Synergy Details
Synergy Of Aggravate-Hyperbloom Team
Character
Detail
Cyno
Cyno works amazing with a Dendro off-field character and buff from Baizhu.
Xingqiu
Xingqiu is there for Hydro infliction and additional damage creating Blooms for Cyno to trigger.
Nahida
Nahida boosts the Elemental Mastery of the team for more DMG and more consistent Dendro Infliction.
The objective of the team is to increase the DMG of Cyno through Aggravate reaction. With Xingqiu and Dendro characters, Hyperbloom can also be triggered. Both of these reactions are boosted by Baizhu's buff.
Pros & Cons Of Aggravate-Hyperbloom Team
Pros
- Uses Various reactions that are boosted by Baizhu creating powerful reactions.
- Flexible team that can deal with single or multiple targets easily.
Cons
- This team requires a fairly strict rotation that needs getting used to.
- Fairly Energy Hungry since many characters require to use their Elemental Burst
Rotation Of Aggravate-Hyperbloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Skill Of Nahida.
2
Switch to Xingqiu Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Baizhu and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Nahida and use Elemental Burst
5
Switch to Cyno, Use Elemental Burst then Skill.
6
Deal damage with Normal Attack and Elemental Skill.
7
Return to No. 1

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Burgeon Team
Character
Detail
Ayato
Ayato and Baizhu can consistently produce Bloom.
Thoma
Thoma can trigger those blooms to deal Burgeon DMG.
Kazuha
Kazuha is there for crowd control and Elemental Mastery boost.
The objective of the team is to continuously create Blooms with Ayato and Baizhu and trigger them with Thoma. Kazuha is a flex pick that is mostly used for DMG increase and Crowd Control.
Pros & Cons Of Burgeon Team
Pros
- Uses Various reactions that is boosted by Baizhu creating powerful reactions.
- Very high and consistent damage from Burgeon.
Cons
- Requires a lot of Energy to be stable. All characters have very high Burst Cost.
- Strict rotations and most of the characters require high investments to be stable.
- The self-DMG is very high for Burgeon so Elemental Burst of Baizhu is very crucial in this team.
Rotation Of Burgeon Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Burst of Ayato.
2
Switch to Kazuha and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Baizhu and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Thoma and use Elemental Skill to burst the Blooms then Elemental Burst.
5
Switch to Ayato and use Elemental Skill.
6
Deal damage with Normal Attack.
7
Return to No. 1

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Spread Team
Character
Detail
Alhaitham
Alhaitham and Baizhu works great because of Dendro Resonance and Baizhu can also be a decent holder of Deepwood.
Raiden Shogun
Raiden Shogun provides consistent Electro to enemies and can also act as a Sub-DPS that fill up Energy of the team.
Zhongli
Zhongli provides resistance shred and resistance to interruption for the team.
The objective of the team is to continuously trigger Spread reaction to deal damage to enemies.
Pros & Cons Of Spread Reaction Team
Pros
- Rotations are not strict and easy to use.
- Many replaceable characters.
Cons
- Needs several 5 Star characters in the team.
- Does not work well against Dendro-immune  enemies.
Rotation Of Spread Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Skill of Raiden Shogun.
2
Switch to Zhongli and use Elemental Skill (Hold).
3
Switch to Baizhu and Use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
4
Use Alhaitham and use Elemental Skill and perform combos with Charged, Normal Attacks and Elemental Burst.
5
When out of energy, use Elemental Burst of Raiden Shogun.
6
Return to No. 1

Synergy Of Spread Team
Character
Detail
Yoimiya
Yoimiya has a quick normal attack that works well with Fishcl and Yelan. With Baizhu, she can produce more reactions while being safe because of shields and healing.
Yelan
Yelan enables Vaporize and Electro Charged. With Baizhu added in the team, Dendro reactions will also frequently happen.
Fishcl
Fischl provides energy and Electro enabling a chaotic series of reactions.
This team make use of various off-field DPS to deal multiple reactions.

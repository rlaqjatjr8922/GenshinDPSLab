Synergy Details
Synergy Of Cryo Support Qiqi Team
Character
Detail
Razor
He is an Electro character that deals Physical DMG. Razor can utilize Qiqi's periodic Cryo application since he is a melee character.
Mika
Mika is an excellent buffer for Physical DPS such as Razor. Cryo resonance is also enabled with Qiqi.
Zhongli
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' resistance and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
The objective of this team is for Qiqi to heal & apply Cryo to nearby opponents. The damage of bubbles from Ocean-Hued Clam set deals additional damage from time to time.
Pros & Cons Of Cryo Support Qiqi Team
Pros
- Can amplify Physical DMG
- Multiple buffs can drastically improve the performance of Razor
Cons
- Qiqi's Skill has long cooldown
- Less effective when facing opponents that are resistant to Physical DMG
Rotation Of Cryo Support Qiqi Team
No.
Procedure
1
Hold Zhongli's Skill for shield
2
Switch to Mika
3
Use Mika's Burst & Skill for buffs
4
Switch to Qiqi
5
Use Qiqi's Skill for Cryo application
6
Switch to Razor
7
Use Razor's Burst for increased attack speed
8
Use Razor's Normal Attack & Skill consecutively until the duration of his Burst ends
9
Repeat to step 1
As long as Zhongli's shield & Qiqi's Skill are both active, Razor can keep on attacking the enemies. You can use Mika's talents if both Skill & Burst can be used simultaneously.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Physical Qiqi Team
Character
Detail
Fischl
At C6, Oz releases an additional projectile whenever Qiqi performs a normal ATK. With Thundering Fury x4, triggering superconduct reduces Skill's CD which is great for sustaining Oz's presence on the field.
Rosaria
She is a good Cryo applicator & CRIT Rate buffer that can improve Jean's damage output.
Xingqiu
He is an excellent off-field Hydro DPS who can easily freeze the nearby enemies with Qiqi's Skill.
The objective of this team is to consistently apply Electro & Cryo to the enemy for superconduct so Qiqi's Physical DMG is higher than usual. Freeze is a crowd control that can also trigger superconduct when Electro is applied.
Pros & Cons Of Physical Qiqi Team
Pros
- Qiqi's normal & charged ATK can deal powerful damage
- Several multipliers can significantly improve Qiqi's damage output
Cons
- Active character can be interrupted during rotation
- Cryo status can slow Qiqi's attack speed
Rotation Of Physical Qiqi Team
No.
Procedure
1
Switch to Rosaria
2
Use Rosaria's Skill for Cryo particles
3
Use Rosaria's Burst for Cryo application & buffs
4
Switch to Xingqiu
5
Use Xingqiu's Burst & Skill for Hydro application
6
Switch to Fischl
7
Use Fischl's Skill for Electro application
8
Switch to Qiqi
9
Utilize Qiqi's normal & charged ATK until the duration of Rosaria's Burst & Oz have ended
※ Use Qiqi's Skill when Rosaria's ice lance has disappeared
10
Return to step 1
Qiqi can deal decent damage with her normal & charged ATK but this can be improved further thru superconduct, RES debuffs, & multiplier buffs.

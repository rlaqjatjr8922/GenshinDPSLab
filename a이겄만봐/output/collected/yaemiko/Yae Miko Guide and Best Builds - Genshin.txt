Yae Miko Guide and Best Builds
Yae Miko is a 5-Star Electro Catalyst user in Genshin Impact who deals
powerful off-field Electro damage. Our Yae Miko guide focuses on her best
builds, artifacts, weapons and best team compositions.

Yae Miko's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Elemental Skill has a short cooldown giving you the ability to have 100% uptime.
Enemies can be dodged using Yae's Elemental Skill.
Elemental Burst deals powerful AoE Electro DMG.
Weaknesses
Turrets need to be positioned carefully and manually adjusted if enemies leave the attack radius.
Elemental Burst has a high Energy Cost.
Yae primarily is played as a SubDPS generally focusing on the
Aggravate
reaction but can be played in
other reactions as well such as
Hyperbloom
,
Overloaded
and
Electro-Charged
.

3.1.
Energy Recharge Requirements
Yae's Energy Recharge requirements can vary based on who is in the party and
how often you use her Elemental Burst. If Yae is the only
Electro
character on the team for example, you will only want to burst every other
rotation otherwise the Energy Recharge requirements become too high.
Burst frequency
Energy Recharge

160 - 180%
2 Electro | Burst Every Rotation
140 - 160%

110 - 140%
Yakan Offering
(C1), roughly lowers Yae's Energy
Recharge requirements down by 40% effectively allowing her to
burst every rotation in a lot more teams. Playing her on-field can also lower it by
roughly 15%.
4.
Combos and How to Play Yae Miko
Yae's gameplay primarily reolves around her
Sesshou Sakura
,
these are the lightning "turrets" which are placed when you use her
Elemental Skill and deal
Electro
DMG periodically.
You have 3 charges of this Elemental Skill and 3 Sesshou Sakura can be on the
field at any time.
When the Sesshou Sakura are near to eachother, their level will increase.
This means that the DMG dealt from their periodic attacks will increase. To
optimise this, you want to place them in a rough triangle-like formation.
Placing them in a line will only allow the middle Sakura to hit level 3.
Yae's Elemental Burst,
Great Secret Art: Tenko Kenshin
deals powerful
Electro
AoE DMG to nearby opponents. When
using this skill, any
Sesshou Sakura
nearby will "unseal".
This means they will also deal additional AoE Electro DMG to enemies. With
Yae's 1st Ascension
The Shrine's Sacred Shade
you can then
immediately re-summon your Sesshou Sakura. This therefore gives the
following rotation:
Yakan Evocation: Sesshou Sakura
x3
Use other character buffs and abilities.
Great Secret Art: Tenko Kenshin
Yakan Evocation: Sesshou Sakura
x3

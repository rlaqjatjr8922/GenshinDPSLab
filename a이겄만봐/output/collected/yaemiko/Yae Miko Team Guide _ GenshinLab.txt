Yae Miko Team Overview
This Page provides the best Yae Miko Team information. Yae will be DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Yae and Fischl to make your own best team.
From SS tier to A tier, Yae best team can be formed in different ways including Catalyze, Thunder, Electro-Charged and Overload team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

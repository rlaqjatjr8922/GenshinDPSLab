Sethos Works Best As Electro On-Field DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Aggravate Team
- Aggravate can increase Sethos' damage based on his Elemental Mastery.
- Xilonen can greatly boost the Electro damage dealt and provide healing.
- Fischl provides Energy Particles for Sethos' Elemental Burst.
2nd
▼ Natlan Overload Team
- With the release of Natlan, Overload Sethos has become much better than before.
- Mavuika can buff Sethos' damage and apply Pyro Off-Field while Ororon can charge her Fighting Spirit.
- Ororon can trigger his Hypersense attack with Mavuika's Nightsoul-aligned Pyro attacks.
3rd
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- Hyperbloom for Sethos can be difficult to play since it's only good with his Charged Attack playstyle.
- The lack of a 2nd Electro means he will have trouble keeping his Burst up, thus his Normal Attack playstyle is not recommended.
Sethos is best used in Aggravate or Overload teams with Mavuika and Ororon. He can be used in Hyperbloom since his Charged Attack has good AoE but he will need a Crowd Control unit to group up enemies for his Charged Attacks to hit the enemy and Dendro cores.

Standard Aggravate team for Sethos with Fischl providing Energy particles for Sethos.
Rotation Of Aggravate Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Fischl and use her Skill to apply Electro
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks
3
Switch to Nahida and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Sethos and use his Skill then Burst
5
Deal damage with Normal Attacks
6
Use Sethos' Skill to regenerate Energy for him once buffs expire
7
Repeat from step 1
If using Sethos' Charged Attack playstyle, all you have to change is using his Burst and use his Charged Attacks instead of Normal Attacks. He will still need the Skill usage at the end of his rotation.

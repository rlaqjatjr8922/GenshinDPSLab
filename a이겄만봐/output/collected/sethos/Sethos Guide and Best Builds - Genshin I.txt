Sethos's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
2 different gameplay styles either focusing on Charged Attacking or his Elemental Burst.
Great Main DPS for Aggravate and Hyperbloom team comps as a result of his multi-target damage.
Weaknesses
Energy can be an even more important resource on Sethos to manage compared to most characters due to Charged Attacks consuming it.
Unable to move while Charging so shielding is incredibly important.

2.
Combos and How to Play Sethos
Sethos has 2 main gameplay styles:
Charged Attack spamming
Normal Attack spamming from his Elemental Burst
Charged Attack spamming is gameplay similar to
Ganyu
and
Tighnari
where you utilise his level 2 Charged Attack to deal
powerful damage. This charged time is reduced due to
Black Kite's Enigma
which consumes energy as a cost making it
near-instant. This is important because Sethos can not move around while
charging.
To manage this, Sethos needs to regularly use
Ancient Rite: The Thundering Sands
which only has an 8 second cooldown and grants 12 energy + 2 particles.
The other gameplay style is using his
Secret Rite: Twilight Shadowpiercer
which converts all Normal Attacks into
Electro
allowing you
a gameplay style more similar to
Yoimiya
.
Because each gameplay style consumes Energy, you can not really use both
at the same time.

1st Ascension
Black Kite's Enigma
When Aiming, the charging time is decreased by 0.285s based on each
    point of Sethos's current Elemental Energy. Charging time can be reduced
    to a minimum of 0.3s through this method and a maximum of 20 Energy can be
    tallied. If a Shadowpiercing Shot is fired, consume the tallied amount of
    Elemental Energy; if it is a Charge Level 1 shot, then consume 50% of the
    tallied amount of Elemental Energy.
4th Ascension
The Sand King's Boon
Sethos gains the "Scorching Sandshade" effect, increasing the DMG
    dealt by Shadowpiercing Shot by 700% of Sethos's Elemental Mastery. The
    Scorching Sandshade effect will be cancelled when any of the following
    conditions are met:
5s after a Shadowpiercing Shot first hits an opponent.
After 4 Shadowpiercing Shots strike opponents.
When a Shadowpiercing Shot affected by Scorching Sandshade first hits
    an opponent, Sethos will regain Scorching Sandshade after 15s.
Sethos requires a range of materials to ascend to the 6th ascension.
Starting from the second ascension he gains Elemental Mastery as he levels,
giving a total of 96 Bonus Elemental Mastery at the final ascension.

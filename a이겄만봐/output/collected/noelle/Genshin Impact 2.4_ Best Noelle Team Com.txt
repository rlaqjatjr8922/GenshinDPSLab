Noelle is a 4 star Geo Claymore character in Genshin Impact
. Overall, she is a flexible character that can easily fill in the gaps of any team. Besides her powerful shield and heal, Noelle can also work as a Main DPS with her powerful Burst. In this article, Gurugamer is going to showcase the best Noelle team comps for Geo DPS and Support.
Table of Contents
1. High-End Noelle Team Comps
2. Mid-Tier Noelle Team Comps
3. F2P Noelle Team Comp
Noelle needs a lot of investments to be effective.
These lineups focus on making use of Noelle's Burst to continuously deal strong Geo DMG against enemies. Overall, Noelle's main issue is damage. She heavily relies on constellations to boost her DMG potential and has low base damage before C6.

This is a C6 Noelle team comp building around her as the main DPS. Running Zhongli and Albedo as Energy support is recommended to get Noelle's burst uptime as close to 100% as possible. While Noelle can shield up on her own, in hard contents like Spiral Abyss floor 12, an additional shield is required.
Zhongli is the best shielder in the game.
Zhongli's shield also provides additional Geo and physical resistance on top of Geo resonance. Albedo serves as off-field damage support, providing a large amount of passive damage over a 30 seconds duration. Players don't even have to worry about the best gear on Zhongli and Albedo, since Noelle can keep the team's HP above 90%.
The last slot can be anything, however, Bennett is probably the best support for this team, as his damage boost is invaluable.

Itto and Noelle are the main DPS for this Noelle team comp 2.3 patch. Players need to alternate between them when one of their bursts is on CD. Gorou is probably the most critical component of the team - he provides Itto and Noelle Geo DMG Bonus(especially when C6) and DEF Bonus.
Gorou and Itto is a good duo who can pick up Noelle as a sub DPS.
With the full set of Archaic Petra, Gorou can further increase the team's GEO DMG bonus by 35% after picking crystals. Zhongli is mostly a flex pick whose main job is to shield and burst. He is also the Millelith wielder, who increases ATK of the whole team.

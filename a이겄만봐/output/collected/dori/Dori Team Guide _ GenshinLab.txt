Dori Team Overview
This Page provides the best Dori Team information. Dori is a Healer in most of the situation. Dori(C6) can be a Main DPS as the Elemental Skill provides Electro infusion
From SS tier to A tier, Dori’s best team can be formed in different ways including Thunder, Superconduct, Electro-Charged, Overload, Aggravate and Catalyze team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon, is a powerful force in Genshin Impact, but her true potential shines when paired with the right allies. To unlock her full potential and dominate the battlefield, consider these five potent team compositions:
Top 5 Teams for Raiden Shogun in Genshin Impact 4.3
Raiden Shogun, Bennett, Xiangling, Xingqiu
This budget-friendly powerhouse features Xiangling, Bennett, and Xingqiu. Their high-cost bursts are perfectly fueled by Raiden’s energy regeneration, while Bennett’s ATK buff and Xingqiu’s Hydro application trigger devastating elemental reactions. Focus on quick rotations and maximizing burst damage for each character.
Raiden Shogun, Yoimiya, Zhongli, Xingqiu
Yoimiya, Xingqiu, and Zhongli join Raiden Shogun in a dance of fire and lightning. Yoimiya’s Pyro fireworks trigger Overloaded and Vaporize alongside Xingqiu’s Hydro blades, while Zhongli shields the team and deals Geo damage. Raiden Shogun, once again, keeps the energy flowing, ensuring Yoimiya and Xingqiu never miss a beat.
Image via HoYoVerse
Raiden Shogun, Sara, Kazuha, Bennett
Go all-in on Raiden’s burst damage with Sara, Kazuha, and Bennett. Sara and Bennett buff Raiden’s ATK, while Kazuha shreds enemy defenses. This team demands a high-investment Raiden with maxed Energy Recharge and damage stats. But the payoff is explosive burst damage that melts enemies.
Raiden Shogun, Xingqiu, Zhongli, Hu Tao
Hu Tao, Xingqiu, and Zhongli’s Vaporize synergy is already potent, but Raiden adds another layer. Her burst fuel Hu Tao’s energy needs and deals additional damage during downtime. Zhongli’s shields keep everyone safe while Xingqiu sets up Vaporize. This team excels in sustained damage and burst-to-burst rotations.
Raiden Shogun, Eula, Zhongli, Diona
Eula and Raiden are a match made in Teyvat. Raiden solves Eula’s high burst cost issue, while her Electro Skill triggers Superconduct with Diona’s Cryo application, further boosting Eula’s physical damage. Diona’s healing and shielding capabilities round out this powerful duo.

Best Teams for Electro Traveler
Electro Traveler Works Best As Energy Support
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Catalyze Team
- Electro Traveler can restore Energy to the DPS.
- An Electro DPS will be able to activate Electro resonance and gain more Energy in rotations.
2nd
▼ Taser Team
- Electro Traveler and Ororon combo is great for Energy generation in the team.
- Furina is the Hydro sub-DPS and can greatly increase the damage of the team.
- Mizuki will be the on-field Swirl DPS that can heal and chain trigger Electro-Charged with her Anemo attacks.
3rd
▼ Hyperbloom
- Hyperbloom Raiden Shogun usually doesn't have her using Burst thus she is unable to restore Energy to her teammates.
- Electro Traveler can charge the active character's Energy.
Electro Traveler is a unique support character that can buff Energy Recharge and give Energy to their teammates.
However, their source for Energy regeneration is based on the Amulets dropped from enemies hit by their Skill. This means they are best used against big enemies and if battling against smaller enemies, Crowd Control is needed.

A standard Aggravate team. Electro Traveler can be used in a Spread team but will likely need a 2nd Electro. Kazuha is used since he is able to gather up enemies so that Electro Traveler's Amulets won't become scattered.
Rotation Of Catalyze Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Clorinde and use her Burst to gain max Bond of Life
2
Switch to Kazuha and use his tap Skill then Plunge Attack
3
Switch to Nahida and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Electro Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
5
Switch back to Clorinde and use her Skill twice
6
Deal damage with Normal Attacks and use her Skill when her Bond of Life reaches Max HP
7
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

Ororon and Electro Traveler is a great duo for flat Energy generation. Furina is the Hydro sub-DPS and Mizuki will be the on-field Swirl DPS that can heal to offset Furina's HP drain.
Rotation Of Taser Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Electro Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Mizuki and use her Burst then Skill
5
Move around and dodge enemy attacks while grabbing the Snacks to trigger healing or damage.
6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire
This rotation does not need to be strictly followed but you have to let Mizuki enter the field last as she is the on-field Swirl driver.

A variant of the Raiden Hyperbloom team. Nahida will be the on-field reaction driver while Raiden Shogun and Yelan will work together to trigger Hyperbloom. Electro Traveler is the support giving Energy Recharge and flat Energy restoration.
Rotation Of Hyperbloom
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida and use her Burst then Skill to mark enemies
2
Switch to Yelan and use her Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Raiden Shogun and use her Skill
4
Switch to Electro Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
5
Switch back to Nahida and apply Dendro with her Normal/Charged Attacks
6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire
Since this team has no good abilities for grouping up enemies, you can use Yelan's Skill to gather the Amulets if wanting so.

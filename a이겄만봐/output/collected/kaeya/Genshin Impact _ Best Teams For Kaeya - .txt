The objective of this team is to funnel all buffs to Kaeya. The Physical RES of the enemy can be reduced with Superconduct & Zhongli's shield.
Physical RES Cap
The Physical RES debuff multipliers can stack and reduce an enemy's Physical RES to 0%. However, excess multipliers beyond 0% can still stack but would have a diminishing return.
Multipliers Work Differently
With the current characters that can reduce an enemy's Physical RES, reducing it beyond 0% is already possible but this works differently to Ruin enemies as this enemy type has a high Physical RES.
Rotation Of Physical DPS Kaeya Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Zhongli's Skill for shield
2
Switch to Raiden Shogun
3
Use Raiden Shoguns's Skill for a coordinated attack that can apply Electro for triggering Superconduct
4
Switch to Mika
5
Use Mika's Burst & Skill for buffs
6
Switch to Kaeya
7
Use Kaeya's Burst & Skill for Cryo application
8
Use Kaeya's Normal/Charged Attack until the buffs are gone
9
Return to step 1
From time to time, you can switch to Zhongli to refresh the shield's duration & Raiden for Electro application. You can also use Zhongli's Burst for crowd control.
When To Use Raiden's Burst
Use Raiden's Burst when Resolve stacks are full. For 7 seconds, Raiden's damage is greatly amplified and ATK SPD is improved. Raiden's Burst is also useful for removing the shield of Abyss Mages that are not immune to Electro.

Bennett is only useful for Kaeya if he has a constellation below C6. When he is already at C6, he will infuse Kaeya with Pyro if she is inside the Burst AoE and this cancels out the Physical DMG. Bennett's
C6 makes him incompatible with Kaeya
.

The objective of this team is to trigger Superconduct & Hyperbloom simultaneously.
Hyperbloom Completely Ignores Enemy DEF
Since the Hyperbloom can completely ignore the DEF of the enemy, only Kaeya can take advantage of the debuff from Superconduct.
Nahida's Circlet Depends On Elemental Mastery
If you can reach 1,000 EM, you can opt for CRIT DMG instead of CRIT Rate since Nahida can gain a maximum of 24% CRIT Rate through EM alone.

8
Use Kaeya's Normal/Charged Attack until the buffs are gone
From time to time,
switch to Kuki Shinobu for healing
since she sacrifices a portion of her health whenever she uses her Skill.

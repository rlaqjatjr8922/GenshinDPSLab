Kaeya has tons of weapons he can use in-game. These can include crit-stat five-star weapons, which will significantly boost his damage, or four-star weapons, which will make his Energy management easier.

This is one of the more universal weapon choices in the game, since it provides Crit stats and a general Elemental DMG Bonus, which can be used on anyone, even Kaeya.
CRIT Damage 44.1%
Gain a 12% Elemental DMG Bonus for all elements and receive the might of the Mistsplitter’s Emblem.

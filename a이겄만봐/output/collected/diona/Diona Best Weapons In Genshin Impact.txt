Genshin Impact
. She also buffs her team if players are able to build her to cast her Elemental Burst frequently. To make the most of what Diona can offer to her team, here are the
best weapons for Diona in
Genshin Impact
.

For newer players or those who only want her to shield and heal,
Recurve Bow
is an acceptable weapon for Diona in

. This
3-star Bow in

increases Diona’s HP, which boosts both her shield and healing.
3
Favonius Warbow
Primary Stat
454 Base ATK
Secondary Stat
61.3% Energy Recharge
R5 Effect
CRIT hits have a 100% chance to generate a small amount of Elemental Particles, which will regenerate 6 Energy for the character. Can only occur once every 6s.
Favonius Warbow
is one of the
best bows for Diona in
Genshin Impact
. Having around 61% Energy Recharge is all that Diona needs to keep casting her Elemental Burst on every rotation. Moreover, when Diona triggers a Crit hit, she will summon even more Elemental Particles, which will make it even easier to cast her Elemental Burst. The only downside to Favonius Warbow is that Diona is not a character with a lot of Crit Rate and can’t score Crit on every hit.

Sacrificial Bow
Primary Stat
565 Base ATK
Secondary Stat
30.6% Energy Recharge
R5 Effect
After dealing damage to an opponent with an Elemental Skill, the skill has a 80% chance to end its own CD. Can only occur once every 16s.

Although
Sacrificial Bow
has a lower Energy Recharge than Favonius Warbow, it’s still a slightly better choice since it allows Diona to cast her Elemental Skill twice per rotation. This will get Diona to summon double the Elemental Particles and
quickly refill her Elemental Burst
.
1
Elegy for the End
Primary Stat
608 Base ATK
Secondary Stat
55.1% Energy Recharge
Effect
Increases Elemental Mastery by 60.
When the Elemental Skills or Elemental Bursts of the character wielding this weapon hit opponents, that character gains a Sigil of Remembrance. This effect can be triggered once every 0.2s and can be triggered even if said character is not on the field.
When you possess 4 Sigils of Remembrance, all of them will be consumed and all nearby party members will obtain the "Millennial Movement: Farewell Song" effect for 12s.
"Millennial Movement: Farewell Song" increases Elemental Mastery by 100 and increases ATK by 20%. Once this effect is triggered, you will not gain Sigils of Remembrance for 20s.
Of the many effects of the "Millennial Movement," buffs of the same type will not stack.
Elegy for the End
has everything Diona needs during combat. Starting from the 5-star weapon’s Energy Recharge Sub-stat, Elegy for the End ensures that Diona can cast her Elemental Burst on every rotation. This will increase the team’s ATK% and Elemental Mastery through her
Noblesse Oblige artifacts set
and C6. The best part is that Elegy for the End will give Diona exactly those passive effects to further increase the team’s ATK% and Elemental Mastery by an extra 20% and 100, respectively.

Anemo Traveler Works Best As Swirl Support
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Swirl Support
- Classic team for Anemo support characters.
- Anemo Traveler uses VV to increase the Elemental damage RES shred on enemies.
- Ranged DPS or characters with mobility can be used if using Anemo Traveler's Burst.
2nd
▼ Taser On-Field Driver
- Anemo Traveler's Skill and Burst can be used to chain Electro-Charged reactions.
- Sacrificial Sword is best for Skill spam.
- A character with mobility skill can be used to chase enemies without using Stamina.
Anemo Traveler is a crowd control character who can use VV to increase the damage taken by enemies. Their Skill can pull in enemies for easy grouping but their Burst can push enemies away, needing mobility skills to quickly catch up to enemies.

Anemo Traveler can apply Anemo consistently and is an option for Swirl support for any PECH DPS. Yelan's Skill can be utilized to quickly reach enemies blown away by Anemo Traveler's Burst.
Rotation Of Swirl Support
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino and use her Skill to mark enemies
2
Switch to Anemo Traveler and use their Burst
3
Switch to Yelan and use her Skill to reach enemies then use her Burst
4
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch back to Arlecchino and use her Charged Attack to gain Pyro infusion
6
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage
7
Once buffs start to expire, end Arlecchino's turn using her Burst
8
Repeat from 1

Anemo Traveler's Skill can be used as on-field Taser reaction driver. Shinobu's healing will offset Furina's HP drain for maximizing Furina's summons damage. Ororon on Scroll set can buff all Elemental DMG dealt by this team.
Rotation Of Taser On-Field Driver
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Shinobu and use her Skill for healing
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Anemo Traveler and use their Skill to chain trigger Electro-Charged
5
Reuse Anemo Traveler's Skill once Sacrificial Sword refreshes the cooldown
6
Repeat from 1
Using Anemo Traveler's Burst is not recommended unless you are willing to use Stamina and time to reach the blown away enemies.

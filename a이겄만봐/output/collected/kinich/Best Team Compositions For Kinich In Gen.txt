Kinich is also a limited Dendro Claymore five-star character who is an on-field main damage dealer. To cook the best Kinich team compositions, you must understand the recipe first. The recipe for a good Kinich team is simple, it is one that works with Burning or Burgeon.
Kinich's Best Overall Team: Kinich And Emilie
Emilie could be called Kinich's best friend. She is one of the
best off-field damage dealers
in Genshin Impact, and she works best with
Burning
, which goes perfectly with Kinich. He takes the field, while Emile provides staggering amounts of off-field damage, making a powerful team that easily shreds even strong enemies.
Kinich
: Main DPS (On-field)
Emilie
: DPS (Off-field)
Xiangling
/
Dehya
: DPS (Off-field)
Bennett
: Attack Buffer and Healer
Xiangling
provides ample off-field damage and
Bennett
buffs the team's damage and both of them enable the
Burning reaction
.
Dehya
can be used instead of Xiangling, as Dehya can provide valuable utilities such as
resistance to interruption
.
Equipping
4pc Deepwood
on
Xiangling/Dehya
will let the team do more overall Dendro damage because of Deepwood's artifact set effect of
decreasing enemy Dendro resistance by 30%
.
Playing this team is very simple:

Kinich's Four Star Team
Kinich is a very
free-to-play, friendly character
owing to his high raw damage and simple conditions for a team. Using a team of Bennett, Xiangling, Collei and Kinich will be able to carry you even through the end game.
Kinich
: Main DPS (On-field)
Collei
: DPS (Off-field)
Xiangling
: DPS (Off-field)

Thoma

at the cost of decreased damage
.
Having high constellations on
Xiangling and Bennett
will have a huge impact on how this team performs in terms of damage output.
This team is simple to play and rotate and just uses skills when needed.

Kinich Burgeon Team
Kinich is a very flexible character that can
fit into a wide variety of team compositions
and one of them is a Burgeon team composition. Burgeon is a Dendro reaction that happens when the
bloom cores created by Dendro and Hydro reaction comes into contact with Pyro
.
Kinich
: Main DPS (On-field)
Yelan
: DPS (Off-field)
Thoma
: DPS (Off-field)
Baizhu
: Shielder and Healer
This team can have a wide variety. Yelan can be swapped with
Kokomi/Xinqiu/Furina
and Baizhu can be swapped with any Dendro character that can provide value to the team while being
off-field.
The team requires fairly
low investment
, since characters expect Kinich only require
Elemental Mastery
on their artifacts.
There is no specific rotation for this team, as characters tend to be switched at random when they are needed. Just make sure to have a skill ready that can provide
hydro application
while Kinich is on-field and have another skill that can provide
off-field Pyro damage
.
An example of the rotation for the above team composition would be;
Use Baizhu's
Elemental Burst and Elemental Skill.
Use Yelan's
Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
Use Thoma's
Burst and then his Elemental Skill.
Defeat the enemies with Kinich.
Kinich Hyper Carry
If you are looking for a team to
maximize Kinich's damage from his Elemental Skill,
then this team might be for you.
Furina, Siegwinne, and Bennett can greatly buff Kinich's damage and, since Furina, Siegwinne, and Bennett can heal each other, acquiring maximum fanfare stacks from Furina is very easy.
Kinich
: Main DPS (On-field)
Furina
: DPS (Off-field), damage buffer and healing
Sigewinne
: Damage buffer and healing
Bennett
: Damage buffer and healing
Make sure to occasionally switch to Bennett and use his
Elemental Skill to trigger burning
. Having
C6 Bennett
will make this team composition work very well.
The rotations for this team composition can vary widely depending on the combat situation. Generally, you should aim to
trigger Burning and Burgeon
, while making sure the buffs from Furina, Siegwinne and Bennett are going into Kinich.
Use Kinich's
Elemental Burst.
Use Bennett's
Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
Use Furina's
Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
Use Siegwinne's
Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
Enjoy your play with Kinich.

Rating
Description
▼ Mavuika Melt Team
★★★★★
- Xilonen is one of the few Natlan supports that can quickly stack up Mavuika's Fighting Spirit to charge her Elemental Burst.
- Xilonen can shred Elemental RES for Mavuika, which amounts to a significant amount when paired with
Citlali's
RES shred.
- Arguably Xilonen's most meta team due to Mavuika's general overall strength.
▼ Elemental (PHEC) DPS Support Team
★★★★・
- Xilonen has a very generalist set of abilities that consolidates healing and RES shred, which lends itself to her flexibility.
- She can support most Pyro, Hydro, Electro or Cryo On-Field DPSes who care about RES shred, don't have strict teammate requirements, and that want DMG% buffs from the
Cinder City
artifact set.
▼ Navia Support Team
★★★・・
-
Navia
wants to be paired with PHEC teammates in order to get Crystallize shards for her enhanced Skill, allowing Xilonen to be an effective pairing for her since she can activate her RES shred in most Navia teams.
Xilonen is a very good Elemental DMG support that can be used for nearly all PHEC DPS
. Not only can she greatly boost Elemental DMG dealt as result of shredding the enemy's Elemental resistances, she can also provide sustain pairs well with
Furina
, who drains teamwide HP to generate Fanfare stacks for her DMG% buff.
Interestingly, Xilonen can also function as an On-Field Geo DPS, but her baseline performance in this role is known to be underwhelming in comparison to the Geo DPSes like Navia and
Itto
. Unless you're willing to invest more into Xilonen vertically, it's best to maintain her position as a support instead.

With the right combos, this team variation allows for Mavuika to output some of the best DPS in the game. Melting the first hit of her Elemental Burst is essential, so it's important to ensure that the enemy has Cryo applied to them prior to using her Burst.
Rotation Of Mavuika Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mavuika. Use her Skill (tap).
2
Use Bennett's Skill into Burst combo.
3
Switch to Xilonen. Use her Skill then follow it up a Normal Attack (x2).
4
Swap to Citlali. Start her combo with her Skill, Burst, then Normal Attack (x1).
5
Switch back to Mavuika. Use her Elemental Burst. You can jump after to allow the Cryo time to re-apply before using Charged Attack (x8).
└
Note that this is the simpler Mavuika Melt combo.
6
Repeat from 1.
Waiting a little bit in between Charged Attacks while you're on Mavuika can allow the Cryo application to catch-up, so you won't accidentally cause Citlali to become the Melt trigger instead.

Xilonen can be flexed alongside most Pyro, Hydro, Electro or Cryo On-Field DPSes that appreciate what she has to offer. Mualani is her other best pairing due to how her passive helps to stack one of Mualani's passives as well, apart from enabling the full Cinder City 4-piece effect and providing RES shred.
Rotation Of Elemental (PHEC) DPS Support Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mualani. Do Normal Attack (x1).
2
Swap to Xilonen. Use her Skill, then Normal Attack (x2). Burst is optional if you need the healing.
3
Use Sucrose's Skill.
4
Switch to Mavuika. Deploy her Skill (tap), then Burst.
5
Swap to Mualani. Use her Elemental Skill. Collide with an enemy 3 times to stack up the Sharky Bite. Press Normal Attack to consume the stacks after you've fully stacked to 3. Repeat this process two more times.
6
If Mualani's Burst is up, you can deploy it here. Repeat from step 1 otherwise.
Mualani's rotation is fairly straightforward, but her gameplay itself may be a little disorienting in practice due to needing to collide with an enemy for stacks. It should become easier to do on instinct after gaining enough experience playing with her gameplay loop.

Since Navia needs PHEC teammates to create Crystallize shards for her Crystal Shrapnel, Xilonen is able to get PHEC Source Samples, therefor triggering her Elemental RES Shred instead of her DPS mode. Note that Xilonen is also able to shred Geo RES in this team since she will always have a Geo Source Sample active. This setup gets even better if one other teammate contributes respectable damage themselves.
Rotation Of Navia Support Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mavuika. Use her Skill (tap).
2
Swap to Xilonen. Use her Skill, then Normal Attack (x2). Burst is optional if you happen to need more healing.
3
Swap to Bennett to do his Skill (tap) into Burst combo.
4
Use Mavuika's Burst for the DMG% Buff.
5
Field Navia to do her usual combo: Skill (hold), followed by Normal Attack (x4) or (x5). You can dash after the 3rd attack to cancel the long animation.
6
Switch to Mavuika. Re-deploy her Skill (tap) to resume Pyro application.
7
Switch to Xilonen again. Repeat step 2.
8
Return to Navia. Repeat step 5.
9
Repeat from step 1.
Since Navia has two charges of her Elemental Skill, you can re-apply Xilonen's Geo RES shred before using her Skill combo a second time. Because Mavuika's Off-field Pyro has a short duration, remember to also use Mavuika's Skill (tap) before swapping back into Xilonen again, this is to maintain the Off-Field Pyro application.
Substitute Characters
Character
Sub
Details
Mavuika
Durin
Furina
- Mavuika contributes her own personal damage apart from the Pyro application, but she can be replaced by Durin for the same purpose, with the performance being relatively the same if you aren't On-Fielding Mavuika for a period of time in this team.
- Durin also provides more RES shred for Navia in his Confirmation of Purity mode.
- Alternatively, a buffer like Furina also pairs well with Navia, and Xilonen can reliably stack her Fanfare faster with her Burst.
Bennett
Iansan
- Bennett is hard to replace for Navia since his ATK% buff is well sought after by any ATK scaling DPS like Navia.
- You could make do with Iansan, but because her buff is tied to movement, you would need to dash in between Normal Attacks in order to maintain it for a time.
Navia
Noelle
- Unlike
Itto
who will usually want to have mostly Geo teammates, Noelle doesn't have any strict teammate criteria to be met, so she can be a perfect Navia alternative who'd use the same teammates.
- Note: Despite scaling off of DEF, when paired with Xilonen, it's best to avoid teaming her with
Gorou
so you're able to get PHEC samples for Xilonen's RES Shred.
Usually Navia will prefer having two Pyro teammates in order to activate Pyro Resonance, which gives an ATK% buff.
Durin
is the best Mavuika alternative for Navia, but he becomes even better if you instead slot in
Albedo
in this team instead of Xilonen, this is to activate his Hexerei passive which gives more RES Shred, only enabled with another Hexerei teammate and once you finish his Witch's Homework task.

Best Weapons for Kaveh in Genshin Impact
Kaveh’s best weapons are mostly four-stars (Image via HoYoverse)
Kaveh, unfortunately, does not have many five-star weapons that work well with him. This is due to his preferred stat being Elemental Mastery, which most of them do not provide. However, there are many four-star options instead.
Best 5-star Weapons for Kaveh in Genshin Impact
As mentioned before, there are not many noteworthy five-star weapon picks for Kaveh. The only one that can even remotely stand out is
Skyward Pride
. The rest will, of course, improve Kaveh’s damage, but it is already so low that it is not worth using on him. Thus, you should consider four-star options instead.
Note:
The above weapon effects are at Refinement 1 and level 90.

Fischl is a 4 star Electro Bow character in Genshin Impact
. She is an excellent sub DPS and off-field damage dealer/enabler, with both her skill and burst staying on the field after swapping. Furthermore, she also provides a lot of Electro particles, giving a large amount of energy to teammates.
In this article, Gurugamer.com is going to showcase the best Fischl team comps for Electro DPS and support.
Table of Contents
1. High-End Fischl Team Comps
2. Mid-Tier Fischl Team Comps

C6 Fischl team comp can deal decent DPS.
The only weakness of Fischl is probably her long burst cooldown and constellation requirements for utility. As she's a 4 star character, you would be able to get her to C6 eventually.

These high-end Fischl team comps are going to focus on building Fischl as a DPS.

With Fischl's ability to trigger elemental reactions constantly using Oz, she can get away with a team of both Hydro and Cryo. Combined with Mona's Hydro, she can clear out waves of mobs with Electro Charge.
Mona's Hydro application allow Fischl DPS team comp to function.
On the other hand, against single target bosses, proccing Superconduct with Diona's Cryo application is the best. As Fischl has ATK as a bonus stat, playing her as a physical DPS is actually above average.
The supporting cast also has crowd control from Venti and healing from Diona. Players can replace Venti with Kazuha if needed.

Zhongli's shield is the strongest in the game. Furthermore, its hold version also provides a 20% physical and elemental resistance reduction debuff to nearby enemies. This boost both Eula's physical damage, Rosaria's Elemental burst and Fischl's skill damage.

Pyro resonance from this team comp boosts Fischl's damage moderately. Combine that with Bennett's attack boost, this team should be able to deal decent damage even without reactions. By abusing Bennett and Xiangling's short CD, players can proc overload very frequently to deal damage.
Venti's main role in this comp is crowd control, grouping up enemies for the combo to clear them out. Players can replace him with a Cryo character to trigger Superconduct for bosses.

This is a super fun Fischl support lineup, with Childe being the main DPS alongside 3 sub DPS. The Fischl Childe combo can easily inflict electro charge constantly, dealing good damage.
Beidou would be the on-field Sub DPS when Childe's burst is on cooldown. She also enables Electro resonance alongside Fischl for extra energy regeneration. Bennett is the final component of the lineup, occasionally triggering Overload and boosting the team's damage. He can also heal when needed.
Fischl is one of the few characters who can support Childe.
This team comp is fairly versatile - outside of Childe and Fischl, you can slot in whichever character you want, such as Venti, Diona or Qiqi. However, a good sub DPS for Childe is a must, as his cooldown is too high.

As the esteemed Chief Justice of Fontaine, Neuvillette wields a Hydro vision. You can catch glimpses of his character in the "Overture Teaser: The Final Feast." He plays a pivotal role in version 4.0 quests.
In case you manage to acquire Neuvillette during his banner event, it's essential to consider his ideal build and team composition. Much like Alhaitham, Neuvillette is known for his seriousness and influential position.
Find out the best Neuvillette team comp and more.
The community buzzes with excitement over his confirmation as a playable character, with expectations of him becoming a formidable Hydro specialist. Leakers have provided a sneak peek at his abilities, fueling speculation about the optimal Neuvillette build and his role in Fontaine.
Check out Neuvillette team comp and best build below.

3. Best team comps for Neuvillette
Neuvillette's versatility makes him a valuable addition to various team compositions. He seamlessly mingles into teams that require Hydro damage, including freeze and vaporize setups. However, his remarkable potential as a Hydro DPS character positions him ideally within Bloom teams, where his abilities truly shine.
You can take a look at some alternative choices for the best Neuvillette team comp below:
3.1. Freeze team - Neuvillette, Ganyu, Mona, Diona
In this team composition, Neuvillette's Hydro application combines harmoniously with Ganyu's Cryo damage to freeze enemies effectively.
Equip Neuvillette with these characters and you will get a Freeze team.
Furthermore, Mona's Elemental Burst can be utilized to enhance the damage output of Neuvillette's Burst ability.
This synergy creates a powerful and strategic dynamic for controlling and defeating foes in various encounters.
3.2. Vaporize team -
Neuvillette, Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu
Neuvillette's Hydro application plays a major role in vaporizing Xiangling's Pyro damage, resulting in potent elemental reactions.
Bennett's Elemental Burst provides an attack buff to the team. Meanwhile, Xingqiu's Elemental Skill comes into play, applying Hydro to off-field enemies. His skill can further enhance the team's ability to deal damage through Vaporize reactions. This well-coordinated synergy optimizes the team's effectiveness in combat scenarios.
Candidates for a Neuvillette team focusing on Vaporize.
3.3. Hyperbloom Team -
Neuvillete, Nahida, Raiden Shogun, Fischl/Xinqiu
This Neuvillette team comp will mostly rely on between Dendro and Hydro elemental reactions. Dendro cores will be subsequently activated via Electro attacks for substantial damage output.
This strategy is presently regarded as one of the strongest DPS reactions in the game, emphasizing the team's formidable damage-dealing capabilities.
3.4. Electro-Charged Team - Neuvillette, Kujou Sara, Zhongli, Kuki Shinobu
This team composition presents a strategic blend of characters designed to maximize the benefit to Neuvillette. He will take on the role of DPS.
Neuvillette best teams can also include Zhongli.
With Kujou Sara as both Sub DPS and Support, Neuvillette is likely to receive substantial damage boosts and elemental synergy, amplifying her damage output.
Kuki Shinobu and Zhongli, as Support characters, provide valuable utilities and shields, further safeguarding Neuvillette during battles.
This balance of damage and support ensures that Neuvillette can focus on dealing high damage without compromising on defense or elemental versatility, making this team composition a promising choice for optimizing her potential in Genshin Impact adventures.

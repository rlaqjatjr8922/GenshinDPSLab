In this article, we will discuss some of the best team comps for Neuvillette in Genshin Impact, taking into account his strengths and weaknesses. We will cover everything from Bloom teams to Electro-Charged teams to Freeze teams, so you can find the perfect team comp for your playstyle.
Understanding Neuvillette
Before diving into team compositions, let’s understand what makes Neuvillette special:
Unique Charged Attack Mechanic:
Neuvillette’s primary source of damage comes from his charged attack, where he generates a formidable torrent of water and unleashes it upon his foes. However, during this state, he remains vulnerable.
HP Drain Mechanic:
Neuvillette possesses an HP drain mechanic, causing him to lose HP over time, even when not taking damage. This adds an extra layer of complexity to managing his health.
Given Neuvillette’s unique characteristics, protecting him and enhancing his damage output are paramount for success.

Neuvillette, Bennett, Xiangling, Kazuha/Venti
This classic Vaporise team aims to trigger the Vaporise reaction. While it can be challenging to determine which character triggers Vaporise, both Neuvillette and Xiangling contribute significantly to the team’s total damage output. Bennett provides healing and support, while Kazuha or Venti adds crowd control and elemental synergy.
Neuvillette, Fischl, Beidou, Kazuha
This electrifying team enables the Electro-Charged reaction. Beidou and Fischl serve as formidable sub-DPS characters, synergizing seamlessly with Neuvillette’s abilities. Kazuha, an Anemo character, helps lower enemy resistances with the Viridescent Venerer set and boosts team damage. While Kazuha is recommended, other Anemo users like Sucrose or Venti can fill this role.
Neuvillette, Ganyu, Layla, Kazuha
This team focuses on creating a freeze-centric strategy with Neuvillette. Thanks to his high Hydro application, Neuvillette can effectively trigger freezes. Ganyu, Layla, and Kazuha complement this strategy, with Ganyu’s Cryo abilities further enhancing the freezing potential.
Neuvillette, Nahida/Dendro Traveler, Nilou, Yao Yao/Baizhu
Neuvillette’s inclusion elevates the already high damage potential of the Nilou Bloom team. This composition relies on generating Dendro Blooms, making it crucial to have two Dendro characters. Neuvillette’s Elemental Mastery (EM) stats are valuable, as he contributes to Bloom creation while dealing substantial Hydro damage.
Neuvillette, Yelan, Mona, Kazuha/Venti
This team focuses on maximizing Neuvillette’s Hydro damage output. Yelan’s burst synergizes with Neuvillette’s abilities, and Mona’s burst further enhances the team’s overall damage. Kazuha or Venti can reduce enemy Hydro resistance and provide additional Hydro damage bonuses.
Neuvillette, Zhongli, Fischl, Nahida
This team configuration is built around creating Bloom cores, which can trigger the powerful Hyperbloom reaction in combination with Fischl. Hyperbloom is one of the game’s most potent reactions, and Neuvillette excels at facilitating it. Zhongli’s shields offer protection to Neuvillette, but if you require more healing, Kuki Shinobu can replace Fischl.
Remember, team compositions can be tailored to your playstyle and the characters you have available. Feel free to experiment with different combinations, adapting the team to suit specific challenges or preferences.

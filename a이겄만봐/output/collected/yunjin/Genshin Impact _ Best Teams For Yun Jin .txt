Yun Jin Works Best As a Support for Normal Attack DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Support Yun Jin
- Yun Jin is paired with a Normal Attack DPS like Arlecchino to maximize on her buff.
- If unable to have 4 Elements in a team, Geo Resonance can help in buffing.
2nd
▼ F2P Yun Jin Team
- F2P team for Yun Jin that uses 4 star characters and weapons.
- For free-to-play DPS choices, there are Yanfei for Vaporize/Melt, Razor for Superconduct, and Noelle for Geo Resonance.
Since Yun Jin gives additional damage to Normal Attacks, she's best used with DPS characters that deal a lot of Normal Attack damage or use it in their rotations.

As a support character, Yun Jin is best used with a DPS that uses a lot of Normal Attacks like Arlecchino. The ideal team comp for Yun Jin should have 4 different Elements, but since Arlecchino needs a shielder, there are only 3 Elements in this team. However,
the Geo Resonance effect can make up for the lower DEF buff from Yun Jin.
Rotation for Support Yun Jin
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino and use her Skill
2
Switch to Zhongli and use his Skill (Hold) to create a shield
3
Switch to Yelan and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Yun Jin and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch back to Arlecchino and use her Charged Attack to infuse her attacks with Pyro
6
Deal damage with Normal Attacks and/or Charged Attacks
7
Use Arlecchino's Burst once buffs end
8
Repeat from Step 1
Arlecchino has to use her Skill first when fighting against a boss, but when fighting against multiple enemies, her Skill can instead be used after Yun Jin's turn.

This is an F2P team for Yun Jin that uses only 4-star characters and 4-star weapons. Dodoco Tales is from a previous event but can be replaced by
The Widsith
or
Mappa Mare
. Change Yanfei's Sands to ATK if not using Dodoco Tales.
Rotation for F2P Yun Jin Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Yanfei and use her Normal Attack to apply Pyro
2
Switch to Xingqiu and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Lan Yan and use her Skill to create a shield
4
Switch to Yun Jin and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch back to Yanfei and use her Burst
6
Deal damage with Normal Attacks and use her Charged Attack when she has full stacks
7
Use Yanfei's Skill to gain stacks while dealing damage
8
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs end
As much as possible, make Lan Yan Swirl both Hydro and Pyro to boost the team's damage. However, this can be hard to achieve when there is only one enemy.

If using Noelle, have at least 2 supports that are of non-Geo Elements to help boost Yun Jin's buff and Noelle's damage.

Kokomi Overview
This Page provides the Best Kokomi Build information.
Kokomi is the Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island. All of the island’s affairs are at this young lady’s fingertips.
Kokomi
is a powerful healer in Genshin Impact. Kokomi DPS Build and Support Build are generally accepted by most travelers. Her elemental burst will heal all the team members while she is dishing out damage.
Kokomi
has different ways to help the party. Not only can she heal the party and dish out the damage by using the DPS build but also she can trigger Elemental Reaction by using the Elemental Skill.
In Genshin Impact, Kokomi is a 5 Stars rarity Hydro character currently in A tier.

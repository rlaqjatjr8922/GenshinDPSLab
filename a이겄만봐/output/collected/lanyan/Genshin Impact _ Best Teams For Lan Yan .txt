Lan Yan Can Be Built for Support or DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Hypercarry Team with support Lan Yan
- Lan Yan's ability to hold VV while providing shield is good for PECH DPS
- Her Burst's crowd control can help gather up enemies
- Best use of Lan Yan's powerful sustain and support capabilities.
2nd
▼ Swirl DPS Lan Yan
- EM Lan Yan can deal good Swirl damage and increase her Skill and Burst damage
- Full constellations can increase her DPS but not entirely required, she's fine even with C0
3rd
▼ DPS Lan Yan with support Faruzan
- Nahida's EM buff and Faruzan's Anemo DMG buff along with her RES shred can increase Lan Yan's damage greatly
- Anemo Resonance and Barbara's passive can decrease stamina consumption, allowing Lan Yan to use her Charged Attacks more
Lan Yan is a flexible character who can be built as a shield support or DPS. As support, she can give shield and hold VV to shred the Elemental RES of enemies. As DPS, she can be the main damage dealer or a hybrid Swirl DPS/Support.
Both her support and DPS builds have equal value in a team, but support Lan Yan is easier to build due to only needing ATK stats

A standard Vaporize Arlecchino team. Lan Yan's Skill dash can easily reach enemies and make it easier for melee DPS to deal damage quickly. VV and Thrilling Tales can buff the DPS' damage greatly.
Rotation of Hypercarry Team with support Lan Yan
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Yelan and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Lan Yan and use her Burst then Skill twice
4
Switch to Arlecchino and use her Skill then Charged Attack to gain infusion
5
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage
6
Once her Bond of Life goes below 30% of her Max HP, use Burst then Skill and finally Charged Attack to regain infusion
7
Repeat from 1 after the off-field characters' Burst/shield expires
6th step can be skipped if your team's buffs have expired, but remember to use Arlecchino's Burst to deal big damage before switching back to Yelan

Electro-Charged is the best reaction for EM-scaling DPS, but other reactions can work as well. Lan Yan can use Gilded Dreams and
Widsith
(only if it has high refines) in this team. However, VV is able to give Anemo DMG Bonus and Swirl DMG Bonus. Since Key and Elegy are able to buff EM,  buffing Lan Yan's Elemental Damage with Cinder City set will increase her overall damage.
Rotation of Swirl DPS Lan Yan
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Shinobu and use her Skill
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Lan Yan and use Skill twice to gain a shield
5
Deal damage using any of her Skills or Normal/Charged Attacks
6
Repeat from 1 after buffs/summons expire
Electro-Charged does not have a strict rotation requirement. Any of the Hydro/Electro characters can go first but make sure Ororon goes before Lan Yan so he can charge her Energy. Lan Yan can be swapped in any time if the Hydro and Electro off-field damage dealers are placed

No.

5

6
Deal damage using any of her Skills or Normal/Charged Attacks
7
Repeat from 1 once the team's buffs expire

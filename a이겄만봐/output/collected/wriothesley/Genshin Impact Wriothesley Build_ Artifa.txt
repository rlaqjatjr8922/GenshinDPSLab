Best Weapons For Wriothesley
Best 3 Stars Catalyst Weapons
For newbies, you can't find 4, 5 stars weapons at low AR. If you just got
Wriothesley
as your first 5-star character and plan to use him as a DPS you can also use these 3-star weapons to deal some DMG on creep in the early game.
1. Emerald Orb
Even though this Catalyst has Sub Stat as EM you can get an ATK buff by 20% by creating Frozen reaction.
Best 4 Stars Catalyst Weapons
When you get higher AR you will start getting 4-star weapons. They are P2P or Craft weapons or Battle Pass to use if you don't have Primogem to do wishes. Here are a few weapons that are suitable for Wriothesley's play style. Here we are looking for CRIT DMG
and also with ability to buff normal/charged ATK will be a big plus.
1. The Widsith
Greate catalysts that have CRIT DMG as sub-stats with weapon effects that increase ATK and elemental DMG as well as Elemental Mastery.
2. Dodoco Tale
This catalyst will buff his Charge ATK and provide some additional ATK% as sub-stats.
3. Flowing Purity
Buff Elemental DMG and provide additional ATK%.
Best 5 Stars Catalyst Weapons
The only way to get a 5-star weapon for Wriothesley is by wishing. It will randomly drop when you are doing a wish if you are a lucky one. Here are some 5 stars bows that are great for him including his signature bow. So we looking for
ATK%
or
CRIT DMG
and some abilities to buff his Charged ATK.
1. Cashflow Super Vision
This is Wriothesley signature bow that fits him the most. The Catalyst provides a huge amount of CRIT DMG with abilities to increase his HP and when his HP changes, Charged ATK DMG will increase (Max 3 stacks), and if he reaches 3 stacks his ATK SPD will increase.
2.Tulaytulah's Remembrance
Provide good amount of CRIT DMG and buff Normal SPD and DMG.

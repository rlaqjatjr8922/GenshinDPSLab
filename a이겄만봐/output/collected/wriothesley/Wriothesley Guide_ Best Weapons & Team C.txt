Wriothesley, the groundbreaking Cryo catalyst user from Fontaine, has solidified his position as a premier main DPS in Genshin Impact's current meta. His unique blend of relentless Cryo application and a high-risk, high-reward HP-fluctuation playstyle offers a thrilling and potent combat experience. As the first of his kind, he opens up dynamic team compositions that rely less on Energy management and more on sustained, aggressive pressure. Mastering his kit involves embracing his health-shifting mechanics to unlock devastating damage potential, making proper build optimization absolutely essential for any serious player looking to dominate the Spiral Abyss or overworld challenges.

👥 Best Team Comps For Wriothesley
Building a cohesive team around Wriothesley is all about enabling his strengths and mitigating his risks. A standard team structure should include:
Wriothesley (Main DPS) + Healer + Off-Field Sub-DPS + Flex Support
. Here are the most effective archetypes:
1. Permafreeze Team (Top Tier Crowd Control)
❄️💧
This comp focuses on locking down groups of enemies, providing immense safety and control.
Core Concept:
Wriothesley applies constant Cryo, while a Hydro character Freezes enemies in place.
Example Team:
Wriothesley +
Yelan/Xingqiu
(Hydro application) +
Kazuha/Sucrose
(VV Shred & grouping) +
Charlotte/Diona
(Healing & Cryo Resonance).
Why it works:
Frozen enemies can't fight back, allowing Wriothesley to unleash his full combo strings without interruption. Blizzard Strayer artifact set gains maximum value here.
2. Melt Team (Maximum Damage Output)
❄️🔥
This composition aims for the highest possible damage numbers by triggering the powerful Melt reaction.
Core Concept:
Wriothesley's Cryo attacks trigger Melt on enemies affected by Pyro.
Example Team:
Wriothesley +
Xiangling
(Off-field Pyro) +
Bennett
(Healing, ATK Buff, Pyro Battery) +
Zhongli/Kazuha
(Shield/Resistance Shred).
Why it works:
Bennett provides essential healing for Wriothesley's HP mechanics while buffing his ATK tremendously. Xiangling's persistent Pyro application enables consistent Melt reactions for explosive damage.
3. Superconduct/Physical Hybrid Team
⚡
A more niche but fun option that leverages his fast attacks.
Core Concept:
Trigger Superconduct to reduce enemy Physical RES, then deal mixed Cryo and Physical damage.
Example Team:
Wriothesley +
Raiden Shogun/Fischl
(Electro application) +
Mika/Yun Jin
(ATK SPD/NA DMG Buff) +
Flex Healer/Shielder

CRIT Rate boost

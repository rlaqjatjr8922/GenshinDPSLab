Xinyan Guide and Best Builds
Xinyan is a 4-star Pyro Claymore character in Genshin
Impact who while being a DPS, also provides shielding to support your team.
Our Xinyan build guide will focus on her Best Artifacts, Team Comps,
Ascension Materials and More.

Xinyan's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Able to provide shielding while also acting as a DPS.
Xinyan's C2 guarantees the ability to CRIT on their Elemental Burst while also making easy shields.
Weaknesses
Split scaling forcing you to choose building her with either ATK or DEF.
Weaker shields than most other Characters.
High Energy Recharge requirements in many situations.
Elemental Skill requires you to be fighting 2-3 enemies to fully create her shield.
Low damage.

ratio instead of the standard 1:2 Rate:DMG most characters are built on.

Xinyan is much easier to build as a Shield support with her BiS weapon
being
Favonius Greatsword
which allows you to provide additional energy to other
party members. That being said, if you find that you need greater uptime on 
shields,
Sacrificial Greatsword
is the weapon to build.
Building Xinyan as a shielder means you want either
Tenacity of the Millelith
if you are able to maximize shield uptime. Otherwise using
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
or
Noblesse Oblige
to buff the
party. Which set you want to build depends on the team.
Xinyan's Shield scales off of DEF, which is your best stat outside of
ensuring you reach your Energy Recharge requirements.

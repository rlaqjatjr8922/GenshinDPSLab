Razor Build
Razor Guide and Best Builds
Razor is a 4-star Electro character armed with a claymore in Genshin Impact.
He focuses on brawling with physical damage attacks, with added Electro damage
from his abilities. While paired with off-field supports to add elemental
reactions to his capabilities, his damage is quite impressive. Razor is one of
the best users of Superconduct, adding to his damage output with Electro focused
reactions.

Wolf's Gravestone
and
Song of Broken Pines
are great
choices for a 5-star weapon for Razor, and they are extremely close in what they
offer us. The
Wolf's Gravestone
can grant a massive boost in ATK
power whenever you strike a enemy with low health (below 30%), but the downside
is that this ability has a cooldown. The attack buff only lasts for 12 seconds with
no way to increase it. It can only occur once every 30s, this means we have
a dead period in between triggers where you will have no buff.
The
Song of Broken Pines
offers a weaker flat attack bonus but is
permanently applied while using the weapon. The skill triggers through normal
and charged attacks, by applying a sigil for every strike. Once you gain 4 sigils
you will activate a party wide buff for an ATK SPD and an ATK %
buff for 12 seconds. This can occur once every 20 seconds. Overall, both weapons
suffer from the drawback of a dead period between buffs, but the strength of the
each makes up for this small drawback.
The top F2P weapon, the 4-star
Serpent Spine
claymore is a great
addition to have, with its bonus CRIT Rate on the base weapon and a stacking buff
to overall damage every four seconds. However, as you gain stacks of damage increases,
your character will also gain stacks of damage received along side it. Basically,
making you do more damage, but also making you more vulnerable as you stack the buff.
This effect can be nullified by having a shielder in your party or a solid back up
healer to heal any extra damage Razor may take.
Lastly, most of Razors equipment offers boost to
his overall Physical and Attack DMG. These artifacts can be split into groups of 2 or
full sets, but you are mainly looking to use which sub stats are the best. However,
if you choose to use the
Thundersoother
set, you will want to put
more emphasis on Energy Recharge (target 80-100%) so you can keep his Electro DMG
up at all times.
2.
Combos and How to Play Razor
Razor is the definition of a Main DPS. He stakes his claim as an on-field fighter
with off-field supports jumping in and out of the battle to augment his abilities.
First, lets talk about his
Razor
which will be used as his primary attack
since we save his
Electro
powers for the right opportunities. Razor's normal attack
combination is fairly straight forward, 3 hits for when you do not have your
Elemental Burst
Lightning Fang
and use a 4 hit combo for when you do
have the ability triggered. Otherwise, Razor is a straight forward play style that
focuses on positional gameplay to maximize the use of his claymore for big AoE attacks.
His abilities are quite powerful when you at the right time. Razor's Elemental
Skill,
Claw and Thunder
, can be performed by tapping the key to initiate
a simple dash forward with an
Electro
attack or holding the key down to perform a
AoE slam attack of Electro DMG. Each version gains Electro Sigils that be used
with his Burst ability,
Lightning Fang
, with 3 Sigils from tapping the
skill and 5 Sigils from holding the skill. The Sigils get absorbed by
Lightning Fang
for more SPD and DMG during his burst ability.
3.
Razor's Best Team Comps
Razor is an on-field DPS with excellent attack power and mediocre survivability.
So we will be targeting off-fielders and support characters that can offer elemental
reactions for his
Electro
abilities to trigger, Damage buffers to keep Razor's DPS
high, and finally healers or shielders to keep him on the field despite taking any
damage.

and stronger. Typically, you want to aim for his max constellation

the max constellation. His constellations helps to increase his DPS significantly,
focusing on damage buffs and CRIT Rate stat increases.

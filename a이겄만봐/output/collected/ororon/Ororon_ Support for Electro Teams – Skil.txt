Ororon: The Enigmatic Support for Electro Teams
The Mysterious Electro Support
In the ever-evolving landscape of elemental combat, Ororon emerges as a fascinating support character who specializes in empowering Electro-focused teams. With his unique combination of an Electro Vision and secret arts derived from the Lord of Bright Flame, Ororon brings a distinctive approach to battlefield support that merits our attention in this article.
Elemental Skill: Celebratory Orb
Ororon’s Elemental Skill manifests a colorful celebratory orb through secret arts passed down from the Lord of Bright Flame. This orb deals Electro damage to enemies upon impact and demonstrates remarkable mobility on the battlefield. When other enemies are nearby, the orb travels between them, dealing Electro damage to each target. It’s important to note that each enemy can only be targeted once per skill activation, creating a strategic element to its deployment. The orb will disappear after a certain number of movements or when no more targetable enemies remain in proximity.
Talent: Catalyst of Spiritual Imagination
After unlocking the “Catalyst of Spiritual Imagination” talent, Ororon’s support capabilities significantly expand. For a short duration after the celebratory orb hits an enemy, active characters in the vicinity can recover elemental energy when their normal attacks, charged shots, or plunging attacks connect with enemies. This energy recovery effect extends to Ororon himself when he’s off-field, making him an excellent battery for energy-hungry teams. This effect can trigger a specific number of times within a set time frame, providing consistent energy regeneration for the team.
Elemental Burst: Ultrasonic Eye
When Ororon unleashes his Elemental Burst, he employs ancient secret techniques to deal AoE Electro damage and summons an Ultrasonic Eye. This mysterious construct continuously taunts surrounding enemies while rotating and dealing sonic Electro damage to nearby foes. The taunt effect provides valuable crowd control, drawing enemy attention away from more vulnerable team members.
Talent: Synesthesia of Night Shadows
With the “Synesthesia of Night Shadows” talent unlocked, Ororon gains access to a complex system of Night Soul Ground acquisition. This resource can be accumulated through various means:
When nearby team members trigger Night Soul Bursts
When other team members’ Hydro or Electro attacks hit enemies within a certain period after Ororon uses his Elemental Skill
When nearby enemies take Electro-Charged damage
Upon acquiring Night Soul Ground, Ororon can enter a state of Night Soul Protection, consuming a portion of this resource to deal Electro damage to a certain number of surrounding enemies. This state persists for a set duration and can be activated even when Ororon is off-field, further enhancing his value as a support character.
Combat Techniques and Aerial Capabilities
Ororon possesses exceptional soul perception abilities, allowing him to detect subtle sounds and find his path forward. In combat, his priority is to accumulate Night Soul Ground through various team interactions. When airborne, Ororon can enter an aiming mode by holding the normal attack button, consuming concentration and stamina. In this state, he can draw information and power from command scribbles and directives.
Optimal Combat Strategy
For optimal performance in battle, we recommend first building up Night Soul Ground through team rotations. Deploy Ororon’s Elemental Skill to summon the celebratory orb, allowing it to bounce between enemies and set up Electro-related reactions for teammates. When his Elemental Burst is charged, summon the Ultrasonic Eye to taunt enemies and deal consistent Electro damage. This strategy maximizes Ororon’s support potential while enabling the team to focus on dealing damage.
Character Personality
Beyond his combat prowess, Ororon stands out for his unique personality. He embraces his eccentric nature, understanding that being different doesn’t equate to being malicious. While some may hide their true selves behind masks, Ororon lives authentically, and his friends and adults in his life seem to accept his unusual behaviors and speech patterns.
In a world filled with inexplicable phenomena, Ororon represents the harmless and intriguing side of mystery—a refreshing perspective that adds depth to his character beyond his battlefield utility.

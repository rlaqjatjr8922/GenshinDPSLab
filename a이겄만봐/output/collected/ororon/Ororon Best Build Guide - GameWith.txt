Ororon is a 4-star Electro character who wields a Bow in Genshin Impact. His gameplay focuses on off-field Electro support and synergy with Hydro-based reactions like Electro-Charged. His Elemental Skill, "Night's Sling," launches a bouncing Electro projectile that builds up Nightsoul points when allies trigger certain reactions or use Electro and Hydro attacks. Once enough points are gathered, he automatically enters Nightsoul's Blessing, firing "Hypersense" Electro damage to up to four enemies at intervals, even while off-field. His Elemental Burst summons the "Supersonic Oculus," a construct that taunts enemies and continuously deals area Electro damage. Lore-wise, Ororon is a calm and mysterious member of the Masters of the Night-Wind from Natlan.

Roles & Playstyle Of Ororon and Its Strengths and Weakness
Off-Field Sub-DPS Ororon
Ororon's main role is to be an Electro sub-DPS for Electro-Charged teams. He can also be used in Hyperbloom but will require an Electro On-Fielder or Sub-DPS.
Pros and Cons of Sub-DPS Ororon
Pros
- Can charge the Energy of teammates with his Passive.
- Natlan character's Nightsoul-aligned damage can also trigger Ororon's Hypersense attack from his Passive.
- Elemental Burst can taunt enemies and hit them at the same time.
Cons
- Often requires a 2nd Electro character to trigger Electro-Charged for Ororon's Hypersense attack.
- Elemental Burst is stationary and you can easily position it wrong if Ororon targets the wrong enemy.

Team Rotation of Ororon and Combos
Team Rotation of Off-Field Ororon
Basic Rotation Of Ororon
1. Use Elemental Skill.
2. Use Elemental Burst before switching characters.
Ororon's Off-Field Sub-DPS gameplay is simple since he only needs to use his Active Skill to activate buffs. Using his Elemental Skill will enable him to regenerate Energy for the active character and him while using his Elemental Burst taunts enemies to approach.
Basics of Using Ororon (Beginner Guide)
As a character from Natlan, Ororon has access to the Nightsoul mechanic. For Ororon, his Nightsoul mechanic allows him to deal an Off-Field Electro Attack when Electro-Charged/Lunar-Charged/Nightsoul-aligned damage is dealt by his teammates.
What Is Nightsoul State / Nightsoul Points for Ororon?
Ororon's Nightsoul State is unique from other Natlan characters. As a strictly Sub-DPS only character, Ororon gains Nightsoul points more than losing it. In a Taser team, his Nightsoul bar is often full.
▼ In-Game Description of Nightsoul State of Ororon
Hypersense Attack when Electro-Charged or Nightsoul-Aligned Damage is Dealt
Ororon consumes 10 Nightsoul points to deal a Nightsoul-aligned Electro Attack when Electro-Charged, Lunar-Charged, or Nightsoul-aligned damage is dealt on enemies. It can hit up to 4 nearby enemies.
Nightsoul Burst for Ororon
▲ Ororon's Nightsoul Burst Effect is only the first part of his Nightshade Synesthesia Passive.
Nightsoul Burst is an effect that activates when Elemental Damage is dealt when there's a Natlan character in the team. This effect has different buffs for the Natlan character. In Ororon's case, it gives him 40 Nightsoul points and allows him to gain 5 more when Hydro or Electro attacks hit enemies.

How To Use Ororon?
1
Gain Nightsoul Points
- Nightsoul Burst occurs. (+40)
- After using E. Skill, use Hydro / Electro from teammates (+5)
2
Enter Nightsoul State
- Deal Electro-charged damage
- Let a Natlan Character do a Nightsoul Attack hit an opponent.
Because of Ororon's passive, he transforms into his Nightsoul State and deal constant damage off-field.  He is very simple to use, you just need to continuesly deal Electro-charged damage to enemies. There is no need to put Ororon onfield.
Use E. Burst to Taunt Enemies and Deal Powerful DMG
The Elemental Burst of Ororon is a summon-type Skill. It summons an Oculus that continuously taunts enemies and also deal DMG to them when they are in range. This helps group up enemies so that the Main DPS will be getting more DMG windows.
Recovers The Energy Of Teammates And Himself Off-Field
After the Elemental Skill of Ororon hits the enemies,
when teammates deal Normal / Charge / Plunging Attack
that character and Ororon will gain energy. While it only gain 9 points, it is really good for Ororon who is often off-field and not receiving energy particles.
Hold Jump Button for Higher Jump Height
Ororon has the ability similar to Iktomisaurus which can jump at great heights. When you have Ororon, you do not need to transform to Iktomisaurus to explore.
Should You Pull for Ororon's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
All of Ororon's Constellations are Good to Have
Constellation Priority
C6 > C4 > C2
Any Constellation Level is a Good Stopping Point
Ororon's peak performance as a supportive character that applies buffs should be at C6, but he will still work as intended and produce decent results even at C0, with his constellations generally increasing his own damage output. Because Ororon is a 4-star, it'll be harder to obtain his constellations even when he's on the rate up-banner. Unless you want to get the rate up 5-star character, it's fine to stop at whatever constellation you end up with for Ororon.

Nightshade Synesthesia
Effect
After a nearby party member triggers Nightsoul Burst, Ororon will gain 40 Nightsoul points. Within 15s after using his Elemental Skill, when other party members' Hydro or Electro attacks hit opponents, Ororon will gain 5 Nightsoul points, an effect that can occur every 0.3s for a maximum of 10 times during this 15s duration. Ororon can have a maximum of 80 Nightsoul points.
Also, when nearby opponents take Electro-Charged or Lunar-Charged reaction DMG or Nightsoul-aligned DMG dealt by other nearby characters, Ororon will consume 10 Nightsoul points (provided he has at least that amount), entering the Nightsoul's Blessing state and triggering the Hypersense effect: Deal Nightsoul-aligned Electro DMG based on 160% of Ororon's ATK to at most 4 nearby opponents. The aforementioned effect can trigger once every 1.8s.
Nightsoul's Blessing: Ororon:
Ororon's Nightsoul's Blessing state lasts 6s.
Aspect Catalyst
Effect
After the Elemental Skill Night's Sling's Spirit Orb hits an opponent, Ororon will gain the Aspect Sigil effect for 15s.
Aspect Sigil:
When a nearby active party member hits an opponent with Normal, Charged, or Plunging Attacks, that character will restore 3 Energy. If Ororon is off-field, Ororon will also restore 3 Energy. This effect can trigger once every 1s, and can trigger 3 times per duration.
Flowing Fog, Spritely Shadows
Effect
While in an area with Phlogiston Mechanics within Natlan, he can use Nightsoul Transmission: Ororon. When the active character is currently in the air, the following will trigger when switching to Ororon: Ororon will leap up high. Nightsoul Transmission can be triggered once every 10s by your party.
Holding Aiming Mode will cause Ororon to enter the Spiritspeaker state, which will allow him to interact with Nightspirit Graffiti and Nightspirit Sigils, extracting information and power from them. The rules for interacting with these objects follow the same rules as those governing Iktomisaurus interactions.
Additionally, holding the jump button will cause Ororon to consume 75 Stamina and leap. In areas within Natlan where Phlogiston mechanics are present, Ororon will prioritize consuming 5 Phlogiston to leap to a height higher still. When in the air, Ororon can Hold his Normal Attack to consume Phlogiston or Stamina and enter Aiming Mode.
The Art Of Skinchanging
Effect
Increases gliding SPD for your own party members by 15%. Not stackable with Passive Talents that provide the exact same effects.
Who Is Ororon?
An Oddball From The Masters Of The Night-Wind
Ororon is an eccentric young man who prefers to live alone than be with his fellow Masters of the Night-Wind tribe members. He was raised by Citlali, who is herself a bit of an oddball too.
One Of The Current Heroes Of Natlan
Ororon is the current bearer of the Ancient Name Bidii and is one of the six Heroes of Natlan. He plays a significant part in Natlan's quest to win against the Abyss.
Has A "Damaged Soul"
Ororon was born with a "Damaged Soul" and was abandoned by his parents because of it. He was then taken in by Citlali. He fondly calls her Granny even now when he's all grown up.

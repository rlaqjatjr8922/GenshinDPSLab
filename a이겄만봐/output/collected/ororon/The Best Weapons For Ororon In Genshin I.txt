Picking the right weapon for Ororon makes every encounter smoother and more fun. The right bow can take him from pretty decent to an outstanding character, letting you focus on nailing those reaction combos and crushing tough fights. This guide breaks down the best 5-star, 4-star, and F2P options to help you get the most out of Ororon in every adventure.
1
Elegy For The End – Team Buffing Powerhouse
Elegy for the End is easily one of the
best five-star weapons
for Ororon if you're building him as a team-oriented sub-DPS. Its high Energy Recharge secondary stat ensures Ororon can spam his Elemental Burst, keeping your team’s energy cycle intact.
It's also got a killer passive ability:
Elegy for the End boosts the Elemental Mastery and ATK of your entire party, which is a huge win for Electro-Charged reaction teams.
Ororon’s multi-hit abilities make it easy for him to stack the Sigils required to activate this weapon’s buffs. The result? Your entire team’s damage output sees a noticeable jump, especially in reaction-heavy setups.
Elegy for the End is an event-exclusive weapon, so you’ll only find it in the
Epitome Invocation Weapon Banner
when it’s featured. Since this weapon is not part of the permanent pool, planning is essential. Save your Primogems and Intertwined Fates if you see it announced in future banners. Keeping an eye on HoYoverse updates and Genshin community news can help you stay prepared for its next rerun.
2
Aqua Simulacra – CRIT Lover’s Delight
Aqua Simulacra is tailor-made for boosting Ororon’s personal damage. Its high CRIT DMG secondary stat ensures he delivers harder-hitting attacks, perfect for sub-DPS playstyles. The passive ability adds even more damage to your arsenal, activating whenever enemies are close — ideal for both on-field and off-field combat.
While it also offers an HP bonus, this stat doesn’t bring much to the table for Ororon, but the sheer power of its offensive capabilities more than makes up for it.
Aqua Simulacra is best suited for players who want Ororon to shine as a damage dealer rather than a pure support.
This sleek 5-star bow is another gem from the Epitome Invocation Weapon Banner, available during select events. To add it to your collection, stockpile Primogems or Intertwined Fates and wait for Aqua Simulacra’s featured banner.
3
Polar Star – Balanced Sub-DPS Option
Polar Star is ideal for players who want to balance raw power and consistent performance. Its CRIT Rate secondary stat boosts Ororon’s reliability in dealing damage. The passive skill increases his Elemental Skill, Burst, and ATK, creating a versatile weapon for sub-DPS builds. Ororon can efficiently gain stacks to amplify his output further, ensuring he contributes consistently to team damage.

4
The First Great Magic – The Burst Specialist
Aqua Simulacra is your weapon of choice if you’re chasing high numbers. Its incredible CRIT DMG secondary stat allows Ororon to unleash devastating damage, especially during Elemental Reactions. The passive skill offers a flat DMG boost, further enhancing his sub-DPS potential.
While the HP percent bonus isn’t particularly useful for Ororon, the weapon more than makes up for it with raw damage potential. This bow is best for players aiming to showcase Ororon’s personal DPS in team compositions where his Elemental Burst is the star of the show. If you value explosive damage over utility, Aqua Simulacra won’t disappoint.
5
Skyward Harp – Easy-To-Use CRIT Weapon
Skyward Harp strikes the perfect balance between offense and utility, making it one of the most beginner-friendly 5-star bows. With CRIT Rate as its secondary stat and an added CRIT DMG boost from its passive, this weapon is perfect for enhancing Ororon’s damage output.
Its unique passive also allows it to deal AoE physical damage to nearby enemies, providing an extra layer of damage versatility. While it may lack specialized abilities compared to other bows, its consistent performance and ease of use make it a solid choice for both new and experienced players.
Skyward Harp is part of the standard wish pool, so it can be obtained through the Wanderlust Invocation Standard Wish Banner or during featured Epitome Invocation Weapon Banners. If you’re regularly wishing on these banners, you might get lucky and pull this powerful bow without waiting for a specific event!
6
Alley Hunter – Affordable And Effective
For a 4-star option, Alley Hunter packs a surprising punch. It increases ATK through its secondary stat and buffs damage when Ororon is off-field, making it a budget-friendly choice for sub-DPS builds. At higher refinement levels, Alley Hunter can rival some 5-star weapons, making it a great investment for players without access to premium bows.

7
The Stringless – Electro-Charged Enhancer
The Stringless is a
fantastic 4-star weapon

8
Favonius Warbow – Energy Recharge Specialist
Favonius Warbow excels at ensuring Ororon’s energy needs are always met, thanks to its high Energy Recharge stat. This makes it perfect for players who want Ororon to double as a team battery.
Its passive generates energy particles upon CRIT hits, giving your team a steady supply of energy for Elemental Bursts. However, Ororon needs a decent CRIT Rate to consistently trigger this effect.
Pairing this bow with
CRIT-enhancing artifacts
or substats can help you fully utilize its potential. Favonius Warbow is a dependable choice for supportive Ororon builds that prioritize team efficiency over raw damage.
9
Chain Breaker – F2P-Friendly All-Rounder
Chain Breaker is a
versatile free-to-play weapon
, offering a solid mix of ATK and Elemental Mastery boosts. Its passive ability further enhances Ororon’s performance by tailoring buffs based on his party composition.
This makes it a flexible weapon for both reaction-focused and general team setups. Since it’s a craftable bow, you can refine it multiple times without relying on the gacha system, making it an excellent long-term investment for players who want to maximize Ororon’s effectiveness without spending resources on banners.
You can forge Chain Breaker at the blacksmith using a Borderland Bow Billet, along with other crafting materials. These billets are obtainable through weekly bosses, events, and special quests.
Once crafted, Chain Breaker can be refined using additional billets, making it one of the easiest weapons to upgrade for consistent performance in your team.

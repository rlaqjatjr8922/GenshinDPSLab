Ningguang Works Great As Geo Damage Dealer
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Geo DPS
- C1 turns Ningguang's Normal Attacks into AoE upon damaging an enemy.
- Elemental resonance like Geo or Pyro resonance can buff Ningguang's damage along with buffs from the supports.
- Sub-DPS characters like Furina or Yelan can be used for additional damage while buffing Ningguang's damage.
2nd
▼ Burst DPS Ningguang
- Ningguang's low Burst cost allows her to be used as a Burst DPS that can be switched out when needed.
- She can activate Geo resonance that can buff the damage of the DPS when shielded.
Ningguang is a Geo Catalyst character that can deal substantial damage. With the right supports, she can be a great DPS. Her Burst is her main source of damage, allowing her to be a quickswap Burst DPS.

DPS Ningguang can make use of Scroll set buff, Bennett's ATK buff and Furina's damage increase buff. If using sub-DPS characters, Xilonen's buff can help them deal more damage.
Rotation Of Geo DPS
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies with her Normal Attack twice
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Ningguang and use her Skill then Burst
6
Deal damage with Normal Attacks
7
Once Ningguang gains 3 Star Jades, use her Charged Attack
8
Repeat Ningguang's Normal Attack → Charged Attack sequence until buffs expire
9
Repeat from Step 1
If Ningguang is C2, you can use her Skill again before starting her Normal Attack combo. You can use it to gain Geo DMG Bonus buff.

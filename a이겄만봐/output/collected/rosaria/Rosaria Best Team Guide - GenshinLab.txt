Rosaria Team Overview
This Page provides the best Rosaria Team information. Rosaria is a DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Rosaria and Ayaka to make your own best team.
From SS tier to S tier, Rosaria best team can be formed in different ways including Superconduct, Permafrost and Melt team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

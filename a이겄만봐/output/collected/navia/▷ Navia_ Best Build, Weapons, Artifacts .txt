Navia is a 5-star Geo character who, despite wielding her powerful and stylish Gunbrella, requires a Claymore as her weapon of choice. She serves as a Main DPS, standing out significantly from other Geo Main DPS characters.
This character excels in dealing incredible damage through her Elemental Skill, which unleashes a powerful shot (you must be very close to enemies to maximize damage, functioning like a shotgun) with her Gunbrella. She also delivers substantial damage with the rest of her kit, including Normal Attacks, as using the Elemental Skill temporarily imbues her with Geo.
What sets her apart is her ability to enhance her Elemental Skill damage as she or her companions
collect Crystallize fragments
(a Geo elemental reaction). She can even automatically collect these fragments by holding down her skill. But that's not all—pairing her with Cryo, Pyro, Hydro, and Electro characters significantly boosts her attack, though she greatly appreciates teaming up with another Geo character for resonance and to generate more Crystallize fragments.

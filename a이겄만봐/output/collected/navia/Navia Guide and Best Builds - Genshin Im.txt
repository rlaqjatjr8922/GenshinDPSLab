Navia Guide & Best Builds
Welcome to our Navia build guide for Genshin Impact.
Navia
is a 5-Star
Geo
Main DPS 
Character. Navia's setup is fairly simple: you want CRIT values and ATK % to scale 
her damage, unlike many other Geo characters who scale on DEF. Her playstyle revolves 
around triggering as much
Crystallize
as possible to buff 
her Elemental Skill.

Navia strictly scales off ATK, unlike older Geo DPS characters who preferred 
DEF. Your primary goal is to reach your Energy Recharge threshold so she can Burst 
every rotation, and then stack as much CRIT and ATK % as possible to maximize her 
"Shotgun" Skill damage.
Recommended Stats for Navia
Navia's Energy Recharge Requirements
In terms of Energy Recharge, you will want about 140% if you 
run her alongside a
Geo
Support (or 110% if you specifically use
Geo Traveler
). You can reduce these numbers by a further 10% if 
you have her Constellation 1,
A Lady's Rules for Keeping a Courteous Distance
.
If you do not use a Geo Support, you will need significantly more, sitting closer 
to around 180%-190% ER. Remember that using Favonius Weapons on any character in your 
team will reduce these ER requirements by about 10% per weapon used.
Navia's Talent Priority
Talent Priority:
Ceremonial Crystalshot
>
As the Sunlit Sky's Singing Salute
>
Blunt Refusal
A massive portion of your damage comes from landing her Elemental Skill, making 
it your absolute highest priority. Her Elemental Burst should be leveled second for 
its consistent off-field damage and Shrapnel generation. Finally, her Normal Attacks 
are leveled last to boost her infused Geo combos.
How to Play Navia
The core of Navia's damage revolves around her Elemental Skill,
Ceremonial Crystalshot
. You gain "Crystal Shrapnel" whenever you 
pick up a Crystallize Shard or on certain intervals during your Burst. You can hold 
up to 6 Shrapnel stacks at a time.
Her Skill functions much like a shotgun, where projectiles spread further the longer 
they travel. The first 3 Shrapnel stacks increase the amount of projectiles fired by 
2 per stack (from 5 up to 11). The more projectiles hit a target, the more damage 
you deal. Hitting all 11 projectiles deals double the base damage. The last 3 Shrapnel 
stacks increase the damage by 15% per stack, counting as a Normal DMG bonus.
After casting her Elemental Skill, Navia's Normal Attacks become
infused
with
Geo
for 4 seconds. 
Due to her attack animations, the optimal combo is to perform 3 Normal Attacks, dash 
to cancel the animation, and then perform 2 more Normal Attacks.
Use her Burst,
As the Sunlit Sky's Singing Salute
, whenever it is available. 
It deals solid damage over its 12-second duration, but more importantly, it automatically 
generates a Shrapnel stack for you every 2.4 seconds when it hits enemies.
Navia's Best Constellations
Generally speaking, Navia is a very complete package at C0. Her constellations 
are not overwhelmingly strong, but they do provide comfortable quality-of-life and 
damage boosts.
Investment Priority:
Constellation 1 > Constellation 2 > Signature Weapon
A Lady's Rules for Keeping a Courteous Distance
(C1) drastically reduces 
your Energy Recharge requirements, bringing them down to about 100%-120% depending 
on your team setup.
The President's Pursuit of Victory
(C2) is her strongest early 
constellation, providing up to 36% free CRIT Rate on her Elemental Skill cast and 
automatically triggering an extra Burst strike upon firing.

Xianyun Plunge Team Fundamentals & Meta Overview
Character Role Analysis
Here's what makes Xianyun special – she's basically the enabler that plunge-focused teams have been waiting for since launch. Released as a 5-star Anemo Catalyst user in Version 4.4, she transforms previously niche characters into legitimate powerhouses.
Her 4th Ascension Passive is where the magic happens. It provides an Additive Base DMG Bonus equal to 200% of her total ATK to Plunging Attack shockwave damage. The sweet spot? You'll want to hit 4,500 total ATK to reach that 9,000 damage cap at C0. At C2, this scaling jumps to 400% with an 18,000 damage cap – effectively doubling her buffing capability.
The Elemental Burst costs 70 energy (yeah, it's steep) but provides 8 stacks of Adeptal Assistance for enhanced Plunging Attacks. That Starwicker mechanism follows your active character around, providing continuous healing based on her ATK while each plunge triggers coordinated AoE Anemo damage.

Core Mechanics
Her Elemental Skill allows up to 3 forward leaps, with Plunging Attack converting to
Driftcloud Wave
dealing AoE Anemo damage. Here's a crucial optimization tip – hitting at least one enemy generates exactly 5 Anemo Particles regardless of leap count, making single-leap rotations optimal for time efficiency.
The A4 Passive applies damage bonus to ground impact damage specifically, affecting only one opponent per plunge with a 0.4-second cooldown per character. This means positioning matters more than you might think.
Talent priority focuses on Elemental Burst > Elemental Skill, with Normal Attack talents remaining unleveled. Leveling to 90/90 is highly recommended since both buff and healing scale directly from total ATK.

Energy Recharge Requirements & Calculations
ER Benchmarks by Team
This is where things get technical. Solo Anemo configurations require 220-300% ER with typical weapons, dropping to 190-260% with Crane's Echoing Call. Multi-Anemo teams significantly reduce requirements: 200-210% ER with Xiao, 185-195% with Xiao and Faruzan, 120-130% with C6 Faruzan.
Each Favonius weapon proc reduces needs by 10-25% – something to keep in mind when building your supports.
Gaming's C4 constellation restores 2 Energy per special Plunge hit, indirectly supporting energy needs through extended rotations. Rotation length affects ER requirements significantly, with longer rotations allowing more skill uses and particle generation.
Optimization Strategies
Artifact substats should prioritize ER until requirements are met, then focus on ATK% to reach the 4,500 ATK threshold. ER% Sands become mandatory in most solo Anemo configurations, while ATK% Sands become viable only when ER needs are satisfied through weapons and substats.
Weapon selection dramatically impacts ER requirements. Favonius Codex solves energy issues at the cost of ATK stats, while Crane's Echoing Call provides both energy restoration and high ATK values. C1 provides additional Elemental Skill charge for increased particle generation.

Teammate Analysis & Build Recommendations
Gaming Build Guide
Gaming requires 180-190% ER at C0-C3 in solo Pyro configurations, dropping to 150-160% with Bennett support. His C4 significantly reduces ER needs to 145-155% solo or 125-135% with Bennett.
4pc Marechaussee Hunter provides CRIT Rate and DMG bonuses from HP fluctuation, synergizing perfectly with his HP-consuming Elemental Skill and Furina's HP drain. Main stat distribution prioritizes ATK% Sands (or ER% if requirements aren't met), Pyro DMG% Goblet, and CRIT Rate/DMG Circlet.
His C6 adds 20% CRIT Rate and 40% CRIT DMG to special Plunges, making him competitive with 5-star DPS units. Honestly, C6 Gaming feels like a different character entirely.
Support Character Builds
Bennett prioritizes high Base ATK weapons like Aquila Favonia or Skyward Blade to maximize ATK buff contribution. 4pc Noblesse Oblige provides team-wide ATK% buffs.
Furina focuses on HP stacking with 4pc Golden Troupe or mixed 2pc sets with HP% and Hydro DMG%. Faruzan requires C6 for major power spike, prioritizing ER for consistent Burst uptime with 4pc Viridescent Venerer or 4pc Noblesse Oblige.
Zhongli can replace healers providing shield support and universal resistance shredding, while Kazuha offers alternative Anemo support with superior crowd control.
Artifact Sets & Stat Priorities
Artifact Comparisons
4pc Viridescent Venerer serves as Best-in-Slot for teams with Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, or Electro DPS characters, providing 40% Elemental RES shred. 4pc Noblesse Oblige becomes superior in Xiao teams, directly buffing Xiao's ATK rather than providing resistance shredding.

Stat Distribution
Main stat priority follows ER% or ATK% Sands depending on energy requirements, ATK% Goblet for maximum buffing potential, and ATK% Circlet. Substat priority emphasizes ER% until requirements are met, then ATK% and Flat ATK to reach the 4,500 total ATK threshold.
CRIT Rate becomes relevant only when using Favonius Codex. The 4,500 ATK threshold represents the optimization target for maximum A4 Passive effectiveness, achievable through high Base ATK weapons, ATK% artifacts, and team buffs from Bennett or Pyro Resonance.

Spiral Abyss Performance & Strategies
Floor-Specific Adaptations
Floor 12's current rotation favors Xianyun teams due to enemy compositions requiring high burst damage and AoE capabilities. Gaming Vaporize teams excel against grouped enemies, while Xiao teams provide consistent damage against mobile formations.
Energy management becomes critical in multi-wave floors where restoration between chambers affects rotation timing. I've found that slightly over-investing in ER pays dividends in these scenarios.
Enemy Matchups
High-mobility enemies challenge plunge-focused teams due to positioning requirements – looking at you, Rifthounds. Single-target bosses favor teams with high individual damage output like Diluc Vaporize or optimized Xiao teams.
Elemental-resistant enemies limit reaction-based effectiveness, favoring Anemo hypercarry teams. Optimized Xianyun teams achieve competitive clear times, with Gaming Vaporize reaching 30-45 second chamber clears and Xiao hypercarry providing consistent 45-60 second clears.
Investment levels significantly impact performance, with C2 Xianyun teams achieving 15-20% faster clears.

Team composition errors include pairing Xianyun with characters who don't benefit from plunge enablement. I've seen too many players try to force her into teams where she provides minimal value.

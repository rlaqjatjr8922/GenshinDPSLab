Arataki Itto Team Overview
This page provides the best Arataki Itto Team information. Arataki Itto is a DPS in most situations.
Itto has the highest DPS in Geo Characters. Itto mainly uses his special Charged Attack to deal DMG. Itto’s DEF affect his ATK and ATK SPD. This makes Itto usually pairs up with character which provide DEF Bonus.
Itto can be assigned to different teams. This guide shows some of the best teams of Itto.

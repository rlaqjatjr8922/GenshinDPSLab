Chiori's Kit and Synergies
HoYoVerse
Chiori's Elemental Skill summons "Tamato" puppets that periodically deal Geo AoE damage to nearby enemies.
The number of puppets spawned increases when party members create Geo constructs
, making her an ideal partner for other Geo characters.
Her Elemental Burst grants her a DEF-scaling damage buff, further enhancing her overall damage output.

Best Characters to Pair with Chiori
HoYoVerse /GameLeap
To
fully utilize Chiori's kit
, it's essential to include characters that can create Geo constructs or provide valuable support. Here are some of the best characters to pair with Chiori:
Navia
: As a potent 5-star Geo DPS, Navia's skills create Geo constructs, allowing Chiori to summon additional puppets. Their synergy results in a formidable Geo damage combination.
Arataki Itto
: Itto's Elemental Burst summons the powerful Geo construct "Ushi," enabling Chiori to maintain multiple puppets for extended periods.
Zhongli
: The Geo Archon's Elemental Skill creates a Geo pillar, acting as a construct for Chiori's puppets. Additionally, Zhongli provides valuable shielding and resistance shred.
Gorou
: While not creating Geo constructs, Gorou's abilities significantly buff Geo characters' damage and provide valuable healing and energy generation.

Best Chiori Team Compositions
Here are the currently best team compositions for Chiori:
Mono-Geo Team
HoYoVerse /GameLeap
Chiori, Navia, Zhongli, Gorou.
This team composition takes full advantage of Geo resonance, providing increased shield strength, damage bonuses, and Geo resistance shred. Chiori's puppets, Navia's Geo infusion, Zhongli's shielding and resistance shred, and Gorou's buffs create a potent and well-rounded Geo team.
Navia Double Geo Team
HoYoVerse /GameLeap
Chiori, Navia, Xiangling, Bennett
This team pairs Chiori with Navia, benefiting from Geo resonance and Chiori's puppets supporting Navia's Geo damage. Xiangling provides consistent off-field Pyro damage, while Bennett offers invaluable healing and buffs.
Arataki Itto Mono-Geo Team
HoYoVerse /GameLeap
Chiori, Arataki Itto, Gorou, Zhongli
Itto's Geo infusion and Ushi construct synergize perfectly with Chiori's puppets, creating a formidable Geo damage team. Gorou buffs the team's Geo damage, while Zhongli provides shielding and resistance shred.

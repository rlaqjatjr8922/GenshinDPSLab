In Genshin Impact, building the right team around a character can make all the difference in maximizing their potential. For Chasca, a character with unique elemental mechanics, finding the perfect companions can be a bit tricky, but also highly rewarding. This guide covers 10 effective team comps for Chasca, showcasing different synergies and playstyles to get the best out of her abilities. Whether you have the key characters or not, there’s something for everyone in this lineup.
10 Best Chasca Team Comps Genshin Impact
1.
Kuki Shinobu & Barbara for Hydro Support
Kuki Shinobu and Barbara are solid choices for teams focused on Hydro application. Kuki’s role is to provide consistent off-field Hydro damage, while Barbara’s C2 boosts Chasca’s Hydro damage, especially against bosses that can’t be frozen. If you don’t have Kuki, Barbara is a great alternative. Additionally, Rosaria’s Cryo support adds crit rate and some extra Elemental damage, making this a versatile setup.
2.
Chongyun & Xiangling for Reverse Melt
Chongyun’s C2 reduces the cooldowns of skills and bursts while on the field, and with Xiangling’s burst-ready Pyro abilities, this team can dish out some strong reverse melt damage. Though this team requires good character builds to shine, it can still be an enjoyable setup. If you prefer another element, you can substitute in Ororon for a boost to both Cryo and Pyro damage, helping Chasca convert her bullets more effectively.
3.
Rosaria & Xingqiu for Cryo and Hydro Synergy
Rosaria is a free-to-play character that provides crucial Crit Rate, helping Chasca hit harder with her abilities. Pairing her with Xingqiu can further strengthen this team’s Hydro synergy. Xingqiu’s Hydro application can trigger reactions like Freeze or Vaporize, while Rosaria’s Cryo abilities help support Chasca’s elemental damage. This composition offers a nice balance between damage output and utility.
4.
Fischl & Xingqiu for Electro and Hydro Reactions
If you’re looking to experiment with an Electro-based composition, Fischl and Xingqiu can provide decent off-field damage. Fischl’s Electro application synergizes well with Chasca’s playstyle, while Xingqiu provides reliable Hydro status for reactions. However, this team isn’t as strong as others due to the loss of bullet conversions, but it can be effective for those who enjoy playing with reactions.
5.
Xiangling, Ororon, & Chasca for Elemental Damage Buffs
This setup works well for those who want to focus on maximizing Chasca’s elemental damage. Ororon’s ability to trigger Chasca’s passive with his burst helps create more bullets, while Xiangling brings Pyro and additional damage support. While Ororon and Xiangling work best with the four-piece “Scroll” set, you can also experiment with other artifact sets to optimize damage output.
6.
Chasca, Bennett, & Ororon for Consistent Damage Buffs
Bennett is a staple in many Genshin teams due to his healing and attack buffs, and his synergy with Chasca is no different. Bennett’s C6 offers a 15% boost to Chasca’s charge attack damage, while Ororon adds more damage through his elemental buffs. This team offers great flexibility and can easily handle various bosses and enemies, making it a popular choice among players who have access to these characters.
7.
Chasca, Ororon, & Xiangling for Pyro and Hydro Synergy
This is another Pyro-focused composition where Chasca benefits from elemental damage boosts. Ororon’s ability to increase elemental damage through his burst makes this team a powerhouse, and Xiangling’s Pyro application complements Chasca’s abilities well. With the four-piece “Scroll” set, this team offers a solid balance between elemental damage and sustained damage over time.
8.
Charlotte & Ororon for Freeze and Healing
Charlotte, while not as popular as some other characters, offers consistent Cryo application and healing to counter any bleed damage. Her presence in this team allows Chasca to remain mobile while still benefiting from healing and Cryo-related reactions. This setup works well in both offensive and defensive situations, giving you more room for experimentation.
9.
Chasca & Faruzan for Anemo DPS
For players who are curious about running Chasca as an Anemo DPS, Faruzan could be a viable option, though it’s important to note that Faruzan’s abilities don’t synergize as well with Chasca compared to other team compositions. The team works better when focusing on reaction-based setups, although Faruzan’s buffs are decent. If you’re looking for something experimental, this might be a fun team to try.
10.
Dendro & Hydro with Kokomi for Extra Bullet Conversions
This team attempts to combine Dendro and Hydro elements to maximize Chasca’s bullet conversions. Kokomi provides solid off-field Hydro damage, which helps with triggering reactions like Freeze. The team could benefit from future updates or more Dendro characters, but for now, it’s a fun and experimental setup that can deal decent damage, especially when using Dendro reactions.
Chasa’s unique abilities allow for plenty of experimentation with different team setups in Genshin Impact. While some of the Chasca Team Comps may be stronger than others, all of them provide valuable insights into how best to utilize her elemental playstyle.
If you’re looking for reliable damage output, teams with Bennett and Ororun are excellent choices. For those who want to experiment, trying out different combinations of Cryo, Hydro, and Dendro can unlock new potentials for Chasa’s abilities.
As more characters are introduced, we may see even more opportunities for synergy, making Chasa a highly versatile and fun character to build around.

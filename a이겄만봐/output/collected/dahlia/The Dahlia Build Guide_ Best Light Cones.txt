The Dahlia Character Overview
The Dahlia represents one of Honkai: Star Rail's Break support units. Her position as a Super Break team enabler comes from her ability to deploy a Zone that converts Toughness Reduction into Super Break damage and her capacity to designate a Dance Partner who gains additional benefits. As a 5-star
Nihility
character, she excels at providing team-wide Break Effect amplification and dealing follow-up attack damage.
The Dahlia's gameplay revolves around managing her
Zone
effect, which can be deployed through her Skill. This Zone lasts for 3 turns and increases all allies' Weakness Break Efficiency by 50% while allowing Toughness damage dealt to enemies who are not yet broken to be converted into Super Break damage. This mechanic is revolutionary as it allows Break teams to deal damage consistently without waiting for the enemy's Toughness bar to deplete.
Her Talent automatically assigns
Dance Partner
status to herself and one ally with the highest Break Effect. When the Dance Partner attacks a broken target, the Toughness reduction becomes Super Break damage. Additionally, when the Dance Partner attacks, The Dahlia launches follow-up attacks that convert their Toughness reduction into Super Break damage at 200% effectiveness.
Her Ultimate,
Wallow... Entombed Ash
, applies the Wilt debuff for 4 turns, reducing enemy Defense and applying the weakness types of all Dance Partners to all enemies. This ability defines her role as a Break team enabler, allowing teams to bypass enemy resistances and trigger Weakness Breaks more consistently.

The Dahlia Best Light Cones
1. 5-Star Options
The Dahlia's best Light Cone is

Additionally, when the wearer applies Weakness to an enemy target, it recovers 1 Skill Point, with the trigger count resetting when using Ultimate. This Light Cone perfectly complements her kit by maximizing her Break Effect while providing crucial Skill Point economy.

The Dahlia Team Compositions
Due to how The Dahlia's kit works, she excels in Break-focused teams, particularly those featuring high Break Effect DPS characters. Her Zone enables Super Break damage before enemies are broken, making her ideal for teams that rely on Toughness reduction.
1. Firefly Teams
Firefly is The Dahlia's ideal partner, as The Dahlia's passive is designed around Fire-type Break DPS characters. The combination of Firefly's high Fire Toughness damage and The Dahlia's Zone creates one of the strongest Super Break teams in the game. Fugue provides additional Break Effect support and Exo-Toughness for the premium setup, while Ruan Mei offers team-wide buffs and Break Efficiency. For F2P players, Harmony Trailblazer (HMC) provides Super Break enabling and works excellently with The Dahlia. Lingsha is the preferred sustain for her additional Break Effect and healing, while Gallagher offers budget-friendly sustain with Break capabilities.

The Dahlia Eidolons
Eidolon 1
The Dahlia's E1 extends her Super Break multiplier to all allies rather than just her Dance Partner, while also adding substantial fixed Toughness damage after Dance Partner attacks. This Eidolon is extremely high value because it increases team-wide Break damage and accelerates Weakness Breaks. For most players, E1 is the most impactful stopping point for investment.
Eidolon 2
The Dahlia's E2 deepens enemy debuffs by adding All-Type Resistance reduction of 20% to the Wilt debuff and automatically applying Wilt as enemies enter the field. This improves consistency and convenience by ensuring the debuff is always active. Combined with E1, E2 makes The Dahlia a complete Break team support and represents the optimal investment threshold for most players.
Eidolon 4
The Dahlia's E4 increases the instances of DMG dealt by Talent's Follow-Up ATK by 5, and every hit increases the target's DMG taken by 12%.
Eidolon 6
The Dahlia's E6 grants 150% Break Effect to all Dance Partners and advances their next action by 20% when using her Talent's Follow-Up ATK.
Conclusion
The Dahlia is an exceptionally powerful Super Break support unit who revolutionizes Break-focused teams in Honkai: Star Rail. Her gameplay focuses on maintaining her Zone effect, strategically using her Ultimate for Defense reduction and Weakness application, and leveraging the Dance Partner mechanic to convert Toughness damage into devastating Super Break hits. Building her with high Break Effect and SPD allows her to maximize both her personal damage and team support potential.
In Break-focused teams, especially paired with Firefly, The Dahlia provides high damage amplification, enabling Super Break damage before enemies are even broken. She's most effective when used as the primary Break support who fundamentally changes how Break teams operate. With proper build and timing, The Dahlia becomes a cornerstone that elevates any Break-focused lineup to top-tier performance.

Bennett Overview
This Page provides the Best Bennett Build information.
Bennett is an orphan discovered by an elderly adventurer as a baby, Bennett was raised in Mondstadt’s Adventurers’ Guild. He is the one and only member of “Benny’s Adventure Team,” as everyone else left the team after experiencing the constant misfortune that follows him.
Bennett is the most powerful Support in Genshin Impact at the moment. Bennett Support Build is mostly used by travelers. His Elemental Burst maximize the team DPS.
Bennett has different ways to help the party. Not only can increase party’s DPS by using the Elemental Burst and his healing ability also keep the team nice and healthy. Energy Recharge is the most important stats for Bennett, so the Support Build will also help travelers to stack the Energy Recharge for Bennett.
In Genshin Impact, Bennett is a 4 Stars rarity Pyro character currently in SS tier.

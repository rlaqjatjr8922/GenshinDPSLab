Reliquary of Truth
This is the best weapon for Klee in most situations. It increases her Crit Rate, Crit Damage, and Elemental Mastery.
CRIT Damage 88.2%
CRIT Rate is increased by 8%.
If the equipping character unleashes an Elemental Skill, they gain the Secret of Lies effect: Elemental Mastery is increased by 80 for 12s.
When the equipping character deals Lunar-Bloom DMG to an opponent, they gain the Moon of Truth effect: CRIT DMG is increased by 24% for 4s.
When both the Secret of Lies and Moon of Truth effects are active at the same time, the results of both effects will be increased by 50%.
Cashflow Supervision
Only usable reliably if Furina is on the team. However, in this scenario, it becomes her best weapon due to a wide range of buffs.
CRIT Rate 22.1%
ATK is increased by 16%.
When the current HP increases or decreases, Normal Attack DMG will be increased by 16%, and Charged Attack DMG will be increased by 14% for 4s. Max 3 stacks. This effect can be triggered once every 0.3s. When the wielder has 3 stacks, ATK SPD will be increased by 8%.
Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds
A decent alternative if you don’t have other crit-stat five-star weapons. However, you can still consider this one over most of the four-star options, apart from The Widsith.
CRIT Rate 33.1%
Increases Movement SPD by 10%.
When in battle, gain an 8% Elemental DMG Bonus every 4s. Max 4 stacks. Lasts until the character falls or leaves combat.
Note:
The above weapon effects are at Refinement 1 and level 90.

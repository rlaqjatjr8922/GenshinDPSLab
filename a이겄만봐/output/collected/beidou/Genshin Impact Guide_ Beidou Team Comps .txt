Beidou is a 4 star Electro Claymore character in Genshin Impact. She is one of the best Electro spreaders in the game, with the additional ability to counter enemies' attacks to deal massive electro damage. In this article, Gurugamer.com is going to showcase the best Beidou team comps to maximize her DPS capability.
Table of Contents
1. High-End Beidou Team Comps
2. Mid-Tier Beidou Team Comps
3. F2P Beidou Team Comp
Her best artifacts are 2 pieces of Thundering Fury set and 2 pieces of Gladiator's Finale set.
1. High-End Beidou Team Comps
Overall, Beidou is hindered by her burst having a high energy cost. Beidou team comps usually help her gain energy faster and increase the power of her Burst with Electro reactions. Overall, the reactions to primarily focus on are electro charge and superconduct.

Raiden Shogun is the main DPS of this team comp. The DMG of her elemental burst relies on the energy consumed by team members using burst. Both Beidou and Kujou Sara consumes 80 energy, which makes them highly effective to combo with Raiden.
Beidou is one of the best support for Raiden teams, just behind Yae Miko.

Eula is the main DPS of this lineup, with her damage mainly from her physical burst. The burst damage is maximized under the effect of Superconduct from Beidou and Zhongli's Tenacity set bonus.
Beidou can provide continuous Electro damage from backstage, which triggers superconduct when combined with Eula's Cryo. Chongyun's here to provide Eula with Cryo Resonance for more crit damage. Furthermore, his elemental skill also lowers the CD of Eula's burst (require C2).
Eula's physical damage potential can be improved by Beidou's superconduct buff.
Zhongli is a flex pick, boosting the team's damage using the Millelith set and providing a shield to keep them alive.

▼Anemo Hypercarry Xiao
★★★★★
- Xiao's DMG increases greatly because of supports.
- Since there are many Anemo characters in the team, the Elemental Burst cost significantly lowers.
- One of the strongest teams in-game.
▼Microwave Xiao
★★★★・
- Xiao in a team with double Geo
- Utilizes raw Anemo DMG & microwave effect from Geo constructs
▼Sunfire Melt Xiao
★★★・・
- Enables Xiao to deal Anemo DMG alongside the consistent Pyro-swirl reaction from the Burst of Jean & Bennett, and Melt reaction from Layla's Skill & Burst

Rotation Of Anemo Hypercarry Xiao Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Furina's Elemental Skill.
2
Switch to Faruzan and use Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Furina and use Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Xianyun and use Elemental Burst and skill.
5
Switch to Xiao and use Elemental Skill twice and use Elemental Burst.
6
Repeat from step 1
Xiao's infusion disappears when he is switched out so you must complete the set up first before using Xiao's Burst.

Rotation Of Microwave Xiao Team
No.
Procedure
1
Hold Zhongli's Skill for shield & monolith
2
Use Zhongli's Burst for crowd control & ATK buff
3
Switch to Albedo
4
Use Albedo's Skill and place the Geo construct near the monolith for resonating vibration
5
Switch to Xianyun
6
Use Xianyun's Burst for Plunge ATK buff and use Elemental Skill.
7
Switch to Xiao
8
Use Xiao's Elemental Skill and use Elemental Burst.
9
Repeat from step 1
Before using Xiao's Burst, ensure that Geo constructs & Xianyun's Burst are all active. Gather the enemies within the geo constructs for high damage.

No.

2

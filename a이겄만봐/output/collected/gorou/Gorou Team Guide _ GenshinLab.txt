Gorou Team Overview
This page provides the best Gorou Team information. Gorou is a Support in most situations.
Gorou provides excellent support to Geo characters. Gorou can provide Geo DMG Bonus and DEF Bonus. This makes Gorou usually pairs up with Geo characters.
Gorou can be assigned to different teams. This guide shows some of the best teams of Gorou.

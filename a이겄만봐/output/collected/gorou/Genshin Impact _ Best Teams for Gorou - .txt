Best Teams for Gorou
Gorou Works Best As Support for Geo DEF-Scaling Damage Dealers
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Mono Geo Team
- Most of Gorou's buffs are better utilized in a Mono Geo team where Itto is the DPS.
- A Geo Sub-DPS can also be used as long as they scale on DEF as well and can benefit from Gorou's buffs.
2nd
▼ Lunar-Crystallize Team
- In a Lunar-Crystallize team, Gorou is a secondary support since Illuga's buffs are more impactful.
- Zibai is the preferred DPS for Lunar-Crystallize teams, but she won't be able to benefit from Gorou's Geo DMG buffs.
3rd
▼ Geo DPS Team with PECH Support
- Another variant of a Mono Geo team that Gorou can support is a team with at least one non-Geo member.
- Durin's ability to shred Geo RES and his Hexerei synergy with Albedo makes him a great pick for a non-Geo support.
- Gorou can also buff Albedo's damage while Albedo and Durin buff Itto.
- Natlan Sub-DPS or Support characters can be used as an alternative to Durin since they can hold the Scroll set.
4th
▼ F2P Geo DPS Team
- F2P version of a Geo DPS team that can either be Mono Geo or Lunar-Crystallize.
- Yun Jin and Bennett are used for Mono Geo while Illuga and Aino for a Lunar-Crystallize variant.
- A Natlan Sub-DPS can be used for Geo DMG buffing like Kachina or Pyro Traveler.
Gorou's kit can buff both DEF and Geo DMG Bonus, but he works better with Geo damage dealers that scale on DEF. While Zibai is a powerful new DPS, she doesn't benefit from Gorou's Geo DMG buff. However,
Gorou is much easier to slot into teams now that newly released supports allow for better flexibility in team comps that need 3 Geo characters.

Gorou is best used in a Mono Geo team where he can support two damage dealers, the on-field DPS and the Sub-DPS. In a no-reaction team like Mono Geo, having more damage dealt via two DPS is better than one.
Rotation Of Mono Geo Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Zhongli and use his Skill (Hold) to create a Shield
2
Switch to Gorou and use his Skill (Tap) then Burst
3
Switch to Chiori and use her Burst then Skill twice to switch to the next character
4
Use Itto's Skill then Burst
5
Start dealing damage with Charged Attack spam
6
Use Itto's Skill or Normal Attacks when he's running out of stacks
7
Repeat from Step 1

Noelle
- Xilonen and Noelle are both DEF-scaling Geo damage dealers and can be used as the main DPS.
- Noelle provides additional healing, improving the team's survivability.

Yun Jin
- If you're replacing Zhongli, you can bring any Geo support such as Illuga or Yun Jin.
- Illuga can provide a Geo DMG buff, but it's much weaker than his Lunar-Crystallize buffs.
- Use Yun Jin as an alternative when the DPS is Xilonen or Noelle.

Albedo

- Albedo works great as an alternative to Chiori since he also scales on DEF. However, his Flowers can be destroyed, so he'll need to recreate them if they break.
- Kachina can be used as a Geo Sub-DPS and scales on DEF, but she's a lot weaker than Albedo and works better as a Scroll set holder with a PECH character or an enemy that applies an element to itself.

Lunar-Crystallize does more damage than Gorou's Mono Geo team but it doesn't fully maximize his buffs. Since his Geo DMG buff isn't used for Lunar-Crystallize DMG, it can only buff Zibai's Normal Attacks that don't deal Lunar-Crystallize. But his DEF buffs help Zibai a lot.
Rotation Of Lunar-Crystallize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Columbina and use her Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Gorou and use his Skill (Tap) then Burst
3
Switch to Illuga and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Zibai and use her Skill
5
Deal damage with her Normal Attacks and use her Skill when she gets enough stacks
6
Use Zibai's Burst to end her rotation
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire

With the addition of more non-Geo supports, a Geo DPS team with at least one non-Geo member is now viable. The Hexerei support duo of Albedo and Durin can be used for RES shred and a small DMG buff for Itto.
Albedo can be built as a damage dealer instead since Itto can't access Albedo's full DMG buff and Albedo also scales on DEF for his damage.
Rotation Of Geo DPS Team with PECH Support
No.

3

Switch to Itto and use his Skill then Burst
5
Start dealing damage with Charged Attack spam
6
Use Itto's Skill or Normal Attacks when he's running out of stacks
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
Substitute Characters
Character
Sub
Details
Itto
Xilonen
Noelle
- Xilonen can be used as a Geo DEF damage dealer as long as there's only one PECH character.
- Noelle can also be used as the F2P alternative to Itto and can provide healing.
Albedo
Kachina
Yun Jin
- It can be difficult to replace the Albedo and Durin duo since most Geo supports have certain restrictions in team compositions.
- Kachina can be paired with Mavuika and provide Nightsoul points consumption for Mavuika's Burst.
- Yun Jin can be used if Xilonen or Noelle is used as the DPS instead of Itto.
Durin
Mavuika
Pyro Traveler
Citlali
Bennett
- Mavuika can buff DMG but needs a second Natlan character like Kachina to help generate energy for her Burst.
- Pyro Traveler can provide a smaller DMG buff and also hold the Scroll set for buffing Elemental DMG.
- Any PECH support can be used, but Natlan characters are preferred for their ability to hold the Scroll set.
- Bennett can be used as an ATK buffer since Itto and Noelle technically scale on ATK. However, he is not compatible with Xilonen.
When replacing the Albedo-Durin duo, have at least one Geo character for Gorou's buffs then use any Natlan PECH character with Scroll set as the non-Geo. You can still use other stat buffers if you don't want to focus on Elemental DMG buffing.

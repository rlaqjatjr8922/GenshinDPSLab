Citlali Allows for More Melt Triggers
Unlike her competitors,
Citlali has the longest Off-Field Cryo application duration which is also not limited to a select area.
The AoE of her Cryo application is also fairly wide, and she provides a shield to boot, making her one of Arlecchino's best pairings for a Melt team due to inherent synergy. The only downside is that the optimal rotation requires you to wait in-between Normal Attack inputs for Arlecchino, since Citlali doesn't apply Cryo faster than Arlecchino can apply Pyro.
Rotation Of Melt Arlecchino
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino's Elemental Skill to apply Blood-Debts to enemies.
└
You can use her Elemental Burst on the second rotation at the start if you need healing. Just make sure to do Burst first before Skill, or use her Skill, Burst then Skill.
2
Swap to Citlali. Use her Skill, then Burst.
3
Swap to Xilonen. Use Skill. Burst is optional. If you have Xilonen on
Archaic Petra
, swap steps 3 and 4 and use Bennett first to apply Pyro. Pick up the Pyro Crystallize shard.
4
Use Bennett's Skill (Tap) into Burst.
5
Switch to Arlecchino and use Charged Attack to absorb the Blood-Debts to gain Bond of Life for her Pyro infusion.
6
Normal Attack (x3) into Dash cancel as Arlecchino. Do this twice, then refrain from attacking to allow for Citlali to reapply more Cryo (around 2.5 seconds). Repeat this whole process until support durations expire.
7
Repeat from step 1.
Arlecchino will always want to use her Elemental Skill first in order for her passive to turn the Blood-Debt Directives into Blood-Debt Dues, which provide more Bond of Life percent and therefor allow her to have longer infusion duration.
Arlecchino's combo with Citlali has a soft requirement wherein she needs to wait in between Normal Attack sequences so she doesn't overwhelm Citlali's application with Pyro, which can lead to lower DPS since Citlali would end up triggering the Melt instead.

Only Electro & Pyro Characters Can Be Used
Chevreuse allows Overload teams to deal competitive DPS due to her Pyro and Electro RES Shred, attack buffs at C0, and DMG% bonus for said elements with C6.
But adding her to a team limits it to only Pyro and Electro characters, since her passive that provides the RES Shred is only active when the party includes two elements exclusively. It's highly recommended to build the team with these restrictions in mind, alongside the fact that you have to continually trigger the Overload reaction so the RES Shred is constantly applied.
Rotation Of Overload Arlecchino
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino's Elemental Skill to apply Blood-Debts to enemies.
└
You can use her Elemental Burst on the second rotation at the start if you need healing. Just make sure to do Burst first before Skill, or use her Skill, Burst then Skill.
2
Swap to Bennett. Use her Skill (Tap), then Burst.
3
Use Fischl's Skill or Burst.
4
Swap to Chevreuse. Use Burst then Skill (Hold).
5
Switch to Arlecchino and use Charged Attack to absorb the Blood-Debts to gain Bond of Life for her Pyro infusion.
6
Normal Attack (x3) into Dash cancel as Arlecchino. Repeat this sequence until support durations expire.
7
Repeat from step 1.
Holding Chevreuse's Elemental Skill activates her passive that provides a modest ATK% buff to the party, so it's recommended to always favor using the Hold version of her skill over the Tap version whenever possible.

Good Into Pyro-Weak Content
Mono-teams are an umbrella term for comps that are predominantly composed of 1 element, in this case Pyro, with the last slot being an Elemental RES shredder.
In the sample above, Kazuha equips the Viridescent Venerer set to shred Pyro by triggering a Pyro Swirl. He also adds an additional Elemental DMG% bonus from his own passive by doing so. This type of team is viable into content that is at least Pyro-Weak or not completely invulnerable to Pyro.
Most of the damage is coming from the raw numbers in Arlecchino and Xiangling's kit, which can perform well if you've invested a decent amount into either.
Rotation Of Mono-Pyro Arlecchino
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino's Elemental Skill to apply Blood-Debts to enemies.
└
You can use her Elemental Burst on the second rotation at the start if you need healing. Just make sure to do Burst first before Skill, or use her Skill, Burst then Skill.
2
Swap to Bennett. Use her Skill (Tap), then Burst.
3
Use Kazuha's Skill (Tap). Burst is optional.
4
Swap to Xiangling. Deploy Skill then use Burst.
5
Switch to Arlecchino and use Charged Attack to absorb the Blood-Debts to gain Bond of Life for her Pyro infusion.
6
Normal Attack (x3) into Dash cancel as Arlecchino. Repeat this sequence until support durations expire.
7
Repeat from step 1.
Because this team example has no shielder, making use of Arlecchino's Burst for healing may become an important part of the rotation. Remember that it clears her current Bond of Life percentage, so time its usage wisely.

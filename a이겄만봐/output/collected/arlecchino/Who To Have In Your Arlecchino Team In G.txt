Arlecchino is a
5-star Pyro polearm
character that works best as a main-DPS. As mentioned, her kit centers hugely around the
Bond of Life
mechanic - meaning she doesn't always get on well with healers. She is a pretty self-sufficient character, meaning that she works best with characters who will either
buff or shield her.
Updated on January 05, 2025, by Sanyam Jain: Xilonen's release has significantly buffed the Mono Pyro team for Arlecchino, and the entry for it has been updated to reflect the following.
Arlecchino’s Best Overall Team: Vaporize
Main DPS:
Arlecchino
Sub DPS:
Xingqiu
Sub DPS:
Kazuha
Support:
Thoma
Arlecchino applies Pyro pretty consistently. Because of this, she works well alongside characters who have good
Hyrdo application
to maximize on
Vaporise
damage. Not only does
Xingqiu's Elemental Burst
excel at this, it grants
interruption resistance
- something else that Arlecchino needs.
Throwing in someone like
Kazuha
allows for even more damage with the
Swirl
reaction. The final character,
Thoma
, provides
shields
with both his
Elemental Skill
and
Burst
. If you've got his
C6
, expect a nice damage bonus to Normal, Charged, and Plunge attacks.
During combat, Arlecchino can only be healed by her Elemental Burst and not by other members of your team.
If you've managed to get
C1
of Arlecchino's constellation, you'll get even more
interruption resistance
while performing Normal Attacks during
Masque of the Red Death.
This state is entered when Arclecchino has a
Bond of Life equal to or greater than 30 percent of her Max HP
, so you'll want to keep on spamming these attacks and using her Burst.
Character
Action
Thoma
Start by generating a shield using Blazing Blessing, Thoma's Elemental Skill.
Xingqiu
Immediately use Xingqiu's elemental skill and his Elemental burst to get Hydro application.
Kazuha
Follow up with Kazuha's elemental skill, plunging attack, and then his Elemental Burst.
Arlecchino
Upon switching to Arlecchino, immediately use her Elemental Skill, do a normal attack, and then begin spamming between charged attacks, her Elemental Skill, and her Elemental Burst - using normal attacks where your stamina runs out.
Free to Play Overload Team
Main DPS:
Arlecchino
Sub DPS:
Fischl
Sub DPS:
Chevreuse
Support:
Thoma
A good option for
F2P
players is utilizing one of Genshin's best
Overload
supports,
Chevreuse
. Using Chevreuse's attack buff and resistance shred alongside both
Thoma's shield
and
Fischl's Electro application
makes for a formidable team. If you fancy keeping your team with two of each element,
Beidou
is a good alternative to Thoma to ensure you have a shielder.
It's best to keep any team with Chevreuse on to Pyro and Electro only to get the most out of her Passive Skills.
If you've got Fischl's
C6
, expect Oz to deal
30 percent of Fischl's attack as Electro damage alongside all
your normal attacks while he's out on the field
. In addition to this, keeping your team to only Pyro and Electro means you can take advantage of Chevreuse's passive skill:
Vanguard's Coordinated Tactics
.
This will decrease the
Pyro
and
Electro
RES
of any opponents who get hit by your Overload reactions. Also, whenever Chevreuse hits someone with her Elemental Skill, characters in the party will gain
one percent increased ATK for every 1,000 Max HP she has.
(Just make sure you've got a good aim).
Character
Action
Thoma
Start by generating a shield using Blazing Blessing, Thoma's Elemental Skill.
Fischl
Summon Oz using Fischl's Elemental Skill.
Chevreuse
Switch to Chevreuse and aim at as many opponents as possible using her Elemental Skill, ensuring you're quick while also aiming well.
Arlecchino
Since Chevreuse will be healing your active character, immediately use her Elemental Skill to start the Bond of Life. Follow this up with her Burst, charged attacks, and then repeat.

Mono Pyro Swirl Team
Main DPS:
Arlecchino
Support:
Kaedehara Kazuha
Support:
Bennett
Support:
Xilonen
While Arlecchino's mono Pyro team was already decent before, it got a massive buff with Xilonen's release. Both Xilonen and Kazuha share the buffs they can provide to this team if you have both their best sets. They can both give a DMG boost and a DEF shred on the enemies, which
buffs Arlecchino's damage by a significant amount
.
Added to this, Bennett's Burst also increases her ATK, and since there are two Pyro characters on the team,
he doesn't have any problems regenerating his energy
. Another great thing about this team is that neither Kazuha nor Xilonen are reliant on their Bursts since they can apply every buff with their Skill.
Of course, Kazuha's buff lasts a lot longer if you use his Burst since
he can consistently apply the RES shred again
.
Character
Action
Arlecchino
It's important to start the rotation for this team with Arlecchino and use her skill on the enemies so that you have the
buffed version of it ready by the time you return to her
.
Bennett
Bennett is a quick-swap character on the team and you just n
eed to use his skill and burst every time
you repeat this rotation.
Kazuha
Kazuha just needs to use his Skill on the
enemy to Swirl Pyro for this team
, but you can also use his Burst if it's ready.
Xilonen
Xilonen can use her Skill and quickly perform two basic attacks to
fill her Nightsoul bar and activate her buffs
. You can also use her Burst but it won't be of much use since Arlecchino can't heal from it.
Double Geo Team
Main DPS:
Arlecchino
Sub DPS:
Chiori
Sub DPS:
Yelan
Support:
Zhongli
Certainly not a team built for F2P players, this team composition is all about aiding Arlecchino's
existing kit
. Not only does this team benefit from both
off-field Hydro
and Geo damage
and application, it also utilizes the
Geo Resonance
brought on by having two Geo users on your team to shield Arlecchino even more.
Make sure you always have the Geo structures from Zhongli's Elemental Skill active, as well as Chiori's Geo constructs, as this will allow for multiple Tamato dolls.
Character
Action
Zhongli
Use Zhongli's Elemental Skill to bring up a shield for your party members.
Yelan
Switch to Yelan by quickly using her Elemental Burst to begin Hydro application.
Chiori
Immediately use Chiori's Burst, followed by tapping her Elemental Skill twice to speedily switch to Arlecchino and summon two Tamato dolls - if Zhongli's Dominus Lapidis is still active.
Arlecchino
Once switched over, use her Elemental Skill and follow it up with her Elemental Burst as soon as you can. After this, use her Elemental Skill again and then a Charged attack. Now that you've got Pyro Infusion, spam normal attacks.

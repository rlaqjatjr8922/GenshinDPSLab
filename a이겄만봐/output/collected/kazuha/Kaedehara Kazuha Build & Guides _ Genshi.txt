Kaedehara Kazuha's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Powerful buffs increasing damage, resistance shred and crowd control.
Off-field elemental application of Anemo and Absorbed element.
Incredibly flexible and used in a lot of the best team compositions.
Weaknesses
Cannot swirl the right elements if the enemy has a persistent Elemental Aura. For example slimes and some bosses.
While still a powerful character, there are now alternatives in the meta such as Escoffier and Xilonen that also provide RES Shred.

2.1.
Kaedehara Kazuha Energy Recharge Requirements
The exact amount of Energy Recharge Kazuha wants heavily depends on your team
and if you use
Chihayaburu
once or twice between each burst as well
as if you simply tap or hold
Chihayaburu
.
General advice is to aim for around 200% - 215% if you tap Kazuha's skill twice in a rotation,
you can lower this amount if you hold your skill or if someone in your team
is using a Favonius weapon. Furthermore, it is better to aim higher than lower 
if you plan to use Kazuha in multiple teams.
3.
Combos and How to Play Kaedehara Kazuha
The goal with Kazuha is to provide massive buffs to the party so that
your DPS and Sub DPS deal significantly more damage. This comes
in 2 main ways:
Through the
Viridescent Venerer
artifact set.
Through triggering swirls and activating
Poetics of Fuubutsu
which gives a ~30-40% DMG bonus to the swirled element.
The important part to be aware of, is both of these effects trigger based
on which elements are swirled and Kazuha therefore needs to swirl when
the enemy has the elemental aura of your damage dealer.
Chihayaburu
is Kazuha's main source of crowd
control due to the fact it can pull a range of enemies towards him. This
also causes Kazuha to jump in the air allowing you to perform a stronger
plunge attack dealing damage based on the absorbed element.
Kazuha Slash
is incredibly powerful providing the majority of
Kazuha's damage as well as applying an absorbed element which is why it is
so important to have enough Energy Recharge. This can help your
team with additional elemental reactions. You just need to be careful that
the right elements are absorbed.
3.1.
Should You Tap or Hold Chihayaburu?
Kazuhas Elemental Skill,
Chihayaburu
has different effects
and utilities depending on if you tap or hold the skill. They generate different
amounts of energy, pull enemies in more and even different amounts of elemental
application.
You want to hold
Chihayaburu
whenever you need more powerful
grouping or additional energy. Otherwise tapping the skill will allow you to
swap to the rest of the party faster and deal more DPS.

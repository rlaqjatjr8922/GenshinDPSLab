Childe Works Best as a Hydro On-Field DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ International Team
- Xiangling's Burst and Kazuha's Pyro-infused Burst will allow Childe to Vaporize consistently.
- Bennett and Kazuha will be able to buff the damage dealt greatly
2nd
▼ Electro-Charged Team
- With the addition of Furina and Ororon, Childe's Taser team can deal great damage damage
- Shinobu will provide healing to offset Furina's HP drain as well as do additional off-field Electro damage
3rd
▼ Burgeon Team
- Utilizing Childe's rapid Hydro application, creating Dendro cores and then exploding them using Pyro will deal powerful damage
Childe is a Hydro Bow character that shines the best if used as a Hydro on-field DPS. He is able to rapidly apply Hydro making him a great on-field Hydro application.

Different from the original International team, the Childe version of it focuses on improving the damage of Xiangling and Childe. With right timing and enemy elemental aura, Kazuha can Swirl both Hydro and Pyro to increase their own personal damage and Vaporize damage.
Rotation of Childe International Team
No.
Procedure
1
Enter with Childe and use his Elemental Skill then Normal Attack once to apply Hydro
2
Switch to Kazuha and tap his Elemental Skill
3
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst
4
Switch to Xiangling and use her Skill and Burst
5
Switch back to Kazuha and use his Burst
6
Switch back to Childe and use his Burst then Elemental Skill
7
Start attacking enemies using his Normal Attack and Charged Attack
8
Repeat from 2

Childe is the on-field Hydro driver for this team comp. With the other three being able to give buffs, healing, energy regeneration and off-field Hydro/Electro damage, Electro-Charged reactions will be plenty.
Rotation of Childe Electro-Charged Team
No.
Procedure
1
Enter with Furina and use both her Skill and Burst
2
Switch to Shinobu and use her Skill
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill and Burst
4
Switch to Childe and use his Burst then Skill to start attacking with Normal Attack and Charged Attack
5
Repeat from 1

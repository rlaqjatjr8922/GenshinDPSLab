Best Team Compositions for Lyney in Genshin Impact
Mono Pyro with Bennett
Lyney’s passive talents incentivize a Mono Pyro team, and his best teammate is Bennett due to his huge ATK buff coupled with his ability to battery Pyro characters. Lyney can gain up to
100% DMG increase by having 2 Pyro teammates
. The last slot is reserved for a buffer or a shielder.
In this section, we will discuss several variations of a Mono Pyro team with Bennett. These Mono Pyro variants feature at least 3 Pyro characters since the DMG bonuses from Lyney’s passives cap out at having 3 Pyro characters in a team.
Kazuha/Sucrose variants
Other Anemo characters like Venti, Jean, and Lynette can also be used here, but none of them are as good as Kazuha/Sucrose in terms of buffing capabilities.

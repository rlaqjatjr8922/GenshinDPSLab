Lyney Shines Best At Mono-Pyro Teams
Team
Rating
Description
Best
▼ Mono-Pyro Lyney
- Best team composition to maximize Lyney's Passive.
- High Damage output to enemies inflicted by Pyro.
- Can be weak against enemies that have natural element like Thunder Manifestation.
2nd
▼ Reaction Lyney
- Lyney as a main DPS with a more flexible team comp.
- Flexible team comp but does not maximize the passive of Lyney.
- Depending on the team-mate, this might deal more damage than Mono-Pyro.

This team comp focuses on buffing Lyney's Pyro DMG and ATK. While it is not fully mono Pyro for Lyney's passive, Xilonen and Kazuha can make up for it with their buffs. It also enables Pyro resonance to further increase ATK%. Another variation of this team is to use Zhongli instead of Kazuha for Geo resonance.
Rotation Of Mono-Pyro Lyney
No.
Procedure
1
Enter with Xilonen and use her Elemental Skill
2
Use Normal Attacks twice to activate Samplers, then use Burst
3
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Kazuha and use Skill then Burst
5
Finally, switch to Lyney and use Charged Attacks 4 times
6
Use Burst and then cancel it early with his Skill (This does not cast Skill)
7
End the rotation with another use of his Skill
8
Repeat from 1
This rotation first activates the buffs and then lets Lyney stack up his Prop Surplus up to five (5) so he can use his Elemental Skill to deal powerful dmg. You must cancel his Burst early to still get the buffs for the finishing Elemental Skill attack. Additionally, the number of Charged Attacks would depend on how fast you are able to do the rotation. Making use of dash or jump cancels will greatly improve rotation time.

Rotation Of Reaction Lyney
No.
Procedure
1
Enter with Furina and use both her Skill and Burst
2
Switch to Xilonen and use Skill
3
Hit enemies 2 times with Xilonen's Normal Attack
4
Use Xilonen's Burst
5
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
6
Switch to Lyney and use his Charged Attack up to 4 times
7
Use Lyney's Burst and cancel with Skill button
8
Use Elemental Skill to finish rotation
9
Repeat from 1
Substitute Characters
Character
Sub
Details
Bennett
Xiangling
- Xiangling will be able to increase Lyney's DMG output with C1 and C6. Outside of that, Guoba can drop a pepper to increase ATK% but for a small amount
Xilonen
Zhongli
Sucrose
- Zhongli can also provide Elemental RES shred, but without healing, Furina's summons will deal lesser damage
- Sucrose can buff EM and hold VV for additional damage
Furina
Kokomi
- Kokomi can bring Thrilling Tales to buff Lyney's ATK%
Substituting in this team is relatively easy.  Any Hydro/Cryo sub-DPS that do not need the active character's Normal Attack to trigger their off-field attack can be used as substitute. Since Lyney will be doing Charged Attacks mostly, it's not recommended to use Normal Attack sub-DPS like Xingqiu, Yelan or Beidou.

Best Teams for Xingqiu
Xingqiu Works Best as a Hydro Sub-DPS for Hydro Application
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Vaporize Team
- Xingqiu is one of the best enablers for Vaporize with Normal Attack Pyro DPS characters.
- Since his Elemental Burst does a coordinated attack, it can trigger Vaporize for them in each Normal Attack hit.
- This team can be turned into a Vapemelt team by including Citlali or other Cryo Sub-DPS characters.
2nd
▼ Freeze Team
- In a standard Freeze team, Xingqiu can be used as an alternative to Yelan and Furina.
- As long as the DPS uses Normal Attacks during their rotations, Xingqiu can work with them.
3rd
▼ Lunar-Charged (Electro-Charged) Team
- Xingqiu's Elemental Burst is great for applying Hydro for Electro-Charged & Lunar-Charged.
- The particles he can generate are also good for Taser teams since most of them rely on Bursts for applying Element.
4th
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- Since Dendro Cores or Bloom is needed for Hyperbloom, Xingqiu is a good Sub-DPS for it.
- Cyno is a good Hyperbloom DPS that needs a lot of Particle funneling for his Burst which makes Xingqiu a great Favonius support for him.
5th
▼ Superbloom Team
- Nilou, as the core for Superbloom, doesn't have good Hydro application thus requiring a 2nd or sometimes 3rd Hydro character for applying the Element.
- Xingqiu's damage reduction and small healing can be somewhat useful in Bloom.
- A dedicated healer is still recommended for Bloom teams.
Xingqiu, as a Hydro Sub-DPS, is used in many teams due to Hydro's involvement in multiple reactions. His defensive buffs also make him a good option for a Hydro Sub-DPS.

Rotation Of Vaporize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino and use her Skill to apply Pyro
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks
3
Switch to Zhongli and use his Skill
4
Switch to Xingqiu and use his Skill then Burst
5
Switch back to Arlecchino and use her Charged Attack to gain Pyro Infusion
6
Deal damage with Arlecchino's Normal and Charged Attacks
7
Use Arlecchino's Burst when her Infusion runs out
8
Repeat from Step 1

Yanfei
- While Hu Tao has weaker damage compared to Mavuika, Hu Tao can easily proc Xingqiu's Off-Field Attacks with her Normal and Charged Attacks while Mavuika will have to adopt a Normal Attack playstyle.
- Hu Tao can also benefit from Hydro Resonance with Xingqiu and Dahlia activating it, giving her damage reduction and faster ATK speed.

- Using Citlali will turn the team into Vapemelt since her Skill summons a shield and Itzpapa, that deals Cryo damage Off-Field.
- Diona is another great option for shielder for Vapemelt because of her healing (and Elemental Mastery buff at C6).

- Sucrose has an Elemental Mastery buff that can increase the Vaporize or Melt damage dealt.
For a Vapemelt team, Citlali is the best Cryo Sub-DPS for it since she is able to shred both Pyro and Hydro RES on enemies. Rosaria with Instructor set can be used as alternative to her.

Lunar-Charged (Electro-Charged) doesn't benefit from damage buffs which makes Xingqiu the preferred Hydro Sub-DPS. His ability to generate a lot of Particles is also a plus. Xingqiu can equip Instructor set if you want more EM.
Rotation Of Lunar-Charged (Electro-Charged) Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Ineffa and use her Skill or Burst
2
Switch to Xingqiu and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Ifa and use his Burst to gather enemies then use his Skill
5
Use Ifa's Normal Attacks or hold the Attack button to apply Anemo and proc Xingqiu's Burst attacks
6
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire.
There's no strict rotation aside from having the Reaction Driver go last so you can mix up Xingqiu and the others rotation order.

Ineffa

Aside from Electro and Hydro, Anemo is a good additional Element for Taser since the Swirl reactions reapplies the Elements causing a Chain Reaction of Electro-Charged.

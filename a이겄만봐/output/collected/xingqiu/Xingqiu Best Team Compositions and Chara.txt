Xingqiu's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Xingqiu team composition guide and character synergy
overview for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams
for Xingqiu, alongside recommended character pairings that help
maximize his off-field damage and elemental application.
Xingqiu is a
Hydro
off-field character who serves two
primary roles in a team: providing off-field damage and applying Hydro to trigger
elemental reactions. Because his Hydro application is only triggered when the
active character performs a Normal Attack, his best teams generally feature
Normal Attack-focused damage dealers. In those setups, he remains one of the
best Hydro applicators in the entire game.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Xingqiu

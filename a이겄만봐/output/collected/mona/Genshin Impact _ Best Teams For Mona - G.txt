Mona Works Best as a Vaporize Support
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Vaporize Team
- With Mona's new buffs as a Hexerei character, she is now a dedicated Vaporize support who boosts the reaction damage.
- Sucrose or Durin can be used as the 2nd Hexerei character for activating Secret Rite effect.
2nd
▼ Pyro Sub-DPS Vaporize Team
- Mona can be used as a reaction driver for Vaporize with the Pyro coming from the Sub-DPS.
- Xiangling is the best Vaporize Sub-DPS and having her damage boosted by 3 supports can work amazingly.
3rd
▼ Freeze Team
- For a non-Hexerei team, Freeze team is able to make use of Mona's Omen debuff.
- Mona can be used for Electro-Charged as well but her Omen debuff doesn't boost much damage there.
4th
▼ F2P Vaporize Team
- F2P Vaporize team with Hexerei buffs.
- Sucrose is used as the 2nd Hexerei and shreds RES for the DPS character.
Mona's new buffs as a Hexerei character fully commit her into being a Vaporize support. The new Mercury buff can boost Vaporize damage but requires her to be on-field to stack it up. Omen can now be extended but also requires Mona's on-field time to do so.
Mona Team Comp Guidelines
Due to Mona's new buffs, her team composition differs a bit compared to before since she now needs a 2nd Hexerei character to activate her Vaporize buff. See below for the recommended 2nd Hexerei character:
Durin
- Durin is able to shred the RES of enemies while also dealing off-field Pyro damage.
- It's also easy for him to charge his Burst compared to other Pyro Sub-DPS characters.
Sucrose
- 4-Star Hexerei option but works well since she has access to the Viridescent Venerer set for shredding RES.
- Can reapply Hydro or Pyro via Elemental Conversion on her Burst.
Fischl can be used as alternative to Sucrose. But her Electro application can affect Hydro and Pyro reactions.

With Durin's release, he can be used as Pyro Sub-DPS and RES shred debuffer. His role overlaps a bit with Xilonen's but Mualani has limited buffer options due to her HP scaling. Xilonen can provide healing but if you want a damage booster, Mavuika can be used.
Rotation Of Vaporize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mualani and use a Normal Attack to apply Hydro
2
Switch to Durin and use his Skill twice then Burst
3
Switch to Mona and use her Burst to debuff enemies with Omen
4
Use Mona's Normal Attacks multiple times to extend Omen and stack Mercury but don't consume too much time
5
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks to activate her Source Samples
6
Switch to Mualani and use her Skill
7
Collide with enemies until she receives 3 stacks and use her Normal Attack
8
Use Mualani's Burst before buffs end
9
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
With a Hydro DPS, the rotation is a bit complicated since you need Mona's Hydro application removed using another character. This is to allow the Pyro aura to remain for Mualani to proc Vaporize with.

F2P Vaporize team with Mona as damage booster. Only 4-Star characters and weapons are used for this team.
Rotation Of F2P Vaporize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Xingqiu and use his Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Yanfei and use her Normal Attacks to erase any Hydro applied and change it with Pyro
3
Switch to Sucrose and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Mona and use her Skill then Burst
5
Use Normal Attacks to extend Omen debuff and stack Mercury
6
Switch to Yanfei and use her Burst then Charged Attack
7
Use Yanfei's Skill to gain max seals then Charged Attack again
8
Deal damage with Normal Attacks and use her Charged Attack if max seals
9
Use Yanfei's Skill whenever it's available
10
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
Mona's Omen extension can be done midway Yanfei's turn if you want to be more efficient with the other teammates' buffs. However this requires precision on the timing to prevent Mona's Omen debuff from expiring.

Genshin Impact: Best Ganyu Team Comp For DPS And Free-2-Play Settings
Kamisato Ayaka is a Cryo Sword user and is one of the most powerful DPS characters in Genshin Impact. She can deal great damage by dashing between opponents with her Senho sprint, freezing them, and then moving on to the next. In this article, we are going to showcase the best Ayaka team comp for DPS, for both Premium and F2P players.

Ayaka is the reason why Cryo is considered the best Element in Genshin Impact for DPS.
1. Best Ayaka Team Comp (High End)
This is actually an Ayaka freeze team comp, which constantly utilizes the Freeze reaction to control the course of the fight and deal Cryo damage to her opponents. This
Ayaka team build
can bring out the maximum potential of Ayaka's Blizzard Strayer Artifact set, which provides up to 40% Crit Rate.
As this lineup consists of three
5 star Genshin Impact characters
, only paid players would be able to max it out.

Mona serves as the key of this build, with her ability to support Ayaka with constant Hydro applications via her Skill. Her Elemental Burst also serves as a good source of burst damage.
Both Mona and Ayaka are DPS characters - they make this the best Ayaka team comp for DPS.
Bennett is the Healer and Attack Buffer of the team. Make sure that you apply Pyro element from Bennett first before using Mona's Elemental Burst... and once the bubble encases the foe, pop it using Bennett. Make sure to apply his attack bonus buff whenever you are going to use Ayaka or Mona's burst.
Lastly, Kazuha serves the crowd control and buffer role. Overall, this is the Ayaka best team comp for DPS.

The reason that this team comp is mid tier is because outside of Ayaka, the rest of the lineup are 4-star characters. If you are a light spender or have played for a long time as F2p, this Ayaka team comp is probably right up your alley.

Similar to the high end lineup, this mid tier Ayaka team comp also makes use of the Freeze reaction. However, it focuses more on the perma-freeze playstyle, which is easier than the above chain reactions.
Xingqiu and Ayaka can work pretty well together
Chongyun's Steady Breathing passive can increase Ayaka's attack speed while his Rimechaser Blade passive can reduce enemy Cryo Res. While his skill is a bit redundant, the added effect can still support Ayaka decently. He also serves as Ayaka's battery so that she can fire off her burst more often.
Furthermore, having 2 Cryo users Chongyun and Ayaka on the same team unlocks the Shattering Ice elemental resonance, which gives you a 15% Crit Rate bonus.
Xingqiu is the main freeze enabler due to his elemental skill. Make sure to give him the Sacrificial Sword so that he can use his burst faster.
Bennett is the best support released in Genshin Impact to date.
Lastly, Bennett is the main healer and buffer. Make sure to buff before unleashing Ayaka's Elemental Burst for the highest damage possible.

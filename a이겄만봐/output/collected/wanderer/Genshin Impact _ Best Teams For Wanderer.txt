Synergy Details
Synergy Of Hypercarry Wanderer Team
Character
Detail
Faruzan
She is a must-have support to pair with any Anemo DPS character such as Wanderer. Her talent kit is tailored for boosting Anemo damage. Also, Elegy for the End provides additional buffs and The Exile provides additional Energy that are both beneficial for the team.
Zhongli
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' resistance and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
Bennett
An excellent healer and buffer who can cleanse any element affecting Wanderer. With Noblesse Oblige x4, he can provide an additional ATK buff that can significantly increase Wanderer's ATK.
The objective of this team is to funnel all buffs to Wanderer. This team highlights the various multipliers that can drastically improve Wanderer's damage output.
Pros & Cons Of Hypercarry Wanderer Team
Pros
- Strong damage for the whole duration of Wanderer's Skill
- Increased damage also includes projectiles from Gales of Reverie passive
- Additional ATK when he triggers Pyro-Swirl with an enemy affected by Pyro or thru Bennett's AoE
Cons
- Jade-Claimed Flower passive is not optimized unless another element besides Pyro is swirled with his Skill
- Needs to sustain all buffs
- Without Zhongli's shield, Wanderer is susceptible to interruption
- Wanderer cannot optimize Zhongli's shield if he is far from the enemy
- Wanderer may not utilize Bennett's ATK buff from Burst if he is floating a little higher above the AoE
Rotation Of Hypercarry Wanderer Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Zhongli's Elemental Skill for shield
2
Switch to Faruzan
3
Use Faruzan's Elemental Skill to gather lightweight enemies
4
Use Faruzan's Elemental Burst for Anemo RES debuff & Anemo DMG bonus
5
Switch to Bennett
6
Use Bennett's Elemental Skill to apply Pyro and produce some Pyro particles
7
Use Bennett's Elemental Burst for ATK buff
8
Switch to Wanderer
9
Use Wanderer's Elemental Skill and utilize it until the duration ends.
Ensure that Wanderer absorbs Pyro when using Skill to increase ATK by 30%. When Burst is used while his Skill is active, the ATK bonus is also applied to the Burst's damage output but it is better to
use his Burst just before the Skill duration ends because using Burst immediately ends the duration of Skill
.

Pyro Is A Must-Have
With Wanderer's Jade-Claimed Flower passive, absorbing Pyro can further increase Wanderer's ATK by 30%, and enabling Pyro resonance can provide an additional 25% which stacks with other multipliers.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Machine Gun Wanderer Team
Character
Detail
Fischl
At C6, Oz releases an additional projectile whenever Wanderer performs a normal ATK. With Thundering Fury x4, triggering Electro-related reactions reduces Skill's CD which is great for sustaining Oz's presence on the field.
Yelan
An excellent off-field Hydro DPS who is recommended with a DPS character that utilizes normal ATK. Also, Elegy for the End provides additional buffs that are beneficial for the team.
Zhongli
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' resistance and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
The objective of this team is to enable coordinated attacks with Wanderer. This team highlights the coordinated attacks in quick succession.
Pros & Cons Of Machine Gun Wanderer Team
Pros
- Jade-Claimed Flower passive is optimized since 2 elements can be absorbed
- Can inflict damage & trigger elemental reaction simultaneously thru Wanderer's normal ATK
Cons
- Yelan needs to have decent ER value for consistent Burst usage
- When affected by Cryo, Wanderer's normal ATK speed is slowed, which also affects the frequency & performance of coordinated attacks
- Difficult to utilize if you are facing an enemy with a debuff that can ignore shield such as corrosion
Rotation Of Machine Gun Wanderer Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Zhongli's Elemental Skill for shield
2
Switch to Yelan
3
Use Yelan's Elemental Skill to inflict Hydro DMG and produce Hydro particles
4
Use Yelan's Elemental Burst for the coordinated attack
5
Switch to Fischl
6
Use Fischl's Elemental Skill to inflict initial Electro DMG and summon Oz
7
Switch to Wanderer
8
Use Wanderer's Elemental Skill and utilize it until the duration ends
9
Repeat from step 1
From time to time, switch to Zhongli to refresh the shield before using Wanderer's Elemental Skill. In addition, you can summon Oz thru Fischl's Burst so you can use it in case her Skill is still in cooldown.

Synergy Of Freeze Wanderer Team
Character
Detail
Yelan
An excellent off-field Hydro DPS who can consistently apply Hydro for sustaining Freeze. Also, Elegy for the End provides additional buffs that are beneficial for the team.
Rosaria
She can inflict Cryo DMG and increase the CRIT Rate of the team with her Burst. Also, ATK buff from Noblesse Oblige x4 increases her & Wanderer's damage output.
Kokomi
A great healer & Hydro applicator for sustaining Freeze, and provider of ATK buff thru Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
The objective of this team is to sustain Freeze so Wanderer can attack freely. Since frozen enemies cannot move,  use crowd control & shield according to preference.
Can Increase CRIT Rate & Skill Duration Of Wanderer
The Jade-Claimed Flower passive can increase Wanderer's CRIT Rate if he absorbed Cryo and contacting with Hydro can prolong the duration of his Skill. Since he can trigger Cryo & Hydro simultaneously, he can activate both buffs.
Pros & Cons Of Freeze Wanderer Team
Pros
- Jade-Claimed Flower passive is optimized since 2 elements can be absorbed
- Can perform free hits to frozen enemies
Cons
- Yelan needs to have decent ER value for consistent Burst usage
- Not all enemies can be frozen like bosses and those with immunity to Cryo
- Removing Cryo shield could be hard
Rotation Of Freeze Wanderer Team

7
Switch to Wanderer
8

From time to time, switch to Rosaria and use her Skill to produce Cryo particles for her Burst.

Resonance Detail & Effect List
Resonance Detail & Effect List
Resonance
Detail
Effect
Anemo
- Decreases Stamina Consumption by 15%, increases Movement SPD by 10%, and shortens Skill CD by 5%
- Can produce elemental particles & execute rotations slightly faster due to reduced cooldown
Cryo
- Increases CRIT Rate against opponents that are Frozen or affected by Cryo by 15%
- Beneficial for DPS
- CRIT Rate bonus from resonance stacks with Wanderer's CRIT Rate bonus from absorbing Cryo & Rosaria's CRIT Rate buff from Burst
Hydro
- Increases Max HP by 25%
- Can boost the damage output of HP-scaling DPS such as Yelan
- Can improve the healing of HP-scaling healers such as Kokomi
Prioritize including 1 Cryo & Hydro that can keep the enemies frozen. For the elemental resonance, enable whichever you want according to your preference.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Explorer Wanderer Team
Character
Detail
Yelan
She is a must-have support to pair with any Anemo DPS character such as Wanderer. Her talent kit is tailored for boosting Anemo damage.
Dehya
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' resistance and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
Sayu
A good healer with a Skill that is convenient for exploring. Sayu can provide an ATK buff with Noblesse Oblige x4.
The objective of this team is to provide convenience when exploring the open world without sacrificing the team's damage capabilities.
Dehya Is Useful For Farming
With Dehya, breaking ores is easy. You can quickly harvest ores along the way which is great for stockpiling.
Pros & Cons Of Explorer Wanderer Team
Pros
- Nimble movement which lets you explore faster than usual
- Average damage output that can clear enemies in the open world
- Can break different ores for enhancing & crafting weapons
Cons
- Casual team that is mediocre in competitive content such as timed event challenge & Spiral Abyss
Rotation Of Explorer Wanderer Team
Sequence When Traversing A Long Distance
No.
Procedure

Use Wanderer's Elemental Skill for brief movement speed boost
↳ Can also be used to ascend
2
Switch to Yelan
3

Sacrificial Bow

Use Sayu's Elemental Skill for a brief speed boost
Wanderer's Skill has a secondary bar that acts as the duration and stamina of his Skill. Meanwhile, the Skill of Yelan & Sayu lets you automatically move forward swiftly for a fixed duration.
Sequence When In Combat
No.
Procedure
1
Use Dehya's Elemental Skill for Pyro DMG, coordinated attack, & resistance to interruption
2
Switch to Yelan
3
Use Yelan's Elemental Skill to trigger Vaporize and produce Hydro particles
4
Use Yelan's Elemental Burst for coordinated attack
5
Switch to Wanderer
6
Use Use Wanderer's Elemental Skill and utilize it until the duration ends.
While Dehya's AoE is active, Wanderer cannot be interrupted while midair, and Dehya also receives a portion of the damage received by Wanderer.
Dehya's Passive Talent For Movement Speed Bonus
When exploring during the daytime (6:00 - 18:00), your movement speed is increased by 10%. This stacks with Anemo resonance and the temporary speed boost from the Skill of Wanderer, Yelan, & Sayu.
Sayu's Burst For Healing & ATK Buff
Whether healing or ATK buff from Noblesse Oblige x4, you can use Sayu's Burst in combat according to your preference.

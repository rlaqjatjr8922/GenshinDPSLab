Best Weapons for Zhongli in Genshin Impact
Staff of Homa (Image via HoYoverse)
Zhongli is a rare unit where his signature weapon, Vortex Vanquisher, is not the best choice for him. This is because the weapon buffs his offensive stats and fails even in that. Instead, Zhongli is great at being a shielder instead. Thus, that weapon is useless for him. So, you can use the following weapons for Zhongli.

Best Teams for Pyro Traveler
Pyro Traveler can Be Used as Sub DPS and On-field Reaction Driver
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Reaction Team with sub DPS Pyro Traveler
- Hydro or Cryo character is used as on-field DPS
- Pyro Traveler can contribute to the team's damage with their off-field Pyro damage
- Pyro Traveler holds Cinder City set and can buff Elemental DMG Bonus of any element that reacts with their Skill's off-field damage
2nd
▼ Burning Team
- Pyro Traveler can use Cinder City set to buff both Pyro and Dendro damage
- Kinich and Emilie will be the main damage dealers
- Bennett can buff the damage of the team with his Burst
3rd
▼ Main DPS Pyro Traveler
- After C6, the Traveler can infuse their Attacks with Pyro
- Obsidian Codex can be used to increase their own damage
- Cinder City set can be used to buff the off-field damage dealers
4th
▼ Burgeon Team
- Pyro Traveler's Skill can be used to trigger Burgeon as sub DPS
- Pyro Traveler with Cinder City set can potentially buff the Elemental DMG of all teammates
5th
▼ Overloaded Team
- Pyro Traveler's consistent sub DPS can be used for Overloaded
- Chevreuse's buff can be activated much easier
Pyro Traveler can deal both on-field and off-field Pyro damage. With C6, their attacks are Pyro infused after using Burst. Since their Skill can also deal Pyro damage, it can be used for off-field and additional on-field Pyro application.

Pyro Traveler's Skill can be used for Vaporize or Melt teams. Mualani can Vaporize on each Sharky's Bite, allowing for her damage to be increased greatly. Both Kazuha and Bennett can increase the team's damage with their buffs and debuffs.
Rotation of Reaction Team with sub DPS Pyro Traveler
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mualani and use a Normal Attack to apply Hydro
2
Switch to Kazuha and use his Skill to Swirl Hydro
3
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Pyro Traveler and use their Burst then tap Skill
5
Switch back to Mualani and use her Skill
6
Use her Normal Attack when she obtains 3 stacks of Marked as Prey
7
Use Mualani's Burst to finish her turn and deal massive damage
8
Repeat from 1
After Pyro Traveler's turn, you can switch to Kazuha and use his Burst to Swirl Pyro if needing more Pyro application

Pyro Traveler replaces Xiangling in Kinich's Burning team. With their ability to hold Cinder City and boost the damage of both Dendro and Pyro, Pyro Traveler can be both a sub DPS and support for Burning teams.
Rotation of Burning Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Emilie and use her Burst or Skill (if her Burst is not ready)
2
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Pyro Traveler and use their Burst then tap Skill
4
Switch to Kinich and use his Skill then use his Normal Attacks to deal damage
5
Use Kinich's Skill when his Nightsoul Gauge is full, otherwise continue using Normal Attacks
6
Use Kinich's Burst to finish his turn
7
Repeat from 1
Burning Team rotations are lax and either Pyro or Dendro can be applied first to trigger Burning. Kinich only needs to go last for his attacking turn.

Baizhu is one of the best Burgeon's Dendro on-field drivers due to his Shield and innate Dendro infusion in his attacks. Yelan and Furina are the Hydro sub DPS who can ensure Burning will immediately be stopped if it happens. The whole team can buff the damage dealt with their abilities and/or artifacts.
Rotation of Burgeon Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Yelan and use her Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Pyro Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Baizhu and use his Burst then Skill
5
Use Normal Attacks to apply Dendro and trigger follow up attacks from the sub DPSes
6
Repeat from 1 after Pyro Traveler's Skill expires
To buff Hydro DMG Bonus using Cinder City set, Pyro Traveler has to trigger Vaporize. Getting the right timing for this may be tricky but the Hydro damage from Yelan and Furina is fine without it.

Due to Chevreuse's requirement of only Pyro and Electro characters for her buff, Anemo characters with VV cannot be used to further increase damage. Pyro Traveler can hold Cinder City and buff both Electro and Pyro damage at once.
Rotation of Overloaded Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Fischl and use her Skill
2
Switch to Pyro Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Chevreuse and use her hold Skill to buff the ATK of the team
4
Switch to Clorinde and use her Burst then Skill twice
5
Use Normal Attacks to deal damage and gain Bond of Life
6
Use Clorinde's Skill to do a plunge attack once Bond of Life is at 100% of her Max HP
7
Repeat from 1 once buffs and summons expire
You can use Fischl's Burst to refresh her summon's duration

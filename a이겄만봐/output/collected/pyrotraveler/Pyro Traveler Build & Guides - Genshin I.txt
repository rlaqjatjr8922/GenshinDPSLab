Pyro Traveler's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Simple and easy to play.
C6 grants a Pyro Infusion making reactions much easier.
Able to hold Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City to buff the team.
Weaknesses
High Energy Recharge requirements.
Constellations locked behind Natlan reputation meaning they take months of weeklies to unlock.
Low damage as a result of poor scaling and low Pyro application.
1.
Pyro Traveler Build Guide, Best Artifacts, Weapons and Talent Priority
The Pyro Traveler has 2 different available builds. The first being as a 
general support and providing team utility and the second being as a 
source of Pyro for elemental reactions in mainly Overload and Burgeon teams.

5.
Combos and How to Play Pyro Traveler
The Pyro Traveler as a support is incredibly easy to play.
Flowfire Blade
is your Elemental Skill and how you apply
Pyro
while off-field.
This skill has 2 variations that both generate the same amount of nightsoul (42):
Tap - Periodically deals
Pyro
to nearby enemies.
Hold - Deals
Pyro
in the form of Coordinated Attacks once every 3s.
Both drain nightsoul over time, whats best can depend but most of the
time
Hold
will be your best option outside of
Burgeon
.
Outside of that, all you need to worry about is your Elemental Burst
Plains Scorcher
which deals AoE
Pyro
DMG but more importantly restores nightsoul. Because of that, you want to burst
every rotation alongside your skill before swapping characters.

Pyro

    that follows the current active character.
When the current active character within a Scorching Threshold deals
    DMG to opponents, the Threshold will launch a Coordinated Attack that
    deals Nightsoul-aligned Pyro DMG based on the Traveler's ATK. This effect can occur once every 3s.
Nightsoul's Blessing: Traveler
Continuously consumes Nightsoul points. The Traveler's Nightsoul's
    Blessing state lasts 12s at maximum, and when their Nightsoul points are
    exhausted, or if the skill is used again, this state will end.
Blazing and Scorching Thresholds will disappear once the Traveler's
    Nightsoul's Blessing state ends, and only 1 Blazing or Scorching Threshold
    summoned by the Traveler themselves can exist at any one time.
Elemental Burst
Plains Scorcher
Condense the flames into a mark, dealing Nightsoul-aligned AoE Pyro DMG to opponents up ahead. Within the next 4s, the Traveler restores 7 Nightsoul points per second.

7.3.
Pyro Traveler's Constellations
Pyro Traveler's Constellations work differently to other characters. Firstly,
the Traveler has a set of constellations from the Archon Quest to be used
exclusively on a weekly boss battle and have no benefits in
normal combat. Secondly, Pyro Traveler's Constellations are obtained with
Blazing Flint Ore
's from Trial Reputation in Natlan. This 
means if you are just starting now, it will take a while before you can 
unlock these constellations.

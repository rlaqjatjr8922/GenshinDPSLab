Heizou is Best Used as Swirl On-Field Reaction Driver
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Heizou National
- Greatly increases the personal DMG of Heizou's skills.
- With
Viridescent Venerer
, he can increase the damage of the sub-DPSes
2nd
▼ Electro-Charged Heizou
- Heizou can decrease the resistance of enemies to Hydro and Electro.
- Easily allows Heizou to trigger swirl for his Elemental Skill and Burst.
3rd
▼ Hypercarry Heizou
- Focuses on using Heizou as the main DPS instead of as a Swirl character
Heizou is a melee Anemo DPS that can easily trigger Swirl reactions with his rapid Normal Attacks that is always infused with Anemo, given his status as a Catalyst user. He is best used for being a reaction driver or to increase the damage of the off-field DPS with Viridescent Venerer. He can also contribute some damage with his Burst's multiplier.

National team variation with Heizou as on-field driver. With Heizou Swirling Pyro and Hydro, he will be able to boost the damage of Furina and Xiangling's off-field damage. Bennett will also buff the damage dealt by the team with his Burst.
Rotation Of National Team With Heizou
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Xiangling and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Heizou and use Normal Attacks until gain 5 stacks and use Skill then Burst
5
Repeat from 1
The goal is to release all off-field so Heizou can swirl multiple Elements. To maximize the DMG of Xiangling, make sure to use her Elemental Burst while in the area of Bennett's Burst.

Heizou will be the on-field driver for Electro-Charged. With Furina's DMG buff, the damage dealt will be increased. Ororon can help in giving energy to Heizou so he can use his Burst more, dealing more damage overall.
Rotation Of Electro-Charged Heizou Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Kuki Shinobu and use her Skill
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Heizou and deal normal attacks and finish with Elemental Skill then Burst.
5
Repeat from 1
Since Electro-Charged can be triggered no matter whether Hydro or Electro is applied first, you don't need to follow the rotations for Furina and Shinobu strictly. Ororon must go before Heizou for him to be able to give energy to Heizou and Heizou must always be the last to go in field after all buffs and off-field damage sources are cast.

Hypercarry Heizou focuses on increases Heizou's personal damage by using Faruzan as Anemo support, Zhongli and Furina as damage support. Furina's off-field damage will allow Heizou to Swirl and gain stacks to deal bigger damage with his Skill.
Rotation Of Hypercarry Heizou
No.
Procedure
1
Start by using Zhongli's Shield (Hold Skill).
2
Switch to Furina and use Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Faruzan, use Elemental Skill then Charged attack.
4
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst then Skill
5
Switch to Heizou and deal damage using Normal Attacks until his stacks are maxed then use Skill and Burst
6
After buffs expire, repeat from 1
Substitute Characters
Character
Sub
Details
Faruzan
Jean (C4)
Bennett
- The next best Anemo support that can replace Faruzan is Jean with her 4th Constellation. However it is not recommended to pull for her constellations as she is a standard character.
- Bennett can buff ATK greatly as long as Heizou is in his Burst range
Zhongli
Diona
Layla
Thoma
Beidou
- Since Zhongli's universal RES shred cannot be found elsewhere, if needing a shield support, any of the above can do.
- They can also be another source of element that Heizou can Swirl so he can get stacks faster
Furina
Xingqiu
Yelan
- Xingqiu can provide more damage mitigation as well as Hydro off-field damage.
- Yelan can buff the damage of the active character as long as her Burst is active. She can also do Hydro off-field damage.
A general rule of thumb in team compositions is at least two (2) characters for buffing the DPS and having one sustain support. As such, Hypercarry team for Heizou can be easy to substitute characters with. Make sure to have characters with off-field damage that he can Swirl so he can get use his Skill more in each rotation.

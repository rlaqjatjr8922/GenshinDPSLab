Best Weapons for Xiangling in Genshin Impact
Engulfing Lightning (Image via HoYoverse)
There is a running joke in the community regarding Xiangling and her affinity for every new five-star polearm. Whenever a new polearm is released, it is, more often than not, usable on Xiangling. This has led to an amazing roster of weapons that are all great for Xiangling.

Barbara is Best Used as a Healer
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- Raiden Shogun's short on-field time can make way for more flexible rotations
- Barbara's healing will be able to offset Furina's HP drain and keep the team above 50% HP
- Charged Attacks by Raiden Shogun will have reduced Stamina consumption with Barbara's passive effect
2nd
▼ Electro-Charged Team
- Barbara's healing can mitigate the damage caused by Electro-Charged
- Ayato can get increased HP from Hydro resonance and if Barbara is C2, she can buff his Hydro damage as well
3rd
▼ Superbloom Team
- Barbara will be the reaction driver for Superbloom
- Barbara's Skill is able to generate Dendro cores rapidly as long as it's in contact with enemies
4th
▼ Swirl DPS with support Barbara
- Two Anemo DPS can benefit from Barbara's healing and Hydro self-application
- Chasca is able to get healing as well as Hydro Bullets for her Charged Attacks
- Wanderer can get the increased point cap for his Windfavored state
Barbara is a Hydro support that is good at healing. She can be a reaction driver but if using her as Hydro sub DPS for her Skill, it is not recommended due to its low application.

Raiden Shogun's build is for increasing her EM for the reaction and giving her personal damage a boost as well. Furina will be the Hydro sub DPS and Barbara can offset her HP drain to ensure her summons' damage are increased.
Rotation of Hyperbloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Nahida and use her Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Barbara and use her Skill. To trigger Sacrificial Fragments' effect, you have to get closer to the enemy
4
Switch to Raiden Shogun and use her Skill then Burst
5
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage
6
Repeat from 1 once Raiden Shogun's Burst expires
Remember to use Barbara's Burst for healing to keep the team's HP above 50%! It can be used for healing any time as well.

Chasca is one of the few Swirl DPS that can have Barbara as a Hydro support. Since she does not need a Hydro sub DPS strictly, Barbara's healing can be used. She can also hold Thrilling Tales to buff Chasca's damage.
Rotation of Swirl DPS with support Barbara
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Barbara and use her Skill
4
Switch to Chasca and use her Skill
5
Use Charged Attack to load Bullets
6
After hitting enemies 4 times with her Charged Attack, use Burst
7
Repeat from 1
Use Barbara's Burst if you need instant healing. Chasca's Burst can be used early if wanting to use it while Thrilling Tales buff is active. But since Chasca's main damage is from her Charged Attacks, it is okay to use only her Charged Attacks until the buff expires.

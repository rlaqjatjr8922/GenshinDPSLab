What to Expect on Your Northern Lights Hunt
Departure:
21:00 with free hotel pickup by Mývatn.
Our Northern Lights tour preperation begins with our guides studdying the forecast during the day.
Based on real-time aurora activity and cloud cover data, we’ll drive to the optimal viewing location—often changing our route mid-tour to chase clear skies and strong aurora displays.
Did you know?
When you stay in the Mývatn area, you can often see the Northern Lights from your hotel window! However, by joining our tour, we take you to carefully selected locations with:
Zero or low light pollution
Dramatic foreground landscapes (waterfalls, lava fields, mountains)
Perfect photography compositions
Expert commentary about the science and folklore of the aurora
We’ll spend 2-3 hours hunting the lights, moving between locations if needed. Our guides will help you set up your camera, explain the aurora’s colors and movements, and share stories of Icelandic folklore surrounding the Northern Lights.

Important Information – Please Read Before Booking
Weather & Aurora Activity:
We cannot guarantee Northern Lights sightings as they are a natural phenomenon dependent on solar activity and weather conditions. However, our local expertise gives you the best possible chance. If we determine the probability is too low, we may cancel the tour—even on short notice—and offer a full refund or reschedule.
Success Rate:
During optimal season (October-March), our tours have an 80% success rate for aurora sightings.

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What are my chances of seeing the Northern Lights?
A: During peak season (October-March) with favorable forecasts, success rate is ~80%. We only run tours when conditions are promising.
Q: Can I see the Northern Lights from Mývatn without a tour?
A: Yes! Mývatn has minimal light pollution. However, our tours take you to optimal locations with better compositions for photography and our guides know where to go based on real-time
conditions.
Q: What if the weather changes?
A: We monitor forecasts continuously and may cancel up to departure time. You’ll receive full refund or reschedule option.
Q: How cold is it?
A: Winter temperatures range from +5°C to -25°C  Dress in layers!
Q: Can children join?
A: Yes, but tours return around 00:00 AM. Consider if your children can handle late nights and cold weather.
Q: Do you provide winter clothing?
A: No, Proper gear is essential for comfort.

Escoffier is a 5-star Cryo Polearm user introduced in Genshin Impact Version 5.6. As a master chef from Fontaine, she brings more than just culinary prowess—she revolutionizes Freeze teams with powerful Cryo application, team healing, and resistance shred mechanics. Whether you’re building her for off-field support or damage amplification, this guide covers everything you need to make Escoffier shine.
Escoffier Genshin Impact Guide: Best Builds, Weapons, Artifacts & Freeze Teams
Why Use Escoffier?
Escoffier consolidates the roles of a sub-DPS, healer, and buffer into one. Her unique talents make her a must-have for Freeze-based teams, especially when paired with characters like Furina, Ayaka, or Ganyu. She also scales well with ATK and CRIT stats, and her Burst allows her to heal allies while enabling key passive buffs.

Talent Priorities and Playstyle
Escoffier’s skills are designed to function off-field, applying Cryo and supporting allies. Her turret can apply Cryo for up to 20 seconds, making her rotations efficient and allowing her to burst and swap.
Talent Priority:
Elemental Skill – Low-Temperature Cooking
Summons a Cryo turret that follows the active character, dealing Cryo DMG periodically. Central to her damage output and Cryo uptime.
Elemental Burst – Scoring Cuts
Deals AoE Cryo DMG and heals all party members based on her ATK. Essential for healing-focused or Furina-based comps.
Normal Attack – Kitchen Skills
Low priority; not used in combat rotations.
Passive Talents:
Rehab Diet
: Heals over time after using Burst. Key for Furina synergy.
Inspiration-Immersed Seasoning
: Shreds Cryo/Hydro RES depending on how many Cryo/Hydro characters are in your team (up to 55%).
Off-the-Cuff Cookery
: Niche food-crafting ability. Minor utility outside combat.

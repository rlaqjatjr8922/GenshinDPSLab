Best Genshin Impact Escoffier Build: Weapons, Artifacts, and Team Comps
Summary
Escoffier excels in Freeze-based teams as a Cryo support and off-field damage dealer.
Best weapons include Symphonist of Scents and Calamity Queller.
Golden Troupe or Blizzard Strayer artifacts maximize her output.
Ideal team comps include Ganyu, Shenhe, and Furina.
Who is Escoffier and What Makes Her Unique?
Escoffier is a 5-star Cryo Polearm user from Fontaine who brings a unique mix of off-field Cryo application, AoE Elemental Skill damage, and team-wide healing. What sets Escoffier apart is her ability to both damage enemies consistently while keeping allies sustained, allowing more aggressive team builds without sacrificing survivability.
Best Weapons for Escoffier Build
Symphonist of Scents
(5★): Escoffier’s signature weapon enhances CRIT DMG and boosts team performance through healing-based effects. It’s tailor-made for her kit and the top recommendation.
Calamity Queller
(5★): Boosts Elemental DMG and fits well with Escoffier’s Skill-centric gameplay. Offers great ATK scaling for consistent output.
Staff of Homa
(5★): Though not optimal, it offers CRIT DMG and HP scaling—useful for builds focused on healing.
Favonius Lance
(4★): Good F2P option that solves Energy Recharge needs and helps generate team particles.
Deathmatch
(4★): Solid CRIT Rate stats, making it suitable for balanced DPS and healing roles.
Best Artifacts for Escoffier
Golden Troupe (4-piece)
: Grants up to 50% bonus Elemental Skill DMG when Escoffier is off-field. This set works perfectly with his core playstyle of supporting from the background.
Blizzard Strayer (4-piece)
: Boosts CRIT Rate against Cryo/Frozen targets—great if you’re running her in Freeze teams with consistent Hydro application.
Main Stats:

Best Escoffier Team Compositions
Freeze Team:
Ganyu (Main DPS), Escoffier (Sub-DPS/Healer), Shenhe (Cryo Support), Furina (Hydro Applier/Healer). This setup keeps enemies frozen consistently while Escoffier adds healing and AoE damage.
Alternative:
Wriothesley + Escoffier + Yelan + Furina for high single-target damage and flexible Cryo-Hydro synergy.
Hypercarry Comp:
Neuvillette + Escoffier + Kazuha + Furina focuses on empowering Neuvillette while Escoffier adds elemental support and healing.

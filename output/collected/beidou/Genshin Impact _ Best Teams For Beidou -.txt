Beidou Is A Good Electro Sub DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Catalyze Team
- Beidou's off-field Electro damage can be used to trigger Catalyze for Quicken or Aggravate reactions.
- Dendro or Electro DPS can be used in this team.
2nd
▼ Taser Team
- A consistent and easy team to play.
- Ororon's off-field Electro Damage can cover for gaps between Beidou's Burst hits.
- Electro Resonance and Ororon's Energy generation can charge Beidou's Burst faster.
3rd
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- With Raiden Shogun triggering Hyperbloom, Beidou can trigger Catalyze reactions with Nahida.
- Yelan can do off-field Hydro DMG and increase the active character's damage.
- Raiden Shogun can battery Beidou's Burst.
Beidou is a decent Electro sub DPS with good Elemental application. Electro Resonance and other Electro characters are often used with her to charge her Burst faster.

Rotation Of Catalyze Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Clorinde and use her Burst to apply Electro on enemies
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with Normal Attacks
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Nahida and use her Burst then Skill to mark enemies
5
Switch to Beidou and use her Skill then Burst
6
Switch back to Clorinde and use her Skill twice
7
Deal damage to enemies with Normal Attacks and use her Skill when her Bond of Life reaches Max HP
8
Repeat from 1

Beidou and Ororon will apply Electro off-field. Kokomi can heal the team and apply Hydro using her Skill. Ayato is the on-field reaction driver and DPS through his Normal Attacks after using his Skill.
Rotation Of Taser Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Ayato and use his Burst
2
Switch to Ororon and use his Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Beidou and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Kokomi and use her Skill
5
Switch back to Ayato and use his Skill
6
Apply Hydro using Ayato's Normal Attacks and trigger Beidou's Burst follow-up attack
7
Repeat from 1
Using Ayato's Burst to apply Hydro at the start can allow Electro-Charged to be happening while the rotation is ongoing. This rotation doesn't have to be followed strictly as you only need Electro and Hydro to be applied in any order.

Alhaitham Team Overview
This Page provides the best Alhaitham Team information. Alhaitham is a DPS in most of the situation.
From SS tier to A tier, Alhaitham best team can be formed in different ways including Catalyze, Bloom, Spread, Aggravate, Burgeon and Hyperbloom team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

Alhaitham's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Alhaitham team composition guide and character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we will explore the best teams to build for Alhaitham, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and Team Compositions to help Alhaitham 
reach his full damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Alhaitham
Alhaitham is a Main DPS that will mainly deal
Dendro DMG
and reactions such as
Hyperbloom
Bloom
or
Spread
. This means that Alhaitham's teams 
revolve around teammates that will allow him to enable these reactions while he does
massive
Dendro DMG
with his infusion and projection attacks.
Alhaitham can require a lot of Energy Recharge, so using another
Dendro
character can help with that if you are low on ER. Otherwise,
Electro
and
Hydro
characters are perfect teammates for Alhaitham
as they will allow him to trigger reactions.
Nahida
Nahida
Nahida is a great battery for Alhaitham, on top of dealing quite good off-field damage 
	and providing some buffs. She also works in multiple team compositions, such as 
	Hyperbloom, Bloom, or Spread for example.
Lauma
Lauma
Lauma is the second best support for Alhaitham. She offers a good
Dendro
application,
	and she can enable the new Lunar-Bloom reaction.
Furina
Furina
Furina is one of the best teammates for Alhaitham. When using Furina, you can
	equip Alhaitham with the
Marechaussee Hunter
Artifact set. Furina
	provides a lot of off-field DMG alongside buffs. Keep in mind that Furina 
  needs a healer to generate Fanfare stacks for her DMG buff, but also as she manipulates your HP, 
  Alhaitham can become very fragile.
Ineffa
Ineffa
Ineffa is a really good shielder and source of
Electro
for characters like Alhaitham. On top of that she also provides Elemental Mastery buffs to 
	herself and the active party member, which makes her a great addition for Alhaitham.
Nilou
Nilou
Alhaitham is a great on-field DPS for
Nilou
bloom teams,
	Nilou exclusively can only have
Dendro
and
Hydro
characters in their party to gain the full effects of Nilou's Bountiful cores.
Yelan
Kuki Shinobu
Baizhu
Alternatives
Xingqiu
and
Yelan
provide good Hydro application
	while
Raiden Shogun
or
Kuki Shinobu
apply Electro, allowing Alhaitham
	to create Hyperbloom reactions.
Baizhu
provides good team-wide healing and a Shield, 
	especially good if you play with
Furina
.

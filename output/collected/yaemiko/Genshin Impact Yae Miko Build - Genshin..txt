Elemental Skill
Yakan Evocation: Sesshou Sakura
Moves swiftly, leaving a Sesshou Sakura behind. Sesshou Sakura Periodically strikes one nearby opponent with lightning, dealing
Electro
DMG. When there are other Sesshou Sakura nearby, their level will increase, boosting the DMG dealt by these lightning strikes. This skill has three charges. A maximum of 3 Sesshou Sakura can exist simultaneously. The initial level of each Sesshou Sakura is 1, and the initial highest level each sakura can reach is 3. If a new Sesshou Sakura is created too close to an existing one, the existing one will be destroyed.

Summons a lightning strike, dealing AoE

Electro

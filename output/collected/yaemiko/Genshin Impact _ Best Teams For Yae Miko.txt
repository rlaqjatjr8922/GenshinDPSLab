Synergy Details
Synergy Of Aggravate Yae Miko Team
Character
Detail
Nahida
She is a great Dendro applicator who can also provide EM buff thru Burst. Dendro resonance is enabled by her & Yaoyao.
Sucrose
She can provide EM buff & shred the enemies' resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 while providing decent crowd control. When C6 is unlocked, elemental damage bonus is also provided thru Burst.
Yaoyao
Her Skill can either heal when active character has low HP or attack the enemy if the active character does not need healing. With Tenacity of the Millelith x4, she can provide ATK buff when Yuegui hits an enemy.
The objective of this team is to funnel all buff to Yae Miko for inflicting powerful damage.
Nahida's Artifacts Can Be Swapped
If one of your supports, like Yaoyao, is already using Deepwood Memories x4, you can use
Gilded Dreams
x4 on Nahida instead to maximize her potential.
Pros & Cons Of Aggravate Yae Miko Team
Pros
- Yae Miko's turrets can deal powerful damage per tick when the enemy is affected by Dendro & when EM buffs are active
Cons
- Yae Miko's raw damage is not high
- Reliant to elemental reaction with Dendro
Rotation Of Aggravate Yae Miko Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Burst for EM buff
2
Use Nahida's Skill for mark
3
Switch to Yae Miko
4
Use Yae Miko's Skill thrice to deploy 3 turrets
5
Switch to Sucrose
6
Use Sucrose's Elemental Skill for crowd control
7
Use Sucrose's Elemental Burst for crowd control & EM buff
※ With C6, Electro DMG bonus is given if Electro gets absorbed
8
Switch to Yaoyao
9
Use Yaoyao's Elemental Skill for healing or Dendro application
10
Return to step 1
You can use Yaoyao's Burst if you need to restore the HP of all teammates quickly.
Let Yae Miko Get The Seed
When a seed from Yaoyao's Moonpiercer is produced, switch to Yae Miko and get it for ATK buff.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Electro DPS Yae Miko Team
Character
Detail
Tighnari
He can optimize Yae Miko's turrets and the bonus EM from Nahida's Burst & Dendro resonance.
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of elemental reactions & raw damage of Yae Miko's turrets.
Kokomi
An excellent healer who can consistently apply Hydro. With Thrilling Tales & Tenacity artifact, she can also provide an ATK buff that can benefit both Yae Miko.
The objective of this team is to sustain the presence of all of Yae Miko's turrets for periodic Electro DPS which is helpful for triggering Electro-related reactions.
Use Hakushin Ring For Buffer Yae
If you do not have Kagura's Verity, the
Hakushin Ring
is a great substitute as it
can provide an Elemental DMG bonus
for Yae Miko and the character's element that reacted with Electro.
Pros & Cons Of Electro DPS Yae Miko Team
Pros
- Yae Miko's turret stays on the field even after she gets switched out
- Bonus EM & ATK buffs are both beneficial for Yae Miko
- Tighnari can taunt enemies with his Skill
- Aside from quicken & aggravate, bloom & hyperbloom can be triggered thru Kokomi's Hydro application
Cons
- Yae Miko needs ample time from time to time to set up all 3 turrets
- The 3 turrets should be connected altogether for maximum damage
- Yae Miko's Burst cost is high so it is often used
Rotation Of Electro DPS Yae Miko Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Burst for EM buff
2
Use Nahida's Skill for mark
3
Switch to Yae Miko
4

5
Switch to Kokomi
6
Use Kokomi's Elemental Skill for heal & Hydro application
7
Switch to Tighnari
8
Use Tighnari's Elemental Skill for taunt & empowered CA
9
Use Tighnari's Elemental Burst to consume energy
10
Use Tighnari's Wreath Arrow thru CA x3
11
Return to step 1
Occasionally, you can use Yae Miko's Skill to reposition or sustain the turrets. Also, you can freely use the burst of Yae Miko & Tighnari whenever available.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Mono Electro Yae Miko Team
Character
Detail
Kujou Sara
She is a great support buffer for Electro, especially with her C6. Also, Electro resonance is helpful for her & Yae Miko.
Kazuha
He can shred the enemies' resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 while providing decent crowd control, and an elemental damage bonus with his Burst.
Zhongli
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' resistance and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
The objective of this team is to funnel all buff to Yae Miko for inflicting powerful damage.
Use Elegy For Kujou Sara If Kazuha Does Not Use Freedom-Sworn
If you do not have Kazuha or your Kazuha does not use Freedom-Sworn, you can let Kujou Sara use
Elegy for the End
for additional ATK & EM buff. It can also give Kujou Sara additional ER.
Pros & Cons Of Mono Electro Yae Miko Team
Pros
- Yae Miko's Burst can deal heavy damage when all buffs are active
- Enemies can be compressed with Kazuha's Skill and nuked with Yae's Burst
Cons
- Yae Miko's Burst cost is high and takes time to fill even with Electro resonance
- The 3 turrets should be connected altogether for maximum damage
- Yae Miko & Kujou Sara needs to have decent ER for Burst & ATK buff
Rotation Of Mono Electro Yae Miko Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Zhongli's Skill for shield
2
Switch to Yae Miko
3
Use Yae Miko's Skill thrice to deploy 3 turrets
4
Switch to Kazuha
5
Use Kazuha's Elemental Skill for crowd control
6
Use Kazuha's Elemental Burst for elemental DMG bonus
7
Switch to Kujou Sara
8
Use Kujou Sara's Elemental Skill for producing Electro particles
※ Crowfeather buff can be produced at C2
9
Use Kujou Sara's Elemental Burst for ATK buff
10
Switch to Yae Miko
11
Use Yae Miko's Elemental Burst for powerful damage
12
Return to step 1
You can use Zhongli's Burst if you need to temporarily prevent the enemy from moving so all ticks from Yae Miko's Burst can successfully hit.

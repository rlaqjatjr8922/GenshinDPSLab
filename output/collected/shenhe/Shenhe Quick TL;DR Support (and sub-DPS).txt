PSA #1: A Support is the group mate that quietly does their part of the work, but finishes quickly so they also help you out on yours. Keep your Supports alive and happy if you want some help!
PSA #2: Both Shenhe's abilities scale of ATK, and if you want her to become that homicidal, killing machine (like in the lore), then make sure you give her lots ATK. Also... Mommy? Sorry. Mommy? 😳
Shenhe can either Tap or Hold E to do different attacks. Provides Icy Quill stacks that boost Cryo character's damage!
Shenhe's E has a Tap and Hold variant:

Whether you hit or miss an enemy, it will provide an Icy Quill buff
that provides additional flat DMG to
any attack that deal Cryo DMG!
This damage is scaled off Shenhe's current ATK
So lets say your Shenhe has
2,500 ATK
-- that means a
level 8 E will provide 1,825 DMG
on top of any Cryo dealing attack.
E.g.: So if your Ganyu's Charged Attack did something like
8,000/14,000
Bloom DMG
, then it would do
9,825/15,825 Bloom DMG
instead (these are without CRIT values -- with the CRIT values in formula, it will actually do a lot more overall DMG).
But these stacks aren't shared between characters
-- each character will receive their own set of stacks once Shenhe uses E
2 IMPORTANT TIPS:
Her E doesn't snapshot
. So if your Icy Quill was cast at 2,500 ATK, but it drops down to 2,000 ATK while the stacks are still active, the DMG bonus will also drop. But also vice versa -- if she gets buffed, then the currently active Icy Quills will also be buffed!
Icy Quill stacks will proc on AoE.
Meaning if you hit had 7 Icy Quill stacks, and Ganyu's Charged Attack hit 5 enemies, you now only have 2 stacks left!
Tapping or Holding E will provide specific buffs to your team!
You will need to level Shenhe to at least level 70 to get this A4 talent!
This is what allows her to work outside of only supporting Cryo DPS characters.

15% DMG buff to

for 10s

If you have C1 (Constellation 1) Shenhe, then you can stack both the Tap and Hold buffs
(BUT NOT the same buff -- so Tapping E twice doesn't mean you'll get a 30% buff!)
Shenhe summons her Cryo Avatar and does a massive AoE Cryo DMG attack. The attack will continuously pulse doing Cyro DMG to enemies inside the AoE. Debuffs enemies!
The main selling point of her Q is that it
reduces enemy Cryo and Phys RES by a set %

Her Q will also
pulse a total of 12 times
(2x every 2 seconds) dealing Cryo DMG to enemies inside the AoE.
This goes without saying, but
heavily recommended that you use her Q before you use her E to maximize the Cryo DMG.

BUT IF YOU'RE C1

Basically her Q also provides +15% Cryo DMG when inside her AoE

WEAPON #1 (Recommended for best Supporting): Calamity Queller
How do I get it D: ? - It's time to roll boys (gacha)
Has base ATK% scaling and gives Shenhe a bunch of ATK bonuses to boost her buffing!
Calamity Queller
is her signature weapon and her best in slot for one single reason: it bolsters her ATK like mad. At level 90/90 and R1, it gives
741 base ATK + 35.7% ATK + 12% Elemental DMG
. Not mention since her playstyle is similar to a quickswap character,
she'll have the double Consummation bonus
-- meaning she'll actually get
38.4% ATK buff from the weapon passive alone
. It's incredibly good for her E's Icy Quill buffs, and practically overkill.
Engulfing Lightning and Skyward Spine
are both close contenders, with
Engulfing exceeding Skyward if you have over 165%-170% ER
on Shenhe. However, running Engulfing would mean you would have to optimize your substats to qualify for the ER% needed to gain the ATK boost (running an ER% Sands also means you would then have to makeup for the lost ATK%!)
Staff of Homa
isn't bad, but it requires to keep your Shenhe below 50% HP to really benefit from the ATK% buff, and
Primordial Jade-Winged Spear
is also great, but requires your Shenhe to be on field for longer to get the ATK% stacks (which wastes Icy Quill duration). However, PJWS is good if you plan on running her main DPS!
Vortex is good if you're running a shielder -- otherwise don't bother
.

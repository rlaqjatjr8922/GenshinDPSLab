Shenhe is a 5-star Cryo character that wields a Polearm in Genshin Impact. Her gameplay focuses on being a powerful Cryo support, enhancing Cryo damage and reducing enemy resistances for both Cryo and Physical teams. Her Elemental Skill, "Spring Spirit Summoning," grants nearby allies the "Icy Quill" effect, boosting their Cryo damage based on Shenhe's ATK. Her Elemental Burst, "Divine Maiden's Deliverance," summons a "Talisman Spirit" that deals continuous AoE Cryo damage and reduces Cryo and Physical RES of enemies within its field. Lore-wise, Shenhe is a reclusive adeptus disciple raised by Cloud Retainer, known for her immense strength and cold aura, both results of her sealed mortal emotions and deep connection to the adepti arts.

Roles & Playstyle Of Shenhe and Its Strengths and Weakness
Cryo DPS Support Shenhe
Shenhe's intended role is to be a damage boosting support for Cryo On-Fielders. Her ability to buff the damage dealt by Damaging Skills and Cryo damage makes her a good support for Cryo damage dealers. She can also apply Cryo herself with her Elemental Burst to help in Freezing enemies.
Pros and Cons of Support Shenhe
Pros
- Icy Quill's Cryo damage increase buff scales on Shenhe's current ATK, making it great to build ATK on her to increase it.
- Shenhe's other buffs don't rely on her Skill levels or stats aside from the RES shred debuff.
Cons
- Elemental Burst reliant and has a high Energy cost of 80. Energy Recharge is the 2nd priority stat for her.
- Can be hard to recover from using the wrong Elemental Skill version since her Skill goes on cooldown immediately.
- Shenhe's Elemental Burst is static and can be placed wrongly if you aren't used to it yet.

Basic Team Rotation for Shenhe
Basic Rotation of Shenhe
1. Use Elemental Skill. Tap or held depends on which damage your DPS deals more.
2. Use Elemental Burst.
3. Switch to your On-Field DPS and execute their damage combo.
Shenhe has a short buff uptime for her Icy Quill and one of the Skill version's buff
. But if you use Thrilling Tales to buff ATK for the DPS, make sure to swap them in before the damage dealer. Viridescent Venerer or Scroll set holders can make use of the Cryo that Shenhe applies during her rotation for boosting the Cryo damage of the DPS.
Basics Of Using Shenhe
Support for Cryo On-Field Damage Dealers
Shenhe's whole kit is centered on buffing Cryo damage dealers. Her Elemental Skill, Burst, and Passives are support abilities that increase the damage dealt by Cryo characters using different kinds of buffs.
Use Elemental Skill to Buff Cryo Damage and Skill Damage
Shenhe's Elemental Skill grants allies with Icy Quill buff. It increases the Cryo damage dealt based on Shenhe's current ATK but it is triggered by a set number of times. Her 2nd Passive works together with her Skill and has an effect that changes depending if the Skill was tapped or held.
Summary of Elemental Skill's Effect
Icy Quill
(Buff)
- Increases Cryo damage dealt by allies based on 82.2% of Shenhe's current ATK.
- Can be triggered 5/7 times and lasts 10/15s if Tapped/Held.
Tap
- Buffs Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst damage by 15% for 10s.
Hold
- Buffs Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attack damage by 15% for 15s.
Use Elemental Burst for shredding Cryo and Physical RES
Shenhe's Elemental Burst creates a field that deals Cryo damage in intervals and also reduces the Cryo and Physical resistances of enemies inside. Her 1st Passive is related to her Burst on that it buffs the Cryo DMG Bonus of allies inside by 15%.

Grants allies the Icy Quill buff that increases the Cryo damage dealt based on 82.2% (Skill Lvl. 10) of Shenhe's current ATK.
Shenhe's Elemental Skill is used for buffing her teammates but it's mostly targetting only the Cryo damage dealt. It has a Tap and Hold version that her 2nd Passive has an additional effect for.
2nd Passive's Effect
Tap

Elemental Burst of Shenhe
Summary of Elemental Burst
- Creates a field that buffs allies and debuffs enemies.
Ｌ Cryo Damage Bonus buff by 15% for allies and 15% Cryo/Physical RES shred on enemies.

Shenhe's Elemental Burst deals AoE Cryo damage and creates a field that buffs allies and debuffs enemies. Only the RES shred scales on Shenhe's Burst level because the Cryo buff is from her 1st Passive.
Should You Pull for Shenhe's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Prioritize Shenhe's Constellations
Signature VS Constellations
C1 > C2 or
Calamity Queller
C2 Is a Good Stopping Point
Apart from her Skill, Shenhe's teams will typically depend on her Burst for the Cryo and Physical RES shred and a modest Cryo DMG Bonus. This requires Shenhe to have relatively high Energy Recharge to ensure you can use her Burst on demand. Because Calamity Queller does not provide Energy in some form, it'll be harder to maintain consistent Burst uptime especially without her first constellation. In comparison, the extra Skill proc from C1 is a decent source of Energy, and C2 lengthens the duration of her Burst and adds an extra Cryo Crit DMG bonus to it, which most Cryo on-fielders will appreciate.
C2 is an ideal stopping point, and will be Shenhe's best constellation prior to C6, both for its buffs and by virtue of activating C1 along the way.
Calamity Queller Can Be a Choice after C1
Her Signature Weapon is a good source of damage increase, but will only be comfortable to use on Shenhe if you have her C1 or if you have a lot of Energy Recharge on your artifacts.
And although the weapon can be used by other Polearm characters, its value and performance may be lackluster compared to other 5-star Polearms due to Calamity Queller not providing any Crit. Consider getting her constellations first, or at least C1, for easier gains.

Who Is Shenhe?
A Mysterious Woman From Liyue
Cool and distant, Shenhe lives up to the element she wields. She's been with the Adepti since she was a child to the point that some people think she's also an Adepti.
Cloud Retainer's Disciple
Shenhe is one of the Cloud Retainer's disciples along with Ganyu. Although they train under the same master, Ganyu does not really know her well beyond Shenhe's destructive temper.
Chongyun's Aunt
Shenhe is actually part of the same clan as Chongyun. She's been separated from them for years due to the tragedy that befell Shenhe's family. They have been trying to reconnect with her now that she's living in Liyue Harbor.

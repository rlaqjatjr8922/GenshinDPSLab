Shenhe Works Best as Support for Cryo Damage Dealers
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Premium Freeze
- Shenhe is best used in a Freeze team since her Off-Field Cryo can be utilized for Freezing the enemy.
- Along with her ability to buff Cryo damage, she can support any Cryo DPS.
2nd
▼ Mono Cryo
- Shenhe in Mono Cryo is now better than Melt teams due to the releases of new characters.
- Escoffier can still shred 55% of the enemy's resistances and Skirk can be used as DPS.
- Though Skirk can't use her Elemental Skill level up buff, she can still deal decent damage.
3rd
▼ Melt Support Shenhe
- Shenhe can be used as Melt support for DPS characters like Wriothesley or Ayaka that can do well in Melt.
- As long as the Pyro aura is maintained using Burning or Anemo characters' burst, Melt can be consistently triggered.
4th
▼ Physical Support Shenhe
- Shenhe's ability to buff Normal Attacks and shred Physical Resistances allow her to be used as Physical DPS support.
- Since Eula is a Cryo character, Shenhe can also funnel Energy Particles to maintain the uptime of her Burst.
Shenhe is a great support for Cryo damage dealers since she can give them a multitude of buffs and funnel Energy particles for them. Her ability to apply Cryo Off-Field can be utilized for Melt and she can also decrease the Physical RES of enemies with her Elemental Burst.

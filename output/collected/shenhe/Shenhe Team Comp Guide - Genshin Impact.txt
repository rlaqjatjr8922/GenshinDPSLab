Shenhe's teams will mainly consist of Cryo units, as she mainly operates as a Cryo Support in
Genshin Impact
. When creating a team for Shenhe, players have to be aware of who will benefit from her Elemental Skill and who won't. Here are some examples of the
best Shenhe teams in
Genshin Impact
.

Shenhe + Skirk + Furina + Escoffier
This Shenhe team depends on
Skirk dealing DMG with her Normal attacks
and Elemental Burst. Players can set the field ready for Skirk and choose to quickly let out her Burst DMG or use her Elemental Skill and start dealing Normal Attack DMG to enemies.
The idea behind having only Hydro and Cryo characters in the team is to activate Skirk’s Ascension passive that grants her over 150% extra DMG on her Elemental Burst and Normal Attack DMG. Of course, Furina and Shenhe further increase Skirk’s Cryo DMG, while Shenhe also participates with Escoffier to massively reduce enemies’ resistance to Cryo DMG. This is not just a perfect team for Shenhe, it’s also one of the
best teams in
Genshin Impact
.
Team Roles
Shenhe:
Buffer, Debuffer, Cryo Enabler
Skirk:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Healer, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Escoffier:
Sub-DPS, Healer, Debuffer, Cryo Enabler
2
Shenhe + Wriothesley + Emilie + Thoma
This
Burning team in
Genshin Impact
works to keep enemies affected by Pyro, while Wriothesley is just enjoying an endless amount of Reverse Melt reactions. Players can swap Wriothesley with
Ganyu
,
Ayaka
, or even
Skirk
.
Nahida
can replace Emilie, and
Xiangling
can take Thoma’s place.

Having a Dendro unit like Emilie ensures that off-field Dendro applications are always present, while Thoma coordinates with Wriothesley to keep applying Pyro. Both Emilie and Thoma will continuously trigger the Burning reaction, which will keep applying the Pyro element on enemies, even after
Wriothesley consumes it to trigger Melt
. This is where Shenhe comes into play. She will amplify Wriothesley’s DMG, which will greatly increase his Melt reactions.
Team Roles
Shenhe:
Buffer, Debuffer, Cryo Enabler
Wriothesley:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
Emilie:
Sub-DPS, Dendro Enabler
Thoma:
Shielder, Pyro Enabler
1
Shenhe + Chasca + Furina + Bennett
Chasca takes the Elements of every character in her team and shoots them out during her Elemental Skill state. She sends a couple of extra Anemo shots just to Swirl these Elements together, which creates an interesting mechanic for her teams. Having buffers join this will not only allow Chasca to shoot their Elements, but they will also amplify them to deal more DMG.
The combo of Shenhe, Furina, and Bennett adds Cryo, Hydro, and
Pyro attacks to Chasca
, which will trigger the Frozen, Melt, and Vaporize reactions when she shoots. Now, imagine all these attacks and reactions amplified by Furina, Bennett, and Shenhe.
Team Roles
Shenhe:
Buffer, Debuffer, Cryo Enabler
Chasca:
Main DPS, Driver
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Healer, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Bennett:
Healer, Pyro Enabler

Wriothesley is a powerful Cryo DPS character, but he can be tricky to build and play. In this article, we will discuss the best team comps for Wriothesley in Genshin Impact 4.1, focusing on team comps that are both effective and easy to play.
Best Team Comps for Wriothesley in Genshin Impact 4.1
Wriothesley, Kazuha, Kokomi, Shenhe
The Permafreeze team is designed for crowd control and freezing enemies. Shenhe and Kazuha support Wriothesley with Cryo DMG bonus, while Kokomi applies Hydro to trigger freeze reactions and heal the team. Note that this team consists of 5-star characters.
Wriothesley, Kazuha, Nahida, Kokomi
In this Fridge team, we’ll want to focus on Cryo damage and Dendro interactions. Wriothesley remains the main DPS, while Nahida applies Dendro off-field. Kokomi triggers Hydro for freeze reactions, and Kazuha groups enemies and buffs Wriothesley’s damage.
Wriothesley, Bennett, Nahida, Thoma

The Melt team focuses on maximizing damage output. Wriothesley applies Cryo continuously, and Thoma and Nahida support with Pyro and Hydro elements for Melt reactions. Bennett’s healing keeps Wriothesley healthy.
Wriothesley, Bennett, Kazuha, Shenhe
The Hypercarry team is all about maximizing Wriothesley’s personal damage. With Shenhe, Kazuha, and Bennett, you can unleash Wriothesley’s full potential. This composition is a great choice for players seeking to maximize Wriothesley’s damage output.
Team Building Tips:
Mix and match characters to create unique and effective team compositions centered around Wriothesley.

Consider the desired elemental reactions and your preferred playstyle.

Wriothesley Talent Breakdown
Wriothesley is a
Cryo DPS
specializing in
fast-paced melee attacks
and
self-sustaining HP management
through his
Chilling Penalty
state. His kit revolves around
punching enemies with Cryo-infused attacks
, making him an
exceptional choice for Freeze, Mono Cryo, or Melt teams
.
Normal Attack: Forceful Fists of Frost
Wriothesley's Basic Attacks are all Cryo-infused
and consist of
5 rapid punches
dealing Cryo DMG.
Charged Attack (Vaulting Fist)
– Consumes
Stamina
to perform an
AoE Cryo attack
.
Plunging Attack
– Deals
AoE Cryo DMG
upon impact.
Combo Count Retention
–
Does not reset
after sprinting or using
Icefang Rush
, making his attack chain smoother.
✅
Best Feature:
All his Normal Attacks deal Cryo DMG, making him a strong
Cryo DPS
even at
C0
.
Elemental Skill: Icefang Rush
Dashes forward and enters the "Chilling Penalty" state.
Enhances Normal Attacks
to deal
higher Cryo DMG
, but attacks will
consume HP
if his health is above 50%.
Gains interruption resistance
while active.
✅
Best Feature:
The
HP-draining mechanic increases DPS output
, and with
self-healing from his Passive
, it keeps him alive.
Elemental Burst: Darkgold Wolfbite
Unleashes a powerful Cryo-infused punch
, followed by
multiple AoE Cryo strikes.
Ousia Alignment:
Leaves behind an
Ousia-aligned Cryo attack

✅
Best Feature:
A
hard-hitting AoE Cryo Burst
that benefits from
Cryo DMG buffs
and
Constellations

Wriothesley Constellations Guide
Wriothesley’s Constellations significantly boost his
damage, healing, and overall combat potential
, making him a
top-tier Cryo DPS
with each upgrade. Here's a breakdown of what each Constellation does and whether they are worth pulling for.
C1 – Terror for the Evildoers
(
Strong Early Buff
)
🔹
Effect:
Enhances his Passive Talent
"There Shall Be a Plea for Justice"
by allowing him to generate Gracious Rebuke effects more frequently.
🔹
Buffs Rebuke: Vaulting Fist
with a
200% DMG increase
and extends the
Chilling Penalty state
by 4s if triggered while in that state.
Why It's Good:
More Charged Attack DMG
means stronger burst damage.
Better uptime on Chilling Penalty
allows for longer enhanced attack cycles.
Worth Pulling?Yes, if you want a noticeable DPS increase with smoother gameplay.
C2 – Shackles for the Arrogant
(
Massive Burst DMG Boost
)
🔹
Effect:
Each
Prosecution Edict stack
(from his Passive Talent
"There Shall Be a Reckoning for Sin"
) boosts
Darkgold Wolfbite's damage by 40% per stack.
Why It's Good:
Turns his Elemental Burst into a nuke,
making it deal
significantly more damage
.
More synergy with Energy Recharge builds
since the Burst now scales even harder.
Worth Pulling?Yes, if you want to maximize his burst potential.
A huge upgrade over C1.
C3 – Punishment for the Frauds
(
Basic Normal Attack Buff
)
🔹
Effect:
Increases
Normal Attack: Forceful Fists of Frost
by
+3 levels (up to level 15).
Why It's Good:
More
consistent DPS increase
for his primary attack sequence.
Works well with
Marechaussee Hunter and Normal Attack-boosting weapons.
Worth Pulling?Decent, but not game-changing.Only go for this if you plan to reach higher Constellations.
C4 – Redemption for the Suffering
(
Major Healing & ATK SPD Buffs
)
🔹
Effect:
Doubles the healing
from Rebuke: Vaulting Fist to
50% of Max HP
.
If
healing overflows
,
ATK SPD increases by 20% (on-field) or 10% for all teammates (off-field) for 4-6s.
Why It's Good:
Improves survivability
, letting him sustain himself in longer fights.
ATK SPD boost = faster DPS output.
Team-wide ATK SPD buff (when off-field)
makes him a great support for other attack-heavy units.
Worth Pulling?Yes, for better sustain and a smoother playstyle.
Great for
self-sufficiency
and team synergy.
C5 – Mercy for the Wronged
(
Elemental Burst Buff
)
🔹
Effect:
Increases
Darkgold Wolfbite
by
+3 levels (up to level 15).
Why It's Good:
More
Burst DMG
scaling.
Synergizes with
C2 for even more Burst impact.
Worth Pulling?Only if you're aiming for C6.
Otherwise, it's
not a must-have.
C6 – Esteem for the Innocent
(
Best Constellation – Massive CRIT Buff & Extra DMG
)
🔹
Effect:
Rebuke: Vaulting Fist gains:
+10% CRIT Rate
+80% CRIT DMG
Spawns an extra icicle
that deals
100% of Rebuke: Vaulting Fist's Base DMG as Cryo DMG
(counts as Charged Attack DMG).
Why It's Good:
Turns him into a CRIT monster
– +80% CRIT DMG is insane.
Extra icicle hit = more DPS per rotation.
Perfectly complements his playstyle
, making his Charged Attack even deadlier.
Worth Pulling?Absolutely, if you want to max out his potential.
This makes Wriothesley a true
endgame DPS.
Final Verdict: Best Constellations to Aim For
🎯
C2
– Huge Burst DMG increase, making his kit significantly stronger.
🎯
C4
– Healing boost & ATK SPD buff, making him smoother to play.
🎯
C6
– CRIT Rate, +80% CRIT DMG, and an extra hit – turns him into a top-tier DPS.
If you're

C6 is his strongest upgrade.

Xiangling: Playstyle and skillset of the character
Xiangling
The character
Xiangling
is a 4-star
Pyro polearm
user. She may not be Genshin Impact’s most powerful character, but she does have a few characteristics that make her very suitable for the party. Xiangling can be used as DPS, sub-DPS, and also as Burst Support, her constellation is
Condensed Pyronado.

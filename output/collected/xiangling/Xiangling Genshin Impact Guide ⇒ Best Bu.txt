Xiangling – Background and Personality
Xiangling is the renowned head chef of the Wanmin Restaurant, a well-known establishment in Liyue that she runs alongside her father, Chef Mao. Her boundless enthusiasm for food and cooking sets her apart, as she is always looking for new and creative ways to prepare meals—often using ingredients that most would consider unusual.
Despite her unconventional approach to cooking, Xiangling is deeply knowledgeable and confident in her skills. She embodies optimism, determination, and an adventurous spirit. Her passion for cuisine extends to her travels across Teyvat, where she constantly seeks new ingredients, flavors, and culinary inspiration.
Xiangling – Combat Abilities
Xiangling wields a polearm and possesses the Pyro Vision, making her a formidable force in battle. Her kit is unique in that it allows her to excel as an off-field Pyro damage dealer, ensuring constant elemental reactions and high DPS potential.
Elemental Skill – Guoba Attack
Xiangling’s Elemental Skill,
Guoba Attack
, summons her companion Guoba, an adorable red panda that spews fire at enemies. This attack deals AoE Pyro damage over a duration of time, making it excellent for continuous elemental applications.
Cooldown:
12 seconds
Damage Type:
Pyro (AoE)
Effect:
Summons Guoba, who breathes fire at enemies for a brief duration
Elemental Burst – Pyronado
Xiangling’s Elemental Burst,
Pyronado
, is her defining move in terms of damage output. Upon activation, a fiery whirlwind encircles her and follows her movements, continuously dealing Pyro damage to any enemies within range. This ability is what makes her a staple for off-field Pyro damage, as it remains active even when Xiangling swaps out.
Duration:
10 seconds
Cooldown:
20 seconds
Energy Cost:
80
Effect:
Creates a rotating Pyronado that follows the active character, dealing Pyro damage to any enemies in its path
Xiangling – Constellations
By obtaining additional copies of Xiangling, players can unlock her Constellations, which significantly enhance her abilities.
C1 – Crispy Outside, Tender Inside
Opponents hit by Guoba’s attacks have their Pyro resistance reduced by 15% for 6 seconds, making them more vulnerable to Pyro attacks.
C2 – Oil Meets Fire
The last hit in Xiangling’s Normal Attack sequence applies the
Implode
status. This causes an enemy to explode after 2 seconds, dealing 75% of her ATK as AoE Pyro damage.
C3 – Deepfry
Increases the level of Pyronado by 3, allowing it to deal more damage for a longer period.
C4 – Slowbake
Increases the duration of Pyronado by 40%, further extending her off-field DPS potential.
C5 – Guoba Mad
Increases Guoba Attack’s level by 3, boosting its overall damage and functionality.
C6 – Condensed Pyronado
For the duration of Pyronado, all party members receive a 15% Pyro damage bonus, increasing team-wide Pyro output.
Xiangling – Passive Abilities
Crossfire
Increases the range of Guoba’s flames by 20%, allowing it to hit more enemies from a distance.
Beware, It’s Super Hot
After Guoba Attack ends, Guoba leaves a chili pepper on the ground. Picking it up grants a 10% ATK boost for 10 seconds.
Chef de Cuisine
When cooking an ATK-boosting dish perfectly, Xiangling has a 12% chance to double the product.

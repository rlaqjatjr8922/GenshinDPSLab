Genshin Impact Ganyu Guide: Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
November 14, 2023
Tips
Share:
Ganyu in Genshin Impact is a
5-star Cryo Archers
. She is one of the best heroes in the game. When properly mastered, she inflicts considerable damage on her enemies. However, her
gameplay
differs from the other characters and will require training to use her to her full potential: you have to stay at a distance, favour charged attacks and target your opponents' weak points. We explain it all in this
complete guide to Ganyu from Genshin Impact
. With our tips and tricks, you'll know which artefacts and weapons to equip, which skills to mount and which team to play with.
How to get Ganyu in Genshin Impact ?
Ganyu is released on 12 January 2021
. You can only invoke her when she is featured in a Character Event Wish.
Ganyu Character Event Wish
You can try to unlock Ganyu during his rerun in the second part of
update 2.4 of Genshin Impact
. His
Passenger Doubt
banner will be available from 25 January until 15 February 2022.
Ganyu Constellations
Ganyu excels in Genshin Impact even without Constellation. But, if you like the character, have some primo gems in stock and still want to improve her, we advise you especially her C1.
Dew-Drinker
reduces the Cryo resistance of enemies hit by Ganyu's charged attack by 15% and recovers 2 more points of elemental energy.
How to play Ganyu in Genshin Impact ?
Ganyu is a support DPS of choice. Her elemental rampage
Celestial Shower
drops icicles over a wide area for 15 seconds. With a good energy recharge, you can use this skill all the time. With the
Harmony between Heaven and Earth
passive, Cryo characters in the area of Celestial Shower gain a 20% bonus to Cryo damage.
The Cryo archer also asserts itself as the main DPS. Its gameplay is based on charged attacks. Her normal
Liutian Archery
attack benefits from two charges. Charging once, you fire
Ice Arrow
generating Cryo damage. Charging twice, you unleash a
Frost Arrow
that deals area damage as it explodes. If you have trouble aiming, the damage will still be applied.
To distract the enemies, use Ganyu's elemental skill:
Qilin's Trace
. The
Ice Lotus attracts
enemies, then explodes.
Upgrade Ganyu's normal attack as much as possible, then his elemental rampage and finally his elemental attack (level 6 will be sufficient). To do this, collect
Scrolls of Diligence
in
Taishan Mansion
on Tuesdays, Fridays and Sundays.
What is the best equipment for Ganyu?
To increase Ganyu's damage in Genshin Impact, choose equipment that improves both his attack and critical damage.
The best weapons for Ganyu
Ganyu fights with a bow Therefore, on the side of the 5 star weapons, we advise you especially :
Amos' Bow (5★)
Skyward Harp (5★)
Bow of Amos
(5★): In addition to providing quite a bit of attack, the bow amplifies the damage of normal and charged attacks.
Azure Vault Wings
(5★): further increases Ganyu's critical rate, and when you hit an enemy, you have a 60% chance to deal additional physical damage.
If you don't have any of these 5-star weapons, free-to-play options are available:
Prototype Crescent (4★)
Black
rock war bow (4★)
Hunting bow
of Favonius (4★)
Peaceful Moon
(4★): as you make this weapon directly from the blacksmith, it will be easier to mount in R5. When you hit your enemies' weak points, Ganyu's movement speed is increased by 10% andATK by 36% for 10 seconds.
Black Rock Warbow
(4★): after defeating an enemy, theATK gets a 12% bonus for 30 seconds. The effect can be accumulated 3 times.
For a support build, you are suggested to equip it with
Favonius Warbow
(4★)
. Critical hits have a 60% chance of causing energy particles to appear.
The best artefacts for Ganyu
On the artifact side, we recommend 3 sets in particular:
Wanderer's Troupe
Blizzard Strayer
Noblesse Oblige
4-piece
Vagabond Band
: You receive an 80-point elemental mastery bonus and your charged attacks are boosted by 35% if you use a bow or a catalyst.
4-piece
Ice Breaker
: The set gives you a 15% Cryo damage bonus and 20% CRIT rate if the enemy is frozen.
Ancient Royal Ritual
in 4 pieces: the set increases the elemental rampage by 20% and the attack of the whole team after activating it. This is a good choice for playing Ganyu in support. It is also possible to opt for only 2 pieces of the set to combine with 2 pieces of Icebreaker.
For the main artifact stats, go forATK% on the hourglass, Cryo damage bonus for the cut and DMG critical on the crown. For subs, go forATK%, critical damage, CRIT rate, elemental mastery and energy recharge.
Ganyu's climbing materials in Genshin Impact
To raise Ganyu to level 90, you must collect the following resources:
419
Hero's Wit
and 2.1 million
Moras
;
1 Sliver, 9 Fragments, 9 Chunks and 6
Shivada Jade
Gemstones, as well as 46
Hoarfrost Core
. Get them from the
Cryo Regisvine
.
168
Qingxin flowers
: you will find them in the heights of Liyue. Find them on the
interactive Teyvat map
. Tip: to get more, grow some in your own garden.
Serenitea Pot
.
18
Whopperflower Nectar
, 30
Shimmering Nectar
and 36
Elemental Nectar
to loot from Whopperflowers.
Which team should Ganyu play in?
Ganyu can be played in any team. As a support DPS, she will be able to continuously inflict Cryo damage with her elemental rampage and thus generate elemental reactions.
To play her as a DPS hand, we advise you to combine her with a Hydro character such as
Mona
Tartaglia,
Xingqiu
or
Kokomi
in order to freeze the enemy.
Ganyu has few HP and DEF. She can thus be quickly defeated. Protect her with a Geo hero who can create shields, like
Zhongli
or
Noëlle
.
In order to gather the enemies more easily, complete the team with an anemo hero:
Kazuha
,
Venti
or
Sucrose
.
You can also combine her with a Cryo character such as
Diona
who will protect her with a shield and heal her or
Shehne
to boost Cryo damage. In this guide, we recommend a team with
Mona
, Zhongli and
Bennett
to increase the damage and heal Ganyu.
What is Ganyu's birthday and height from Genshin Impact ?
Ganyu is said to be
over 3000 years old
! She is the secretary general of the Yuehai pavilion, a position she has held for many years. Half-adept, half-human, she has chosen to live among mortals, even if it is not always easy. She is distinguished by her horns, which she owes to her Qilin origins. The follower was born on December 2nd and
is estimated to be 160 cm tall
.
You can find out more about him in his history quest.
We hope you found this
guide to Ganyu from Genshin Impact
useful. If you like ranged combat and know how to aim (or not), you will inflict a lot of damage! Enough to take down the toughest creatures in the Abyss. What's more, Ganyu comes in very handy against ranged bosses like Stormterror. She'll make short work of them!

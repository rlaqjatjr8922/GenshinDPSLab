Synergy Of Freeze Ganyu Team
Character
Detail
Shenhe
She can provide buffs for boosting normal & charged attacks, Skill, Burst, and Cryo DMG which is beneficial for Ganyu. She can also provide ATK buff to the team with Noblesse Oblige x4.
Kazuha
He can shred the enemies' resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 while providing decent crowd control, and an elemental damage bonus with his Burst.
Kokomi
An excellent healer who can consistently apply Hydro for sustaining Freeze. With Thrilling Tales & Tenacity artifact, she can also provide an ATK buff that can benefit both Ganyu & Shenhe.
The objective of this team is to funnel all buffs to Ganyu while sustaining the Freeze status on enemies so Ganyu can freely hit the enemies. Kazuha's Freedom-Sworn also increases Ganyu's normal & charged attacks when the effect has been triggered.
Variation Of Morgana Team
▼Morgana team
is a team comp that can trigger Freeze consistently which immobilizes the enemies. This team is widely known since it offers a safe yet effective combat environment for Ganyu.
Pros & Cons Of Freeze Ganyu Team
Pros
- Ganyu can deal heavy damage consistently thru charged ATK
- Sustained Freeze can halt enemies' actions
- Can focus on raising CRIT DMG since Ganyu's passive talent & Cryo resonance can already provide CRIT Rate
Cons
- Not all enemies can be frozen like bosses and those with immunity to Cryo
- Removing Cryo shield could be hard
- Amos' Bow effect is not optimized when attacking near the target
Rotation Of Freeze Ganyu Team
No.

2
Use Ganyu's Elemental Skill for taunt
3

switch to Kazuha if you need crowd control
. If he triggers Cryo-Swirl, the resistance shred debuff is refreshed.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Reverse Melt Ganyu Team
Character
Detail
Xiangling
An excellent off-field Pyro DPS who can trigger a Burning reaction with Nahida's mark & reverse Melt with Ganyu. Also, Guoba's chili can increase Ganyu's ATK when approached.
Nahida
She can sustain the Burning status on the enemies so Ganyu can consistently trigger Melt reaction. In addition, Nahida's Burst increases the team's EM which boosts the damage of all reactions.
Zhongli
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' resistance and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
The objective of this team is to trigger Burning for Ganyu to reverse Melt. This team is flexible and can simultaneously defeat enemies.
Trigger Melt From Afar
Since Nahida can mark enemies from a distance, you can damage the enemies simultaneously when Xiangling triggers a Burning reaction. When the marked enemies are already burning, use Ganyu's Frostflake Arrow to trigger Melt.
Pros & Cons Of Reverse Melt Ganyu Team
Pros
- Ganyu can deal heavy damage consistently thru reverse Melt
- All members in the team can inflict decent damage even when Ganyu is the active character
- Numerous buffs & debuffs that can further boost not only Ganyu's damage output but also Xiangling & Nahida
Cons
- Difficult to utilize if you are facing an enemy with a debuff that can ignore shield such as corrosion
- Xiangling's Pyro application must be consistent for sustained Burning
Rotation Of Reverse Melt Ganyu Team
No.

2

3
Use Nahida's Elemental Skill to mark enemies
4
Switch to Xiangling
5

6
Switch to Nahida
7
Use Nahida's Elemental Burst for buff
8
Switch to Ganyu
9
Use Ganyu's Elemental Burst for Cryo DMG bonus
10
Use Ganyu's Elemental Skill for taunt
11
Use Ganyu's Frostflake Arrow thru CA
From time to time, you can
switch to Zhongli to refresh the shield's duration
. You can also use Zhongli's Burst for crowd control.

Tweak Ganyu's Artifacts Accordingly
With Bennett / Pyro Resonance
Pyro resonance / Bennett's Burst can already provide an ATK boost, especially with Noblesse Oblige, so it would be great to use
Wanderer's Troupe
x4 on Ganyu for the additional 80 EM.
Without Bennett
To increase Ganyu's damage output,
Blizzard Strayer
x2 +
Any ATK
x2 is recommended to improve Ganyu's DMG when triggering Melt with other attacks aside from charged ATK. Also if Nahida is included in the team, she can already boost the team's EM thru Burst so it is better to go for 2+2 artifacts than Wanderer's Troupe x4.
You can adjust Ganyu's artifact depending on the team's synergy but
consider the artifact set with better sub-stats
.
More Elemental Reactions
When Dendro is retained and Hydro is included, the team can trigger Freeze, Melt, Vaporize, Bloom, & Burgeon reactions which are all great.

Synergy Of Mono-Cryo Ganyu Team
Character
Detail
Shenhe
She can provide buffs for boosting normal & charged attacks, Skill, Burst, and Cryo DMG which is beneficial for Ganyu.
Kazuha
He can shred the enemies' resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 while providing decent crowd control, and an elemental damage bonus with his Burst.
Diona
A great shielder, healer, & battery. She can increase the team's ATK with Noblesse Oblige x4 and EM at C6 which is beneficial for Kazuha.
Similar to
▲Freeze team
, the objective of this team is to funnel all buffs to Ganyu. Kazuha's Freedom-Sworn also increases Ganyu's normal & charged attacks when the effect has been triggered.
Use Skyward Harp Instead Of Amos' Bow
Since the enemies are not frozen, they are going to approach you whenever possible. You
cannot optimize the effect of Amos' Bow if the distance between you and the target is not far
so use Skyward Harp instead. Ganyu's CRIT Rate may be overkill with this bow but a higher chance of a guaranteed CRIT hit is great nonetheless.
Skyward Harp Over Hunter's Path
While Hunter's Path can improve Ganyu's charged ATK periodically, it is better to use Skyward Harp since its effect provides additional CRIT DMG that improves not only her charged ATK but also her Skill & Burst.
Pros & Cons Of Mono-Cryo Ganyu Team
Pros
- Ganyu can deal heavy damage consistently
- Can produce a lot of Cryo particles which is beneficial, especially for Ganyu & Shenhe
Cons
- Not all enemies can be slowed such as bosses
- Disadvantageous to enemies with immunity/resistance to Cryo and protected with Cryo shield
Rotation Of Mono-Cryo Ganyu Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Diona's Elemental Skill for increased movement speed, shield, & Cryo application to enemies
2
Switch to Kazuha
3
Use Kazuha's Elemental Burst for Cryo-Swirl then Elemental Skill for crowd control
4
Switch to Shenhe
5
Use Shenhe's Elemental Burst for Cryo DMG Bonus
6
Use Shenhe's Elemental Skill for buff: Press for boosting Skill & Burst & Hold for boosting NA/CA/PA
7
Switch to Ganyu
8
Use Ganyu's Elemental Burst for Cryo DMG bonus
9
Use Ganyu's Elemental Skill for taunt
10
Use Ganyu's Frostflake Arrow thru CA
From time to time, you can
switch to Kazuha if you need crowd control
. If he triggers Cryo-Swirl, the resistance shred debuff is refreshed. Also, you can
switch to Diona if you need a shield or heal
. Since this team produces a lot of Cryo particles, Diona's Burst can be used more often.

Ganyu Team Overview
This Page provides the best Ganyu Team information. Ganyu will be Main DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Ganyu and Ayaka to make your own best team.
Ganyu’s Pros and Cons:
High Damage: Ganyu’s damage output is comparable to Neuvillette. She only requires Crit DMG and ATK when used in a Freeze composition. Otherwise, if not using the Blizzard Strayer or Hunter’s Path set (with Hunter’s Path being more effective with Furina), she needs around 35% Crit Rate.
(Thank you for Spencer’s suggestions. He has run the calculations multiple times, considering damage multipliers, charged attack durations, and other factors.)
From SS tier to S tier, Ganyu best team can be formed in different ways including Permafrost and Melt team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

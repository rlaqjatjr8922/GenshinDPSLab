Riptide
Opponents affected by Riptide will suffer from
AoE Hydro DMG
effects when attacked by Tartaglia in various ways. DMG dealt in this way is considered Normal Attack DMG.

When Tartaglia is in Foul Legacy: Raging Tide's Melee Stance, he cannot perform a plunging attack.

Foul Legacy: Raging Tide
He chose the bow as his weapon precisely to overcome his weakness with it. But a serious fight demands that he gets serious...
Unleashes a set of weaponry made of pure water, dealing
Hydro DMG
to surrounding opponents and entering Melee Stance.
In this Stance, Tartaglia's Normal and Charged Attacks are converted to
Hydro DMG
that cannot be overridden by any other elemental infusion and change as follows:
Normal Attack

Hydro
strikes.
Charged Attack
Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to unleash a cross slash, dealing

Riptide Slash
Hitting an opponent affected by Riptide with a melee attack unleashes a Riptide Slash that deals
AoE Hydro DMG
. DMG dealt in this way is considered Elemental Skill DMG, and can only occur once every 1.5s.
After 30s, or when the ability is unleashed again, this skill will end. Tartaglia will return to his Ranged Stance and this ability will enter CD. The longer Tartaglia stays in his Melee Stance, the longer the CD.
If the return to a ranged stance occurs automatically after 30s, the CD is even longer.

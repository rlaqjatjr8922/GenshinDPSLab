Childe (Tartaglia) Skill Guide & How To Use Childe (Tartaglia)
Recommended Rotation For Childe
Recommended Rotation
1
Unleash the off field skills of Supports that you use.
2
Use Childe's Elemental Skill, use your Normal Attack or charge attacks for 6-8 seconds only
3
Repeat from 1.
*
Use Ranged Elemental Burst if you need to apply Riptide across multiple enemies. This does not use all of the energy so use this at Start Combo if you have energy.
Use Melee Elemental Burst against enemies with riptide/for higher damage. A great finishing move.
Uses Of Childe's Elemental Skill
Switches Childe's Attacks To Melee
Childe is the first character introduced that can change his type of attacks with the activation of his skill. From a bow, you can switch to a melee stance to get up close and personal to your enemies.
Riptide Applied When Hitting Critical Shots
Whenever Childe gets crits while in melee stance, his unique debuff Riptide will be applied to that enemy. Riptide will cause additional Hydro damage when activated and will spread to other enemies.
Hydro Applied With Every Attack
Childe becomes a Hydro enabler in his melee stance since all of his normal and charge attacks will apply Hydro. He also applies them fairly fast due to his quick normal Melee attack animation.
Childe's Elemental Burst
Deals Different Attack Depending On Childe's Current Stance
The type of attack Childe does when using his burst will change depending on the stance that he is currently in. The main difference between them are their effects. Ranged stance will apply Riptide to enemies while the Melee ELemental Burst will activate Riptide.
High Damage Burst That Hits Multiple Enemies
Childe's Burst has a high damage output on his own and even more so when dealing Vaporize. It's one of the highest damage ceiling single hit Burst damage currently in the game!
Should You Pull for Childe (Tartaglia)'s Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Consider Getting Polar Star First
Signature VS Constellations
Polar Star
> C1 > C3
Polar Star Is a Great on Him, Decent on Others
Apart from being a usable bow for other characters that would appreciate the bonuses it gives,
Tartaglia will benefit a lot more from equipping Polar Star over getting C1
, which is mostly due to the Quality of Life nature of this constellation. Cooldown Reduction on his skill may be good to have, but there are ways to mitigate the long cooldown of "Foul Legacy: Raging Tide", which often boils down to playing him a little more optimally.
For an increase to his personal damage, Polar Star will be the ideal pull.
The value of this weapon also increases if you do not have any of the other universal 5-star bows,
such as
Aqua Simulacra
,
The First Great Magic
,
Thundering Pulse
or even
Skyward Harp
, which are competitive alternatives for Tartaglia. Polar Star, being his Signature Weapon, will still outperform these weapons however.
Recommendation if You Have
The First Great Magic
C1 > C3 or
Polar Star
Other 5-Star Bows are Competitive
If you happen to have any of his best 5-star alternative choices, such as The First Great Magic,
Aqua Simulacra
,
Thundering Pulse
or
Skyward Harp
, you can choose to get C1 instead
. The Quality of Life improvement may not be the best damage boost but it will contribute to a smoother gameplay experience on Tartaglia. C3 can also be a choice if you want to get more talent levels on his skill for an adequate damage upgrade.
Polar Star will remain a viable priority pick-up if you want to maximize Tartaglia's possible damage
or if you value the aesthetics. Consider your priorities before pulling.

Who Is Tartaglia (Childe)?
A Warrior At Heart
Childe is a warrior at heart, always seeking to better himself. He regularly seeks a challenge and pushes himself to learn all kinds of different weapons.
Skirk's Student
When Childe was young, he found himself lost in the Abyss. He came upon Skirk who then trained him after. They haven't seen each other in years and he has sought to find her again ever since.
One Of The Fatui Harbingers
Childe may be young, but don't let his friendliness fool you. He's the 11th of the Harbingers, the leaders of Snezhnaya's Fatui. He works for the Tsaritsa, but he has has a somewhat good relationship with the Traveler.

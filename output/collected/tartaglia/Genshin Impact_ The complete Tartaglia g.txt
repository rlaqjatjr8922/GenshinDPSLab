Genshin Impact just got back one of its best characters: Tartaglia. If you’re planning on getting him you’ll want to know what materials you need to prepare. Our Genshin Impact Tartaglia guide will help you get you everything you need to level up Tartaglia as fast as possible.
Genshin Impact: Tartaglia Ascension Guide
Tartaglia is a Bow user with a Hydro vision. At each Ascension level, he gets a boost to Hydro DMG. To get Tartaglia to his final Ascension, you’ll need the following:
Ascension 1- Lv 20
1 Varunada Lazurite Sliver
3 Starconch
3 Recruit’s Insignia
20,000 Mora
Ascension 2- Lv 40
3 Varunada Lazurite Fragment
2 Cleansing Heart
10 Starconch
15 Recruit’s Insignia
40,000 Mora
Ascension 3- Lv 50
6 Varunada Lazurite Fragment
4 Cleansing Heart
20 Starconch
12 Sergeant’s Insignia
60,000 Mora
Ascension 4- Lv 60
3 Varunada Lazurite Chunk
8 Cleansing Heart
30 Starconch
18 Sergeant’s Insignia
80,000 Mora
Ascension 5- Lv 70
6 Varunada Lazurite Chunk
12 Cleansing Heart
45 Starconch
12 Lieutenant’s Insignia
100,000 Mora
Ascension 6- Lv 80
6 Varunada Lazurite Chunk
20 Cleansing Heart
60 Starconch
24 Lieutenant’s Insignia
120,000 Mora
In total, you’ll need 1 Varunada Lazurite Sliver, 9 Varunada Lazurite Fragments, 9 Varunada Lazurite, 6 Varunada Lazurite Gemstones, 18 Recruit’s Insignia, 30 Sergeant’s Insignia, 36 Lieutenant’s Insignia, 46 Cleansing Heart and 168 Starconch to get him ascended to Lv 80/90.

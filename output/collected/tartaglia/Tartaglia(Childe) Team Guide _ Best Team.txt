Tartaglia Team Overview
This Page provides the best Tartaglia Team information. Tartaglia is Sub DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Tartaglia and Xingqiu to make your own best team.
Tartaglia best team can be formed in different ways including Permafrost, Vaporize or Electro-Charged team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

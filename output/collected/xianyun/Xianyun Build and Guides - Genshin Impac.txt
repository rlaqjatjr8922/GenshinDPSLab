Xianyun's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Unlocks a new Plunge playstyle for many characters by increasing jump height and buffing Plunge DMG.
Strong healing allowing them to fit many roles Jean can play while also being on a guaranteed banner.
Weaknesses
High Energy Requirements when played without other Anemo characters.
C1 and C2 provide incredibly significant upgrades to her core functionality.

3.
Combos and How to Play Xianyun
Xianyun generally has quite low field-time only really requiring you
to use your Elemental Skill for Energy and then your Elemental Burst
afterwards so that your DPS can start dealing high amounts of Plunge DMG.
White Clouds at Dawn
is Xianyun's Elemental Skill.
When triggered, it uses the first instance of
Skyladder
and from there you can use 2 more times to gain additional height during
exploration, deal bigger Driftcloud Wave DMG, and also generate more Energy
on the plunge of the third use. Energy is only generated on the plunge not
on use of the skill.
Xianyun's Elemental Burst
Stars Gather at Dusk
is both
her healing as well as the primary way in which she supports the team. This
skill summons a
Starwicker
allowing you to
Plunge 8 times on any character to deal massive DMG.
4.
Xianyun's Best Team Comps
Xianyun brings an entirely new playstyle to many characters allowing DPS
characters to instead focus on Plunging instead of their main method of DPS.
This can be an overall DPS increase to the following types of characters:
Characters who already focus on Plunging such as
Varesa
,
Xiao
and
Gaming
;
Characters with high Plunge Attack scaling such as
Diluc
and
Razor
;
Characters who have or provide
Elemental Infusion
to themselves or the party.
For example
Bennett
,
Hu Tao
,
Chongyun
and
Furina
(C6) to name a few.
Additionally, Catalyst characters who have an AoE plunge such as
Yae Miko
.
The
Elemental Infusion
aspect is incredibly important because
Plunge Attacks have no
Internal Cooldown
as a result of
having longer animations meaning every
Plunge Attack infused with an element will deal Elemental DMG and always
react with any Elements already on an enemy.

Genshin Impact Xianyun Build: Best Artifacts and Weapons
Heizo
January 30, 2024
Tips
Share:
Xianyun Genshin Impact
, or
Cloud Retainer, is a
5-star Anemo female support character
. She excels at bringing in
plenty of utility from
plunging attack damage
and
attack boosts, and teamwide healing
in
Genshin Impact
. But, to harness the full spectrum of Xianyun's abilities and amplify the buff she provides,
targeting the right stats
when building her is recommended. Fortunately, this
Genshin Impact Xianyun build
is here to help you. For more information about Xianyun, you can learn more by visiting our Xianyun guide.
Genshin Impact Xianyun Build: Best Artifact Set
Xianyun's effectiveness hinges on scaling with
Attack stats
, making artifacts rich in this attribute optimal for her. But aside from it, one must also consider the rest of her kit to unleash her full potential as a support. Prioritize
enhancing her healing
,
attack stats
, and
energy recharge
for smoother rotations. While focusing on these stats is crucial, exploring alternative artifact sets also opens up possibilities to optimize Xianyun's role as an
Anemo buffer and healer
. Our Genshin Impact Xianyun Build suggests the best artifact set to use.
Name
Icon
Description
Viridescent Venerer
Viridescent Venerer is a staple to most anemo supports such as Xianyun for as long as she can swirl the element if the damage dealer. Having that said, equipping the 4-piece artifact set for an Anemo DPS such as Xiao may not be ideal.
Songs of the Days Past
Songs of the Days Past enhances Xianyun's healing ability. Moreover, aside from the innate buffs that Xianyun can offer, the passive of this artifact can allow her DPS to indulge in more damage buffs.
Noblesse Oblige
In general, Noblesse Oblige is a great artifact for Genshin Impact Xianyun Build, that supports can equip to boost the damage output of any ATK-dependent DPS. While the 2-pc effect does not have that much impact on her kit, the 4-pc kit enables her to bring additional attack buff to allies.
Vermillion Hereafter
Vermillion Hereafter is the best-in-slot artifact for C6 On-field DPS Xianyun. Using this set allows the Anemo character to stack up more ATK, therefore increasing the damage of each plunge attack she does.

newer players
who have recently acquired Cloud Retainer, consider utilizing these artifact sets from this
Genshin Impact Xianyun Build

Xianyun ascension materials
!

The Exile
The Exile set aims to help Xianyun cast her burst every rotation without encountering any issues. This also allows her to become a battery for her team, especially when paired with energy-hungry characters.
Brave Heart
Brave Heart is also can be a worthy alternative due to the 18% attack bonus it gives.
Xianyun Genshin Build: Best Weapon Set
Find all of her
best Cloud Retainer weapons
in this Genshin Impact Xianyun build. Ideally, weapons bearing the
attack, energy recharge attributes, and buffs
must be equipped to make her effective in her role.
Name
Icon
Description
Crane's Echoing Call
Without any question, Crane's Echoing Call is her best-in-slot weapon. Xianyun demands a hefty amount of attack and has a high burst energy cost, and both of these can be solved by considering pulling for this weapon. Furthermore, this weapon offers a plunge attack damage bonus to the active character, perfect for her (C6) and her DPS.
Skyward Atlas
The Skyward Atlas is Xianyun's second-best weapon if you want to maximize her ATK stats. This weapon boasts one of the highest base ATK among all catalysts, which means it can provide her with more attack stats, in addition to the secondary substats.
Memory of Dust
Apart from its substats that enhance attack, having a 5-star rating is a significant advantage for this weapon, as it comes with a high base attack. Although Xianyun doesn't benefit from the additional shield strength, the second component of this weapon can still be useful as it provides an ATK% bonus.
Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds
If you choose to use Xianyun as an on-field DPS, the Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds is a superb option. This weapon has a high base attack and is particularly useful if you need crit rate.
Genshin Impact Xianyun Build: Best Free-to-Play Weapons
Luckily for
Genshin Impact Xianyun Build
, she has plenty to choose from when it comes to weapon choices. If you barely have any 5-star weapon available for her, there are alternatives that can perform well, especially at higher refinements.
Name
Icon
Description
Oathsworn Eye
The Oathsworn Eye appears to be the ideal free-to-play weapon for Xianyun as if it is customized for her. This weapon provides Attack stats and enhances her Energy Recharge for a considerable amount of time. But, the Oathsworn is an event-specific weapon, which could be the reason why some players are unable to obtain it.
The Widsith
For C6 Xianyun, we recommended in this Genshin Impact Xianyun Build to give her a decent crit ratio to dish out consistent damage to the enemies. With that said, The Widsith could be one of the best 4-star options for her.
Prototype amber
The Prototype Amber provides Energy Recharge and healing, which are essential components for her kit.
Thrilling Tales of the Dragon Slayers
TTDS is the most free-to-play weapon for Xianyun as it is only a 3-star catalyst. After switching to another character, the active one will receive additional attack attributes from this weapon. However, this is only recommended if she has reached a good amount of ER and ATK. Since it is very easy to obtain, we recommend max refining it.

Popular Teams For Xianyun

Description
▼Xiao Hypercarry
★★★★★
- A very powerful team using Xiao and Furina with Xianyun.
- Greatly increases the DMG of Xiao using Xianyun and Faruzan.
- Since both Xiao and Xianyun are Anemo, they make their required Energy Recharge lighter.
▼Varesa Plunge Carry
★★★★・
- Varesa is also a DPS that focuses on Plunge Attacks.
- Because of lack of access to powerful reaction, the damage boost provided by Xianyun is amazing for Varesa.
▼ Vaporize Gaming
★★★・・
- A team using Gaming with Xianyun with Hydro characters to produce powerful Vaporize reaction.
- Gaming can reach top-class DPS level with Xianyun.
▼ Dragon Strike Diluc Team
★★★・・
- Team uses the "Dragonstrike" Attack of Diluc to produce high damage.
- Uses the high plunge attack of Diluc with Xianyun's skill to produce various reactions like Vaporize or Melt.

Xianyun will work as all-around unit in this team being a healer for the team. The whole team is centered on pushing Xiao as the main DPS, with Furina being the sub-DPS and DMG Buffer and Faruzan providing Anemo Resistance Shred and DMG Bonus at higher constellation.
Rotation Of Xiao Hypercarry Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start by using Furina's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to Faruzan and use Elemental Skill with charged attack then Elemental Burst.
3
Next, use Xianyun's Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Xiao and use his Elemental Skill twice then his Elemental Burst.
5
Spam Xiao's Plunge Attack until Elemental Burst expires.
6
Repeat from no. 1

Varesa is also an amazing Plunge DPS that works with Xianyun. While Varesa has other effective teams, Varesa's lack of access to Vaporize and Melt make Xianyun more valueable in her teams.
Rotation Of Varesa-Xianyun Teams
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina's Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to Xianyun and use Elemental Skill to get energy then Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Iansan and use Elemental Skill then Normal Attack then Elemental Burst.
4
Bring out Varesa use Elemental Skill → NA → Elemental Burst combo.
5
Continuously use Plunge attack until the buffs ran out and repeat from no.1
The point of the rotation is to release all the buffs before moving to Varesa. Since Xianyun releases Anemo when plunge happens, you can swirl Electro this way. You can also use Iansan first before Xianyun for early swirl.

Rotation Of Vaporize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina's Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to Bennett and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Xianyun and use Elemental Burst.
4
Bring out Gaming and use Elemental Skill, plunge and use Elemental Burst.
5
Continuously use Plunge attack while Elemental Burst is active.
Make sure to have all of the buffs out and Furina's skill before using Gaming's special Plunge attacks.

Dragon Strike Diluc is now possible with Xianyun on the team. With this, he will be able to consistently deal Pyro plunge attacks while also dealing Melt with Ganyu's Burst.
Rotation Of Dragon Strike Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Ganyu's Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst
2
Switch to Bennett and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Use Xianyun and use her Elemental Burst
4
Go with Diluc and use his Burst, then spam Plunge Damage until his Pyro infusion runs out.
It's important to only use Xianyun's Burst when you have Diluc's Burst active. This will ensure continuous Pyro application through his Pyro attacks.

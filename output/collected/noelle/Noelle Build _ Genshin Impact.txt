Noelle Overview
This Page provides the Best Noelle Build information.
Noelle is a maid in the service of the Knights of Favonius that dreams of joining their ranks someday.
Noelle is a Geo Main DPS or Sub DPS in Genshin Impact at the moment. Noelle DPS Build is mostly used by travelers. Her Elemental Skill can provide a shield to protect the team during the fight.
Noelle has different ways to help the party. Not only can protect the team by using the Elemental Skill, but her Elemental Burst also helps to deal AoE damage. DEF is the most important stats for Noelle, so the DPS Build will also help travelers to stack the DEF for Noelle.
In Genshin Impact, Noelle is a 4 Stars rarity Geo character currently in  tier.

Noelle is a 4-star Geo character in Genshin Impact who wields a Claymore. She is available to all players for free through the Beginner's Wish Banner. She can be played as an on-field DPS with solid personal damage output, while also providing the team with shields and healing. Alternatively, she can serve as a support by equipping the 4-piece Archaic Petra set to offer buffs to the entire team. Lore-wise, she is affiliated with the Knights of Favonius as a maid and resides in Mondstadt.

What Kind of Character is Noelle?
Noelle is an On-Field Geo DPS who uses her Elemental Burst to deal damage and provide teamwide healing.
She can use her Normal Attacks or Charged Attacks while in her Burst state to deal decent amounts of Geo damage over a wide AoE.
Noelle becomes a better main DPS with more investment into her Constellations, weapon, and supports.
Because she's a 4-star, her personal damage can be underwhelming in comparison to a 5-star DPS, but she can still deliver a decent performance if you have all the right pieces for it.

Noelle's Roles, Playstyle, Strengths, and Weaknesses
Noelle as a Geo DPS and Healer
Noelle is designed to be an on-field character, as her healing is tied to her attacks during her Elemental Burst. However, her ability to condense both damage and healing into a single character brings valuable flexibility to the team.
Pros and Cons of Geo DPS Noelle
Pros
- Her healing is team-wide, making her a good pick against enemies or mechanics that can drain the entire team's HP.
- Teammate options are very flexible.
Cons
- Her C6 is mandatory to deal viable damage.
- Requires
Redhorn Stonethresher
for her best performance.
- Has high Energy Recharge requirements despite being an on-field DPS, due to her poor Energy Particle generation.

Focus On Her ER Requirements
Most of Noelle's damage comes from her Elemental Burst, and due to her naturally low Energy generation, Noelle requires a lot of Energy Recharge in order to reliably get her Burst up every rotation, despite being an On-Field DPS. It's recommended to focus on reaching her desired ER stat first before moving on to other stats.

Noelle's Team Rotations and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for On-Field DPS Noelle
Basic Rotation For On-Field DPS Noelle
1. Activate your off-field supports' and sub-DPS' abilities, from the longest to shortest duration.
2. Use Noelle's Elemental Skill, then her Elemental Burst.
3. Use Normal Attack 3 times, then dash to cancel the final attack's animation. Repeat this process until your support abilities expire. You can also hold her Normal Attack to perform a spinning Charged Attack, which is a viable playstyle at high investment.
4. Repeat from step 1.
Noelle's gameplay boils down to utilizing her Normal Attacks while her Burst is active. Dashing after the 3rd Normal Attack allows you to avoid the longer animations, but holding down her Normal Attack to spam her spinning Charged Attack is also an option, especially against groups of enemies or in multi-target situations.
Basics of Using Noelle (Beginner Guide)
Flexible On-Field Geo DPS
Noelle is a Geo On-Field Claymore DPS whose Elemental Burst is not only the main source of her damage, but also provides teamwide healing that lets her flex into a lot of comps that don't have strict teambuilding requirements.
DPS That Can Sustain with Burst
Noelle's Elemental Burst is her primary source of damage,
but her ability to sustain the entire team while in her Burst state showcases her versatility. However, because she doesn't generate Energy particles easily, giving her enough Energy Recharge via artifacts is essential for her personal damage. Additionally, her Burst is one of the few special states that doesn't expire or deactivate when you switch to another character.
Skill Activates a Shield
The shield from her Skill further increases her durability, allowing her to use her Normal Attack string without being interrupted. She generally doesn't require another shielder on the team, but she can still work well with them.
Converts DEF Into ATK
Her Elemental Burst increases her ATK by a percentage of her DEF upon activation. However, you'll still want to prioritize building DEF artifacts, especially if you have C6, though any ATK% substats are still usable.
Best At C6
With her C6, Noelle's Burst duration extends for every enemy defeated, while also increasing the DEF to ATK conversion.
This is her strongest Constellation by far and is necessary for her to reach her highest potential as an On-Field DPS.

Basic Rotation Of Noelle

A lot of Noelle's intended role on the team is fulfilled while her Elemental Burst is active, so ensuring that she's able to use it every rotation is imperative for her DPS.

- Creates a Geo Shield that does damage upon activation.

This Skill doesn't generate Energy particles upon activation, which is unlike a majority of the playable characters' abilities, and thus contributes to Noelle's high energy requirements.

C6 Greatly Improves Her DPS
Icon
Constellation 6 Description
Must Be Spotless
Sweeping Time increases Noelle's ATK by an additional 50% of her DEF. Additionally, every opponent defeated during the skill's duration adds 1s to the duration, up to 10s.
Noelle's C6 bumps up the percentage of her DEF that she converts after using her Burst, which boosts her damage potential by a substantial amount. The Burst duration increase is also highly appreciated since Noelle is heavily dependent on her Burst to deal damage.
Should You Pull for Noelle's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim for C6 if You Can
Constellation Priority
C6 > C5 > C3
C6 Greatly Increases Noelle's Damage Potential
Noelle's effectiveness as an on-field Geo carry heavily depends on her Constellation level.
Besides upgrading her weapon, your main priority should be getting her to C6. This provides a significant damage boost to her Burst by adding an extra DEF to ATK conversion and allowing her to extend its duration. The damage difference between a C0 and C6 Noelle is substantial, making it a worthwhile investment if you're dedicated to maximizing her potential.
If you plan to use Noelle mainly for her shield and healing, her C1 is a good stopping point. It guarantees that her attacks heal while her shield is active, unlike at C0 where healing is based on chance. However,
reaching C6 is the ideal goal to make her a powerful on-field Geo carry who can also provide team-wide healing. If you intend to use her in this role, prioritize obtaining her C6 when possible.
Keep in mind that as a 4-star character, it can be difficult to guarantee obtaining her from pulls.
It's more efficient to pull for her constellations when she is featured on a banner with a 5-star or other 4-star characters you also want.
Always consider your options carefully before pulling.

Devotion
Effect
When Noelle is in the party but not on the field, this ability triggers automatically when your active character's HP falls below 30%:
・Creates a shield for your active character that lasts for 20s and absorbs DMG equal to 400% of Noelle's DEF.
・The shield has a 150% DMG Absorption effectiveness against all Elemental and Physical DMG.
This effect can only occur once every 60s.
Nice And Clean
Effect
Every 4 Normal or Charged Attack hits will decrease the CD of Breastplate by 1s. Hitting multiple opponents with a single attack is only counted as 1 hit.
Maid's Knighthood
Effect
When a Perfect Cooking is achieved on a DEF-boosting dish, Noelle has a 12% chance to obtain double the product.
Who Is Noelle?
A Maid Who Wants To Be A Knight
Noelle is a maid at the Knights of Favonius headquarters with aspirations to become a knight one day. Jean, the acting Grand Master, knows she's strong enough to be one but doesn't knight her due to her inexperience.
Doesn't Know Her Own Strength
Noelle's physical strength is outstanding as she's able to break even the weapons that she wields. However, it's her inexperience that's holding her back.

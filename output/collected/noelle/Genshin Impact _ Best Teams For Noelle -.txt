Noelle Works Best As Hybrid DPS & Healer
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Lunar Reaction Noelle Team
★★★★★
- Pairs Noelle with the strong
Ineffa
and
Columbina
core that deal a substantial amount of DPS by themselves.
- Linnea also further increases the DPS potential of the team thanks to her personal damage.
- Noelle is a decent On-Fielder for the Ineffa-Columbina pairing since she's very durable with her healing and self-shielding, and her Burst AoE complements the multi-target nature of the Lunar-Charge reaction.
▼ Noelle-Furina Team
★★★★☆
- The
Marechaussee Hunter
set can greatly increase Noelle's damage thanks to the generous amount of CRIT Rate it provides.
- Furina's HP drain enables the Marechaussee 4-piece set effect, while Noelle's healing on her Burst also ensures you quickly build up Furina's Fanfare stacks as well.
▼ Double Geo Team
★★★☆☆
- Noelle appreciates having another Geo teammate to help her generate Energy to keep using her Elemental Burst, which is the bulk of her damage.
- She can be played as the sole DPS supported by a Geo buffer, or alongside another flexible Geo DPS character, like
Navia
, in a 'Quickswap' kind of playstyle. Both are viable ways of running Double Geo Noelle teams.
▼ Triple Geo Team
★★★☆☆
- Similar to the Double Geo team, Noelle's flexibility lets her run a team comp with three Geo teammates in it without it hindering how her abilities work.
- She can also be played Quickswap here with Navia, provided there is at least one other PHEC (Pyro, Hydro, Electro, Cryo) teammate in the team, or alongside 2 other Geo buffers or Off-Field Sub-DPSes.
▼ Solo Geo Team
★★☆☆☆
- Noelle can also be used as a flex unit in other teams where she's the only Geo character, preferably as an On-Field DPS.
- Because she has no Geo teammate, her Energy needs will be a lot higher, which can lower DPS due to requiring more Energy Recharge rolls on your artifacts over offensive stats like CRIT.
Noelle is a decent On-Field DPS character for new players due to her ease of use and flexible teammate options. She can easily cover your Geo DPS needs for pre-endgame content, and has ways to increase her DPS potential through vertical investment into her constellations, her teammates' constellations, and 5-star weapons.

There's a lot of inherent synergy between Furina and Noelle due to Furina's fanfare stacks, which is where her DMG% buffs come from, requiring constant HP fluctuation to stack faster. Her Skill has a teamwide HP% drain effect, and when paired with Noelle's teamwide healing on Burst, it makes for an efficient way of building Furina's fanfare stacks.
This team also makes use of the Moondrifts created by the
Lunar-Crystallize
reaction. They deal Lunar-Crystallize damage at an interval dependent on the rate of Geo application made by any teammate, and Noelle can quickly trigger the Moondrifts' joint attack using her Elemental Burst.

Every hit from Noelle's Elemental Burst will apply Geo, which allows her to generate a decent amount of Crystallized Shards to enable Navia's stronger Skill. At the same time, Navia can drop a lot of Energy particles to fuel Noelle's Burst, further facilitating their ability to work in tandem, especially since Noelle's Burst doesn't end after you swap out of her.

Most teams in this archetype orient Noelle towards an On-Field Driver role, whose attacks are mostly there to activate key support abilities rather than to contribute the most damage.

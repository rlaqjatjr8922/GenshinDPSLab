Character Overview
Playstyles
Noelle should be played as an on-field unit. She excels in multi-target content but is still viable in single-target scenarios.
On-Field DPS
Noelle is a good on-field damage dealer. After using her Skill and Burst, she provides strong personal damage and teamwide healing with her Geo-converted Normal Attack Talent. The cooldown of her DEF-scaling shield decreases by 1s with every 4th Normal Attack Talent hit, which is enough to maintain longer uptime in most on-field teams.
Noelle can freely swap in and out without losing the Geo Conversion granted by her Burst, so teammates can swap in to refresh their abilities. Some of her on-field teams could be classified as “quickswap” for this reason.
Off-Field Support
Noelle should not be played as an off-field support. She requires field time to heal her teammates and her shield only has 50% uptime in an off-field playstyle. If you already have a dedicated on-field unit in your team, opt for a different support over Noelle.
Talents
Level and Talent Priority
Level 90 > Normal Attack = Burst > Skill
You should raise Noelle to Level 90 to maximize her Base DEF. Level her Normal Attack and Burst equally since they account for all of her personal damage. Her Skill can be leveled to increase her healing and shielding effectiveness.

Elemental Skill
Elemental Skill
|
Breastplate
Talent Description
Summons protective stone armor, dealing Geo DMG to surrounding opponents and creating a shield. The shield’s DMG Absorption scales based on Noelle’s DEF.
The shield has the following properties:
When Noelle’s Normal and Charged Attacks hit a target, they have a certain chance to regenerate HP for all characters.
Possesses 150% DMG Absorption efficiency against all Elemental and Physical DMG.
The amount of HP healed when regeneration is triggered scales based on Noelle’s DEF.
Noelle’s Elemental Skill creates a Geo shield, but it doesn’t generate Geo Particles. Noelle can maintain high shield uptime with the help of her A4 Passive. While the shield is active, Noelle’s Normal Attacks have a chance to heal the team.
If you have C4 Noelle, stay close to enemies to ensure that they get hit by the shield expiration damage.
Elemental Burst
Elemental Burst
|
Sweeping Time
Talent Description
Gathering the strength of stone around her weapon, Noelle strikes the opponents surrounding her within a large AoE, dealing Geo DMG.
Afterwards, Noelle gains the following effects:
Larger attack AoE.
Converts attack DMG to Geo DMG that cannot be overridden by any other elemental infusion.
Increased ATK that scales based on her DEF.
Noelle’s Elemental Burst infuses her Normal Attack Talent with Geo and increases her ATK stat based on her DEF stat. Her Geo-infused Normal Attacks have a significantly longer range than her Physical Normal Attacks.
Ascension 1 Passive
Ascension 1 Passive
|
Devotion
Talent Description
When Noelle is in the party but not on the field, this ability triggers automatically when your active character’s HP falls below 30%:
Creates a shield for your active character that lasts for 20s and absorbs DMG equal to 400% of Noelle’s DEF.
The shield has a 150% DMG Absorption effectiveness against all Elemental and Physical DMG.
This effect can only occur once every 60s.
Noelle’s A1 Passive is mostly useless since her Normal Attacks’ healing during her shield duration prevents teammates from falling below 30% Max HP. Nonetheless, it can be a lifesaver if you somehow wind up in that situation in Spiral Abyss.

Nice and Clean
Talent Description
Every 4 Normal or Charged Attack hits will decrease the CD of Breastplate by 1s.

Noelle’s A4 Passive allows her Normal or Charged Attacks to reduce her Skill cooldown by 1s for every 4th hit. This is valuable in maintaining shield and Geo Resonance uptime in teams without another shielder.

Artifact Sets
Recommended Sets
4pc Husk of Opulent Dreams
4pc Husk of Opulent Dreams is the best general use set.
If Noelle has Serpent Spine equipped, prioritize the weapon stacks over this 4pc set bonus. Otherwise, Noelle needs to remain off-field for 12s to fully stack the 4pc set bonus.
4pc Marechaussee Hunter
4pc Marechaussee Hunter is the best set to use when Noelle is paired with Furina.
Other Sets
4pc Gladiator’s Finale
4pc Gladiator’s Finale is a good, standard option since you likely have good pieces from farming Bosses.
4pc Retracing Bolide
4pc Retracing Bolide performs just slightly worse than 4pc Gladiator’s Finale. It is not worth farming.
2pc Mixed Sets
2pc Bonuses: Husk of Opulent Dreams, Archaic Petra, Marechaussee Hunter, ATK%
These are transitional set options while you farm for 4pc Husk of Opulent Dreams or Marechaussee Hunter.
4pc Archaic Petra
4pc Archaic Petra is only used when Noelle is an on-field driver and healer, not a damage dealer. This set is a significant reduction to her personal damage. Noelle must pick up the Elemental Shard from Crystallize to grant the corresponding Elemental DMG% buff.
This is most notably used in a team with Furina, Yelan, and Xingqiu.
Weapons
Redhorn Stonethresher
Redhorn Stonethresher is Noelle’s Best-in-Slot weapon.
Serpent Spine
Serpent Spine is Noelle’s second-best option overall. It performs well at R1 and benefits from refinements. If Noelle has 4pc Husk of Opulent Dreams, it is better to prioritize Serpent Spine stacks over the 4pc set bonus in the first rotation.
Verdict
Verdict is a good option for Noelle; however, it is better used on Navia if you only have one copy. Verdict is not worth pulling just for Noelle.
Beacon of the Reed Sea
Beacon of the Reed Sea performs on par with Verdict. Its passive applies to Noelle’s first 6 Normal Attacks after using her Skill or after her shield pops from her C4.

Whiteblind
Whiteblind is Noelle’s best free option. Tidal Shadow is just 2–3% worse, so feel free to use it if you already crafted it for another Claymore user.
Skyward Pride
Skyward Pride is a good option if Noelle has high ER requirements (around 140% or higher). It performs on par with Whiteblind at low ER requirements.
Wolf’s Gravestone, The Unforged
These 5-star Claymores perform worse than R5 Whiteblind, so they aren’t worth using on Noelle. You could use them if you already have them, but Whiteblind is more consistent.
Favonius Greatsword
Favonius Greatsword may be necessary in Solo Geo teams where Noelle has incredibly high ER requirements. Although this comes at a cost to her personal DPS, it allows her to remain a viable driver in such teams.

Teambuilding
Flexibility
: Noelle’s flexible field time allows her to work with a variety of characters. Units who can refresh their abilities or have multiple charges can freely swap in without Noelle losing her Geo Conversion.
Geo Teammates:
At least one Geo teammate is recommended in Noelle’s most recommended team archetypes to lower Noelle’s ER requirements and enable Geo Resonance.
Furina:
Furina is one of Noelle’s premier non-Geo teammates. Her DMG% buff is extremely potent and Noelle easily heals back Furina’s teamwide HP drain.

Geo
Gorou
Gorou is an exceptional teammate for Noelle in Triple Geo teams. His DEF buff and additional buff at C6 are incredibly potent. He also is a great battery for Noelle.
Chiori
Chiori is an excellent off-field Geo teammate for Noelle due to her high damage output and Geo Particle generation. While C0 Chiori generally appreciates teammates like Albedo, Zhongli, or Geo Traveler who can create Geo Constructs, she is still great even in Noelle’s teams without them.
Albedo
Albedo is a great off-field Geo damage dealer who can slightly lower Noelle’s ER requirements. His Geo Construct lasts the entire rotation, but it can be broken by heavy enemies and bosses, so be careful with placement. Albedo can always swap in to refresh his Skill, however.
Navia
Navia is a great quickswap partner for Noelle. Noelle’s flexibility allows Navia to use her Skill up to three times per rotation. Navia also generates a good amount of Geo Particles to lower ER requirements.
Zhongli
Zhongli provides a supplemental shield and 20% Universal RES Shred. Zhongli has poor particle generation, so he is usually the third or fourth Geo teammate.
Geo Traveler
Geo Traveler is a free-to-play option who batteries Noelle and deals decent damage. Their Geo Constructs may occasionally interfere with Noelle’s mobility, so careful placement is crucial to avoid griefing yourself.
Ningguang
Ningguang can generate Geo Particles, give a Geo DMG Bonus, and buff Noelle’s ATK with Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers.
At C2, Ningguang can use her Skill twice per rotation, but you must wait at least 4s between uses to generate Geo Particles due to her Skill’s particle ICD.
Yun
Jin
Yun Jin provides an Additive Base DMG Bonus to Noelle’s Normal Attacks for up to 30 instances of damage. As such, she is only recommended in scenarios with 3 or fewer enemies to avoid consuming this quota too quickly.
Hydro
Furina
Furina is one of Noelle’s best non-Geo teammates. She provides good personal damage and a strong DMG% buff, but she also drains the team’s HP. Noelle takes good advantage of Furina’s buff and her healing can counteract the teamwide HP drain.
Yelan
Yelan is a strong off-field DPS unit who also provides a ramping DMG% buff. Noelle’s flexibility allows Yelan to perform her Skill twice per rotation, lowering her ER requirements.
Xingqiu
Xingqiu is another strong off-field DPS unit who provides additional defensive utility with his damage reduction (that increases the durability of Noelle’s shield).
Candace
Candace provides a Normal Attack DMG% buff, but she has poor damage output and high ER requirements. She is not usually recommended over other options.
Electro
Fischl
Fischl is a great off-field, single-target DPS unit who slightly reduces the team’s ER requirements. Fischl can freely swap in to refresh Oz thanks to Noelle’s flexibility. Noelle is also a great driver for Fischl’s C6.
Yae Miko
Yae is a potent off-field DPS unit who can use her Burst and refresh her totems more freely in teams with Noelle. However, she has lower particle generation and higher ER requirements compared to Fischl.
Beidou
Noelle is a good driver for Beidou’s Burst in scenarios with 2–3 enemies. Beidou should be paired with Fischl in Double Geo teams since her ER requirements are quite high otherwise.
Lisa
Lisa provides enemy DEF Shred with her Burst and A4 Passive and can buff Noelle’s ATK with Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers and 4pc Noblesse Oblige. However, her damage is on the low side and she has high ER requirements, so Lisa is only recommended alongside Fischl in Double Geo teams.
Pyro
Bennett
Bennett provides a Flat ATK buff via his Burst; however, Noelle already has a good DEF-to-ATK conversion, so she benefits less from Bennett’s buff than other DPS units. Nonetheless, ATK-scaling teammates like Navia, Fischl, or Xiangling also benefit from Bennett in Double Geo teams.
Bennett does restrict Noelle’s mobility, however, since Noelle must stay within the AoE of Bennett’s Burst to benefit from his buff. Additionally, his buff expires prior to Noelle finishing her combo in longer rotations.
Xiangling
Xiangling is a good off-field DPS unit that can be used in Double Geo teams with Bennett.
Other
Xianyun
Xianyun allows Noelle to become a Plunging Attack DPS unit with her Geo Conversion. Further testing is underway!
Rosaria
Rosaria can provide a CRIT Rate buff and decent off-field Cryo damage in a limited AoE. Favonius Lance and 4pc Noblesse Oblige is a good support build for her with Noelle.
Triple Geo
Noelle — Gorou (C6) — Geo — Flex
This is a strong Noelle team that capitalizes on Gorou’s buffs, Geo Resonance, and Geo Particles (to lower ER requirements). The flex slot is usually a Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, or Electro character to enable Crystallize, with Furina being the best option.
Example Teams
This is not a comprehensive list of teams. The inclusion or exclusion of any given team does not necessarily reflect its power level.
Noelle — Gorou — Albedo — Furina
A fantastic Noelle team. Gorou and Furina buff Noelle and Albedo’s damage. You can also play Chiori instead of Albedo.
Sample Rotation
Gorou EQ > Albedo E > Furina ED Q > Noelle E Q N3D N3 > Gorou E > Noelle 3[N3D] N2
Noelle — Gorou — Albedo — Chiori
A very solid Mono Geo Noelle team. Albedo allows Chiori to spawn 2 Tamoto dolls.
Sample Rotation
Gorou EQ > Albedo E > Chiori (Q) 2[E] > Noelle E Q N3D N3 > Gorou E > Noelle 3[N3D] N2
Noelle — Gorou — Navia — Furina
Another good Noelle team where she and Navia share field time in a quickswap playstyle.
Sample Rotation
Furina ED > Navia Q > Gorou EQ > Furina Q > Noelle E Q N1 > Navia E > Noelle N3D N3 > Gorou E > Navia E > Noelle N3D N3 > Navia E
Noelle — Gorou — Geo Traveler — Zhongli
A full Geo team where Zhongli provides a supplemental shield and Geo RES Shred. Geo Traveler serves as a good battery for Noelle and their Geo Constructs can resonate with Zhongli’s pillar for additional incidental damage. Albedo also works instead of Geo Traveler.
Sample Rotation
Zhongli hED > Gorou EQ > Traveler EQ E > Noelle E Q N3D N3 > Gorou E > Noelle 2[N3D] N3 > Traveler E > Noelle N3
Noelle — Gorou — Yun Jin — Fischl
An accessible 4-star–only team. Yun Jin provides an Additive Base DMG Bonus to Noelle’s Normal Attacks. Fischl deals damage off-field and enables Crystallize.
Sample Rotation
Fischl E > Gorou Q E > Yun Jin Q > Noelle E Q N3D N4 > Fischl Q > Gorou E > Noelle 2[N3D] N3 > Yun Jin E > Noelle N3

Noelle — Navia — Fischl — Furina
A great team for Noelle that has potential for front-loaded and sustained damage in a variety of enemy scenarios. This team frequently swaps between teammates.

This team takes advantage of Hydro Resonance to increase Yelan and Furina’s damage output. Yelan and Furina both provide a DMG% buff that benefits Noelle while she’s on-field. This team allows Yelan to use her Skill twice per rotation. You can also play Albedo instead of Chiori.

A free-to-play–friendly team that capitalizes on Pyro Resonance and Bennett’s Flat ATK buff. Xiangling provides good AoE damage.

Solo Geo
Noelle — Flex — Flex — Flex
In Solo Geo teams, Noelle serves as an on-field driver with incredibly high ER requirements. She can drive a Mono Element team, a Burgeon team, or even a Hyperbloom team. Favonius Greatsword and 4pc Archaic Petra are recommended here.

Noelle — Xingqiu — Yelan — Furina

Noelle’s healing improves survivability in Raiden’s Hyperbloom teams, and her on-field flexibility allows Nahida to recast her Skill for new waves of enemies. However, while Noelle is viable, other units generally perform better, including but not limited to Baizhu, Yelan, and Beidou.

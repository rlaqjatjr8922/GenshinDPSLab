Noelle Guide and Best Builds
Noelle is a 4-star Geo character that wields a Claymore to crush her enemies.
She has team-wide healing, shield capabilities, and with (C6) some
truly powerful AoE hits while being swapped in and out of battle with some very
flexible team options.

1.
Noelle On-field DPS
Noelle's is extremely flexible in what she can bring to a team, playing the
role of both on-field and off-field play styles that relay on similar setups.
Her claymore usage provides her with strong AoE attacks, buffed by her shields
and great DEF/HP. However, she does struggle with energy generation so pairing
her with another teammate that can produce lots of energy particles quickly is
ideal. While Noelle has some off-field support abilities they are very lackluster
and she is much stronger played as an on-field damage dealer, even without her
Must Be Spotless
(C6) constellation.

2.
Combos and How to Play Noelle
Noelle is best played as a strong character swap for clashing with the
enemies on the front line. She provides healing through her elemental skill
Breastplate
that can cause her normal and charged attacks to
have a chance to heal. This ability also grants her high amounts of damage
absorption and a shield based upon her DEF stat. Her DPS really shines after
reaching
Must Be Spotless
(C6), altering her Elemental Burst
Sweeping Time
to scale additionally from her DEF and add up to 10 seconds of duration.
This ability can utterly crush mobs in a single strike and is absolutely
awesome to see in action with the right buffers.
While Noelle's skills do offer some ability to play as an off-fielder,
it is not recommended due to how little your team benefits from her
abilities. There are simply much better choices for support characters. Overall,
she is brawler at heart with a skill kit that compliments that very well. Even
without her C6 constellation, Noelle is a very tanky and useful brawler that can
excel in the right environment and team composition. Her great survivability and
powerful Elemental Burst help her standout as great on-field character.
3.
Noelle's Best Team Comps
Noelle is somewhat limited for team compositions, being forced to focus on the
right
Elemental Reactions
or go full
Geo
which can be very difficult against
certain elemental shields. However, the potential teams she can partner with
are very simple to play and can be quite powerful. With that being said we
have chosen our team builds based upon character availability (4 stars vs 5 stars),
overall damage potential and best conditions for Noelle to shine.

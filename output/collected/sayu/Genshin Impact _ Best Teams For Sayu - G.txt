Sayu Is Great As Swirl Support & Healer
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Swirl Support
- Swirl support is the easiest build for Sayu.
- VV for Elemental DMG Res or Gilded Dreams if another character is holding Scroll set.
- C6 Sayu can be built on EM instead of both ATK% and EM.
2nd
▼ Taser On-Field Driver
- Ororon can apply Electro and charge Sayu's Burst with his Passive's ability.
- Furina is the Hydro sub-DPS with good Hydro application.
- EM Sayu can lack healing pre-C6 so Shinobu is used for additional healing and she can also trigger Ororon's Hypersense follow-up attack.
3rd
▼ Vapemelt Driver
- Premium team using three (3) 5-star characters.
- Pyro Traveler can be used instead of Mavuika.
- Utilizes Citlali's unique RES shred debuff with 3 PECH sub-DPS dealing most of the damage.
Sayu is an Anemo support who can heal with her Burst and when she triggers Swirl. However, her scaling pre-C6 is both ATK% and EM. She can be used as a heal support or on-field driver thanks to her unique hold Skill mobility.

Sayu as a Swirl support can use VV or healing artifact set. Arlecchino is the DPS for this team with Zhongli as shield support and Yelan as sub-DPS.
Rotation Of Swirl Support
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Zhongli and hold his Skill for a shield
2
Switch to Arlecchino and use her Skill to mark enemies
3
Switch to Sayu and use her Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Yelan and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch back to Arlecchino and use her Charged Attack to gain Pyro infusion
6
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage before ending her turn by using her Burst
7
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire
Be careful not touse Sayu's hold Skill because it will make her enter Rolling mode. But it can be easily canceled by switching to another character.

With Furina and Ororon as the dedicated sub-DPS applying Hydro and Electro, Sayu can comfortably trigger Electro-Charged through Swirl. Shinobu is there for additional healing and more Electro application. Ororon and the Electro resonance can easily charge Sayu's Burst, reducing her ER requirements.
Rotation Of Taser On-Field Driver
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Shinobu and use her Skill
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Sayu and use her Burst then hold her Skill to start attacking enemies
5
Roll around the enemies to trigger Swirl for Electro-Charged chain reaction
6
Repeat from 1

A premium team consisting of three (3) 5-star characters. Citlali's Hydro & Pyro RES shred is used to increase the damage dealt by Mavuika and Furina. Sayu is used for healing and triggering Swirl for spreading affected Elements.
Rotation Of Vapemelt Driver
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst to gain Nightsoul points
3
Switch to Mavuika and use her Skill
4
Switch to Sayu and use her Burst then hold her Skill to enter Rolling mode
5
Roll around enemies to trigger Swirl
6
Repeat from 1
You can use Mavuika's Burst on her turn to buff Sayu. However, her Burst is unlikely to be at full charge after one (1) rotation so it's not recommended to use.

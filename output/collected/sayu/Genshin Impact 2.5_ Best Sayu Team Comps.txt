Sayu is a 4 star Anemo Claymore character
in Genshin Impact. While her kit is pretty decent, she doesn't have any niche that other characters cannot do. However, with the right lineup, players can definitely run Sayu as a healer/support while dealing decent damage as a sub DPS.
In this article, Gurugamer is going to showcase the best Sayu team comps for sub DPS and Support.

Sayu is amongst the least popular characters in Genshin.
1. High-End Sayu Team Comps
Sayu's biggest strength is her consistent Anemo procs alongside a decent healing ability. With EM as her hidden stat, she can serve as a battery for quite a few characters. Furthermore, her kit's decent AoE can generate a lot of particles and energy. This makes Sayu much easier to play and gear compared to other supports.
Sayu's weakness is probably her 80 cost Burst and a rather clunky Elemental Skill. She can be interrupted or knocked out of rolling. Additionally, she also needs at least C1 to be an effective healer.

Xiao is the main DPS of this comp, with plunge attacks dealing wide AOE Anemo DMG. This team builds around Xiao's Elemental Burst, which allows him to do consecutive plunge attacks. Sayu serves as a battery, allowing Xiao to use his Elemental Burst as soon as possible.
Chongyun’s Elemental Skill (requires C2) lower CDs of Xiao's Elemental Burst. He also deals extra Cryo damage backstage.
Zhongli is the flex pick of the team. His sturdy shield allows players to fight constantly without having to worry about HP.
Xiao is the most powerful Anemo DPS who can carry the team by himself.

This is a f2p friendly lineup, with all characters being 4 stars. This team focuses on off-field abilities while rolling around with Sayu E to proc massive reaction damage. Beidou can be used as the main DPS when bursts and Sayu E are on CD.
Xiangling’s Elemental Burst provides a huge amount of DMG and continuous Pyro element attachment for Sayu to trigger Anemo Swirl. Bennett provides the whole team with Pyro Resonance and healing when needed. Players can replace Bennett with a Cryo enabler like Kaeya for Melt damage triggers.
Xiangling is a cheap source of Pyro damage without the need of heavy investment.

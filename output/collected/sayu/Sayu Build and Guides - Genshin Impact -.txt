Sayu's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Heals and deals damage simultaneously with her Elemental Burst.
Great mobility with her E skill (rolling dash).
Strong support for both healing and Swirl reactions.
Passive ability provides extra healing when triggering Swirl.
Weaknesses
Before C6 can be tricky to distribute both ATK and EM required.
Limited damage compared to other Anemo Characters.
Needs high Energy Recharge for frequent Burst uptime.

as her BiS weapon.
You will need to build some CRIT rate for it however. This weapon will help her

Recommended Endgame Build Stats for Sayu
Build recommendations assume a "decently built" level of artifact rolls alongside 
Sayu's best weapon. Using other weapons may increase or decrease certain stats relative to these recommendations.
HP:
15,000 - 20,000
ATK:
1200 - 1400
CRIT Rate (if Favonius):
40 - 60%
EM:
+500
ER:
160 - 200%
Sayu requires a lot of Energy Recharge for this build: you will want to aim for 
at least 200 %+ if you want to use her Burst every rotation. Her C4 will lower it to 160-180 %.
Favonius or another
Anemo
character will lower it more. 
Before C6, you will have to balance her ATK and EM: her heal scales on ATK, and her swirl damage on EM.
After C6, her heal will also scale with EM, making it easier for you.
3.
Sayu Combos and How to Play
Sayu's burst
Yoohoo Art: Mujina Flurry
as a support build is what you will want to use the most. You will 
deal
Anemo
damage to nearby enemies, and heal your allies.
Sayu is not very hard to play. Your role with her will be to swap her in once you applied some elements
so she swirl and spread it, you can then use your Burst to heal if needed or deal extra Swirl damage.
The optimal rotation for Support Sayu is:
Swap on her after applying one or multiple other Elements to the enemies
Use
Yoohoo Art: Fuuin Dash
to Swirl
Use
Yoohoo Art: Mujina Flurry
to heal or deal additional DMG
Swap back to a DPS

Note:
All teams listed here are based on characters with
Constellation 2 or lower
. High-const setups are not discussed in this article.
1. Skirk + Furina + Charlotte + (Mona/Yelan/Shenhe)
Skirk’s base stats are extremely high. Her best team revolves around
Furina and Charlotte
, who not only greatly boost Skirk’s damage but also deal substantial sub-DPS themselves, making the overall team DPS very impressive. This "Skirk-Furi-Charlotte" trio alone can clear all content except Cryo-immune enemies.
For the fourth slot, you can pick according to preference or availability:
Mona
for big burst damage in an ultimate-focused playstyle
Yelan
for smoother rotations and consistent output
Shenhe
for more stable overall Cryo damage
2. Marvicca + Signora + Cythria + Bennett
Marvicca remains the strongest DPS character in Genshin to date. Her high stats and solid mechanics make the “
Natlan Battleship
” team one of the most powerful in the game.
Marvicca’s explosive damage, enhanced by
Signora
’s buff/debuff support and
Cythria
’s Cryo application + resistance shred, can create devastating output. This team currently represents the ceiling of Pyro team performance.
3. Neuvillette + Furina + Sigewinne + Wriothesley
Known as the "
Fontaine Battleship
," this team brings together some of the game’s strongest supports around Neuvillette, who himself has top-tier solo performance.
Whether you’re pushing high-difficulty content or just daily farming, this team is brain-dead simple, highly adaptable, and extremely effective—unless you're facing Hydro immunity, it's often the best answer.
4. Arlecchino Teams (Arlecchino + Bennett variants):
Arlecchino + Sigewinne + Wriothesley + Bennett
(pure Pyro)
Arlecchino + Yelan + Zhongli + Bennett
(Vape variant)
Arlecchino + Signora + Cythria + Bennett
(Melt variant)
Arlecchino is another powerhouse from Fontaine, boasting both high stats and strong mechanics. She allows for flexible team-building, especially when paired with
Bennett
.
You can opt for:
Pure Pyro comp for consistent damage
Vape comp for stronger reactions
Melt comp for the highest ceiling (with the right hand skill)
These variants cover multiple shield-breaking requirements and all offer solid performance and comfort.
5. Valesa Overload Team
Valesa’s best team in terms of cost-performance is
Valesa + Marvicca + Chevreuse + Emilie
. While this team may not reach the extreme power level of the four listed above, it deserves a top 5 spot due to its
excellent value
.
In a meta where most top teams cost 3-4 five-star units, this “Overload Battleship” needs only 2—or even 1 if you swap Furina for Xiangling—yet still outputs solid damage.
This comp maximizes
Chevreuse
’s amplification, while
Emilie
supports both Pyro and Electro units. The synergy between them creates a powerful, low-investment option.
And that’s a wrap for the top 5 teams in Genshin Impact Version 5.7!
If you’re lucky enough to own these teams, congratulations — you’re already among the winners.

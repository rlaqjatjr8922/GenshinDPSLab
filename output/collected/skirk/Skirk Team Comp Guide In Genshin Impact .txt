Since she needs Hydro and Cryo characters in her team, Skirk teams might face some limitations in
Genshin Impact
. On the other hand, there are a lot of Cryo and Hydro units that can join Skirk teams. So, here’s a list of the
best teams for Skirk in
Genshin Impact
.

The teams listed below are not in any particular order.
1
Skirk + Escoffier + Furina + Ayato
Ayato
’s
Elemental Burst in
Genshin Impact
continuously applies Hydro in a large area, even when he’s off-field. Not only that, but Ayato’s garden of purity increases Skirk’s Normal Attack DMG by 20% with a near 100% uptime, which is more than enough when considering the team’s rotation.
Furina
also helps with the Hydro application while she’s notably buffing the entire team’s DMG.
Of course, to pull Furina’s buff, players need a healer in this Skirk team, which is
Escoffier
. She’s not only a sufficient healer who quickly meets Furina’s requirements, but
Escoffier also greatly reduces enemies’ resistance
to Hydro and Cryo while dealing a lot of off-field Cryo DMG.
Players can also replace Ayato with
Shenhe
to team three strong Supports with Skirk, or
Mona
to hold Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers. Even
Mualani
can help Skirk if she holds the Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City artifact. Basically, the fourth slot is flexible; as long as the team has only Cryo and Hydro buffers and debuffers, it will maximize Skirk’s buffs from her kit and allow her to deal maximum DMG.
Team Roles
Skirk:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
Escoffier:
Support, Sub-DPS, Cryo Enabler, Debuffer, Healer
Furina:
Support, Sub-DPS, Hydro Enabler, Buffer, Healer
Ayato:
Sub-DPS, Hydro Enabler, Buffer
2
Skirk + Citlali + Shenhe + Yelan
To add more variety and showcase how easy it is to build a Skirk team in
Genshin Impact
, this team will have three different Hydro and Cryo characters. In this team, Skirk’s Normal Attacks also trigger
Yelan
’s coordinated Hydro Attacks, which create an endless amount of Frozen reactions. Although
Yelan is the only Hydro unit
, she’s more than capable of fulfilling her role as a Hydro Enabler.
Citlali
utilizes her strong Cryo application to trigger more Frozen reactions while shredding enemies’ resistance to Yelan’s Hydro. As for
Shenhe
, she operates as a buffer for Skirk’s Cryo DMG while further reducing enemies' resistance to Cryo.

All in all, as long as there are Cryo and Hydro characters dealing off-field DMG and offering decent buffs and debuffs, Skirk teams will always shine in
Genshin Impact
.
Team Roles
Skirk:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
Citlali:
Support, Shielder, Cryo Enabler, Debuffer
Shenhe:
Support, Cryo Enabler, Buffer, Debuffer
Yelan:
Sub-DPS, Hydro Enabler, Buffer
3
Skirk + Escoffier + Furina + Kazuha
Skirk operates at her best when she’s teaming with Hydro and
Cryo characters in
Genshin Impact
. That gives players a lot of freedom when building Skirk’s team, as there are a lot of useful characters that can join her team. At the same time, it also restricts Skirk from teaming with other elements since it’s not easy to compensate for the DMG loss.
The only characters
outside Hydro and Cryo
that can effectively team up with Skirk in
Genshin Impact
are
Kazuha
and
Xilonen
, since their buffs and debuffs are universally useful. Kazuha giving Skirk ~40% extra Cryo DMG alongside shredding enemies' resistance to Cryo DMG by 40% is worth it.
On the other hand, team Skirk with
Escoffier
and
Furina
to maximize the buffs and debuffs Skirk can get from this team. Kazuha also has good Crowd Control, where he’ll collect enemies in one spot to make the most of Skirk's Normal Attacks.
Team Roles
Skirk:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
Escoffier:
Support, Sub-DPS, Cryo Enabler, Debuffer, Healer
Furina:
Support, Sub-DPS, Hydro Enabler, Buffer, Healer
Kazuha:
Support, Crowd Control, Buffer, Debuffer
4
Skirk + Nahida + Shenhe + Thoma
Skirk works best with Frozen reactions, but she can still work in
Melt teams in
Genshin Impact
, although it's more of a fun comp since
Melt is not included in her A1
. The idea is to add one Dendro and one Pyro character to Skirk’s team so they can trigger the Burning reaction. The reaction will continuously apply Pyro on enemies as Skirk consumes them to trigger Melt, greatly increasing her DPS.

First, Skirk needs a strong Dendro Enabler like
Nahida
or
Emilie
in
Genshin Impact
. After that, adding a decent Pyro Enabler completes the task and sets the field ready for Skirk to dominate it. The downside to this Skirk team comp is the massive DMG buff Skirk loses when her team isn’t fully made of Cryo and Hydro units.
Team Roles
Skirk:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
Nahida:
Support, Sub-DPS, Dendro Enabler, Buffer
Shenhe:
Support, Cryo Enabler, Buffer, Debuffer
Thoma:
Support, Shielder, Pyro Enabler, Buffer
5
Skirk + Xingqiu + Dahlia + Layla
For an F2P Skirk team in
Genshin Impact
, team Skirk with
Xingqiu
to allow both to continuously apply their elements when she’s attacking. Both
Dahlia
and
Layla
operate as shielders with a small buff to Skirk’s DPS. Dahlia increases Skirk's Attack SPD, which notably improves her Normal Attacks. As for
Layla in
Genshin Impact
, her fourth Constellation increases Skirk’s Normal Attack DMG based on her Max HP. Although this Skirk team is not comparable to the previous ones, it’s still a sufficient team that can get the job done.
Other 4-star alternatives for Dahlia/Layla include
Candace
,
Rosaria
,
Mika
,
Diona
, and
Charlotte
.
Team Roles
Skirk:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
Xingqiu:
Sub-DPS, Hydro Enabler, Minor Healer
Dahlia:
Support, Shielder, Buffer
Layla:
Support, Shielder, Cryo Enabler, Buffer

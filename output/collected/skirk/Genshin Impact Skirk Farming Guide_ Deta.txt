Mob Drops
The materials required for
Skirk
’s progression include the
Meshing Gear, Mechanical Spur Gear,
and
Artificed Dynamic Gear
Mob Drops (Image via HoYoverse)
These items can be obtained from
Clockwork Meka Materials
enemies scattered across
Fontaine
. You’ll need these materials for
Skirk
’s Ascension and Talent upgrades, so it’s a good idea to farm extra for future use.
Local Specialty
Genshin Impact Skysplit Gembloom Location (Image via HoYoverse)
To
Ascend Skirk
, you’ll also need to gather material called
Skysplit Gembloom
, a Local Specialty from
Natlan
. Like other Local specialists,
Skysplit Gembloom
respawns every
48 real-life hours
, so it could take up to a week to collect enough to
ascend Skirk
fully.
Boss Drops
You will need
46 total boss drops
to ascend
Skirk
to
level 90
. The boss she needs is called “
Tenebrous Papilla,”
which is located in
Quahuacan Cliff, Natlan
.
Mora
In addition, you’ll need
420,000 Mora
to complete all of Skirk’s Ascension phases, plus an additional
1,637,400 Mora
to level her up using
Character EXP Materials
fully. Be sure to challenge
Blossoms of Wealth
to gather more Mora. If possible, take on these challenges in Fontaine, as they often feature the Mob bosses required to ascend the character.

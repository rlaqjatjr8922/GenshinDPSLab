Ayato’s abilities in Genshin Impact explained
Elemental Skill

Kamisato Ayato shifts positions and enters the Takimeguri Kanka state.
After this shift, he will leave a watery illusion at his original location. After it is formed, the watery illusion will explode if opponents are nearby or after its duration ends, dealing AoE Hydro DMG.
In the Takimeguri Kanka state, Kamisato Ayato deals a ton of AoE Hydro DMG through quick slashes. The Elemental Skill is the main source of his damage.

Best artifacts for Ayato in Genshin Impact
Four-piece Echoes of an Offering
Echoes of an Offering is the tailor-made artifact set for Ayato in Genshin Impact. The two-piece grants 18% ATK and the four-piece increases the Normal Attack damage. Both buffs are desirable for Ayato and complement his playstyle.
Four-piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence
The two-piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence set grants 18% ATK to Ayato in Genshin Impact. The four-piece set consumes 15 Energy while using an Elemental Skill but hands out 50% increased Normal Attack DMG.
Article continues after ad
Four-piece Heart of Depth
The two-piece Heart of Depth set in Genshin Impact gives 15% more Hydro DMG and the four-piece set increases Normal Attack DMG after you use an Elemental Skill. Like many Hydro characters, Ayato can utilize these buffs easily.
Article continues after ad
Four-piece Noblesse Oblige
Use the Noblesse Oblige set for Ayato if you want to use him as a support Burst DPS in Genshin Impact. The two-piece set increases Elemental Burst DMG by 20% and grants an ATK buff to teammates after using the Elemental Burst.

Best weapons for Ayato in Genshin Impact
Haran Geppaku Futsu
Haran Geppaku Futsu is the tailor-made weapon for Ayato in Genshin Impact. At level 90, you get 33.1% Crit Rate and 608 Base Attack, and both these numbers are truly broken for any DPS character.
However, Haran Geppaku Futsu outshines other five-star Swords due to its passive that increases Elemental DMG and Normal Attack DMG.
Primordial Jade Cutter
The Primordial Jade Cutter grants 44.1% Crit Rate and 542 Base Attack at level 90 and this alone is enough for you to use it with Ayato. On top of that, the weapon’s passive increases ATK based on the wielder’s max HP.

Another five-star Sword in Genshin Impact that will grant a 44.1% Crit Rate to Ayato is the Mistsplitter Reforged. Amenoma Kageuchi
Amenoma Kageuchi is the best free-to-play weapon for Ayato in Genshin Impact. It grants 55.1% ATK at level 90 and helps in Energy regeneration with the passive ability.
Best team compositions for Ayato in Genshin Impact
National team – Ayato, Xiangling, Bennett, and Kazuha
Ayato’s constant Hydro application and Xiangling’s equally powerful Pyro application make a perfect Melt/Evaporate team in Genshin Impact. In addition, you get heals and ATK buffs from Bennnet while Kazuha boosts the overall elemental damage.

Ayato, Ayaka, Venti, Diona
You can use Ayato and Ayaka together in Genshin Impact to make an overpowered Freeze team as powerful as Morgana. Freezing enemies with the likes of Ayaka and Ayato will be as easy as it gets and Venti primarily helps with crowd-controlling and reducing enemy resistance.
The rotation for this team should be
Ayato’s and Ayaka’s Bursts to freeze the enemies > Diona’s Skill and Burst > Switch to main DPS and hit frozen enemies.

Best Dendro team- Ayato, Nahida, Fischl, Kuki Shinobu
With the arrival of Dendro and Hyperbloom reaction in Genshin Impact, a lot of new team-building opportunities have opened up. Ayato is no exception, as you can now pair him with Dendro and Electro enablers to deal unreal amounts of reaction DMG.
You’ll have three element enablers for Dendro, Electro, and Hydro. Lastly, you can use a healer with element application like Kuki Shinobu.
The rotation for such a team is simple. Start by using a
Dendro skill to apply Dendro > Ayato’s Elemental Burst > Electro move to apply Electro > Use Ayato’s Elemental skill.

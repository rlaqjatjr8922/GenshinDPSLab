Ayato Story Quest - Story Unlock
Requirements
Story Key x 1
How to Unlock
- Reach Adventure Rank 40 or higher.
- Complete
Inazuma Archon Quests - Omnipresence Over Mortals
- Complete
Chisato's Letter
Adventure Rank Farming Guide
Use Story Keys to Unlock
To unlock Ayato's Story Quest, you need to use story keys. You can obtain Story Keys by doing
daily commissions
and keep a
maximum amount of 3 keys
. You can unlock the story by heading to the Quest page of Menu.
Ayato Story Quest - Walkthrough Guide
Cypressus Custos Chapter - Firmiana Leaf Falls
No.
Procedure
1
Talk to Ippei and Shinnojou in Inazuma City
2
Head to Kamisato Estate and enter the Yashiro Commission Headquarters
3
Speak To Thoma & Ayato
4
Report back to Ippei and Shinnojou
5
Go to the Kujou Estate and talk to Kujou Kamaji
6
Go to the Hiiragi Estate in Ritou
7
Kill some time in Ritou
8
Meet Hiiragi Chisato
9
Leave the Hiiragi Estate and go over what you know
10
Go and save Shinnojou
11
Defeat the Guards inside the cave
Character Trial:
Kamisato Ayato
12
Look for the key that the Bake-Danuki made off with
13
Check the current situation using the city Bulletin Board
14
Talk to Kujou Kamaji
15
Follow Hideo and do not be discovered
16
Wait for two days
17
Head to Ritou and talk to the people
18
Meet and talk to Hiiragi Chisato
19
Speak to Ayato at Yashiro Commission Headquarters
12. Look For The Key That The Bake-Danuki Made Off With
No.
Procedure
1
Destroy the stone monument beside the cage to find the bake-danuki.
2
Destroy the wooden crates just in front of the cage to find the bake-danuki
3
Head towards the shrine at the end of the cave to find the bake-danuki. It will then drop the key in front of it.
15. Follow Hideo And Do Not Be Discovered
For this part you will need to tail an informant of the Kanjou Commission. Hide behind walls and thick grass to hide from his sight. Be careful not to lose track of him since he tends to run away.

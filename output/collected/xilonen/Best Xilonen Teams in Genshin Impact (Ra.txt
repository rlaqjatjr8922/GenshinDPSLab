In Short
Xilonen is a Geo off-field Support unit who can work with most Hypercarry teams and characters, excluding Anemo and Dendro.
The best teams of Xilonen will capitalize on her Elemental Reduction on enemies and clear battles with ease.
The best Xilonen teams are Hypercarry teams, the best one being Neuvillette Hypercarry.

Xilonen is a 5-star Geo Support who wields a Sword and her kit is good for most Hypercarry teams. However, Xilonen’s kit only works alongside Pyro, Hydro, Electro, Geo, and Cryo characters, losing a ton of value when paired with Anemo and Dendro characters. Her Skill reduces the enemy Elemental Resistances based on the elemental type of the character in the party, excluding Dendro and Anemo. The best teams of Xilonen will need to capitalize on her damage buffs from
Xilonen’s best build
. Here I have ranked the 5 best teams for Xilonen in Genshin Impact.
5. Kamisato Ayaka-Shenhe-Xilonen-Furina (Ayaka Freeze)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
The freeze teams in Genshin Impact can benefit from Xilonen, as her Crystallization reaction doesn’t shatter the frozen enemies. Xilonen can still reduce enemy resistance with her kit in this team, allowing the damage from
Ayaka
to increase, especially with
Furina
in the team. This team will also
trigger the two different ally element requirements in Xilonen’s kit
, making it one of the stronger teams for her in Genshin Impact.
4. Navia-Xiangling-Xilonen-Bennet (Navia Hypercarry)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Navia
teams also received a good buff with Xilonen’s release
as she can swap
Zhongli
in the team and decrease enemy resistance. Xiangling and Bennet are standards for this Navia Hypercarry team so you don’t need to replace them. However, this team won’t be able to trigger the two different element threshold requirements from allies, limiting the heal from her ultimate. Still, this is one of the stronger teams for Xilonen and one of the best teams for Navia in Genshin Impact.
3. Mualani-Xiangling-Xilonen-Zhongli (Mualani Hypercarry)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
The
Mualani
Hypercarry team is one of the best Xilonen teams
in Genshin Impact. Since Mualani is a Natlan character and has Nightsoul Burst, Xilonen’s kit is allowed to perfectly pair with her, creating a strong synergy that heightens both the kits, other than that, use Xiangling for the Vape reaction and Zhongli for the extra energy shards for Xilonen and shield for Mualani to increase her interruption resistance. You can swap Zhongli with Kazuha in this team for extra Elemental Resistance shred, but you will need Xilonen’s constellation 2, which gives her allies Interruption Resistance.

2. Hu Tao-Yelan-Xilonen-Furina (Hu Tao Vape)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
The
Hu Tao
double-Hydro Vape team is another strong option for Xilonen. Here Xilonen can replace Kazuha, implementing Elemental Resistance shred, providing shields with her reaction, and healing them with her Ultimate. Having Yelan and Furina in this further increases the damage output, making it one of the best Xilonen teams in Genshin Impact. You can change Yelan with other Hydro appliers like Xingqiu to get a similar result.
1. Neuvillette-Kazuha-Xilonen-Furina (Neuvillette Hypercarry)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
The
Neuvillette
hypercarry is the best Xilonen team in Genshin Impact
. This team by itself is extremely broken, with Neuvillette widely considered the strongest DPS unit in the game. Adding Xilonen alongside Kazuha and Furina makes this one of the strongest teams in the game, with both Kazuha and Xilonen reducing the Elemental Resistance of enemies and Furina buffing their damage further with her kit.

Geo has not been a popular element in the current meta, especially for its support characters in
Genshin Impact
. This is largely due to their inherent limitations, coupled with the Crystallize reaction being less valued except for a few specific characters like
Navia
. However, Xilonen arrives with a unique kit that has the potential to challenge this status quo. Her ability to fill multiple roles within a team, whether as support, healer, or even DPS, makes her a standout character.

Xilonen's capacity to utilize the "
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
" artifact set, combined with the buffs she provides, makes her
utility comparable to Kazuha
's. She can easily buff multiple elements without relying on complex double-Swirl setups. Furthermore, her healing ability through her Elemental Burst (when the team includes at least two characters with elements other than Geo, Anemo, or Dendro) creates excellent synergy with characters like
Furina
. This allows for team compositions that combine healing and damage amplification for much-needed sustain. Especially at C0, players should aim for elemental diversity in their teams to fully benefit from her artifact set and her Elemental Burst's healing capabilities. Ultimately, Xilonen can fit in almost any team composition with any hypercarry, and here are some of Xilonen best team options in
Genshin Impact
:
Xilonen + Neuvillette + Furina + Kazuha
This team composition, featuring
Neuvillette
, Furina, Kazuha, and Xilonen, is one of the best teams for showcasing Xilonen's potential. It effectively utilizes three buffers to amplify the damage of a strong Hydro DPS like Neuvillette.
Since Neuvillette isn't reliant on reactions
like Vaporize or Electro-Charged to achieve high damage, he can benefit greatly from buffers instead of needing specific element team members.
Neuvillette's HP gain and drain mechanic synergizes perfectly with Furina, allowing her to quickly accumulate Fanfare stacks while remaining in a sub-DPS role. Xilonen's healing ensures the team's HP stays above 50%, further contributing to Furina's Fanfare generation. Combining Kazuha and Xilonen elevates the team's DPS potential to exceptional levels. Viridescent Venerer's Elemental RES debuff stacks with Xilonen's Source Sample debuff, and the Elemental DMG bonuses from Xilonen's artifact set and Kazuha's own buffs also stack. While there are diminishing returns on Elemental RES debuffs when pushing enemy resistance into the negatives, the combined effect is still incredibly potent. For players without Kazuha, or those who prefer to use him elsewhere, this slot offers flexibility. Strong alternatives include Sucrose for additional support, or
Raiden Shogun
and
Yae Miko
for an Electro-Charged focused team.
Team Roles
Xilonen:
Healer, Buffer, Debuffer
Neuvillette:
Main-DPS
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Buffer
Kazuha:
Crowd Control, Buffer, Debuffer
Xilonen + Navia + Xiangling + Bennett
As a character who thrives on Crystallize reactions, Navia benefits greatly from having another Geo character on her team, while also utilizing other elements—a perfect synergy with Xilonen. While Xilonen's healing might not be crucial in this team due to Bennett's presence, it still provides a valuable safety net. This team composition is straightforward, with a variety of buffs provided by characters like Bennett and Xilonen, and it effectively utilizes both Geo and Pyro Resonance.

For those
who prefer Itto over Navia
as a Geo hypercarry, it's recommended to avoid a mono-Geo team to maximize the benefits of the "Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City" artifact set. Including a consistent sub-DPS from another element enables the Crystallize reaction needed to activate the set's bonus. However, since both Itto and Xilonen benefit from Geo Source Samples, a 3-Geo team composition with a character like
Gorou
becomes ideal.
Team Roles
Xilonen:
Healer, Buffer, Debuffer
Navia:
Main-DPS
Xiangling:
Sub-DPS, Pyro Enabler
Bennett:
Healer, Buffer, Battery
Xilonen + Mualani + Xiangling + Xingqiu
Another great team composition features Xiangling alongside Mualani this time, while offering players a chance to utilize synergistic Natlan characters while achieving impressive damage numbers. Unlike Neuvillette, who
doesn't rely on elemental reactions
, Mualani thrives with Vaporize, making Xiangling an ideal teammate to enable this reaction. The last slot is flexible, but can be effectively filled by Xingqiu (or
Yelan
) for additional Hydro application and resonance. This makes the Mualani team more free-to-play friendly than the Neuvillette composition, requiring fewer 5-star characters for high damage output.
Team Roles
Xilonen:
Healer, Buffer, Debuffer
Mualani:
Main-DPS
Xiangling:
Sub-DPS, Pyro Enabler
Xingqiu:
Support, Sub-DPS
Xilonen + Raiden + Yelan + Furina
Xilonen can effectively replace Jean in Electro-Charged Raiden team compositions and even Kazuha in Raiden hypercarry teams (Raiden, Sara, Kazuha, Bennett). This is particularly valuable in scenarios with multiple waves of enemies, such as the Spiral Abyss, as her debuffs don't need to be constantly
reapplied like Viridescent Venerer
with each new wave.
In an Electro-Charged team, Raiden benefits significantly from strong Hydro sub-DPS units like Yelan and Furina, with Furina providing valuable additional buffs. Xilonen can seamlessly take on the healer and buffer role in this composition, previously occupied by Jean. This double-buffer setup with Xilonen and Furina can result in a significant damage increase. However, it's worth noting that in a hypercarry team composition where C6
Chevreuse
is used instead of Kazuha, Chevreuse might still offer greater synergy and overall benefit.
Team Roles
Xilonen:
Healer, Buffer, Debuffer
Raiden:
Main-DPS, Battery
Yelan:
Sub-DPS, Hydro Enabler
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Buffer
Xilonen + Chiori + Gorou + Fischl
One last team composition worth considering focuses on Xilonen as the on-field main DPS. While her support capabilities outshine her DPS potential, this team allows players to explore her damage output with a fun and effective setup. Although her healing contribution is reduced in this role, it can be easily compensated for by including a well-invested Gorou (C4 or higher).
This team composition allows players to utilize
Chiori
, who is receiving a rerun alongside Xilonen's debut banner. Alternatively,
Albedo
can fill the same sub-DPS role, offering similar Geo damage scaling with DEF. Gorou enables all three of his buffs in this team, benefiting both Xilonen and Chiori. The last slot offers flexibility for a sub-DPS, as long as they are Cryo, Hydro, Pyro, or Electro character. Fischl is an easily accessible 4-star option to fill this slot. However, with the release of future Natlan characters, there may be even more synergistic options to explore.
Team Roles
Xilonen:
DPS, Buffer, Debuffer
Chiori:
Sub-DPS
Gorou:
Healer (C4), Buffer
Fischl:
Sub-DPS, Battery

WHERE TO PLAY
SUBSCRIPTION
DIGITAL
Step into Teyvat, a vast world teeming with life and flowing with elemental energy.
You and your sibling arrived here from another world. Separated by an unknown god, stripped of your powers, and cast into a deep slumber, you now awake to a world very different from when you first arrived.
Thus begins your journey across Teyvat to seek answers from The Seven — the gods of each element. Along the way, prepare to explore every inch of this wondrous world, join forces with a diverse range of characters, and unravel the countless mysteries that Teyvat holds...
MASSIVE OPEN WORLD
Climb any mountain, swim across any river, and glide over the world below, taking in the jaw-dropping scenery each step of the way. And if you stop to investigate a wandering Seelie or strange mechanism, who knows what you might discover?
ELEMENTAL COMBAT SYSTEM
Harness the seven elements to unleash elemental reactions. Anemo, Electro, Hydro, Pyro, Cryo, Dendro, and Geo interact in all sorts of ways, and Vision wielders have the power to turn this to their advantage.
Will you vaporize Hydro with Pyro, electro-charge it with Electro, or freeze it with Cryo? Your mastery of the elements will give you the upper hand in battle and exploration.
BEAUTIFUL VISUALS
Feast your eyes on the world around you, with a stunning art style, real-time rendering, and finely tuned character animations delivering you a truly immersive visual experience. Lighting and weather all change naturally over time, bringing every detail of this world to life.
SOOTHING SOUNDTRACK
Let the beautiful sounds of Teyvat draw you in as you explore the expansive world around you. Performed by the London Philharmonic Orchestra, the soundtrack changes seamlessly with the time and gameplay to match the mood.
BUILD YOUR DREAM TEAM
Team up with a diverse cast of characters in Teyvat, each with their own unique personalities, stories, and abilities. Discover your favorite party combinations and level up your characters to help you conquer even the most daunting of enemies and domains.
JOURNEY WITH FRIENDS
Team up with friends across various platforms to trigger more elemental action, tackle tricky boss fights, and conquer challenging domains together to reap rich rewards.
As you stand atop the peaks of Jueyun Karst and take in the rolling clouds and vast terrain stretching out before you, you may wish to stay in Teyvat a little longer... But until you are reunited with your lost sibling, how can you rest? Go forth, Traveler, and begin your adventure!

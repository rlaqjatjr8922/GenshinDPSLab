Elemental Burst: Ocelotlicue
Point
Xilonen’s Elemental Burst is the highlight of her kit. Upon casting, Xilonen summons a massive AoE Geo attack, scaling off her
DEF
. If she has 2 source samples then she will trigger healing. But if she has only one source sample then she will trigger additional AoE damage. This situation is targeted towards her main DPS role.

Team Compositions for Xilonen
Support Xilonen
works best in teams that benefit from Geo shredding and DMG boosting. There are multiple variations that you can include as long as your main DPS is not Dendro or Anemo. Some of my current favorites are the following:
Itto + Albedo + Xilonen + Zhongli
Navia + Xilolen + Bennet + Xianling
Neuvillette + Xilolen + Kazuha + Furina
Xilonen + Yelan + Hu Tao + Zhongli
DPS Xilonen
works well in more general teams where she can serve as the main damage dealer:
Xilonen + Albedo + Bennett + Zhongli
: Increases Xilonen’s overall DPS while Albedo and Bennett provide strong support.

DEF-scaling
abilities and access to powerful weapons and artifact sets, she’s an easy-to-build character with high utility and fun gameplay.

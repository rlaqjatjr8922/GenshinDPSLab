Xilonen Overview
Xilonen is a 5-star Geo Sword user from Natlan who brings an incredible amount of utility to the table. As the first true Geo-based support with resistance shredding and healing capabilities, she reshapes team compositions for both Geo and non-Geo lineups. Her ability to provide off-field RES shred, single-target healing, and DEF-scaling buffs makes her a versatile pick for various content, especially in Spiral Abyss.

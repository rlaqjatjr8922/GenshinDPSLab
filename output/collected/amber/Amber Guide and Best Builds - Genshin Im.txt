Amber's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Is your first free character.
A viable option for new players who need a Pyro character.
Her Bunny can taunt enemies making bow combat easier.
Weaknesses
Lacks any degree of good personal damage, other Pyro characters will be a better option.
Long Cooldowns on Amber's Elemental Skill.
Requires a LOT of investment to catch up to other Pyro DPS.

Amber's BiS weapon depends heavily on whether you plan to build her as a DPS or
a Support. As a DPS,

For Amber's Artifact choice, similar to the weapons, the choice depends entirely on
what role you want Amber to play:
As a Charged Shot focused DPS, Amber will need

Playing Amber as Explosive Bunny DPS,

Noblesse Oblige
For Amber's talents, as a DPS you will have to upgrade her Normal Attack first if you
play with the Charged Shot mechanic. A C4+ Amber for Explosive Bunny will require
you to focus on her Elemental Skill first.
If you build Amber as a support, you upgrade her Elemental Burst first, and you can completely disregard
her normal attack.

4.
Amber Combos and How to Play
Amber can be a viable Sub DPS option. This will require you to use the Charged Shots mechanic,
meaning aiming and charging your bow shot before shooting. This way you will deal
Pyro
damage, which will enable
Melt
.
Once your Amber reaches C4 or higher, you can use the Explosive Bunny mechanic. This
means using her Elemental Skill to spawn her Bunny, which will taunt and lure in enemies, and
then charging your shot to shoot at the Bunny and make it explode on the surrounding enemies.
The Bunny is also a good way of gaining energy, as it produces a lot of particles upon exploding.
If you play Amber as a support, your only goal will be to swap her in to use her Burst
and put down her Bunny. She will only be there to support a main
Pyro
DPS.
5.
Amber's Best Team Comps
As a DPS Amber will want to be paired with at least one
Cryo
character for
Melt
teams. Adding a Shielder can be beneficial as well, especially for Charged Shots, as
she can be interrupted easily when charging.
As a Support, you will want to pair her with a main
Pyro
DPS, as Amber's
main role as a support is to apply
Pyro
.

Amber Skill Guide & How To Use Amber
Recommended Rotation For Amber
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Elemental Skill as an opener, as well as to get charge for ult
2
While enemies are distracted, either spam Normal Attacks or Charged Attacks
3
Bring out Baron Bunny again, and unleash Elemental Burst on enemies around it to guarantee hits
4
Repeat from step 1
Uses Of Amber's Elemental Skill
Distract & Taunt The Enemies
Baron Bunny will take the aggro of enemies and distract them from attacking. You can either use it defensively to move away from enemies or offensively by gathering them in a single place to attack them.
Use To Gather Energy For Burst
It is important to use Baron Bunny as an opener since it can only produce energy once it explodes. Placing it early in battle makes energy gain faster.
Uses Of Amber's Elemental Burst
Set Off High Damage Pyro Reactions
Amber's Burst has the highest damage amplifier for a skill in the game. It is a great finisher to either multiple or solo enemies since it can activate reactions for high damage.
Should You Pull for Amber's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Constellations Are Ideal, But Hard to Acquire
Constellation Priority
C6 > C4
C6 Is Her Best Constellation
Amber's performance as an on-field DPS at C0 is questionable at best, more so because other characters output better damage with less effort. However, getting her constellations is one way of upgrading Amber's usability.
Each constellation improves upon the next, so the small improvements she gets across each level combines into a decent overall buff once you get to the last one.
C4 gives Amber an extra Skill Charge and is a good compliment to her C2, which allows her Baron Bunnies to deal more damage when manually detonated. The extra charge can also generate more energy to consistently proc her Burst and be a great pairing with C6. Her C6 grants the whole party a decent amount of Attack whenever Amber uses her burst, making it beneficial for herself as a DPS or for others as a Burst support, and
this is likely her best constellation due to having access to every other prior constellation while also adding a benefit to Amber's burst, apart from Pyro application.
It is worth mentioning that while her constellations make her a lot more usable, Amber is one among the three 4-stars characters who are given for free during the first few chapters of the Monstadt Archon Quest and have also not been featured in any rate-up banner since the game's release.
It isn't realistic to get her to any constellation level apart from the off-chance you get her from a Standard Banner Pull or if you diligently save enough Starglitter to get Amber every time she appears in
Paimon's Bargain
, which totals to twice a year.
It will be significantly easier to invest into a better catalyst or into her premium supports options, but if you are dedicated enough, getting Amber's constellations will only be a matter of time.

Who Is Amber?
Knight Of Favonius' Only Outrider
Amber is currently the only outrider of the Knights of Favonius. Always willing to help, Amber is known all over Mondstadt for her warm presence.
Gliding Champion Of Mondstadt
She is the three-time winner of the Gliding Champion in Mondstadt and was one of the few to test the Traveler in Gliding.
Close Friends With Collei & Eula
Amber is such a warm and friendly person that she was able to befriend the stoic Eula and the foreign Collei. They continue to be friends today, even though Collei has returned to Sumeru.

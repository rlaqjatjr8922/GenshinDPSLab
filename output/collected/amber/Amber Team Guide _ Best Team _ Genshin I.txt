Amber Team Overview
This Page provides the best Amber
Team information. Amber will be Sub DPS in most of the situation.
You can also swap the position of Yoimiya to make your own best team.
From S tier to A tier, Amber best team can be formed in different ways including Vapourize, Melt and Overload team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

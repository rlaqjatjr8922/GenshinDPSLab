Genshin Impact Amber Guide - Best Builds and Teams
Posted by:
BlueStacks Content Team
Editor’s Rating:
Mar 26, 2026
Play Genshin Impact on PC
Amber is the very first character to join your party in
Genshin Impact
, acting as the enthusiastic Outrider of the Knights of Favonius. While many players set her aside as they pull for flashier units, Amber possesses a surprisingly versatile kit that allows her to function as a reliable Pyro support or a dedicated Melt-focused sharpshooter. This guide will walk you through the best ways to build the champion glider of Mondstadt, ensuring she remains a fiery asset to your roster.
Amber Genshin Impact Abilities
Ambers combat style centers on long-range precision and tactical distractions. Whether you are using her to solve puzzles in the open world or triggering reactions in the Abyss, understanding her talent priority is essential. Check out her core skills:
Basic Attack: Sharpshooter
– Amber performs up to five consecutive shots with her bow. Her charged attack is where the real value lies, as it inflicts Pyro damage on hit. If you are building her as a main DPS, this will be your bread and butter, especially when aiming for weak points to guarantee critical hits.
Elemental Skill: Explosive Puppet
– Amber throws out the legendary Baron Bunny. This dancing puppet taunts nearby enemies, drawing their fire away from your active character. After a set duration or once its health is depleted, it explodes to deal area-of-effect Pyro damage. At higher constellations, specifically C4, Amber can manually detonate her bunnies and carry two charges, significantly increasing her burst potential.
Elemental Burst: Fiery Rain
– Amber fires a shower of arrows that deal continuous area-of-effect Pyro damage. This burst is incredibly fast and has a low energy cost, making it perfect for shredding elemental shields or triggering rapid reactions. However, because the arrows fall in a specific area, mobile enemies can sometimes wander out of the zone before taking full damage.
Amber Genshin Impact Strengths and Weaknesses
Strengths
Tactical Utility
: Baron Bunny is one of the few taunts in the game, providing excellent breathing room in difficult fights.
Reaction Potential
: Her fast Pyro application during her burst makes her great for setup.
Accessible Melt Playstyle
: Even at zero constellations, she can perform well in Melt teams with basic investment.
Excellent Exploration
: Her passive reduces gliding stamina consumption, making her a permanent fixture for many players roaming the map.
Weaknesses
High Competition
: As a starter unit, she is statistically outclassed by almost every other Pyro character in terms of raw damage.
AoE Limitations
: Both her skill and burst have relatively small ranges, requiring good positioning.
Long Cooldowns
: Before reaching C4, the gap between Baron Bunny deployments can feel sluggish.
Best Artifacts for Amber in Genshin Impact
Your choice of artifacts depends entirely on whether you want Amber to facilitate your other carries or take the spotlight herself. Check out our top artifact recommendations for Amber:
Noblesse Oblige
: This is the premier choice for a supportive Amber. The 2-piece bonus increases her burst damage, while the 4-piece set provides a 20 percent attack buff to the entire team after she uses her Elemental Burst. Given her low burst cost, she can maintain this buff with high uptime.
Emblem of Severed Fate
: If you find yourself struggling to recharge her energy or want her burst to hit as hard as possible, this set is the way to go. It converts her Energy Recharge into burst damage, making her off-field contributions much more impactful.
Shimenawas Reminiscence
: This set is perfect for a charged shot playstyle. The 4-piece bonus grants a massive 50 percent damage increase to normal and charged attacks at the cost of some energy. Since Amber often focuses on her bow shots, this tradeoff is usually worth the raw power.
Wanderers Troupe
: A classic choice for bow users. It provides extra Elemental Mastery for stronger Melt reactions and a 35 percent boost to charged attack damage. It is a very consistent set that doesn’t require any energy management.
Best Weapons for Amber in Genshin Impact
Amber can utilize a wide range of bows depending on her role. Whether you have 5-star options or are sticking to free-to-play choices, there is a bow that fits her perfectly. Check out our top weapon recommendations for Amber:
Elegy for the End:
This is her best-in-slot support weapon. It provides a massive amount of Energy Recharge and a team-wide Elemental Mastery and Attack buff. Amber can trigger the weapons passive almost instantly with her multi-hit Elemental Burst.
The First Great Magic
: For a main DPS Amber, this bow is a powerhouse. It increases charged attack damage and provides different buffs based on the elemental types in your party. If you are running her in a double Pyro team, the attack bonuses are significant.
Skyward Harp
: A fantastic general-purpose 5-star bow. The high base attack and critical rate stat help balance her build easily, and the critical damage bonus from the passive is a nice cherry on top.
The Stringless
: The best 4-star option for a sub-DPS build. It boosts the damage of both her skill and burst while providing Elemental Mastery for stronger reactions.
Favonius Warbow
: The ultimate free-to-play support choice. It solves Ambers energy needs while generating particles for the rest of the team. This is often the most comfortable weapon for a support Amber in high-pressure content like the Spiral Abyss.
Best Teams for Amber in Genshin Impact
Amber thrives when paired with characters who can capitalize on her Pyro application or protect her while she aims her shots. Some of her best teams are as follows:
Melt Focus Team
: Amber works beautifully alongside Cryo units. A team featuring Rosaria or Layla provides consistent Cryo application and shields. When paired with Bennett for the attack buff and Pyro resonance, Amber can land massive Melt procs with her charged shots or Baron Bunny explosions.
Vaporize Support Team
: In this setup, Amber acts as a secondary Pyro applicator and buffer for a main DPS like Hu Tao. By using her burst to apply Pyro, she allows an Anemo character like Kazuha to swirl Pyro and shred resistance before your main carry takes the field. This also allows her to carry supportive gear like Noblesse Oblige to further boost the team’s total output.
Mono-Pyro Team
: If you enjoy seeing constant fire on the screen, pair Amber with Lyney and Bennett. Amber provides the necessary Pyro aura for Lyney’s passives and can distract enemies with Baron Bunny while Lyney charges his heavy attacks. Kazuha is usually the fourth slot here to group enemies and increase elemental damage.

Genshin Impact - FAQs
Who is Amber in Genshin Impact?
Amber is a Pyro bow user and the Outrider of the Knights of Favonius from Mondstadt. She is one of the first characters players receive in the game and specializes in ranged combat, Pyro reactions, and tactical utility through her Baron Bunny skill.
What is the best weapon for Amber in Genshin Impact?
The best weapon depends on her role. Elegy for the End is ideal for support builds because it boosts Energy Recharge and team buffs. For a DPS build, The First Great Magic or Skyward Harp provides strong attack and critical stats.
Which artifacts are best for Amber in Genshin Impact?
Support Amber works best with Noblesse Oblige, which boosts her burst damage and increases team attack. DPS builds benefit from Shimenawa’s Reminiscence or Wanderer’s Troupe, both of which significantly increase charged attack damage.
What stats should players prioritize for Amber in Genshin Impact?
Players should prioritize Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Pyro Damage Bonus, and Attack percentage for DPS builds. For support builds, Energy Recharge and Elemental Mastery can help increase her burst uptime and reaction damage.
What constellation improves Amber the most in Genshin Impact?
Amber’s C4 constellation is particularly powerful because it allows her to deploy two Baron Bunnies and manually detonate them, greatly increasing her damage and utility during fights.
Which characters work best with Amber in Genshin Impact?
Amber pairs well with Cryo characters for Melt teams and supports who boost attack or elemental damage. Strong teammates include Bennett, Rosaria, Kazuha, and Hu Tao.

There are many characters to choose from in Genshin Impact, including the ones that are given to you at the very beginning of the game. This even includes the four characters you are given to start the game with. Amber is a pyro character, and with the right build, she can perform relatively well.

sub-DPS

Here is the best build for Amber:
Noblesse Oblige
Artifact Set:
Amber gets a 20 percent boost to burst damage, and when she uses her burst, the entire party gets a 20 percent increase to their attack for twelve seconds.
Elegy for the End:
Amber's elemental master is increased by 60 points, and when she uses her burst or skill to hit an opponent, she gets a sigil of remembrance. Once she receives four sigils, the party will receive a 100 point increase to elemental mastery and a 20 percent attack increase for 12 seconds.

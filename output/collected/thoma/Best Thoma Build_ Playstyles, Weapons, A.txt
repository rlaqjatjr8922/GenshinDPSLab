A Summary Of Thoma’s Kit
Thoma
, Titled “Protector From Afar”, is a
4-star Pyro polearm user
from Mondstadt. His kit revolves around his capabilities as a
Shielder
/
Pyro Applier
for your
Burgeon
teams.
Thoma
‘s Elemental Skill and Burst are the main focus of his build. A supportive
Thoma
build would equip him with the
Noblesse Oblige
artifact set, with his preferred weapon for such a build being the
Favonius Lance
.
A
Burgeon
–
reaction-based
build would equip him with the
Flower of Paradise Lost
artifact set and the
Kitain Cross Spear
polearm.

Thoma’s Elemental Skill and Burst
One way to play
Thoma
is as a
Pyro shielder/support
. His Elemental Skill and Burst
create a translucent red shield
around the active character that
blocks incoming Pyro DMG
and
inflicts Pyro DMG
on enemies.
Blazing Blessing
(Thoma’s Elemental Skill) is a
shield that scales off of Thoma’s max HP.
The barrier is reinforced and stacks whenever
Thoma
gets hit.
On the other hand,
Thoma
’s Elemental Burst
Crimson Ooyoroi
deals AoE Pyro DMG
and
summons another shield
similar to AoE
Pyro
DMG similar to
Xingqiu
’s Rainswords.
Typically, players can invest into HP and Energy Recharge pieces to build
Thoma
as a
Pyro support/shielder
.
His
Crimson Ooyori
has an 80 Energy Cost and lasts 15s, just like his Elemental Skill.

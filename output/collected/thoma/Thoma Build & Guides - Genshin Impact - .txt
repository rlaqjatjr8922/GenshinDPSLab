as shielder but can also contribute great damage to Burgeon teams.

Thoma's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Powerful shield from both his Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
Consistent Off-Field Pyro application making him great for Burgeon and Burning reactions.
Weaknesses
Extremely high Energy requirements for his Elemental Burst.
Elemental Skill has a relatively long cooldown compared to its 8s duration.
Low damage outside of the Pyro application Thoma brings for Burgeon reactions.

4.1.
Thoma's Energy Recharge Breakpoints
Thoma has various Energy Recharge breakpoints depending on
his weapon choices and what Constellations Thoma has. Generally
you want around 220 - 230% with
Long-Term Planning
helping to reduce requirements by around 25%.
Favonius Lance
lowers your requirements by around 20% or
alternatively
Kitain Cross Spear
lowers requirements by roughly
5% per Refinement.
5.
Combos and How to Play Thoma
Both
Blazing Blessing
and
Crimson Ooyoroi
create shields when used which scale based on his max HP. They also have a
unique effect of allowing Thoma's shields to stack on each other further
increasing your shield strength instead of replacing them like other
shield characters. With the effects of
Imbricated Armor
, the
first 5 stacked shields gain increased shield strength.
Thoma is great for
Burgeon
! With his Elemental
Burst
Crimson Ooyoroi
, you can slowly apply
Pyro
when your active character uses a Normal Attack.
This provides Thoma with the perfect amount of Pyro application to create
Burgeon without
Burning
too frequently.
5.1.
Thoma is Great for Fishing!
Thomas passive
Snap and Swing
provides a 20%
chance of doubling your fishing yield in Inazuma. Thoma
does not need to be in the party for this to take effect, simply having Thoma
makes it easier to catch Inazuman fish.

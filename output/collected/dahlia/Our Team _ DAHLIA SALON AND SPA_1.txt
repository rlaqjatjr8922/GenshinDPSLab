Lindsy
Owner/Senior Stylist
Hi, I'm Lindsy, the proud owner of Dahlia Salon and spa. I absolutely love the beauty industry, and my dream was to open a space where our team could be creative, with the continuous support of Martensville and the surrounding areas over the last 12 years that dream became a reality! So thank you!
Outside of the salon, I have an amazing husband and children. We enjoy road trips, exploring show homes, and having cozy movie nights. Lately, I've been diving into the world of cooking, trying out new recipes and finding it to be another creative outlet for me. A fun fact about me is that I know almost every lyric to songs from the 90s and 2000s, but I'm all about country music these days. I may think I'm a karaoke rock star, but that's definitely not in my cards!
My passion lies in creating lived-in colour and making sure every person who sits in my chair feels their absolute best. As a hairstylist, I absolutely adore creating lived-in looks! Soft, beautiful balayages and grey blending are my specialties, helping to enhance and embrace every individual's unique beauty. I strongly believe in educating our guests on the "how-to's" of styling and maintaining their hair at home. It's important to me that you leave the salon not only looking fabulous but also equipped with the right tools to recreate that perfect style on your own.
One of my all-time favourite products is Anti Gravity by Kevin Murphy. It's a must-have for everyone, thanks to its simplicity and effectiveness. This product works like a charm for all hair types, making styling a breeze and leaving your hair looking and feeling fantastic. Trust me, Anti Gravity will be a game-changer in your hair care routine!
I’m excited for you to sit in my chair!

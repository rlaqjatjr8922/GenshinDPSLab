Genshin Impact Upcoming New Character Dahlia Preview
India Today Gaming
May 16, 2025 03:30 (UPDATED: May 16, 2025 03:30 IST)
Dahlia, a new Hydro sword user from Mondstadt, joins Genshin Impact in Version 5.7 as a support specialist.
dahlia
Genshin Impact continues to expand its diverse roster with the introduction of Dahlia, a 4-star Hydro character arriving in Version 5.7. Hailing from Mondstadt, Dahlia serves as a Deacon of the Church of Favonius and is widely respected for his calm demeanor, spiritual devotion, and gentle approach to those in need. He is believed by many to be favored by Barbatos himself, reinforcing his deep-rooted ties to Mondstadt’s cultural and religious fabric.
Abilities
In combat, Dahlia wields a sword and specializes in Hydro-based support. Early details from official sources suggest that his abilities are tailored for synergizing with Cryo characters, making him a strong addition to Freeze-based team compositions. His Elemental Skill is rumored to provide consistent Hydro application, while his Burst may offer healing or buffs to nearby allies. This design positions him as an effective enabler in both standard and Spiral Abyss team builds.

Constellation
His Constellation, “Cantus Choralis,” appears to follow a musical motif, hinting at skills inspired by hymn-like performances. As such, players can expect a blend of thematic elegance and strategic utility in his playstyle. Though he is unaware of Venti’s divine identity, Dahlia is said to share a friendly bond with the bard, potentially leading to compelling interactions and revelations as the story progresses.
Narratively, Dahlia’s release coincides with the Traveler’s return to Mondstadt after the Natlan arc. His character is set to offer a fresh perspective on the region and its changing dynamics. With strong ties to both lore and gameplay, Dahlia promises to be a meaningful addition to the Genshin Impact universe.
As anticipation builds up for Version 5.7, players look forward to welcoming this soft-spoken yet powerful figure into their teams and exploring his role in the evolving world of Genshin Impact.

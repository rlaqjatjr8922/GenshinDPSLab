Dahlia is one of the upcoming units for Genshin Impact Version 5.7. He is a 4-star sword-wielding Hydro support unit that focuses on providing the team with HP-scaling shields and an ATK SPD increasing buff. Dahlia’s kit seems like an interesting addition to the game, and given that ATK SPD buffers haven’t been explored all that much, it is interesting to see how he will fit into the meta. If you’re planning on building Dahlia, then this guide will come in help, as below you can find all of the materials you will need to build him up.

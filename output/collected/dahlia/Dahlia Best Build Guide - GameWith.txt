Dahlia is a 4-star Hydro character that wields a Sword in Genshin Impact. His gameplay revolves on buffing his allies with shields and ATK speed. His Elemental Burst is his main source of buffing while his Elemental Skill can apply Hydro and has different effects if stepped on by enemies or allies. Lore-wise, Dahlia is the Deacon of the Church of Favonius and the Herald of Barbatos. He is also friends with Venti.

Roles & Playstyle Of Dahlia and Its Strengths and Weakness
ATK Speed Buffer Dahlia
Dahllia's intended role is to buff his teammates with ATK Speed increase and provide Shields. While his Support abilities work when he's Off-Field, he is unable to apply Hydro regularly thus, requiring a 2nd Hydro character for applying the Element.
Pros and Cons of Support Dahlia
Pros
- Great with any Normal Attack DPS. Buff is only 2% worse than Mika's ATK Speed buff.
- Scales on Max HP only, allowing for easier building.
- Shield can be great for damage mitigation.
- Elemental Skill can be used for elevation.
Cons
- Fragile shield even with high HP stat.
- ATK Speed Buff is reliant on whether his Shield is active, meaning dodging will be needed to maintain the shield.
- Elemental Burst reliant meaning Energy Recharge is a priority aside from HP.
Best Build for Dahlia
Dahlia's only feasible build is as an ATK SPD buffer with all his stats focused on HP and Energy Recharge. This is to maintain the uptime and tankiness of his Shield.

Team Rotation of Dahlia and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for Dahlia
Basic Rotation For Dahlia
1. Activate other party members' Abilities.
2. Tap Elemental Skill, then use Elemental Burst.
3. Switch to the On-Fielder.
4. Repeat rotation.
It's ideal to use Dahlia last in the support rotation since his buff has a short uptime. Be aware that
his Elemental Skill has a Held version where it adjusts the placement of his Elemental Skill's summon
. If using Scroll set Dahlia, apply the Element you want to be buffed before his turn.
Basics Of Using Dahlia
Dahlia is mainly used for shielding and buffing his teammates with ATK speed. His most important Skill is his Elemental Burst where he applies the Favonian Favor buff that gives the shield and ATK speed buff.
Build HP for Shield and ATK Speed Buff
Dahlia's Elemental Burst uses his Max HP for the Shield HP and ATK Speed buff he can give. For every 1,000 HP Dahlia has, the ATK speed buff goes up by 0.5% and caps at 20%. While his ATK speed buff seems decent, the Shield he can grant is very fragile.
Gain Benison Stacks to Refresh the Shield
Dahlia has a unique stack effect called Benison that allows him to refresh the Shield if it breaks. He has two ways of obtaining Benison stacks.

during his Elemental Burst duration. Since Dahlia gains more Stacks when Frozen is triggered, he is most compatible to be used in a Freeze team. But if the DPS is a fast Normal Attacker, he can be used with them as well.

Hydro Shielder With Attack Speed Buffs
Basic Rotation Of Dahlia
1. Deploy other support abilities.
2. Swap to Dahlia and use Elemental Skill.
3. Use Elemental Burst.
4. Swap to your On=Field DPS to deal damage.
5.Repeat from number 1.
Dahlia's core gameplay is very simple.
He is an Off-Field Hydro Support that gives a shield with his Elemental Burst while also buffing Attack Speed to the active party member.
He's best used with damage dealers that attack using their Normal Attacks or in a team that can trigger Frozen, since this is his way of gaining Benison Stacks that help ensure his shield is maintained.

Dahlia's Elemental Skill is primarily used for applying Hydro to enemies but outside combat, it can be used for exploration help.
This Skill has a Held version where you can precisely position the Sacramental Shower it summons.
Elemental Burst Provides a Shield That Can Be Refreshed
Summary of Elemental Burst
- Grants a Shield to allies and buffs ATK speed.
- ATK Speed buff is tied to his Shield uptime but it can be refreshed via Benison stacks.
Ｌ Benison Stacks can be gained from Normal Attacks and Frozen Reaction.
Dahlia's Elemental Burst deals AoE Hydro damage and grants the Favonian Favor buff to the team which creates a Shield and buffs ATK speed. While the ATK speed buff is tied to the Shield's uptime, Dahlia is able to refresh his Shield if it's broken thanks to his Benison stacks.
Dahlia is able to gain Benison stacks for refreshing his Shield from Normal Attacks or when an ally triggers Frozen.
Hitting enemies 4 times with Normal Attacks gives him 1 stack while Frozen triggers grant him 2 stacks.
Buffs Attack Speed Based on His Max HP
The buff that Dahlia provides to a party besides his Artifact set is a modest Attack Speed boost that scales based on his Max HP. You would need to give Dahlia almost 40,000 HP to reach the maximum value. It isn't the most substantial buff in comparison to what is currently in-game, but it can be considered a nice add-on to his utility.
May Require a Lot of Energy Recharge%
▼Dahlia's C1 is good to have for Energy purposes.
Dahlia's Elemental Burst is the highlight of his kit and he wants to use this every rotation.
Because he's designed to be played Off-Field, he needs to be built with more Energy Recharge% so he can reliably get his Burst back up to full.
He might struggle to do this if he is the only Hydro character in the party without over 200% Energy Recharge,
so pairing him with another Hydro teammate to help him by generating Hydro Energy Particles is recommended. You can also have Dahlia or other party members equip Favonius weapons to further lower his Energy requirements.
His first constellation can also make his usage a little smoother.

Radiant Psalter (Elemental Burst)
Effect
Appeals to the divine with earnestness and song, dealing AoE Hydro DMG to nearby opponents and granting his party members Favonian Favor, summoning a Shield of Sacred Favor. The Shield of Sacred Favor's DMG absorption is based on Dahlia's Max HP, and it will absorb Hydro DMG with 250% effectiveness.
Favonian Favor
Effect
While this effect is active, 1 Benison stack may be consumed to summon a new Shield of Sacred Favor when an existing Shield is broken via DMG.
Dahlia can obtain Benison via the following methods:
・He will gain 1 Benison stack when his own active party member hits opponents 4 times with Normal Attacks while affected by Favonian Favor.
・After triggering the effect of his Passive Talent, "The Wind's Gentle Grace" (You must first unlock this Passive Talent.)
Dahlia can gain a maximum of 4 Benison stacks per Favonian Favor duration.

The Wind's Gentle Grace
Effect
When characters affected by the Favonian Favor effect of the Elemental Burst Radiant Psalter trigger Frozen reactions on opponents, they will grant Dahlia 2 Benison stacks. This effect can trigger once every 8s.
Prayer Of Well-Wrought Joy
Effect
When your active party member is affected by the Favonian Favor effect of the Elemental Burst Radiant Psalter, their ATK SPD will increase based on Dahlia's Max HP: Every 1,000 Max HP will cause an increase of 0.5%, up to a maximum of 20%.
Pilgrimage Upon Returning Winds
Effect
During the day (06:00 - 18:00), your party members gain the Swift Stride effect: Movement SPD increased by 10%. Does not take effect in Domains, Trounce Domains, or the Spiral Abyss. Swift Stride does not stack.
Who Is Dahlia?
Deacon Of The Church Of Favonius
Dahlia is a deacon of the Church of Favonius. He works together with Barbara and Rosaria in guiding the people in worship to Barbatos.
Representative Of The Anemo Archon
Dahlia is also the representative of the Anemo Archon. He relays messages that come directly from their god. Of course due to being Barbatos' representative, Dahlia is aware of Venti's true identity.

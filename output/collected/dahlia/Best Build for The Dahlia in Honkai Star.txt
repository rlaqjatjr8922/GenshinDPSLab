About The Dahlia
The Dahlia uses a special mechanic called the Gloomy Zone that makes Break teams extremely powerful. Her Skill deploys this Zone for 3 turns, which increases all allies’ Weakness Break Efficiency by 50%. The most important part is that Toughness damage dealt to enemies who are not yet broken can be converted into Super Break damage. This solves the biggest weakness of Break teams since they can now deal consistent damage without waiting for enemy Toughness bars to deplete.
When entering combat, The Dahlia automatically assigns herself and one ally as Dance Partners. The Dance Partner is usually the teammate with the highest Break Effect. After a Dance Partner attacks a Weakness Broken enemy, the Toughness Reduction from that attack converts into Super Break damage at 60%. When the other Dance Partner attacks any enemy, The Dahlia launches a follow-up attack that bounces between enemies and deals additional Super Break damage.
Her Ultimate applies a Wilt state to all enemies for 4 turns. This reduces enemy Defense by 18% and applies the elemental weaknesses of all Dance Partners to every enemy. This means your team can break enemies regardless of their original weaknesses. The Dahlia also provides Speed boosts and Energy restoration to Fire allies when they apply weaknesses to enemies.

Best Relics for The Dahlia
Relics are crucial for building The Dahlia correctly. Here’s what you need:
Best Relic Set
The 4 piece Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge is The Dahlia’s best relic set. The 2 piece bonus gives her 16% Break Effect. The 4 piece bonus increases her Break Effect by an additional 25% and boosts her own damage when Break Effect reaches 150% or higher. At 250% Break Effect, she gains even more damage and can ignore 15% of enemy DEF.
Best Planar Ornaments
For planar ornaments, use Forge of the Kalpagni Lantern for the Speed bonus and Break Effect increase. This set is perfect because The Dahlia needs both Speed and Break Effect to function optimally.
Kingdom of Banditry is another solid choice that provides Break Effect and helps The Dahlia move faster when her Speed reaches 145 or higher.

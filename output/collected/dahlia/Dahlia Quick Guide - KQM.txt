Immersive Ordinance
Talent Description
Time for a fun little trick! Summons the Sacramental Shower in different ways depending on whether you Tap or Hold it.
Tap
After a short prayer, summons the Sacramental Shower in front.
Hold
Engages in continuous prayer, and when done, summons forth a Sacramental Shower in the designated position.
Sacramental Shower
When opponents or nearby party members touch the Sacramental Shower, different effects will occur depending on the one who touches it:
If an opponent touches the Shower, it will explode and deal AoE Hydro DMG to nearby opponents.
If a nearby party member touches it, it will bounce the one who touches it high in the air.
Only 1 Sacramental Shower summoned by Dahlia himself can be on the field at once.
Dahlia’s Tap Skill auto-targets an enemy and summons a circle on the ground that reacts differently depending on who steps in. If an enemy walks into it, it deals AoE Hydro damage and generates 3 Hydro particles. But if a party member steps in, they get launched into the air, enabling them to perform a Plunging Attack — though this means the Skill won’t do any damage or generate particles, so it is almost never worth it outside of exploration.
Dahlia’s Hold Skill lets you summon the circle at a designated position. However, his Tap Skill is almost always used instead since it auto-targets anyway and is faster.
Elemental Burst
Elemental Burst
|
Radiant Psalter
Talent Description
Appeals to the divine with earnestness and song, dealing AoE Hydro DMG to nearby opponents and granting his party members Favonian Favor, summoning a Shield of Sacred Favor.
The Shield of Sacred Favor’s DMG absorption is based on Dahlia’s Max HP, and it will absorb Hydro DMG with 250% effectiveness.
Favonian Favor
While this effect is active, 1 Benison stack may be consumed to summon a new Shield of Sacred Favor when an existing Shield is broken via DMG.
Dahlia can obtain Benison via the following methods:
He will gain 1 Benison stack when his own active party member hits opponents 4 times with Normal Attacks while affected by Favonian Favor.
After triggering the effect of his Passive Talent, “The Wind’s Gentle Grace” (You must first unlock this Passive Talent.)
Dahlia can gain a maximum of 4 Benison stacks per Favonian Favor duration.
Dahlia’s Burst applies the Favonian Favor effect to all party members, granting the on-field character a shield. With his A4 Passive and Constellation 6, it also provides an Attack SPD buff.
While his Burst is active, Dahlia can obtain Benison stacks, which can be consumed to create new shields if the current one is broken. Benison stacks are obtained either by triggering his A1 Passive, granting 2 Benison stacks, or when the on-field character hits enemies with Normal Attacks 4 times, granting 1 Benison stack. A maximum of 4 Benison stacks can be accumulated throughout Favonian Favor’s duration.

Ascension 4 Passive
Ascension 4 Passive
|
Prayer of Well-Wrought Joy
Talent Description
When your active party member is affected by the Favonian Favor effect of the Elemental Burst “Radiant Psalter,” their ATK SPD will increase based on Dahlia’s Max HP: Every 1,000 Max HP will cause an increase of 0.5%, up to a maximum of 20%.
This passive provides the on-field character with up to 20% Attack SPD based on Dahlia’s Max HP.
To reach the maximum buff, Dahlia requires 40,000 Max HP. However, this is only realistic to achieve if Dahlia is at least Level 80/90 or has external HP buffs (e.g., Hydro Resonance).

Artifact Sets
4pc Noblesse Oblige (NO)
Dahlia Burst is used every rotation, so 4pc NO is a natural choice to buff ATK-scaling teammates.
4pc Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City (Scroll)
4pc Scroll is an alternative set to buff non-ATK scalers (e.g., Neuvillette, Mualani) or if another party member is already holding 4pc NO.
4pc Archaic Petra (AP)
Dahlia can equip 4pc AP in teams with a Geo unit (e.g., Xilonen).
2pc Mixed Sets
2pc Set Bonuses: HP%, Energy Recharge, Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City*
2pc mixed sets can be used if the other supportive sets are already held by other units in the team or if none of them would work anyway (e.g., in Wanderer teams).
*Requires a Natlan unit in the team.
Weapons
4★ Favonius Sword
Favonius Sword is the general best weapon for Dahlia due to his high ER requirements.
Note that due to Dahlia only having 2 hits with his EQ combo, it can be inconsistent to proc Favonius Sword without building additional CRIT Rate or performing a few Normal Attacks.
5★ Freedom-Sworn
Though this weapon lacks the ER that Favonius Sword offers, it provides a modest Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attack DMG Bonus and ATK% buff, provided that Dahlia triggers a reaction to activate its passive with both his Skill and Burst.
Note that the passive has a 20s cooldown, meaning in teams with a rotation shorter than 20s it will only trigger once every other rotation.
5★ Peak Patrol Song (PPS)
PPS can be a good option even with minimal DEF stats. However, opting for DEF stats instead of HP — thereby lowering Dahlia’s Attack SPD buff and his shield’s HP — can still result in a damage gain in certain teams.

Dahlia — DPS — Flex — Flex
Dahlia is best paired with characters who can utilize his (up to) 30% Attack SPD buff, e.g., on-field DPS units who mainly use their Normal Attacks. Outside of that, he can provide shields to any other on-field DPS. However, Dahlia is almost always outclassed in that role by units who provide more utility than just shielding.

Dahlia — Skirk — Escoffier — Furina
Dahlia is a viable Hydro option for Skirk, providing her with a modest Attack SPD buff and a shield; the latter also enables her to equip Calamity of Eshu, which is Dahlia’s biggest benefit.

Gameplay
Dahlia
’s gameplay revolves around providing
consistent shielding
,
team utility
, and
HP-scaling support effects
, while enabling his team’s survivability through his
Elemental Burst
. His kit focuses on maintaining the
Favonian Favor
state, which allows him to generate
refreshable shields
using his unique
Benison mechanic
, making him a reliable
defensive support
in sustained combat scenarios.
His
Elemental Skill, Immersive Ordinance
, summons a
Sacramental Shower
that provides utility depending on interaction. When enemies touch it, it deals
AoE Hydro DMG
, while allies can use it for
mobility
, being launched into the air. While not the core of his kit, this ability adds
situational Hydro application
and utility during rotations.
When Dahlia uses his
Elemental Burst, Radiant Psalter
, he deals
AoE Hydro DMG
and grants all party members
Favonian Favor
, summoning a
Shield of Sacred Favor
that scales with his
Max HP
. While this effect is active, Dahlia can generate
Benison stacks
through
Normal Attacks
or
Freeze reactions
, which are consumed to
automatically summon new shields
when the current one is broken, ensuring
continuous protection
for the team.
Dahlia’s
passive talents
further enhance his utility by increasing the team’s
Attack Speed
based on his
Max HP
and granting additional
Benison stacks
when triggering
Freeze reactions
. While his personal damage is limited, he performs best when built around
HP%
,
Energy Recharge
, and strong
team synergy
to maximize his overall support potential.

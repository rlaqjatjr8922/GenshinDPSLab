Introductions
Welcome to our team composition guide for The Dahlia in Honkai: Star Rail. In this guide, 
  we'll explore the best teams to build for The Dahlia, including recommended 
  team compositions, character synergies and everything you need to know 
  to let your Dahlia teams shine.
Best Synergies for The Dahlia
Firefly
Firefly is the character who receives the most effect out of Dahlia's A2 
      Trace
Outgrow the Old, Espouse the New
as it is designed 
      around Firefly using it. With the addition of The Dahlia, Firefly's best 
      teams are now entirely mono-fire too which makes damage significantly 
      more reliable.
Boothill
Boothill is a powerful Break Unit that can apply
Physical
Weakness which allows him to trigger Dahlia's A2 Trace
Outgrow the Old, Espouse the New
. As Boothill is is a
Physical
character he cannot get the full effect out of 
      this talent but the 30% SPD Boost is still very potent. Boothill benefits 
      significantly from being able to trigger Super Break at all times and
Rappa
Rappa is a strong Break DPS that deals high damage and gains an 
      incredible DPS boost from being able to deal Super Break DMG at all times 
      but she does not apply Weakness which means she cannot trigger Dahlia's 
      A2 Trace
Outgrow the Old, Espouse the New
to gain the SPD 
      boost making her synergise worse than other break DPS.
Fugue
Fugue is next strongest Super Break support as she offers a large boost 
      to Super Break damage, lowers enemy DEF, provides Break Effect to your 
      main DPS and allows for enemies to be weakness broken a second time 
      (with a toughness bar equal to 40% of max Toughness) which can trigger 
      all break effects an additional time.
Harmony Trailblazer
Harmony Trailblazer is a
Harmony
Support that enables your 
      entire team to deal Super Break Damage. The doubling up of Super Break 
      boosts increases Team DPS significantly. Harmony Trailblazer also does a 
      large amount of Toughness Damage thus doing a very large amount of Super 
      Break when played with Dahlia.
Silver Wolf
Silver Wolf is a
Nihility
debuffer that reduces enemy DEF, 
      All Type RES and implants Weaknesses which all boost the damage of break 
      characters. Additionally, as Silver Wolf implants weaknesses she will get 
      the SPD buff from Dahlia's A2 Trace
Outgrow the Old, Espouse the New
.
Despite getting the SPD buff Silver Wolf would not normally synergise 
      that well with Dahlia as she does not inherently have very high Toughness 
      damage per turn but with Silver Wolfs Eidolon 1 and the
Before the Tutorial Mission Starts
Lightcone Silver Wolf can use her 
      ultimate every turn which does a very significant amount of Toughness 
      damage, especially for AoE, each turn. This DPS is further boosted when 
      Silver Wolf runs
Eagle of Twilight Line
Relic Set as it a huge 
      effective SPD boost.
Ruan Mei
Ruan Mei is a
Harmony
Support that gives 50% Weakness Break 
      Efficiency to the entire team which will boost all Super Break damage 
      being done.
Lingsha
Lingsha is a
Abundance
Healer who does a large amount of 
      healing and AoE Toughness Damage while applying an increased Break DMG 
      taken debuff to all enemies which synergises well with any break team.
Gallagher
Gallagher is a
Abundance
Healer who can apply an increased 
      Break DMG taken vulnerability to enemies and does a large amount of 
      Single Target Toughness Damage which can do a lot of boss DPS.
The Dahlia's Best Teams
The Dahlia is an incredibly powerful unit made to support break teams, 
  however she is at her most powerful when supporting
Firefly
but this does not mean this is her only viable team. The Dahlia is the best 
  support for
all
Break Teams regardless of their element.
The Sustain Unit used for each team can be swapped out but
Lingsha
is the best healer for DPS in Break Teams but
Gallagher
is a fully viable option if you do not have 
  Lingsha and he still does good DPS.
This is not a comprehensive list of teams. The inclusion or exclusion of any
  given team does not necessarily reflect its power level.

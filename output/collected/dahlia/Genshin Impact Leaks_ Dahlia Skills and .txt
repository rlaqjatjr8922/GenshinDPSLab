Dahlia is an upcoming 4-star Hydro unit. He is a support-type unit who focuses on applying an HP-scaling Shield and increasing the rest of the team’s ATK SPD, which is one of the stats that is not touched upon much in the game. Dahlia seems like a simple character to play, and below you can find leaked information on his skills and constellations.

Best Quicken and Hyperbloom teams for Clorinde in Genshin Impact
Put together your hunting party.
Christine Choi
| Jun 6, 2024 2:09 pm
Screenshot via Genshin Impact YouTube
Clor
inde
is a 5-star
Electro DPS
in
Genshin Impact
who synergizes well with Dendro teammates. Here are the best teams for Clorinde focusing on two of her best reactions:
Aggravate and Hyperbloom
.
Recommended Videos
Teams for Quicken Clorinde
Aggravate is the easiest reaction for Clorinde to trigger consistently. All you need is an off-field Dendro teammate to apply the occasional hit of Dendro. Clorinde’s best Dendro teammates are
Nahida, Dendro Traveler,
Baizhu
,
and
Yaoyao
.
Kirara and Collei can also work, but they’re harder to recommend. Pick one or two of these Dendro teammates to trigger Quicken, which will enable Clorinde to trigger Aggravate.
Once you have your Dendro teammate(s) picked out, you can fill in with some flex units of your choice. I recommend an Anemo unit like
Sucrose
or
Kazuha
to amplify Clorinde’s Electro damage. Alternatively,
Zhongli’s
shield provides some defensive utility while making enemies weaker to all elements. You can also double up on Electro units with
Fischl
,
Beidou
, Yae Miko,
or
Kujou Sara.
Mix and match as you like!
Example teams for Quicken Clorinde
Clorinde – Nahida – Baizhu – Zhongli
Clorinde – Dendro Traveler – Sucrose – Beidou
Clorinde – Yaoyao – Sucrose – Fischl
Clorinde – Yaoyao – Kazuha – Kujou Sara
Image via HoYoverse
Teams for Hyperbloom Clorinde
Hyperbloom teams are similar to Quicken teams, but with added Hydro. When Hydro reacts with Dendro, it creates a Dendro Core on the ground. When Clorinde hits a Dendro Core with an attack, she will trigger Hyperbloom, dealing Dendro damage to enemies.
Since Hydro can remove the Quicken status from enemies, having constant Dendro application becomes more important. This increases the value of
Nahida
and
Dendro Traveler
, since these characters are better at applying Dendro from off-field. As with Quicken teams, pick one or two Dendro teammates, making sure you have at least one who can provide consistent off-field Dendro application.
Then, pick a Hydro teammate. My recommendations are
Furina
,
Xingqiu
,
Yelan
,
or
Kokomi
.
These characters are all capable of applying Hydro from off-field. Make sure you only have one Hydro unit, though, since too much Hydro can reduce Clorinde’s damage by interfering with Quicken.
Screenshot by Destructoid
If you still have an open team slot, and you have your Dendro and Hydro needs covered, feel free to add a flex unit of your choice.
Fischl
is still good, since Oz won’t target Dendro Cores, letting Clorinde trigger most of the Hyperblooms. Anemo units like
Sucrose
or
Kazuha
are great too.
Zhongli
makes for a good shielder, since he will increase both Clorinde’s Electro damage and Hyperbloom damage.
Example teams for Hyperbloom Clorinde
Clorinde – Nahida – Baizhu – Furina
Clorinde – Dendro Traveler – Xingqiu – Zhongli
Clorinde – Nahida – Kokomi – Fischl
Clorinde – Dendro Traveler – Yelan – Sucrose

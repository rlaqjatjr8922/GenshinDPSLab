▼ Hyperbloom With Furina
★★★★★
- Uses Clorinde with Furina to boost personal DMG of Clorinde.
- Dendro supports for Aggravate and frequent Hyperbloom damage.
↳ The DMG of Hyperbloom is lower than most comps but still competitive.

★★★★・
- Flexible team for Clorinde and also very powerful.

- Easy to proc and the team rotation is forgiving.

★★★・・

Powerful Personal DMG From Clorinde & Additional DMG Through Hyperbloom
This team comp provides plenty of DMG Bonus because of Furina while still having enough Dendro application for Clorinde to trigger Aggravate for higher damage. Baizhu is there for sustenance and Nahida for EM Buff and Dendro application.
Rotation Of Electro-Charged Clorinde
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida's Elemental Skill.
2
Switch to Furina and use Elemental Skill then Burst.
3
Switch to Baizhu and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
4
Switch to Clorinde and use Elemental Burst.
5
Use Clorinde's Skill then press Skill again to drain the BoL from Elemental Burst.
6
Perform 3NA → 1ES until the uptime of Elemental Skill of Clorinde rans out.
7
Repeat from no.1
This rotation ensures that plenty of Blooms are created while setting up Clorinde for damage.

Use Aggravate To Increase Electro DMG
This team is centered around Aggravate which will highly increase the damage of Clorinde and Fischl's attacks. Nahida will allow for continous Dendro application even when she's off the field, while Kazuha directly buffs Electro DMG with Swirl and shred.
Rotation Of Aggravate Clorinde
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida's Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Fischl and use her Skill
3
Switch to Kazuha and Elemental Skill to gather enemies and use his Burst
4
Next, quickly bring out Clorinde and use her Skill.
5
Perform 3NA → 1ES combo until the effect of Elemental Skill rans out.
*
Bring out Fischl and use her Burst if the Skill of Fischl is still on cooldown or she needs healing.
This rotation is pretty forgiving since Kazuha can only Swirl Electro, ensuring that there is always Electro Buff and Electro shred. Just make sure to always have Fischl's Skill and Nahida's skill before bringing out Clorinde to ensure it activates her buff and will have Aggravate|

High Explosive Damage With Overload Team
This team is very specific since it does require Chevreuse and specific Character elements. Thoma is the main sustain in this team and at C6, he will also provide Normal Attack buff and proc Overload with both Fischl and Clorinde. Clorinde will be the on-field DPS for this.
Rotation Of Overload Clorinde
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Fischl's skill
2
Switch to Chevreuse and use her Burst
3
Switch to Thoma and use his Skill and Burst
4
Next, switch to Clorinde and use her Burst then Skill.
5
Use E. Skill to remove the BoL then perform Normal Attack 3 times then Elemental Skill repeatedly until her stance expires.
6
Switch to Chevreuse and hold her Skill to create a large explosion
If low on health, just tap to get healing
So long as the buffs of Chevreuse is active when Clorinde is dealing damage, the rotation can be adjusted based on situation.

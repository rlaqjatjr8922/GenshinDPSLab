Clorinde is a 5-star Electro sword character that works best as a main-DPS. As mentioned, her kit revolves around the Bond of Life mechanic - meaning she doesn't always get on well with healers. However, she does have an ability to heal herself, so you don't necessarily need a healer for every team competition.
Team 1: Aggravate
Main DPS:
Clorinde
Sub DPS:
Fischl
Sub DPS:
Nahida
Support:
Kazuha
Clorinde's damage comes primarily from
electro
as well as its application, meaning that any team focusing on electro-related reactions will do well to support her.
Aggravate
, in particular, works well as Clorinde kickstarts the reaction by following her teammates' application of the elements.
In addition, with two electro characters on the team, the
elemental resonance
effect generates more
elemental particles
whenever there's an electro-related reaction.
Kazuha
may be an addition to a lot of teams, but there's a good reason why; with the high damage already achieved from
Clorine
,
Kazuha
will apply
Anemo
which will create the
swirl
reaction for even more damage when combined with the electro element.
With Clorinde's
C1
, her normal attacks under the effect of
Night Vigil
will unleash additional electro attacks - for even more damage. To turn
Clorinde
into a character who can destroy virtually every enemy with ease, her
C6
will give her a whopping
70 percent increase in Crit Damage;
combined with her
C2
, you can expect to do even more damage whenever a party member triggers an
electro-related reaction
.
Character
Action
Nahida
Start by throwing down Nahida's Elemental Burst, followed by holding down her Elemental Skill and tagging all enemies with it.
Fischl
Immediately use Fischl's Elemental Skill to summon Oz.
Kazuha
Follow up with Kazuha's elemental skill, plunging attack, and then his Elemental Burst.
Clorinde
Upon switching to Clorinde, use her Elemental Burst to give yourself a Bond of Life. Immediately follow up with her Elemental Skill, and use a lunge attack. Shoot a few pistol shots and then alternate back to another lunge. Repeat this until you leave the Night Vigil state.
Team 2: Hyperbloom
Main DPS:
Clorinde
Sub DPS:
Furina
Sub DPS:
Nahida
Support:
Baizhu
Hyperbloom
is always a reliable option for doing some damage, benefiting from three different elements and the damage from
Dendro Cores
. This team in particular maximizes on having
Clorinde
remain on the field for as long as possible, and both
Furina
and
Nahida
have long durations of their Elemental Skills and bursts, with
Baizhu
only being required to join in a pinch.
Two
Dendro
characters also allows for increased
Elemental Mastery
, which increases even more whenever the
Bloom
reaction is achieved - allowing for almost
100 extra Elemental Mastery
. Furina's
Elemental Skill
allows for almost uninterrupted
Hydro application
, so if you've built Furina correctly using the
Golden Troupe
artifact set,
having her off-field gives her an even bigger increase to her skill damage
.
Furina's
C2
is her best addition to this team, as it lets her hit the cap of her
Let the People Rejoice
faster. You can have Furina gain up to
140 percent Max HP
, making for a dramatic increase to her skill damage.

Nahida
being on the team acts as your main applicator of
Dendro
- thus allowing you to create the
Dendro Cores
whenever an enemy she's attacked is hit by Furina's minions. Nahida's
C2
allows for
Hyperbloom
to hit
Critical hits
, something that will grant massive extra damage for your team.
You'll also lower the enemy's
DEF
whenever the
quickening
effect happens too; overall, if you're getting over
800 Elemental Mastery
for your Nahida, then your reactions will do a great deal of damage. Finally,
Baizhu
is on the team as support (both to
heal
and
shield
), and his C2
is a great addition, as it limits the amount of time he needs to be out on the field - by allowing for more healing
every time an enemy gets hit
.
Since Furina constantly drains everyone's
HP
with her skill, Baizhu is there to balance things out.
Character
Action
Furina
Start by summoning Furina's Salon Solitaire Elemental Skill. Follow up with her burst.
Nahida
Use Nahida's burst, and then hold down her Elemental Skill and hit every opponent.
Baizhu
Switch to Baizhu and throw down one of his totems with his Elemental Skill. If you're in a pinch, use his Burst too.
Clorinde
Finish by switching to Clorinde and use her Elemental Burst to give yourself a Bond of Life. Immediately follow up with her Elemental Skill, and use a lunge attack. Shoot a few pistol shots and then alternate back to another lunge. Repeat this until you leave the Night Vigil state.
Team 3: Electro-Charged
Main DPS:
Clorinde
Sub DPS:
Raiden
Sub DPS:
Furina
Support:
Kazuha
Once you've ascended Clorinde once, you'll unlock
Dark Shattering Flame
, which is the key to utilizing her as an Electro carry. With this skill, whenever you trigger an electro reaction, the electro damage dealt from her normal attacks and burst are increased by 20 percent of her attack - up to a maximum increase of 1,800.
Although you can swap
Raiden
for someone like
Xingqiu
or
Yelan
, Furina's hydro application is so strong that you don't need two hydro characters. You can also then instead benefit from
Raiden's
additional elemental damage from her skill.
However, with
Furina
draining everyone's HP with her skill, this team is better suited to
quick fights and bosses
as opposed to longer-timed battles - as the latter will require
Furina
to switch to healing
whenever you take damage
.
Character
Action
Raiden Shogun
Begin your rotation by using Raiden's Elemental Skill.
Furina
Switch to Furina and use her Elemental Skill, and then her Burst.
Kaedehara Kazuha
Next, move to Kazuha and use his Elemental Skill, followed by a plunging attack. Finish with his elemental Burst.
Clorinde
Finish by switching to Clorinde and use her Elemental Burst to give yourself a Bond of Life. Immediately follow up with her Elemental Skill, and use a lunge attack. Shoot a few pistol shots and then alternate back to another lunge. Repeat this until you leave the Night Vigil state.
Team 4: Double Geo Carry
Main DPS:
Clorinde
Sub DPS:
Fischl
Sub DPS:
Chiori
Support:
Zhongli
A slightly more unconventional team, this group of characters relies on
buffs
and
sustains
to highlight Clorinde - as opposed to
elemental reactions
. Having
Zhongli
on the team also gives a bit of safety with his shield, which is one of the strongest in the game, so if you've got his
C2
, then it will also give you a shield as soon as you use his Elemental Skill.
If you want to take full advantage of Clorinde's Tamato doll skill damage, make sure you always have a Geo Construct from Zhongli active.
Fischl is on the team to deal additional
electro damage
, as well as generate more
Elemental Shards
to help shield your characters. However, make sure you have her
C6
to have the most Electro damage possible for Fischl to deal.
Character
Action
Zhongli
Use Zhongli's Elemental Skill to bring up a shield for your party members. You can also use his Burst if you have it ready.
Fischl
Switch to Fischl and use her Elemental Skill.
Chiori
Immediately use Chiori's Burst, followed by tapping her Elemental Skill twice to speedily switch to Clorinde and you'll summon two Tamato dolls - if Zhongli's Dominus Lapidis is still active.
Clorinde
Finish by switching to Clorinde and use her Elemental Burst to give yourself a Bond of Life. Immediately follow up with her Elemental Skill, and use a lunge attack. Shoot a few pistol shots and then alternate back to another lunge. Repeat this until you leave the Night Vigil state.

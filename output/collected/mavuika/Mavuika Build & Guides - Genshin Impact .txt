Mavuika has two main build options depending on how you want to use her:
a Main DPS build focused on maximizing her on-field Burst damage, and a
Support/Sub-DPS build focused on buffing the active character and providing
off-field Pyro application.

How to Play Mavuika
Mavuika has two main playstyles: an on-field Main DPS style where she acts as the
primary damage dealer, and a support/off-field damage dealer style where she
buffs the active character while providing Pyro application and extra damage.
Her Main DPS playstyle is one of the strongest archetypes in the game. Unlike
most DPS units, Mavuika does not rely on Energy Recharge for her Elemental Burst.
Instead, she uses
Fighting Spirit
. Fighting Spirit can be generated by
Mavuika herself, and it is also generated efficiently when you pair her with at
least one Natlan teammate who consumes Nightsoul points. Once you have at least
50% Fighting Spirit, you can unleash her Burst to deal enhanced damage and
enter her high-damage window with increased ATK.
Her basic on-field rotation is to set up your supports while generating Fighting
Spirit, then use Mavuika’s Burst, followed by her Skill, and deal damage during
the next ~7 seconds. During this window, you can use multiple combos depending
on comfort and investment. A common baseline combo is using her Charged Attack
spin, while more advanced options include techniques like dash-canceling Charged
Attacks to increase damage and speed up inputs.
Her second playstyle is her support mode. In this setup, you mainly use her Burst
and then swap into your main DPS. With full Fighting Spirit, Mavuika can provide
up to a 40% damage bonus to the active character. At the same time, she continues
to contribute off-field Pyro application and damage, making her a strong option
for reaction teams and Natlan DPS teams that help charge her Burst quickly.

The arrival of Mavuika, the Pyro Archon, in Genshin Impact 5.3 has shaken up the meta with her remarkable flexibility as a DPS character. Whether you’re looking to utilize her as a primary damage dealer or integrate her into complex elemental reaction-based teams, Mavuika’s potential is vast. This guide breaks down the best team compositions for both premium and free-to-play (f2p) players, offering in-depth strategies to maximize Mavuika's potential in the current patch.
Understanding Mavuika’s Kit
Before diving into team compositions, it's crucial to understand Mavuika's abilities to fully exploit her strengths in different team setups:
Elemental Skill
: Mavuika summons a Pyro-infused area that deals continuous Pyro damage to nearby enemies while increasing the damage of subsequent Pyro-related reactions.
Elemental Burst
: Mavuika unleashes a powerful Pyro AoE attack that scales with her ATK and provides a unique buff to Pyro characters, further enhancing their elemental damage for a period of time.
Passive Talents
: Mavuika’s passive talents provide additional buffs to Pyro-related reactions and offer increased energy regeneration when paired with specific elements like Hydro or Cryo.
Understanding these abilities helps determine the best team compositions to optimize Mavuika’s damage output and reaction potential. Now, let’s explore the best premium and free-to-play team comps that make the most of her versatile skill set.
Best Premium Teams for Mavuika
These teams feature 5-star characters who synergize well with Mavuika's Pyro abilities, focusing on maximizing elemental reactions and overall DPS potential.
1.
Mavuika "Vaporize" Team
Mavuika (Main DPS)
Yelan (Sub-DPS/Hydro Enabler)
Nahida (Sub-DPS/Support)
Bennett (Support/Healer)
This team maximizes the
Vaporize
reaction, which can yield incredibly high damage output, especially when applied correctly. Yelan provides consistent Hydro application through her Elemental Skill, while Nahida boosts elemental reactions with her Dendro-based abilities. Bennett not only provides healing but also buffs the team's ATK through his Burst, increasing Mavuika’s damage potential significantly.
Unique Insights
:
Artifact Suggestions
: Mavuika benefits greatly from
Crimson Witch of Flames
for increased Pyro damage. Yelan, on the other hand, should ideally use
Emblem of Severed Fate
to boost her energy recharge and damage output.
Weapon Suggestions
: The
Staff of Homa
is a perfect weapon choice for Mavuika, as it boosts both her Crit DMG and ATK. Meanwhile, Yelan can benefit from
The Stringless
for increased elemental skill and burst damage.
2.
Mavuika "Melt" Team
Mavuika (Main DPS)
Ganyu (Sub-DPS/Cryo Enabler)
Kazuha (Support/Anemo)
Diona (Support/Shielder/Healer)
This team is designed to exploit the
Melt
reaction, which can deal devastating damage when Cryo and Pyro are combined. Ganyu provides a solid Cryo application, while Kazuha’s ability to group enemies and increase elemental damage through his Anemo abilities complements the team’s synergy. Diona provides both shields and healing, ensuring that the team stays alive in the heat of battle.
Unique Insights
:
Cryo Application
: Ganyu’s charged shots are ideal for freezing enemies in place, allowing Mavuika to unleash high-damage Melt reactions.
Kazuha's Role
: Kazuha’s Swirl can absorb the Pyro element, boosting Mavuika’s damage further. His Anemo damage also increases elemental mastery across the team.
Swap Ganyu
: Against enemies that can't be frozen, consider switching out Ganyu for
Ayaka
or
Shenhe
for a more reliable Melt trigger.
Best Free-to-Play Teams for Mavuika
If you’re a free-to-play (F2P) player or someone who doesn’t have access to premium 5-star characters, fear not! There are still plenty of viable team compositions that can work wonders with Mavuika.
1.
Mavuika "Overload" Team
Mavuika (Main DPS)
Fischl (Sub-DPS/Electro Enabler)
Sucrose (Support/Anemo)
Barbara (Support/Healer)
This
Overload
team focuses on creating constant explosions with Fischl’s Electro application and Mavuika’s Pyro attacks. Sucrose’s Swirl can group enemies and further increase elemental mastery, amplifying reaction damage, while Barbara provides healing through her Elemental Skill and Burst. This team excels against large groups of enemies, as Overload can hit multiple targets at once.
Unique Insights
:
Artifact Suggestions
: Mavuika can benefit from the
Crimson Witch of Flames
set, while Fischl should run
Thundering Fury
for better Electro reaction potential.
Energy Recharge
: Consider using
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
on Barbara to boost Mavuika’s ATK and energy generation, ensuring smoother rotations.
2.
Mavuika "Burning" Team
Mavuika (Main DPS)
Dendro Traveler (Sub-DPS/Dendro Enabler)
Collei (Sub-DPS/Dendro Enabler)
Bennett (Support/Healer)
This team focuses on the
Burning
reaction, which triggers when Dendro and Pyro elements interact. Dendro Traveler and Collei provide constant Dendro application, while Bennett buffs the team's damage output and heals when necessary. Though the Burning reaction isn't as powerful as other elemental interactions, it is still a viable option, particularly against enemies weak to Dendro.
Unique Insights
:
Elemental Mastery
: Consider using
Instructor's Set
on Bennett to boost the team’s elemental mastery and further increase Burning damage.
Synergy with Dendro
: The Dendro Traveler and Collei’s Dendro application can also synergize well with Mavuika’s abilities, setting up additional elemental reactions for consistent damage output.
Conclusion
Mavuika's arrival in Genshin Impact 5.3 opens up a world of possibilities for team compositions. Whether you opt for premium 5-star teams or more accessible free-to-play setups, there are numerous ways to maximize her potential. Remember, the key to success with Mavuika is flexibility—her versatility allows her to fit into a variety of team compositions depending on your available characters and playstyle. Don't be afraid to experiment with different setups to find the one that best suits your needs.

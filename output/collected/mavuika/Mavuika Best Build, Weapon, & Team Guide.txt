Roles & Playstyle Of Mavuika and Its Strengths and Weakness
On-Field & DPS Mavuika
Mavuika's best role is an On-Field Damage Dealer. She has access to both huge instantaneous damage with her Elemental Burst and sustained damage from Charged Attack. This makes Mavuika one of the best characters in the role no matter what content you are facing.
Pros and Cons of On-Field / DPS Mavuika
Pros
- Top Class pyro DMG and is among the best in-game.
- Amazing in both Single Target (Bosses) and AoE (Multiple Target).
- Easy to use and amazing at any type of contents.
Cons
- For consistency, you are required to put her in a team with Multiple Characters that consumes
Nightsoul Points
.
ＬThis makes Mavuika's teams restricted and her damage potential locked on specific characters.
- Long Combo and on-field time compared to other DPS.
Off-Field, Sub-DPS, & Support Mavuika
Mavuika also functions as a decent off-field character providing a total damage buff of  80% damage buff with
Scroll Of The Hero Of Cinder City
and Elemental Burst while also dealing huge damage and off-field Pyro application to enemies.
Pros and Cons of Off-Field Mavuika
Pros
- Ability to provide huge amount of damage bonus.
- Wide range off-field damage and works in both single and multiple targets.
- Even in support build, she can still produce powerful one-shot damage with Elemental Burst.
Cons
- Compared to her competitors like Xiangling, she only apply Pyro on enemies once every 3 seconds.
Ｌ Makes Mavuika not very good on teams that requires plenty of Pyro like teams with Tartaglia.
- The damage buff from Elemental Burst decreases with time.
- The Uptime of Elemental Skill is short making it not enough when used on teams that has longer on-field.

Team Rotation of On-Field Mavuika
Basic Rotation Of Mavuika
1. Use Elemental Skill (Tap) then change characters.
2. Use other characters to charge Mavuika's Fighting Spirit to 100~200.
3. Switch back to Mavuika and use Elemental Burst.
4. Deal damage with Normal Attack or Charged Attack (
CA deals powerful damage!
)
As an on-field Character, you will normally
use the abilities of the support characters in between Mavuika's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst
. Use Elemental Skill to activate Nightsoul State of Mavuika, switch to a different character use their skills then move to Mavuika to use Elemental Burst and proceed attacking.
Optimal Combo for On-Field Mavuika
Recommended Combo
Elemental Skill → Switch to Supports → Elemental Burst → Charged Attack x8 → Finishing Attack → Repeat
The most simple yet effective combo for Mavuika is using Elemental Burst then straight to doing Charged Attack. You can opt to finish the Charged Attack faster by stop holding on the attack button. Charged Attack has bigger damage percentage and wider AoE than Normal Attack so it is recommended to use it over Normal Attacks.
For Off-Field Use Elemental Skill then Switch to Other Characters
Recommended Combo
Elemental Skill → Elemental Burst →Switch to DPS → Repeat
When using Mavuika Off-field, it is recommended to use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst before switching to the Main DPS. Keep in mind that to maximize the Buff from Elemental Burst, make sure to have a maximum amount of Fighting Spirit in your Mavuika.

Basics of Using Mavuika (Beginner Guide)
Mavuika is a character from the nation in Teyvat called Natlan. In Natlan, all of the characters is deeply related to the "Night-soul" Mechanics.   Specifically for Mavuika, Nightsoul allows her to ride her Motorcycle (Flamestrider) and the consumption of Nightsoul Points charges "Fighting Spirit", the Energy-equivalent status used uniquely by Mavuika.
What Is Nightsoul State / Nightsoul Points for Mavuika?
Mavuika will be in Nightsoul State upon using Elemental Skill (both Tap and Hold).
She will have a glowing hair while in this state and a gauge will appear beside her. The gauge beside her shows the amount of Nightsoul Points she has and it will continuously decrease. Reaching 0 Nightsoul point will release her Nightsoul State.
▼ In-Game Description of Nightsoul State of Mavuika
What is Fighting Spirit and How to Generate it Quickly?
Mavuika, unlike other Genshin Impact Characters, does not use any Elemental Energy. Instead, she uses a special energy called "Fighting Spirit".  Because of this gimmick, Mavuika does not use Energy Recharge Sub-Stat in Artifacts.
To charge Fighting Spirit, you can either consume Nightsoul Points or use Normal Attacks from other characters.

Who Is Mavuika?
The Current Pyro Archon
Bearing the names "Haborym" and "Kiongozi," Mavuika is Natlan's current Pyro Archon. She is an active participant in the nation's matters and leads it in its fight against the Abyss.
A Human Archon
Mavuika is a human turned Archon, similar to that of the Pyro Archons before her. She was part of the Huitztlan or the Scions of the Canopy prior to being the Pyro Archon.
A Powerful Warrior
Mavuika is among the strongest characters canonically in Genshin's lore. The nation is well-versed in her great deeds and she never fails to live up to their expectations. She was even able to go toe-to-toe with Il Capitano, who is known for being tremendously strong, and come out on top.

Mavuika's Role in Genshin Impact
Mavuika is primarily designed as a
main DPS

Skill Priority and Ascension Materials
Mavuika’s abilities are designed for heavy elemental damage, and it’s important to prioritize her skills based on their role in combat.
Skill Priority:
Normal Attack: Flames Weave Life (Priority: 3)
Mavuika’s Normal Attack chain is crucial for her sustained damage, especially during charged attacks. This is your primary source of damage in longer fights.
Elemental Skill: The Named Moment (Priority: 2)
Her Elemental Skill is an important part of her damage rotation, as it creates a Pyro AoE effect and increases her total DPS.
Elemental Burst: Hour of Burning Skies (Priority: 1)
Her Elemental Burst is the highlight of her kit, dealing massive AoE Pyro damage. Prioritize leveling this skill to maximize Mavuika's burst potential.
Ascension Materials:

Constellations and How They Affect Mavuika
While Mavuika is strong even at
Constellation 0
, unlocking her constellations can enhance her playstyle. Here’s what each Constellation provides:
C1 - Fiery Surge
: Increases the damage of her Elemental Burst significantly when her Normal or Charged attacks hit an enemy. This makes her burst even more powerful in combat.
C2 - Blazing Fury
: Reduces the cooldown of her Elemental Skill after using her Elemental Burst, allowing her to use her Elemental Skill more frequently and trigger more reactions.
C3 - Ember Ascendant
: Increases the level of her Elemental Burst, enhancing her burst damage.
C4 - Ignition
: Adds an additional layer of Pyro damage after using her Elemental Burst, improving her sustained DPS.
C5 - Inferno
: Increases the level of her Elemental Skill, making her AoE Pyro damage more potent.
C6 - Scorched Dominion
: Provides a significant Pyro DMG bonus to her abilities after using her Elemental Burst, turning Mavuika into a true powerhouse.
If you can, aim for

The Pyro Archon of Natlan, Mavuika, rides into Genshin Impact 5.3 as a 5-star Claymore wielder who can dominate the battlefield with blazing AoE and nuking potential. Her dual-role flexibility as a Main DPS or Sub-DPS, combined with her signature motorcycle and energy-free Burst, makes her one of the most dynamic units ever introduced. Here’s a deep-dive guide to Mavuika’s builds, teams, weapons, and playstyle.
Mavuika Genshin Impact Guide: Best Builds, Weapons, Teams & Combos
Character Overview
Element:
Pyro
Weapon:
Claymore
Role:
Main DPS / Sub-DPS
Mechanic:
Uses “Fighting Spirit” instead of Energy Recharge
Release Version:
5.3
Constellation:
Sol Invictus
Mavuika’s kit features an alternate energy system called Fighting Spirit, gained through allies’ actions. Her Elemental Burst is a nuke followed by enhanced Pyro attacks while riding her Flamestrider, providing massive field presence.

Talents and Abilities
Here’s how her kit works and the mechanics that define her gameplay.
Elemental Skill (The Named Moment):
Choose between Tap for support or Hold to ride Flamestrider for full DPS mode.
Tap: Pyro Ring for off-field AoE damage (Nightsoul-aligned Pyro)
Hold: Ride Flamestrider for Pyro-infused Normal/Charged attacks and terrain traversal
Elemental Burst (Hour of Burning Skies):
A powerful nuke that fuels her Crucible state for huge follow-up damage.
Costs 50–100+ Fighting Spirit (not Energy)
Massive AoE nuke + activates Crucible of Death and Life
Converts all attacks into boosted Pyro, with full interruption resistance
Fighting Spirit Mechanics:
Understand how to build and manage the resource that powers her Burst.
Gained when party members consume Nightsoul points or land Normal Attacks
1.5 Spirit gained per Normal Attack (max 200)
Burst is stronger the more Spirit you consume
Passives:
Support and traversal bonuses that round out her kit.
Gift of Flaming Flowers: +30% ATK for 10s when ally triggers a Nightsoul Burst
Kiongozi: Post-Burst, team gets up to 40% DMG Bonus for 20s based on Spirit used
Night Realm’s Gift: Automatically gains Nightsoul points when terrain traversal triggers
Utility Passive: Reduces Nightsoul Transmission cooldown for the whole party by 20%
Constellation Highlights
Invest in her constellations to dramatically increase her effectiveness across both roles.
C1: +25% Fighting Spirit gain, +40% ATK boost
C2: DEF shred (20%) with Ring; Burst/Skill ATK scaling boost (60–120%)
C3: +3 Burst levels
C4: Kiongozi buff no longer decays, adds +10% DMG
C5: +3 Skill levels
C6: Extra AoE Pyro explosions during Flamestrider & Ring; restores 80 Nightsoul points when not in combat
C2 is highly impactful for both Off-field utility and On-field DMG ceiling. C6 is game-breaking.
Combos & Rotations
Here are some sample move sequences to make the most of her Burst uptime.
Melt Combo:
Use this to optimize Cryo-triggered damage with high-speed dashes.
Q > C3F > Dash > C3F (timed with Cryo application from Citlali or Rosaria)
Simplified Vape Combo:
Best for Hydro reactions with less mechanical complexity.
Q > Jump > C8 or C4 > Jump > C4 (lets Hydro apply first)
Rotation Tips:
Optimize her uptime and team support synergy.
Open with Tap Skill > Run support rotations (Citlali/Bennett) > Burst at full Spirit > Ride and spam Charged Attacks
Off-Field Sub-DPS Use:
Keep it simple when she’s not the star.
E > Q (when needed) > swap out
Exploration Utility
Take advantage of her traversal-focused mechanics to explore Natlan like no other character.
Mavuika’s Flamestrider lets her ride over water, mountains, and fly short distances
Nightsoul Transmission gives instant remount and restores 50–80 Nightsoul points (every 10–15s)
Perfect for overworld mobility in Natlan, matching or exceeding other Archons
Final Thoughts
Mavuika is not just a
fiery spectacle
; she’s a new standard for Pyro carries. Her unique energy mechanics, mobility, and nuking capability make her a top-tier pick for players willing to invest in synergies. Whether blazing through Melt comps or shredding enemies with Scroll buffs, Mavuika delivers style and substance in equal measure.
She outshines older Pyro DPS like Xiangling and rivals Arlecchino in raw potential, though she’s more dependent on Natlan units and team setup.
Prepare your teams, master her Burst timing, and let the Night-Igniting Flame reign supreme!

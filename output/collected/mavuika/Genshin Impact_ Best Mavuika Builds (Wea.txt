Obtaining the best build for Mavuika in
Genshin Impact
is an important step in optimizing her as a damage dealer for your team comps, regardless of whether she operates as a main DPS or a sub-DPS. The Pyro Archon became a playable character in Version 5.3 following her debut as an NPC in the Natlan story arc. As confirmed by HoYoverse,
Mavuika is a versatile character who can operate as an on-field DPS or an off-field Pyro sub-DPS
.
Mavuika’s kit in
Genshin Impact
allows such flexibility, but it depends on players to focus on either aspect of her abilities.
With her Elemental Skill, Mavuika can either summon a Ring of Searing Radiance to deal constant Nightsoul-aligned Pyro DMG to nearby enemies, even when she is off-field, or summon her Flamestrider to imbue her Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks with Nightsoul-aligned Pyro DMG.
While both roles in Mavuika’s kit see her dealing damage, there are some subtle changes to her build depending on which job she is tackling
, so it’s necessary to build her adequately in order to take advantage of her powers. Of course, this comes after you obtain
Mavuika’s materials in
Genshin Impact
and Ascend her.
Best Weapons For Mavuika (Best Claymores)
Look For Claymores With Great Offensive Stats
Genshin Impact's Mavuika smiles and moves forward with an explosion in the background.
Custom Image by Bruno Yonezawa
The best weapon for Mavuika is her signature 5-star claymore, A Thousand Blazing Suns
. This weapon has a high base ATK stat (741 at level 90) and a useful CRIT Rate sub-stat (11% at level 90). Furthermore, when Mavuika uses her Elemental Skill or Burst, she gains Scorching Brilliance, which boosts her CRIT DMG by 20% and her ATK by 28% for six seconds – the effect can be triggered once every 10 seconds. Scorching Brilliance’s duration can be extended by two seconds for every Normal or Charged Attack that deals Elemental DMG, up to six seconds.

The best 4-star weapon for Mavuika’s on-field DPS role is Serpent Spine
. The weapon offers a low base ATK (510 at level 90) but a huge CRIT Rate sub-stat (27.6% at level 90). With Serpent Spine, every four seconds Mavuika is on the field, she will deal 6% more DMG and take 3% more DMG, up to five stacks. For both her DPS and sub-DPS roles, you can also equip Mavuika with
Earth Shaker in
Genshin Impact
, which has a 565 base ATK (at level 90) and a 27.6% ATK sub-stat (at level 90).
When a nearby ally triggers a Pyro-related reaction (which only needs to interact with Mavuika’s Pyro abilities, for example), Mavuika’s Elemental Skill DMG is increased by 16%. This effect can be triggered even if Mavuika is off-field.
While these are the recommended weapons for Mavuika, you have other options
. Look for weapons that increase offensive stats like CRIT and ATK, and that can potentially benefit her damage output even when she is off-field in
Genshin Impact
.
Best Artifacts & Stats For Mavuika
Artifacts Vary Depending On Her Role In Team Comps
Genshin Impact's Mavuika smiles with her arms open as flames burn behind her.
Custom Image by Bruno Yonezawa
Choosing an Artifact set for Mavuika will depend on her role in team comps.
For her on-field DPS role, the best Artifact set for Mavuika is currently Obsidian Codex
. The set’s two-piece bonus increases her DMG by 15% when she is in her Nightsoul’s Blessing state and on the field. The set’s four-piece bonus increases Mavuika’s CRIT Rate by a whopping 40% for six seconds when she consumes a Nightsoul point, an effect that can trigger every second.
Obsidian Codex Artifacts in
Genshin Impact
have been a staple for Natlan DPS units.

However, there is a big problem with the Obsidian Codex set when applied to Mavuika. When the Pyro Archon uses her Elemental Burst, she enters the Crucible of Life and Death state. In this state, Mavuika stops consuming Nightsoul points to keep her Nightsoul’s Blessing state active for seven seconds. This means that,
after using her Burst, she will be unable to gain the 40% CRIT Rate bonus from the set’s four-piece bonus because she cannot consume Nightsoul points
. In turn, this will likely force players to hold back on using Mavuika’s Burst in
Genshin Impact
.
This issue could be resolved in the future with the release of a new Artifact set for Natlan DPS units that does not punish Mavuika’s lack of Nightsoul point consumption following her Burst.
If you are using Mavuika as an off-field sub-DPS, the best Artifact set is Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
. With its two-piece bonus, when a party member triggers a Nightsoul Burst, Mavuika restores 6 Elemental Energy – which is useless for her since her Burst is charged by a unique mechanic called Fighting Spirit. With
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
’s four-piece bonus, when Mavuika triggers a Pyro-related reaction, all nearby allies gain a 12% Elemental DMG Bonus for the elements involved in the reaction for 15 seconds.
If Mavuika is in the Nightsoul’s Blessing state when triggering the bonus, all nearby allies gain an extra 28% Elemental DMG Bonus for the elements in the reaction for 20 seconds. Mavuika can trigger this effect while off-field.
The effects from Artifact sets of the same name don’t stack
– if players are running Kachina or Xilonen with Mavuika, only one of the character’s sets will activate.

When farming either of the Artifact sets for Mavuika, look for CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK, and Pyro DMG
. These are the priority stats for Mavuika’s build as both a DPS and sub-DPS and what determines her damage output. A bit of Elemental Mastery can also help you increase the power of the elemental reactions she triggers with her allies in
Genshin Impact
– this can be particularly helpful in an Overloaded, Burning, or Burgeon team comp, for example.
Best Team Comps For Mavuika
The Pyro Archon Is Versatile
Because of Mavuika’s build and kit, she is a versatile character in both her roles.
For her DPS role, it’s best to consider a useful sub-DPS that can constantly guarantee elemental application to interact with Mavuika’s Pyro DMG
. A Burning team with Emily, for example, can work exceptionally well. In her sub-DPS role, you can put her in a variety of team comps.
It is worth noting that Mavuika’s ATK can be boosted by 30% for 10 seconds when an ally triggers Nightsoul Burst, so having Natlan characters like Mualani or
Kinich in
Genshin Impact
could improve her off-field value.

Skill Priority For Mavuika
The Character’s Elemental Skill Is The Most Important Part Of Her Tool Kit
Genshin Impact's Mavuika holds her glasses as she rides her motorcycle.
Custom Image by Bruno Yonezawa
When leveling up Mavuika’s abilities, prioritize improving her Elemental Skill above all else, regardless of her role in team comps
. The next step is to increase her Elemental Burst, which is a powerful nuke to execute in battles. The last order of business is improving her Normal Attacks, which should not be overlooked.

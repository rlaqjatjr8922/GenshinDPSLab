Yoimiya is a 5-star Bow-wielding Pyro character in
Genshin Impact
. She is the owner of Naganohara Fireworks, and is known as the "Queen of the Summer Festival". Let's take a look at what materials you will need to ascend and fully upgrade Yoimiya's talents, and where you can get them.

Where To Find Yoimiya's Ascension and Talent Materials
Gemstones and Boss Loot
Agnidus Agate Slivers, Fragments, Chunks, and Gemstones
can be received from the Pyro Regisvine and
Pyro Hypostasis
bosses. Both of these bosses require 40 Original Resin to obtain rewards from.
Additionally, the Pyro Hypostasis boss will drop
Smoldering Pearl.
Fighting the
Hypostasis
rather than the Regisvine will be more efficient for gathering Yoimiya's materials. This boss is found in Kannazuka, Inazuma.
Common Drops and Books
Naku Weed
is an Inazuma specialty flower. It can be found around Lightning Trees, and near Tatarasuna in Inazuma.
Teachings, Guides, and Philosophies of Transience
can be obtained from the Violet Court Domain on Monday, Thursday, and Sunday. This Domain is located on a small island to the west of Inazuma City, and costs either 20 Original Resin or one Condensed Resin to obtain rewards from.
Divining, Sealed, and Forbidden Curse Scrolls
can be obtained from Samachurl enemies. You can find this enemy all over Teyvat, usually accompanied by Hilichurls.
Mora and Experience Books
Yoimiya will need around
seven million Mora
to fully upgrade her talents and ascend her to level 90. You can get Mora by doing almost anything in the game, but the best place to gather it is the
Ley Line Outcrop: Blossom of Wealth
. This will cost 20 Original Resin or one Condensed Resin to open.
To get Yoimiya to level 90, you will need the following
Experience Books.
414 Hero's Wit
15 Adventurer's Experience
12 Wanderer's Advice
You can get Experience Books from things like the Battle Pass, Daily Commissions, and side quests. The best place to obtain a lot of Experience Books is the
Ley Line Outcrop: Blossom of Revelation
. You'll have to spend 20 Original Resin or one Condensed Resin to open this Ley Line Outcrop.
Rare Items
Dragon Lord's Crown
is a reward from the weekly
Beneath the Dragon-Queller Domain
. You can only face Azhdaha once a week, and this material is not a guaranteed drop. Be sure to challenge this domain every week to ensure you have enough Dragon Lord's Crown to upgrade Yoimiya's talents.
Crown of Insight
is an extremely rare item that can only be obtained from time-limited events. The event Thunder Sojourn is currently ongoing and is a rare opportunity to receive a Crown of Insight, so don't miss out.

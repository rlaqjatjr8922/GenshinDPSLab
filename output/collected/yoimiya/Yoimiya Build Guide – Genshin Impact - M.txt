Yoimiya’s Skills
Normally,
every bow user has the same fundamental attack abilities, but Yoimiya is unique
. Her normal attack consists of five successive bow strokes. Her charged strike adds charge level on top of the regular.
Charging further will result in up to three Kindling Arrows that will hunt out opponents and inflict Pyro damage.
Niwabi Fire Dance – Yoimiya’s Elemental Skill
Yoimiya holds a sparkler and envelops herself in saltpeter. All regular attacks are transformed into
Blazing Arrows, which deliver Pyro damage and have a higher damage output.
Kindling Arrows cannot be generated while active.
Ryuukin Saxifarge – Yoimiya’s Elemental Burst
Yoimiya hurls fireworks that burst and inflict AoE Pyro damage
to one opponent, marking them with Auros Blaze.
Any attack, Skill, or Burst used by a character other than Yoimiya that connects with a Blaze-marked adversary causes an explosion. This may happen every two seconds.
If the opponent dies before the effect wears off, the effect is passed on to another close foe.

Yoimiya Build – All About Weapons & Artifacts
Yoimiya may be built in a few different ways, depending on whether
you want to optimize elemental damage or just utilize her as a high DPS character

Weapons
The
new forgeable Demon Slayer Bow is a strong contender for the greatest Yoimiya weapon
. The refining skill of Demon Slayer significantly improves regular and charged attack damage. Because it is easier to get than other four-star weapons, maxing up the refining skill is simpler.
Artifacts
Yoimiya’s apparent option is
Crimson Witch of Flames
. The two-piece set enhances Pyro damage by 15%, while the four-piece bonus boosts Burning, Vaporize, Melt, and Overload power.
Pale Flame
is a viable option for bolstering Yoimiya’s already powerful offensive strength.
The four-piece set bonus boosts attack when an Elemental Skill is applied, in addition to increasing physical damage by 25%.

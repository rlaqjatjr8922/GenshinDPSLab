Mona Genshin Impact Guide for Artifacts, Weapons, and Skills
Mastering the art of hydro manipulation, Mona, the elusive (and broke lol) astrologist, graces the world of Teyvat as a 5-star Hydro Catalyst user. Her versatile abilities make her a support addition to any team, whether as a DPS powerhouse or a supporting force in elemental reactions. You will want a Mona
Genshin Impact
guide for this to happen though, and that’s where we come in.
In this comprehensive guide, we’ll delve into the best artifacts, weapons, and skill priorities to elevate Mona to her full potential in
Genshin Impact
.
The Best Artifacts for Mona
Selecting the right artifacts is crucial to maximize Mona’s effectiveness in combat. Here are our top recommendations:
Emblem Of Severed Fate
2-Piece Set Bonus:
Energy Recharge +20%
4-Piece Set Bonus:
Increases Elemental Burst DMG by 25% of Energy Recharge. A maximum of 75% bonus DMG can be obtained in this way.
This build with the artifact set focuses on increasing the damage for Mona’s Burst while ensuring a good uptime for her Burst. Energy Recharge is essential to keep her Burst available consistently, and Crit Rate/DMG boosts her damage output.
Heart Of Depth
2-Piece Bonus:
Hydro DMG Bonus +15%
4-Piece Set Bonus:
After using an Elemental Skill, increases Normal Attack and Charged Attack DMG by 30% for 15s.
As a Hydro Main DPS, prioritize Crit Rate/DMG and ATK% for increased overall damage. Energy Recharge is still valuable for maintaining her Burst. This isn’t exactly the best use for Mona as per the meta, but if she’s the only DPS you have, then by all means.
Artifact Stats to Prioritize
Aiming for specific stats on Mona’s artifacts is crucial for optimal performance:
Sands (Main Stat):
ATK% / Energy Recharge
Goblet (Main Stat):
Hydro DMG Bonus
Circlet (Main Stat):
Crit Rate/DMG
Sub-Stats:
Crit Rate/DMG > Energy Recharge > ATK% > Elemental Mastery
Ensure Mona’s Crit Rate and Crit DMG are well-balanced for consistent and powerful attacks. Energy Recharge is crucial for maintaining her Burst, while Elemental Mastery contributes to stronger
Genshin Impact
elemental reactions.

Mona’s effectiveness is further enhanced by wielding the right weapon. Here are some of our choices. The first one is the ideal weapon while the third one is a budget alternative.
Skyward Atlas
ATK%: Increases Elemental DMG Bonus by 12%. Normal Attack hits have a 50% chance to earn the favor of the clouds, which actively seek out nearby opponents to attack for 15s, dealing 160% ATK DMG. Can only occur once every 30s.
Lost Prayer To The Sacred Winds
CRIT Rate: Increases Movement SPD by 10%. When in battle, gain an 8% Elemental DMG Bonus every 4s. Max 4 stacks. Lasts until the character falls or leaves combat.
The Widsith
CRIT DMG: When a character takes the field, they will gain a random theme song for 10s. This can only occur once every 30s. Recitative: ATK is increased by 60. Aria: Increases all Elemental DMG by 48. Interlude: Elemental Mastery is increased by 240.
Despite its RNG-dependent buffs, The Widsith is a popular choice for Mona for her budget option, offering a mix of ATK, Elemental DMG, and Elemental Mastery.
Skill Priorities for Mona
Understanding the priority of Mona’s skills is essential for effective gameplay and for saving up on the rather laborious materials grind. Follow this priority for Mona.
Ripple of Fate (Elemental Burst)
Mona summons the sparkling waves and creates a reflection of the starry sky, applying the Illusory Bubble status to opponents in a large AoE.
Mirror Reflection of Doom (Elemental Skill)
Creates an illusory Phantom of Fate from coalesced waterspouts.
Ripple of Fate (Normal Attack)
Performs up to 4 water splash attacks that
deal Hydro DMG
. Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to deal AoE Hydro DMG after a short casting time.
Mona’s strength and usefulness in the team stems from her Elemental Burst which is a huge damage boost for just about any character. That’s something you want to keep in mind while using her in a rotation.
Mona Team Comp
Mona synergizes well with various characters, allowing for versatile team compositions. Here are some recommendations for characters with whom she has great synergy.

Mona excels in both main DPS and support roles. Consider teaming her up with characters that can provide additional elemental reactions and buffs.
Mastering Mona’s hydro abilities opens up a world of strategic possibilities in
Genshin Impact
assuming you use our Mona
Genshin Impact
guide. Whether unleashing devastating Burst attacks or weaving intricate elemental reactions, Mona proves to be an indispensable asset to any adventurer. Customize her artifacts, choose the right weapon, and prioritize her skills to unlock her full potential in the world of Teyvat.

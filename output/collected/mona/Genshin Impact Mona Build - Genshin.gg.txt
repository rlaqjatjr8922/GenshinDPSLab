Mona Constellations
Constellation 1
Prophecy of Submersion
The effects of Hydro-related Elemental Reactions are enhanced for 8s after a character hits an enemy affected by an Omen: Electro-Charged DMG is increased by 15%. Vaporize DMG is increased by 15%. Hydro Swirl DMG is increased by 15%. The duration for which enemies are Frozen is increased by 15%.
Constellation 2
Lunar Chain
When a Normal Attack hits, there is a 20% chance that it will be automatically followed by a Charged Attack. This effect can only occur once every 5s.
Constellation 3
Restless Revolution
Increases the Level of Stellaris Phantasm by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Constellation 4
Prophecy of Oblivion
When a character attacks an enemy affected by the Omen status effect, their CRIT Rate is increased by 15%.
Constellation 5
Mockery of Fortuna
Increase the Level of Mirror Reflection of Doom by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Constellation 6
Rhetorics of Calamitas
Upon entering Illusory Torrent, Mona gain a 60% increase to the DMG her next Charged Attack per second of movement. A maximum DMG Bonus of 180% can be achieved in this manner. The effect lasts for no more than 8s.

Chevreuse is a new 4-star Pyro user introduced in Genshin Impact. Even before her official release, many fans have wondered "
Is Chevreuse a gun user?" or "What weapon will Chevreuse have?". The reason for this is she is seen
holding a musket in her animation showcase. However, it turns out that Chevreuse uses a polearm and she’s a support unit that can improve the outcome of the Overloaded reaction. This allows your DPS to carry out serious damage. She even functions as a healer also.
In case you have not known, Chevreuse
scales off HP,
so you should prioritize HP or Energy Recharge equipment when building her. So if you're looking for Chevreuse best weapon, here's a list you can consider.
Weapons that help Chevreuse deal more damage will always rank lower than Polearms that allow her to shine as a
Pyro Support in
Genshin Impact
.
1. Rightful Reward (4-star)
Primary Stat
: 565 Base ATK
Secondary Stat
: 27.6% HP
R5 Effect
: When the wielder is healed, restore 16 Energy. This effect can be triggered once every 10s, and can occur even when the character is not on the field.
Rightful Reward is one of the best weapons for Chevreuse. It provides her with the stat she needs the most to be an outstanding Support in Genshin Impact. Combining this with a full set of Noblesse Oblige artifacts with HP stats can boost the whole team even better.
In case your team only features Pyro and Electro units,
triggering Overloaded
lets you decrease both Pyro and Electro RES from opponents.
Do note that Chevreuse can increase your team’s ATK by up to 40% when she has 40 thousand HP.
2. Black Tassel (3-star)
What weapon will Chevreuse have? Black Tassel is a good polearm for her.
Primary Stat:
354 Base ATK
Secondary Stat:
46.9% HP
R5 Effect:
Increases DMG against slimes by 80%.
The best Chevruse weapon is also Black Tassel. With its HP sub-stat,  players can get 40,000 HP without the Circlet. This lets the purple-haired musketeer's Circlet HP% stat turn into Healing Bonus. If you build her on 4pc of Song of Days Past, she can become an even better support.
3. Favonius Lance (4-star)
Primary Stat:
565 Base ATK
Secondary Stat:
30.6% Energy Recharge
R5 Effect:
CRIT Hits have a 100% chance to generate a small amount of Elemental Particles, which will regenerate 6 Energy for the character. Can only occur once every 6s.
If you're playing Chevreuse as a support, it's recommended to use a full support build. In this case, Favonius Lance is a better choice compared to most other polearms. This weapon enhances Chevreuse's support skills by letting her generate energy and function a bit like a battery.
Chevreuse can play in Overloaded teams in Genshin Impact.
4.
Staff of Homa
Primary Stat:
608 Base ATK
Secondary Stat:
66.2% CRIT DMG
R1 Effect:
HP increased by 20%. Additionally, provides an ATK Bonus based on 0.8% of the wielder's Max HP. When the wielder's HP is less than 50%, this ATK bonus is increased by an additional 1% of Max HP.
Staff of Homa is also a great choice as Chevreuse best weapon.
The
Staff of Homa
is an excellent weapon for Chevreuse in Genshin Impact. It provides a 20% HP boost, aligning well with her abilities. Moreover, it enhances her Crit DMG and ATK% based on her HP, allowing for a powerful support with damage capabilities.
5.
Engulfing Lightning
Primary Stat:
608 Base ATK
Secondary Stat:
55.1% Energy Recharge
R1 Effect:
ATK increased by 28% of Energy Recharge over the base 100%. You can gain a maximum bonus of 80% ATK. Gain 30% Energy Recharge for 12s after using an Elemental Burst.
In Genshin Impact, Engulfing Lightning is an upgraded version of
The Catch
for Chevreuse. It significantly boosts her Energy Recharge, and the more Energy you stack, the more damage she can deal with her Elemental Skill and Burst abilities.

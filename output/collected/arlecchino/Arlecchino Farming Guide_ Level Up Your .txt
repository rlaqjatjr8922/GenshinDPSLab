Ascension Materials
To ascend Arlecchino to her maximum level of 90, you’ll need to gather a variety of materials. These include⁚
Rainbow Rose⁚
This new flower is found in Fontaine, and you’ll need 168 in total to fully ascend Arlecchino. You can start farming them now as they are accessible in regions already open to players.
Agnidus Agate⁚
These are dropped by Pyro bosses, such as the Pyro Hypostasis in Inazuma and the Pyro Regisvine in Liyue. You’ll need a total of 168 Agate Fragments, 18 Agate Chunks, 46 Agate Gemstones, and 9 Agate Sliver.
Insignia⁚
These are dropped by Treasure Hoarders, so keep an eye out for them during your adventures. You’ll need 46 Recruit’s Insignia, 18 Sergeant’s Insignia, and 9 Lieutenant’s Insignia.
While some of these materials can be pre-farmed, it’s important to note that this information is based on leaks and may be subject to change. However, it provides a good starting point for preparing for Arlecchino’s arrival.

Agnidus Agates are a common material used for ascending Pyro characters, and Arlecchino is no exception. These fiery gemstones are dropped by Pyro-affiliated enemies throughout Teyvat. To farm Agnidus Agates efficiently, you’ll want to target specific locations.
The Pyro Hypostasis in Inazuma is a reliable source of Agnidus Agates. This powerful boss can be found on the island of Seirai, and defeating it will reward you with a good amount of these gemstones. The Pyro Regisvine in Liyue is another excellent option; You can find this giant, fiery plant in the Guili Plains, offering another consistent source of Agnidus Agates.
While you can acquire Agnidus Agates from other Pyro enemies, focusing on the Pyro Hypostasis and Pyro Regisvine will provide the most efficient farming method. Be sure to bring your best Pyro-elemental damage dealers to quickly defeat these bosses and earn the Agnidus Agates you need to level up Arlecchino.
Insignia
Insignia materials are dropped by Fatui enemies, who can be found scattered throughout Teyvat. These materials come in three tiers⁚ Recruit’s Insignia, Sergeant’s Insignia, and Lieutenant’s Insignia. You’ll need these to ascend Arlecchino and unlock her full potential.
The easiest way to farm Insignia is by defeating regular Fatui soldiers. These enemies are commonly found in open world areas, particularly in regions like Mondstadt, Liyue, and Inazuma. Look for them in camps or patrolling the roads. Be sure to keep an eye out for Fatui agents who are more powerful and may drop higher-tier Insignia.
You can also obtain Insignia from the Treasure Hoarders, Fatui-affiliated bandits who can be found in various locations. While these enemies are typically more challenging to defeat, they often drop a good amount of Insignia.
To farm Insignia efficiently, prioritize areas where Fatui soldiers and Treasure Hoarders are commonly encountered. Use your best DPS characters to quickly defeat these enemies and collect the Insignia you need for Arlecchino.

Arlecchino, the upcoming Pyro character in
Genshin Impact version 4.6
, has leaked materials needed for ascension and talent level-ups. Here’s what you can pre-farm to be ready for her arrival.
Here’s a breakdown of what you’ll need to gather for Arlecchino
Arlecchino
, a beacon of Pyro fury, craves the fiery essence of Agnidus Agate gemstones for her ascension. Here’s a breakdown of what you’ll need to gather:
Ascension materials
Agnidus Agate
: These Pyro gemstones come in various forms – Sliver, Fragment, Chunk, and Gemstone. You’ll need a total of 1 Agnidus Agate Sliver, 9 of each Fragment and Chunk, and 6 Gemstones. Defeating Pyro bosses like the Pyro Hypostasis and Pyro Regisvine will net you these.
Rainbow Rose
: You’ll need 168 of these local specialties found in the Beryl Region.
Fatui Insignia
: As with other Fatui characters, Arlecchino requires these dropped by Fatui mobs. You’ll need 36 Recruit’s, 96 Sergeant’s, and 129 Lieutenant’s Insignia.
New boss material
: 46 of new material from a yet-to-be-released weekly boss in version 4.6.
Talent level-up materials
Order series
: These talent books come in Teachings, Guide, and Philosophies of Order. You can farm them from the Pale Forgotten Glory Domain on Wednesdays, Saturdays, and Sundays.
New weekly boss material
: 18 of new material from the same weekly boss as above (version 4.6).
Crown of Insight
: Three Crowns are needed to max out a character’s talents. These are very rare and come from events or bosses.
Other resources
Mora
: 7.1 million Mora is needed for character ascension.
Hero’s Wit
: 418 Hero’s Wits are needed to level Arlecchino’s talents.
Leaks surrounding Genshin Impact version 4.6 have unveiled not only the fiery Harbinger Arlecchino but also her potential signature weapon, the Crimson Moon’s Semblance. This 5-star polearm promises to be a powerful companion for Arlecchino, boasting a unique design and a special effect tailored to her fighting style.
To upgrade Crimson Moon’s Semblance to Level 90, here are all of the materials you will need:
Weapon ascension materials

14 Wine Goblet of the Pristine Sea
14 Silver Goblet of the Pristine Sea
6 Golden Goblet of the Pristine Sea
Clockwork Meka drops:

Golem Guard drops:

27 Splintered Hilt

Other resources:

Remember
: This guide is based on current leaks, and information might change upon Arlecchino’s official release in version 4.6. Stay tuned for further updates, Travelers!

With the release of Genshin Impact 5.3, Arlecchino has made a grand entrance, captivating players with her unique abilities and versatility. This guide dives into the
best Arlecchino teams
, including both meta-dominating and F2P-friendly setups. Whether you prefer Melt, Vaporize, Overload, or Hypercarry playstyles, this guide has you covered.
Arlecchino Best Teams Overview
Arlecchino thrives in various roles, especially as a Main DPS, making her a flexible character for several team compositions. Below are some of the best teams that synergize with her abilities:

1. Citlali Melt Team
Team Composition
: Arlecchino, Citlali, Xilonen, Bennett
This team focuses on triggering Melt reactions for massive damage.
Arlecchino
serves as the main DPS, applying Pyro consistently.
Citlali
and
Xilonen
provide Cryo application for efficient Melt reactions, while
Bennett
boosts attack and heals the team.
Playstyle
:
Use Citlali’s and Xilonen’s Cryo abilities to apply the element.
Switch to Arlecchino to trigger Melt with her Pyro-infused attacks.
Activate Bennett’s Burst for buffs and healing.
2. Double Cryo Melt Team
Team Composition
: Arlecchino, Citlali, Rosaria, Bennett
This variant of the Melt team brings
Rosaria
into the mix, offering extra Cryo application and crit rate buffs. With both
Citlali
and
Rosaria
, Melt reactions occur more frequently, ensuring higher DPS potential.
Playstyle
:
Deploy Citlali and Rosaria’s abilities to maximize Cryo uptime.
Follow up with Arlecchino’s skills and attacks to trigger Melt.
Bennett ensures consistent healing and buffs.
3. Vaporize Team
Team Composition
: Arlecchino, Yelan, Xilonen, Bennett
This composition leverages
Vaporize
reactions for enhanced damage.
Yelan
acts as the Hydro enabler, while
Xilonen
offers additional Cryo support.
Bennett
remains the cornerstone for healing and attack buffs.
Playstyle
:
Use Yelan’s Hydro application and Arlecchino’s Pyro abilities to trigger Vaporize.
Xilonen can freeze enemies or act as a backup Cryo enabler.
Bennett boosts attack for Arlecchino’s devastating Vaporize combos.
4. Overload Chevreuse Team
Team Composition
: Arlecchino, Fischl, Chevreuse, Bennett
This team focuses on
Overload
reactions, combining
Electro
and
Pyro
for AoE damage.
Fischl
and
Chevreuse
apply consistent Electro, while
Arlecchino
triggers Overload with her Pyro attacks.
Playstyle
:
Use Fischl’s and Chevreuse’s abilities to apply Electro.
Switch to Arlecchino to trigger Overload with her Pyro-infused attacks.
Bennett ensures the team stays healed and buffed.
5. Hypercarry Team
Team Composition
: Arlecchino, Xilonen, Kaedehara Kazuha, Bennett
In this setup, Arlecchino takes center stage as the
Hypercarry DPS
, supported by elemental amplification.
Xilonen
provides Cryo resonance and reactions, while
Kaedehara Kazuha
boosts elemental damage.
Playstyle
:
Use Kazuha’s Elemental Skill and Burst to swirl Pyro and Cryo for crowd control and buffs.
Deploy Xilonen for Cryo application.
Switch to Arlecchino to maximize her damage, supported by Bennett’s buffs.
6. F2P Vaporize Team
Team Composition
: Arlecchino, Xingqiu, Sucrose, Bennett
For players seeking a
budget-friendly team
, this Vaporize composition uses readily accessible characters.
Xingqiu
applies Hydro, while
Sucrose
amplifies elemental damage with her crowd control and buffs.
Playstyle
:
Use Sucrose to gather enemies and swirl Hydro or Pyro for damage amplification.
Xingqiu applies Hydro with his Burst.
Arlecchino triggers Vaporize reactions with her Pyro attacks, supported by Bennett.
Tips for Maximizing Arlecchino’s Teams
Artifact Optimization
: Focus on artifacts that boost Pyro damage, crit rate, and crit damage for Arlecchino. Sets like
Crimson Witch of Flames
or a hybrid set can work well.
Energy Management
: Ensure a high energy recharge stat for Arlecchino and your support characters to maintain uptime on bursts.
Rotation Discipline
: Properly sequence skills and bursts to maximize elemental reactions and DPS output.
Final Thoughts
Arlecchino’s rerun in Genshin Impact 5.3 has brought fresh opportunities for team-building and combat strategies. Whether you prefer high-damage Melt setups, consistent Vaporize reactions, or AoE Overload teams, Arlecchino fits seamlessly into any playstyle. Experiment with these team compositions and dominate the Spiral Abyss and open-world challenges alike!

How to Get Fragment of Golden Melody in Genshin Impact?
Fragment of Golden Melody is a character level-up material used to ascend Arlecchino. Players can acquire it by defeating the Golem Captain boss. Take it down and claim rewards which includes the Fragment of Golden Melody, Agnidus Agate Jewel, Prithiva Topaz Jewel and more.
As of 4.5, it is yet unknown where the boss is located. However, leaks suggest that a new region is slated for a release in version 4.6, which could home this new world boss.
How to Get Rainbow Rose in Genshin Impact?
Rainbow Rose is a local specialty you can find within Fontaine. It is described as a delicate and tender pink flower. Unlike other ascension materials in the game, acquiring Rainbow Rose is rather easier as it is commonly seen on the ground, sitting in obvious spots.
Interestingly enough, one of her “children”, Lyney uses the same ascension material. And if you have Lyney, you can also have him in your party to make farming more easier as he will reveal the nearby Fontaine local specialty on the minimap, thanks to his passive. You can also rely on the
Official Genshin Impact Interactive Map
to easily locate any materials you’re looking for.

How to Get the Order Talent Books in Genshin Impact
Order Talent Books in Genshin Impact is a Character Talent Ascension material used by mostly Fontaine characters, Chevreuse, Lynette, Wriothesley and Arlecchino. In order to acquire order talent books, prepare to spend some resin and enter the Pale Forgotten Glory domain every Wednesday, Saturday and Sunday.

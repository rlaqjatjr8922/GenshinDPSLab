The Best Build For Arlecchino
Here's a brief explanation on Arlecchino's best build. | © Hoyoverse
The best build for Arlecchino focuses on improving her ATK%, HP%, CRIT Rate%, CRIT DMG%, and Pyro DMG%. Of these attributes, the HP% plays a vital role, as most of her character kit benefits from max HP using a new unique gameplay mechanic called Bond of Life that rewards aggressive play with a healing bonus based on max HP.
But this doesn't mean you have to focus on the HP attribute when choosing your weapons and artifacts, as Arlecchino is a pure DPS unit whose damage-dealing capability greatly increases when focusing more on ATK%, CRIT Rate%, CRIT DMG%, and Pyro DMG%.
This is evident from her ascension scaling, which is CRIT DMG%, her signature weapon that provided CRIT DMG bonus and artifacts catered towards her character kit, focusing on increasing ATK% and DMG based on change in Bond of Life value.
With that being out of the way, below you can find the best weapons, artifacts and teams for Arlecchino that will help you achieve the best build with her.
Best Weapon For Arlecchino
Here's a look at Arlecchino's signature Polearm. | © Hoyoverse
The best weapon for Arlecchino in Genshin Impact is her signature weapon, Crimson Moon's Semblance, a ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Polearm weapon which grants a Bond of Life equal to 25% of Max HP when a Charged Attack hits an opponent, triggering every 14 seconds.
Moreover, when you switch to a character with a Bond of Life, they gain a 12% DMG Bonus. If the value of the Bond of Life is greater than or equal to 30% of Max HP, then the character gains an additional 20% DMG Bonus.
Since Arlecchino gains a Bond of Life from using her Elemental Skill, 'All is Ash,' and Elemental Burst, 'Balemoon Rising,' along with other ways like from her signature weapon, the new Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy artifact and so on, Crimson Moon's Semblance is by far the best weapon to use on her.
Also, do I really have to convince you to get this cool scythe? Do I? See, if you are going to get Arlecchino, you have to get her weapon, whether it be for aesthetics or for dealing a few thousand more damage from her abilities. Just get it, or you will feel bad. I know! 😌
If I don't get her signature, I will be using this. | © Hoyoverse

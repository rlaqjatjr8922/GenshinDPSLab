Who is Arlecchino?
Arlecchino, also known by her codename “The Knave,” is one of the most compelling figures in Teyvat. As the Fourth of the Eleven Fatui Harbingers, she commands respect and fear in equal measure. But beneath the stern exterior and formal title lies a complex history of tragedy, rebellion, and a fierce, unconventional form of love for her family. To truly understand how to play her, you first need to understand who she is. She’s a playable Pyro character who brings a dark and fascinating story to the forefront of the game.
From Peruere to “The Knave”
Before she was a Harbinger, Arlecchino was a young girl named Peruere. She was raised in the House of the Hearth, an orphanage run by the previous Knave, Crucabena. This wasn’t a place of warmth; it was a crucible designed to forge children into loyal Fatui soldiers. Peruere witnessed firsthand the cruelty of the system and the cold, manipulative nature of her “Mother.” This experience ignited a fire in her, leading her to eventually challenge and kill Crucabena, claiming the title of “The Knave” for herself. Her Role at the House of the Hearth
As the new head of the House of the Hearth,
Arlecchino
operates under the title of “Father.” This isn’t just a name change; it’s a complete shift in philosophy. Where the previous Knave saw children as disposable tools, Arlecchino sees them as her family, raising them with a strict but genuine sense of duty and protection. She still trains them to be capable spies and agents for the Fatui, but her ultimate goal is to empower them to survive and choose their own paths. She is a disciplinarian, but one who acts out of a desire to keep her children safe in a dangerous world, making her one of the most morally gray Harbingers.
Her Khaenri’ah Heritage
Adding another layer to her character is her hidden lineage. Arlecchino is a descendant of the Crimson Moon Dynasty, a royal line from the fallen nation of Khaenri’ah. This connection is more than just a cool piece of trivia; it ties her to one of the greatest tragedies in Teyvat’s history and likely fuels her distrust of the gods and Celestia. Being from a nation destroyed by the divine gives her motivations a powerful edge. Fans have spent a lot of time dissecting what her Khaenri’ah heritage means for her future and the Fatui’s ultimate goals.
Key Allies and Enemies
Arlecchino’s world is defined by her relationships. Her most important allies are her “children” from the House of the Hearth, particularly Lyney, Lynette, and Freminet, for whom she serves as a protective and demanding father figure. She is fiercely loyal to them and will go to any length to ensure their safety. Her primary enemy was the previous Knave, Crucabena, whose death represents Arlecchino’s foundational act of rebellion. Among the other Harbingers, her relationships are tense. She openly despises Il Dottore for his unethical experiments and maintains a professional but wary distance from the others, always prioritizing her own family and agenda.

How Does Arlecchino Fight?
Arlecchino isn’t your typical button-masher; she’s a strategic powerhouse who brings a fresh and aggressive playstyle to the field. As a 5-star Pyro Polearm user, she’s designed to be an on-field main DPS, meaning she’s the star of the show when she’s in your party. Her entire kit revolves around a unique mechanic called the “Bond of Life,” which transforms her normal attacks into a flurry of fiery strikes. Mastering her flow feels incredibly rewarding, as you juggle her abilities to maintain high damage output. She’s all about calculated aggression, marking her foes for death before cashing in on their debts for a huge power spike. This makes her one of the most engaging characters to play, demanding your full attention to execute her combos perfectly.
Her Pyro Abilities
As a Pyro character, Arlecchino is built to burn through enemy health bars. Her core strength lies in her ability to infuse her Polearm with Pyro, ensuring her attacks trigger powerful elemental reactions. Unlike some characters who rely solely on their Skill or Burst for elemental damage, Arlecchino’s gameplay loop allows her to apply Pyro consistently with her normal attacks. This makes her an excellent driver for reaction-based teams, especially with Vaporize or Melt setups. Her Elemental Skill applies a “Blood-Debt Directive” to enemies, which is the first step in her deadly combat sequence. Think of her as a fire-wielding assassin who methodically sets up her targets before going in for the final, devastating blow.
The Bond of Life Mechanic
The Bond of Life is the heart of Arlecchino’s kit and what makes her truly unique. You can think of it as a special gauge that, when filled, empowers her attacks. When Arlecchino has a Bond of Life active that is equal to or greater than a certain percentage of her max HP, her normal, charged, and plunging attacks are converted to Pyro damage. This state is called “Masque of the Red Death.” The Bond of Life prevents her from being healed by other characters, so you have to rely on her Elemental Burst to restore her health. It’s a high-risk, high-reward system that encourages an aggressive playstyle, and a detailed
Arlecchino quick guide
can help you master its nuances.
Using Blood-Debt Directives
Blood-Debt Directives are special marks Arlecchino applies to enemies using her Elemental Skill. These marks are crucial for building her Bond of Life. After marking one or more enemies, you can use her charged attack or her Elemental Burst to absorb the Directives. Absorbing them grants her a significant amount of Bond of Life, fueling her empowered “Masque of the Red Death” state. Her Elemental Burst is particularly effective, as it absorbs all nearby Directives, deals a large blast of AoE Pyro damage, and resets the cooldown of her Skill. This creates a smooth and satisfying rotation: apply Directives, absorb them to power up, unleash fiery attacks, and repeat.
Mastering Her Polearm
While she wields a Polearm, Arlecchino’s fighting style feels more like she’s dancing with a deadly scythe. Once the Bond of Life is active, her normal attacks become swift, wide-arcing slashes infused with Pyro. Her attack animations are some of the most fluid and visually stunning in the game, making her incredibly fun to play. Because her damage comes from her empowered normal attacks, you’ll want to focus on keeping her on the field as much as possible. Mastering her Polearm means mastering the rhythm of her mechanics—knowing when to apply Directives, when to absorb them with her Burst, and when to unleash a relentless assault with her “Masque of the Red Death” attacks.
How to Build Arlecchino
Alright, let’s get into the fun part: turning Arlecchino into an absolute powerhouse on the battlefield. As a 5-star Pyro Polearm user, she’s designed to be your main DPS, staying on the field and dishing out incredible damage. Building her correctly means focusing on artifacts, weapons, and talents that amplify her unique Bond of Life mechanic. When you get these elements right, she truly shines as the Fourth Fatui Harbinger.
Your goal is to maximize her Pyro DMG and ATK, with a healthy amount of CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG. Unlike many other characters, she doesn’t need Energy Recharge or HP% for her own damage, since her Burst’s main function is to reset her Skill cooldown and heal her based on her Bond of Life and ATK. Getting her build right is a game-changer, transforming her from a good character into an unstoppable force. Let’s break down exactly what you need to farm for.
Best Artifact Sets
When it comes to artifacts, Arlecchino has a clear winner. Her best-in-slot set is the
4-piece Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy
. This set was practically made for her, increasing her damage every time her Bond of Life value changes. Since her entire kit revolves around manipulating this mechanic, the synergy is perfect. You’ll see a massive damage increase as you build and consume her Bond of Life during combat.
If you’re still farming for a perfect Harmonic Whimsy set, the
4-piece Gladiator’s Finale
is a fantastic alternative. It provides a solid ATK% bonus and a hefty 35% DMG increase to her Normal Attacks, which is where the bulk of her damage comes from. You can also temporarily use a combination of any 2-piece ATK% set (like Gladiator or Shimenawa’s Reminiscence) and a 2-piece Pyro DMG Bonus set (Crimson Witch of Flames) while you hunt for better pieces.
Choosing the Best Weapons
Arlecchino’s abilities are built around the
Bond of Life mechanic
, so her weapon choice should reflect that. Her signature 5-star polearm,
Crimson Moon’s Semblance
, is tailor-made for her. It not only provides a huge CRIT Rate stat but also grants her a Bond of Life and increases her damage when she has one. If you were lucky enough to pull it, this is your best option by a long shot.
For more accessible choices, the 5-star

Deathmatch

White Tassel
is a surprisingly effective 3-star weapon that gives a nice CRIT Rate boost and a significant Normal Attack DMG bonus, which is perfect for her on-field playstyle.
Prioritizing Her Talents
To get the most out of Arlecchino, you need to level her talents in the right order. Arlecchino deals on-field damage by empowering her attacks through her unique Bond of Life mechanic, so you’ll want to focus on her Normal Attack first. This is her bread and butter, and leveling it up directly increases the damage of her Pyro-infused strikes.
After her Normal Attack, your next priority should be her Elemental Skill. This is the ability that applies Blood-Debt Directives to enemies, which is essential for creating and absorbing Bond of Life. Finally, level her Elemental Burst. While its damage is significant, its primary utility is healing and resetting her Skill’s cooldown. A good talent priority to follow is
Normal Attack > Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst
. This ensures your resources are going where they’ll make the biggest impact on her performance.
Perfecting Her Combat Rotations
A smooth combat rotation is key to maximizing Arlecchino’s damage output. You want to start by setting up your support characters’ abilities first—think shields from Zhongli or off-field damage from Fischl or Yelan. Once your buffs and sub-DPS skills are active, switch to Arlecchino.
A standard rotation looks like this: use her Elemental Skill to apply Blood-Debt Directives to enemies, then use a Charged Attack to absorb them and gain Bond of Life. From there, unleash a flurry of her Pyro-infused Normal Attacks. If you need healing or want to reset your cooldowns, you can
use her Burst
at the start of the rotation. This allows you to immediately use her Skill again to reapply Blood-Debt, ensuring you maintain high damage uptime.
Building the Best Arlecchino Teams
Arlecchino is a powerhouse on her own, but like any great leader, she’s at her absolute best with a solid crew backing her up. As an on-field main DPS, your goal is to build a team that amplifies her damage, keeps her safe, and sets up devastating elemental reactions. Her entire kit revolves around the unique Bond of Life mechanic, which means you can’t just throw any three characters with her and hope for the best. A well-crafted team will feel like a symphony of destruction, while a poorly planned one can leave her feeling clunky and vulnerable.
Thinking about team composition means looking at a few key roles: characters who can set up reactions, supports who can shield or buff her, and a sub-DPS to provide extra damage and elemental application. Getting this balance right is the key to making Arlecchino one of the most formidable characters in your roster. Let’s break down how to assemble the perfect team for the head of the House of the Hearth.
Elemental Reaction Setups
Arlecchino’s damage skyrockets when she’s triggering elemental reactions. Since she’s a Pyro character, your go-to reactions are going to be Vaporize and Overloaded. Her abilities are built around the Bond of Life mechanic, which gets a massive damage increase when she’s paired with other elemental characters who can set the stage for her. Think of them as the opening act for her fiery main event.
For a Vaporize team, you’ll want to pair her with strong off-field Hydro applicators like Yelan or Xingqiu. They can consistently apply Hydro to enemies, allowing Arlecchino’s Pyro-infused attacks to trigger Vaporize for huge damage multipliers. If you prefer a more explosive approach, an Overloaded team with characters like Fischl or Beidou can be incredibly effective. Chevreuse is a fantastic support for this specific setup, as she provides buffs that make
Overloaded reactions
even more potent.
Pairing Her with Supports
While Arlecchino has a self-healing mechanic tied to her Elemental Burst, she can’t be healed by other characters when she has a Bond of Life active. This makes shielders an absolute must-have to prevent interruptions and keep her alive. A character like
Zhongli
is her best friend, providing an unbreakable shield that lets her attack without a care in the world. Other shielders like Thoma can also work, offering Pyro resonance for an extra ATK increase.
Beyond shields, you’ll want buffers who can further increase her damage. Bennett is a classic choice for his massive ATK buff, though his healing circle won’t affect Arlecchino directly. Kazuha is another top-tier option, offering crowd control and an Elemental DMG bonus. For Overloaded teams, Chevreuse is practically essential, providing buffs and resistance shred that are tailor-made for that reaction.
Finding the Right Sub-DPS
A good sub-DPS is the secret ingredient to a top-tier Arlecchino team. You need characters who can deal damage and apply their element even when they’re not on the field. This allows Arlecchino to stay active and drive the reactions. As a 5-star Pyro Polearm character, she truly shines as a main DPS when paired with characters that can provide these continuous
elemental reactions
.
For Vaporize teams, Yelan and Xingqiu are the gold standard. Their bursts provide a constant stream of Hydro that perfectly syncs with Arlecchino’s fast attacks. For Overloaded teams, Fischl is an amazing choice, as her raven, Oz, provides consistent off-field Electro application and solid damage. These characters ensure that you’re not losing out on damage while Arlecchino is busy dishing out her own punishment, creating a seamless and powerful offense.
Executing Optimal Team Rotations
Putting the team together is only half the battle; you also need to execute your rotation correctly to maximize damage. A smooth rotation ensures all your buffs and off-field abilities are active right when Arlecchino takes the field. You should use her Burst at the start of the rotation if its healing is needed, which also allows her marks to upgrade while your supports do their thing.
A typical rotation might look like this: Start with your shielder (e.g., Zhongli’s Hold Skill) to get protection up. Then, switch to your sub-DPS and use their off-field skill or burst (e.g., Yelan’s Burst). Follow up with your buffer (e.g., Kazuha’s Skill and Burst). Finally, switch to Arlecchino, use her Skill to apply Blood-Debt Directives, and then unleash her Normal Attacks to consume the marks and deal incredible damage. Mastering this flow will make your team feel unstoppable.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is Arlecchino actually a villain?
It’s complicated, and that’s what makes her so interesting. While she is a Fatui Harbinger, a group we typically see as antagonists, her personal motivations are all about protecting her family at the House of the Hearth. Why can’t I heal Arlecchino with my other characters?
This is a core part of her unique playstyle, tied directly to her Bond of Life mechanic. When she has a Bond of Life active, which is necessary to empower her attacks with Pyro, she is unable to receive healing from any other character in your party. This design choice encourages an aggressive, high-risk playstyle where you rely on her own Elemental Burst for healing. It means you have to be more strategic about when you fight and when you heal.

What’s the story behind her being called “Father”?

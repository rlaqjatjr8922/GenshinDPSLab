Arlecchino is a Pyro hyper carry with a focus on Normal attacks, and her kit prevents her from getting healed.
The best teams of Arlecchino will have someone to protect her with shields and provide off-field DPS.

Arlecchino is a 5-star Pyro DPS who wields a Polearm and her kit is very unique. Arlecchino’s kit is tied to the Bond of Life status effect, which increases her attack but prevents her allies from healing her. She is also a Pyro Hypercarry with a focus on Normal Attacks. The best teams of
Arlecchino
will need to have characters that can protect her without healing and who can maximize her damage output by staying off-field. Here I have ranked the 5 best teams for Arlecchino in Genshin Impact.
5. Arlecchino-Fischl-Chevreuse-Bennett
Image Courtesy: In-game Screenshot Captured and Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti
Arlecchino overload teams are going to be pretty strong, especially with the correct characters. Pairing Arlecchino with Fischl, Chevreuse, and Bennett will maximize her Overload damage but make her susceptible to damage. Bennett’s burst heal sadly won’t work on Arlecchino, so her main healing source in this team would be her own Ultimate.
4. Arlecchino-Fischl-Bennett-Zhongli
Image Courtesy: In-game Screenshot Captured and Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti
This overload team is going to be a bit easier to play at the cost of damage output for Arlecchino.
Zhongli
will allow Arlecchino to continue attacking without worrying about getting interrupted. This team will lose out a bit of damage output compared to the 5th-ranked overload team, but it will be much easier to play and may allow you to do more overall damage.

3. Arlecchino-Xiangling-Kazuha-Bennett
Image Courtesy: In-game Screenshot Captured and Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti
This is a mono Pyro team for Arlecchino, where Kazuha or Sucrose can be used to crowd control. Bennett is important in this team over a shielder like Zhongli to maximize on the Pyro damage. Other than that, Xiangling is always a solid choice for off-field Pyro damage. This Arlecchino team is one of the strongest, so definitely give it a try.
2. Arlecchino-Xingqiu-Bennett-Zhongli
Image Courtesy: In-game Screenshot Captured and Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti
Arlecchino vape teams have the most damage output and having Zhongli in the team makes it much easier to play. This team consists of Xingqiu as a Hydro applier and Bennett as support with Arlecchino. This team synergy is easily one of the strongest teams in the game for her.
1. Arlecchino-Yelan-Kazuha-Zhongli
Image Courtesy: In-game Screenshot Captured and Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti
This is the best Vape team for Arlecchino and can also be considered her team. With Yelan over Xingqiu, hydro application on enemies will become much simpler, and having Kazuha or Sucrose will allow superior crowd control to deal the maximum damage. Zhongli will prevent Arlecchino from getting interrupted making this team much easier to play.

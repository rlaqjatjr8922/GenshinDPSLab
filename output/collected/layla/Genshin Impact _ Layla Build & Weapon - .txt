Strengths & Weakness Of Layla
Strengths
- Amazing uptime of Elemental Burst.
- Powerful shields and consistent Cryo infliction.
- All abilities scales from HP making her easy to build.
- Tough shields that is one of the strongest in-game.
Weakness
- Fairly weak personal damage.
- Does not provide much other than Cryo infliction and shields.
- Support Abilities are locked behind constellations.
- Does not produce many Energy Particles.

Layla Requires HP Weapons
To maximize the strength of the shields of Layla, it is important to increase HP. Weapons that increase HP stats or provides support to the team is recommended.

Layla Skill Guide & How To Use Layla
Recommended Rotation For Layla
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Layla's Elemental Skill to summon a shield.
2
Switch to another character and deal attacks
3
Use Elemental Burst once it fills out.
4
Switch to another character.
5
Return to step 1.
Uses Of Layla's Elemental Skill
▲Creates a "Shooting Star" every 1.5 Seconds.
Provides An HP Scaling Shield
The main strength of Layla is with her powerful shields that scales from her HP.  This shield comes from the Elemental Skill of Layla.
Off-Field Cryo Infliction
The shield creates a "shooting star" that tracks enemies and hit them with Cryo. The DMG scales from the ATK of Layla but unleashing its passive "Sweet Slumber Undisturbed" makes it scale from Max HP of Layla as well.
Uses Of Elemental Burst
Consistent Cryo DMG That Scales From HP
Elemental Burst of Layla allows consistent infliction of Cryo at a certain area. This has a low burst cost and has 100% uptime making this a great move for reactions.
Gives Additional "Shooting Star" For Each Hit
Every Hit from Elemental Burst will give shooting star to players protected by Layla's shields. This allows more damage from Elemental Skill to happen.
Should You Pull for Layla's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
All of Layla's Constellations are Good to Have
Constellation Priority
C1 > C2 > C3-C4 > C6
C2 is a Good Stopping Point
Layla's personal damage is noticeably increased by her C6, which can be good in her teams due to the additional damage contribution. Her C6 is hardly necessary though, and
getting Layla to C1 to improve her shielding and even C2  for better burst uptime is going to be the ideal option
Her constellations help ease her gameplay and improve her performance in her intended role, but you'll often find that Layla is already fully functional at C0.

Nights of Formal Focus (Elemental Skill)
Effect
Puts forth a shield known as the Curtain of Slumber, dealing AoE Cryo DMG.
The Curtain of Slumber's DMG Absorption is based on Layla's Max HP and absorbs Cryo DMG with 250% effectiveness. When the shield is deployed, Layla will have Cryo applied to her briefly.
Night Stars and Shooting Stars
Effect
While the Curtain of Slumber is active, it will create 1 Night Star that will be attached to it every 1.5s. When a character protected by this shield uses an Elemental Skill, 2 Night Stars will be created. Night Stars can be created once every 0.3s in this way. A maximum of 4 Night Stars can be accumulated at any one time.
Once the Curtain of Slumber has accumulated 4 Night Stars and there are opponents nearby, these Night Stars will transform into homing Shooting Stars that will be fired off in sequence, dealing Cryo DMG to any opponents hit.
If the Curtain of Slumber's duration ends or it is destroyed, the Night Stars will disappear. If they are already being fired off as Shooting Stars, these Shooting Stars will last until this wave of shots ends.
New Night Stars cannot be created until the previous wave of Shooting Stars has been fired completely.

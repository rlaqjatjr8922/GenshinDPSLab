Layla is the very first hero you’ll use when starting your journey in
Mobile Legends: Bang Bang
. As a
marksman
armed with her powerful energy gun, she’s incredibly beginner-friendly, making it easy to learn the core mechanics of the game. However, don’t underestimate her potential—when mastered, Layla can become a true powerhouse on the battlefield. In this complete guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about playing Layla, including her best build, recommended battle spell, emblem setup, skill tips, and the most effective combos to help you dominate your matches.
Layla skills overview in Mobile Legends
Passive – Malefic Gun
Layla’s basic attacks deal more damage the farther away she is from her target (this bonus doesn’t apply to turrets).
•	Always position yourself at maximum range during fights to take full advantage of this passive.
•	Use advanced targeting settings to easily select heroes, minions, or turrets without misfiring, allowing you to maximize her range effectively.
Layla fires a Malefic Bomb in a straight line, dealing physical damage to the first enemy it hits (this attack can crit). If it lands, Layla’s basic attacks and Void Projectile gain bonus range for a few seconds, and she gains a temporary speed boost. Hitting enemy heroes doubles the speed boost duration.
•	Unlock this skill at level one to poke and pressure enemies early on.
•	You don’t need to hit a hero—tagging a minion or jungle monster will still grant you bonus range.
•	Smart players use this trick to snipe opponents or hit turrets safely from outside their attack range.
•	Always manually aim Malefic Bomb for better precision.
Second Skill – Void Projectile
Layla fires an orb of energy that explodes on contact, dealing physical damage and marking enemies with Magic Mark. Hitting enemies with basic attacks or abilities while they’re marked triggers bonus damage and stuns them briefly.
•	Early in the game, it’s safest to use Void Projectile after hitting an enemy with Malefic Bomb, so you don’t have to overextend.
•	Malefic Bomb and Destruction Rush can also trigger the bonus damage and stun from Magic Mark.
Ultimate – Destruction Rush
Layla fires a powerful energy blast in a line, dealing massive physical damage to everything it hits.
Passive: Each time you upgrade this skill, it permanently increases Layla’s attack range, her Void Projectile range, and slightly boosts her vision range.
•	Use Destruction Rush to finish off fleeing enemies or chunk their health to force them back to base.
•	Be mindful of its slight casting delay—timing is key.
•	You can even steal major objectives like Lord or Turtle if timed perfectly.
•	Thanks to the passive, Layla eventually gains the longest attack range in Mobile Legends, letting her siege turrets without getting into danger.
How To Play Layla In Mobile Legends
Best battle spell
Since Layla has no built-in mobility skills,
Flicker
is the best battle spell for her. It gives her an instant dash to either escape when enemies close in or to chase down fleeing, low-health targets.
•	A well-timed Flicker can save you from being caught by assassins or divers aiming for your backline.
Another solid option is
Purify
. As a marksman without crowd-control immunity, Layla is highly vulnerable to stuns, slows, and immobilizations.
•	Purify instantly removes these debuffs, giving her a crucial window to reposition, keep attacking, or retreat safely from dangerous fights.
Best emblem
The
Assassin emblem
is the top choice for Layla, as it greatly boosts her penetration, attack power, and movement speed—everything she needs to deal consistent damage and reposition quickly.
•	For your first-tier talent, pick
Fatal
to increase both critical chance and critical damage, making her basic attacks even more deadly.
•	Next, go for
Weapon Master
, which gives her bonus physical attack from all sources—items, emblems, and skills—scaling her damage even faster.
For your main talent,
Quantum Charge
is highly recommended. It enhances your sustain by granting movement speed and HP regen whenever you land basic attacks, allowing you to survive longer in extended fights.
•	If you prefer a more aggressive, kite-heavy playstyle,
Weakness Finder
is a great alternative. It slows down your enemy’s movement and attack speed, pairing perfectly with Layla’s long-range advantage to keep enemies at bay while you dish out damage safely.
Best Layla Build
As your team’s primary damage dealer, your goal with Layla is to maximize physical attack, critical damage, attack speed, and survivability.
Start with
Swift Boots
for early mobility, then rush
Windtalker
to gain a massive boost in attack speed, crit chance, and movement speed. Its unique passive,
Typhoon
, also lets you deal bonus splash damage—perfect for quicker farming and poking enemies.
Next, pick up
Berserker’s Fury
to heavily boost your critical damage output.
Moving into the mid and late game, invest in
Haas’ Claws
for lifesteal and sustain during fights, followed by
Malefic Roar
to shred through enemy armor, especially against tanks.
Round out your build with
Sea Halberd
to counter heroes with strong regen effects like Uranus, Esmeralda, or Yu Zhong.
If the enemy team has heavy burst assassins or mages, consider swapping out one item for
Wind of Nature
(against physical burst) or
Rose Gold Meteor
(for a lifeline against magic damage).
Best Layla build in Mobile Legends:
•	Swift Boots
•	Windtalker
•	Berserker’s Fury
•	Haas’ Claws
•	Malefic Roar
•	Sea Halberd
Best Combos to Learn
During the laning phase, start by hitting your opponent with
Malefic Bomb
, then immediately follow up with
Void Projectile
and basic attacks.
Always make sure you land
Malefic Bomb
first to activate the bonus range—this lets you attack safely from a distance without putting yourself at risk.
Focus on slowly chipping away at your enemy’s HP and avoid overcommitting; the enemy roamer or jungler could be hiding nearby, ready to punish you if you push too far.
When your opponent’s health gets low, finish them off with
Destruction Rush
from a safe distance.
Time it right—wait until they’ve used their blink or mobility skills before unleashing your ultimate to ensure it lands.
In team fights, stay positioned safely at the backline, constantly poking with
Malefic Bomb
and basic attacks.
If things get dangerous, use
Flicker
to quickly reposition and stay alive.
Conclusion
Layla may be the first hero you encounter in
Mobile Legends
, but she remains a powerful choice even at higher levels of play when used correctly. By mastering her range, timing your combos, and building the right items, you can become a serious threat from the backline. Whether you’re poking enemies during the laning phase or dishing out massive damage in team fights, Layla’s strength lies in her ability to control fights from a distance. Keep practicing her combos, stay aware of your positioning, and you’ll find yourself carrying games with the Energy Gunner in no time!

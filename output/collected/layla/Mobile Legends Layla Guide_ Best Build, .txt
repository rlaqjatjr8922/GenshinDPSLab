To help you destroy your enemies using the Energy Gunner, we have made a complete ONE Esports guide that covers the best build, recommended battle spell, emblem, skill tips, and combos.
Layla’s skills in Mobile Legends
Passive – Malefic Gun
Layla deals increased damage to enemies farther away from her. This does not include Turrets.
Always try to attack from the furthest range to maximize Malefic Gun.
Use the advanced targeting method, so you can specifically attack a hero, minion, or turret to maximize her range.
First skill – Malefic Bomb
Layla fires a Malefic Bomb in the target direction, dealing Physical Damage to the first enemy hit (can critically strike). Upon hitting an enemy, Layla’s Basic Attacks and Void Projectile Void Projectile gain extra range for a few seconds, and she gains Movement Speed that decays over time. The duration of the Movement Speed boost is doubled if an enemy hero is hit.
Get this at level one to effectively harass your opponent in the lane.
You don’t necessarily have to hit a hero to activate the additional range. You can strike a minion with it then use the bonus range to hit your enemy with Void Projectile and basic attacks.
You can also do the same to jungle creeps near you.
Once you hit an enemy with this, you can use basic attacks against a turret from outside its range.
Manually target this skill for higher accuracy.
Second skill – Void Projectile
Layla fires an orb of Malefic Energy that explodes on hit, dealing Physical Damage to targets in the area and applying Magic Mark on them for a few seconds. When Layla hits an enemy with a Magic Mark, she deals Physical Damage to nearby enemies and stuns them for a duration.
In the early game, use this only when you hit an enemy with Malefic Bomb, so you won’t need to go near them and get into a dangerous position.
Damage from Malefic Bomb and Destruction Rush (ultimate) can trigger the extra damage and stun effect.
Ultimate – Destruction Rush
Layla fires a blast of Malefic Energy in the target direction, dealing Physical Damage to enemies in a line.
Passive: The range of Layla’s Void Projectile Void Projectile and Basic Attacks is increased by a certain number of units. Her sight range is also slightly increased each time this skill is upgraded.
You can use this to finish off enemies or reduce their HP significantly, forcing them to retreat back to their base.
Keep in mind that this has a slight delay.
With the right timing, you can steal a Turtle or Lord kill using this.
Thanks to the ultimate’s passive, Layla has the longest range in the game. At max level, she can attack turrets from outside their range, making her one of the best heroes at sieging.

Recommended battle spell

As Layla lacks a reliable mobility skill, choose Flicker as your battle spell. Use it to escape from approaching enemies, or chase down opponents with low health.

Purify is another good battle spell option. As a marksman with no escape abilities, the hero often becomes an easy target for enemies with stuns, slows, or immobilizations.
Using Purify allows her to cleanse these effects instantly, providing her with a crucial moment to reposition, continue dealing damage, or escape from dangerous situations.
Recommended emblem
Credit: ONE Esports
Assassin emblem works best for Layla, for it grants a significant boost to her Penetration, Attack Damage, and Movement Speed.
In terms of standard talents, select Fatal for extra critical chance and damage, as well as Weapon Master for additional physical attack from equipment, emblem, talents, and skills.
Credit: Moonton Games, ONE Esports

For your core talent, choose Quantum Charge, which improves your survivability. It provides you with additional movement speed and HP restoration.
Alternatively, if you want to play aggressively, you can opt for Weakness Finder. It enables you to slow down your target’s movement and attack speed. Additionally, it synergizes well with her long attack range.
Layla best build
Credit: ONE Esports
As the marksman, you need to serve as the main damage dealer of your team. Purchase items that will boost your physical attack, critical chance and damage, attack speed, and lifesteal.
Start with Swift Boots, then buy Wind Talker for a boost your Attack Speed, Crit Chance, and Movement Speed. It also has a unique passive called Typhoon that allows you to deal splash damage from time to time.
Follow with Berserker’s Fury for additional Physical Attack and Crit Chance.

Easy combos to learn
In the laning phase, hit your opponent with Malefic Bomb followed by Void Projectile and basic attacks.
Remember, you have to hit Malefic Bomb and get the bonus range first before going for subsequent attacks so you won’t get into a dangerous spot.
Be patient in chipping away the HP of your enemy, and don’t dive mindlessly, as the other team’s roamer or jungler might just be waiting in the bushes.

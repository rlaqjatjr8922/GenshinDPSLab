How to play Layla
:
Due to her skills, Layla scales incredibly well to the late game and is fairly strong in the early game. Don’t be hesitant to assist your teammates or engage in teamfights. Farm more if you’re unsure! Despite Layla’s lack of mobility, when used correctly, she may be quite safe.
Take advantage of Layla’s wide range compared to the other heroes. Stay safe, attack and position yourself.
Never move in advance of your teammates, always stay behind them.
Feel free to change the items to suit your preferred playing style.
Tactics
:
Early game (level 1-7): Focus on getting the gold bonus of from the minions and avoid chasing the enemies or you might ganked by their jungler.
Mid game (level 8-12): You should play stealthily and seek protection from your tank or fighters.
Late game (level 13+): To ensure your safety stick with your tank all the time so you could last longer in team fights and to contribute to kills and support more for more items and exp.

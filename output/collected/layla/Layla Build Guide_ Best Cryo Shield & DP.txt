Layla's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Stone Shielding with great uptime.
Low Energy requirements for her Elemental Burst.
Easy to build due to scaling on HP.
Weaknesses
Elemental Skill can accidentally Freeze yourself on use.
Needs later Constellations to unlock further supportive and damage capabilities.
Losing the shield ends her Elemental Skill early and stops Energy generation.

3.
Combos and How to Play Layla
Layla's gameplay focuses on her ability to generate Night Stars and Shooting
Stars using
Nights of Formal Focus
. When you use this Elemental
Skill, it creates a Night Star every 1.5 seconds and 2 Night Stars when a
character protected by Layla's shield uses their Elemental Skill.
After creating 4 Night Stars, they
transform into Shooting Stars that attack opponents, dealing damage and
applying
Cryo
. When a Shooting Star hits an enemy it generates Energy.
3.1.
Be Careful with Your Shield
Most mechanics surrounding Layla's gameplay require her shield to
be available. If your shield breaks you lose any related effects such
as the Shooting Star damage and Energy generation. This therefore
lowers your damage and limits your Elemental Application.
Layla's shield will also only last for 12 seconds but does have a cooldown
of 12 seconds making it possible to have 100% uptime on said shield. The
problem however is that most team compositions will not have 12 second
rotations. This does mean you will need to use
Nights of Formal Focus
a second time.

Layla's most important constellations are

and

increase to Layla.

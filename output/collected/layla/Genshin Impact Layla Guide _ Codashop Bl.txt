Genshin Impact features several 4-star Cryo support characters that serve as powerful units during games. They complement other characters, creating a stronger team for various challenges. Layla is the new 4-star Cryo character that players have been eyeing. Layla has excellent uptime with her Elemental Burst, provides a powerful shield, and can scale all her abilities from her HP. This post will help you build this character, including her skills, weapons, artifacts, and more.
Layla Spotlight
Layla is a 4-star cryo character known for her excellent Cryo application with Night Stars and her Elemental Burst that fires Starlight Slugs at enemies. Playing with Layla requires little to no effort to deploy her Cryo projectiles, making her an excellent support character. Layla has a great design and gameplay; players would love to have her. She is also powerful defensive support for the Morgana team.
Layla Best Build & Weapon
As a support unit, focusing on Layla’s support build is the better option. It centers on strengthening her shield to the max while simultaneously providing a buff to the team.

How to Use Layla
Layla is known for her shield, so it is best to use her Elemental Skill that triggers the shield first. Switch to other characters to deal attack. Go back to Layla once the shield fills out and trigger her Elemental Burst. Again, switch to another character for attacks. Have it as a rotation, trigger her shield again, and so on.

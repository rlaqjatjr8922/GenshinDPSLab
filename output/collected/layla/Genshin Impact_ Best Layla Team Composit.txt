Layla in
Genshin Impact
is somewhat similar to Diona. The two characters are a Cryo shielder that can provide Cryo application via their Elemental Burst, but unlike Diona whose ultimate can heal and has a small AoE, Layla’s Burst is offensive and has a larger area. This means Layla is more balanced on both offense and defense, unlike Diona who’s fully supportive on her kits.
Nevertheless, Layla’s best team comp in
Genshin Impact
is similar to Diona’s, where people can either play a Permafreeze, Superconduct, or Melt team with Layla.

Morgana Team
Team composition:
Layla + Cryo DPS + Hydro Enabler + Anemo Support
Cryo DPS
: Ganyu or Kamisato Ayaka
Hydro Enabler
: Mona or Sangonomiya Kokomi
Anemo Support
: Venti, Kaedehara Kazuha, or Sucrose
For the
Morgana team comp in
Genshin Impact
, Layla will take the role as a shielder that grants extra Cryo application. As usual, Mona or Kokomi will be the ones applying Hydro, while Ganyu or Ayaka is the main DPS. Finally, the Anemo support is typically Venti, but people can also opt for Kazuha or Sucrose.
Freeze Team
Team composition:
Layla + Hydro DPS + Anemo Support + Sub-DPS or Support
Hydro DPS
: Kamisato Ayato, Sangonomiya Kokomi, Tartaglia
Anemo Support
: Venti, Kaedehara Kazuha, or Sucrose
Sub-DPS or Support
: Yelan, Xingqiu, Qiqi, or Kaeya
Layla can also perform in Freeze
team comp that uses Hydro as the main DPS
. For this, players can either use Ayato, Kokomi or Childe. An Anemo support will also come in handy to crowd control the enemies and maintain Cryo or Hydro on them. As for the last slot, Travelers can either use Yelan, Xingqiu, or Kaeya as a sub-DPS, or Qiqi as a sub-DPS and healer.
Superconduct Team
Team composition:
Layla + Physical DPS + Superconduct Support + Sub-DPS or Support
Physical DPS
: Eula, Razor, Sangonomiya Kokomi, or Rosaria
Electro Enabler
: Raiden Shogun, Yae Miko, Fischl, Beidou, Kuki Shinobu, Lisa, Dori, or Kujou Sara
Sub-DPS or Support
: Yun Jin, Zhongli, Candace, Bennett, Qiqi, Xingqiu, or Yelan
Layla’s Superconduct team comp revolves around a Physical DPS. This can either be Eula, Razor or Rosaria, but people can also use this team to
maximize Kokomi’s Ocean-Hued Clam damage
. Other than Layla,
if the main DPS isn’t Razor, there should also be an Electro character
.
Once players have Layla, an Electro enabler, and a main DPS, the final slot should be filled with a sub-DPS or Support.
Yun Jin
: Grants flat damage bonus to Normal Attack
Zhongli
: Provides stronger shield and debuff
Candace
: Increases Normal Attack DMG
Bennett
: Provide massive ATK buff
Qiqi
: A healer that will benefit from Superconduct if equipped with Ocean-Hued Clam
Xingqiu or Yelan
: Powerful Hydro sub-DPS
Melt Team
Team composition:
Layla + Anemo Support + Pyro DPS + Sub-DPS or Support
Pyro DPS
: Diluc, Yanfei, Xiangling, Klee
Anemo Support
: Venti, Kaedehara Kazuha, or Sucrose
Sub-DPS or Support
: Bennett, Ganyu, Rosaria, Kaeya, Zhongli, Qiqi
Layla’s Cryo application is not powerful enough to host a Melt team, but with an
Anemo support and another Cryo sub-DPS
, players should be able to play this team comp. The Pyro damage dealer can either be Diluc, Yanfei, or Klee, but Xiangling also works as a sub-DPS.

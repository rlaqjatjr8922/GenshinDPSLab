Raiden Shogun is the third playable Archon in Genshin Impact. Like Venti and Zhongli before her, Raiden is a powerful support unit that utilizes Energy Recharge to power up your team. Our complete Raiden Shogun guide for
Genshin Impact
will help you Ascend her as fast as possible, with detailed looks at Materials, Bosses and Builds.

Raiden Shogun is (surprisingly) an Electro Polearm user. To get Raiden Shogun Ascended to Lv 80/90, you’ll need the following:
Ascension 1- Lv 20
1 Vajrada Amethyst Sliver
3 Amakumo Fruit
3 Old Handguard
20,000 Mora
Ascension 2- Lv 40
3 Vajrada Amethyst Fragment
2 Storm Beads
10 Amakumo Fruit
15 Old Handguard
40,000 Mora
Ascension 3- Lv 50
6 Vajrada Amethyst Fragment
4 Storm Beads
20 Amakumo Fruit
12 Kageuchi Handguard
60,000 Mora
Ascension 4- Lv 60
3 Vajrada Amethyst Chunk
8 Storm Beads
30 Amakumo Fruit
18 Kageuchi Handguard
80,000 Mora
Ascension 5- Lv 70
6 Vajrada Amethyst Chunk
12 Storm Beads
45 Amakumo Fruit
12 Famed Handguard
100,000 Mora
Ascension 6- Lv 80
6 Vajrada Amethyst Gemstone
20 Storm Beads
60 Amakumo Fruit
24 Famed Handguard
120,000 Mora
In total, you’ll need 1 Vajrada Amethyst Sliver, 9 Vajrada Amethyst Fragments, 9 Vajrada Amethyst Chunks, 6 Vajrada Amethyst Gemstones, 18 Old Handguard, 30 Kageuchi Handguard, 36 Famed Handguard, 46 Storm Beads and 168 Amakumo Fruit to get Ascended to Lv 80/90.

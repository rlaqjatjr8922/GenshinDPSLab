Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon in Genshin Impact, is without a doubt a popular character among players. While there were several controversies regarding her playstyle upon release, she has now become one of the most used characters in the Spiral Abyss.

Five best teams for Raiden Shogun (Baal) in Genshin Impact
Here are the best teams for Raiden Shogun that players can use in Genshin Impact:
Ad
Trending
1) Raiden, Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu - National team
The
Raiden National team
is one of the most popular meta teams in the Spiral Abyss because it is extremely F2P friendly. Apart from Raiden, it only contains four-star characters which are heavily used in the Abyss.
Both Xiangling and Xingqiu have high burst costs because Raiden is the most suitable teammate for them. Bennett, as usual, provides the much-required ATK boost and healing.
Ad
Ad
Players must build all the characters in this team for quick rotations and elemental reactions. With Xingqiu and Xiangling as off-field support characters, Raiden's Elemental Burst can be used to deal extensive amounts of damage.
2) Raiden Shogun, Nahida, Xingqiu, Sangonomiya Kokomi
Nahida, as seen in-game (Image via miHoYo)
Hyperbloom is one of the strongest reactions in Genshin Impact, and there's no doubt that a team with Raiden Shogun at the forefront will be extremely strong. Players can use Nahida to apply Dendro off-field alongside Xingqiu and Kokomi to apply Hydro, resulting in a lot of Dendro cores.
Ad
Lastly, Raiden Shogun uses her Electro abilities to trigger Hyperbloom dealing a lot of damage to the enemies.
3) Raiden Shogun, Kujou Sara, Kazuha, Bennett - Hypercarry team
It is self-evident that this team focuses on utilizing Raiden Shogun's burst damage. Both Sara
and Bennett
have been added to provide ATK buffs, while Kazuha has to decrease enemies' resistance.
Ad
Players need to invest heavily in their Raiden Shogun's Energy Recharge and damage output to manage this team successfully.
4) Raiden Shogun, Kujou Sara, Chevreuse , Bennett
Ad
This is a variation of the Hypercarry team which switches Kazuha with the new
4-star Pyro support, Chevreuse
. After triggering an overload reaction, Chevreuse's abilities can buff the Electro and Pyro damage of her teammates.
Similar to Kazuha, Chevreuse's role here is to buff Raiden Shogun's elemental damage, increasing the overall output of this team comp.
5) Eula, Raiden Shogun, Diona, Zhongli
It won't be an overstatement that Eula and Raiden Shogun form one of the most potent duos in the game. The only prominent issue
with Eula
was her Elemental Burst cost, and the Electro Archon is the ideal character to resolve this.
Ad
Eula, as seen in-game (Image via miHoYo)
Eula's physical damage is buffed with the Superconduct reactions triggered by Raiden's Elemental Skill and a Cryo support character like Diona. The Cryo Bow user also plays the role of a healer in this team.
Ad
Even though the Raiden Shogun team compositions mentioned above are the most popular, players can always experiment with their favorite characters in Genshin Impact and create special teams.

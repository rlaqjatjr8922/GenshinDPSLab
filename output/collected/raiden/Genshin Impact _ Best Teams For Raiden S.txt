Synergy Of Hyperbloom Raiden Shogun Team
Character
Detail
Nahida
Elemental Skill allows continuous Dendro application for Bloom with Kokomi
Kokomi
Great Hydro application across her Elemental Skill and Normal Attacks for Bloom creation. Healing will allow the team to survive
Dendro Traveler
Will provide additional Dendro application. Dendro shred with Deepwood Memories
This team is centered around Hyperbloom as the main reaction, although it also deals a ton of additional damage through Electro-charged and Aggravate/Spread. Raiden Shogun is the main Hyperbloom driver and will work well with Kokomi or Nahida as they act as the on-field character.
Pros & Cons Of Hyperbloom Raiden Shogun Team
Pros
- High damage team comp due to multiple reactions it can deal
- Fairly easy and forgiving rotation between characters
Cons
- Team will require good dodging since there's no shielder
- Requires 3 5-star characters
- Will require a ton of farming since most of the artifacts are newly released
Rotation Of Hyperbloom Raiden Shogun Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.

2

3
Switch to Kokomi and use her skill

Switch to the Dendro Traveler and use her Burst, then skill
5
Switch to Raiden Shogun and use her Burst
6
Repeat 1
This team build has a pretty flexible rotation. You don't have to follow a specific rotation because the most important part is to just refresh their skills as much as possible. For Nahida, you need to have her skill and Burst up as much as possible.

Synergy Of National Raiden Shogun Team
Character
Detail
Xiangling
Xiangling's Burst will retain the damage buff from Bennett's Burst. It's the main reaction driver, dealing the most damage out of all the characters
Xingqiu
Xingqiu's burst also benefits from Bennett's Burst. Best when his Burst is used right before using Raiden Shogun's Burst
Bennett
Bennett can easily increase the damage of the whole team with his Burst. With him and Xiangling on the team, you also get an ATK increase through the Pyro resonance
This team is a multi-reaction team. Xiangling, Xingqiu, and Raiden's Bursts work well together to deal multiple reactions at once. Bennett can help sustain the group, while also increasing the damage of the team.
Pros & Cons Of National Raiden Shogun Team
Pros
- Easy to coordinate team
- Raiden Shogun provides energy for the team allowing for easier rotations
- high damage through multiple reactions happening
- Only requires 1 5-star character
Cons
- High investment team since all 4-star members must be fully built and with constellations to get the best damage
- 4-Star characters are used a lot in other teams, making it hard to build other team comps when they're all in a National Comp
Rotation Of National Raiden Shogun Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Raiden Shogun's Skill
2
Switch to Bennett and release his Burst
3
Use Xiangling and use her Burst
4
Then, use Xingqiu's Burst
5
Switch back to Raiden and use her Burst. Spam Normal Attack until it expires
When running this team, the rotation is pretty flexible. The only parts you need to remember is to always have Raiden's Skill up, and use Bursts immediately after you've put down Bennett's Burst.

Synergy Of Hypercarry Raiden Shogun Team
Character
Detail
Kujou Sara
Kujou Sara directly increases Raiden Shogun's damage through her Elemental Skill. She can also deal additional damage with her Burst
Bennett
Bennett can increase Raiden Shogun's damage and heal her. With a full Noblesse Oblige set, he can directly increase her Burst as well after using his Burst

As a Hypercarry team, this team is centered around stacking different buffs from Support characters to increase Raiden Shogun's damage.
Pros & Cons Of Hypercarry Raiden Shogun Team
Pros
- Easy rotation to keep up with
- High damage potential
- Doesn't require Elemental Reactions
Cons
- High investment team since all 4-star members must be fully built and with constellations to get the best damage
- Best with C2 Raiden Shogun
- Weak against Electro-resistant enemies
Rotation Of Hypercarry Raiden Shogun Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Raiden Shogun's Elemental Skill
2
Switch to Kazuha and use his Burst, then skill
3
Next, drop Bennett's Burst
4
Switch to Kujou Sara and use her Burst then skill
5
Quickly switch to Raiden Shogun and use her Burst. Spam Normal Attack until it expires
When it comes to rotation, there are just two parts that are integral. When you use Sara's Skill, make sure that the next character you bring out is Raiden Shogun. It's also important that Kazuha swirls Electro so time that too.

Character
Detail
Eula

Cons

Procedure
1
Start with Raiden Shogun's Elemental Skill
2
Switch to Eula and use her skill
3
Next, use Rosaira and unleash her Burst, then Skill
4
Switch to Mika and use his skill
5
Use Eula next. Unleash her Burst, then hold skill. Spam Normal Attack until it becomes full
6
Switch to Raiden and use her Burst
7
Repeat 1
This rotation is forgiving enough. Just remember to always refresh Raiden's skill and use her Burst only after you used Eula's Burst to refill it quickly. Mika and Rosaria's support skills should be used right before Eula.

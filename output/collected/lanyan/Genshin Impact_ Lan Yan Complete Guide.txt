One such character who was introduced alongside the 2025 Lantern Rite festival was Lan Yan. She is an Anemo Catalyst user in the game and if you're interested in pulling for her, here's a guide that will help cover all the necessary details.
Best Lan Yan Weapons
Lan Yan is a
Catalyst user in Genshin Impact,
and she primarily functions as a support. As such, you'll need Catalysts that can buff her own abilities as well as allies.
Damage buffs won't be as useful, so you can
avoid all the Crit Catalysts
. However, anything that can
boost ATK, shield strength, and Swirl efficiency
will be perfect on Lan Yan.
Name
Rarity
Synergy With Lan Yan
Memory of Dust
Five Star
Memory of Dust is perfect for Lan Yan as this Catalyst boasts a
massive base ATK, and it also comes with ATK percent
as a secondary stat. Additionally, it also provides a
boost to shield strength,
which is perfect for Lan Yan.
Starcaller's Watch
Five Star
If you don't want to use Lan Yan as a shielder, but instead
focus on her Swirl ability
, Starcaller's Watch is a great choice. You will receive a lot of
Elemental Mastery bonus
both from its passive and substats, while also
providing a shield
that works on the active character.
This
shield increases the damage the active character deals
, further improving the teamwide utility of Lan Yan.
Skyward Atlas
Five Star
Lan Yan's
shield strength scales off ATK,
which makes
Skyward Atlas
a pretty good choice for her. This Catalyst provides
a lot of Base ATK and comes with ATK percent substats
which helps push the value of Lan Yan's shield.
Oathsworn Eye
Four Star
If you're looking for a powerful four-star option,
Oathsword Eye
is fantastic due to its
ATK percent substat
. Additionally, it provides
a lot of Energy Recharge through its passive
which makes it easier for Lan Yan to use her ultimate ability.
Sacrificial Fragments
Four Star
If you're looking for a more affordable option for your Swirl Lan Yan, then
Sacrificial Fragments
is worth considering. You receive
Elemental Mastery from its substats
which improves the effectiveness of the Swirls and thereby boosts Elemental Reaction damage.
Best Lan Yan Artifacts
The Artifact choice for Lan Yan is
pretty standard,
regardless of whether you build her as a Shield support or a Swirl support. However, you have to ensure that, irrespective of your choice of Artifacts, she
is able to use both the 2-pc and 4-pc passives
effectively.
Here are some of the
best Artifacts for Lan Yan:
Viridescent Venerer
Irrespective of how you build Lan Yan,
Viridescent Venerer
is the best Artifact set for her. Apart from the
Anemo DMG bonus,
it also
boosts Swirl Damage
and
reduces the RES of enemies
. This means
regardless of your build, Lan Yan will
increase the damage output for her team.
Gladiator's Finale
Since Lan Yan's shield strength depends on her
ATK values
,
using 2-pc Gladiator's
Finale is worth it. However, the
4-pc stats don't benefit her
, so you don't need to worry
about farming them.
Shimenawa's Remiscence
Similar to Gladiator's Finale,
2-pc Shimenawa's Reminiscence provides ATK boost,
and you can
pair it with 2-pc Gladiator's Finale.
This will significantly
boost Lan Yan's
shield and help you stay alive in battle for longer.
Lan Yan Stat Priority
The kind of stats you go for when building Lan Yan
depends on how you want to use her
. If you are using her purely for the
shield,
then
focusing on ATK stats
will be more beneficial. However, if you're looking to use her as an
Anemo support
, then
Elemental Mastery
will be a better stat for her.

Best Lan Yan Team Composition
Lan Yan, being an Anemo unit, can
slot into a wide variety of teams
because of her ability to use the Viridescent Venerer set. Regardless of how you play her, she will be
buffing the damage output for her entire team,
which is a trait that is hard to come by in this game.

Lan Yan Talent Priority
Despite how you decide to use Lan Yan, her
primary utility comes from the shield,
followed by her ability to provide decent crowd control. This means you need to level up her abilities properly so that she can become viable for end-game content.
You can also level up Lan Yan's Normal Attack, but it doesn't offer any value to her kit and unless you really like the character, its worth skipping.
Her
Elemental Skill is the most important part of the kit
as it increases the overall shield strength.
You can also level-up the Elemental Burst
as it can deal damage while providing
crowd control
.

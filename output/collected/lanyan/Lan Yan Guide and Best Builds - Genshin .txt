Lan Yan's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Some of the strongest shields in the game.
Being an Anemo character allows Lan Yan to use Viridescent Venerer while shielding which provides role compression.
AoE grouping with Elemental Burst.
Weaknesses
Short Shield duration being unable to last a full rotation.
Can be difficult to swirl the right Element for your Main DPS to gain the benefits of VV.
Crowd Control is tied to use of Lan Yan's Elemental Burst.

Lan Yan's biggest asset is her shielding potential, allowing her to provide
shields stronger than even the Geo Archon himself at times. This build focuses on Lan Yan's potential
as a supportive character in the form of increasing shield strength as
well as benefits that Lan Yan provides to the team.
Lan Yan's best weapons in a support build focus either on increasing her shield
strength or providing buffs to the team.
Memory of Dust
and
Skyward Atlas
are best at increasing shield strength while
Starcaller's Watch
and
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
are
best at providing buffs to the team if you do not need as much shielding.
Viridescent Venerer
is Lan Yan's best artifact set for the RES Shred
increase which further amplifies her value as a support. Other artifact sets you
can build for Lan Yan include
Noblesse Oblige
or
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
.
Lan Yan's Elemental Skill is by far her most important Talent to level with
her Elemental Burst being second if used. Normal Attacks are rarely needed on Lan Yan unless
you plan to use her to drive reactions occasionally.

Lan Yan wants around 220-280% ER as the Sole Anemo or 180-190% with Double 
Anemo to burst every rotation if needed. 
That being said, many teams do not want/need the grouping provided by her burst or
the EM buff provided by her C4. This means Lan Yan's ER requirements realistically 
will often be lower but it is better to build higher to cover a range of teams unless 
your Lan Yan feels squishy.
4.
Combos and How to Play Lan Yan
Lan Yan is a straightforward character to play and a refreshing change from
recent releases. Lan Yan's Elemental Skill
Swallow-Wisp Pinion Dance is your is your primary ability. It grants a
strong shield lasting 12.5 seconds, though not the longest duration. Holding
the skill activates a mobility ability instead. Her Elemental Burst,
Lustrous Moonrise, offers AoE grouping (and an EM buff at C4) but might not always be necessary,
depending on your team setup.
The shields are particularly notable thanks to the Ascension Passive
Four Sealing Divination Charms, which allows the shield to absorb
Hydro
,
Pyro
,
Cryo
and
Electro
damage. Additionally, her C2 constellation, Dance Vestments Billow Like Rainbow Jade,
enhances shielding by restoring some damage absorption when Normal Attacks hit enemies.
To play Lan Yan effectively as a shielder, you need to get the timing right
for your team. With
Viridescent Venerer
's effect only lasting 10s and the
shield lasting 12.5s you want to use your Elemental Skill where possible right before
your DPS enters the field to gain the full benefits but you also need to ensure
you swirl the right element.
5.
Lan Yan Best Teams
Being an
Anemo
character, Lan Yan can be used
in a wide range of team compositions. Most importantly, she acts as a great
substitute for both
Kaedehara Kazuha
and
Zhongli
due to being able to provide both Shielding and
RES Shred. In teams that use both Kazuha and Zhongli, using Lan Yan instead allows you to
add either another DPS or support character while still retaining some of the benefits
these characters have.
Lan Yan can also be played in any team that simply wants a strong shielder.

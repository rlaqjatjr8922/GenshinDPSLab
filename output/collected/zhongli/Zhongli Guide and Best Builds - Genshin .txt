Zhongli's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
The strongest shield in the game.
Shield gives a universal 20% resistance shred increasing DPS for the team.
Incredibly flexible, can be used in a large range of team compositions.
Elemental Burst petrifies enemies providing CC.
Weaknesses
Shields have no impact on corrosion attacks.
Elemental Burst can result in a DPS loss due to its long animation.
Low energy generation.

shield to its absolute limits. Alternatively, Zhongli can instead be built as a

4.
Combos and How to Play Zhongli
Zhongli's main focus is on his shield, simply
hold your elemental skill,
Dominus Lapidis
, to create a shield.
Zhongli's placement of the Stone Steles can be very important however,
if placed at a bad angle or against larger enemies it may
automatically break losing you any benefits outside of the shield.
Planet Befall
summons a meteor in front of
your character meaning that positioning of your character
and grouping of enemies is important to ensure enemies are
petrified. When petrified you have the ability to easily set
up additional reactions and support your team without interruption.

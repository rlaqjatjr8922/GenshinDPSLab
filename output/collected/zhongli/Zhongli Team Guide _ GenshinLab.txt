Zhongli Team Overview
This page provides the best Zhongli Team information. Zhongli is a Shield/Support in most situations.
Zhongli’s Elemental Skill provides shield to teammates and reduces Elemental and Physical RES of enemies. This makes Zhongli becomes a flexible character and suitable in different teams.
Zhongli can be assigned to different teams. This guide shows some of the best teams of Zhongli.

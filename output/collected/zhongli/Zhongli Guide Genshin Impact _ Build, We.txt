Genshin Impact Zhongli Guide: Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
April 11, 2024
Tips
Share:
Are you looking for an effective way to protect your characters' health points in
Genshin Impact
?
Zhongli
shines in this area: his shield takes most of the damage. The
Archon Geo
also deals a lot of damage. He is an
excellent support
in any team! But to take full advantage of his potential, you'll first need to
give
him
a good build
. With this
complete guide to Zhongli from Genshin Impact
, you'll know which artefacts and weapons to equip on him, how to play him, etc.
How to get Zhongli in Genshin Impact ?
Zhongli was released on December 1, 2020
. To hope to unlock the Geo Archon, he must be featured in an Character Event Wish.
Zhongli's Character Event Wish in Genshin Impact
Zhongli has a rerun in the second part of
update 2.4 of Genshin Impact
from 25 January until 15 February 2022. This will give you a small chance to summon him by making wishes in the
Mountains and Markets
banner.
Zhongli Constellations
Zhongli is one of those characters who doesn't need constellations to be strong. However, if you really want to invest in him, his C2
Stone, the Jade Cradle
is quite interesting. Zhongli's elemental rampage in turn creates a shield. This way, you can ensure that your characters are always protected.
How to play Zhongli in Genshin Impact ?
Zhongli is a versatile character who fits perfectly into any team. While he can be played as a main DPS, we recommend that you use him as a support character; his normal attack lacks power.
On the other hand, his
Dominus Lapidis
elemental attack makes him a very good tank. The simple support generates a
Stone Stele
that causes zone Geo damage. It can resonate with other Geo constructs and these will then inflict Geo damage. With a long press, Zhongli also creates a
Jade Shield
that absorbs an amount of damage (physical and elemental) proportional to its HP. Enemies' resistance is also reduced by 20% and Zhongli absorbs the Geo element in his opponents, which can then break their shields.
Thanks to its
Resonant Waves
passive, each attack hitting the Jade Shield increases its strength by 5%. Furthermore, the effect can be cumulated 5 times. However, Zhongli's shield does not protect against corrosion. You will still lose health points.
You will also inflict a lot of damage with
Planet Befall
, Zhongli's elemental rampage. As the name suggests, a huge meteor crashes down on enemies. It immobilises its victims by petrifying them.
For a support build, mostly mount the elemental attack and elemental rampage (at least level 6). For this, collect
Teachings of Gold
,
Guide to Gold
and
Philosophies of Gold
in the
Taishan Mansion
dungeon on Wednesday, Saturday and Sunday.
What is the best equipment for Zhongli?
Zhongli from Genshin Impact needs to accumulate as many HP as possible in order to provide the best protection for his team. In addition, his
Dominance of Earth
passive increases the damage of all his attacks according to his HP.
The best artefacts for Zhongli
For a really HP oriented build, we recommend equipping 4 pieces of the
Tenacity of the Millelith
set. This set increases Zhongli's HP by 20% and, when an elemental skill hits an enemy, theATK of the whole team is boosted by 20% and the strength of the shield by 30% for 3 seconds. The effect can be triggered every 0.5 seconds, even if the wearer of the set is not present on the field.
Tenacity of the Millelith
Vourukasha’s Glow
An indestructible Zhongli shield requires feeding him lots of HP stats. Hence, opting for artifacts that could grant him the highest amount is optimal. In this case, equipping 2PC
Tenacity of Millelith
and
Vourukasha’s Glow
is recommended as each pair provides 20% HP.
For a more aggressive build, prefer the combination of two artefact sets: 2 pieces of
Ancient Royal Ritual
and 2 pieces of
Ancient Rock
. You will obtain a bonus of 20% for the elementary unleashing as well as a bonus of damage Geo of 15%.
Noblesse Oblige
Archaic Petra
In terms of stats, focus
on HP
if you want equipment that increases Zhongli's shield considerably. To do more damage, go for HP% on the hourglass, Geo damage bonuses on the cup and damage or critical rate on the crown. For secondary stats, look for critical rate, critical damage, HP%, attack and energy recharge.
The best weapons for Zhongli
In order to perfect Zhongli's shield build in Genshin Impact, we recommend weapons that increase the character's health points such as the
Homa Staff
(5★). This hast weapon brings a lot of critical damage and HP. Whether playing offensively or defensively, this is the best spear for Zhongli level build. The other 5-star option remains the
Prismatic Piercer
(5★), his signature weapon. It increasesATK and shield strength.
Staff of Homa (5★)
Vortex Vanquisher (5★)
If you play free-to-play, you can choose the
Black Tassel
(3★) which directly increases HP. It will also be easy to mount!
Black Tassel (3★)
To play more offensively, any hast weapon with critical rate or damage will work with Zhongli. For example, the Battle Pass weapon the
Scion of Victory
(4★) increases critical rate andATK and DEF by 16% if there are two or more enemies. If there are fewer,ATK is increased by 24%. Or "
The Grip"
(4★) which increases the damage of the elemental rampage by 16% and the CRIT rate by 6%.
Deathmatch (4★)
The catch" (4★)
Zhongli's ascension materials in Genshin Impact
In order to reach level 90 in Genshin Impact, Zhongli will need several resources:
419
Lessons from the Hero
and 2.1 million Moras ;
1 sliver, 9 fragments, 9 chunks and 6 gemstones of
Prithivia topaz
as well as 46
basalt pillars
to be recovered from
the Geo Hypostasis
.
18
Blob Slime
, 30
Blob Mucus
and 36
Blob Essences
;
168
Cor Lapis
to collect in Liyue. You are advised to refer to the
interactive map of Teyvat
to locate them more easily.
Which team should Zhongli play in?
Zhongli can be adapted to all the teams of Genshin Impact. But it will be even more valuable in teams where the main DPS has little HP and needs protection, like
Yoimiya
and
Ganyu
. It will also protect the two archers from interruptions.
The archon is also a good support choice for
Xiao
and
Hu Tao
who lose health points quickly because of their skills.
Finally, it works particularly well with heroes with Geo builds such as
Albedo
and
Ningguang
. You can for example play it in a team of Geo characters with Albedo,
Arataki Itto
and
Gorou
.
What is Zhongli's birthday and height from Genshin Impact ?
Zhongli is no less than Morax, the Geo Archon! He has made many contracts over the millennia. However, he has now chosen to live incognito among men in Liyue, where he is a consultant for Wangsheng's funeral. He is a very detail-oriented character who knows almost everything there is to know about Teyvat.
Zhongli is approximately 1.85m tall
: he is one of the tallest characters in the game. He was born on December 31st and is said to be
over 6000 years old
. You can learn more about him by completing his 2 story quests.
To conclude this guide to Zhongli from Genshin impact, it can only be repeated that it excels as a support. Thanks to its shield, you will be able to protect your characters effectively and concentrate on attacking rather than dodging. If you are having trouble keeping your heroes alive in the abyss, Zhongli will probably save the day.

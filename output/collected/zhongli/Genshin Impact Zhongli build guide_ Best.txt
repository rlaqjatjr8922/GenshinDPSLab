Zhongli’s abilities in Genshin Impact explained
Elemental Skill
Zhongli’s Elemental Skill in Genshin Impact can be used in two ways:
Press the Elemental Skill button once to create a Geo construct that blocks attacks and you can climb.
Press and hold the Elemental Skill button to create the Geo construct and form a shield that absorbs damage based on Zhongli’s max HP.
The Geo construct is called Stone Shele. It deals AoE Geo DMG upon creation while the Jade Shield decreases the opponent’s Elemental and Physical RES and grants 150% DMG absorption against all Elemental and Pyshical DMG.

Elemental Burst
Zhongli literally summons a meteor through his Elemental Burst in Genshin Impact. Upon hitting the ground, this meteor deals massive Geo DMG to opponents in its radius and Petrifies them.
The Petrification status in
the game
stops enemies from moving for a short duration.

How to play Zhongli in Genshin Impact
You can use Zhongli either as a primary shielder or as a burst-DPS character in Genshin Impact. As a shielder, you simply have to hold the Elemental Skill button to summon the shield and swap him with your main damage dealer.

Best artifacts for Zhongli in Genshin Impact
Four-piece Tenacity of the Millelith
If you want to use Zhongli as a shielder, the four-piece Tenacity of the Millelith artifact set in Genshin Impact is ideal. The two-piece increases HP by 20% while the four-piece set increases the ATK and shield strength of all nearby party members after using the Elemental Skill.
Both these buffs are desirable for Zhongli because his shield strength is based on his max HP. Moreover, the four-piece bonus benefits the entire team which is exactly what you’d expect from a supporting character.

Four-piece Noblesse Oblige
Noblesse Oblige is a
universally brilliant artifact set
for Genshin Impact support characters, and Zhongli is no exception. The two-piece increases Elemental Burst DMG and the four-piece buffs the ATK of all party members for 12 seconds after using the Elemental Burst.
The four-piece Noblesse Oblige is perfect for a Burst-DPS Zhongli build in Genshin Impact. You’ll get massive Geo DMG from the Burst and after petrifying the opponents, you can swap to another damage dealer and make the most of the ATK buff from the four-piece set.

Two-piece Archaic Petra with two-piece Noblesse Oblige/Tenacity of the Millelith
The two-piece Archaic Petra set grants a 15% Geo DMG Bonus which is great if you want Zhongli to deal more damage in Genshin Impact. The four-piece set isn’t really useful so we recommend pairing it with a two-piece Noblesse Oblige or Tenacity of the Millelith.

Use Tenacity of the Milelith if Zhongli is the primary shielder of the team. If the Geo Archon is being used as a Burst-DPS, the two-piece Noblesse Oblige is a better option.

Which artifact stats are best for Zhongli?
If you want Zhongli to create unbreakable shields in Genshin Impact, simply go for HP% on your Sands, Circlet, and Goblet.
However, to maximize Zhongli’s damage as a sub-DPS/Burst-DPS, you should choose Geo DMG Bonus for the Goblet and Crit Rate/Crit DMG for Circlet. HP% is still a brilliant stat for your Sands artifact.
Best weapons for Zhongli in Genshin Impact
Staff of Homa
Although the Staff of Homa is a five-star Polearm tailor-made for Hu Tao, it is also the best weapon for Zhongli. This is because you get over 66% Crit DMG at Lv. 90 and the passive ability increases HP by 20%.

With a Staff of Homa, your Zhongli’s Elemental Burst can be an overpowered skill that deals massive damage. At the same time, the HP buff from the passive will improve the shield strength.
Vortex Vanquisher
Vortex Vanquisher is the tailor-made five-star weapon for Zhongli in Genshin Impact. It grants a ton of ATK and the passive ability improves shield strength.
Best four-star Polearms for Zhongli in Genshin Impact
As it turns out, many four-star Polearms like the Deathmatch, Lithic Spear, Blackcliff Pole, and Favonius Lance work well with Zhongli. None of these Polearms will improve shield strength but they all buff your ATK/damage in unique ways.

Based on availability and your team’s need, feel free to use any of these Polearms with Zhongli and make him a solid Burst-DPS.

Black Tassel
If you want a weapon to buff Zhongli’s shield strength in Genshin Impact, you can choose the three-star Black Tassle. The weapon grants HP% but do note that the passive ability is almost useless.
Best team compositions for Zhongli in Genshin Impact

elemental reactions
and the damage dealers will be able to fulfill their role more freely.

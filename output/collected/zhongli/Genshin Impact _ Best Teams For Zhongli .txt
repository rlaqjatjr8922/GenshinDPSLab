Zhongli Works Best as a Shielder
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Shielder Zhongli
- Makes the best use of Zhongli's team to protect DPS characters from getting hit.
- Elemental shred from his shield allows him to buff all damage dealt.
2nd
▼ Mono Geo Team
- Itto is the On-Field DPS character and is able to create a Geo construct to resonate with Zhongli's Stone Stele.
- Zhongli's Resistance shred works for Geo damage and allows Itto to deal more damage without worrying about interruptions in his Charged Attack spamming.
3rd
▼ Burst DPS Zhongli
- Utilizes the high multiplier in Zhongli's Elemental Burst to deal powerful damage.
- Reliant on damage buffers and needs Energy funneling for his Burst.
Zhongli is a character whose teams aren't built around him since his main role is to be a Support for a DPS character. But he has certain teams that work particularly well with his kit like Itto's Mono Geo team.

In meta DPS teams, Zhongli's role is to be a Shield support that prevents interruptions and provides Resistance shred. Since this is a flexible team based on Zhongli's shielder role, he can be used with any On-Field DPS characters.
Rotation Of Shielder Zhongli
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Zhongli and use his Skill (Hold) to create a shield
2
Switch to Furina and use her Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks
4
Use Xilonen's Burst for continuous healing before switching her out
5
Switch to Neuvillette and use his Skill then Burst
6
Deal damage with Charged Attacks and refresh the Sourcewater Droplets with Skill or Burst
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire

Itto's Mono Geo team utilizes Zhongli's shield to prevent Itto's Charged Attack spam from getting interrupted and the resistance shred for boosting the damage dealt.
Rotation Of Mono Geo Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Zhongli and use his Skill (Hold) to create a shield
2
Switch to Gorou and use his Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Chiori and use her Burst then Skill twice
4
Once Itto is on the field, use his Burst then Skill
5
Gain stacks with Itto's Normal Attacks before using his Charged Attacks
6
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
Adjust your team setup to have Itto be positioned to the right of Chiori.

Raiden Shogun buffs Elemental Burst DMG, Bennett buffs ATK% and Xilonen boosts Elemental Damage dealt with Scroll set and her res shred. If Staff of Homa has higher refines, HP% sands is a better choice. Raiden Shogun can be used as On-Field DPS if Zhongli's Burst didn't defeat enemies.
Rotation Of Burst DPS Zhongli
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Raiden Shogun and use her Skill
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Zhongli and use his Burst
6
You can also use his Shield if needing Raiden Shogun to finish off enemies.
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
If enemies are still alive, you can use Raiden Shogun's Burst to finish off enemies with her Normal and Charged Attacks.

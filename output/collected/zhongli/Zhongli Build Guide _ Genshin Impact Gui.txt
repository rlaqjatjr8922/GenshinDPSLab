C2 will make you everyone’s best friend in co-op. It creates a shield around all other nearby players when Zhongli uses his Elemental Skill.

C4 increases Zhongli’s Elemental Burst AoE and slightly increases the time his opponents are left petrified. This is useful but isn’t significant enough to pull for.
This increases the level to which Zhongli’s Elemental Burst can be increased.
C6 turns Zhongli into a healer. Generally, you don’t need a healer with Zhongli as his shield is so powerful, therefore this constellation really only benefits you against Rifthound opponents. If you love Zhongli then you can definitely aim for this! Otherwise, it is not worth C6ing this 5-star character.

Tenacity of the Millelith
is Zhongli’s best artifact set. The 2-piece set increases his HP which in turn makes his shield more powerful. As Zhongli’s shield comes from his Elemental Skill, the 4-piece set increases the ATK and Shield Strength of his teammates whenever he uses this. With this set equipped Zhongli becomes an even stronger Shielder and a great buff character.
2-Piece:
Elemental Burst DMG +20%.
4-Piece:
Using an Elemental Burst increases all party members’ ATK by 20% for 12s. This effect cannot stack.
Noblesse Oblige
does not directly benefit Zhongli but instead helps to improve his teammates. It is nowhere near as effective as Tenacity of the Millelith but is a potential substitute.
2-Piece:
Geo DMG Bonus +15%.
4-Piece:
Upon obtaining an Elemental Shard created through a Crystallize Reaction, all party members gain 35% DMG Bonus for that particular element for 10s. Only one form of Elemental DMG Bonus can be gained in this manner at any one time.
Archaic Petra
is a very niche set that Zhongli can take advantage of. It does require Zhongli to be the player that obtains the Elemental Shard which makes team rotation a bit more difficult.
Staff of Homa
is Zhongli’s best 5-star weapon option. It not only increases his HP but also provides an ATK Bonus. This is perfect for an all-rounder Zhongli who is capable at both defence and offence.
Vortex Vanquisher
, Zhongli’s signature weapon, does not actually fit him very well. Its passive of increasing Shield Strength can be useful, but its secondary-stat of ATK focuses on building Zhongli as a DPS character - which is not his primary purpose. If you want this weapon for its aesthetic appeal then by all means pull for it. However, it is not worth getting it if you want a powerful shielder build.
Favonius Lance
helps Zhongli regenerate Energy and act as a battery. This increases his utility as a whole. It is important to note that Favonius Lance’s passive does not work when Zhongli is off-field.
Black Tassel
may be a 3-star weapon but it is the polearm with the highest HP% available. As Zhongli’s shield scales off HP this is the absolute best weapon for a shielder build.

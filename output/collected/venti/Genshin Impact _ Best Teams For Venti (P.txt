▼ On-Field Anemo DPS Team
★★★★★
- Following his buffs as a Hexerei, Venti can be played as On-Field DPS that uses his Anemo-infused Normal Attacks to deal damage.
- He functions similarly to other Anemo DPSes in this playstyle, with some reliance on his Elemental Burst.
▼ Electro-charged/Lunar-charged Team
★★★★・
- One of Venti's known strengths is how good he is into multiple enemies, especially if these enemies can be affected by the suction effect of his Burst.
- Lunar-Charged and Electro-Charged favor AoE content, so Venti is a viable teammate for this comp.
▼ PHEC DPS Team
★★★・・
- Venti was originally designed to be an Off-Field Anemo Sub-DPS or Support that can be flexed into teams where he can Swirl elements, which is every element except Geo and Dendro.
- Teams composed of Pyro, Hydro, Electro and Cryo characters can opt into pairing with Venti for the Crowd Control and Elemental RES Shred via
Viridescent Venerer
.
Venti is best used as a Crowd Control support that can hold VV for Elemental DMG buffing, but his Hexerei buffs enable a a Normal-Attack based On-Field DPS playstyle
. His unique and powerful Crowd Control from his Elemental Burst can contribute to fast wave clearing but it has a drawback; certain characters are unable to land hits when smaller enemies are lifted by his Burst, and the suction doesn't work on enemies classified as heavy.

Venti Buff Guide
Rotation Of Anemo DPS Venti
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Durin. Use his Elemental Skill twice to enter Confirmation of Purity mode. Deploy his Burst.
2
Switch to Faruzan. Use her Skill into Elemental Burst. If no Energy, use a few Charged Attacks.
3
Make use of Bennett's Skill (Tap) into Burst combo.
4
Switch to Venti. Use his Skill (Tap), then Elemental Burst, before using Normal Attacks to deal damage.
5
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

Venti is a decent fourth-slot option for a Lunar Reaction team composed of Lunar-charged and Lunar-Bloom due to how good he is into content that these two reactions naturally excel at. His Burst's suction power makes it easier to gather light enemies closer together, making combat a lot more efficient.
Rotation Of Electro-charged/Lunar-charged Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Lauma. Deploy her Skill (hold), then Burst.
└
If this is the first rotation, you can use her Skill (tap) since you wouldn't have enough Verdant Dew generated for the Hold just yet.
2
Switch to Columbina. Use her Skill followed by her Burst.
3
Swap to Ineffa. Use her Skill. Burst is optional and can be used every other rotation instead.
4
Use Venti's Skill then Burst to gather enemies.
5
On-Field Columbina. Do a combo of ( Normal Attack (x2) -> Charged Attack -> Dash) six times.

- Columbina enables the conversion of all related reactions to Lunar Reactions. If you want to keep this team a Lunar Reaction team, she is irreplaceable. Lauma
Aino
Nahida
Flins
- Lauma's slot in this team can be considered a flex slot. If you have Columbina and Ineffa, Lauma functions as an Off-Field Support and can be substituted with Nahida to maintain Lunar-Bloom.
- If you don't have Columbina and Lauma, you can favor Aino to maintain Ascendant Gleam, since keeping this Moonsign level is vital for most Lunar teams.
- If you don't have Ineffa but have Columbina, you'll ideally want Aino or maybe even Flins to, again, sustain the Ascendant Gleam benefits.
As a baseline, this team has a fair amount of flexibility involved when choosing alternatives, but is a lot stricter if you want to keep it a Lunar Reaction team due to how Ascendant Gleam works. Venti himself is also replaceable in this team, but the fact he can freely flex it into it without it being detrimental to DPS makes it a decently powered comp overall.

Venti's intended role on release is to be an Off-Field Anemo with both supportive and Sub-DPS capabilities. He can be flexed into any team that can accommodate an Anemo unit, as well as can make use of his Elemental Burst's ability to gather enemies quickly. The On-Field DPS needs to be able to reliably hit the enemies within his Burst in order to avoid it being a time-waster.
Rotation Of Generalist Support Venti Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina. Use her Skill, then Burst.
2
Switch to Bennett, then deploy Skill (Tap) into Burst.
3
Do Venti's Skill into Burst combo.
4
Switch into Klee, Use Normal Attack (x2), Skill, then Burst immediately. Spam Normal Attacks while Burst is active.
└ This assumes you've done Klee's Witch's Homework.
5
Repeat from 1 support abilites expire.
Klee allows the team to have the Hexerei: Secret Rite resonance active, allowing Venti access to his new Hexerei passives and abilities.

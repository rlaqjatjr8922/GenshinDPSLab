Charlotte Is Best Used As Healer & Cryo Applier
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Freeze Support Charlotte Team
★★★★★
- Charlotte's Cryo application with her Burst and Skill helps maintain the Frozen status on enemies that can be Frozen.
- Her simple abilities allow her to synergize with most, if not all, Freeze team variations as both a healer and buffer.
▼ Furina Support Charlotte Team
★★★☆☆
- Charlotte makes for a decent partner alongside Furina in various teams that can make use of her Cryo application.
Her Burst provides the necessary teamwide healing for Furina to generate Fanfare stacks, which is the source of her DMG% buff.
▼General Cryo Healer Charlotte Team
★★☆☆☆
- Charlotte's heals let her flex into comps that might lack a source of sustain.
- Most of these teams either don't focus on reactions that would be disrupted by Charlotte's Cryo application.
Charlotte typically fills the supportive role across most of her team archetypes despite having talents that aim to increase her personal damage.
The lack of complex mechanics in her abilities allows her to be a flexible Cryo healer, but her lack of any other meaningful buffs means that she's often not the first choice for these teams overall.

Skirk's premium Freeze team, with Furina and Escoffier, doesn't have a concrete best option for the fourth teammate. The differences between each 5-star option are relatively marginal, and you can instead flex other Cryo or Hydro characters into the team to fit the content you're trying to clear.
Charlotte is one of the viable choices for a Skirk comp, especially if you need more Cryo application than usual.

4

5
Use Skirk's Elemental Burst to buff her Normal Attacks. This will also absorb nearby Void Rifts.
6

A Eula Physical or Superconduct team (with Raiden especially) can slot in Charlotte without much fuss, filling the role of healer and additional Cryo particle generator for Eula, whose DPS is very Burst dependent.
Rotation Of Furina Support Charlotte (Physical) Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina. Use her Skill, dash to cancel the long animation, then Burst.
2
Switch to Raiden Shogun. Deploy her Skill.
3
Use Charlotte's Skill (tap) then Burst.
4
Swap to Eula. Perform her best damage combo.
└ Skill (tap) → Normal Attack (x4) → Skill (hold) →  Normal Attack (x4)
5
Switch to Raiden again. Use her Burst, then perform her best damage combo for this setup.
└ [ Normal Attack → Charged Attack ] (x2) → Normal Attack (x2)→ Charged Attack
6
Repeat from step 1.
The On-Field time is split between Eula and Raiden due to Raiden's ability to restore flat energy to her teammates, which further lowers Eula's energy requirements.

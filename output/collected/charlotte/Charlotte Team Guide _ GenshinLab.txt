Charlotte Team Overview
This Page provides the best Charlotte Team information. Charlotte will be DPS and Charlotte DPS build will be used in most of the situation.
You can also swap the position of Charlotte and Diona to make your own best team.
From SS tier to S tier, Charlotte best team can be formed in different ways including Freezing, Permafrost and Melt team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

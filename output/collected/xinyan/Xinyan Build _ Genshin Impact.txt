Xinyan Overview
This Page provides the Best Xinyan Build information.
Xinyan is Liyue’s sole rock ‘n’ roll musician. She rebels against ossified prejudices using her music and passionate singing.
Xinyan is one of the lowest use rate characters in Genshin Impact at the moment. Xinyan Sub DPS Build and Support Build are mostly used by travelers. Her Elemental Skill can provide a shield to protect the team.
Xinyan is useful for pairing up with Eula. Her 4th constellation can decrease opponents’ Physical DEF. ATK% is the most important stats for Xinyan, so both the Sub DPS Build and Support Build will also help travelers to stack the Energy recharge for Xinyan.
In Genshin Impact, Xinyan is a 4 Stars rarity Pyro character currently in  tier.

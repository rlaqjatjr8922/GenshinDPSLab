Xinyan is a 4 star Pyro Claymore character
in Genshin Impact. Released in the earliest versions of the game, she is considered one of the weakest characters in the game (C-tier), with a lackluster skill set and low DPS.
However, she can definitely still shine in the right team. In this article, Gurugamer.com is going to showcase the best Xinyan team comps to maximize her DPS and support capability.

Xinyan team build is very important if you want her to deal enough damage.
1. High-End Xinyan Team Comps
When build as a main DPS, Xinyan mostly deals Physical DMG. Therefore, having a combination of a Cryo and Electro Sub-DPS character in the party is a must. Their Superconduct triggers will lower enemies' Physical DMG Resistance and allow Xinyan to hit harder.
As Xinyan is a Pyro character, it is rather easy to bolster her damage by adding another Pyro character to the party. This would unlock Elemental Resonance and increase her ATK by 25%.

Eula is the main DPS of this team, dealing Physical damage with her fantastic burst. She can also trigger Melt with Xinyan when needed.

Bennett is the last piece of the puzzle, providing the team with Pyro resonance for even more damage. He can also heal and boost damage, which are the best abilities a support can have.

The objective of this team is to funnel all buffs to Ayaka while sustaining the Freeze status on enemies so the effect of Ayaka's Blizzard Strayer x4 could take effect consistently. Kazuha's Freedom-Sworn also increases Ayaka's normal & charged attacks when the effect has been triggered.
Variation Of Morgana Team
▼Morgana team
is a team comp that can trigger Freeze consistently which immobilizes the enemies. This team is widely known since it offers a safe yet effective combat environment.

The objective of this team is to let Kokomi be the main DPS. The team can effectively deal damage with the input of off-field DPS and the damage from Ocean-Hued Clam x4.
Kokomi With Healer Build Even As Main DPS
Kokomi's passive makes her not ideal for CRIT stats build. However, with Ocean-Hued Clam x4 set artifact, Kokomi can deal damage whenever she heals. The over-healing is also counted in the accumulated damage done by the bubble.

The effect of Kokomi's Burst disappears when she gets switched out so complete the setup first beforehand.

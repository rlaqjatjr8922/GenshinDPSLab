The best team compositions for Sangonomiya Kokomi in
Genshin Impact
revolve around various Elemental reactions. When she was first released, many people thought that Sangonomiya Kokomi was underwhelming when it came to damage dealing. Luckily, the developer understood this, and
Genshin Impact
fans were treated with an artifact set one version later.
This new
Ocean-Hued Clam artifact set
successfully dethrones Heart of Depths from being the best artifact set for offensive Kokomi. Now, Kokomi can brilliantly do different works, whether it's support, sub-DPS, or main DPS. Considering how the Divine Priestess can take multiple roles, her best teammates also adjust to the job players choose for their Kokomi. To elaborate, here are some examples of
Kokomi team comp in
Genshin Impact
.

: There are many viable
teams for Kokomi in Genshin Impact
. Players can use her as a main DPS, Hydro enabler, buffer, or simply a dedicated healer. Thanks to Furina being one of the best buffers in the game who demands a second healer in their team, Kokomi's viability becomes more apparent in the meta. Although Kokomi's Hydro application enables her to participate in reactions such as Vaporize, Superconduct, and Electro-charged, the current meta mostly revolves around her playing as a main DPS, as a Freeze support, or Hydro provider for Bloom teams.
4
Kokomi + Yelan + Furina + Kazuha
Kokomi is more than capable of playing as the main DPS. For that, this team will focus wholly on DPS Kokomi. This Kokomi team consists of three Hydro units and one
Anemo to trigger Hydro Swirl
and reduce the enemy's Hydro RES with Viridescent Venerer set. Naturally, the best Anemo support for this role is
Kazuha
, who can buff the entire team's Hydro DMG based on his Elemental Mastery.
Kokomi,
Yelan
, and
Furina
all scale with HP, meaning they can greatly
benefit from the Hydro Resonance
they create together. With Kokomi's constant Normal Attack and healing, she can synergize with both Yelan and Furina, maximizing Yelan's damage and Furina's buff at the same time.
Team Roles
Kokomi:
Main DPS, Healer
Yelan:
Sub-DPS, Buffer
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Healer, Buffer
Kazuha:
Sub-DPS, Debuffer, Buffer
3
Kokomi + Ayaka + Shenhe + Escoffier
For a Kokomi Perma-Freeze team, players will need a Cryo and Anemo character or more than two Cryo units along with Sangonomiya Kokomi.
In a Morgana team comp
, players can opt to make Kokomi a support/sub-DPS or the main DPS.
If the Divine Priestess is taking the main DPS role, then the Cryo unit should be a sub-DPS, and vice versa
.
In this example, Kokomi will play as the Hydro enabler, while
Ayaka
takes the spotlight as the main DPS. Supporting Ayaka are
Shenhe
and
Escoffier
, whose combined abilities can provide a massive DMG boost for Ayaka and RES reduction for the enemies.
Team Roles
Kokomi:
Sub-DPS, Healer, Hydro Enabler
Ayaka:
Main DPS
Shenhe:
Buffer, Debuffer
Escoffier:
Sub-DPS, Debuffer
2
Kokomi + Nilou + Nahida + Baizhu
Nilou
's Bloom Team is incredibly powerful but at the same time limited, making it an underdog among the
best team comps in
Genshin Impact
. Nilou Bloom team can only consist of Hydro and Dendro characters, meaning players must specifically invest in these two Elements to make her viable.

Kokomi Gameplay
Her gameplay centers around a highly reliable kit, which transforms her elegant combat style into a seamless blend of potent off-field Hydro application and absolute team survivability. Her kit primarily scales on her
Max HP
, which uniquely boosts both her damage output and her massive healing capabilities, and she enables highly flexible team compositions by providing constant elemental reactions and unparalleled defensive comfort.
Her
Elemental Skill
, Kurage's Oath, summons a water-based phantom known as a Bake-Kurage to heal allies and deal AoE Hydro DMG. When deployed, it remains stationary on the battlefield and pulses at regular intervals, actively restoring a massive amount of health to your current on-field character based on her Max HP, a mechanic heavily favored for keeping fragile DPS units alive; otherwise, it serves as her primary tool for continuous off-field Hydro application. This steady elemental presence ensures she can constantly enable powerful reactions like Bloom, Vaporize, or Permafreeze.
When she unleashes her
Elemental Burst
, Nereid's Ascension, she dons a Ceremonial Garment that instantly empowers her combat capabilities based on her Max HP, while dealing a burst of Hydro DMG to nearby enemies. It essentially turns her into a sturdy on-field driver, significantly boosting the damage of her Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and her deployed Bake-Kurage. The best part is that her attacks now actively heal all party members on hit, meaning she effectively brings the entire squad back to full health just by staying on the offensive, allowing your team to confidently stand their ground against the toughest enemies or domain modifiers.
Her
Passives
are all about doubling down on her unique scaling while ensuring her supportive utility is consistently available. One of them famously drops her CRIT Rate by 100% in exchange for a massive, permanent
25%
Healing Bonus, while another directly refreshes the duration of her active Bake-Kurage the moment her Burst is cast to ensure you never have a gap in your elemental application. It makes her super satisfying to play because you're essentially bringing an unkillable tank who can keep the squad perfectly safe with a few simple attacks while effortlessly managing 100% uptime on her jellyfish.

Kokomi Best Artifacts and Stats
1. Support / Off-Field Kokomi
When playing off-field, Kokomi's primary goal is to keep your team healthy with her jellyfish while simultaneously buffing your main damage dealer. Because her jellyfish has a wide area of effect, pulses damage every two seconds, and can be kept on the field almost indefinitely by using her Elemental Burst to refresh its duration, she is one of the most reliable characters in the game at triggering artifact sets that provide constant team-wide buffs. Instead of focusing on her personal damage, this build turns her into the ultimate utility unit. She ensures your active character can comfortably face-tank enemy attacks without needing to dodge, all while enjoying a massive attack boost to maximize their own damage output.

Sangonomiya Kokomi Skill Guide & How To Use Sangonomiya Kokomi
Recommended Rotation For Kokomi
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Elemental Skill and switch to other characters
2
Use other characters to deal reactions her Elemental Skill
3
If the Elemental Skill is down, use her Elemental Burst
4
Use her Elemental Burst and use charged attacks near her Jellyfish
5
Once the Burst is over, repeat step 1
Uses Of Kokomi's Elemental Skill
Consistent Infliction Of Hydro At A Certain Area
▲Triggers reaction per Tic making it great for any reaction comps.
Kokomi summons a jellyfish that lasts for 12 seconds. This
heals allies and damages enemies around it while dealing Hydro
. The jellyfish does not disappear even if you switch character which makes it an amazing skill for triggering Hydro Elemental reactions.
Uses Of Kokomi's Elemental Burst
Resets The Duration Of Elemental Skill
Kokomi's Elemental Burst mainly has two roles depending on the role of Kokomi: First, is to
refresh the duration of her Elemental Skill
. For support Kokomi, the main role of Elemental Burst is to reset the Elemental skill enabling it to have an almost 100% uptime in the field.
Increases The DMG Capability Of Kokomi
The second role is to increase Kokomi's personal damage. Elemental Burst will increase the damage of Kokomi's skills that scales from her Max HP. The effects of this, however, disappears after switching characters.
Heals Team In Tics
While you are in Elemental Burst, each tic will heal the team. This can also increase the damage because of the artifact,
Ocean-Hued Clam Set
. The Elemental Burst of Kokomi combined with Elemental Skill can easily reach the max 30,000 of the artifact set.
Should You Pull for Sangonomiya Kokomi's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Prioritize Sangonomiya Kokomi's C1
Signature VS Constellations
C1 > C4 or
Everlasting Moonglow
C1 Is a Decent Damage Increase
The extra swimming fish generated from Kokomi's C1 contributes a fair amount of damage and also acts as another source of Hydro, making her on-field state after using her Burst more rewarding.
Her C1 is arguably her most valuable constellation, with C4 and C6 being the next best, and is a good stopping point for most of Kokomi's use-cases.
Her Signature Weapon is typically not recommended, since the craftable
Prototype Amber
is almost as good. Kokomi's performance with her Signature Weapon is slightly better when she goes on-field more, but unless the other weapon on the banner is something you can realistically use,
getting Everlasting Moonglow (and the rest of her constellations) is considerably lower on the pull priority list.

Who Is Sangonomiya Kokomi?
Divine Priestess Of Inazuma's Watatsumi Island
Kokomi is young, but she carries a tremendous responsibility as the Divine Priestess of Inazuma's Watatsumi Island. She's basically the leader of her island community, always working for the better of her people.
A Master Strategist
Kokomi is a master strategist both in ruling and in battle. She led the rebellion in Watatsumi Island against the Raiden Shogun herself.

Sangonomiya Kokomi Guide and Best Builds
Sangonomiya Kokomi is a 5-star Hydro Healer in Genshin Impact who primarily
focuses on Healing the party while also having incredibly powerful Hydro
application. This guide will focus on Kokomi's best builds, artifacts, weapons
and best team compositions.

Sangonomiya Kokomi's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Incredibly powerful healing for both single-target and AoE healing.
Great Hydro application.
Decent damage output for a healer while being able to heal the party at the same time.
Weaknesses
Starts with -100% CRIT Rate.
High Energy Recharge requirements.
Uses multiple competitive builds all with different artifact sets which can be hard to manage if you wanted to have different Kokomi builds.
1.
Sangonomiya Kokomi Build Guide, Best Artifacts, Weapons and Talent Priority
Sangonomiya Kokomi has several effective builds that can be used. Kokomi's
main build would be as a healer however if you want to play Kokomi in
Bloom teams she has different priorities and artifacts to build.

6.
Combos and How to Play Kokomi
Kokomi's playstyle mainly involves her
Bake-Kurage
summoned
using
Kurage's Oath
to apply
Hydro
for reactions
and heal the party. This has incredibly powerful
Hydro
application
due to having no
internal cooldown
. The duration
of
Bake-Kurage
can also be extended when using
Nereid's Ascension
allowing
for constant uptime.
When playing as a DPS/On-field, it is more damage to do your Normal Attack,
The Shape of Water
twice (or 3 times at
C1) and then dash cancel the animation to be able to attack again.

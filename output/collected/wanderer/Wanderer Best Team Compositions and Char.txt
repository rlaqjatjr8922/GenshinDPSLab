Wanderer's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Wanderer team composition guide and character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we will explore the best teams to build for Wanderer, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and Team Compositions to help Wanderer 
reach his full damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Wanderer
Wanderer is a Main DPS that will mainly deal
Anemo DMG
and functions primarily as a Hypercarry, meaning he needs teammates who can buff him.
Due to his Ascension passive
Jade-Claimed Flower
he also wants one or two characters with another element. 
This allows him to get up to two buffs if his
Hanega: Song of the Wind
comes into contact with the other elements when it is used.
Faruzan
Faruzan
Faruzan is overall the best support you can use with Wanderer. Keep in mind that 
	in order to get the most out of Faruzan's buffs, it is best if she is C6. She can 
	provide a massive Anemo and CRIT DMG buff, and she also decreases Anemo RES. Under C6 
	you miss out on the 40% CRIT DMG bonus, but she is still worth using.
Bennett
Bennett
Bennett is a very good option as he gives a huge ATK Boost, provides healing that
	Wanderer can often need since he can easily get knocked down when in the air, and he also
	allows Wanderer to get his Pyro Buff. The only thing you need to pay attention to
	is to make sure to not fly outside of Bennett's circle when you are in the air with
	Wanderer.
Furina
Furina
Furina is one of the best supports and off-field DPS for Wanderer, providing Hydro, 
	heavy DMG, and DMG bonuses to Wanderer. However, she does eat your health away, so
	you might want to make sure you have a good healer in your team as well, especially since 
	Wanderer is not the most tanky character. If you do not have Furina, other alternatives are
Yelan
or
Xingqiu
.
Venti
Kaedehara Kazuha
Grouping Options
Both Venti and Kazuha are able to group up enemies, making it easier for
	Wanderer to hit multiple enemies at once. Wanderer benefits from Venti
	as this allows him to have a massive burst window to attack while Venti packs
	and controls enemies in his Vortex. Kazuha on the other hand provides some buffs on top
	of the crowd control as well.
Mavuika
Xiangling
Thoma
Double
Pyro
Options
Double Pyro teams are good compositions for Wanderer since it makes use of
	Pyro Resonance to increase Wanderer's damage even further with
Xiangling
or
Mavuika
helping to provide additional damage, or
Thoma
for shields.
Zhongli
Shielders and Healers
As mentioned, Wanderer is not exactly tanky and he tends to be easy to knock down
	when he is in the air, which can quickly become a problem, especially against some Bosses
	or multiple targets. To fix this, you can play Wanderer with a shielder, and 
	while Zhongli is your best option, there are many alternatives that 
	provide either shields or healing:
Layla
Citlali
Lan Yan
Diona
Dahlia
Mika
Jean
Charlotte

Rosaria Guide Genshin Impact : Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
November 14, 2023
Tips
Share:
Rosaria is a
4-star Cryo heroine
. Versatile, she can occupy several roles depending on her build: physical DPS, Cryo DPS or support. She can also increase the critical rate of your characters. We'll help you exploit her potential with this
complete guide to Rosaria from Genshin Impact.
Thanks to our tips and advice, you'll know which
equipment
to choose: weapons and artifacts, but also in which teams to play her.
How to get Rosaria in Genshin Impact ?
As Rosaria is a 4-star character, you have a small chance of summoning her every time you make wishes in the Permanent Banner and Character Event Wish.
Rosaria's banner
The chances of
unlocking Rosaria
in Genshin Impact are increased when it is featured in a
Character Event Wish
Ayaka's banner
:
Heron Prestance
. This is available from 19 April to 10 May 2022.
The constellations of Rosaria
Unlocking the constellations of Rosaria will improve the character.
His C1
Unholy Revelation
helps to play Rosaria as a DPS. When Rosaria's attacks deal a critical hit, his ATK speed and normal attack damage are increased by 10% for 4 seconds.
Her other constellations make her a very good support. Her
Land Without Promise
C2 extends the duration of her elemental rampage by 4 seconds, allowing the Cryo element to be applied for longer.
Finally, his C6
Divine Retribution
reduces the physical resistance of enemies by 20% for 10 seconds when you cast his elemental rampage.
How to play Rosaria in Genshin Impact ?
Rosaria is a
versatile character
in Genshin Impact. You can play her as a physical DPS, Cryo DPS or support (especially if you have unlocked her second constellation). For a DPS role, the trick is to increase all her skills and especially her
normal attack
. Rosariabenefits from
high damage multipliers
for her normal attacks and has the advantage of attacking quickly.
If you prefer to use it as a medium, you can focus on its elemental unleashing.
In order for Rosaria to deal more damage, start by activating its
Ravaging Confession
elemental skill. This is a dash where Rosaria deals Cryo damage and teleports behind enemies. Through the passive
Regina Probationum
, Rosaria then has its critical rate increased by 12% for 5 seconds. However, this ability will not work on enemies that are too large.
Then, as soon as possible, cast his Elemental Unleashing
Rites of Termination
. Rosaria plants a spear in the ground, dealing area Cryo damage for 8 seconds. The
Shadow Samaritan
passive then grants all characters (except Rosaria) for 10 seconds a critical rate bonus of 15% of Rosaria's critical rate (this takes into account her elemental attack buff). This will allow her to boost your team and apply the Cryo element if you play her as a support.
What is the best equipment for Rosaria?
The equipment of Rosaria in Genshin Impact will depend on the role you wish to assign to it. A little tip for a
DPS
build, increase his attack, critical rate and critical damage, but also his physical or Cryo damage depending on the type of DPS you choose. And for a build of
support
, the trick is to increase as much as possible the critical rate of Rosaria to boost that of the members of your team as well as its recharge of energy in order to benefit regularly from its elementary unleashing.
The best artifacts for Rosaria
For a
physical DPS build
, several sets of artifacts are possible:
Gladiator's Finale
Bloodstained Chivalry
Pale flame
Shimenawa's Reminiscence
4 pieces of the
Gladiator's Finale
set: in addition to increasing your ATK by 18%, damage from normal attacks will be increased by 35%;
2 pieces of
Bloodstained Chivalry
and 2 pieces of
Pale Flame
equipment for a total bonus of 50% physical damage;
2 pieces of
Bloodstained Chivalry
or
Pale Flame
sets + 2 pieces of
Gladiator's Finale
or
Shimenawa's Reminiscence
to increase physical damage (25%) and ATK (18%).
Blizzard Strayer
Do you prefer a
Cryo DPS build
? Then the best artifact choice for Rosaria in Genshin Impact is the 4-piece
Blizzard Strayer
set. With 2 pieces of this set, you get a 15% Cryo damage bonus and with 4, you increase the critical rate by 20% when attacking an enemy affected by the Cryo element. You will get an additional 20% if the enemy is frozen.
Noblesse Oblige
To
play Rosaria as a support
, we recommend the 4-piece set from the
Noblesse Oblige
set. It will boost the elemental rampage and the ATK of all your characters after casting the ability.
The best weapons for Rosaria
For a
DPS build
, here are our tips for weapons:
Staff of Homa (5★)
Primordial Jade Winged-Spear (5★)
Deathmatch (4★)
Staff of Homa
: This is the best weapon to equip on Rosaria, regardless of its role in your team. You will increase its critical damage, its HP and its ATK;
Primordial Jade Winged-Spear
: The spear confers a significant gain in critical rate and ATK;
Deathmatch
: The weapon increases critical rate, attack and defence.
Crescent Pike (4★)
Lance Dosdragon (4★)
For a
physical DPS build
, you can make the
Crescent Pike
(one of the best weapons for Rosaria when refined) or the
Dragonspine Spear
from the blacksmith.
Finally, for a
support build
, focus on weapons that offer critical rate such as the
Deathmatch
or the energy recharge:
Favonius Lance (4★)
Prototype Starglitter (4★)
Favonius Lance
: The hast weapon provides energy recharge and critical hits can generate energy particles;
Prototype Starglitter
: You will also increase your energy recharge and attack damage after using an elemental skill.
Climbing materials from Rosaria in Genshin Impact
To
get Rosaria to level 90 in Genshin Impact
and increase its attack to the maximum, several resources will be needed:
18
Recruit's Insignia
, 30
Sergeant's Insignia
and 36
Lieutenant's Insignia
to be collected from the Fatui;
168
Valberry
to collect in the north of Mondsdadt. Locate them more easily with the
interactive map of Teyvat
;
1 Shard, 9 Fragments, 9 Pieces and 6
Shivada Jade
Stones as well as 46
Hoarfrost Core
to loot from the
Cryo Regisvine
.
In which team should you play Rosaria?
Rosaria's versatility allows her to play in many teams. If you want to use her as a
physical DPS
, our advice is to play her with an Electro character to produce
Superconduct
and reduce the physical resistance of your enemies. For example, you can pair her with
Fischl
to easily apply Electro, and then with two Pyro characters such as
Bennett
and
Xiangling
.
In a
Cryo team
, Rosaria will take advantage of
elemental resonance
and will have her critical rate increased when attacking enemies affected by this element. For maximum Cryo attacks, play her with
Chongyun
,
Xingqiu
to freeze enemies with Hydro and
Zhongli
or
Diona
to place a shield.
You can also play it as Cryo support in teams such as
Eula
/
Raiden
/
Rosaria
/
Zhongli
. But also with
Ayaka
in a team designed to freeze enemies:
Ayaka
/
Rosaria
/
Kokomi
or
Mona
/
Kazuha
,
Venti
or
Sucrose
.
What is the birthday and size of Rosaria of Genshin Impact ?
Rosaria is a sister of the Church of Favonius, based in Mondstadt. She is reportedly
1.76m
tall and
24 years
old.
That's it for this
guide on Rosaria from Genshin Impact.
All you have to do is follow our tips and tricks to shine with this character. The heroine is really fun to play, especially since she can take on several roles depending on the build you give her. Although powerful as a main DPS, she will struggle to compete with 5-star characters. She will be more relevant in the abyss in a support role to apply the Cryo element and increase the critical rate of your characters.

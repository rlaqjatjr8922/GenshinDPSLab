Rosaria is one of the most efficient physical damage dealers in Genshin Impact. Check this article to find out more about how to build a team around her.
Rosaria is a 4 star Cryo Polearm character
in Genshin Impact. Her kit is fairly versatile - Rosaria can fill physical carry, Cryo carry, and several supporting roles. While her damage output might not be as high as a 5-star character, she can definitely deal a comparable amount of damage with the same level of investment.
In this article, Gurugamer is going to showcase the best Rosaria team comps to maximize her DPS potential.

Rosaria has a great character design, with beautiful model and outfit.
1. High-End Rosaria Team Comps
Rosaria's biggest strength is the ability to provide Crit % to herself and her team. With ATK% as the hidden stat, she can serve multiple roles from Physical to Cryo DPS. Rosaria is one of the Cryo characters that can deal huge damage while spreading Cryo off-field.
Being a 4 star character with a fairly straightforward kit, Rosaria is extremely easy to gear and play. Investing in her constellation is a good idea, as they are extremely useful. Her weaknesses are not that severe - the only problem with her kit is that her Elemental Skill cannot backstab shields.
Being 4 star, Rosaria is a very underrated damage dealer.

Eula's Elemental Burst is the core of this team. Her Physical Burst damage is maximized under the effect of Superconduct, DMG Bonus from Raiden Shogun’s Elemental Skill and Set Bonus of Tenacity of the Millelith from Zhongli.
Rosaria provides Eula with an extra Crit rate boost and Cryo Resonance, which also increases Crit rate. Raiden Shogun can be replaced with Fischl for constant Superconduct application.
Raiden single-handedly brings the Electro element back to the top.

Rosaria is the main DPS in this team comp, dealing damage and spread cryo using her skill and burst. Fischl provides additional damage while spreading Electro, applying constant superconduct debuff to enemies.
Diona provides shields, heals and elemental resonance to Rosaria, increasing her Crit rate. The last slot, Albedo, is just some extra Geo damage off-field. Players can replace him with something like Bennett for the ATK boost.
Fischl's Oz is the best tool if you want to keep superconduct up constantly.

Rosaria Gameplay
Rosaria’s gameplay revolves around applying reliable
Cryo
from off-field to support
reaction-based teams
. Her kit focuses on maintaining supportive utility while enabling consistent
elemental reactions
through her
Elemental Burst
. While she is not designed to be a primary damage dealer, her ability to apply
Cryo
and trigger reactions makes her a valuable
support
option in many team compositions.
Her
Elemental Skill
,
Ravaging Confession
, allows Rosaria to strike enemies with a swift Cryo attack, dealing
Cryo damage
and generating energy for her
Elemental Burst
. This skill also enhances her off-field
Cryo application
, helping her trigger reactions like
Melt
and
Superconduct
consistently during combat.
When Rosaria uses her
Elemental Burst
,
Rites of Termination
, she summons a spectral spear that continuously deals
Cryo damage
to enemies whenever the active character performs Normal Attacks. These attacks allow Rosaria to apply
Cryo
from off-field effectively, enabling frequent
elemental reactions
while contributing supportive utility to the team.
Rosaria’s
passive talents
further enhance her support role by improving her off-field
Cryo application
and energy regeneration. With proper
energy management
and
ATK investment
, she can provide consistent support while enabling powerful
elemental reactions
for the rest of the team.

Burst
summons a spectral spear that triggers
Cryo damage

Elemental Skill – Ravaging Confession
Rosaria’s
Elemental Skill
deals
Cryo damage
to nearby enemies and generates energy for her
Elemental Burst
. While the skill alone does not deal massive damage, it plays an important role in applying
Cryo consistently
and supporting
elemental reactions

Normal Attack – Spear of the Church
Rosaria’s
Normal Attacks
are rarely used in her typical support role, as she generally stays off-field after activating her abilities. Because of this, leveling her Normal Attack talent provides minimal value and should be considered the lowest priority.

Rosaria Works Best As a Cryo Sub-DPS for Reactions
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Melt Team
- Rosaria works best in a Melt team where a Pyro DPS is used.
- A slow Pyro DPS like Mavuika is preferred, but a fast Pyro DPS like Arlecchino or Hu Tao can also be used.
- This can be turned into a Vapemelt team if Rosaria's application is too slow for the Pyro DPS.
2nd
▼ Freeze Team
- 2nd best team for Rosaria is a Freeze team where her application can help trigger Freeze.
- Allows her to easily gain Energy since another Cryo character is used.
3rd
▼ Physical Team
- Rosaria's Cryo application is enough for triggering Superconduct with an Electro character.
- If Rosaria is C6, she can also reduce Physical Resistance of the enemies.
Rosaria's CRIT Rate buff and ability to apply Cryo off-field make her a decent Sub-DPS/Support hybrid for Cryo teams. With Cryo Resonance, CRIT Rate requirements are much lower.

Rotation Of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mavuika and use her Skill to apply Pyro
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then attack enemies twice with her Normal Attacks
3
Switch to Rosaria and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Iansan and use her Skill then hit enemies once with her Normal Attack
5
Use Iansan's Burst before switching her out
6
Switch back to Mavuika and use her Burst
7
Deal damage with Charged Attack & Dash combo
Xilonen's Burst can be used after activating her Source Samples if you need healing as well.

No.

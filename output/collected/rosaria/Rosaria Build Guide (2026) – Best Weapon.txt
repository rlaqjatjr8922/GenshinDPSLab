Best Rosaria Build in Genshin Impact

Rosaria stands out because she can function in two main roles:
Crit support
Cryo sub-DPS
Her Elemental Burst deals consistent Cryo damage while also increasing the Crit Rate of your team based on her own Crit Rate. This makes her one of the few characters that directly boosts team-wide damage through stat sharing.
A strong Rosaria build focuses on:
high Crit Rate
Cryo damage bonus
Energy Recharge for Burst uptime
Best Weapons for Rosaria
Rosaria benefits most from polearms that increase Crit stats or Energy Recharge.
Top Tier Weapons
Staff of Homa
While typically used on DPS characters, this weapon provides strong Crit Damage and overall scaling, making it a powerful option for Rosaria in sub-DPS roles.
Deathmatch
One of the best options for Rosaria due to its Crit Rate bonus. This directly increases both her own damage and the Crit Rate she shares with the team.
Favonius Lance
An excellent support option that provides Energy Recharge and generates additional particles, helping maintain consistent Burst uptime for both Rosaria and her team.

How to Use Rosaria Effectively
Rosaria’s gameplay is fast and flexible.
Her strengths come from:
quick skill cooldowns
consistent Burst uptime
Crit Rate sharing
You should:
use her Elemental Skill to reposition and generate energy
activate her Burst regularly to maintain Cryo application
build enough Crit Rate to maximise her team buff
Timing her Burst correctly ensures your team benefits from her Crit Rate boost during key damage windows.

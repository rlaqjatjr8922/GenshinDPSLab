Kaeya's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Easy to play and understand.
Requires low field-time and has a good off-field Cryo application.
Low Energy Requirements.
Can help you cross large bodies of water in the open-world.
Is a free character given rather early in the game.
Weaknesses
Kaeya's Burst and Skill both are short range, forcing you to play in melee range.
While being a good Cryo battery, other similar characters have more utility.
Constellations are among the hardest to get.

4.
Kaeya Combos and How to Play
Kaeya is a rather straightforward character. The primary goal is to generate particles with 
his skill that has a very low cooldown, and can be used often, while also inflicting
Cryo
DMG. His Burst will be important in both Freeze and 
Reverse Melt teams, as it stays around the active character for a short while, allowing 
you to either trigger Melt often, or maintain enemies frozen all the time.
Kaey's rotation in both cases is rather simple: you want to use his skill when it's up, and 
Burst every rotation to always have the burst active around the on-field character.

Kaeya Skill Guide & How To Use Kaeya
Recommended Rotation For Kaeya
Recommended Rotation As Support
1
Start with Elemental Skill and spam it until his Burst is up
2
Unleash Burst then switch to another character to combo Elemental Reactions
Uses Of Kaeya's Elemental Skill
Quick Cryo Application
Kaeya's Elemental Skill is quick to match its very short cooldown. It can easily apply Cryo to enemies and with its short duration, it can be done in short successions.
Fast Way To Gather Energy
Because of the short cooldown, Kaeya's Elemental Skill can easily be spammed to gather energy for his Burst. It's a pretty reliable way of ensuring he always has enough energy for his burst.
Uses Of Kaeya's Elemental Burst
Continuous Cryo Application
Kaeya's Elemental Burst will apply Cryo to enemies hit by his circling icicles. So long as it's active, all enemies hit by it will receive Cryo damage.
Set-Up For Elemental Reactions
As an on-field Burst, it's easy to pair Kaeya's Burst with another character to continuously deal Elemental Reactions. Just ensure you're near enough the enemies to apply Cryo to them with Kaeya's Burst.
Should You Pull for Kaeya's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Constellations Are Ideal, But Hard to Acquire
Constellation Priority
C6 > C5 > C2
C6 Is His Best Constellation
Kaeya's constellations up to C5 aren't very significant in isolation, and more so stepping stones to his best and last constellation.
His C6 provides him with some substantial buffs overall.
His damage is significantly boosted by gaining an additional icicle on his Burst while also refunding Energy upon cast, which help him deviate from Energy Recharge for more offensive stats on his artifacts.
In addition, C5 adds more talent levels to his Burst, which make
the combination of C5 and C6 very synergistic.
Similarly, while C2 typically isn't that useful, it can be strong against multiple enemies as the duration of his Burst increases with each enemy taken down,
which also benefits from his C6.
It is worth mentioning that while his constellations make him a lot more capable as a Cryo DPS, Kaeya is one among the three 4-stars characters who are given for free during the first few chapters of the Monstadt Archon Quest and have also not been featured in any rate-up banner since the game's release.
It isn't realistic to get him to any constellation level apart from the off-chance you get him from a Standard Banner Pull or if you diligently save enough Starglitter to get Kaeya every time he appears in
Paimon's Bargain
, which totals to twice a year.
It will be significantly easier to invest into a better sword or into his premium supports options, but if you are dedicated enough, getting Kaeya's constellations will only be a matter of time.

Who Is Kaeya?
Aide To The Acting Grand Master
Kaeya is a Cavalry Captain in the Knights of Favonius. Although unofficial titled, he also serves as the most trusted aid to the current acting Grand Master, Jean.
Adopted Son Of The Ragnvindr Family
Kaeya was left as a child near the Dawn Winery and was subsequently adopted by the Ragnvindr family. He grew up as brothers with Diluc, but the two has since had a falling out. They are civil now, but their relationship remains strained.
A Descendent From Khaenri'ah
Kaeya has the unique set of eyes only found in descendents of Khaenri'ah. His origins and mission in Mondstadt were well-kept secrets that he has only revealed once, costing him his relationship with Diluc.

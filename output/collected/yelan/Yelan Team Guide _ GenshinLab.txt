Yelan Team Overview
This Page provides the best Yelan Team information. Yelan will be Support DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Yelan and Xingqiu to make your own best team.
Yelan best team can be formed in different ways including Permafrost, Vaporize or Electro-Charged team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

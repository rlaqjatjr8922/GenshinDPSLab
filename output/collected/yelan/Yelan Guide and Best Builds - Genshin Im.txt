Yelan's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Powerful Hydro application and off-field damage due to their Elemental Burst.
Has good Synergy with other Hydro characters such as Furina and Xingqiu.
Mobility skill is great for exploration.
Weaknesses
Energy hungry often wanting a lot of Energy Recharge which can be fixed with her C1.
Has no defensive functionality unlike Xingqiu.

Combos and How to Play Yelan
While off-field for 5 seconds, Yelan enters
Breakthrough
dealing a powerful
Charged Attack
. It will however apply the same
amount of
Hydro
as any Charged Attack.
You can also reset
Breakthrough
when using
Lingering Lifeline
's
hold functionality. This will be indicated by the bracelet on Yelan's right arm
glowing blue.
Lingering Lifeline
is also a great skill to group enemies in
combat. You will generally use Lingering Lifeline before swapping to your
Elemental Burst,
Depth-Clarion Dice
. From there, simply swap
to other supports or your Main DPS to deal high damage while
the Dice provides
Hydro
application.

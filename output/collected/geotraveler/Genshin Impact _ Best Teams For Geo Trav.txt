Best Teams for Geo Traveler
Geo Traveler Works Best In Geo Teams
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Microwave Team
- Geo Traveler's ability to create multiple constructs at once synergizes with Zhongli's Steele resonance.
- Zhongli is primarily used for his Steele's resonance that allows Geo constructs to deal AoE Geo DMG.
- Kachina and Bennett will be buffing the team's damage.
2nd
▼ Double Geo Team
- A variant of the Microwave team but utilizes the Geo and Pyro element resonance to deal powerful damage.
- Arlecchino is the on-field DPS with Zhongli and Geo Traveler as sub-DPS.
Geo Traveler is able to create multiple Geo constructs from their abilities. Zhongli and Chiori are characters that can make use of other teammates' Geo constructs and are great when paired up with the Geo Traveler.

The Geo crystals from Geo Traveler's Burst don't count towards the construct limit but can resonate with Zhongli's Steele. This allows for AoE Geo DMG spam and fast shieldbreaking.
Rotation Of Microwave Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Kachina and use her Burst
3
Switch to Geo Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Zhongli and use his Skill then Burst
5
Refresh anyone's Geo construct duration until Bennett's Burst expires
6
Repeat from 1
There is no strict rotation needed in this team comp. You only have to make sure Kachina triggers Crystallize for the Scroll set buff.

Zhongli
Chiori
- Chiori is a good Geo sub-DPS that can make use of an ally's Geo construct.
- Do note that, without Zhongli's Steele resonance, the Microwave team's damage is considerably lessened.

Albedo
- Albedo is a good Geo sub-DPS that can be used.
- However he does not have direct supportive abilities and will be relegated to a sub--DPS role instead.

Kujou Sara
- Mavuika can buff the active character's DMG and can be main DPS if using Kachina.
- Gorou can buff Geo damage dealers.
- Sara can buff ATK but needs Constellations to be easier to use.

Rotation Of Double Geo Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino and use her Skill to mark enemies
2
Switch to Geo Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Zhongli and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
5
Switch back to Arlecchino and use her Charged Attack to gain Pyro infusion
6
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage
7
Refresh Arlecchino's Pyro infusion with her Skill or Burst before buffs expire
8
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

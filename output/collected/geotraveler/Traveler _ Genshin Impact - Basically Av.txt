The Traveler is going to be the
first character that players have access to for free
when playing Genshin Impact and will be the character who appears in all of the cutscenes that take place, even if they are not in the current active party.
Unlike all other characters, players will have the option of selecting whether or not their Traveler is male or female, though this has no effect on the character’s abilities, the reality is that choosing the sex of the character will simply be for the comfort of the player and dependant on which character the player prefers.
While all players have access to the Traveler, the reality is that players will want to switch out the Traveler as soon as they have a character that has either Geo or Anemo that is better.
The Traveler is unique in the sense that players will be able to rotate between the Geo element or Anemo element depending on which
Statue of The Seven
they visit. Due to the ability to rotate between elements, the Traveler sees quite a bit of use early on, especially when players are lacking in having certain elements.
As the Traveler can change between two different elements, players will have access to two different sets of abilities. Well, three sets if you consider the unaligned element as a move set, which only provides the Traveler with a basic attack and none of their abilities, though this is only for a short while.
ANEMO TALENTS
When the Traveler is aligned to Anemo the following will be your talents.
BASIC ATTACK
The Traveler has a few different variations to its basic attacks.
If players simply click on their auto-attack they will be able to attack up 5 times rapidly. This is called the “
Normal Attack
.”
If players hold down their auto-attack they will consume some Stamina and will unleash 2 rapid sword strikes at enemies. This is called the “
Charged Attack
.”
If players are mid-air they can use their auto-attack to strike from mid-air to the ground below, damagining all enemies in an AoE fashion.
ABILITY 1
The Traveler has the ability
Palm Vortex
as it’s first ability, which will deal continuous Anemo Damage to all opponents in front of them.
Upon the attack being completed it will explode dealing more Anemo Damage in an AoE fashion.
If players hold down their Palm Vortex it will deal more damage and will have a bigger AoE.
Players will be able to cause Elemental Absorption when the Vortex comes into contact with Hydro, Pyro, Cyro or Electro elements.
Elemental Absorption will add the element that Palm Vortex comes into contact with and can only be used once per use.
ABILITY 2
The Traveler has the ability
Gust Surge
as its second ability which when casted will cause a wind current to come forward and push objects towards it dealing Anemo Damage every 0.5 seconds up to 8 times.
If the Gust Surge comes into contact with Hydro, Pyro, Cryo or Electro it will deal 30.7% additional elemental damage of that type and can only be triggered once per use.
1ST ASCENSION PASSIVE
After the Traveler is upgraded to the 1st Ascension, players will gain their first passive which will cause the Normal Attack’s final hit to become a wind blade, dealing 60% of the attack as Anemo Damage to all enemies that are in it’s path.
4TH ASCENSION PASSIVE
After the Traveler is upgraded to the 4th Ascension, Palm Vortex will now regenerate 2% of the Traveler’s HP for 5 seconds when it kills an enemy. Players will only be able to activate this passive every 5 seconds.
GEO TALENTS
BASIC ATTACK
The Traveler has a few different variations to its basic attacks.
If players simply click on their auto-attack they will be able to attack up 5 times rapidly. This is called the “
Normal Attack
.”
If players hold down their auto-attack they will consume some Stamina and will unleash 2 rapid sword strikes at enemies. This is called the “
Charged Attack
.”
If players are mid-air they can use their auto-attack to strike from mid-air to the ground below, damagining all enemies in an AoE fashion.
ABILITY 1
The Traveler has the ability
Starfell Sword
as it’s first ability, which will cause a meteorite to appear from the depths of the earth dealing AoE Geo damage.
Players will be able to climb these meteorites or use them to block attacks as they are considered Geo Constructs.
If players hold down
Starfell Sword
they will be able to move the skill’s positioning.
ABILITY 2
The Traveler has the ability
Wake of the Earth
as its second ability which when casted will set off expanding shockwaves.
Shockwaves launch surrounding enemies back and will deal AoE Geo damage, making a stone wall at the edges of the shockwave.
Similar to the ability above, the walls will be considered a Geo Construct, allowing players to both climb them and block attacks with them.
1ST ASCENSION PASSIVE
After the Traveler is upgraded to the 1st Ascension, players will gain their first passive which will cause the Starfell Sword’s Cooldown to reduce by 2 seconds.
4TH ASCENSION PASSIVE
After the Traveler is upgraded to the 4th Ascension the final hit of the Traveler’s Normal attack combo will trigger a collapse and deal 60% extra attack as AoE Geo damage.

ANEMO CONSTELLATION
As players are trying to ascend their characters players will also want to unlock pieces of their Constellation which will add more passives or upgrades to their talents.
Constellations for the Anemo Traveler must be levelled up separately from the Geo ones and can be upgraded using the
Memory of Roving Gales
.
Players will be able to gather a maximum of 6 Memories of Roving Gales in the following ways:
Completing the quest
For a Tomorrow Without Tears
Completing the quest
Song of Dragon and Freedom
Purchasing a limit of 1 from the Souvenir Shop
Upon reaching Adventure Rank 27
Upon reaching Adventure Rank 37
Upon reaching Adventure Rank 46
The following are the Constellations players can acquire for their Anemo Traveler:
LEVEL 1 – RANGING VORTEX
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the first Anemo Constellation players will unlock
Ranging Vortex
which allows Palm Vortex to pull in enemies in a 5m radius.
LEVEL 2 – UPRISING WHIRLWIND
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the second Anemo Constellation players will unlock
Uprising Whirlwind
which provides the Traveler with 16% Energy Recharge.
LEVEL 3 – SWEEPING GUST
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the third Anemo Constellation players will unlock
Sweeping Gust
which will increase the level of Gust Surge by 3.
It should be noted that the maximum a player can upgrade Gust Surge is to level 15.
LEVEL 4 – CHERISHING BREEZES
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the fourth Anemo Constellation players will unlock
Cherishing Breezes
which reduces the damage taken when players are casting Palm Vortex.
Players will receive 10% less damage.
LEVEL 5 – VORTEX STELLARIS
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the fifth Anemo Constellation players will unlock
Vortex Stellaris
which increases the level of Palm Vortex by 3.
Similar to Sweeping Gust, it should be noted that the maximum level of Sweeping Gust is level 15.
LEVEL 6 – INTERTWINED WINDS
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the sixth Anemo Constellation players will unlock
Intertwined Winds
which targets which enemy takes damage from Gust Surge and has their Anemo Resistance reduced by 20%.
If players have Elemental Absorption, their resistance towards the corresponding Element is also decreased by 20%.

Constellations for the GeoTraveler must be levelled up separately from the Anemo ones and can be upgraded using the

The following are the Constellations players can acquire for their Geo Traveler:
LEVEL 1 – INVINCIBLE STONEWALL
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the first Geo Constellation players will unlock
Invincible Stonewall
which allows party members within the radius of Wake of Earth will have their Crit Rate increased by 10% and will have an increased resistance against interruption.
LEVEL 2 – ROCKCORE MELTDOWN
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the second Geo Constellation players will unlock
Rockcore Meltdown
.
Whenever the meteorite from Starfell Sword explodes it will deal additional AoE Geo damage equal to the amount of damage dealt by the Starfell Sword.
LEVEL 3 – WILL OF THE ROCK
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the third Geo Constellation players will unlock
Will of the Rock
which will increase the level of Wake of Earth by 3.
It should be noted that the maximum a player can upgrade Wake of Earth is to level 15.
LEVEL 4 – REACTION FORCE
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the fourth Geo Constellation players will unlock
Reaction Force
which will regenerate 5 Energy for every enemy hit up to a maximum of 25 Energy.
LEVEL 5 – METEORITE IMPACT
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the fifth Geo Constellation players will unlock
Meteorite Impact
which increases the level of Starfell Sword by 3.
Similar to Wake of Earth, it should be noted that the maximum level of Starfell Sword is level 15.
LEVEL 6 – EVERLASTING BOULDER
Upon upgrading your Traveler with the sixth Geo Constellation players will unlock
Everlasting Boulder
which causes the barrier created by Wake of earth to last 5 seconds longer.
The meteorite created by Starfell Sword will also last 10 seconds longer.

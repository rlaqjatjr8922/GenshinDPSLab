Welcome to our Genshin Impact Emilie Guide. Emilie’s build is one of the most essential components of her overall development and impact on the game. The build should be ideal for various team compositions and what Artifacts and Weapons should be associated with it to maximize its potential must be known by players. Keeping that in mind, we have prepared this guide. In this guide, we will discuss the Artifacts, Weapons, Team Composition and all such things related to Emilie. Let’s get started then.

Best Weapons for Emilie in Genshin Impact
Emilie’s weapons (Polearm) are mostly CRIT. She is a DPS character and has both CRIT and ATK. Here are all the weapons suited for her. To get the best build for Emilie, finding a suitable weapon is a must. All the Genshin Impact weapons mentioned here are 5-star weapons. If you don’t possess any of them, you can try finding one with 4-star ratings.
Lumidouce Elegy
– This is the main weapon or signature weapon of Emilie. Lumidouce Elegy offers several benefits and has good stats. At 90th level, it has a base ATK as 608 and base CRIT Rate of 33% at the same level. This is not all. After a user has used Burning on any target, the base ATK is boosted by 15%. This will also happen, if the user deals Dendro Damage to the opponents on which Burning is used. In this case the damage is increased by 18%. This effect can be stacked 2 times and remains active for 8 seconds.
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
– Another excellent five-star option is Cyno’s Staff of the Scarlet Sands. This weapon has a modest base ATK stat of 542 at level 90, complemented by an impressive 44.1% CRIT Rate sub-stat. The character wielding this staff gains 52% of their Elemental Mastery as bonus ATK. Additionally, when their Elemental Skill hits enemies, it activates the Dream of the Scarlet Sands effect for 10 seconds, providing 28% of their Elemental Mastery as extra ATK. Although this setup demands some investment in Elemental Mastery, it aligns well with Emilie’s focus on the Burning reaction, making it a great choice for her in Genshin Impact.
Calamity Queller
– Lumidouce Elegy is a top weapon. Calamity Queller takes the second spot for best 5-star weapon for Emilie. The base ATK stat and ATK sub-stats for Calamity Queller at Level 90 are 741 and 16.5%. Base ATK is good, sub-stat is decent but not bad. Additionally, there is an extra Elemental Damage of 12% provided by this weapon. After using their Skill, the character receives the Consummation effect for 20 seconds, which boosts their ATK by 3.2% every second, up to a maximum of six stacks. The ATK boost from Consummation is doubled when the character is not on the battlefield, making this ability highly beneficial for Emilie, who primarily functions off-field in Genshin Impact.
We know getting the 5-star weapons is extremely hard. But do not worry; you can
top up Genshin Impact Genesis Crystal
to get 5-star weapons easily. You can also go for 4-star weapons. There are some excellent 4-star weapons for Emilie such as Missive Windspear and Deathmatch. Deathmatch boosts ATK. But Missive Windspear increases both Elemental Mastery and ATK. Always try to find a weapon which has high stats for offense. These kinds of weapons generally suit her in Genshin Impact.
Best Artifacts & Stats for Emilie in Genshin Impact
After weapons comes the Artifacts followed by stats. Stats and Artifacts are important for Emilie’s builds too. Our advice for Artifact would be Unfinished Reverie. It is by far the best suited Artifact for Emilie’s build. Any user that has it receives an 18% increase in ATK with 2 pieces. If you manage to get 4 pieces of this Artifact, the damage you dealt would be increased by 50% in case of no combat for 3 seconds.
While in Battle, if there are enemies (Burning) around you, your damage is increased by 10% per second up to 50%. When there are no enemies, Damage dealt decreases by 10% every second, until it becomes 0.
Unfinished Reverie aligns well with Emilie’s emphasis on the reactions from Burning. However, if you’re not building her for Burning, you can opt for Deepwood Memories instead. Team Compositions
Now that we are done with Weapons, Artifacts and Stats, let’s discuss the Best Team Compositions for Emilie. Emilie got high DMG and Burning reactions, so the best team composition would be one that focuses on these two. Such a team composition would be a Pyro DPS that could start the Burning reaction on enemies. So, having characters such as Hu Tao, Lyney, and Arlecchino are going to be the top options for the team composition. Then you can have Bennett as your buffer and healer. She is one of the best, especially C6-Bennett.
Certainly, if you’re looking to prevent Burning, you can integrate Emilie into a Hyperbloom or Quicken team composition. However, she will need to equip the Deepwood Memories Artifact set instead of Unfinished Reverie. Emilie can serve as an effective sub-DPS for characters like Cyno or Clorinde, and she can even collaborate well with Alhaitham or Nahida.
Conclusion
This would be all in this Genshin Impact Emilie Guide. We have discussed the best Weapons, Artifacts, Stats, and Team Composition for Emilie. The Weapons for Emilie are not very good, but decent enough to have lasting impact on the battle. Use the right strategy and make full use of these mentioned items and weapons to create a powerful build for Emilie.

Emilie's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Emilie team composition guide and 
character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we'll explore the best teams to build for Emilie, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and team compositions to help Emilie 
reach her full damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Emilie
The most important characters to pair with Emilie are
Pyro
characters,
as they are required to activate the Burning reaction. Without Burning, Emilie
will deal negligible damage and have almost no value on a team. As long as the
reaction is consistently present, Emilie can fit into many different teams,
dealing good off-field damage alongside your main DPS.
Durin
Durin
Durin is one of the best possible supports for Emilie, providing strong, 
	long-lasting off-field Pyro damage and application, alongside RES Shred
	to increase both Emilie and your on-field DPS' damage.
Mavuika
Pyro Traveler
Pyro Scroll Holders
Mavuika
and
Pyro Traveler
both provide off-field
Pyro application, while being able to wield the
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
set for team-wide DMG % buffing.
Nicole
Nicole
Nicole is one of the greatest supports you can use with Emilie,
	especially when paired with
Durin
. The teamwide ATK she 
	provides, along with the DMG% from The Bounty of Nature, allows
Emilie and Durin to deal fantastic damage together with your on-field DPS.
Because Nicole counts as a Hexerei character, Durin will also get his full
35% RES Shred for additional buffing.
Note that The Bounty of Nature set will only buff Dendro DMG% for
Emilie if you are using a Dendro character on field.
Bennett
Bennett
Although Bennett does not provide off-field Pyro application,
	Burning can still be sustained against a single wave of enemies, as long as
	you do not apply any other Elements that would remove it.
Iansan
Ororon
Scroll Holders
Iansan
and
Ororon
can both buff Dendro
	with the
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
set. Iansan in particular
	will also provide great buffing to your active character, especially ones
	that move around a lot.
Furina
Furina
If your team has a good healer, Furina can provide a good DMG% buff
	that both Emilie and your main DPS will benefit from. Furina can also
	Vaporize some of her damage off the Burning aura, allowing her to do 
	good damage of her own.
Kinich
Kinich
Kinich is a great DPS to pair with Emilie, since they both want the
	Burning reaction to maximize their damage. Since Kinich is Dendro,
Nicole
will be able to buff Dendro with The Bounty of Nature.
	Having a second Dendro unit on the team also makes it much easier for Emilie
	to use her Elemental Burst every rotation, if desired.
Klee
Lyney
Pyro DPS
Pyro DPS, especially ones like
Klee
and
Lyney
that don't work as well in Vaporize/Melt teams, can make great use of
	Emilie.
Ganyu
Wriothesley
Cryo DPS
Cryo DPS like
Ganyu
and
Wriothesley
can make 
	great use of Emilie, thanks to the Burning reaction supplying extra Pyro 
	application, ensuring they always have enough to Melt.
Mualani
Neuvillette
Hydro DPS
Similar to Cryo, Hydro DPS like
Mualani
and
Neuvillette
can use the extra Pyro application from 
	Burning to allow for more consistent Vaporize damage.
Clorinde
Cyno
Electro DPS
Electro DPS have a unique interaction with Burning. With enough
	Electro application, the Burning aura can be Overloaded away before the
	Quicken aura decays. This allows for near full Quicken uptime, while getting
	Overload damage as well.
	However, this team archetype is currently underpowered compared to other
	team options for Electro, like Overload and Lunar-Charged.

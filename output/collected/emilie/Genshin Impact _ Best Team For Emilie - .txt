Emilie Works Best As Burning Sub-DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Burning Team
- Emilie is best used in a Burning team as the Sub-DPS.
- Since Natlan's release added Kinich, he became the best On-Field DPS for Burning. Both Kinich and Emilie provide powerful damage since both complement each other's kit.
- Any Pyro Sub-DPS can be used but Mavuika is preferred due to her buff and attack AoE.
2nd
▼ Burnmelt Team
- Emilie's great Dendro application allows her to maintain a Burning reaction as long as you have a good Pyro Sub-DPS.
- Cryo On-Field DPS like Wriothesley will be able proc Melt regularly and both Emilie and the DPS can deal good damage.
- Since Cryo and Dendro don't interact, Wriothesley will not be able to erase the Dendro aura.
3rd
▼ Burnvape Team
- Another team utilizing Emilie's Dendro application.
- Mualani is the best DPS for Burnvape since her slower attacks has no chance of triggering Bloom which can erase the Dendro aura.
Emilie's niche is enabling Burning and dealing powerful Dendro damage when Burning happens. Her amazing Dendro application allows her to be used for Burnmelt and Burnvape teams that utilize Burning for extending the Pyro aura.

Emilie's powerful Dendro application and synergy to Burning makes her a great Sub-DPS for it. With Kinich being buffed by Iansan and Mavuika, he'll be able to deal good damage while also gaining more Nightsoul points from Burning.
Rotation Of Burning Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Emilie and use her Skill or Burst to spawn a Lumidouce Case
2
Switch to Iansan and use her Skill then hit enemies once with her Normal Attack
3
Use Iansan's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Mavuika and use her Skill (Tap) then Burst
5
Switch to Kinich and use his Skill (Hold) before quickly letting go
6
Deal damage with Kinich's Normal Attacks and use his Skill when his Nightsoul bar is full
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire

Emilie and Mavuika works together to maintain a Burning aura that Wriothesley can trigger Melt on. Bennett can buff Wriothesley's Attack while giving him healing if his HP gets too low for enemy hits.
Rotation Of Burnmelt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Emilie and use her Skill or Burst to spawn a Lumidouce Case
2
Switch to Mavuika and use her Burst then Skill (Tap)
3
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Wriothesley and use his Skill
5
Deal damage with his Normal Attacks and use his Charged Attack when he gains Graceful Rebuke
6
Use his Burst to end his rotation
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
Mavuika goes before Bennett so she can funnel Energy particles to him. You may need a dash at the end of Mavuika's rotation for her to not get the Particles instead.

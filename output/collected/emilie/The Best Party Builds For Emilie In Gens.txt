To cook the best
Emilie
teams, you must understand how Emilie's skills and talents work and the recipes of teammates required. Emilie's skillset is mainly geared towards being a Dendro damage dealer. She deals most of her damage from her Elemental skill, "Fragrance Extraction," and in all team compositions, Emilie is best paired with a Pyro character and used as an off-field damage dealer.
Emilie's Best Overall Team
Emilie and Arlechinno
Arlechinno
is one of the strongest damage dealers in Genshin Impact at constellation zero. Pairing her with Emilie's great Off-field damage will let you shread through any enemy easily.

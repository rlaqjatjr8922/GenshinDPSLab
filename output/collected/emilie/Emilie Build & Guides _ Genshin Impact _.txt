Emilie's Stat Priority
:
ATK %
:
Dendro DMG
or ATK %
:
CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG > ATK %
Substats:
CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > ATK % > Energy Recharge
Emilie should always use an ATK % Sands. Dendro DMG and CRIT are usually
best for the Goblet and Circlet, but ATK % is close enough to use
temporarily. Triple ATK % main stats are not recommended.
Energy Recharge substats can usually be ignored, and Emilie can use her Elemental Burst
every other rotation. If using an Energy weapon, however, getting a few substats to
Burst every rotation is worth it.

Shadow-Hunting Spear (Custom)
Most of Emilie's damage comes from her Elemental Skill, so this talent
should be focused first. Her Elemental Burst also deals good damage and should
be leveled too. If your Emilie is C4+, most of her damage comes from the Burst,
so it should be prioritized instead in that case.
The Normal Attack talent can be ignored entirely.
How to Play Emilie
Emilie's playstyle is focused around providing consistent and high Dendro
damage and application from her Elemental Skill in Burning teams. Her rotation
will usually see her cast her Skill and/or Burst, then swap to the next
character in the rotation.
Emilie's Best Constellations
Emilie's strongest Constellation overall is her C4, making her Burst
substantially stronger and now worth using every rotation. C4 is a much larger
increase than C6, making it the recommended long-term stopping point.
C1 is also a strong damage increase, while C2 provides good support for Dendro
DPS like
Kinich
.

Emilie
is a 5-star Dendro Polearm character that primarily plays the role of a Sub-DPS.
You can find some popular team compositions and builds below, as well as recommended team rotations!

Burgeon
For Burgeon to occur Hydro and Dendro must interact to create Dendro Cores. These Cores then explode with the addition of Pyro dealing significant AoE damage. A shield or healing character adds to the survivability of these teams as characters can take damage from the exploding Dendro Cores. Below is an example of a Burgeon team that includes

Burn
Burn teams focus on causing the Burn reaction through a combination of Dendro and Pyro. A Crown Control Anemo character typically assists by grouping enemies together, allowing for easier Burn application and amplifying Elemental Mastery, which in turn increases the damage of the reactions.

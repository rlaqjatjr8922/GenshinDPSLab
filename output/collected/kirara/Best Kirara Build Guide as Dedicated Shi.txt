Best Kirara Build: Dedicated Shielder
Kirara is shield support. During your run with her, most of the time you will just switch to Kirara, use her skill and burst if it’s ready, then switch back to your other character. It’s the same way when we are using Layla or Diona.
When building Kirara, our focus will be on her Elemental Skill:
Meow-teor Kick
. One tap will make her leap and kick enemy in front of her and will generate dendro shield based on her max HP. Using hold will do the same but turned her into cat-box with increased speed movement and able to climb a wall, especially useful for exploration.
Taking that into account. When raising her skill, Kirara telent priority will be like this:
Talent Priority
Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst > Normal Attack
Building Kirara as dedicated shield means stacking as much HP as you can with exception of Energy Recharge stats. You can choose between using Energy Recharge Sand or Energy Recharge HP and go all the way with HP.

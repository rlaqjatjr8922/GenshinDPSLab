Kirara is a Gold Level Courier of the Komaniya Express, an Inazuman shipping company that works around the clock to ensure that people receive their packages safely and on time. Despite being a nekomata, Kirara has adapted well to human society and is loved for her excellent customer service. She's a four-star Dendro sword user who
specializes in shielding allies
.

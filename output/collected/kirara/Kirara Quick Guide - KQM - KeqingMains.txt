Updated for Version 4.0
Kirara is a 4-star Dendro Sword character in Genshin Impact who provides shielding, fast on-field Dendro application, and unique mobility. Learn about Kirara’s best builds, Talent priority, best weapons, best artifacts, and best teams in this quick guide.
Note that the information given may change if new discoveries are made.
More extensive testing is in progress.

Character Overview
Playstyles
Off-Field Support
Kirara uses the shield from her Skill to increase the survivability of her team.
On-Field Driver
Kirara uses her Hold Skill to quickly apply Dendro and literally drive reactions.
Talents
Level and Talent Priority
To maximize Kirara’s HP and thus her Shield Absorption, it is recommended to level her to 90.
Talent Priority
Skill > Burst
For both her off-field and on-field playstyles, Kirara’s Skill provides the majority of her utility. Her Normal Attacks are not an important part of her kit and can be left unleveled.

Meow-teor Kick
Talent Description

Leaps into the air with all the agility of a cat passing through the bushes, and thwacks her foes with a flying kick that deals

Hold
Out of her desire to “deliver within half a day,” Kirara deploys a Shield of Safe Transport identical to the one that can be created by pressing the skill. She will also curl up into a special express delivery box, entering the Urgent Neko Parcel state in order to move and fight more swiftly.
Urgent Neko Parcel
Deals Dendro DMG to opponents she crashes into. This effect can be triggered once on each opponent every 0.5s.
When in this state, Kirara’s movement speed, climbing speed, and jumping power are all increased, and her Stamina Consumption from climbing is increased.
When the duration ends or the skill is used again, a Flipclaw Strike more powerful than the attack in the Mode will be unleashed, dealing
AoE Dendro DMG
.
The Urgent Neko Parcel state lasts a maximum of 10s. When the state ends, the skill will enter CD. The longer Kirara spends in this state, the longer the CD will be.
Sprinting or actively canceling climbing will end this state early.
This Skill deals Dendro damage and generates a shield; it also allows Kirara to enter an augmented movement state (known as “Urgent Neko Parcel”) when held.
Tap Skill
It is generally not recommended to use Kirara’s Tap Skill due to the existence of the Short Hold Skill tech.
Short Hold Skill
This method involves briefly holding Kirara’s Skill before pressing the Skill button again to cancel the Urgent Neko Parcel state. The Short Hold Skill tech still takes around as much field time as a Tap Skill but generates 4 Dendro Particles instead of 3, gains more A1 stacks, deals more damage, and applies Dendro twice instead of once.
Hold Skill (Full)
Kirara’s Hold Skill can apply Dendro very quickly, although it requires frequent contact with enemies.

Bewitching, Betwitching Tails
Talent Description
When Kirara is in the Urgent Neko Parcel state of Meow-teor Kick, each impact against an opponent will grant her a stack of Reinforced Packaging. This effect can be triggered once for each opponent hit every 0.5s. Max 3 stacks. When the Urgent Neko Parcel state ends, each stack of Reinforced Packaging will create 1 Shield of Safe Transport for Kirara. The shields that are created this way will have 20% of the DMG absorption that the Shield of Safe Transport produced by Meow-teor Kick would have. If Kirara is already protected by a Shield of Safe Transport created by Meow-teor Kick, its DMG absorption will stack with these shields and its duration will reset.
Kirara gains stacks when she hits enemies during her Urgent Neko Parcel state, which are then converted into additional DMG absorption — up to 60%, 3 stacks — when the state ends. It is possible to receive all stacks using the Short Hold Skill tech.

Kirara’s ER requirements are generally low and can be met easily with an ER weapon and some ER substats. Teams
Teambuilding
Kirara slots best in quickswap teams due to her relatively short cooldowns. Below we examine some aspects of her kit that impact her other potential team options.
Off-Field Dendro Application
Kirara’s off-field Dendro application is rather poor even at C4; if using her as an off-field support in a team that relies on Bloom reactions, it is highly recommended to bring a second Dendro character to trigger Blooms consistently.
Battery Potential
Kirara’s Short Hold Skill tech generates 4 Dendro particles at once, with a relatively short cooldown — this makes her a good battery for other Dendro characters, such as Alhaitham.
Defensive Utility
As a Dendro shielder, Kirara provides notable survivability against Bloom self-damage, particularly in Burgeon or Nilou Bloom teams.
Baizhu and Yaoyao are stiff competition as off-field Dendro defensive supports.
On-Field Driver
It can be difficult to utilize Kirara as an on-field driver due to the following reasons:
Kirara’s Hold Skill cannot trigger certain abilities in the game such as Xingqiu, Yelan, Thoma, and Beidou’s Bursts.
By extension, on-field Kirara lacks many effective off-field Hydro options for Bloom teams.
Her Skill’s long cooldown if using the full Hold duration causes significant downtime in her personal Dendro application.
Kirara’s low personal damage makes her lackluster as a Spread DPS.

Quicken
Kirara’s shield uptime aligns well with the short rotations of many Quicken teams, such as those with Keqing or Tighnari. She synergizes well with an on-field Dendro (Spread) DPS by providing Dendro particles.
Hyperbloom / Burgeon
Kirara mainly provides survivability through her strong shield. As an off-field support, a second Dendro unit is required to keep up Dendro application. Kirara is not recommended as an on-field driver due to the lack of synergistic Hydro appliers and Burgeon triggers.
Nilou Bloom
Kirara provides a strong shield for Nilou Bloom teams, although it is still recommended to bring a healer to cover for possible downtime.
In a purely off-field role, a fast Dendro applier such as Alhaitham or on-field Nahida is highly recommended to make up for Kirara’s poor Dendro application. On-field Kirara is a viable option, particularly when paired with Barbara.
General Flex Shielder
Kirara provides moderate shield strength and low off-field Dendro application that will barely interfere with other reactions. She works best in quickswap teams due to her shield’s relatively short duration of 12s. However, she faces stiff competition from other shield units who provide greater utility — such as being able to hold 4TotM — damage, and/or ease-of-use.

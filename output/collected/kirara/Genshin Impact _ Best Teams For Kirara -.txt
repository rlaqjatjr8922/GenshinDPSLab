does not have an internal cooldown
so it can consecutively apply Electro and trigger any possible reactions when the enemy inside the AoE. It is recommended to
use when both Nahida's Burst & Xingqiu's barrier are active

The objective of this team is to produce Bountiful cores as fast as possible. Since Bountiful cores can only be produced in a Dendro-Hydro team with Nilou, the active character should be consistent on applying either Dendro or Hydro. The best candidate for this is either Nilou with Hydro infusion, Nahida, or Kokomi.
Quirky Playstyle With Kirara's Cat Form
Since Kirara can apply Dendro in her cat form, you can also make her as your active character and roam around as you pass through the enemies.

The objective of this team is to trigger vaporize reactions. Thru Hydro resonance, Kirara's max HP is increased which also strengthens her shield. Also, it is strategic to include a main DPS that scales with HP.
Rotation Of Vape Kirara Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Kirara's Elemental Burst for Dendro DMG
2
Tap Kirara's Elemental Skill for shield
3
Switch to Xingqiu
4

5
Switch to Yelan
6
Use Yelan's Elemental Burst for coordinated attack
7
Switch to Hu Tao
8
Use Hu Tao's Elemental Burst for Pyro infusion and utilize it until the duration ends
When Hu Tao's Pyro infusion has ended, switch to other characters and use their Skill to produce particles for Burst. When everything is ready, repeat the rotation again. It is recommended to set up everything first before using Hu Tao's Skill for optimized damage output.
When To Use Hu Tao's Burst
You can use Hu Tao's Burst if you need to restore a decent amount of HP. It is recommended to have 50% HP or below to optimize Hu Tao & Staff of Homa.

This team is centered around Hyperbloom as the main reaction. Kirara's role in this comp is a shield support to prevent interruption and also a Burst DPS that deals instantaneous DMG towards enemies. The more constellation your Kirara has, the better she is in this team comp. Kirara is a flexible shielder that can fit many comps so long as they react well with Dendro.
Rotation Of Support Kirara Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Elemental Burst of Xingqiu and use Elemental Skill.
2
Switch to Kirara and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Next, switch to Kuki Shinobu and use Elemental Skill.
4
Switch to Alhaitham and use Elemental Skill then charged attack.
5
Deal Normal Attack and Elemental Burst until the skills ran out.
6
Repeat 1
The rotation itself is flexible. You just need to activate all the off-field by the time that you use your main DPS.

Aloy Genshin Impact Guide: Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
January 29, 2024
Tips
Share:
Aloy
is a
5-star Cryo archer
, but before coming to shoot frozen arrows in Genshin Impact, she is first and foremost the heroine of the action/adventure video game
Horizon
. She is thus the first character to be issued from a collaboration with another universe. She benefits from a dynamic and easy to handle
gameplay
. Often underestimated, she is nevertheless a convincing
DPS burst
and is ideal for beginners. We explain how to
play Aloy from Genshin Impact
in this
complete guide
. With our tips and tricks, you'll know which artifacts and weapons to equip her with, and who to play her with.
How to get Aloy in Genshin Impact ?
Aloy is a unique character. She belongs to the Horizon universe. This collaboration was to celebrate the
Genshin Impact release on PS5
. You could then
unlock Aloy in Genshin Impact for free
if you play on PS4 or PS5. Then, the Cryo heroine was offered from October 13 to November 24, 2021 to PC and mobile players.
Unfortunately, it is no longer possible to get Aloy. She doesn't get any forward bids in an Character Event Wish or a Standard Wish. Also, unlike all other characters in the RPG, she has no constellation.
How to play Aloy in Genshin Impact ?
While one might expect Aloy to be played as the main DPS, she proves to be better as a
sub-DPS
. Her normal attacks are unfortunately too weak to make her a full-fledged DPS. She does not compete with
Ayaka
or
Ganyu
in this area. On the other hand, thanks to her elemental attacks, she can effectively assist your team.
Statistics of Aloy's elemental attack at level 5
With her
Frozen Wilds
elemental ability, Aloy fires a
gel bomb
that explodes on impact in the targeted direction and deals Cryo damage. It then splits into many smaller bombs, similar to the elemental attack of
Klee
. When a bomb hits an enemy, the enemy's ATK drops (up to 15% once the ability reaches level 10) and Aloy gets 1
Coil stack
. If she has 4, Aloy enters a state of
Rushing Ice state
. Her normal attacks will be infused with Cryo and do more damage for 10 seconds.
As an added bonus, the
Combat Override
passive increases Aloy's ATK by 16% and nearby characters by 8% for 10 seconds when affected by at least one coil. The
Strong Strike
passive increases Aloy's Cryo damage bonus by up to 35% when she is in a
Rush Ice state
.
Statistics of Aloy's elemental rampage at level 5
This is the perfect time to activate her
Prophecies of Dawn
elemental rampage. Aloy shoots an arrow that generates large DMG Area Cryo. This ability only costs 40 elemental energy points, so you can cast it regularly.
The trick is to only mount Aloy's elemental attack and elemental rampage. To do good damage, aim for level 8 for these two abilities.
What is the best equipment for Aloy in Genshin Impact ?
As for most offensive characters, we recommend a build with a maximum of ATK, critical rate and critical damage.
We reveal the
best artifacts and weapons to equip on Aloy in Genshin Impact.
The best artifacts for Aloy
To
play Aloy as a DPS burst in Genshin Impact,
the best artifact set is definitely the 4 piece
Noblesse Oblige
set. With 2 pieces of equipment, you increase elemental rampage by 20%. The 4-piece set will boost the attack of the whole team after an elemental rampage. This effect is not cumulative, however.
Noblesse Oblige
If a character on your team is already wearing this set, you can start with the 4-piece
Emblem of Severed Fate
set. It increases energy recharge by 20% and elemental rampage damage by 25% of your character's total energy recharge.
Emblem of Severed Fate
For your Aloy to benefit from a more offensive build, our advice is to opt for 2 pieces from the
Noblesse Oblige
and 2 pieces from the
Blizzard Strayer
set, in order to benefit from a 15% Cryo damage bonus.
Blizzard Strayer
It is also possible to choose 2 pieces of equipment from the
Blizzard Strayer
set and 2 pieces from a set of artifacts boosting the ATK of 18% such as
Gladiator's Finale
or
Shimenawa's Reminiscence
.
Gladiator's Finale
Shimenawa's Reminiscence
For the main statistics, prefer :
Hourglass: ATK%;
Cut: Cryo damage bonus;
Crown: critical rate or critical damage.
For secondary statistics:
ATK% ;
Critical rate ;
Critical damage ;
Elemental Mastery;
Energy recharge.
The best weapons for Aloy
In order to perfect
Genshin Impact Aloy build
, choose preferably weapons with ATK, critical damage or rate, or even energy recharge.
Here are the best weapons to equip on Aloy in Genshin Impact :
Polar star (5★)
Skyward Harp (5★)
Thundering pulse (5★)
Pole Star
: The bow increases the
critical rate
and damage of elemental skills by 12% and the ATK by up to 48%;
Skyward Harp
: You boost
critical rate
, critical damage, while triggering additional damage after hitting an enemy;
Thunder Pulse
: This is the best weapon to equip on Aloy for a DPS build. The bow increases the
critical damage
, the ATK of 20% and the damage of the normal attacks up to 40%.
There are also some excellent options on the 4-star side:
The Stringless (4★)
Alley Hunter (4★)
Sacrificial Bow (4★)
The Stringless
: this bow is excellent on Aloy: it increases his
elemental mastery
and the damage of his elemental skills;
Alley Hunter
: This weapon boosts
ATK
and will increase Aloy's damage by up to 20% when not deployed;
Sacrificial Bow
: Not the best choice, but the bow will give you a significant energy recharge bonus and allow you to reset Aloy's elemental skill.
Aloy's climbing materials in Genshin Impact
To
raise Aloy to level 80+ in Genshin Impact
and thus increase her Cryo damage bonus, you will need to collect the following resources:
18
Spectral Husks
, 30
Spectral Hearts
and 36
Spectral Nucleus
to loot on
Specter
;
1 Sliver, 9 Fragments, 9 Chunks and 6 Gemstones of
Shivada Jade
, as well as 46
Crystalline Bloom
. You will get them by fighting the
Cryo Hypostasis
;
168
Crystal Marrows
: you will find them in
Inazuma
. Locate them directly with the
interactive map of Teyvat
.
Which team should Aloy play in?
Aloy can fit into almost any team in Genshin Impact. She will be able to apply the Cryo element to create elemental reactions and generate good damage with her elemental rampage.
For example, you can perform
Melt
by playing it with
Xiangling
/
Bennett
/ an Anemo character such as
Venti
,
Kazuha
or
Sucrose
to gather the enemy and weaken them.
You can also choose two Cryo characters: Aloy /
Diona
/
Xiangling
/ Kazuha or another Anemo character.
Aloy will also work in a
Gel team
with
Ayaka
/
Kazuha
/
Kokomi
.
And we have one last fun option for you:
Klee
/ Aloy /
Sucrose
or
Kazuha
/
Bennett
. The trick is that Sucrose or Kazuha will be able to gather the bombs of Klee and Aloy to generate a maximum of explosions.
How old and how tall is Aloy from Genshin Impact ?
Aloy is about
18 years
old and
1m62
tall. She is the heroine of the game Horizon released on PS4 and PS5. She has no story in Genshin Impact.
That's it for our tips and tricks in this
Genshin Impact Aloy guide
Although the Cryo heroine has no constellation and a weak basic attack, she has many strengths. She can lower the enemy's ATK and increase your team's, easily apply the Cryo element and regularly perform her elemental rampage. These qualities make the Archer a very good sub-DPS, especially since she is one of the easiest characters to play in Genshin Impact and will work in almost all your teams.

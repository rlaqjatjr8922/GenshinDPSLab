Rate Aloy!
Strengths & Weakness Of Aloy
Strengths
- Can increase the ATK of the whole team
- Low energy cost for Elemental Burst.
- Free character that can work as a DPS or a Sub-DPS
Weakness
- Needs Coil stacks to convert her Normal Attack from Physical to Cryo
- Long CD for Elemental Skill
- Currently does not have any constellation.
- Fairly low damage and needs a lot of investments.

DMG-Boosting Weapons Are Ideal
Aloy has multiple sources of damage though her Burst is her strongest one. Weapons that can increase Burst DMG like Polar Star and Mouun's Moon are also great options for DPS Aloy.

Aloy Skill Guide & How To Use Aloy
Recommended Rotation For Aloy
Recommended Rotation
1
Use her Elemental Skill to throw Freeze Bombs towards the enemies and then switch to any another character.
2
Attack enemies using your Main DPS while luring them to the Ice bombs for additional Buff.
3
Once Aloy's Energy is full, use her Elemental Burst then return to number 1.
How To Use Aloy's Elemental Skill
Used To Buff Aloy's Attack
▲ Coil Stacks countdowns from 1 to 4 making her normal attack infuse with Cryo element.
Aloy's Elemental Skill inflicts Cryo element enabling reactions making an opening to attack the opponents. When her Coil Stacks increases, Aloy's normal attack increases.
Use Of Aloy's Elemental Burst
Aloy's Elemental Burst gives a powerful blast that gives huge damage to the enemies which is a good finishing touch for one-shot comps.
Should You Pull for Aloy's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
No Need To Pull For Aloy
Aloy was a free character during Version 2.1 ~ 2.2 that cannot be obtained via wishes. You do not need to pull for Aloy.
Aloy Has No Constellation
Aloy is the only character, so far, that has no constellations in her kit.

Frozen Wilds (Elemental Skill)
Effect
Aloy throws a Freeze Bomb in the targeted direction that explodes on impact, dealing Cryo DMG. After it explodes, the Freeze Bomb will split up into many Chillwater Bomblets that explode on contact with opponents or after a short delay, dealing Cryo DMG. When a Freeze Bomb or Chillwater Bomblet hits an opponent, the opponent's ATK is decreased and Aloy receives 1 Coil stack. Aloy can gain up to 1 Coil stack every 0.1s.
Coil
Effect
・Each stack increases Aloy's Normal Attack DMG.
・When Aloy has 4 Coil stacks, all stacks of Coil are cleared. She then enters the Rushing Ice state, which further increases the DMG dealt by her Normal Attacks and converts her Normal Attack DMG to Cryo DMG.
While in the Rushing Ice state, Aloy cannot obtain new Coil stacks. Coil effects will be cleared 30s after Aloy leaves the field.

Combat Override
Effect
When Aloy receives the Coil effect from Frozen Wilds, her ATK is increased by 16%, while nearby party members' ATK is increased by 8%. This effect lasts 10s.
Strong Strike
Effect
When Aloy is in the Rushing Ice state conferred by Frozen Wilds, her Cryo DMG Bonus increases by 3.5% every 1s. A maximum Cryo DMG Bonus increase of 35% can be gained in this way.
Easy Does It
Effect
When Aloy is in the party, animals who produce Fowl, Raw Meat, or Chilled Meat will not be startled when party members approach them.

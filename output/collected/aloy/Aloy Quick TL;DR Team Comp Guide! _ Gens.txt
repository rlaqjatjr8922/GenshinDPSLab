Aloy
(I would have honestly preferred Minecraft Steve over this tbh...)
She basically found a portal and just walked right through
Element:
Cryo
Weapon:
Bow
Pros:
Free 5-star character
Decent self-buffs
Cryo Yoimiya but slightly weaker
Has Cryo DMG as hidden stat
High damage multipliers
40 Cost burst + massive AoE
Very viable as burst DPS
Extremely easy to gear/play
Not reliant on constellations (rather has none)
Cons:
Doesn't have any constellations
E has long CD
Needs E stacks to get Cryo DMG
Bombs don't get picked up by Venti Q
Needs Spectral Hearts
Suffers against shields a lot
PSA #1: I mean it's a free 5-star DPS, and she can hit fairly hard too so can't complain too much! She struggles with a bit of energy generation, but running a battery pretty much solves that problem. Run her like any Cryo DPS -- either a AoE Melt comp OR a Freeze comp!
Kazuha
E has massive AoE that gathers enemies, spreads Cryo, and can pull Aloy's E bombs
Q can buff Cryo output, or Swirl with Pyro to give Aloy consistent Melt procs
Does a lot of damage as well, plus has extremely low CD to be a battery for Aloy's Q
CAN BE REPLACED WITH VENTI OR SUCROSE FOR HIGHER EM REACTION DAMAGE!
Xiangling
Guoba can spread and inflict Pyro on enemies with decent AoE + hits pretty hard
Xiangling's Q works great with Aloy's Cryo buffed NA + her E/Q (pretty much in perfect range)
Can be a reliable sub DPS option when Aloy's Cryo DMG is on CD/off rotation
CAN BE REPLACED WITH ROSARIA OR XINGQIU IF FREEZE COMP!
Bennett
Low E CD lets you spam some energy + spread Pyro everywhere
Extremely low cost Q that provides massive healing, ATK buff, and inflicts Pyro on enemies inside the AoE (free Melt procs)
Can be run with 4x Noblesse to further buff ATK for Aloy's DPS
CAN BE REPLACED WITH BARBARA OR DIONA FOR CRYO RESONANCE!
PSA #2: The example above is for a really consistent/easy to play Melt comp. Just remember to get the 4 Coil stacks so Aloy can get Cryo DMG infused to her NA. Otherwise you'll just be doing Phys DMG. A Melt comp fits her playstyle a bit more, whereas a Freeze comp is more suitable if you're running her burst DPS/need CRIT% assistance!
Great flex slot alternatives:
Venti -
Even though his Q can't pick up the bombs from Aloy's E, still provides a lot of energy + great AoE grouping for her Q
Sucrose -
Her E and Q both pull the bombs to the center + great AoE. Plus running TTDS gives Aloy a 48% ATK buff
Rosaria -
Great Cryo battery and good burst DPS/sub DPS option for when Aloy's E is on CD
Xingqiu -
Awesome off-field DPS that can work well with Aloy's Cryo infused NA. Works even better in a Freeze comp
Mona -
Alternative option for Xingqiu. Taunt is great for grouping enemies for her Freeze + Q damage
Barbara -
Her E lets you easily proc Freeze on enemies that get too close
Diona -
Great energy battery, strong shield, and decent heals. Also provides Cryo resonance, so free CRIT% for Aloy
Anemo MC -
E and Q both pull in the bombs, so use it as a VV option if you somehow don't have any other Anemo character
Albedo -
His E provides a lot of decent off-field DPS, and great for Aloy's NA damage when her E is on CD (if she's still on the field)

Aloy Overview
This Page provides the Best Aloy Build information.
Aloy is formerly an outcast, now a hunter of unparalleled keenness. Ready to do the right thing with her bow at any given time.
Aloy
is a Cryo Main DPS in Genshin Impact at the moment. Aloy Main DPS Build is mostly used by travelers. Her Elemental Skill can decrease opponents’ damage.
Aloy
has different ways to deal large amounts of damage. Not only decrease opponents’ damage by using the Elemental Skill, but also provide great damage by using Elemental Burst. ATK% is the most important stats for Aloy, so the Main DPS Build will also help travelers to stack the ATK% for Aloy.

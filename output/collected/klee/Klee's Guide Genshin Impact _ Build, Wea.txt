Klee Guide Genshin Impact : Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
November 14, 2023
Tips
Share:
Klee is a
5-star Pyro
character. Behind her adorable appearance lies a formidable fighter and a lover of explosions of all kinds. The heroine is played as the
main DPS
. She will be able to apply the Pyro element with each of her attacks and inflict great damage in the abyss. However, her
gameplay
is rather demanding. But don't worry, we explain
how to play Klee in Genshin Impact
in this
complete guide
. Our tips and tricks will help you to know which artifacts and weapons to equip him with to give him the
best build
.
How to have Klee in Genshin Impact ?
Klee is a 5 star character, you can only summon her when she is highlighted in a Character Event Wish.
Klee's banner
Klee was released on 20 October 2020. She enjoyed a first rerun in June 2021 and is expected to return to the RPG soon.
Klee's Constellations
You can play Klee in Genshin Impact without mounting his constellations. However, if you particularly like the character, you can try to unlock his first constellations.
C1
Chainez Reactions
gives Klee's attacks a chance to generate sparks that bombard enemies. They do 120% of the damage of Klee's elemental rampage.
And thanks to C2
Explosive Frags
, enemies hit by Klee's elemental attack mines lose 23% defence for 10 seconds.
How to play Klee in Genshin Impact ?
Klee is intended to be played as the
main DPS
. She will be able to inflict heavy damage on enemies with her bombs. Fighting with a catalyst, she has the advantage of applying the Pyro element with all her attacks. However, she has a short range and must be relatively close to her opponents to hit them.
Klee's gameplay in Genshin Impact relies heavily on his
normal and charged attacks
. However, their animations are long and by staying still, you have an easier chance of getting hit. Our advice is to jump or rush forward right after performing a normal or charged attack to skip the animation and perform another attack.
The elemental attacks, on the other hand, are easier to handle. With
Jumpy Dumpty
, Klee throws a bomb that bounces 3 times. The projectile inflicts Pyro damage with each bounce and then splits into several mines. These explode on contact with the enemy. You can use this ability twice in a row.
Thanks to the
Pounding Surprise
passive, Klee and her Bouncing Bomb's normal attacks have a 50% chance of giving her an explosive spark when dealing damage. The next charged attack will do 50% more damage without consuming stamina.
Finally, with his
Sparks 'n' Splash
elemental rampage, Klee bombards enemies with explosive flowers for 10 seconds, dealing Pyro area damage.
The best rotation is to cast your support skills, then Klee's elemental attack and elemental rampage and then chain his normal and charged attacks together as quickly as possible. You will need to upgrade all of her skills in this way.
What is the best equipment for Klee?
To do maximum damage in Genshin Impact, Klee will need a build that increases both his attack, critical hit rate and damage, but also his elemental control and Pyro damage. Find out in this guide our advice on the
best artifacts and weapons to equip on Klee
.
The best artifacts for Klee
For a versatile build, the trick is to opt for 2 pieces of equipment from the
Crimson Witch of Flames
set. You will benefit from a bonus of damage Pyro of 15%. Combine it with 2 pieces of the
Gladiator's Finale
or
Shimenawa's Reminiscence
set to increase the attack by 18%.
Crimson Witch of Flames
Gladiator's Finale
Shimenawa's Reminiscence
You can also opt for a 4-piece
Crimson Witch of Flames
set, especially if you play Klee in a team based on
elemental reactions
. Overloaded and Burning damage will be increased by 40% and Vaporize and Melt damage by 15%.
The third equipment option is the
Wanderer's Troupe
artefact set. With 2 pieces, you increase your elemental mastery by 80 points, and with 4, the damage of charged attacks is increased by 35%.
Wanderer's Troupe
If in doubt, our advice is to choose the artifact sets with the best statistics:
Hourglass: ATK%;
Cut: Pyro damage bonus;
Crown: critical rate or critical damage.
Secondary statistics :
ATK% and ATK;
Critical rate ;
Critical damage ;
Elemental Mastery;
The best weapons for Klee
To complete Klee's equipment in Genshin Impact, several weapons are possible:
Skyward Atlas (5★)
Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds (5★)
Solar pearl (4★)
Skyward Atlas
: This catalyst increases the
ATK
and elemental damage. Normal attacks have a 50% chance to gain a favour from the heavens attacking enemies for 10 seconds at 160% of ATK damage;
Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds
: The weapon increases
critical rate
, movement speed and grants an 8% elemental damage bonus (cumulative up to 4 times) every 4 seconds while Klee is active;
Solar Pearl
: Battle pass weapon increases
critical rate
, damage from normal attacks and elemental rampages.
And on the free-to-play side, here are our tips:
The Widsith (4★)
Dodoco Tales (4★)
Mappa Mare (4★)
The Widsith
: The catalyst increases
critical damage
and a random melody is cast when Klee arrives at the active post. This can increase ATK by 60%, elemental damage by 48% or elemental mastery by 240 points;
Dodoco Tales
: You gain a significant bonus from ATK and hitting enemies with normal attacks increases the damage of attacks by 16% for 6 seconds. The ATK is then boosted by 8% for 6 seconds;
Mappa Mare
: This weapon also provides elemental mastery. The passive increases elemental damage for 10 seconds after an elemental reaction.
Klee's climbing materials
In order to
raise Klee to level 90 in Genshin Impact
to do more Pyro damage, collect the following resources:
1 Shard, 8 Fragments, 8 Pieces and 6 Stones of
Agnidus Agate
as well as 46
Everflame Seeds
to be harvested from the
Pyro Regisvine
;
18
DiviningScrolls
, 30
Sealed Scrolls
and 36
Forbidden Curse Scrolls
to be obtained from
Shamans Hilichurl
;
168
Philanemo Mushrooms
: you will find them mainly under the Mondstadt and Deauclaire ledges. Locate them with the
interactive map of Teyvat
.
Which team should Klee play in?
Klee sets fire to everything within her reach. Thus, playing her with Electro, Cryo or Hydro characters will allow you to easily perform elemental reactions with greater damage. We also recommend that you play Klee with a character that can deploy a shield like
Zhongli
,
Diona
or
Noëlle
as Klee fights hand-to-hand.
Another tip is to choose an anemo character such as
Sucrose
. In addition to gathering her mines and blowing them up, she will also increase her elemental mastery.
Among the best teams are :
Klee /
Xingqiu
/ Zhongli / Kazuha (to group enemies and increase elemental damage);
Ganyu
/ Klee / Kazuha / Diona ;
Klee / Sucrose /
Xingqiu
/
Bennett
.
How old and how tall is Klee in Genshin Impact ?
Klee, aged about
10
, is one of the youngest characters in Genshin Impact. This does not prevent her, at
1.27m
, from flouting Favonius' orders to go on adventures and blow up fish (her great passion), even if it means getting into trouble.
That's it for this
guide to Klee from Genshin Impact.
The heroine will easily do huge damage in the abyss if you manage to chain her normal and charged attacks together while passing the animations. Her gameplay is thus demanding and difficult to master. In the meantime, you can always go and practice by applying our tips and tricks on fish, that will please Klee!

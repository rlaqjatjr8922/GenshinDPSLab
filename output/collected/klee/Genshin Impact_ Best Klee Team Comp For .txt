Klee is a 5-star Pyro Catalyst
character in Genshin Impact. She is a great Pyro DPS with the ability to deal continuous Pyro damage through her elemental skills, burst or normal attack. Overall, the best part of her kit is the charged attack - it has one of the highest scalings in the game.
In this article, we are going to showcase the best Klee team comp for high DPS.

Klee is the main DPS of this lineup with her whole kit and the 4-piece Crimson Witch of Flames set.
Venti is the main support of this Klee team comp and it is vital that he has the 4-piece Viridescent Venerer set. By triggering Pyro Swirl reaction, you can reduce enemy pyro resistance by 40%.
Ganyu is one of the best characters for a dual-core Klee team comp Genshin Impact.
Ganyu is Klee's Melt Enabler. This is amongst the best reaction in the game, as you can double Klee's Pyro damage. C4 Ganyu is the best for this task, as her burst would increase the damage taken of opponents within its AoE by up to 25%.
Lastly, Bennett is the healer, buffer and battery of the team. He can further enhance Klee’s overall damage with his elemental burst and the 4-piece Noblesse Oblige.

Venti plays the same role on this comp, with the 4-piece Viridescent Venerer set.
Mona allows the Vaporize elemental reaction, which is the second-best type of elemental reaction you can get from Pyro, with a 1.5 multiplier. As Mona's elemental skill has a very short cooldown, you can proc the vaporize reaction constantly. Furthermore, it also taunts opponents, making it easier for Klee to unleash her damage.
Mona is one of the best Hydro Enabler, however, you need decent investment to make her powerful.
Mona's own burst is a good source of damage as well - you can reverse vaporize to deal 2x damage as long as you apply Pyro from Klee first.
As usual, Bennett plays the role of battery, healer and buffer of the team. He is truly one of the best support available for any Pyro character, enabling Elemental Resonance.

Overall, this lineup works almost the same as the previous one, with Venti replaced by Sucrose and Mona replaced by Xingqiu. Sucrose can boost Klee's elemental mastery via passive talents, and at constellation 6, she can increase team damage just as much as Venti's constellation 6 does.
Xingqiu is still top tier amongst Hydro support to build team Klee.
While Xingqiu deals less damage than Mona on his own, he allows Klee to procs Vaporize more consistently. He also provides a shield for increased survivability.
As usual, Bennett's role in this team is to provide Klee with heal, boost and elemental resonance.

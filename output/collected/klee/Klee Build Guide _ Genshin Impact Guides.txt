This is by no means an exact guide on what is best - this is just my personal suggestion.
When Jumpy Dumpty and Normal Attacks deal DMG, Klee has a 50% chance to obtain an Explosive Spark. This Explosive Spark is consumed by the next Charged Attack, which costs no Stamina and deals 50% increased DMG.
When Klee's Charged Attack results in a CRIT Hit, all party members gain 2 Elemental Energy.

Trifolium
C2 < C4
C1 increases Klee’s ability as a Main-DPS by a medium amount. Basically it increases her Elemental Burst DMG by 20%, something that can easily be achieved through specific artifact sets or buffs from other characters.
Klee’s C2 is an absolute priority that is worth every primogem. Decreasing opponents' DEF allows your character to deal higher damage as they will encounter less resistance. C2 is a good place to stop for a powerful DPS Klee.
This increases the level to which Klee’s Elemental Skill can be increased to.
C4 is not as vital as C2 but can still come in handy when building Klee as a Sub-DPS. By switching Klee out of the field during her Elemental Burst she can deal a massive amount of Pyro DMG.
This increases the level to which Klee’s Elemental Burst can be increased to.
C6 is not very important and isn’t really worth pulling for in the scheme of things. It allows her to regenerate energy for her teammates during her Elemental Burst. It also buffs the Pyro DMG of her teammates. These things are not very important for Klee as a Main-DPS but can be useful as a Sub-DPS.

Lavawalker

As we can see from the mathematics section of this guide, regardless of which artifact set you choose,
Lost Prayers to the Sacred Winds
is always the best weapon for Klee. It maximises her damage through its high CRIT Rate, Base ATK and its passive ability to increase Klee’s movement speed. This weapon does require Klee to stay on-field for extended periods of time in order to take full advantage of its passive abilities.
Skyward Atlas
is Klee’s second best performing weapon mathematically. Its high Base ATK value and simple passive ability makes it a very effective option for Klee.
Solar Pearl
is the best 4-star weapon option for Klee. Unfortunately it is a paid weapon that is only available by purchasing the Battle Pass. If you do have Solar Pearl, remember that mathematically it performs better than many of the 5-star catalyst options available.
Dodoco Tales
was released for a limited time during the Version 1.6 Event Midsummer Island Adventure. There is currently no way to obtain this weapon if you did not get it during this time. Generally Dodoco Tales is the second best performing 4-star catalyst. Its aesthetic suitability with Klee shouldn’t be forgotten either!

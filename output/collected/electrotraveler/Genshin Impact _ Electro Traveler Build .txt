Off-Field Electro And Battery For The Team
Electro Traveler is great at spreading Electro to enemies while providing additional Energy for other members through its skills and burst. This build focuses on increasing the energy traveler can give while simultaneously increasing DMG through Emblem of Severed Fate.

Energy Recharge Weapons Or Weapon That Provide Additional Buff Are Recommended
Energy Recharge is a crucial stat for Electro Traveler because its battery capabilities scales from Energy Recharge and the energy cost of Elemental Burst is very high. Weapon that increases Energy Recharge is great because of it.
If you have enough Energy Recharge through artifacts, weapon that provides buff to the team is also great for Traveler.

Electro Traveler Skill Guide & How To Use Electro Traveler
Recommended Rotation For Electro Traveler
Recommended Yanfei Rotation
1
Use Elemental Skill to produce Abundance Amulet
2
Switch to another character and collect the Amulets
3
Repeat from number 1
*
Use Elemental Burst when you have it to regenerate Energy.
Uses Of Electro Traveler's Elemental Skill
Restores & Increases Elemental & Energy Recharge
Upon casting the Elemental Skill, collect the three (3) Abundance Amulets in the field in order to replenish your selected character on field and at the same time adding damage buff to your attacks.
Uses Of Electro Traveler's Elemental Burst
Regenerates Energy
Activating the Elemental Burst will knock off the approaching or nearby enemies, while it also increases the team's ATK damage that continuously aiding a good amount of Electro DMG.

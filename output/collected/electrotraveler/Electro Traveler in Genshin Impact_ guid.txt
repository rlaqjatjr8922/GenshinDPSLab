Skills
When resonating with Electro, the protagonist gains a unique set of skills and talents. You need to know how they work in order to use them effectively.
Basic (Active) Skills
Foreign Thundershock (Normal/Charged Attack)
Performs up to five quick strikes with the sword in a simple attack.
When charged, performs two quick swings that consume stamina.
With a plunging attack, the hero, on contact with the ground, deals AoE damage.
Lightning Blade (elemental skill)
The hero releases three fast thunder shadows in front of him, hitting opponents with Electro. A successful hit leaves an Abundance Amulet on the ground. Initially, only two amulets are created. They disappear when the E is used again.
When approaching Abundance Amulets, party members and teammates absorb them, gaining additional energy and increasing its recovery rate.
Bellowing Thunder (elemental burst)
The Traveler summons a protective lightning bolt that knocks back and damages enemies. At the same time, the Lightning Shroud effect works: if the active character鈥檚 normal or charged attack hits the enemy, Falling Thunder deals additional Electro damage to the target (once every 0,5 seconds). When the effect is triggered, the attacking hero restores his own energy.

How to play the Electro Traveler
Added to Genshin Impact, the Electro MC is a great
alternative for free Lisa.
He is also much
more effective than some supports in terms of energy recovery.
If the player's squad needs exactly this effect, then it is possible to equip Aether or Lumine with the right weapons and artifacts to go into battle.
When the Abundance Amulets appear, they should be caught by the hero, who needs to charge faster to use his E or elemental burst.
The ult of the Electro Traveler himself should be used for the subsequent introduction of the main DPS into the fight
or his replacement. In this case, all damage will be supplemented by Falling Thunder.

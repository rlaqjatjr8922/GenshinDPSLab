Neuvillette is one of the major faces in
Genshin Impact
Fontaine, being the Chief of Justice in the Land of Justice itself. He's also known to be the Hydro Dragon, which further increases his popularity as people quickly assume he has higher strength than average
Genshin Impact
characters.
When it comes to playing Neuvillette, his main feature is his Charged Attack, where he unleashes a large surge of water torrent in a long distance forward. His Skill and Burst support the charging process of this ability by producing Sourcewater Droplet, which cuts down his charging time from a few seconds to an instant cast. Playing Neuvillette requires players to not only pay attention to the Droplet mechanics but also to ensure he reaches his top potential by feeding his second Ascension passive. The passive demands that people include frequent Elemental reactions in
Neuvillette team comp

With better Supports in Genshin Impact, Neuvillette only grows stronger. The Chief of Justice can match the strength of the new DPS characters when teaming with Nod-Krai units that can activate Lunar reactions, such as Lunar-Charged and Lunar Bloom reactions. When building a Neuvillette Nod-Krai team, it's important to always include characters that can work from the off-field, as he needs to remain active on the field during combat to deliver his Charged attacks. For that reason, this list will be updated to include all the new characters that work in Neuvillette team comps in Genshin Impact, including Ineffa, Columbina, and Lauma.
Neuvillette + Furina + Ineffa + Columbina
If Neuvillette and Furina can fully activate Neuvillette's mechanics, Ineffa and
Columbina can operate on their own
to further raise this team's DMG. The idea with this Neuvillette team is to fully buff his Charged Attacks while Ineffa is triggering off-field Lunar-charged reactions. Both Ineffa and Columbina operate as off-field buffers, playing a major role in greatly increasing Neuvillette's DPS while also maximizing the Lunar-charged DMG.
As for Furina, she buffs Neuvillette's DMG while constantly consuming his HP, helping him meet his build and mechanics' requirements faster. Not to mention, she's also a strong off-field DPS who can constantly deal Hydro DMG with her Elemental Skill. Finally, this team has some sustainability with Ineffa's shield that can increase Neuvillette's resistance to interruption, helping him complete all his Charged Attacks without getting interrupted.

Neuvillette + Lauma + Nahida + Columbina
When building Neuvillette with a team that can trigger off-field Lunar Bloom, it's important to team the Chief of Justice with Lauma​​​​​, who enables this Lunar reaction for her team. She also greatly increases the DMG of Lunar Bloom reactions since her entire kit revolves around her team triggering that reaction. Nefer also triggers Lunar Bloom reactions, but she competes with Neuvillette over field time, which will notably affect the team's DPS. After Lauma, you need a strong Dendro character that can constantly deal off-field Dendro DMG like Nahida, who links enemies with her Elemental Skill, and keeps dealing Dendro DMG to all of them whenever her team triggers a
Dendro reaction in
Genshin Impact
.
The final character can be anyone, as the team is mostly complete with Neuvillette, Nahida, and Lauma. Columbina is always an amazing second Nod-Krai character to get, as she enables the same reaction while also greatly buffing it and inflicting her own version of it. Ineffa is also good since she enables the Lunar-charged reactions alongside triggering the Lunar Blooms into Hyperbloom DMG. Of course, you can't go wrong with Furina, too.

Neuvillette + Furina + Xilonen + Zhongli
Having Zhongli and Furina in the same team greatly boosts Neuvillette’s Charged attack to deal some of the best DMG in
Genshin Impact
. Furina quickly buffs Neuvillette’s DMG as both of them are perfect for her Elemental Burst’s requirements. As for Zhongli, he shields Neuvillette and reduces enemies’ resistance to Hydro, which increases both the DPS of Neuvillette and
Furina in
Genshin Impact
. Moreover, pairing Xilonen and Zhongli in the same team triggers the Geo Resonance on top of the Hydro Resonance to add HP and +15% extra DMG for Neuvillette’s attacks.
Xilonen can add so much to the team because of her debuff that she can smoothly trigger alongside her artifact set, Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City, which grants the team a whopping 40% extra Elemental DMG. Not only that, but Xilonen’s signature weapon, Peak Patrol Song, also grants the team around 25% extra DMG.
Adding Xilonen to this team removes Elemental reactions from it, which affects Neuvillette’s first Ascension Passive. However, it’s still possible to maximize his stacks through the Hydro Crystallize Reaction since you only need to trigger it three times and each lasts for 30 seconds. On the other hand, once you get the buffs and debuffs ready, Neuvillette will start hitting slightly harder than his previous best team of Furina, Zhongli, and
Kazuha in
Genshin Impact
. Only this time, the team’s buffs and debuffs last longer, allowing Neuvillette to stay longer on the field.

Neuvillette + Furina + Kazuha + Zhongli
This Neuvillette team is working perfectly to maximize his damage while protecting him. Neuvillette and Furina are arguably the
best pairs in
Genshin Impact
. What makes them so special is their synergy together. Furina wants her team to lose and gain HP in order to maximize her buff while Neuvillette’s mechanics greatly drop his own HP and then fully heal him back, instantly getting the most out of what Furina can offer without a healer.
Since Neuvillette will be attacking enemies with his Charged attacks non-stop, he should not be interrupted, and that’s where Zhongli can be useful. Not only does
Zhongli provide a perfect shield
for Neuvillette, but he also shred enemies' RES from around Neuvillette, providing further buffs to the Iudex of Fontaine. Finally, Kazuha doesn’t need any introduction as he will collect enemies in one spot, massively reduce their resistance, and greatly increase Neuvillette’s Hydro DMG. This Neuvillette team in
Genshin Impact
is, so far, one of the best teams in the entire game.

Neuvillette + Sucrose + Lisa + Ororon
In this F2P Neuvillette team, Lisa, Ororon, and Sucrose all work together to trigger and maximize every

As the only source of Hydro, it only makes sense to keep Neuvillette on the field the longest to keep applying his Hydro Element, in addition to his high beam DMG.

Lisa and Sucrose both debuff enemies' DEF and RES to help increase every Electro-charged reaction and Neuvillette’s DMG. Build Lisa with 4-pc of Noblesse Oblige to give her a 20% ATK buff and maximize her Elemental Mastery so she can make the most of every Electro-charged reaction she triggers. Not only that, but as a
Catalyst user in
Genshin Impact
, Lisa can wield Hakushin Ring, which further buffs everyone’s Electro and Hydro DMG. As for Sucrose, build her with 4pc of Viridescent Venerer so she can reduce enemies’ resistance to Hydro and Electro every time she triggers a Swirl reaction. Additionally, give her as much Elemental Mastery as possible so she can pass it on to her teammates.
Finally, Ororon shines when his team is built around the Electro-charged reactions since it helps him enter the Nightsoul's Blessing state and that triggers the Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City effect that grants Neuvillette 40% extra DMG. When he enters his Nightsoul's Blessing state, Ororon will start dealing more Electro DMG and triggering more reactions. He can also taunt enemies to enter his Supersonic Oculus, which is very helpful considering both Lisa and Sucrose operate in a fixed area.

Neuvillette + Nahida + Xiangling + Zhongli
Neuvillette's Ascension passive grants him higher Charged Attack damage when a party member triggers Hydro-related reactions, up to three stacks, so Neuvillette
team composition in
Genshin Impact
should include at least two other reactive characters.
Against bosses, this team comp is amazing because:
The party includes three Elements that can react with Hydro
Zhongli provides reliable resistance to interruptions to ensure Neuvillette can Charge Attack smoothly
Nahida will continuously apply Dendro and trigger Elemental reactions as long as her Seeds of Skandha is available
Xiangling is an excellent Pyro enabler and allows Neuvillette to Vaporize some of his hits
The way this team works is by using Zhongli's Skill first, then applying Nahida's Skill to the enemy. Then, cast Xiangling's Skill and Burst to
Burn
the enemy, which allows Neuvillette to Vaporize his Elemental Skill. Next, Charge Attack with the Chief of Justice to apply Hydro, which will make Xiangling's Pyronado trigger Vaporize. Due to Pyronado's prominent Pyro application, the enemy will eventually be affected with Pyro and Burn with Dendro, allowing Neuvillette to Vaporize some of his Charged Attacks in the rotation.
Note that while this team is excellent against bosses or single-target opponents, it can be hard to maintain the
Vaporize idea against hordes of enemies
. Players also should remember not to be too far from the enemy as Pyronado has limited range.

: Dendro enabler, buffer

: Sub-DPS, Pyro enabler

: Shielder, debuffer

While it's not as good against bosses, the Hyperbloom/Electro-Charged combo is better against multiple enemies
.

Baizhu will provide healing and shielding, which allows Neuvillette to Charge Attack without getting interrupted. Then, once the field is filled with Dendro Cores, Raiden Shogun's Skill will apply Electro to trigger Hyperbloom. In the process, she'll also trigger Electro-Charged for Neuvillette's Ascension passive. Finally, Venti should Swirl Hydro to reduce the enemy's Hydro RES and restore Energy for Neuvillette's Burst. The grouping Venti provides will also come in handy for the Iudex's water torrent. Neuvillette can easily aim towards enemies flying in Venti's Vortex, and
both Baizhu and Raiden Shogun will auto-target the enemies
, so players don't have to worry about the opponents being out of range.

Neuvillette + Nahida + Kuki Shinobu + Kazuha
There are many Hyperbloom teams players can use to create a Neuvillette team comp. For example, the
viable Dendro characters are
Nahida
, Baizhu, Dendro MC, Kirara, Yaoyao, and Collei
. As for Electro, people can use
Kuki Shinobu, Raiden Shogun, Yae Miko, or Dori
. For the final slot, it can be either Anemo or Geo, but if the team has enough reactions to maximize Neuvillette's passive, then a second Hydro to increase HP is also a good option. Examples of units Travelers can use include
Zhongli, Albedo, Lynette, Kazuha, Sayu, Venti, Sucrose, Kokomi, and Mona
.
In this example, Kuki Shinobu is the one triggering Hyperbloom with Nahida enabling Dendro. Since Neuvillette is self-sufficient and can heal himself easily, the ex-Shrine Maiden's healing is not too important, but will still come in handy if players take damage with other units. Just remember that this team doesn't have a shielder, so Neuvillette must be positioned properly to avoid interruptions when using his Charged Attack. Meanwhile,
Kazuha will debuff the enemy's Hydro RES
, buff Neuvillette's Hydro DMG, and group the enemies all at the same time. Unlike the Venti team where Raiden Shogun can easily reach inside the Anemo Archon's Burst, Kuki's range is more limited, so Kazuha is a great replacement.

Best Teams for Neuvillette
Neuvillette Works Best in a Hypercarry or Taser Team
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Hypercarry Lunar-Charged Team
- With the addition of Lunar-Charged, it has been the best reaction to use with Neuvillette for boosting his damage.
- Furina can still be used to buff Neuvillette's damage but Columbina can be used if you want to buff Ineffa's damage.
- Similar comp to his Hypercarry team, but Ineffa replaces one of the supports.
2nd
▼ Freeze Team
- Escoffier and Citlali's release made Neuvillette's Freeze team better.
- Escoffier-Furina duo allows Furina to reach her buff cap easily.
3rd
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- Lauma can be used along with another shred support to boost Hydro DMG dealt on enemies.
- Hyperbloom DMG is Dendro and is buffed by Lauma's Dendro shred.
Neuvillette is a Hydro DPS that works better as a Hypercarry with characters who can buff his damage or act as Sub-DPS and support. Lunar-Charged is the best reaction for team damage that works with Neuvillette, only requiring Ineffa or Columbina with an Electro Sub-DPS.

Neuvillette's Hypercarry team can now use only one shred support and add Ineffa for shielding and additional damage via Lunar-Charged DMG. Her ability to buff EM also helps in Neuvillette's reaction damage.
Rotation Of Hypercarry Lunar-Charged Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out.
4
Switch to Ineffa and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Neuvillette and use his Skill then Burst
6
Start dealing damage with his Charged Attacks
7
Use his Skill or Burst to refresh the Sourcewater Droplets
8
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire

Rotation Of Hyperbloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Neuvillette and use his Skill then Burst to apply Hydro
2
Switch to Sucrose and use her Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Lauma and use her Burst then Skill (Hold)
4
Switch to Fischl and use her Skill or Burst
5
Switch back to Neuvillette and start dealing damage with his Charged Attacks
6
Use his Skill or Burst to refresh the Sourcewater Droplets
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire

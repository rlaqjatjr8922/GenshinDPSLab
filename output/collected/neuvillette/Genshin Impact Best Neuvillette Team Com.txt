Genshin Impact Best Neuvillette Team Comp
Here is a Genshin Impact team guide article on how to build Neuvillette team comp including details of the team members and free-to-play team.
Neuvillette Breakdown
Neuvillette
is a character that mostly deals DMG on his Charged ATK which scales on his HP%. Since he has a lot of AoE abilities he is very suitable in any team.
Here is some team build that is suitable for him:
Neuvillette F2P - Electro Charged Team Comp
This free-to-play team is a great option for a player who doesn't want to spend Primogem or a newbie who doesn't have any 5-star characters. Even though, this is a F2P team you still can maximize his potential as well. This team focuses on maximizing Hyperbloom reaction DMG by having Neuvillette as Main DPS to react with Yao Yao who can apply Dendro to enemies and Beidou to apply Electro to trigger Hyperbloom.
Beidou
She is a Sub DPS who can apply massive electro DMG which can trigger bloom to create hyper bloom. She also increases Neuvillette's resistance interruption and works really well with his charge ATK.
Yaoyao
Provide healing for Neuvillete since she is low on HP after using charge ATK and provide shields for allies as well as apply Dendro to trigger bloom. Using 4 sets of Tenacity will boost DMG for the whole team.
Kuki
Increase DMG of Hyperbloom and also apply electro to trigger Hyperbloom.
Neuvillette Taser Team Comp
This team comp focuses on maximizing Taser DMG by using Neuvillette and Beidou to create Electro Charge and Kazuha to support the team.
Zhongli
Provide a safe environment for the team by creating a shield which is great for Neuvillette to maintain his HP. Using 4 sets of Tenacity will boost DMG for the whole team.
Kazuha
Provide DMG buff to all allies, reduce EM resistance, and group up enemies which makes it easier to create Electro Charge and Neuvillete to apply Charged ATK.
Neuvillette Freeze Team Comp
This team comp focuses on continuously creating frost reactions from Ayaka's Cryo burst DMG and Hydro from Neuvillette.
Ayaka
Her EM Burst can apply AoE Cryo DMG with the help of Kazuha which makes it easier to use Neuvillette Charged ATK to create a freeze reaction.
Neuvillette Vaporize Team Comp
This team comp focuses on continuously creating frost reactions from Ganyu's Cryo and Hydro from Neuvillette.
Xiangling
Her EM burst can apply massive Pyro DMG as well as apply Pyro enemies around her to react with Neuvillette which creates a Vaporize reaction.
Bennett
Bennett Ult can buff Allies ATK DMG boost as well as keep the entire team survive with healing abilities.
End of Best Neuvillette Team Comp.

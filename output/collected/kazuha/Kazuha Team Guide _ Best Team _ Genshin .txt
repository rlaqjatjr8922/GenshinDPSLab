Kazuha Team Overview
This page provides the best Kazuha Team information. Kazuha is a Support DPS in most situations.
You can also swap the position of Kazuha and Sucrose to make your own best team.
From S tier to A tier, Kazuha best team can be formed in different ways from the Elemental RES team to the Swirl Team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

In the (C6) scenario, Clorinde gains built-in damage reduction and damage boost, without needing Shield or healer support.

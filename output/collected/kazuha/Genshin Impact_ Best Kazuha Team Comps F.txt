In this lineup, Kazuha takes on both the main DPS and debuffer role with the Venerer set. He gains damage by amplifying the element he Swirls. Furthermore, he also boosts Ganyu and Klee's damage with his innate support skill.
Most Kazuha team comps DPS requires C6 to actually deal damage.
While Ganyu is one of the
best DPS in the game
, this lineup only uses her as a Cryo enabler so that you can trigger Melt with Klee. Her Elemental Burst is a great damage booster for both Klee and Kazuha. Klee is the sub DPS who increases Kazuha's Pyro damage via her elemental burst.
Bennett is the support of this lineup, providing attack bonus, healing, and elemental resonance.

With Hu Tao, Bennett, and Xingqiu being a Vaporize team, Kazuha is the perfect character for buff support and sub-DPS. His ability boosts damage for the whole team and its crowd control effect allows players to deal damage more effectively.
Kazuha support team comps can boost the elemental damage of other characters to an absurd level.
In this lineup, Hu Tao is the main damage dealer, with Xingqiu being the enabler. Bennett is again the main support, providing healing and additional damage for all characters.

This is the absolute simplest amongst Kazuha team comps if you are a new player and do not have many characters to choose from. Amber, Kaeya, and Barbara are all free and can be acquired by playing the story.

Lastly, Lisa is the sub DPS of the team, whose Elemental Burst spread Electro allowing Kazuha to deal more physical damage.

Kazuha's skills
are geared toward supporting. He is generally a buff + CC character unless you managed to unlock his last constellation, Crimson Momiji. Outside of that, Kazuha DPS is pretty lackluster, being an Anemo character. Below is the full effect of Crimson Momiji:
After using Chihayaburu or Kazuha Slash, Kaedehara Kazuha gains an Anemo Infusion for 5s. Additionally, each point of Elemental Mastery will increase the DMG dealt by Kaedehara Kazuha's Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attack by 0.2%.
Getting 5-star characters to max constellation costs a lot of real money, therefore, the vast majority of Genshin players wouldn't have him maxed. Because of that, only very lucky players or whales would be able to use Kazuha as a DPS character.

Kazuha (Kaedehara Kazuha) is a 5-star Anemo character that wields a Sword in Genshin Impact. He is an excellent support character who excels at crowd control, buffing elemental damage for his team through Swirl reactions, and dealing respectable Anemo damage himself. His Elemental Skill allows him to pull enemies in and launch himself upwards for a plunging attack, while his Burst creates a large Anemo field that can absorb another element. Lore-wise, Kaedehara Kazuha is a wandering samurai from Inazuma with a gentle and poetic disposition.

What Kind Of Character Is Kazuha?
Kazuha Is An Elemental DMG Boost Support Character
Kazuha is an amazing character that greatly increases the DMG of swirl-able elements while providing crowd-control. With Viridescent Venerer,
Kazhua can both buff the team and shred resistance of enemies
. At C2, he can also provide Elemental Mastery boost which will further increase the overall DMG of the team.
Rate Kaedehara Kazuha!
Roles & Playstyle Of Kazuha and Its Strengths and Weakness
Swirl Support Kazuha
Kazuha's intended role is as a Swirl Support that utilizes
Viridescent Venerer
to increase the team's Elemental Damage. He can also help in re-applying Elements for reactions if the sub-DPS character has slower Application compared to the DPS character.
Pros and Cons of Support Kazuha
Pros
- Can be used in any PECH Element team.
- Great for grouping up enemies while also triggering Swirl on them if they were Affected by an Element beforehand.
- Elemental Burst has big AoE for reapplying Elements on enemies.
Cons
- Has no sustain ability and is a pure damage-boosting support.
- Can be tricky to boost multiple Elements' damage at the same time without precise positioning.
Best Build for Kaedehara Kazuha
Kazuha's most important stat for Support is Elemental Mastery. Depending on your weapon choice, he may not need to be built with more Energy Recharge but you can also prioritize it if you want to use his Elemental Burst every rotation.

Team Rotation of Kaedehara Kazuha and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for Kaedehara Kazuha
Basic Rotation For Kazuha
1. Activate other party members' skills
2. Tap Elemental Skill, then plunge
3. Use Elemental Burst
4. Swap to your On-Field DPS to execute their damage combo
5. Repeat rotation
Kazuha ideally wants to be last in the support rotation due to
Viridescent Venerer
having a short buff uptime.
You also want to make sure to apply your On-Field DPS or your Sub-DPSes' element on the target so Kazuha is able to Swirl it. This is to ensure he provides his sizeable Elemental DMG buff to the correct character.
Kazuha's gameplay is very straightforward.
Usually if grouping is not necessary, you will want to use the tap version of his Skill over the Hold version, since it uses less time and results in a shorter cooldown.
Kazuha Skill Grouping Demonstration
The Hold version of Kazuha's Skill has decent reach, provided the enemies aren't too spread out.
The Skill also leaves behind a small vortex that keeps the targets suspended in a cluster and will continue to lightly pull enemies towards it for a moment, which further strengthens his grouping ability.
Note that like other Anemo grouping skills, Kazuha's only applies to enemies that aren't classified as Heavy, such as the
Kairagi
from Inazuma.
Basics Of Using Kazuha
Kazuha works similarly to other Anemo Supports. His only major difference is the powerful Elemental reapplication ability his Elemental Burst has.
Poetics of Fuubutsu, the Core of Kazuha's Kit
After unlocking his Level 40, Kazuha gains a Passive Ability that gives him the ability to buff Elemental DMG Bonus. Along with his compatibility to Viridescent Venerer, he can massively increase the Elemental Damage dealt by the team.
Use Elemental Skill (Tap) to Swirl and Buff Teammates
Kazuha's tap Elemental Skill is the fastest way to trigger Swirl. After unlocking his Level 40, you can unlock his Passive Ability that buffs Elemental DMG Bonus to the Swirled Element.
Elemental Skill (Hold) For Crowd Control
Kazuha's held Elemental Skill makes him drag enemies for a longer time then triggers Swirl after his Plunging Attack. But most of the time, only the Tap version is needed if you want faster rotations.
Elemental Burst for Reapplying Swirled Elements
Kazuha's Elemental Burst, "Kazuha Slash," is best for reapplying Elements in a big AoE. Similar to how Dendro is used to continuously reapply Pyro with Burning, Kazuha Slash is great for reapplying any Swirled Elements for reactions.

Uses Of Kazuha's Elemental Skill
▲Gathers enemies and deals powerful Plunge Damage.
Quick & Easy To Use Crowd Control Skill
Kazuha's Elemental Skill is a great Crowd Control ability since it can group enemies easily and is on a relatively short cooldown. The first vacuum
reduces the resistance to interruption of enemies
greatly allowing even huge monsters to be pulled. A
Tap & Hold Changes The Duration & Damage
You have the option of simply tapping or holding Kazuha's Elemental Skill. They both have the same effect, but
holding Kazuha's Elemental Skill will expand the area of the vortex
and the damage of the Skill itself.
Elemental Skill Is Good For Quick Swirl
If Kazuha swirls an element, it would give raw Elemental DMG bonus of the swirled element to the team.  Together with the
Viridescent Venerer
, Kazuha will be able to provide Buff and debuff for the team.
Also Good For Traveling & Farming
Elemental Skill of Kazuha can gather drops from enemies for easy gathering. You can also use it mid-air to reach areas that is normally not reachable by other characters.
Uses Of Kazuha's Elemental Burst
Mainly Used To Inflict Additional Elemental DMG & Consistent Swirls
Kazuha's Elemental Burst is mainly used to inflict the element absorbed. The Burst
absorbs an element and continuously apply the absorbed element to enemies
. This is great for comps that requires frequent infliction of certain elements like Vaporize teams.
At C2, It Provides Elemental Mastery Boost
At C2, Kazuha's Elemental Burst provides 200 Elemental Mastery to characters inside the field and Kazuha himself. This becomes an amazing buff for the team's reaction damage.
Should You Pull for Kaedehara Kazuha's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Prioritize Kaedehara Kazuha's Constellations
Signature VS Constellations

Freedom-Sworn

C2 Provides High Value But Not Necessary
The bulk of Kazuha's value as a support is from his "Poetics of Fuubutsu" passive and the Elemental DMG bonus it gives in accordance to the Swirled elements and his own Elemental Mastery.
With this in mind, his C2 becomes his most impactful constellation before C6 not only because it gives Elemental Mastery to the on-field character inside his Burst, but also due to Kazuha also receiving the same amount.
This results in a higher Elemental DMG% buff from "Poetics of Fuubutsu" and enables Kazuha to provide free Elemental Mastery; a feature that isn't available in his C0 kit.
The added addition of his C2, in combination with his effective usage of the
Viridescent Venerer
set and his base value from C0 result in a sizeable amount of buffs for your on-field carry. which amounts to higher damage overall.
Vertical investment into Kazuha's constellations comes down to personal preference and funds.
If you really want to, and if you aren't going all the way to C6 to run him as an on-field DPS, C2 will net you positive results in teams that would greatly benefit from what Kazuha brings to the table.
However, note that his C2 is hardly required and you'll find that his baseline at C0 is going to add value to your account regardless.
4-star Swords are Just as Good
If you only have enough budget for either C1 or Freedom-Sworn, choosing to go for his Signature Weapon should give the more significant increase
, since his C1 doesn't offer much in actual practice. Freedom-Sworn will be better than the 4-star alternatives, but Kazuha with any of his other weapon options such as the
Sacrificial Sword
,
Favonius Sword
,
Xiphos' Moonlight
and even the craftable
Iron Sting
will still perform relatively well, and you probably do not need the Freedom-Sworn's buffs to clear content.
With that said, it'd also be ideal to consider allocating the funds for other characters instead
, since neither options provide as much impact as Kazuha's C2.

Who Is Kaedehara Kazuha?
A Wandering Samurai From Inazuma
Kaedehara Kazuha is a mysterious figure as a wandering samurai who hails from Inazuma. He fled his nation after experiencing a tremendous loss during the Raiden Shogun's Vision Hunt Decree.
Currently Traveling With The Crux
He now travels with The Crux, an armed fleet from Liyue. Beidou, its leader, befriended him and took him in. His relationship with the crew started off rocky, but now they see themselves as comrades.

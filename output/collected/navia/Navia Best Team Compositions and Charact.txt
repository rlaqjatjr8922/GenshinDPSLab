Navia's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Navia team composition guide and character synergy 
overview for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams 
for Navia, alongside recommended character pairings that help 
maximize the value of her Crystal Shrapnel mechanic.
Navia is a Main DPS who relies heavily on regular
Crystallize
triggers to deal damage. Because of this, it is very important to play her alongside 
at least one or two characters from
Cryo
,
Pyro
,
Electro
, or
Hydro
. This 
ensures your team can continuously trigger Crystallize, allowing Navia to build 
Shrapnel stacks quickly for massive Skill damage.

Geo Supports
Currently, there are two extremely strong Geo supports for Navia.

's Elemental Skill,
Yohual's Scratch

Linnea
is a Lunar character, giving Navia's team access to the

      provides a 30% Geo RES Shred through her 1st Ascension Passive,
Field Observation Notes

Hydro Supports
These are some of the best Hydro supports if you want to play a Hydro-focused 
      team with Navia.
Furina
provides up to a 75% DMG bonus 
      through her Burst, Let the People Rejoice, alongside consistent off-field Hydro 
      application and damage for Crystallize triggers.
Yelan
performs a similar job; her buffs are slightly lower, but her personal damage is higher.
Columbina
provides Lunar Crystallize reaction access to Navia's 
      team, and performs exceptionally well when paired with another Geo support like
Illuga
or
Linnea

Geo characters may not be many in
Genshin Impact
, but they surely are some of the most impactful, and that also applies to Navia. The Commander-in-Chief of the Spina di Rosula is a strong damage dealer
who can smoothly take the role of the main DPS
.
While her Elemental Burst triggers an endless barrage of cannon bombardments, the secret to her damage is her Elemental Skill, especially when Navia fully stacks her Crystal Shrapnel so her Crystalshot will delete opponents from the field. To maximize her Elemental Skill damage, it's important to compose the
best Navia team comp in
Genshin Impact
.
Navia + Zhongli + Xiangling + Bennett
This is easily one of Navia's
best team compositions in
Genshin Impact
. As someone who needs Crystallized reaction, Navia desperately needs an enabler in her team. Not only that, she also depends on her ATK to deal damage, which makes
Bennett
a perfect Support for her. As for the enabling,
Xiangling
is a strong contender because of her off-field Pyro, which can help Navia endlessly trigger Crystallized reactions thanks to her lack of ICD.
When included in Navia team comp, both
Xiangling and Bennett create a synergy together
. Combat-wise, Xiangling’s Elemental Burst Pyronado Snapshot when you cast it on Bennett’s field. Stat-wise, both Pyro users activate the P
yro Resonance, which will buff everyone's ATK by 25%
.
The team can also benefit from another Resonance when pairing another Geo unit with Navia. First, the Geo Resonance will increase the Crystallized shields’ strength by 15%. Navia needs this extra shield strength since the Elemental Shards are what keep
Nighttime Whispers In The Echoing Woods
' and
Verdict's
effect active. Additionally, the Resonance will directly affect Navia’s damage by increasing it by 15% and decreasing enemies’ Geo resistance by 20%, which is incredible for
any Geo user in
Genshin Impact
. When it comes to who would be best to join Navia’s team, it is, without a doubt, the Geo Archon himself, since he can further debuff enemies and buff Navia’s ATK with his
Tenacity of the Millelith
set.
Team Roles
Navia
:
Main DPS
Zhongli
:
Shielder, buffer, debuffer, Geo enabler
Xiangling
:
Sub-DPS, Pyro enabler
Bennett
:
Healer, buffer
Navia + Xingqiu + C6 Fischl + Bennett
Navia can operate as a main DPS and count on her Normal attacks to deal damage. That opens up a strong possibility for her to take the driver role and lead her team’s off-field Elements and mix them with hers.
Xingqiu
and
Fischl
can greatly help her achieve that role since they are some of the
best Elemental enablers in
Genshin impact
. With this trio, you’ll be able to continuously trigger the Electro-charged alongside Crystallized reactions, and that will allow Navia to build up her Crystal Shrapnel stacks more effectively.
In this Navia team comp, the
total team damage is split among Fischl, Xingqiu, and her, so although Navia deals lower damage, the team's DPS is still incredible
. Of course, as for Bennett, he’s the unit that should never leave Navia's side, since he’s the only one who can take her damage to the next level by laying his Burst on the field.
Team Roles
Navia:
Main DPS, driver
Xingqiu:
Sub-DPS, Hydro enabler
C6 Fischl:
Sub-DPS, Electro enabler
Bennett:
Healer, buffer
Navia + Furina + Bennett + Kokomi
Wanting to maximize Navia’s damage comes with many complications. To do so, you must add
Bennett
and
Furina
since they’re
the best buffers in
Genshin Impact
. Activating both Furina and Bennett’s Elemental Burst will surely allow
Navia
to hit some insane numbers. But, since
Navia needs a strong enabler in her team
, both Support units fail to apply their Elements effectively, which will notably reduce her damage. Additionally, to make the most of Furina’s buff, on one hand, the team must constantly keep healing as Furina’s Elemental Skill keeps draining the team’s HP. On the other hand, Bennett isn’t capable of maintaining a strong ongoing healing since he only heals the active character to a certain point.
Solving those two issues allows Navia to enjoy both amazing buffs without any limitations. To do so, you must
include a strong healer and enabler
to increase Furina’s buff and help Navia stack for a devastating Elemental Skill. A healer like
Jean
is great since she can create the Sunfire synergy with Bennett and continuously trigger Pyro Swirl.
Kuki
is also viable since she has a strong Electro application and decent healing. However, the
best healer to add to this
Genshin Impact
Navia team should be
Kokomi
. She has a wide Hydro application and impressive healing abilities. You can also give her
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
and
Tenacity of the Millelith
to buff Navia even further.
Team Roles
Navia:
Main DPS
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Hydro enabler, buffer
Kokomi:
Healer, Hydro enabler, buffer
Bennett:
Healer, buffer

Genshin Impact Tighnari Guide: Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
January 29, 2024
Tips
Share:
Tighnari is the
first 5-star Dendro character in Sumeru
. The ranger fights with a
bow
and is mostly played as a
DPS
. He will be able to inflict a lot of
single-target
damage with his charged attacks. We explain in detail the
Genshin Impact Tighnari gameplay
with this
complete guide
. Thanks to our tips and tricks, you'll know which artifacts and weapons to equip to give him the
best build
.
How to get Tighnari in Genshin Impact ?
Tighnari is a 5 star character, you can only theoretically summon him when he is featured in a
Character Event Wish
. But starting with the
Genshin Impact 3.1 update
, you will have a small chance to
unlock Tighnari
by summoning on the
Standard Wish
or by losing your 50/50 on a Character Event Wish.
However, you will have more chance to summon Tighnari in Genshin Impact on his
Verdant Strider
Wish available
until September 9, 2022
at 6pm.
Tighnari constellations
From Genshin Impact 3.1 version, Tighnari will be part of Standard Wish, so its constellations will be easier to unlock.
With her C1, you can increase the critical rate of Tighnari's charged attacks. His C2 will give him a Dendro damage bonus, while the C4 can boost the elemental mastery of your entire team. The C6 will significantly reduce the charge time of Tighnari's charged arrows.
How to play Tighnari in Genshin Impact ?
Tighnari's gameplay
revolves around his
loaded arrows
, which are the ones he will use to do the most damage. However (unless you have his C6), it will take you more than 3 seconds to get to the 2
nd
charge and shoot a
flower arrow
. This one inflicts Dendro damage and generates 4 self-guided arrows that rush to the enemy.
Fortunately,
Vijnana-Phala Mine
, Tighnari's elemental attack, reduces the charge time of the next 3 floral arrows by 2.4 seconds.
For optimal rotation, after activating your support characters' abilities, always start by casting Tighnari's elemental attack in Genshin Impact. This will also inflict area Dendro damage and distract enemies. Use this opportunity to fire your 3 loaded arrows.
After firing a floral arrow, the
Keen Sight
passive increases Tighnari's Elemental Mastery by 50 points for 4 seconds.
This is the perfect time to unleash Tighnari's elemental rampage:
Fashioner’s Tanglevine Shaft
. You'll be able to cast it regularly as it only costs 40 energy points and takes 12 seconds to recharge.
Tighnari is best played as a DPS in quick-swap teams. To take advantage of its full potential, increase its normal attack, its elemental rampage, and then its elemental ability.
What is the best equipment for Tighnari?
Tighnari's
Scholarly Blade
passive increases the damage of her charged attack and elemental rampage by 0.06% for each of her mastery points (up to 60% maximum). But for a balanced build, don't just focus on this stat and also up the ATK, critical rate and critical damage.
In this guide we list the
best artifacts and weapons to equip on Genshin Impact Tighnari
.
The best artifacts for Tighnari
Wanderer's Troupe
To maximize Tighnari's damage, we recommend equipping her with the 4-piece
Wanderer's Troupe
set. It increases elemental mastery by 80 points and the damage of charged attacks by 35%.
Deepwood Memories
You can also opt for the
Deepwood Memories
artifact set. The set of 2 pieces of equipment gives a 15% bonus to Dendro damage. With 4 pieces, the Dendro resistance of enemies is lowered by 30% when they are hit by an elemental skill.
If you are playing a Dendro support with Tighnari, it is recommended that you equip this artifact set on him to favor a more offensive set on Tighnari.
Gilded Dreams
The third choice is the 4-piece
Gilded Dreams
equipment set. It boosts elemental mastery and increases ATK by 18% for each member of the same elemental type as the wearer. The elemental mastery is increased by 50 points for each member of the same type.
For the statistics we recommend :
Hourglass: elementary mastery or ATK% (if you already have a lot of mastery);
Cut : bonus damage Dendro ;
Crown: critical rate or critical damage.
For secondary statistics
CRIT Rate;
Critical damage ;
Elemental Mastery;
ATK;
Energy recharge.
The best weapons for Tighnari
The Hunter's Path
is the best weapon to equip on Tighnari in Genshin Impact. The bow increases its
CRIT
and elemental damage. With the passive, the damage of charged attacks is boosted to 160% of elemental mastery for 10 seconds after a charged attack hits an enemy.
The Hunter's Path (5★)
But other 5-star weapons are also possible: the
Amos' Bow
(bonus of ATK), the
Aqua Simulacra
(bonus of DMG critical) or the
Polar Star
(bonus of critical rate).
Polar star (5★)
Aqua Simulacra (5★)
Amos' Bow (5★)
If you don't have these 5 star weapons, you can choose the following 4 star bows:
The Viridescent Hunt (4★)
Prototype Crescent (4★)
The Stringless (4★)
The Viridescent Hunt
: The bow in the battle pass increases the
critical rate
and normal and charged attacks have a 50% chance to create a cyclone that attracts enemies when hit;
Prototype Crescent
: this bow is made by the blacksmith and boosts
ATK
%. If a charged attack hits the enemy's weak point, it increases the movement speed by 10% and the ATK by 36% for 10 seconds;
The Stringless
: This weapon focuses on
elemental control
and increases the damage done with elemental skills.
Genshin Impact Tighnari's ascension and skill materials
To
raise Tighnari to level 90 in Genshin Impact,
you will need:
18
Fungal Spores
, 30
Luminescent Pollen
and 36
Crystalline Cyst Dusts
. You will find these on the
Fungus
(you will also need them for the skills);
168
Nilotpala Lotus
: they grow in the rivers of Sumeru. Locate them directly with the
interactive map of Sumeru ;
1 Sliver, 9 Fragments, 9 Chunks and 6 Gemstones of Nagatus Emerald as well as 46
Majestic Hooked Beaks
to loot on the
Jadeplume Terrorshroom
.
To increase Tighnari's abilities, you will need:
Teaching of Praxis
,
Guide to Praxis
, and
Philisophies of Praxis
. These items are available Monday, Thursday and Sunday in the
Steeple of Ignorance
dungeon;
From
The Meaning of Aeons
(from level 7), you will get them by facing
Magatsu Mitake Narukami no Mikoto
.
Which teams does Tighnari play in?
To maximize Tighnari's damage, we recommend that you play it with
Electro
characters. You will greatly increase the Dendro damage with the
Propagation
reaction and the Electro damage with
Stimulation
.
Among the
best Dendro teams
are:
Tighnari / Collei or the
Dendro Traveler
(in sub-DPS) /
Fischl
in support and Electro applicator /
Shinobu
(care and Electro resonance)
Tighnari /
Raiden
or Fischl /
Yae Miko
/
Jean
Zhongli
or
Kokomi
If you choose Kokomi or Barbara, you can also do Exuberance. You'll create
Dendro Cores
with the Dendro and Hydro elements, then turn them into projectiles that rush at the enemy with Electro. You can also play a single Electro character to place an Anemo hero.
Tighnari / Fischl / Zhongli /
Kazuha
or
Sucrose
: you will lower the electro resistance of the enemies with the character Anemo. The latter will also gather the enemies.
How old and how big is Tighnari from Genshin impact ?
Tighnari is a scholar from Sumeru, yet he chose to be a forest ranger in the Avidya forest. He is said to be
about 25 years
old and is a medium-sized character. He would be
1m62
tall (even if he seems taller with his long ears that you want to scratch).
That's it for our tips and tricks in this
Genshin Impact Tighnari
guide. In summary, this is a good
single target DPS
. Even if you're not an ace at aiming, its gameplay will be easy to pick up. With an adapted build and good elemental reactions, it will be able to do good damage in the abyss on big bosses. However, he will have more difficulty in multi-target, unless you play him with an Anemo hero. As a bonus, Tighnari has another asset: it shows on your mini-map all the Sumeru products around you. This comes in
handy when farming resources
in this new region.

Tighnari Team Overview
This Page provides the best Tighnari Team information. Tighnari is a DPS in most of the situation.
From SS tier to A tier, Tighnari best team can be formed in different ways including Catalyze, Burning, Bloom, Spread, Aggravate, Burgeon and Hyperbloom team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

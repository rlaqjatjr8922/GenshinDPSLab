1.
Tighnari Main DPS Build
Tighnari's primary method of damage comes from his Charge attacks and his 
Elemental Burst. He is quite sought after for
Quicken
and
Aggravate
teams with great
Elemental Mastery scaling and the ability to apply
Dendro
quite consistently with his abilities. His Energy 
Recharge requirements are quite straight forward with 150% ER for a single 
skill per burst. Tighnari is incredibly flexible and a great choice for a 
ranged DPS.

2.
Combos and How to Play Tighnari
Tighnari is a very straight forward ranged user focused around Charged 
Attacks with limited roles for anything other than a
Dendro
DPS. Similar to
Ganyu
's charge attacks, Tighnari has two 
stages of his Charged Attack. The first phase is a standard Dendro charge shot, 
similar to any other bow user in the game. His second stage Charged Attack 
fires normally with a Dendro strike of damage, but then transforms into 
multiple projectiles to home in on the enemy. These additional projectiles 
will still track to a nearby enemy even if the initial hit is missed. The 
drawback to his second staged Charged Attack is the delay while you prepare 
your shot. However, this is where Tighnari's abilities offer a fantastic 
synergy with his charged attacks.
His Elemental Skill,
Vijnana-Phala Mine
, creates a 
field of
Dendro
energy that produces illusions to taunt the 
enemies inside the AoE, taking the focus off of Tighnari. It also grants him 
the Vijnana Suffusion effect which grants him a rapid charge time for three 
of his charged attacks. This buff is represented by green orbs that float 
around him and stay with him even after he is swapped out, helping to setup 
some great
Elemental Reactions
with his teammates before having 
to use his rapid charged attacks. This is particular effective within
Quicken
and
Aggravate
teams. 
His Elemental Burst,
Fashioner's Tanglevine Shaft
is a series 
of 6 Dendro attacks that can be focused on a single target or multiple 
enemies, allowing for some flexibility with it's use.
3.
Tighnari's Best Team Comps
Tighnari works well with quickswap teams, focusing on teammates who have 
short on-field time with abilities that can stay on-field or buff Tighnari's 
damage. The ideal team will be a combination of other
Electro
and
Dendro
aligned characters to support him while he fights 
on-field. Tighnari does not synergize well with characters who require 
extensive on-field time or require Normal attacks to trigger reactions.

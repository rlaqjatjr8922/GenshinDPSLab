Diona's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Very good Cryo Battery.
Easy to build since she scales mostly on HP.
Good support, her shield is strong if you invest in it.
Powerful C6 which buffs the team's Elemental mastery.
Weaknesses
Low damage, to be mostly played as an off-field Support.
Very slow Cryo application.
Requires a lot of Energy.

For Diona's Support build,
Sacrificial Bow
is the best choice. It 
provides Diona with some nice Energy Recharge and the high possibility to reset her shield, 
allowing her to put her shield up more often. The other weapon choices will either cover 
her Energy Recharge requirements, or increase her HP, which will increase her 
shield and heal efficiency!
For this Support build, you want to use
Noblesse Oblige
for Diona.
It provides solid buffs for her teammates. Combined with the
Sacrificial Bow
,

already wears
Noblesse Oblige
, you can go for a 2pc
Tenacity of the Millelith
and 
2pc

for some HP and Energy Recharge to buff her shields.

Recommended Endgame Build Stats for Diona
Build recommendations assume a "decently built" level of artifact rolls alongside 
Diona's best weapon. Using other weapons may increase or decrease certain stats relative to these recommendations.
HP:
25,000 - 30,000
Healing Bonus:
>20%
ER:
160 - 180%
Diona's support build focuses on her shielding and healing, which both scale on HP, so 
you want to focus HP stats. Diona needs a lot of Energy Recharge as well. You want to 
aim for around 170% or more if you want to Burst every rotation.
3.
Diona Combos and How to Play
Diona is mainly used as an off-field support, which means she will mostly only be 
brought on-field to apply shields using
Icy Paws
. You will want to 
hold the skill down to activate the shield in order to get the additional 75% damage 
absorption bonus. Holding the skill will create a much better shield.
When you bring Diona on the field, you will also want to use her Burst
Signature Mix
before switching to another character. Her burst puts down a
Cryo
AoE that will deal damage and heal your
characters at the same time, if you stay in the Area of Effect. The AoE is also great for 
triggering Elemental Reactions.
4.
Diona's Best Team Comps
As we have previously mentioned, Diona is best used as a support for her shield and heal.
This gives her various team possibilities, as she can fit into basically any team 
that can have the use of a shield. We will focus on team examples that revolve around also
using Diona's Burst to trigger elemental reactions, while she shields and heals your 
other teammates.

Crowns of Insight
The Crown of Insight can be obtained by upgrading several key elements: reaching level 11 on the Frostbearing Tree, upgrading the Sacred Sakura’s Favor to at least level 15, advancing the Sumeru Tree of Dreams to at least level 5, enhancing the Fountain of Lucine to at least level 5, leveling up Tablet of Tona to at least level 5 and participating in Genshin Impact’s various limited-time events.

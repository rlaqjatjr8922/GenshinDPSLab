Best Weapons for Sucrose in Genshin Impact
A Thousand Floating Dreams (Image via HoYoverse)
Sucrose is one of the best four-stars in the game, due to her support role. She can use a number of great five-star and four-star weapons. These can either be Elemental Mastery weapons or Energy Recharge ones.
Best 5-star Weapons for Sucrose in Genshin Impact
The best five-star weapons for Sucrose are mentioned below, along with their reasons:
Weapon
Reason
Stat
Effect
A Thousand Floating Dreams
The main Elemental Mastery stat is great. It increases the rest of the party’s Elemental Mastery as well, which helps enhance Sucrose’s support role.
Elemental Mastery 265
Party members other than the equipping character will provide the equipping character with buffs based on whether their Elemental Type is the same as the latter or not. If their Elemental Types are the same, increase Elemental Mastery by 32.
If not, increase the equipping character’s DMG Bonus from their Elemental Type by 10%. Each of the aforementioned effects can have up to three stacks.
Additionally, all nearby party members other than the equipping character will have their Elemental Mastery increased by 40. Multiple such effects from multiple such weapons can stack.
Nightweaver’s Looking Glass
This weapon is similar to A Thousand Floating Dreams in the sense that it increases the equipping character’s and the rest of the team’s Elemental Mastery.
Elemental Mastery 265
When the equipping character’s Elemental Skill deals Hydro or Dendro DMG, they will gain Prayer of the Far North: Elemental Mastery is increased by 60 for 4.5s.
When nearby party members trigger Lunar-Bloom reactions, the equipping character gains New Moon Verse: Elemental Mastery is increased by 60 for 10s.
When both Prayer of the Far North and New Moon Verse are in effect, all nearby party members’ Bloom DMG is increased by 120%, their Hyperbloom and Burgeon DMG are increased by 80%, and their Lunar-Bloom DMG is increased by 40%.
This effect cannot stack. The aforementioned effects can be triggered even if the equipping character is off-field.
Note:
The above weapon effects are at Refinement 1 and level 90.

This is a great weapon to increase your Elemental Mastery while allowing you to use your Elemental Skill twice in a row.

After dealing damage to an opponent with an Elemental Skill, the skill has a 80% chance to end its own CD. Can only occur once every 16s.

Grants a good Elemental Mastery increase, while also converting this stat into an Attack buff for the team.
Elemental Mastery 165
The following effect will trigger every 10s: The equipping character will gain 48% of their Elemental Mastery as bonus ATK for 12s, with nearby party members gaining 30% of this buff for the same duration. Multiple instances of this weapon can allow this buff to stack. This effect will still trigger even if the character is not on the field.
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayer
If you don’t have any of the above weapons, then this can be an option you consider to grant the main DPS of the team a significant Attack buff.
HP 35.2%
When switching characters, the new character taking the field has their ATK increased by 48% for 10s. This effect can only occur once every 20s.

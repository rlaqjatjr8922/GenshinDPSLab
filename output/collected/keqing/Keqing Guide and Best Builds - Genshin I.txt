Keqing's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Has a Low cooldown on her Elemental Skill.
Keqing's Charged attack charges faster than usual.
Great ability to trigger Electro-related reactions such as Aggravate.
High AoE DMG.
Can be useful during exploration due to her Elemental Skill.
Weaknesses
Consumes a lot of Stamina on charged attacks which are part of her combo.
Heavily relies on invested Sup-DPS units to supplement her own DPS output.
Restricted team capabilities.

Electro
on-field DPS 
and Aggravate DPS. Keqing can also be played as a Physical DPS, however, it is important to

are interested nonetheless.

4.
Keqing Combos and How to Play
Keqing is not the easiest character to play, and has a specific rotation and playstyle 
depending on whether you play her as an
Electro
DPS or Physical DPS.
For Electro Keqing you want to follow this specific rotation:
Elemental Skill - Place the Stiletto
Elemental Burst
Reactivate the Stiletto
x1 Normal Attack
x1 Charged Attack
Repeat
Physical Keqing will have a slight alteration to her rotation and goes as follows:
Elemental Skill - Place the Stiletto
Elemental Burst
x1 Charged Attack
x1 Normal Attack
x1 Charged Attack
Repeat

Keqing's Constellations
Almost all of Keqing's constellations are useful, but players are usually
after her C6 to completely invest in her personal damage. However, Keqing
being a Standard banner character, it is not recommended to specifically farm for her 
constellations. She will work fine at C0.

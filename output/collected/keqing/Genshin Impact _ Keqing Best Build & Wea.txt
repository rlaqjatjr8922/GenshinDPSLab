When it comes to building an Electro main DPS, a 2-Piece Thundering Fury and a 2-Piece ATK Artifact will best for her. This will buff her whole kit and it's fairly easy to build.

Keqing Skill Guide & How To Use Keqing
Recommended Rotation For Keqing
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Elemental Skill as opener
2
Activate Elemental Skill again to get short Electo infusion. Spam Normal Attacks until it expires
3
Use Elemental Burst when available
3
Repeat Step 1
Uses Of Keqing's Elemental Skill
Reposition With Second Activation
You can easily reposition upon the second activation of Keqing's Elemental Skill. This can be used as either an opener to engage enemies or as a way to move away from enemies when needed!
Get Short Electro Infusion
Whenever you activate Keqing's Elemental Skill the second time, she will get a brief Electro Infusion. This will last for a few seconds and can be reactivated upon using Keqing's Skill again.
Use Of Keqing's Elemental Burst
Attack Multiple Enemies At A Time
Keqing's Burst is a multi-target attack. It will damage all enemies inside her range, no matter how many they are in the area. It's the only multi-target attack that she has.
Knock Back Enemies Briefly
After her Burst, enemies hit by Keqing's attack will be briefly knocked back. You can use this to disengage briefly from battle or get the enemies mobbing you to move away.
Should You Pull for Keqing's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Constellations Are Nice But Not Necessary
Constellation Priority
C4 > C6
C4 and C6 are Her Best Constellations
As a Standard Banner character, Keqing does not have a designated Signature Weapon. To get her constellations, you would need to wait for a
Chronicled Wish
banner or chance upon her in your rolls on the Standard Banner.
Her strongest constellations start from her C4, which gives a modest Attack increase after triggering Electro-reactions, and C6 that gives her Electro DMG bonus whenever she attacks.
As a general rule, unless you really want to vertically invest into a Standard Banner character, it's best to allocate funds elsewhere, such as in their best weapons and their best supports.
Recommended Pull Priority
Mistsplitter Reforged
> C4 > C6
Mistsplitter Reforged Is Her Best Weapon
Keqing can easily meet the conditions needed to fully stack Mistsplitter Reforged's passive. In addition, this sword is a strong universal weapon overall and can be used by a variety of characters that would appreciate the Crit substat and the Elemental DMG bonus, making it a good pick-up for a majority of accounts.
If you want to be able to upgrade Keqing's performance without having to rely on chance, then getting Mistsplitter Reforged is ideal.

Who Is Keqing?
Yuheng Of Liyue Qixing
Keqing is the Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, meaning she's responsible for land and livelihood management. She's quiet young, but she's very dedicated to her job.
A Known Skeptic
Unlike the rest of Liyue who regard the Adepti and the gods highly, Keqing does not believe in putting your faith in them. She believes that humans are responsible for their own fates.

Faruzan of
Genshin Impact
has a lot of utilities to offer her team. First, with her Elemental Burst, she grants Anemo DMG Bonus while reducing the enemy’s Anemo RES. Other than that, Faruzan also grants a flat DMG Bonus to Anemo hits, scaling with her Base ATK.
If players unlock Faruzan’s Constellation, she’ll eventually get the ability to grant 40% Anemo Crit DMG,
similar to
Genshin Impact
Kujou Sara
. Because of her Anemo-centric kits, the best team comps for Faruzan in
Genshin Impact
heavily revolve around Anemo units.

4
C6 Faruzan + Wanderer + Yun Jin + Bennett
Faruzan is an amazing support for Wanderer after she unlocks all of her
Genshin Impact
Constellations. In this team, players will focus on maximizing Wanderer’s Normal Attack DMG.
Yun Jin will grant a flat DMG Bonus to Scaramouche’s Normal Attacks, while Bennett will provide ATK buff and healing. Using Wanderer’s Skill while affected by Bennett’s Pyro will also cause Pyro Swirl, increasing Wanderer’s ATK by 30%. Here, Bennett can be replaced by other buffers like Candace, Thoma, or even Shenhe. However, note that the team will be missing a healer if players opt for the other characters.
Team Role
Faruzan:
Buffer and debuffer
Wanderer:
Main DPS
Yun Jin:
Buffer
Bennett:
Buffer and healer
3
C6 Faruzan + Shikanoin Heizou + Xingqiu + Fischl
Faruzan is meant to be the dedicated support for Wanderer, but she works great with other Anemo DPS too. With Shikanoin Heizou, Xingqiu, and Fischl, the team will work as a Taser team that focuses on Electro-Charged reactions. Alternatively,
players can also use Xiangling in
Genshin Impact
instead of Fischl for a Vaporize team.
Team Role
Faruzan:
Buffer and debuffer
Shikanoin Heizou:
Main DPS
Xingqiu:
Sub-DPS
Fischl:
Sub-DPS
2
C6 Faruzan + Xiao + Zhongli + Albedo
Anemo-Geo Xiao team is
one of the most meta teams in
Genshin Impact
. Usually, the team consists of Xiao + Jean + Zhongli + Albedo, but here, Jean will be replaced by Faruzan. Faruzan can grant Anemo RES reduction, Anemo DMG Buff, Anemo Crit DMG bonus, and even flat DMG buff, so she’ll prove to be worthy of replacing Jean. However, of course, this means there won’t be any healer on the team. Alternatively, if players don’t mind losing the Geo Resonance, Albedo can be replaced by either Jean or Bennett.
Team Role
Faruzan:
Buffer and debuffer
Xiao:
Main DPS
Zhongli:
Shielder and debuffer
Albedo:
Sub-DPS and Geo Resonance
1
C6 Faruzan + Venti + Wanderer + Bennett
Similar to the first Faruzan team comp, this one also involves Wanderer and Bennett. However, instead of Yun Jin,
players will use Venti for crowd control in
Genshin Impact
. Wanderer has a lot of potential if enemies are grouped together, so the combo of the two can be amazing if the enemies are light and can be pulled into Venti’s vortex. Here, Faruzan will buff both Venti and Wanderer.
If players have Venti at C2 or C6, he will further decrease the enemy’s Anemo RES. Alternatively, if players wish to have another source of Anemo RES reduction, they can also use Zhongli or C4 Jean.
Team Role
Faruzan:
Buffer and debuffer
Venti:
Sub-DPS and crowd controller
Wanderer:
Main DPS
Bennett:
Buffer and healer

Faruzan's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Faruzan team composition guide and 
character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we'll explore the best teams to build for Faruzan, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and Team Compositions to help Faruzan 
reach her full potential
Best Pairings and Synergies With Faruzan
Faruzan works best when paired with an
Anemo
DPS unit, as she provides some 
of the best buffing possible for this Element, making her the single best 
support for most Anemo DPS.
Venti
Venti
Venti is one of the most powerful characters Faruzan can be paired with. 
	In patch 6.2, Venti received direct kit buffs in patch 6.2, allowing him 
	to deal good
Anemo
damage with his Normal Attacks if there is a second 
	Hexerei character on the team. Venti can also instead be used off-field while
	another Anemo DPS is on-field, and his Burst will still deal good damage 
	while be buffs the on-field Anemo unit's DMG %.
rotations.
Wanderer
Xiao
Anemo
DPS Units
Characters such as
Wanderer
and
Xiao
deal entirely Anemo damage, so they 
	fully utilize all of Faruzan's buffs. Faruzan is one of the best supports 
		to pair with them, even if she is not yet C6.
Varka
Varka
Varka only partially deals
Anemo
damage, with most of his damage coming 
	from
Pyro
/
Hydro
/
Electro
/
Cryo
, so Faruzan doesn't buff all of his damage. 
	This makes her inferior to characters like
Venti
and
Sucrose
for Varka, but 
	she can still be used if the other options aren't available. If using
		Faruzan with Varka, you will want to have her on 4pc
Viridescent Venerer
.
Durin
Durin
Durin is one of the best supports to use alongside
Faruzan
in
Anemo
DPS 
	teams. He provides Anemo RES Shred and deals good Pyro damage from 
	off-field. Durin is also a Hexerei character, not only will he activate 
	the Hexerei abilities for characters like Venti and Varka, but also gain an 
		additional 15% RES Shred of his own when paired with a second Hexerei 
		character.
Furina
Furina
Furina is another good support to use on Anemo DPS teams, thanks to her 
		universal DMG buff. If using her, you will just want to ensure you have a Healer on 
		the team so that you can generate Fanfare stacks.
Bennett
Bennett
Most
Anemo
DPS scale off of Attack, making Bennett one of the best 
		supports they can use giving her great synergy with Faruzan. 
    Furthermore, as Bennett also provides healing, Furina can be 
		used effectively.
Jean
Jean
At Constellation 4, Jean becomes a good support to pair alongside Faruzan 
	in
Anemo
DPS teams, providing 40% Anemo RES Shred. In particular, Jean works 
		well with Anemo DPS that can also make good use from the 15% ATK SPD from 
		her C2, such as
Wanderer
. Jean also provides great healing, 
		allowing her to work well if using
Furina
.

Faruzan is a 4-star Anemo character that wields a Bow in Genshin Impact. She is an off-field Anemo character who can provide support by providing damage buffs. Lore-wise, Faruzan is a Haravatat scholar, who is highly knowledgeable with machines and ancient language.

What Kind of Character is Faruzan?
In battle,
Faruzan is a 4-Star Anemo
Off-field
Support for Anemo DPSes
She can shred Anemo RES with her Burst or Charged Attack (through her passive), and increases her teammates' Anemo DMG Bonus after using her Burst. After using her Skill, her Charged Attacks become Hurricane Arrows that trigger a suction effect after hitting an enemy or area, while also generating Energy.
Faruzan is a core piece of most Anemo DPS teams due to her buffs, but because most Anemo DPSes are hardly meta nowadays, Faruzan's placement in the meta is also fairly low. She is niche, but fills her designated role in a team very well.

Anemo Buffer for Anemo DPSes

Wanderer

Venti

Pros and Cons of Support Faruzan
Pros
- Easy to play.
- Provides a lot good buffs for Anemo DPSes even at C0.
- Doesn't require a lot of particular stats apart from Energy Recharge and ATK.
Cons
- High Energy Burst that leads to high Energy requirements.
- Pre-C6 Faruzan needs to hit a Charged Attack to reliably charge her Burst every rotation, which can be cumbersome in practice.
- Her C6 further increases her buffing power and eases Energy requirements, but getting a 4-Star's C6 can typically be hard to achieve with limited fund.

Team Rotation of Faruzan and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for Off-Field Support Faruzan
Basic Rotation For Off-Field Support Faruzan
1.Start with the supports with longer ability durations if there are any. If any Sub-DPS or DPS needs to deploy their abilities first as part of their mechanic, such as
Durin
sometimes, then ensure they do so beforehand.
2. Swap to Faruzan. Use her Skill, Charged Attack (x1), then use her Burst.
3. Swap to any other supports that need to apply their abilities after if there are.
4. Swap to the main On-Field DPS to execute their primary damage combo.
5. Repeat from Step 1.
When forming a rotation, you typically want to deploy the supports with longer ability durations before the shorter ones. Most of the time Faruzan will be the very first one to execute her combo or near the middle, just before the support with the shortest duration. This is especially true if you have her C2, which increases her Burst's duration.
Basics Of Using Faruzan
Faruzan is the dedicated
Off-Field
Anemo
Support
of most typical On-Field Anemo DPSes
She lowers the enemy's Anemo resistance while also increasing the Anemo DMG Bonus of all party members using her Elemental Burst, greatly enhancing her party members' damage potential.
Apply Buffs Using Burst
Most of Faruzan's innate buffs, which are Anemo RES shred and additional Anemo DMG bonus, are applied once she activates her Elemental Burst.
She needs 80 Energy to deploy her Burst, which is considered pretty high for an Off-Field Support. Because of this, Faruzan naturally wants a lot of Energy Recharge (ER) so she can continuously use her Burst without downtime.
Gain Energy Through Skill and Charged Attack
Faruzan can gain Energy by using her Skill and by hitting a Charged Attack while in her Manifest Gale state.
Since her Charged Attack, which is an important part of her comb, can often miss, her gameplay can feel a little clunky and time-consuming.
Equipping her with an Energy weapon, having a lot of ER on her artifacts, pairing her with two Anemo teammates or having Faruzan's C6 all help to mitigate the issue with the high Energy cost for her Elemental Burst.

How To Use Faruzan's Skills & Passives
How To Play Faruzan

▼Elemental Skill activates Manifest Gale state.

Core Anemo Support for Anemo DPS
Basic Rotation Of Faruzan
1.Start with the supports that have longer ability durations.
2. Switch to Faruzan. Start with her Skill to enter Manifest Gale state.
3. Aim and shoot a Charged Attack as Faruzan, then use her Burst.
4. Swap to supports with shorter ability durations.
5. Switch to your On-Field DPS to do their main damage combo.
6. Repeat from Step 1 after other abilities have expired.
Faruzan wants to use at least 1 Charged Attack every rotation
to reliably get enough Energy to use her Burst every rotation. Her gameplay loop is extremely simple otherwise.
Elemental Skill Activates Manifest Gale State
Icon
Elemental Skill Summary
Wind Realm of Nasamjnin
- Faruzan deploys a polyhedron, dealing AoE Anemo DMG and entering Manifest Gale state.
- In Manifest Gale, her next fully charged shot becomes a
Hurricane Arrow
, consuming the state and dealing Anemo DMG based on a fully charged Parthian Shot.
Pressurized Collapse:
- Hurricane Arrow applies
Pressurized Collapse
, which detonates after a short delay into a vortex.
- The vortex deals AoE Anemo DMG and pulls in nearby enemies and objects.
- If no target is hit, the effect triggers at the arrow’s impact point.
- Vortex DMG is considered Elemental Skill DMG.
Faruzan enters Manifest Gale state after activating her Skill.
This gives her Charged Attacks a suction effect after it collides with an enemy or surface, which is called Pressurized Collapse. The Pressurized Collapse is the focal point of one of her passives and some of her Constellations as well.
Elemental Burst Shreds Res and Buffs Party
Icon
Elemental Burst Summary
The Wind's Secret Ways
- Faruzan deploys a Dazzling Polyhedron, dealing AoE Anemo DMG and releasing a Whirlwind Pulse.
- While active, the Polyhedron moves along a triangular path, unleashing an additional Whirlwind Pulse at each corner.
Whirlwind Pulse Effects:
- Applies
Perfidious Wind’s Bale
, reducing enemies’ Anemo RES.
- Grants nearby party members
Prayerful Wind’s Benefit
, increasing Anemo DMG Bonus.
Faruzan's Elemental Burst is where she applies the Anemo RES shred and Anemo DMG bonus for her teammates,
so she needs to be built around it. This ability has an 80 Energy cost, so her ideal Energy Recharge stat is naturally higher than other supports, so its best to focus getting artifacts pieces that can meet these requirements.
Passives Increase Her Utility
Icon
Passive Summary
Impetuous Flow
- While in Manifest Gale (from Skill), Faruzan’s charged shot charge time is reduced by 60%.
- Opponents hit by the Pressurized Collapse vortex will also be affected by Perfidious Wind’s Bale (Anemo RES shred).
Lost Wisdom of the Seven Caverns
- When characters affected by
Prayerful Wind’s Benefit
(Anemo DMG Bonus buff) deal Anemo DMG (via Normal, Charged, Plunging Attacks, Skills, or Bursts), they trigger
Hurricane Guard
.
-
Hurricane Guard
increases that DMG based on 32% of Faruzan’s Base ATK. Can trigger once every 0.8s.
- The DMG Bonus is removed after it triggers once or when Prayerful Wind’s Benefit expires.
Her passives help enhance her usability as a support.
The first passive makes using her enhanced Charged Attack after using her Skill faster and more rewarding, since it applies her Anemo RES shred. The second passive provides a small DMG bonus whenever any party member deals damage.
Best Paired With Anemo DPSes
Anemo DPSes that can use Faruzan
Wanderer
Xiao

Faruzan's best niche is being a dedicated buffer for Anemo DPSes whose DPS is primarily Anemo DMG.
Because of this, she has limited use-cases, but she also has no real competition as a result. Most of the above DPSes greatly appreciate Faruzan in their teams if they want to maximize their damage potential, especially if you have her C6.

Wind Realm of Nasamjnin (Elemental Skill)
Effect
Faruzan deploys a polyhedron that deals AoE Anemo DMG to nearby opponents. She will also enter the Manifest Gale state.
While in the Manifest Gale state, Faruzan's next fully charged shot will consume this state and will become a Hurricane Arrow that contains high-pressure currents. This arrow deals Anemo DMG based on the DMG of a fully charged Aimed Shot from "Normal Attack: Parthian Shot."
Pressurized Collapse
Effect
The Hurricane Arrow will apply a Pressurized Collapse effect to the opponent or character hit. This effect will be removed after a short delay, creating a vortex that deals AoE Anemo DMG and pulls nearby objects and opponents in. If the Hurricane Arrow does not hit any opponent or character, it will create a Pressurized Collapse effect at its point of impact.
The vortex DMG is considered Elemental Skill DMG.

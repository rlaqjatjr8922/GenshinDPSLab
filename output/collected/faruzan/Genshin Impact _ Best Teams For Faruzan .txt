Synergy Details
Synergy Of Anemo Support Faruzan Team
Character
Detail
Wanderer
A great Anemo DPS that can optimize Faruzan's buffs & trigger Xingqiu's coordinated attacks.
Yelan
She is one of the best off-field Hydro DPS who can increase the DMG of the main DPS not just personal attacks.
Bennett
He is a healer that can provide ATK through Burst & Noblesse Oblige x4.
The objective of this team is to funnel buffs & synergize with the Anemo DPS while consistently applying element for triggering swirl.
Additional Anemo DMG & Intermittent Subtle Crowd Control
Aside from additional 40% CRIT DMG for Anemo,
Faruzan's C6
can also let the active character to trigger the Pressurized Collapse effect every 3s while her Burst is active.
Faruzan Requires A Huge Amount Of Energy Recharge
To be able to have a smooth rotation with Faruzan, pre-C6, it is required to have Energy Recharge close to 300%. As a buffer for Anemo Teams, she does not require other stats so the 4 Star Artifact Set, The Exile is recommended.
Pros & Cons Of Anemo Support Team
Pros
- Anemo DMG can react with the elements in the surrounding
- Can consistently trigger Swirl that is great for scattering the elements & breaking shields
- Anemo Resonance can increase the movement SPD & reduce stamina consumption which are useful in combat & exploration
Cons
- Anemo DPS has different playstyles: raw Anemo DMG or Anemo DMG with consecutive swirl reaction
- Not preferrable to pair with Geo & Dendro since both do not react with Anemo
- It could be difficult to defeat enemies that are resistant or immune to Anemo such as Anemo slime & specter
Rotation Of Anemo Support Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Faruzan's Elemental Burst for Anemo buff
2
Switch to Bennett
3
Use Bennett's Elemental Burst for ATK buff
4
Switch to Yelan
5
Use Yelan's Elemental Burst & Skill for off-field DPS & resistance to interruption
6
Switch to Wanderer
7
Use Wanderer's Elemental Skill and utilize it until the duration ends.
From time to time, you can switch to Faruzan and utilize the Pressurized Collapse effect for producing energy for her Burst & subtle crowd control.
When To Use Wanderer's Burst
Ensure that Wanderer absorbs Pyro when using Skill to increase ATK by 30%. When Burst is used while his Skill is active, the ATK bonus is also applied to the Burst's damage output but it is better to
use his Burst just before the Skill duration ends because using Burst immediately ends the duration of Skill
.

Support & Healer For Swirl Reaction
Anemo is an element that can react with 4 other elements: Pyro, Hydro, Electro, & Cryo and it is great to
include a support & healer that can apply element for triggering swirl reactions
for additional damage. Swirl damage highlights not only the raw Anemo DMG but also the cumulative damage of swirl reactions.
Support & Healer For Raw Anemo DMG
With the release of Dendro & Dendro-related reactions, raw damages became but it can still inflict powerful damage. Currently, Xiao is a hypercarry who deals an impressive raw Anemo DMG. Unlike swirl reaction team,
the team's element is not necessary as long as the members can boost the Anemo DMG of the main DPS
.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Sunfire Melt Faruzan Team
Character
Detail
Layla
She is a great shielder & Cryo applicator which can trigger reverse melt with sunfire effect from Jean & Bennett. She can also provide EM buff to the team by enabling the effect of the Key of Khaj-Nisut.
Jean
The active character affected by Pyro from Bennett's Burst can consistently swirl while inside Jean's Burst. Pumping Jean's EM can substantially increase swirl damage while providing buff to the team when the effect of Freedom-Sworn is activated.
Bennett
He is a healer that can provide ATK thru Burst & Noblesse Oblige x4. He is also the best candidate to applying Pyro to the active character without any setback.
The objective of this team is to sustain the Burst of Jean & Bennett for sunfire effect and Layla's Skill & Burst for protection and Cryo application. Since Faruzan has an Anemo buff, both her & Jean can optimize it.
Pros & Cons Of Sunfire Melt Faruzan Team
Pros
- Pyro is consistently applied & spread which can easily break shields, especially Cryo
- Technical immortality while the AoE of Jean & Bennett's Burst
- Faruzan can be offensive & supportive at the same time
Cons
- Could have a hard time dealing with enemies that are immune or resistant to Pyro
- The AoE of sunfire is stationary so the active character must stay inside the range
Rotation Of Sunfire Melt Faruzan Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Bennett's Elemental Burst for ATK buff & Pyro application
2
Switch to Jean
3
Use Jean's Elemental Burst for sunfire effect
4
Switch to Faruzan
5
Use Faruzan's Elemental Burst for Anemo buff
6
Use Faruzan's Elemental Skill then Charged ATK twice for Pressurized Collapse
6
Switch to Layla
7
Use Layla's Elemental Skill & Burst for shield & Cryo application
From time to time, you can switch to Faruzan and utilize the Pressurized Collapse effect for Anemo DMG, producing Anemo particles, & subtle crowd control.
Jean's C4 Stacks With Faruzan's Burst
Jean's C4 can reduce the Anemo RES of enemies by 40% and it can stack with Faruzan's Burst. This
can substantially increase the damage output of sunfire effect & Pressurized Collapse effect
.

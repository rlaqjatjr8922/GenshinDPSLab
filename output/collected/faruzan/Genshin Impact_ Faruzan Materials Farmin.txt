Faruzan Materials Farming Guide
Faruzan stands out in Genshin Impact as a highly respected scholar from Sumeru, known for her intellect and significant contributions to research. Despite her youthful appearance, she possesses knowledge and experience spanning a century, having been deeply influenced by the ancient power of the Scarlet King.
As a 4-star Anemo bow user, Faruzan also teaches at the Haravatat Darshan, guiding new generations of students. Her unique background enriches the lore of Sumeru, making her a fascinating addition to any adventurer’s team.
Locations and Routes for Faruzan’s Character Ascension Materials
Overseer Matrix Boss and Light Guiding Tetrahedrons
The primary boss for this material is located at The Dune of Elusion in the Great Red Sand region of Sumeru. Defeating this boss yields Light Guiding Tetrahedrons, and players will need a total of 46 for Faruzan’s ascension. Reaching the location involves traversing desert terrain and can be accessed after unlocking the area.
Anemo Elemental Boss and Vayuda Turquoise
The Anemo Hypostasis, found to the northeast of Mondstadt, provides Vayuda Turquoise for ascension. The total needed includes 1 Sliver, 9 Fragments, 9 Chunks, and 6 Gemstones. This boss has a straightforward route; teleporting nearby allows for efficient farming.
Gathering Redcrests in the Desert
Henna Berries, also known as Redcrests, are only located in the Great Red Sand within Sumeru. Collecting 168 is required.
Defeating Eremites for Fabric Materials
Eremites appear throughout Sumeru. To fully ascend Faruzan, players need 18 Firm Faded Red Satins, 30 Trimmed Red Silks, and 36 Rich Red Brocades, which drop from these enemies. Following efficient grinding routes speeds up the collection process.
Locations and routes of Faruzan materials for Talent Ascension
Puppet Strings from Weekly Scaramouche Challenge
To collect Puppet Strings, players must tackle the weekly boss in the domain north of Chinvat Ravine in Sumeru. This Trounce Domain is only accessible after completing the necessary Archon Quest in Chapter III, Act V. Each clear may reward a few of the 18 needed Puppet Strings, making weekly battles essential.
Quick tips:
Only 1 clear per week counts for loot.
Plan resin use to maximize boss drops.
Talent Books from Steeple of Ignorance
The domain west of Chatrakam Cave in Sumeru drops the Talent Books required for Faruzan. Specifically, three types must be farmed: Teachings, Guides, and Philosophies of Admonition.

Materials Dropped by Eremites
Eremites in Sumeru are the main source for Faded Red Satins, Trimmed Red Silks, and Rich Red Brocades, which are used for talent upgrades. Combat with different Eremite types scattered throughout the desert and forest regions will yield these materials.
List of Needed Materials:
18 Faded Red Satins
66 Trimmed Red Silks
93 Rich Red Brocades
Enemy drop rates vary, so repeated farming is often necessary.
Obtaining Crowns of Insight
Crowns of Insight are the rarest material and are required to fully maximize any talent. There are several methods to obtain these, including reaching certain levels in the Frostbearing Tree, Sacred Sakura’s Favor, Sumeru Tree of Dreams, Fountain of Lucine, and Tablet of Tona. They are also sometimes offered as rewards during limited-time events.
Each talent requires one Crown of Insight for the final upgrade, and Faruzan needs three in total to triple crown her abilities. Players should plan ahead to gather these, as they are not farmable through normal domains or enemy drops.

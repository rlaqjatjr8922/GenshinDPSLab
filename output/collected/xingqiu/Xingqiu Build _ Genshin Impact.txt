Xingqiu Overview
This Page provides the Best Xingqiu Build information.
Xingqiu is a young man carrying a longsword who is frequently seen at book booths.
Xingqiu is the most powerful Hydro Support in Genshin Impact at the moment. Xingqiu Sub DPS and Support Build are mostly used by travelers. His Elemental Burst maximize the team DPS even he is off field.
Xingqiu has different ways to help the party. Not only can increase party’s DPS by using the Elemental Burst, but also help other characters to trigger element reaction easily. Energy Recharge is the most important stats for Xingqiu, so the Sub DPS Build and Support Build will also help travelers to stack the Energy Recharge for Xingqiu.
In Genshin Impact, Xingqiu is a 4 Stars rarity Hydro character currently in A tier.

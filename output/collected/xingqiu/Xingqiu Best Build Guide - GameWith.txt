Xingqiu is a 4-star Hydro character that wields a Sword in Genshin Impact. His gameplay focuses on being a highly effective sub-DPS and Hydro enabler, especially in reaction-based teams. His Elemental Skill, "Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen," strikes enemies with dual sword slashes and creates "Rain Swords" that reduce damage taken and improve interruption resistance. His Elemental Burst, "Guhua Sword: Raincutter," summons sword rain attacks that follow the active character's Normal Attacks, enabling consistent Hydro application and powerful reactions like Vaporize and Electro-Charged. Lore-wise, Xingqiu is the second son of the Feiyun Commerce Guild in Liyue, a cultured and well-mannered young man who secretly lives out his ideals of chivalry through his swordsmanship and adventurous spirit.

Roles & Playstyle of Xingqiu and His Strengths and Weakness
Off-Field DPS Xingqiu
Xingqiu's intended role is as an Off-Field Hydro DPS who can enable multiple reactions. His Elemental Burst has great Hydro application and damage. Since a lot of Pyro/Electro/Cryo DPS characters use their Normal Attacks, Xingqiu works great as a Sub-DPS for them.
Pros and Cons of Off-Field DPS Xingqiu
Pros
- Has Interruption RES increase and can reduce damage taken.
- Multi-hit Elemental Skill makes Xingqiu great for Energy Particle generation using either Favonius Sword or Sacrificial Sword.
- Great Hydro application as a 4-star, considering that Yelan and Furina are the 5-star alternatives to what Xingqiu's Burst can do.
Cons
- Compared to Yelan, Xingqiu's melee Elemental Skill can miss if the enemy moves away from his range.

Team Rotation of Xingqiu and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for Xingqiu
Basic Rotation For Xingqiu
1. Use Elemental Skill once. Twice if you have Sacrificial Sword on Xingqiu.
2. Use Elemental Burst.
3. Switch to next character.
Xingqiu's rotation in the team is the same each rotation unless he doesn't have his Elemental Burst available
. After his Elemental Skill is used, absorb the Particles before using his Burst. Since he generates a lot of Particles, he can easily max out his Energy if he has good Energy Recharge and holds Favonius Sword or Sacrificial Sword.
Basics Of Using Xingqiu
Amazing Hydro Sub-DPS for Damage and Reactions
Xingqiu is one of the best Hydro Sub-DPS characters in Genshin Impact. His Elemental Burst allows him to apply Hydro easily while Off-Field and can be used in multiple teams.
High Energy Cost in Burst But Generates Enough Particles with Certain Weapons
Similar to
Xiangling
, Xingqiu has a high Energy cost of 80. However, it is easy to mitigate this with the
Emblem Of Severed Fate
set and
Favonius Sword
or
Sacrificial Sword
. Favonius Sword is generally better compared to Sacrificial Sword since it takes less time.
Use Elemental Skill for Increasing Interruption RES and Decreasing Damage Taken
Xingqiu's Elemental Skill summons a sword aura that floats around the active character. It can increase the character's interruption resistance and decrease the damage taken. The damage reduction buff relies on stacks, where 1 hit taken consumes 1 stack.
Summary of Xingqiu's Elemental Skill Effects
- Summons a Sword Aura with 3 Swords. 4 Swords are summoned if he is C1.
- Buffs Interruption RES and decreases damage taken.
Ｌ Damage Reduction buff can be increased based on Xingqiu's Hydro Damage Bonus. Maxes at 24% damage reduced.
- A Rain Sword will be consumed when taking damage.
Also Great for Generating Particles
Xingqiu's Elemental Skill is multi-hit. This makes it great for Sacrificial Sword but it's better to use Favonius Sword since it would take less time but generates Particles that non-Hydro teammates can utilize.
Use Elemental Burst to Deal Damage Off-Field
Xingqiu's Elemental Burst summons a Sword Rain that floats around the active character. Upon using Normal Attacks, it will do consecutive Hydro attacks to the enemy. His Elemental Burst's Effect is similar to Furina and Yelan's.
Elemental Skill, Burst and Abilities Guide for Xingqiu
How To Use Xingqiu?
How To Use?
▼ A Hydro Sub-DPS great for applying Hydro
▼ Use Elemental Skill for Interruption RES and Damage Taken Reduction buff
▼ Use Elemental Burst for Off-Field Hydro Attacks
A Hydro Sub-DPS Great For Applying Hydro
Xingqiu is a Hydro Sub-DPS that is great at applying Hydro and providing defensive buffs for the team. His Elemental Burst summons Swords that can do a coordinated attack with the On-Field character's Normal Attacks.
Use Elemental Skill For Interruption Res And Damage Reduction Buff
Xingqiu's Elemental Skill creates a Sword Aura that floats around the character. It has an initial value of 3 Swords, increased by 1 with his Constellation 1.
This sword aura buffs the active character with increased Interruption RES and a Damage Taken Reduction buff
. The max value the reduction buff has is 24%.
Use Elemental Burst For Off-Field Hydro Attacks
Xingqiu's Elemental Burst creates a Sword Rain that also floats around the character. It shares the same value of Swords created with his Skill. This Sword Rain activates with the active character's Normal Attacks, causing it to do 3-4 consecutive Hydro attacks after the Normal Attack.
Should You Pull for Xingqiu's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim For C6 if You're Able To
Constellation Priority
C6 > C2 or C3
C6 Is Ideal For Every Use-Case
At C0, Xingqiu is already a very capable off-field Hydro support character whose contributions to a team only get better with his constellations.
His C6 will be his best constellation overall
since it greatly increases Xingqiu's damage and Hydro application with his Burst due to the additional sword rain attack while also lowering his Energy Recharge requirements due to the Energy refund Xinqiu gets after every 3rd sword rain attack.
If you cannot get his C6, his C2 is a viable stopping point
since it extends the duration of his Burst and reduces the Hydro RES of enemies damaged by a sword rain attack. This improves his value as a sub-DPS unit and as a support with other Hydro characters. C2's effect is very synergistic with C6, further giving a reason to get his C6 if you have the means to do so.
Beyond C2,
his C3 can be another viable stopping point
due to the added levels to his Burst, which is again, made better with the activation of his C6. With this in mind, it can be said that Xinqiu's C6 is valuable not just because of its effect, but by virtue of being a strong compliment to his best early constellations. However,
not getting any of his constellations isn't completely disheartening, since he will still be very viable in his select teams even at C0.

Who Is Xingqiu?
The Second Son Of The Feiyun Commerce Guild
Xingqiu is the second son of the Feiyun Commerce Guild, an influential merchant guild in Liyue. As the second son, he does not have the responsibilities of the heir and relatively lives a more freeing life.
A Lover Of Books & Martial Arts
Xingqiu is a lover of arts, whether it is literature or in martial arts. He pursues to learn martial arts better, while still perusing the shelves of the Wanmen Bookhouse for something new to read.
Has A Playful & Mischievous Side
Although Xingqiu often carries himself with quiet dignity, there is a mischievous side to him. He often plays pranks on his friends and it's Chongyun who mostly bears the blunt of his bizarre humor.

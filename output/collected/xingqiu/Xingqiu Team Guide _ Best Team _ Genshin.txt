This Page provides the best Xingqiu Team information. Xingqiu will be Support DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Xingqiu and Tartaglia to make your own best team.
Xingqiu best team can be formed in different ways including Permafrost, Vaporize or Electro-Charged team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

Chasca is a 5-star Anemo character that wields a Bow in Genshin Impact. She is an on-field DPS Anemo character who can deal damage through her charged attacks and is able to deal multi-elemental damage through the use of her unique bullet mechanic. Lore-wise, Chasca is a descendant of the Flower-Feather Clan and is regarded as a peacekeeper who resolves tribal disputes.

Using her Elemental Skill will make her ascend into air and holding the Attack button will make Chasca begin loading Bullets that undergo Elemental conversion depending on her teammates. Once finished loading, Chasca will unleash the Bullets swiftly, dealing damage and triggering reactions. Her Elemental Burst has a similar function where 2 out of 6 Bullets change Elements.

Roles & Playstyle Of Chasca and Its Strengths and Weakness
On-Field DPS Chasca
Chasca's main role is to be the On-Field DPS. Her playstyle is unique in that she can deal multiple Elemental DMG outside of her Anemo damage. Chasca uses the Elements of her PECH teammates to change her Bullets Elemental damage.
Pros and Cons of On-Field DPS Chasca
Pros
- Auto-targeting mechanic can make her easier to play.
- Her team can be built with all supports and fewer sub-DPS since Chasca will still apply their Elements.
- Powerful AoE damage along with Anemo spreading Elements allows her to easily clear waves of enemies.
Cons
- Incompatible with sub-DPS that requires Normal Attacks to proc their Off-Field Attacks.
- Stationary buffs like Bennett's Elemental Burst is difficult to use with Chasca since moving around the field will be needed if targeting far away enemies.
- Performs the best with 3 PECH Elements in her team but performs okay with only two.

ATK Goblet For Flexibility
Chasca can shoot multiple elemental bullets on her own, and the damage from the bullets is her main source of damage. These bullets are ATK Scaling, so for a more flexible build, it is recommended to use an ATK Goblet.
Aim For Max CRIT Rate
Because of Obsidian Codex and Chasca's Ascension stats, you already get around 79% CRIT Rate at Lvl. 90, so you only need to have 20% (from artifacts) for 100% CRIT Rate. Because of this, you can easily focus on CRIT DMG, where having 180% to 200% or more is ideal.

Team Rotation of Chasca and Combos
Team Rotation of On-Field Chasca
Basic Rotation Of Chasca
1. Use Support/Sub-DPS characters' abilities.
2. Switch to Chasca and use her Elemental Skill.
3. Hold the Attack button while aiming at enemies and have Chasca automatically attack enemies.
3. (Optional) Use Chasca's Elemental Burst after her Nightsoul gauge empties.
As an On-Field DPS character, Chasca's rotation is relatively simple. Use her Skill then hold the Attack button and aim at enemies. Chasca automatically attacks until she runs out of Nightsoul points. You can choose to use her Elemental Burst but it's generally unused for faster rotations.
Basics of Using Chasca (Beginner Guide)
As a character from Natlan, Chasca has access to the Nightsoul Mechanic. This is a unique mechanic for Natlan characters where it can allow them to use Nightsoul points and gain a unique traveling ability. For Chasca, she uses it to ride her Soulsniper and travel in air. Her Nightsoul state is also her DPS state where she can deal Nightsoul-aligned damage to enemies.
What Is Nightsoul State / Nightsoul Points for Chasca?
Upon using her Elemental Skill, Chasca enters Nightsoul state.
Chasca gains glowing tattoos and outline on her. A gauge will also appear beside her. This gauge displays the amount of Nightsoul points she has and it will continuously decrease. Reaching 0 Nightsoul points will end her Nightsoul state.
▼ In-Game Description of Nightsoul State of Chasca
Nightsoul Burst for Chasca
Nightsoul Burst is an effect that automatically activates when a Natlan character is in your team. The effect depends on the character you have. In Chasca's case, Nightsoul Bursts allow her to deal a Nightsoul-aligned Off-Field Elemental Attack whose Element can also change.

Spirit Reins, Shadow Hunt (Elemental Skill)
Effect
Chasca and the will residing in her weapon resonate, dealing Nightsoul-aligned AoE Anemo DMG. After using this Skill, Chasca gains 80 Nightsoul points and enters the Nightsoul's Blessing state.
Nightsoul's Blessing: Chasca
Effect
Continually consume Nightsoul points. When Nightsoul points are fully depleted or the skill is used again, Chasca's Nightsoul's Blessing state will end. Her Nightsoul's Blessing has the following traits:
・Mounts Soulsniper: Ritual Staff, increasing Chasca's Movement SPD and interruption resistance. In this state, Chasca will continually consume Nightsoul points to remain airborne. When Sprinting or increasing height, extra Nightsoul points will be consumed.
・When using Normal or Charged Attacks, Tap or Hold to switch between using Multitarget Fire in different ways.
Multitarget Fire
Effect
Tap
・Deal Nightsoul-aligned Anemo DMG to opponents in front. This DMG is considered Normal Attack DMG.
Hold
・Enter Aiming Mode and choose a certain number of opponents within a certain range, before loading up to 6 Shadowhunt Shells based on the duration for which the skill was charged. When 6 Shells have been loaded or charging is stopped, these Shells will be fired at the chosen opponents in sequence.
Shadowhunt Shells
Effect
・Deal Nightsoul-aligned Anemo DMG, which is considered Charged Attack DMG.
・The 4th, 5th, and 6th Shells loaded into the cylinder will undergo Elemental Conversion based on the Elemental Types of the other characters in the party: For each Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, or Electro character in the party, one Shadowhunt Shell will randomly be Converted into a Shining Shadowhunt Shell of one of these corresponding Elemental Types, dealing Nightsoul-aligned DMG corresponding to that Elemental Type. This DMG is considered Charged Attack DMG.
・The Shells loaded into the cylinder are fired starting from the last Shadowhunt Shell to be loaded, in a "last in, first out" order.

Bullet Trick
Effect
When the Elemental Skill Spirit Reins, Shadow Hunt's Multitarget Fire is being charged, if there are party members who meet the Elemental Conversion Type requirements, gain the following effects based on the number of different eligible Elemental Types present:
・There is a 33.3%/66.7%/100% chance for each Type to trigger Spiritbinding Conversion and cause the 3rd Shadowhunt Shell loaded into the cylinder to be randomly converted into a Shining Shadowhunt Shell from one of the corresponding Elemental Types.
・Each Elemental Type will grant Chasca 1 stack of Spirit of the Radiant Shadow, increasing the DMG of Shining Shadowhunt Shell by 15%/35%/65%. This effect lasts until the current Multitarget Fire ends. Max 3 stacks.
Intent To Cover
Effect
When a nearby party member triggers a Nightsoul Burst, Chasca will fire a Burning Shadowhunt Shot at a nearby opponent, dealing Nightsoul-aligned Anemo DMG equal to 150% of the Shadowhunt Shell DMG from her Elemental Skill Spirit Reins, Shadow Hunt. If the party has Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, or Electro characters, the Burning Shadowhunt Shot will be converted to deal Nightsoul-aligned Elemental DMG of the corresponding Elemental Type equal to 150% of the DMG dealt by Spirit Reins, Shadow Hunt's Shining Shadowhunt Shells.The DMG done in this way is considered Charged Attack DMG.
Everburning Heart
Effect
After her Nightsoul points are exhausted, Chasca will switch to consuming Phlogiston to maintain her Nightsoul's Blessing. While in an area with Phlogiston Mechanics within Natlan, she can use Nightsoul Transmission: Chasca. When the active character is in the air, the following will occur when switching to Chasca: Chasca will enter the Nightsoul's Blessing state and gain 32 Nightsoul points. Nightsoul Transmission can be triggered once every 10s by your own team.
Mediation's True Meaning
Effect
25 Phlogiston will be restored when your own party members defeat an opponent. This effect can trigger once every 12s. This has no effect in Domains, Trounce Domains, or the Spiral Abyss.
Who Is Chasca?
Known Peacemaker In Natlan
Chasca is a famed "peacemaker" in Natlan due to her ability to resolve any conflict. On the other side of the coin, she is a fighter for peace for her nation as she has participated in the Night Warden Wars.
Currently One Of The Heroes Of Natlan
Chasca is one of the current bearers of an Ancient Name from the Heroes of Natlan. She received hers in the climax of Natlan's war with the Abyss.
Raised By A Qucusaurus
Although she hails from the Flower-Feather Clan, Chasca was actually raised by a Qucusaur when she was a child. She sees the Qucusaur as her mother and its children as her siblings.

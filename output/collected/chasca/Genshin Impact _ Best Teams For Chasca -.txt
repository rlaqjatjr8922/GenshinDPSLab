Chasca Works Best with Multi-Element Teams
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Melt Team
★★★★★
- Utilizes Cryo and Pyro teammates to enable Chasca to trigger Melt by ensuring she has Cryo and Pyro shells or bullets.
- Has the highest DPS potential at the cost of splitting field time with
Mavuika
.
-
Escoffier
and
Citlali
offer strong Cryo application that lets Chasca and Mavuika trigger true Melt (Pyro on Cryo) consistently.
▼ Lunar-Charged Team
★★★★☆
- Chasca can act as a convincing On-Field DPS for the strong
Ineffa
and
Columbina
pairing that can deal a good amount of Off-Field damage just by themselves. This leads to the team being her second best option by virtue of damage alone.
- Chasca's personal damage takes a backseat due to her not really benefitting from Columbina's buffs, but adding a Pyro character to the team, like
Durin
, does let her at least trigger Vaporize or Overload occasionally, which can help boost her contribution to the team's DPS.
▼ Reverse Vaporize Team
★★★☆☆
- Hydro and Pyro characters, like the Melt team, similarly allow Chasca to have Hydro and Pyro shells to produce Vaporize reactions.
- Most Vaporize Chasca teams want to run two Pyro characters (instead of two Hydro characters) due to how Hydro and Pyro interact with each other.
- Vaporize with Hydro on Pyro has the stronger damage multiplier versus the opposite. At the moment there isn't a Pyro character that can apply enough Pyro for Chasca to consistently access the x2 multiplier from the stronger Vaporize, making this a weaker Vaporize team and therefore worse than her top two comps in terms of DPS potential.
▼ Vapemelt Team
★★★☆☆
- With Cryo, Pyro and Hydro teammates, Chasca's bullet selection allows for Vaporize, Melt and Frozen reactions to happen intermittently.
- Highlights Chasca's Bullet mechanics that make use of Swirls to trigger and occasionally spread different reactions all at once.
▼ Overvape Team
★★★☆☆
- Like her Vapemelt team, this setup takes advantage of the power of several different reactions triggering consecutively.
- Electro-Charged, Vaporize and Overload are the primary reactions involved here.
- Since Chasca's attacks track enemies, she naturally offsets the knockback effect of Overload.
Chasca's Bullet conversion mechanic during her Nightsoul state allows her to work with supports and even other DPS characters to deal non-Anemo Damage. However, she works best with Sub-DPS characters to trigger more reactions and contribute to the team's damage.

Note!
Despite having similar setups and rotations, Chasca's Melt team will deal better damage than her Vaporize team due to it being a true Melt (Pyro on Cryo) team thanks to Citlali's strong application.
This team has some of the highest DPS ceiling for a baseline C0 Chasca, assuming you split the field time between her and Mavuika.
Rotation Of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start the Rotation with Mavuika's Skill.
2
Swap to Bennett. Use his Skill (tap), then Burst.
3
Switch to Citlali to deploy her Skill then Burst.
4
Field Mavuika, then perform your preferred Mavuika combo.
5
Switch to Chasca and use her Skill then hold the Attack button to start releasing her Bullets.
6
(Optional) Use Chasca's Burst before repeating the rotation.
7
Repeat from Step 1.
You can also opt to simply use Skill into Burst on Mavuika (to enable the DMG% buff from her Burst), then have Chasca be the sole On-Field DPS.

The Ineffa and Columbina core can be complemented by a lot of characters without strict teammate preferences while still outputting a substantial amount of Off-Field damage.
Chasca is one among the many On-Field characters that can slot them into her team to find good results.
Durin is there to slightly increase Chasca's damage contribution to the team's DPS by allowing her to occasionally trigger Overload and Vaporize, but he's very replaceable in this setup.

No.
Procedure

4

6
(Optional) Use Chasca's Burst before repeating the rotation.
7
Repeat from Step 1.

Note!
Despite having similar setups and rotations, Chasca's Vaporize team is generally weaker than her Melt team due to them being mostly Reverse Vaporize (Pyro on Hydro) setups, which has a lower multiplier than the opposite.
At C0, it's best to avoid including more than 1 Hydro teammate due to Chasca not benefitting from Hydro Resonance (as an ATK-scaling DPS),
as well as double Hydro significantly lowering Chasca's chances of triggering true Vaporize (Hydro on Pyro) due to her Bullet mechanics and the fact that there isn't a Pyro Support in the game with application equivalent to
Citlali's
.
Like the Melt team, this Vaporize comp has better damage potential if you split the field time between Chasca and Mavuika.

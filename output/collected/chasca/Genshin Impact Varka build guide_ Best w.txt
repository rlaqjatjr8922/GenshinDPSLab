Best Varka build guide (Image via HoYoverse)
Varka in Genshin Impact is a new 5-star Anemo unit. He is a Claymore user who excels as a main damage dealer and has a pretty interesting playstyle that allows him to deal Elemental DMG based on the Elemental Types of his party members, similar to Chasca. Thanks to that, building the Knight of Boreas is also fairly easy, and he has a lot of good weapons and team options.

This article briefly covers all the best artifacts, weapons, and teams for Varka in Genshin Impact.

A Day Carved From Rising Winds will always be the best artifact set for Varka since it significantly increases his DMG output and boosts his Crit stats. Alternatively, you can go with a 2-pc VV and a 2-pc set of any set that gives Elemental DMG Bonus depending on the party, or a mix of ATK sets.

Best weapons for Varka in Genshin Impact
Gest of the Mighty Wolf (Image via HoYoverse)
Listed below are the best weapons for the
Grand Master of the Knights of Favonius
:
Ad
Gest of the Mighty Wolf
: It is Varka's Signature Weapon, and it has high Base ATK and a CRIT Rate second stat. The passive also provides everything he needs, such as ATK SPD, CRIT DMG, and DMG boost.
A Thousand Blazing Suns
: The second best option for Varka. Has a really high Base ATK and provides ATK%, CRIT Rate, and CRIT DMG boosts.
Beacon of the Reed Sea
: Another great 5-star weapon. Provides significant CRIT Rate and ATK boosts.
Serpent Spine
: Great 4-star option because of the CRIT Rate boost. Also increases the DMG dealt. With higher refinements, it can even be better than some of the other 5-star options.
Tidal Shadow
: The best F2P option for Varka. Provides a lot of ATK, but requires a healer in the team to get all the buffs.
Ad
Talent priority
You can level up Varka's talents in the following order:
Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst > Normal Attack
Most of Varka's damage comes from his enhanced Normal Attacks after casting his Elemental Skill, so prioritizing it is recommended. After that, you can upgrade his Elemental Burst for a little extra damage every rotation. Lastly, there's no need to nevel his Normal Attack talent.

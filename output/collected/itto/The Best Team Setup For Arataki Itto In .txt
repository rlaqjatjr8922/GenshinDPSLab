Arataki Itto
in
Genshin Impact
, it's important to understand that he'll function as the main DPS unit for the group. This is thanks in large part to his abilities and the fact that his damage is boosted based on his DEF.

While trying to build a team for Arataki Itto, you'll commonly find yourself running out of options because of his element and strict play style. Thankfully, as more characters got released with time, it became a lot more valuable to spend your resources on building this character. Here are all the team synergies you can use to beat the hardest content with the Oni himself.
Updated on March 25, 2023, by Sanyam Jain:
In Genshin Impact, you can only get five-star limited characters like Arataki Itto when they're featured on the limited banners, and the developers usually rerun them alongside a new synergistic character release, which happens to be Chiori in Itto's case.
8
Xinyan
Element
Pyro
Weapon
Claymore
We'll start with the second choice for the key roles on the team and the second-best choice when it comes to the battery of the team. This
honor goes to Xinyan
, who uses pyro Claymores that match her metal punk aesthetic. In terms of team fit, Arataki Itto can help make her shields stronger as she provides damage as the non-geo user of the group.
She may not be as great of a fit for the team as the main choice for battery, Raiden Shogun, but Xinyan holds her own alongside Itto. Even the smallest amount of synergy can go a long way in making a team in Genshin Impact functional and effective.
7
Ningguang
Element
Geo
Weapon
Catalyst
As a sub-DPS, the most important thing this role can provide is damage that comes easily and often. Luckily Ningguang fits that mold quite nicely as she comes equipped with consistent powerful auto attacks that work hand in hand with both her Elemental Skill and Burst, which rely on the Geo element.
Itto can only deal massive damage on the field when you have his Elemental Burst active, so you can use Ningguang to deal a bit of damage for the rest. If you're getting him to constellation two, Ningguang's Geo element can also reduce his Burst's CD by 1.5 seconds.
6
Xianyun
Element
Anemo
Weapon
Catalyst
Xianyun has been one of the fun-enabling supports in Genshin Impact ever since her release. This is due to her Elemental Burst allowing any character to do eight Plunge Attacks while also buffing this damage. Usually, you'd deal the most damage from Itto when he has his Superlative Superstrength stacks with Burst.

However, once these stacks run out, you can use Xianyun's Plunge Attacks to keep the damage up while his Geo Infusion is still there. The Burst will also heal him at regular intervals, making it much easier to keep the oni alive.
5
Raiden Shogun
Element
Electro
Weapon
Polearm
Raiden Shogun is recommended for Arataki Itto's ultimate team lineup, and you could argue that she's the most important. She can work as the main battery of the team as she regenerates a significant amount with her Burst while dealing a ton of damage at times when Itto can't do it himself.
Combined with a couple of other Geo characters, you'll never have energy problems with Itto. The only unfortunate part about Raiden Shogun being in this team is that you likely won't have any Elemental Reactions increasing her damage unless the enemy has an element applied to them.
4
Chiori
Element
Geo
Weapon
Sword
A team in Genshin Impact consists of four members, but you might have quite a difficult time finding the other three when it comes to Itto. This is because he doesn't benefit from the Crystallize reaction, which already makes it useless to place characters from other elements here. However, you might feel like Itto's damage alone isn't enough for endgame battles.

This is where Chiori comes into play. If you're lucky enough to get this five-star, you can have a DEF-scaling off-field DPS unit dishing out significant damage at regular intervals. She works perfectly with Itto since both of them can enjoy the buffs provided by Gorou.
3
Zhongli
Element
Geo
Weapon
Polearm
At this point, you likely already know that putting Geo characters in a team with Itto is the best choice, especially if you go for a few constellations on him. This opens up the possibility of having the best character from this element, Zhongli, in the team. His Elemental Skill provides Itto with a shield, making it almost impossible to defeat him with the existing high DEF.
But the main reason for having Zhongli here is the 20 percent RES shred provided by his shield to the surrounding enemies. This type of buff is difficult to come by in Genshin Impact, making it valuable enough to have a slot on this team.
2
Albedo
Element
Geo
Weapon
Sword
Albedo will serve as the sub-DPS on the team and as the Geo element battery for the team. He also happens to scale on defense which isn't too dissimilar to how Arataki Itto functions.
Albedo's area of effect attack called Flower Explosion will pulsate once detonated and provide Geo Energy to other characters in the area, which means that he can provide a damage source while at the same time functioning as a supporting character for everyone else. If placed correctly, you can also use Albedo's Skill to reach flying enemies with Itto's attacks.
1
Gorou
Element
Geo
Weapon
Bow
When it comes to the support unit of the team, the number one choice
has to be Gorou
since he's very similar to Arataki Itto in several ways. First, he also happens to be a Geo element user, but instead of Claymores, he uses Bows. He provides defense and Geo damage bonuses, making him the perfect counterpart to Itto.
By boosting Itto's defense, he also buffs the character's damage, which is in addition to the Geo damage boost he's already providing. The synergy between the two is unreal and it's clear they were both designed to share the same roster.

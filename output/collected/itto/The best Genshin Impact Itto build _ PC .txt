Looking for a great
Genshin Impact
Itto build? The Oni gang leader is back for a banner re-run, alongside the new Electro hero,
Kuki Shinobu
. Arataki Itto isn't a slouch—except in the delinquent aspects of his character. Just as
Kokomi
was a super-
Barbara
, Itto is kind of a super-
Noelle
. He doesn't have the maid knight's shields or healing, but he does have the same defense-based attack buff for his burst state, as well as Geo infusion.
Like Noelle, this makes him pretty easy to build, and with the Geo-focused artifact set from the
Slumbering Court
domain, he's set to be a strong addition to any team, especially if you run him with Gorou. In this Genshin Impact Itto build guide, I'll give you the usual details in regards to abilities and ascension materials, but also a theorycrafted build for the five-star Geo character.
Builds
The best Genshin Impact Itto build
Arataki Itto's real strength is his normal and charged attacks and how he buffs them with stacks of Superlative Superstrength and the Raging Oni King state he gains from his burst.

Weapon:
Redhorn Stonethresher
As usual, the best weapon for Itto is the five-star claymore that's dropping alongside him. The Redhorn Stonethresher is a pretty straightforward weapon, boosting defense by 28% and normal and charged attack damage by 40% of defense. Considering the attack buff from Itto's burst is also based on defense, this should help him deal some impressive damage.
A good alternative four-star claymore would probably be Whiteblind, since it also increases defense, and when you land a normal or charged attack, boosts both attack and defense by 6% for six seconds, stacking up to four times. Whiteblind is also a decent free-to-play weapon as you can forge and refine it yourself.
Artifact:
(4)
Husk of Opulent Dreams
The Husk of Opulent Dreams is one of the two new artifact sets that were introduced in
Genshin Impact 2.3
. While the other set is made for healers, this one was basically made for Itto. First, it boosts defense by 30% for two-pieces, which further adds to Itto's burst attack buff, and for four-pieces it introduces a stackable called Curiosity.

Normal Attack: Fight Club legend
Normal
Perform up to 4 consecutive strikes. When his 2nd and 4th attack hits opponents, Itto will gain stacks of Superlative Superstrength. Max 5 stacks. Triggering this effect will refresh the current duration of any existing stacks.
Charged
When holding to a initiate a Charged Attack, you may continuously press to use Arataki Kesagiri slashes without using any Stamina. Each Arataki Kesagiri slash will consume 1 stack of Superlative Superstrength. When the final stack is consumed, Itto will unleash a powerful attack. If there are no more stacks, he will perform a Saichimonji Slash.
Plunging
Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE damage upon impact.
Swipe to scroll horizontally
Elemental Skill
Masatsu Zetsugi: Akaushi Burst!
Hurls Ushi, the young akaushi bull and auxiliary member of the Arataki Gang, dealing Geo damage to opponents. When Ushi hits opponents, Arataki Itto will gain 1 stack of Superlative Superstrength. Ushi will remain on the field and provide support in the following ways: Taunts surrounding opponents and attracts their attacks. Inherits HP based on a percentage of Arataki Itto's Max HP. When attacked, it will provide Arataki Itto with 1 Superlative Superstrength stack. One stack can be gained this way once every 2 seconds. It will flee when its HP reaches 0 or its duration ends. It will grant Arataki Itto 1 stack of Superlative Superstrength as it leaves.
Hold
Adjust throwing angle. Ushi is considered a Geo construct and Arataki Itto can only have 1 Ushi that he has deployed on the field at any one time.
Swipe to scroll horizontally
Elemental Burst
Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil!
For a time, Itto lets his inner Raging Oni King emerge, using his Oni King's Kanabou in battle. This state has the following special properties: Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks will be converted to Geo. This cannot be overridden. Itto's ATK will be increased based on his DEF. His Normal Attack SPD will also be increased. On hit, the first and third attacks of his Normal Attack combo will grant Itto 1 stack of Superlative Superstrength. Itto's Elemental and Physical RES is reduced by 20%. The Raging Oni King state will be cleared when Itto leaves the field.

Passives
Arataki Ichiban
When Arataki Itto uses consecutive Arataki Kesgiri, he obtains the following effects: Each slash causes the subsequent slash to have 10% more ATK SPD. Max ATK SPD increase is 30%. Greatly increases his resistance to interruption. These effects will be cleared once he stops performing consecutive slashes.
Bloodline of the Crimson Oni
Arataki Kesagiri DMG is increased by 35% of Arataki Itto's DEF.
Woodchuck Chucked
When a party member uses attacks to obtain wood from a tree, they have a 25% chance to get an additional log of wood.
Swipe to scroll horizontally
Constellations
Stay Awhile And Listen Up
After using Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil, Itto gains two stacks of Superlative Superstrength. After one second, Itto will gain a stack of Superlative Superstrength every 0.5 seconds for 1.5 seconds.
Gather 'Round, It's a Brawl!
After using Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil, each party member whose element is Geo will decrease that skill's CD by 1.5 seconds and restore 6 energy to Arataki Itto. A maximum of 4.5 seconds CD can be decreased and 18 Energy restored in this manner.
Horns lowered, Coming Through
Increases level of Masatsu Zetsugi:Akaushi Burst! By 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Jailhouse Bread and Butter
When the Raging Oni King state caused  Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil, Itto has ended , all party members gain 20% increased defense and attack for 10 seconds
10 Years of Hanamizaka Fame
Increases the level of Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil by 3. Maximum upgrade level is 15.
Arataki Itto, Present!
Arataki Itto's Charged Attacks deal 70% increased Crit DMG. Additionally, when he uses Arataki Kesagiri, he has a 50% chance to not consume stacks of Superlative Superstrength.

Arataki Itto's Ascension materials
Character
You'll need to find specific Ascension materials to level up Arataki Itto. Geo Hypostases and Golden Wolflords drop the
Prithiva Topaz
items, and
Riftborn Regalia
are dropped by Golden Wolflords over level 30. You'll also need the following items:
Onikabuto
Slime Condensate/Secretions/Concentrate
Onikabuto
are found in various areas in Inazuma, but these strange beetles are often seen on Narukami Island.
Slime
items are dropped—rather unsurprisingly—by slimes. You shouldn't have any issue finding these enemies, but they're often found around bodies of water.
Talents
Here are the items you'll need to level up Arataki Itto's talents:
Teachings/Guide/Philosophies of Elegance
Slime Condensate/Secretions/Concentrate
Ashen Heart
Crown of Insight
The
Elegance
items are obtained from the Violet Court domain on Tuesdays,  Fridays, and Sundays while, as mentioned above, the
Slime
items are dropped from slime enemies of different world levels. The
Ashen Heart
is a reward for defeating the weekly boss La Signora at level 70 or above. Lastly, the
Crown of Insight
is a reward for upgrading the Frostbearing Tree in Dragonspine or the Sacred Sakura in Inazuma. You can also get it from various seasonal activities, like the recent Shadows Amidst Snowstorms event.

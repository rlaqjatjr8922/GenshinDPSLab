Synergy Of Mono Geo Itto Team
Character
Detail
Gorou
He is tailored to support for DEF-scaling characters. Including him with Itto & Albedo can increase the damage output of them both. Also, he can restore a bit of HP at C4.
Albedo
His Geo construct can deal off-field Geo DMG thru coordinated attacks and also inherit the pulse from Zhongli's monolith when placed nearby.
Zhongli
His shield can reduce the elemental resistance of nearby enemies & his monolith can synergize with the Geo construct of Albedo & Itto.
The objective of this team is to utilize a team of 4 Geo characters. Since Zhongli's shield can be sustained, Crystallize is no longer needed.
Pros & Cons Of Mono Geo Itto Team
Pros
- Geo resonance can increase the damage output as long as the shield is active
- Geo resonance improves the durability of the shield
Cons
- Albedo's Skill is a Geo construct which can be destroyed easily
- It could be difficult to defeat enemies that are resistant or immune to Geo such as Geovishaps & Geo Specter
- Itto's rotation can be interrupted if accidentally elevated by Albedo's Skill
- Difficult to utilize if you are facing an enemy with a debuff that can ignore shield such as corrosion
Rotation Of Mono Geo Itto Team
No.
Procedure
1
Hold Zhongli's Skill to produce a shield & monolith
2
Switch to Albedo
3
Use Albedo's Skill near Zhongli's monolith so it also pulsates
4
Switch to Gorou
5
Use Gorou's Skill for Geo particles
6
Use Gorou's Burst for DEF buff
7
Switch to Itto
8
Use Itto's Skill for Geo DMG and Superlative Stacks
9
Use Itto's Normal ATK to gain Superlative Stacks
10
Use Itto's Burst then utilize Charged ATK to consume Superlative Stacks
11
Repeat from step 1
The setup must be completed before using Itto's Burst because this can be cancelled when Itto gets switched out.

Due to the current available Geo characters, the "Mono Geo" terminology is discredited when using a character with a different element. However, the attack rotation and effectiveness of the team stays the same.
Three Geo Characters For Optimized Gorou
To fully optimize Gorou's buff from Burst, you must include at least 3 Geo characters in a team. Since Itto & Gorou are both Geo, you can go for 2 Geo characters or 1 Geo character and 1 non-Geo character.
ATK Can Still Be Used By Itto
It is true that Itto's damage scales mainly from DEF. However, ATK can also improve Itto's damage output so you can still use supports like Bennett in a team with Itto.
Geo Construct Is Capped At 3
The number of Geo constructs that can simultaneously exist is up to 3. When another Geo construct is produced beyond the cap, the initially created Geo construct gets destroyed.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Duo Geo Itto Team
Character
Detail
Zhongli
His shield can reduce the elemental resistance of nearby enemies and enable Geo resonance.
Xiangling
Xiangling is an excellent off-field Pyro DPS who can fit in most teams. She can conveniently apply an element for Crystallize.
Kuki Shinobu
Her healing circle follows the active character and each heal, including the over-healing done, accumulates then deals damage when the bubble pops.
The objective of this team is to utilize the Geo resonance and an elemental reaction that can deal damage simultaneously with Itto.
Pros & Cons Of Duo Geo Itto Team
Pros
- Geo resonance can increase the damage output as long as the shield is active
- Geo resonance improves the durability of the shield
- Freedom-Sworn can increase the damage output of Itto's Normal/Charged ATK
Cons
- Kuki Shinobu needs ample on-field time when her HP gets low
- Difficult to utilize if you are facing an enemy with a debuff that can ignore a shield such as corrosion
Rotation Of Duo Geo Itto Team
No.
Procedure
1
Hold Zhongli's Skill for shield
2
Switch to Xiangling
3
Use Xiangling's Skill & Burst for off-field Pyro DMG
4
Switch to Kuki Shinobu
5
Use Kuki Shinobu's Skill for periodic heal and Electro application
6
Switch to Itto
7
Use Itto's Skill for Geo DMG and Superlative Stacks
8
Use Itto's Normal ATK to gain Superlative Stacks
9
Use Itto's Burst then utilize Charged ATK to consume Superlative Stacks
10
Repeat from step 1
The setup must be completed before using Itto's Burst because this can be cancelled when Itto gets switched out.
Heal Kuki Shinobu When HP Is Very Low
Since Kuki Shinobu consumes HP when she uses Skill, let her be the active character to recover health.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Shatter Itto Team
Character
Detail
Gorou
He is tailored to support for DEF-scaling characters such as Itto. Also, he can restore a bit of HP at C4.
Rosaria
Her Burst can deal Cryo DMG and provide CRIT Rate bonus.
Kokomi
She is an excellent Hydro applicator and healer whose heal, including the over-healing, can deal damage thru Ocean-Hued Clam x4.
The objective of this team is to utilize the Geo resonance with the help of Crystallize. The 2 other characters' elements should be able to freeze the enemies so Itto can shatter them.
Pros & Cons Of Shatter Itto Team
Pros
- Freeze is an effective crowd control
- Geo resonance can increase the damage output as long as the shield is active
- Geo resonance improves the durability of the shield, including the one produced by Crystallize
Cons
- Not all enemies can be frozen such as those with immunity to Cryo and bosses
- Skill of Rosaria & Kokomi are both stationary which is disadvantageous against mobile target that cannot be frozen
Rotation Of Shatter Itto Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Rosaria's Skill & Burst for Cryo particles & CRIT Rate bonus
2
Switch to Kokomi
3
Use Kokomi's Skill for periodic heal and Hydro application
4
Switch to Gorou
5
Use Gorou's Skill & Burst for Geo particles & DEF buff
6
Switch to Itto
7
Use Itto's Skill for Geo DMG and Superlative Stacks
8
Use Itto's Normal ATK to gain Superlative Stacks
9
Use Itto's Burst then utilize Charged ATK to consume Superlative Stacks
10
Repeat from step 1
Ensure that the target is frozen before using Itto's Burst. If the enemy cannot be frozen, you can skip Kokomi and use her Skill only when healing is required.

Workaround For Geo Immunity
Shatter deals subtle Physical DMG. Together with Itto's Normal and Charged ATK without the infusion from his Burst, this is a viable combat method when facing an enemy that is immune to Geo DMG.

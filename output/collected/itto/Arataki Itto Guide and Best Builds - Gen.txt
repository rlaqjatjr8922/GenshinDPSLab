Arataki Itto's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Great at AoE due to his Charged Attacks hitting in large surroudning area.
Itto has great survivability as a result of being a Geo character.
High resistance to interruption during Charged Attacks.
Weaknesses
Strict team composition being heavily tied to 3 Geo characters.
Burst is cancelled if you switch him out during the duration making you loose DPS.

For this build, Arataki Itto's best weapon is his signature weapon
Redhorn Stonethresher
. 
It will provide Itto with CRIT DMG, while also scaling Itto's DEF further. This also increases his Normal
and Charged Attacks, which you will be using a lot. The other weapons mentioned are all
good enough options.
Serpent Spine
is a bit better, however since it is a Battle Pass weapon 
it is less obtainable than the others.
For Itto's build you only really need one set of Artifacts:
Husk of Opulent Dreams
,
which is available as a Strongbox, making it easy to farm. Using a 2-piece
Husk of Opulent Dreams
with a 2-piece
Archaic Petra
is viable if you want to benefit from some additional
Geo
DMG, but it will not perform better than the full
Husk of Opulent Dreams
set.
The primary source of damage for Itto's build will come from his Charged Attacks and Elemental Burst,
so leveling those in priority is important. We recommand you do not leave the Elemental Skill out
completely, as it is still important, but it can be saved for last.

3.
Arataki Itto Combos and How to Play
Arataki Itto is an On-field DPS. This means that it is not recommended to swap him out during his Burst, 
as it will cancel it. You will have to use his teammate's abilities first, before switching to Itto.
In order to make the most out of Itto, you will need to use his Elemental Burst,
Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil!
first. Granting him Superlative Superstrength stacks, and converting his Normal Attacks 
into
Geo
DMG. You can get a total of 5 stacks of Superlative Superstrength.
The number of stacks is indicated behind Itto by some shiny little markers. Once you have 
stacks, you can use Itto's Charged Attack which will deal increased DMG and consume zero stamina.
His special Charged Attack deals massive damage, and has a very powerful finisher hit.
There are multiple ways Itto can gain Superlative Superstrength stacks:
When his Second and Fourth Normal Attack hits an enemy
Through his Elemental Skill:
When Ushi deals damage
When Ushi is being hit
When Ushi leaves the field
During the Elemental Burst on the First and Third Normal Attack
Itto's basic rotation for this build is the following:
Use his teammates abilities first
Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil!
Masatsu Zetsugi: Akaushi Burst!
Fight Club Legend
Charged Attacks
Charged Attack Finisher
Masatsu Zetsugi: Akaushi Burst!
Fight Club Legend
x4 times
Charged Attacks until you are out of stacks
Always make sure that you use your charged attacks when you have stacks available, 
otherwise you loose a lot of DPS.
Never
swap him out before the end of his burst as it 
will cancel it.

Arataki Itto's Best Team Compositions
Arataki Itto does not have many teammates he can be paired with.
Gorou

his best partner, and should always be used with Itto. After that, it is recommended to 
use a third
Geo

Albedo

Chiori

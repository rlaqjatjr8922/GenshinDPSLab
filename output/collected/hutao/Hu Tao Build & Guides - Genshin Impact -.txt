Hu Tao Guide and Best Builds
Hu Tao is a 5-star Pyro Polearm Character main DPS in Genshin Impact who
mainly focuses on Charged Attacks and Vaporize to deal powerful on-field damage
to single target enemies. Read our Hu Tao guide to learn more about
Hu Tao's kit, Hu Tao's best Builds and also her Ascension and Talent Materials.

Hu Tao's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Powerful Single Target damage.
Self Healing using their Burst allowing Hu Tao to manage her HP conversion.
Can provide CRIT Rate for the party.
Weaknesses
Without C1, stamina is consumed on Charged Attacks making gameplay clunky and more dangerous.
Very Reliant on either
Xingqiu
or
Yelan
to apply Hydro for Reactions due to fast Pyro application.
Lacking in AoE attacks outside of Xianyun teams.

Combos and How to Play Hu Tao
Using Hu Tao's Elemental Skill (
Guide to Afterlife
) allows you to
infuse your weapon with
Pyro
making all damage from Hu Tao,
while on-field, Pyro damage as well as providing you with a massive attack
boost at the cost of HP.
Having your HP under 50%, your damage will be further
increased (once you unlock her second passive talent
Sanguine Rouge
) meaning you want to always keep her under 50% HP.
Being under 50% HP also increases the damage and healing of your elemental
burst,
Spirit Soother
.
When under
Guide to Afterlife
, your charged attacks also apply
Blood Blossom
, making enemies take Pyro damage every 4 seconds.
With this and high scaling on charged attack damage, charged attacks are your
main way to deal damage and how you normally cause
Vaporize
with any
Hydro
application currently impacting enemies.
At C0 you ideally want to jump cancel as
soon as damage is dealt to save stamina but this can be difficult especially on
mobile so do not worry if you cannot always time it right. Practice makes
perfect! At C1 however, you can simply dash cancel due to not needing to save
stamina, dash canceling at the end of the attack is also faster therefore
increasing your damage as well as being more comfortable.
When possible, use
Spirit Soother
near the end of
Guide to Afterlife
so that the animation does not take time away
from your damage buff and
Pyro
infusion.

Hu Tao's Best Team Comps
Hu Tao's main team compositions involve
Xingqiu
and/or
Yelan
. Using one or both of these characters for hydro
application,
Hydro resonance
to buff HP as well as
incredibly off-field damage.
Even though Hu Tao benefits from having lower HP, characters such as
Furina
provide massive benefits to the point a
healer becomes valuable.

(C1) is the constellation you want to aim for. This helps to

stamina to avoid attacks before and after your burst.

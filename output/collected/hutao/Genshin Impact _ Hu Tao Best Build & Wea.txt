Hu Tao Skill Guide & How To Use Hu Tao
How To Use Hu Tao?
How To Play Hu Tao?
▼ High damage DPS through her Elemental Skill
▼ Consistent damage through her Charged Attacks
▼ Elemental Burst deals damage and heals
▼ Play around Hu Tao's talents to make the best out of them
▼ A little difficult to use in multiplayer
High Damage DPS Through Her Elemental Skill
▲ Her Elemental Skill lowers her health whenever it's used
Hu Tao is a high damage on-field main DPS. She does most of her damage through her Elemental Skill, wherein during its effect, she gains an attack buff when her HP is under 50%. Use her Normal Attack + Charged Attack to deal the most damage during this state!
Elemental Skill Effects
- Interruption resistance increased
- Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks all become Pyro infuse
- Charged attacks inflict Blood Blossom
- Skill resets when switching character, incapacitation, or duration ends
Consistent Damage Through Her Charged Attacks
▲ Her rotation is Charged Attack → Cancel with Jump → Charged Attack
Hu Tao's Charged Attack has high, consistent damage so it's recommended to do that attack rotation instead of her normal attacks when her Elemental Skill is active. By getting C1, it removed Charged Attack Stamina consumption, making it much easier to spam.
Elemental Burst Deals Damage And Heals
▲ Damage is increased when HP is below 50%
When you use her Elemental Burst and it successfully hit an enemy, Hu Tao will recover HP. The effect is activated with each enemy that the Burst hits (this can be up to 5 enemies), making it great against multiple enemies. Additionally, it can deal damage in wide range, making it great for both offense and defense.
Play Around Hu Tao's Talents To Make The Best Out Of Them
Talent 1
All allies in the party will have a Crit Rate buff after Hu Tao's skill ends
Talent 2
When Hu Tao's HP is 50% or less, her Pyro DMG is increased
There are two things to keep in mind when using Hu Tao. To make the most out of her low HP state, use a shielder on the team like Zhongli. Meanwhile, to take advantage of the increase Crit Rate, have a sub-DPS in your party.
A Little Difficult To Use In Multiplayer
Since her damage can only be generated while her Elemental Skill is in effect, it's difficult to use in multiplayer where characters cannot be switched. Since the deployment of shields and Elemental Reactions cannot be triggered at will, it makes it hard to use her well in multiplayer.
Should You Pull for Hu Tao's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Choose Based on Preference
Recommendation for General-Purpose
Staff of Homa
> C1 > C3 or C6
Staff of Homa is a Strong Generalist Weapon
Hu Tao's Signature Weapon since its release has been considered one of the universal choices for damage-dealing Polearm characters,
even if most of them cannot activate the other half of the passive. Naturally it's at its most synergistic with characters that have fluctuating HP baked into their kit, such as
Xiao
and Hu Tao herself.
Pulling for her Signature Weapon is usually a sound decision pull-wise, especially if the other weapon on the banner is something you can use. But most importantly, it's still Hu Tao's best performing weapon overall thanks to its Crit DMG substat, HP% bonus and Attack bonus that scales with Max HP, which is everything her kit wants.
Consider getting Staff of Homa for both the versatility of use and the damage boost it provides to Hu Tao.
Recommendation for Hu Tao Focus
C1 >
Staff of Homa
> C3 or C6
C1 is a Good Stopping Point
For Hu Tao specifically, having full Refinements (R5) on
Dragon's Bane
and
Deathmatch
may decrease the value of her Signature Weapon, since the two polearms are relatively close in performance to Staff of Homa.
So if you happen to already have the Staff of Homa, or are have max refinements on the two polearms mentioned, then you might want to consider getting her C1 both to smoothen her gameplay feel and as a buff to her damage floor.
Hu Tao's basic and most optimized gameplay loop involves her Pyro-infused Charge Attacks, and
with C1, these Charge Attacks will no longer consume stamina while under the effects of her Skill, allowing you to perform more within a given time frame
This amounts to a significant increase to Hu Tao's personal damage versus her C0 version, and is worth a consideration as both a starting and stopping point for vertical investment. And although C6 is likely an incredible boost in damage, it requires Hu Tao to be at 1 HP. With this in mind, C1 starts becoming her best constellation overall.

Who Is Hu Tao?
77th Director Of The Wangsheng Funeral Parlor
Hu Tao is the young but dedicated 77th Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor. She takes her job seriously, putting the best effort in providing the best funeral services to every type of client.
Eccentric And A Bit Odd
Hu Tao's personality can rub people the wrong way. From befriending stone lions to pushing people to be clients of the funeral parlor, she has a tendency to weird people out. Qiqi, on the other hand, hates Hu Tao for wanting to bury her on multiple occasions.

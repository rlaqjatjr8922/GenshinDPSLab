Ayaka kit
Ayaka is a
5-Star Cryo character who uses a sword
, and keeping in mind that all of the percentages mentioned below are based on Level 1 Skills, here's a summary of
Ayaka's kit in Genshin Impact
:
Image credit:
HoYoverse
Element
: Cryo.
Rarity
: 5-Star.
Weapon
: Sword.
Normal Attack
: Kamisato Art - Kabuki.
Elemental Skill
: Kamisato Art - Hyouka.
Elemental Burst
: Kamisato Art - Soumetsu.
Alternative Sprint
: Kamisato Art - Senho - Ayaka consumes Stamina and cloaks herself in a frozen fog that moves with her. In Senho form, she moves swiftly upon water. When she reappears, Ayaka unleashes frigid energy to apply Cryo on nearby opponents. Additionally, coldness condenses around Ayaka's blade, infusing her attacks with Cryo for a brief period.
Passive one
: Amatsumi Kunitsumi Sanctification - After using her Elemental Skill, Ayaka's normal and charged Attacks deal 30% increased damage for six seconds.
Passive two
: Kanten Senmyou Blessing - When the Cryo application at the end of Ayaka's Elemental Burst hits an opponent, Ayaka restores 10 Stamina and gains 18% Cryo Damage Bonus for 10 seconds.
Passive three
: Fruits of Shinsa - When Ayaka crafts Weapon Ascension Materials, she has a 10% chance to receive double the product.
Ayaka Normal Attack - Kamisato Art: Kabuki
Ayaka Performs up to five rapid strikes, if tapping the normal attack button. Holding it down instead makes Ayaka consume a certain amount of stamina to unleash a flurry of sword ki.
As with most plunging attacks, Ayaka plunges from mid-air and strikes the ground below for AOE (area of effect) damage on impact.
Ayaka Elemental Skill - Kamisato Art: Hyouka
Summons blooming ice to launch nearby opponents, dealing AOE Cryo Damage.
Image credit:
HoYoverse
Ayaka Elemental Burst - Kamisato Art: Soumetsu
Ayaka Summons forth a snowstorm with flawless poise, unleashing a Frostflake Seki no To that moves forward continuously. Frostflake Seki no To is a storm of whirling icy winds that slashes repeatedly at every enemy it touches, dealing Cryo Damage. The snowstorm explodes after its duration ends, dealing AOE Cryo Damage.

standard and beginner Banners

Ayaka Constellations
By getting duplicates of Ayaka from wishing on her Banner, you will receive her Stella Fortuna. This material allows you to unlock Constellation levels, with each upgrade either improving an existing Talent or Passive ability.
Image credit:
HoYoverse
Here are all of
Ayaka's Constellations in Genshin Impact
:
Snowswept Sakura (C1)
: When Ayaka's normal or charged attacks deal Cryo Damage to opponents, it has a 50% chance of decreasing the cooldown of Ayaka's Skill by 0.3 seconds. This effect can occur once every 0.1 seconds.
Blizzard Blade Seki no To (C2)
: When casting her Elemental Burst, Ayakaa unleashes two smaller additional Frostflake Seki no To, each dealing 20% of the original storm's damage.
Frostbloom Kamifubuki (C3)
: Increases the Level of Ayaka's Elemental Burst by three, with a maximum upgrade level of 15.
Ebb and Flow (C4)
: Opponents damaged by the Elemental Burst's Frostflake Seki no To will have their defense decreased by 30% for six seconds.
Blossom Cloud Irutsuki (C5)
:  Increases the Level of Ayaka's Elemental Skill by three, with a maximum upgrade level of 15.
Dance of Suigetsu (C6)
: Kamisato Ayaka gains Usurahi Butou every 10 seconds, increasing her charged attack damage by 298%. This buff will be cleared 0.5 seconds after Ayaka's charged attack hits an opponent, after which the timer for this ability will restart.
Good luck levelling up Ayaka in Genshin Impact!

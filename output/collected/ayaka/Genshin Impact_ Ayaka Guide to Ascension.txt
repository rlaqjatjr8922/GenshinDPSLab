With Inazuma here, we’ve got a huge cast of brand new characters from the land of lightning. Kamisato Ayaka was in game’s beta way back when, and players have been looking forward to her since. Now that she’s finally here, she does not disappoint. Our complete Ayaka guide will help you prepare the proper items to get her Ascended as fast as possible and how to best use her.
Ayaka Ascension Materials Guide
Ayaka is a Sword user that makes full use of her Vision with a Cryo infusion after her alternate sprint. She gains points in CRIT DMG as she Ascends.
Ascension 1- Lv 20
1 Shivada Jade Sliver
3 Sakura Bloom
3 Old Handguard
20,000 Mora
Ascension 2- Lv 40
3 Shivada Jade Fragment
2 Perpetual Heart
10 Sakura Bloom
15 Old Handguard
40,000 Mora
Ascension 3- Lv 50
6 Shivada Jade Fragment
4 Perpetual Heart
20 Sakura Bloom
12 Kageuchi Handguard
60,000 Mora
Ascension 4- Lv 60
3 Shivada Jade Chunk
8 Perpetual Heart

Ascension 5- Lv 70

12 Perpetual Heart

In total, you’ll need 1 Shivada Jade Sliver, 9 Shivada Jade Fragments, 9 Shivada Jade Chunks, 6 Shivada Jade Gemstones, 18 Old Handguard, 30 Kageuchi Handguard, 36 Famed Handguard, 46 Perpetual Heart and 168 Sakura Bloom to get her ascended to Lv 80/90.

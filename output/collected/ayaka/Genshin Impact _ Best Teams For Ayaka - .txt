Popular Teams For Ayaka
Team
Rating
Description
▼Freeze Ayaka
★★★★★
- Ayaka's meta team
- Utilizes Freeze & Cryo-Swirl reactions for boosting Ayaka's damage output
- Can produce a decent amount of elemental particles for Ayaka
▼Reverse Melt Ayaka
★★★★・
-  Enables Ayaka to deal heavy damage thru elemental reactions
- Utilizes Burning & Melt reactions that can boost Ayaka & Pyro character's damage output
- When a Hydro character is included, can even utilize Freeze, Vaporize, Bloom, & Burgeon reactions
▼Mono-Cryo Ayaka
★★★・・
- Good team for Ayaka
- Utilizes Cryo-Swirl reaction that can boost Cryo DMG
- Can produce a lot of elemental particles for all Cryo characters in the team
Ayaka is a Cryo DPS character that mainly deals damage through Elemental Burst and Charged Attacks. You need to form a team around Ayaka to maximize her damage potential. She works especially well at Freeze teams since it allows the enemies to stay still and get all the damage from her Elemental Burst.

Rotation Of Freeze Ayaka Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Furina and activate Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to Shenhe and use Elemental Skill (Press)
3
Switch to Jean and use Elemental Skill to swirl Cryo.
4
Switch to Ayaka and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
5
Switch to Jean and use Elemental Burst.
6
Switch to Shenhe and use Elemental Skill (Hold)
7
Switch to Ayaka and use special dash and deal damage with Normal and Charged Attacks.
8
Once the buffs expire, repeat from number 1.
The goal is to keep the buffs up before using Ayaka's Normal Attacks, Elemental Skill and Burst.

switch to Zhongli to refresh the shield's duration
. You can also use Zhongli's Burst for crowd control.

Since Cryo & Dendro do not react with each other, a Melt reaction is guaranteed to occur
.
More Elemental Reactions
When Dendro is retained and Hydro is included, the team can trigger Freeze, Melt, Vaporize, Bloom, & Burgeon reactions which are all great.

Rotation Of Mono-Cryo Ayaka Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Diona's Elemental Skill for increased movement speed, shield, & Cryo application to enemies
2
Switch to Kazuha
3
Use Kazuha's Elemental Burst for Cryo-Swirl then Elemental Skill for crowd control
4
Switch to Shenhe
5
Use Shenhe's Elemental Burst for Cryo DMG Bonus
6
Use Shenhe's Elemental Skill for buff: Press for boosting Skill & Burst & Hold for boosting NA/CA/PA
7
Switch to Ayaka
8
Use Ayaka's alternate sprint for Cryo DMG Bonus
9
Use Ayaka's Elemental Burst then Elemental Skill for boosting NA/CA
From time to time, you can
switch to Kazuha if you need crowd control
. If he triggers Cryo-Swirl, the resistance shred debuff is refreshed. Also, you can
switch to Diona if you need a shield or heal
. Since this team produces a lot of Cryo particles, Diona's Burst can be used more often.

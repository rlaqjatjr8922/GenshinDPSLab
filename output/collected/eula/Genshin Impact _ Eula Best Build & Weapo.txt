Eula is a 5-star Cryo character that wields a Claymore in Genshin Impact. Her gameplay centers on high physical damage output as an on-field DPS. Eula builds up stacks to unleash a devastating burst of physical damage, making her one of the hardest-hitting characters in the game when fully set up. She excels in teams that can quickly trigger Superconduct to reduce enemy physical resistance.

Roles & Playstyle Of Eula and Its Strengths and Weakness
Physical DPS Eula
Eula's intended role is to be played as a Physical DPS with her teammates supporting her. With ATK SPD buffs from her teammates and a shield, Eula can deal damage effectively and efficiently.

Team Rotation of Eula and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for On-Field DPS Eula
Basic Rotation For On-Field DPS Eula
1. Activate Off-Field Support/Sub-DPS abilities in order of longest duration to shortest duration.
2. Use Elemental Skill (Tap) then Elemental Burst.
3. Use Normal Attack x4 to start gaining stacks.
4. Use Elemental Skill (Hold) then Normal Attack x4.
Eula is a DPS character who needs specific rotations to gain stacks for her Burst. Her Normal Attacks and Elemental Skill uses each contribute a stack after they deal damage to an enemy.
Basics of Using Eula (Beginner Guide)
Physical DMG Dealer That Uses Fast Attacks
Eula is a damage dealer that specializes in Physical DMG. Her Normal Attacks are her most used attacks for stacking her Elemental Burst to deal a powerful Physical attack.
Only Needs Superconduct for Reactions
Similar to Mono Element DPS characters, Eula only needs one reaction to boost her damage. Superconduct works well with her since she's able to apply few instances of Cryo so you will only need an Electro Sub-DPS to help apply Electro. See below for the best Electro Sub-DPS to use with Eula:
Character
Explanation
Ineffa
While Ineffa won't be able to trigger Lunar-Charged, her Shield and Electro application can help Eula a lot.
Raiden Shogun
Raiden Shogun's 100% uptime on her Elemental Skill allows Eula to have more field time.
Fischl
Fischl will need more field time compared to Raiden but she can generate more Energy Particles and apply Electro easily.
ATK SPD Buffs Are Really Good For Eula
Since Eula is a Physical DMG dealer, she is reliant on her Normal Attacks to help deal damage aside from stacking for her Burst. ATK SPD buffers like Mika and Dahlia allow Eula to deal more Normal Attacks and in turn gain more stacks for her Elemental Burst.

Powerful Damage in Burst But Requires Stacking
Eula's Elemental Burst does the most damage in her rotations. She does use her Normal Attack a lot but her Burst does more damage. However her Burst needs 30 stacks to reach the maximum damage it can do. Her Normal Attacks and Skill gives 1 stack per 0.1s.
ATK SPD Is Greatly Needed for Burst Stacking
Eula's stacking mechanic on her Burst wasn't the best since she can only gain at most 13 to 16 stacks per rotation. Since ATK SPD buff caps at 42%, it becomes harder to obtain more stacks unless her Constellation 6 is obtained. Even with C6, reaching 30 stacks is RNG-reliant.
Elemental Skill, Burst and Abilities Guide for Eula
How To Use Eula's Skills & Passives
How To Play Eula
▼Cryo DPS that deals primarily Physical damage.
▼Burst is her main source of DPS.
▼Build stacks with her Normal Attacks and Skill.
▼Passives help define her combos.
▼Make use of the Superconduct reaction to increase her damage.
Cryo DPS That Deals Primarily Physical Damage
Basic Rotation Of Eula
1.Start the rotation with the supports, ideally in order of longest ability duration to shortest.
2. Swap to Eula. Use her Skill (tap), then deploy her Burst to summon the Lightfall Sword.
└ Her ideal combo for gaining her Lightfall stacks should be: Normal Attack (x4) → Skill (hold) →  Normal Attack (x4).
3. Repeat from Step 1.
Despite being Cryo,
Eula was designed to be a Physical DPS with a stacking mechanic tied to the amount of damage her Elemental Burst can deal.
She's best played alongside supports that can help amplify her damage either by triggering Superconduct or by providing helpful buffs.
Burst Is Her Main Source of DPS
Elemental Burst Summary
Icon
Elemental Burst Summary
Glacial Illumination
Brandishes her greatsword, dealing Cryo DMG to nearby opponents and creating a Lightfall Sword that follows her for up to 7s.
Lightfall Sword:
- Increases Eula's interruption resistance while active.
- When Eula's Normal Attacks, Elemental Skill, or Elemental Burst deal DMG, the Lightfall Sword gains 1 energy stack (max 1 stack every 0.1s).
- When the duration ends, the Lightfall Sword descends and explodes, dealing Physical DMG to nearby opponents.
- DMG scales based on the number of energy stacks accumulated.
- If Eula leaves the field, the Lightfall Sword explodes immediately.
The majority of Eula's DPS comes from her Elemental Burst.
This ability summons a Lightfall Sword, and whenever her Normal Attack Elemental Skill or Burst hits an enemy, she gains a stack of Lightfall. The Sword will then explode after some time, dealing Physical damage according to the amount of Lightfall stacks that Eula generates prior to the explosion.
Because this is her most important ability, it's recommended to prioritize getting to her ideal Energy Recharge stat, apart from including another Cryo character in the team to help with her energy needs.
Note!
Eula's Burst can miss if the target moves away from the explosion's range before it goes off, so try to position yourself accordingly. You can also swap out of Eula early to prematurely detonate her Burst if needed.
Build Stacks With Her Normal Attacks and Skill
Elemental Skill Summary
Icon
Elemental Skill Summary
Icetide Vortex
Tap:
- Slashes swiftly, dealing Cryo DMG.
- When it hits an opponent, Eula gains 1 stack of Grimheart (max 2 stacks).
- Can gain 1 stack every 0.3s.
Hold:
- Consumes all Grimheart stacks.
- Unleashes a forward slash, dealing AoE Cryo DMG.
Grimheart Stack
:
- Increases Eula's interruption resistance.
- Increases Eula's DEF.
If Grimheart stacks are consumed:
- Decreases nearby opponents' Physical RES and Cryo RES.
- Each consumed stack becomes an Icewhirl Brand, dealing Cryo DMG to nearby opponents.
Eula's Elemental Skill, which has 2 versions, generates 2 different stacks.
Firstly, dealing damage with the Skill is counted towards her Lightfall Stack mechanic. Secondly, tapping her Skill creates a Grimheart stack, which provides her with increased DEF and reduces an enemy's Physical and Cryo RES once you consume the stack by holding her Skill.
Eula's ideal combo involves using both the Tap and Hold version of her Skill since consuming a Grimheart stack does contribute to her overall DPS.
Passives Help Define Her Combos
Passive Descriptions
Icon
Ascension 1 Passive Summary
Roiling Rime
If 2 stacks of Grimheart are consumed upon unleashing the Holding Mode of Icetide Vortex, a Shattered Lightfall Sword will be created that will explode immediately, dealing 50% of the basic Physical DMG dealt by a Lightfall Sword created by Glacial Illumination.
Icon
Ascension 2 Passive Summary
Wellspring
of War-Lust
When Glacial Illumination is cast, the CD of Icetide Vortex is reset and Eula gains 1 stack of Grimheart.
Eula's passives both accomplish two different things individually, but together
they help shape her best DPS combo and therefore her overall gameplay feel.
Her Ascension 1 Passive makes strongly encourages you to use her Hold Skill to consume her Grimheart stacks for more damage, and her Ascension 2 Passive expands on this by allowing her Skill CD to be reset after you use her Burst.
Make Use of the Superconduct Reaction to Increase Her Damage
Sample Superconduct Eula Team
Eula
Raiden

Superconduct is a reaction that happens after applying
Cryo
on a target already inflicted with
Electro
(Electro on Cryo also works), though the order of application doesn't really matter in practice.
This reaction inflicts AoE Cryo DMG on all affected targets, reducing their Physical RES at the same time.
When combined with Eula's innate Physical RES shred, this amounts to a decent damage boost overall.
Should You Pull for Eula's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Prioritize Eula's Constellations
Signature VS Constellations
C1 >
Song of Broken Pines
or C3 > C6
C1 is a Good Stopping Point
Of course, because it was tailored around her kit, Eula's Signature Weapon is going to be her best in slot choice, but
Beacon of the Reed Sea
and
Wolf's Gravestone
will be very competitive and far more universal than Song of the Broken Pines. Because of this, it's recommended to consider pulling for her constellations instead.
Most notably, the Physical DMG bonus that she can get from her C1 is a noticeable damage increase at the cost of nothing except but a basic understanding of how Eula is supposed to be played.
Eula will still function in her intended niche as a Physical On-Field carry at C0, but if you would like to upgrade her performance without considering her supports, then constellations, especially C1, are a good starting and stopping point.
Beyond C1, the ideal milestones would be C3 that adds more levels to her Burst, which is the bulk of her damage, and C6 which compounds everything from C1 to C5, and even lets her accumulate more stacks than usual for her Burst to achieve incredibly high instances of damage.
In contrast, her Signature Weapon increases in value if you do not have
Beacon of the Reed Sea
or
Wolf's Gravestone
, or if you already have constellations on her.
This may be a good enough reason for you to pull, especially if you only have her 4-star alternatives and if you're okay with losing to the other weapon on the banner. Just remember that realistically, only Eula can truly benefit from Song of Broken Pines.
In general,
if you want to invest only into Eula, pulling for her constellations is going to be more valuable than her signature weapon, but the choice will ultimately come down to your preference and funds.

Effect
Brandishes her greatsword, dealing Cryo DMG to nearby opponents and creating a Lightfall Sword that follows her around for a duration of 7s.
While present, the Lightfall Sword increases Eula's resistance to interruption. When Eula's own Normal Attack, Elemental Skill, and Elemental Burst deal DMG to opponents, they will charge Lightfall Sword, which can gain energy once every 0.1s.
Once its duration ends, the Lightfall Sword will descend and explode violently, dealing Physical DMG to nearby opponents.
This DMG sales on the number of energy stacks the Lightfall Sword accumulated.
If Eula leaves the field, the Lightfall Sword will explode immediately.

Who Is Eula?
Captain Of The Knights Of Favonius Reconnaissance Company
Eula is the Captain of the Reconnaissance Company of the Knights of Favonius. As her role suggests, she's often out of the city to do missions within the region's wilderness. Their goal is to keep any monsters or Abyss Order operatives from approaching Mondstadt.
A Descendant Of The Lawrence Clan
Eula comes from the disgraced Lawrence Clan, a family known in Mondstadt's history as an oppressive and corrupt aristocracy. This gives her an unwarranted bad reputation with people often being wary of her or thinking she's spying on the Knights of Favonius.
Close Friends With Amber
Because of her reputation, Eula does not have many friends. She did find a really good friend in Amber who would often join her shopping or to go around town. Just being with Amber will make people treat Eula a little bit better.

The Columbina-Ineffa core is a strong Sub-DPS and Support pairing that can output respectable damage just on their own
, allowing you to run various DPSes that have bare minimum synergy with either of them. Eula still benefits from the presence of Electro in the party, which enables her to trigger Superconduct.
Essentially, this team combines the reliable DPS from both Lunar characters with Eula's single-hit nuke on her Burst. It's a viable Eula comp, if not her strongest, but will obviously pale in comparison to the majority of the top meta teams.

3
Swap to Mika. Use his Skill (hold), then Burst if you need the healing.
4
Swap to Eula. Perform her best damage combo.

5
Repeat from step 1.
Substitute Characters

Mika

Sucrose
- Mika is a synergistic Cryo support for Eula due to his modest ATK SPD buffs and healing, but he's still replaceable.
- Any generic Cryo Support, Sustain or Sub-DPS, like Rosaria or Charlotte, can be decent substitutes.
- Alternatively, you can also make use of Sucrose for her EM buffs, which can raise Ineffa and Columbina's DPS a bit more.
Ineffa
Fischl
Ororon
Raiden Shogun
Yae Miko
- Ineffa's personal damage makes up a large chunk of the total team's DPS, so replacing her will noticeably impact the team's performance.
- Her best alternatives are other Electro Sub-DPSes with decent personal damage.
Columbina
Aino
Yelan
Furina
- Columbina provides a lot of Lunar Reaction buffs on top of her personal DPS, replacing her will inevitably lower the team's performance.
- You can substitute her with any Off-Field Hydro support, but just note that DMG% buffers like Yelan and Furina wouldn't buff Ineffa's damage due to how Lunar-Charged is calculated.
It's best to keep either Ineffa or Columbina on the team to maintain its identity as a Lunar-Charged composition, as they enable the conversion from the base Electro-Charged reaction. An Eula team relying only on Electro-Charged will be underwhelming; you will likely get better results by running Eula in a traditional Superconduct team.

This team is designed to maximize Eula's Physical DPS, making it her best composition if you want to focus on her personal damage.
Rotation Of Physical or Superconduct Eula Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina. Use her Skill, dash to cancel the long animation, then Burst.
2
Switch to Raiden Shogun. Deploy her Skill.
3
Use Mika's Skill (hold) then Burst if you need the healing.
4
Swap to Eula. Perform her best damage combo.
└ Skill (tap) → Normal Attack (x4) → Skill (hold) →  Normal Attack (x4)
5
Switch to Raiden again. Use her Burst, then perform her best damage combo for this setup.
└ [ Normal Attack → Charged Attack ] (x2) → Normal Attack (x2)→ Charged Attack
6
Repeat from step 1.
Due to Raiden's Burst providing Energy, her role in the team is more than just an Electro applier and Burst DMG% buffer. After Eula's combo, you'll swap to Raiden and perform her full damage combo to provide Energy for the entire team.

Raiden Shogun

Kuki Shinobu
- No other Electro Sub-DPS or Support can truly replicate what Raiden does and provides for this Eula team, but they can at least do the bare minimum of Electro to trigger Superconduct.
- Raiden is best substituted with other Electro Sub-DPSes with decent personal damage.
Furina
Bennett
Yelan
Zhongli
- Furina's spot on the team is less of an Elemental applicator and more of a damage buffer, so she can be replaced by any other generic buffer that can provide Eula useful stats like ATK%, Elemental RES Shred or DMG%.
Mika
Rosaria
Charlotte
Shenhe
- Alongside acting as Eula's Cryo particle battery, Mika also gives ATK SPD buffs and healing. He is still very replaceable however, and you can make do with any other Cryo Support or Sub-DPS.
To consistently trigger Superconduct, it's best to reserve one slot for an Electro character. Additionally, having another Cryo character not only lowers Eula's Energy requirements,
it also enables Cryo Resonance, which raises Eula's CRIT Rate against enemies affected by Cryo.
Extra sources of CRIT Rate are beneficial for Eula, especially since her Signature Weapon doesn't provide CRIT.

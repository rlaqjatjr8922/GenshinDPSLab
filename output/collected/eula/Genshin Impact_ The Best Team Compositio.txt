It's been a while since Eula's banner expired in
Genshin Impact
, yet new world records are still coming in. The latest right now is a whopping
six million points of Physical damage
. That makes Eula the most powerful damage dealer in
Genshin Impact
in a certain category. What's even more astounding is that it's surprisingly easy to make her deal tons of damage.
All she needs is a certain elemental reaction and some support characters that shred enemy resistance. Granted, most players likely won't reach the dizzying heights of that aforementioned world record. They can, however, make Eula consistently deal six-digit damage numbers. To reach those, figures, here are some optimal Eula teams.
Updated on July 14, 2023, by Nahda Nabiilah:
Like any other DPS, Eula’s kit and damage are directly connected to the Genshin Impact’s meta. Thanks to the constant update and additional characters, Eula now has new pairings that she works brilliantly with. As an example, Huffman's brother, Mika, is the perfect support for Physical DPS. For that, this list will be updated to include all the new
team compositions for Eula
in Genshin Impact.
17
Eula
-
Beidou
-
Bennett
-
Diona
This team functions similarly to traditional Superconduct Eula teams involving an Electro DPS backup, buffers, and healers. In this case, there are two healers. The reason Diona is there instead of Zhongli is to
capitalize on Cryo Resonance
. The gameplay for this team is also straightforward.
How To Use It
Activate Diona's Elemental Skill (hold variant).
Lay down Bennett's Elemental Burst.
Use Beidou's Elemental Burst.
Use Eula's Elemental Skill (tap variant).
Use Eula's Elemental Burst.
Attack with Eula four times, then use her Elemental Skill (hold variant) before attacking again four times to build up her Burst damage stacks.
Beidou can act as a backup DPS if Eula's Burst isn't ready yet.
Repeat step 1 once Eula's Elemental Burst is ready.

Yet another Superconduct team, this one's a little more expensive, but is very much worth it since
Raiden Shogun ensures that Eula's Elemental Burst
is ready after cooldown. No healers are present here, but Zhongli ensures everyone stays alive. Rosaria at C6 also lets her Elemental Burst decrease an opponent's Physical resistance by 20 percent, which is quite big.

How To Use It
Activate Zhongli's Elemental Skill (hold variant).
Use Raiden Shogun's Elemental Skill.

Here's an alternative for those who want a bit of everything minus the healing.
Yun Jin at C6 has an Elemental Burst that boosts Normal Attack speed

How To Use It

use Raiden Shogun's Elemental Burst

-

Kujou Sara
One of the biggest problems with Eula's abilities is that
her Elemental Burst costs a lot
. Often, players will find themselves just waiting for it to fill up. Having Raiden Shogun on the team helps immensely with this. Not only that, but she also acts as a
secondary DPS
for when Eula's stubborn Elemental Burst takes too long to fill up. Meanwhile,
Kujou Sara provides even more energy
, and synergizes well with Raiden Shogun being a damage dealer.
How To Use It
Use Diona's Elemental Skill (hold variant) to procure a shield.
Use Raiden Shogun's Elemental Skill.
Activate Kujou Sara's Elemental Burst.
Eula's Elemental Skill (tap variant) should be used next.
Use Eula's Elemental Burst.
Attack with Eula four times, then use her Elemental Skill (hold variant) before attacking again four times to build up her Burst damage stacks.
Use Diona's abilities to generate energy particles, then switch back to Eula immediately so she catches the particles.
If Eula's Burst isn't available yet, switch to Raiden Shogun and use her Elemental Burst.
Keep attacking with Raiden Shogun until Eula's Burst is available.
Repeat step 1 once Eula's Elemental Burst is ready.

For something simpler (albeit
less effective in the Spiral Abyss
), this Eula team comp might be more preferable. It also features Raiden Shogun, but her utility here is the same as Albedo and Fischl: to provide some automatic supplemental attacks for Eula. If all goes well, then only Eula would be using her Elemental Burst.
Lack of healing is notable
but easily circumvented using NRE
. Besides, Eula's a tough girl anyway.
How To Use It
Place Albedo's Elemental Skill near enemies
.
Use Raiden Shogun's Elemental Skill.
Use Fischl's Elemental Skill or Burst.
Stay inside Albedo's Skill circle.
Use Eula's Elemental Skill (tap variant).
Use Eula's Elemental Burst.
Attack with Eula four times, then use her Elemental Skill (hold variant) before attacking again four times to build up her Burst damage stacks.
If enemies are still alive and Eula's Burst isn't available yet, then use Albedo's Elemental Burst.
Use Fischl's Elemental Burst next (if applicable).
Activate Raiden Shogun's Elemental Burst and keep attacking.
Repeat step 1 once Eula's Elemental Burst is ready.

Eula – Diona – Fischl – Zhongli in genshin impact (1)
Maximizing Eula for damage can be an initially complex affair, but it all revolves around one thing:
dealing hefty Physical damage.
Players can do this either by buffing Eula up a lot or reducing the enemy's defenses. That's where the
Superconduct (Cryo + Electro)
elemental reaction comes in. It reduces enemy Physical resistance.
It just so happens that
Fischl is one of the best at applying Electro to enemies
.
Eula's inherent Cryo abilities can trigger superconduct
. Diona is there for healing or backup Cryo application, while Zhongli further reduces enemy defenses with his Jade Shield.
How To Use It
Use Zhongli's Elemental Skill (hold variant) to procure a Jade Shield.
Use Fischl's Elemental Skill.
Eula's Elemental Skill (tap variant) should be used next.
Use Eula's Elemental Burst.
Attack with Eula four times, and then use her Elemental Skill (hold variant) before attacking again four times to build up her Burst damage stacks.
Use Diona's abilities to generate energy particles, then switch back to Eula immediately, so she catches the particles.
Repeat step 1 once Eula's Elemental Burst is ready.

Eula – Mona – Bennett – Xinyan (C4) in genshin impact (1)
Looking for the team composition that dealt the current highest damage with Eula? This is the one. Mona's Elemental Burst grants an absurd amount of damage bonus. Meanwhile, Bennett and Xinyan's
Pyro resonance, buffs, and Physical resistance
shred
all add up to that obscene damage.
All that's left is for Eula to destroy the survivors. The only problem here is that there's no Cryo battery for Eula, so players will have to make up for that by
giving her Energy Recharge.
How To Use It
Start off with Mona's Elemental Burst.
Use Bennett's Elemental Burst.
Use Xinyan's Elemental Skill
.
With the tap variant, use Eula's Elemental Skill.
Use Eula's Elemental Burst.
Attack with Eula four times, then use her Elemental Skill (hold variant) before attacking again four times to build up her Burst damage stacks.
Repeat step 1 once Eula's Elemental Burst is ready.

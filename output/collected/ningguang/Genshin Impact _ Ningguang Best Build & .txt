Strengths & Weakness Of Ningguang
Strengths
- Powerful Charged Attack especially when there are max Star Jade.
- Can increase Geo DMG & block enemy projectiles with her Elemental Skill
- Low energy cost for Elemental Burst
- Does not rely on reactions to deal maximum damage.
Weakness
- Fairly slow movements as a Main DPS and has difficulty dodging.
- Requires a fairly high amount of investment in End-Game

Ningguang Skill Guide & How To Use Ningguang
Recommended Rotation For Ningguang
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Elemental Skill, then pass through its veil
2
Spam normal attacks until you get 3 Star Jades. Unleash Charged Attack for additional damage
3
If Elemental Skill is still active, pass through it again then use Elemental Burst. Ideal to use Burst when you also have 3 Star Jades
4
Return to no.1
Uses Of Ningguang's Elemental Skill
Creates A Protective Veil Against Projectiles
Besides the Geo buff, the veil can also protect characters from incoming projectiles. However, because the Skill can be destroyed, this is not a reliable way of escaping enemy attacks.
Amplifies Geo Damage
A character that passes through the veil of Ningguang's Elemental Skill receives Geo DMG bonus. This is a set-up ability and should be used before going on the offensive.
Additional Projectiles Upon Using Elemental Burst
While the veil is active, it retracts and produces additional projectiles when Ningguang has used Elemental Burst.
Uses Of Ningguang's Elemental Burst
Explosive Geo Burst Damage
Ningguang's Elemental Burst is a purely offensive ability. Unleash it as much as you can with the right set-up to get the most out of it.
Constellation 6 Greatly Increases The Damage Created
At Constellation 6, Ningguang's Elemental Burst will create 6 Star Jades that will greatly increase the damage of your next charged attack through its raw power.
Normal Attack & Charge Attack Is The Main Source Of Damage
When using Ningguang as a DPS, most of the damage will come from the combination of Normal Attacks and Charged Attacks.
Perform 3 Normal Attacks Then Use Charged Attack
Every Normal Attack that hit the enemies will create Star Jades. You can unleash these jades by using Charged Attack.
Should You Pull for Ningguang's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim For C6 if You're Able To
Constellation Priority
C6 > C2
C6 Is Ideal For Damage
If you have the means to do,
Ningguang's C6 will be her best constellation performance-wise as a Geo DPS character, since this allows Starshatter to generate more Star Jades, further improving the damage output from one Charge Attack.
Getting her to C6 is the ideal scenario, but you can settle for her C2, due to her Skill cooldown reset allowing you to combo a Skill, then Burst, then Skill again, which is a nice boost to her damage per rotation while also lowering her Energy requirements.
Ningguang's damage may not be as impressive as other characters in the same niche, but if you would really want to invest in her and want to see her perform decently outside of investing into her weapon and premiere supports, then
her constellations are going to be the first step.
As a note, always consider your preferences and fund availability prior to pulling.

Who Is Ningguang?
Tianquan Of The Liyue Harbor
Ningguang as the Tianquan of Liyue Harbor is the richest person in the region. As the Tianquan, she helps to regulate the rules of business in the region.
Cares For Liyue Beyond Business
People might think that Ningguang would be a selfish person whose only goal is to make herself rich. However, it's been proven that she does care for her nation and is willing to sacrifice even her beloved Jade Chamber to save it.
Has Great Ambitions
Ningguang's ambitions seemingly has no end. What was once just a room in the skies is now the Jade Champer that casts a shadow in Liyue. Someday, she believes, it will cast a shadow across the whole of Teyvat.

Dori Breakdown
Dori
is a Healer with ok healing abilities. She does provide the team with some healing and energy recharge but lacks energy herself.
Is it worth it to pull Dori?
There are a lot of good healers in the game, I think it isn't worth it to pull her because she does require a lot of ER.
How to get Dori
Wishes

Constellations
C4-Discretionary Supplement:
Buff member party HP and Energy if their HP and Energy are Lower than 50%.
C2-Special Franchise:
Party members who get JInni heal will deal 50% of Dori's ATK DMG.
C1-Additional Investment:
Increase Troubleshooter by 1.

1. Burst:
If you planning to build Dori as a healer, this talent should be prioritized. This will increase  more healing and energy regeneration.

3. Normal ATK:
Lastly, This will be the last skill that we need to upgrade since she is a healer.
Best Weapons For Dori
Best 3 Stars Claymore Weapons
For newbies, you can't find 4 5 stars weapons at low AR. If you just got
Dori
as your first 4 stars character and plan to use her as a healer or support reaction you can also use these 3 stars' weapons to maximize
Dori's
healing abilities as well as buff party members. Since
Dori
is a healer and his healing abilities are base on Her base HP, we will focus on Claymore which provides an
HP
or
EM
if you want some to do some reaction.
1. Ferrous Shadow (Best)
Since this Claymore has sub-stats as
HP%
, this will best 3 stars weapon for Dori, and When HP is below 70%, increase the charge ATK DMG.
2. Bloodtainted Greatsword
If you looking for some EM DMG this will be a great weapon for Dori to deal with reaction DMG.
3. Quartz
Great for Overload and Vaporize build.
Best 4 Stars Claymore Weapons
When you get higher AR you will start getting 4 stars weapons. They are P2P or Craft weapons or Battle Pass to use if you don't have primo gem to do wishes. Here are a few weapons that are suitable for Dori's play style. Here we are looking for
HP%
and also with ER will be a big plus.
1. The Bell (Best For healer)
This is only 4 stars Claymore with
HP%
as sub-stats and when taking DMG generate a shield which absorbs DMG up to 20% of Max HP.
2. Favonius Greatsword(Support)
Give a huge amount of ER as Sub stats, and have a chance to regenerate a small amount of energy if Crit Hit.
3. Sacrificial Sword
After the Elemental skill hits the skill has 40% chance to end its own CD which is great for supporters to use Elemental burst more often.
4. Forest Regalia(Best for Reaction Support)
Great for Dori reaction build since after triggering Quicken, Aggravate, and Hyperbloomn will create Leaf will grant 60 EM buff.
Best 5 Stars Claymore Weapons
The only way to get a 5-star weapon for Dori is by wishing. It will randomly drop when you are doing wish if you are a lucky one. Unfortunately, there are not many 5 stars Claymore that suit him yet since we looking for EM sub stats.
1. Skyward Blade(Best 5 stars)
There aren't a lot 5 stars Claymore that is compatible with her. however, you can use Skyward blade since this provides some ER and increase DMG after using burst.

4 Pieces:

Ocean-Hued Clam
2 Pieces:
Healing Bonus +15%
4 Pieces:
When the character equipping this artifact set heals a character in the party, a Sea-Dyed Foam will appear for 3 second, accumulating the amount of HP recovered from healing (including overflow healing). At the end of the duration, the Sea-Dyed Foam will explode, dealing DMG to nearby opponents based on 90% of the accumulated healing. (This DMG is calculated similarly to Reactions such as Electro-Charged, and Superconduct, but is not affected by Elemental Mastery, Character Levels, or Reaction DMG Bonuses). Only one Sea Dyed Foam can be produced every 3.5 seconds. Each Sea-Dyed Foam can accumulate up to 30,000HP (including overflow healing). There can be no more one Sea Dyed Foam active at any given time. This effect can still be triggered even when the character who is using this artifact set is not on the field.
Nobless Oblige
2 Pieces:
Elemental Burst DMG +20%
4 Pieces:
Using an Elemental Burst increases all party members ATK by 20% for 12s. This effect cannot stack.
Flower of Paradise
2 Pieces:
Increase Elemental Mastery by 80.
4 Pieces:
The equipping character's Bloom, Hyperbloom, and Burgeon reaction DMG are increased by 40%. Additionally, after the equipping character triggers Bloom, Hyperbloom, or Burgeon, they will gain another 25% bonus to the effect mentioned prior. Each stack of this lasts 10s. Max 4 stacks simultaneously. This effect can only be triggered once per second. The character who equips this can still trigger its effects when not on the field.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Superconduct Dori Team
Character
Detail
Eula
Eula is a melee Physical DPS and who can improve Physical DMG thru Superconduct so pairing her with Dori is great.
Zhongli
Zhongli's durable shield has a Physical RES debuff which makes him an amazing support for Eula.
Mika
Mika is a great support for Physical team comp who can give buffs for ATK SPD & Physical DMG Bonus which are beneficial for Eula.
The objective of this team is to trigger superconduct with Dori's tether and a Cryo application from another character which can improve the damage output of Physical DPS.
Pros & Cons Of Superconduct Dori Team
Pros
- When you already have a Cryo to pair with Dori, the other 2 slots are free for support & off-field DPS
Cons
- You need to be tethered to Dori's lamp so you need to be close to its location or you cannot apply Electro
Rotation Of Superconduct Dori Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Zhongli's Skill for shield
2
Switch to Eula
3
Press Eula's Elemental Skill for Physical RES debuff & stack
4
Switch to Dori
5
Use Dori's Elemental Burst for Electro application thru tether
* With C2, Electro projectiles are released while on tether
6
Switch to Mika
7
Use Mika's Elemental Skill & Burst for buffs
8
Switch to Eula
9
Press Eula's Elemental Skill then utilize normal ATK until Elemental Skill cooldown has refreshed
10
Hold Eula's Elemental Skill to consume 2 stacks
11
Use Eula's Elemental Burst for Lightfall Sword & refresh Skill cooldown
12
Do step 7 until you gain 2 stacks then proceed with step 8
13
Return to step 1
From time to time, you can use Dori's Skill to produce Electro particles for Burst.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Electro Battery Dori Team
Character
Detail
Yae Miko
Yae Miko's turrets can intermittently deal Electro DMG. With Dori as battery & Electro resonance, Yae Miko can conveniently accumulate energy for her Burst that costs 90 energy.
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of Hyperblooms.
Xingqiu
The consistent Hydro application can produce Dendro cores, especially when the active character is beside the enemy marked with Nahida's Skill.
The objective of this team is to produce blooms with Nahida's mark & Xingqiu's Hydro application then transform into homing hyperbloom projectiles with Yae Miko's turrets & Dori's tether.
Pros & Cons Of Electro Battery Dori Team
Pros
- Yae Miko's Burst can be casted more
- Either Yae Miko or Nahida can be the on-field DPS
- Dori can easily use Burst due to abundant Electro particles
Cons
- You need to be tethered to Dori's lamp to heal the active character
- Dori's C2 may interfere with hyperbloom
Rotation Of Electro Battery Dori Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Burst for EM buff
2
Use Nahida's Skill for mark
3
Switch to Yae Miko
4
Use Yae Miko's Skill thrice to deploy 3 turrets
5
Switch to Dori
6
Use Dori's Elemental Burst for Electro application thru tether
* With C2, Electro projectiles are released while on tether
7
Use Dori's Elemental Skill for producing Electro particles
* Can use twice if Sacrificial Greatsword refreshed Dori's Skill cooldown
8
Switch to Xingqiu
9
Use Xingqiu's Elemental Skill & Burst for Hydro application & coordinated attack
10
Switch to either Yae Miko or Nahida
11
Utilize normal ATK until Yae Miko's turrets start to disappear
12
Return to step 1
From time to time, you can use Dori's Skill to produce Electro particles for her & Yae Miko's Burst.

1.
Heizou Main DPS Build
Heizou has the unique ability to infuse
Anemo
into all of
his standard attacks, this allows him to
Swirl
with really good consistency. This unique combination makes him a very good
elemental driver on-field, as long as he has a team designed to take advantage
of this.
Heizou also has a stacking mechanic called Declension stacks that
build up power for his Elemental Burst
Heartstopper Strike
.
With proper timing and good practice, Heizou can become a decent melee brawler,
weaving both his Elemental abilities into his standard attacks for a really
strong flow of Elemental Reactions.

2.
Combos and How to Play Heizou
Heizou can be played as an on-field brawler or as a quick-swap Elemental Reaction
driver. These are his two primary playstyles for DPS, with each of them coming
down to personal preference and enjoyment. They both can deal damage
moderately well, but the damage output does not put him in the top tier of
DSPers. However, Heizou is a very smooth character to experience combat with
and the fantasy of a melee martial artist is very much at the heart of his
playstyle.
Heizou's standard skills are infused with
Anemo
and he can
use his charged attack to
Swirl
enemies on top of dealing a
decent amount of Anemo damage. His Elemental Burst,
Heartstopper Strike
, has a built in stacking mechanic
called 'Declension'. These stacks are built automatically each
time you Swirl an enemy up to 4 stacks. The skill can also be charged(held
down) to build these stacks manually then unleashed. His Elemental Burst,
Windmuster Kick
, is a great skill to drive Elemental
Reactions as it can group enemies together for a blast of Anemo damage, but
also unleashes additional damage if those enemies are under the effects of
another element. This Elemental Burst also has a low cooldown at 12 seconds and
an Energy cost of only 40 which means Heizou's Energy Recharge requirement
aren't massive, so aiming for 175% ER lines up his rotations quite nicely.
3.
Heizou's Best Team Comps
Heizou is an on-field DPS with smooth combat and some fun quick-swap
potential. The majority of Heizou's damage comes from producing Elemental
Reactions so his teammate selection is very important. It is best to place
match him teammates who can take advantage of quick-swapping with fast
rotations and solid off-field skills that can last. Another top priority is
strong Elemental application, and keeping the enemies debuffed.

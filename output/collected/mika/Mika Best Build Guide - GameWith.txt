Mika is a 4-star Cryo character who wields a polearm in Genshin Impact. His gameplay focuses on being a Support, using his Elemental Skill to boost team Attack Speed and Physical Damage. His Elemental Burst provides instant party healing and grants the Eagleplume state, enabling additional healing through Normal Attacks. Lore-wise, Mika serves as a Surveyor of the Reconnaissance Company for the Knights of Favonius.

What Kind Of Character Is Mika?
Mika is a Cryo support for Physical DPS. He can buff Physical DMG, ATK SPD, and provide healing for the DPS character. Constellations increases his support capabilities even further. However, his buffs are easier to apply when there are multiple enemies.

Sample Team
This build focuses on the up-time of Elemental Burst for healing and using Ocean-Hued Clam for additional DMG. You can also opt to use Noblesse Oblige for additional ATK buff but the 2pc set effect is wasted since Mika's Elemental Burst does not have damage.
Aim For A 200% Energy Recharge
Mika requires a fairly high amount of Energy Recharge to keep Elemental Burst activated. 200% should be enough but if you are running on triple Cryo or double Cryo, you can also opt for a 180%.

Uses Of Mika's Elemental Skill
Buffs Physical DMG Upon Unlocking "Suppressive Barrage"
▲The number that appears on Mika refers to the number of stacks gained.
Mika's Elemental Skill uses a crossbow and deal damage Cryo DMG. It can be aimed with the hold button. Upon hitting the opponents, arrows will bounce off to other opponents.
Each hit will increase your Physical DMG by 10% if you got "Suppressive Barrage" unlocked. It also releases plenty of Energy particles making it good for battery.

Note: You Cannot Use Elemental Skill To Hit Weakpoints
While the Elemental Skill shows a crosshair, it only marks the enemies within it similar to the targeting of
Nahida
. You cannot use Elemental Skill to actively hit weak points of enemies.
Uses Of Mika's Elemental Burst
Heals The Team & Deals Additional Healing Every Normal Attack
After activating the Elemental Burst of Mika, he will heal the whole team. Every hit will also heal the character based on Mika's Max HP.
Should You Pull for Mika's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim for C6 if You're Able To
Constellation Priority
C6 > C4 > C2
C6 is Preferable for Physical Teams
While Mika at low constellation levels can be an okay sustain option that brings a few nice to have buffs, Physical teams would appreciate his presence more if his C6 activated, mostly due to the significant Physical Crit DMG bonus it provides.
If you plan to include Mika in your Physical teams, then it's recommended to pull for C6 if you are able to.
Otherwise, stopping anywhere between C0 and C4 is a viable option.

Mika Team Overview
This Page provides the best Mika Team information. Mika is a Support/Healer in most of the situations. You can also swap the position of Mika and Diona to make your own best team.
From SS tier to S tier, Mika best team can be formed in different ways including Superconduct and Physical Team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

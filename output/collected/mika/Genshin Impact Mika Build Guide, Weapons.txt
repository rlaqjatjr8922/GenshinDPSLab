Mika
Gameplay
Mika’s gameplay revolves around providing consistent
healing
,
team utility
, and
HP-scaling support
, while enhancing his team’s performance through his Elemental Burst. His kit focuses on maintaining the
Soulwind state
, which allows him to increase
Attack Speed
and enhance
Physical DMG
, making him a reliable support in sustained combat scenarios.
His
Elemental Skill
, Starfrost Swirl, uses his crossbow to attack and grants all nearby party members the Soulwind state. When active characters are affected by Soulwind, their
Attack Speed
is increased. This ability can be used in different ways depending on whether it is tapped or held, dealing
Cryo DMG
to opponents and enabling his core buffs. While not focused on damage, it plays an important role in his overall utility during rotations.
When Mika uses his
Elemental Burst
, Skyfeather Song, he regenerates HP for all nearby party members based on his
Max HP
and grants them the
Eagleplume state
. While this effect is active, active characters can regenerate HP when their
Normal Attacks
hit opponents, ensuring consistent sustain for the team during combat.
Mika’s passive talents further enhance his utility by improving his
support capabilities
and overall
team synergy
. While his personal damage is limited, he performs best when built around
HP%
,
Energy Recharge
, and strong team synergy to maximize his overall support potential.

Attack Speed
and enhances his
Physical DMG

Normal Attack

Mika’s
Elemental Burst
is the core of his kit, making it his highest priority. It regenerates HP for all nearby party members and grants the

Normal Attacks
hit opponents, ensuring consistent sustain for the team. Leveling this talent directly improves his
healing output
and overall
support performance
, making it essential for maximizing his utility.
Elemental Skill – Starfrost Swirl
Mika’s
Elemental Skill
provides additional utility, making it a secondary priority. It deals
Cryo DMG
and grants the
Soulwind state
, increasing
Attack Speed
for active characters. Although its damage contribution is moderate, leveling this talent improves his overall effectiveness during rotations.
Normal Attack – Spear of Favonius - Arrow's Passage
Mika’s
Normal Attack
is the least important part of his kit, as he is not played as an
on-field DPS
. His gameplay does not rely on dealing damage through
Normal Attacks
, and investing in this talent provides minimal value. As a result, it should be prioritized last or left at a low level.

Mika Strengths & Weaknesses
Can Restore AP for 0 Cost
"Essence of Water" can restore AP for 0 Cost. This gives you an AP surplus, making it easy to pair with high-cost characters.
Continuous AP Recovery with Wave is Also Excellent
Mika also has an ability called Wave, and she restores 1 AP by accumulating 3 Waves. Continuous AP recovery is also possible by using "Deluge".
Abundant Heal Cards
Mika has 5 types of Heal Cards. There are also 0-Cost Cards that can heal, allowing for stable HP recovery.
How To Use Mika?
Pair with High-Cost Characters
Mika
generates AP for the team by accumulating Waves
. This makes her an excellent partner for characters with high-cost, powerful skills.
Adjusting Waves
Mika's core mechanic revolves around the Wave effect. By accumulating 3 Waves, she
gains AP
. However, be mindful of the timing, as excess AP can be wasted.
Acquiring Deluge Stabilizes Her Performance
Deluge is a Unique Card that grants a Wave at the start of every turn. This allows you to use Mika more consistently from the beginning of the battle.
Specialize as an AP Recovery Character
With her ability to generate AP, Mika is a top-tier pick for AP-demanding teams. If you obtain an AP recovery Epiphany in Chaos, she becomes an even more effective and easy-to-use supporter.

Genshin Impact Lynette: banner, constellation and how to play
Heizo
Updated
February 9, 2024
Tips
Share:
Genshin Impact Lynette
is one of the two new characters added to
Genshin Impact
together with the new region’s release, Fontaine. This cat-girl from the Nation of Justice is an
Ousia-aligned 4-star character
that brings lots of value especially during exploration. And if you're eager to know more about her, you can rely on us.
In this Genshin Impact Lynette guide, you will find all the necessary information about the Anemo support and sub-DPS which includes
how to get her
,
her constellations
and
her talents
. Also, there are some
fun facts
about her, so stick around for that.

Genshin Impact Lynette Guide: how to get her?
Lynette is one of the many
Genshin Impact Characters
. You can get her for free,
once you’ve unlocked the archon quest in
Fontaine
. If you want her constellations, you can get them through any of the gacha banner. Currently, she's not among the rate-up characters, but there's a slim chance that she would appear on any of the current banners.
Banner of Genshin Impact Lynette
Lynette is one of the rate-up 4-star characters in
Lyney’s Character
Event Wish Banner
. It was officially released on August 16, 2023, and it is the first phase of the Character Event Wish of version 4.0 of Genshin Impact.
Lynette Constellations
Genshin Impact Lynette at C0 is already functional enough to make her
an excellent support or semi-DPS character
. Still, with her Constellations, she can get perfect utility and even have a playstyle-changing mechanic.
Her C2 is one of the best constellations for her, as it adds a Vivid Shot to her Elemental Burst, applying an element to more enemies. Her C4 provides
an extra charge to her Elemental Skill
, which enables her to deal more damage if you build her as a damage dealer, and she has the chance to produce more Anemo energy to help with her
Energy Recharge needs
. Check out our recommanded
Genshin Impact Lynette build
to learn more about it!
This Constellation is also essential in exploration, as you can cover twice the distance with the two charges of her Elemental Skill. To learn more about constellations and other Genshin Impact features, read on our
Genshin Impact beginner guide
.
Lastly, Lynette’s C6 infuses her normal attacks whenever she uses her Elemental Skill. It also provides a
20% Anemo Damage Bonus,
which is viable for an on-field Anemo DPS. Her Anemo infusion also enables her to shred Elemental resistances whenever she swirls an element with a certain artifact build.
How to play Lynette in Genshin Impact?
In this Genshin Impact Lynette Guide, we will demonstrate how to actually play Lynette and the points that you should take note of before using her.
Genshin Impact Lynette is
an off-field Anemo character
support that shreds enemies’ elemental resistance with her Elemental Burst and Elemental Skill.
In this play style, you have to
focus on having the maximum Energy Recharge
she can have to have an excellent Elemental Burst uptime since it is the most used ability. This type of gameplay is almost identical to that of other Anemo support characters, such as Sucrose or Venti, as you have to use your Elemental Burst as much as possible in every rotation.
Additionally, you can view this play style as
a sub-DPS support because of her 4th Ascension Passive
, which provides more damage to her Elemental Burst when it infuses an element. We also have a
Lynette Ascension Materials
farm guide to help you. Lynette’s C4 is also essential in this play style, as it adds extra Anemo energy particles due to her Elemental Skill having two charges, giving twice the energy particles.
At the same time,
Genshin Impact Lynette is a flexible character
, and one of her alternate play styles is a viable on-field Main DPS character. Suppose you’re wondering how she can be the main DPS. In that case, it is because of her C6, which infuses her normal attacks with Anemo Damage while also buffing her with a
20% Anemo Damage Bonus.
Check out the recommended
Lynette Team
to help you choose her perfect companions.
Additionally, this Lynette play style requires a lot of resources because it needs a good set of stats in her artifacts and a great DPS weapon. If you build her well as
an Anemo DPS character
, you’d also have to level up her Normal attacks to increase its damage further.
Who is Genshin Impact Lynette?
Of the three siblings,
Lynette is the most reserved
. Lyney and Freminet chatter, but she keeps her cool. Lynette's unusual responses make discussion difficult. She speaks concisely and directly, leaving no opportunity for misinterpretation.
There hasn’t been an official announcement regarding Lynette’s age yet, but we know
Lynette is younger than his brother, Lyney
. We also know Lynette’s birthday is
on February 6th
, according to Genshin Impact.
Is Lynette good in Genshin Impact?
In this Genshin Impact Lynette guide, we believe that
Lynette is a good character
and can be a
great addition to any team composition
due to her versatility. Be it a
DPS or an Anemo support
, Lynette's flexible kit has plenty to offer especially for lineups requiring an Anemo unit. Acquiring her constellations also gives her a higher spot in the list of all the best Genshin Impact characters in the ever-evolving meta.
The only drawback to picking Lynette is
the presence of other Anemo characters
who, arguably, outshine her in various aspects. While she brings a lot to the table, some might argue that other Anemo characters like Kazuha, Venti and Sucrose can pretty much do everything she does and in certain scenarios, even better.
And that’s all. It is the end of our Genshin Impact Lynette guide. Overall,
learning Lynette's kit and building her is never a waste of resources
. She is an excellent support and damage dealer who can be the key to clearing the in-game content and Spiral Abyss stages, or a perfect companion in the exploration. Not only that, but she also has an eye-catching appearance that may attract you to pull for her even more, especially if you're a cat person.

Genshin Impact tips and guides
.

Looking for the best build options for Lynette in Genshin Impact?
Look no further, we have you covered with our build guide.
With the new region of Fontaine available for players to explore in Genshin Impact, players will get a chance to meet plenty of new characters. As always, some of them can join your team as you progress through the story. This is the case with Lynette, sister to Lyney, who’s a sword-wielding Anemo character that can bring some nice versatility to your squad. The best builds for Lynette can utilize her as main DPS or sub-DPS support, with the primary focus in both cases landing on her Elemental Mastery. Let’s dive in and discuss how you can build up your Lynette in Genshin Impact.
Best DPS Build for Lynette in Genshin Impact
If you’re building Lynette as your main DPS, you’ll want to focus on her
Elemental Burst
stat. This lets Lynette deal substantial damage even when she’s swapped away off-field. This involves using her
Enigmatic Feint
attack and
Magic Trick: Astonishing Shift
.
Weapon choices
The best weapon for Lynette is the
Freedom-Sworn
sword. The skill effect from this weapon triggers even when Lynette is off-field, which combined with its great stats and damage potential make for a deadly combo. Another possible option that could be difficult to get is the
Primordial Jade Cutter
. However, if you don’t have access to either of them, there are some alternatives:
Iron Sting
Wolf-Fang
Favonius Sword
Sacrificial Sword
Artifact choices
For artifacts, you should look to use the 4-piece artifact set of either
Noblesse Oblige
or
Emblem of Severe Fate
.
The Noblesse Oblige 4-piece bonus will help with providing your party with extra ATK stat whenever you perform
Elemental Burst
.
Meanwhile, the Emblem of Severe Fate 4-piece bonus will increase your
Elemental Burst DMG
stat by 25% of your
Energy Recharge
. So the choice is between buffs for the whole party with Lynette or frontloading damage through her alone.
Stat priorities
When you’re building Lynette as the main DPS, her main strength should lie in her Anemo Burst capabilities. For that reason, look for the following stats:
Sands:
Elemental Mastery
or
Energy Recharge
Goblet:
Elemental Mastery
or
Anemo DMG Bonus
Circlet:
Elemental Mastery
or
CRIT Rate
or
CRIT DMG
Besides those main stats, your sub-stat priority on artefacts should be:
Elemental Mastery > Energy Recharge > CRIT Rate = CRIT DMG > ATK% > HP%
.
Best Support Build for Lynette in Genshin Impact
In case that you’re building Lynette as your Support character, you’ll want to use her status as an Anemo character to perform as your team’s
Elemental Mastery
buffer. This will let Lynette increase your main DPS’ output substantially while remaining off-field. The main goal of this build is to maximize Lynette’s Swirl damage output through
Elemental Mastery
while buffing the whole party (and main DPS most importantly) through her artefact set.
Weapon choices
The best weapon for Lynette still remains the
Freedom-Sworn
sword. With her being mostly off-field with this build, the triggered skill effect from this weapon triggers is second to none. However, if you don’t have access to it, here are some alternative picks:
Xiphos’ Moonlight
Favonius Sword
Iron Sting
Toukabou Shigure
Artefact choices
This Lynette build only has one crucial 4-piece set that enables the build to work. In other words, the best artifact set for this Lynette build is the
Viridescent Venerer
set. It not only gives you an
Anemo DMG
bonus, but also adds
Increased Swirl DMG
and decreases enemy
Elemental RES
to the element that Swirl was infused with. This alone will make your main DPS burst down opponents that Lynette sets up for them.
Stat priorities
When you’re building Lynette as Support, you should look for every chance to get
Elemental Mastery
as much as possible. Even more so than when you build her for DPS. Therefore, you should look for the following stats:
Sands:
Elemental Mastery
or
Energy Recharge
Goblet:
Elemental Mastery
or
Anemo DMG Bonus
Circlet:
Elemental Mastery
or
CRIT Rate
or
CRIT DMG
Besides those main stats, your sub-stat priority on artefacts should similarly be:
Elemental Mastery > Energy Recharge > CRIT Rate = CRIT DMG > ATK% > HP%
.
Lynette Character Build Guide
While getting weapons and artefacts for your Lynette build in Genshin Impact is important, upgrading the character also plays an important role. Here’s everything that you’re going to need to get the most out of Lynette.
Talent Priority
Lynette’s strongest ability is her Elemental Burst
Magic Trick: Astonishing Shift
. It’s AoE damage and Taunt all wrapped up in a neat package. Most importantly, the
Bogglecat Box
it summons will stay even after you take her off-field, dealing that damage at regular intervals.
Her second priority should be her Elemental Skill
Enigmatic Feint
. It’s both a damage and mobility tool, depending if you press or hold the skill.
Lastly, her Normal Attack
Rapid Ritesword
can be used to fill out the rotation between the other skills. Use it with quick taps or a charge attack version. Therefore, when building Lynette, your Talent priority should be:
Elemental Burst – Magic Trick: Astonishing Gift
Elemental Skill – Enigmatic Feint
Normal Attack Rapid Ritesword

Best Team Comps for Lynette
Depending on what type of build you’re running for Lynette, there are a few team comps that you can use. Primarily, you will want to use characters that synergize with her Anemo abilities. For example, you could use Fischl for Electro and Anemo combos. For support, Barbara could act as your healer, replacing Sucrose or Bennett as Anemo enabler. However, here are a few examples of best comps featuring Lynette.
Main DPS Lynette Elemental Damage Team
This team consists of the following characters:
Main DPS:
Lynette
Sub-DPS:
Xiangling
Sub-DPS:
Ganyu
Support:
Kokomi
The goal of this team is to set up elemental chain reactions for Lynette’s Elemental Burst attack. Use her Anemo power of Swirl to combo with the elemental abilities of everyone else for massive AoE bursts.
In this setup, you’ll use Kokomi to sustain your team, especially because Lynette loses health as well as stamina when using her abilities. She takes care of Lynette’s HP, and also brings
Hydro
to the table. Similarly, you will use Xiangling for
Pyro
and Ganyu for
Cryo
applications.
Perma Freeze Team
You’ll need these characters for this team build:
Main DPS:
Ayato
Sub-DPS:
Lynette
Sub-DPS:
Ganyu
Support:
Kokomi
This team looks to benefit from the off-field support build for Lynette. Her Swirl is the effect that you’ll use to combine Ayato’s
Hydro
and Ganyu’s
Cryo
to form perma-freeze effects. In this comp, Lynette adds consistency and duration to an already awesome duo.
Burst Vaporization Team
To build this team comp, you will need the following party members:
Main DPS:
Hu Tao
Sub-DPS:
Yelan
Sub-DPS:
Lynette
Support:
Zhongli
In this team, Lynette enables the elemental reaction potential for combining Hu Tao’s
Pyro
with Yelan’s
Hydro
to produce
Vaporization
elemental effect. While Hu Tao is technically the main DPS of the team, you’d look to use Lynette together in synergy as a sort of co-DPS. Meanwhile, use Zhongli’s shielding powers to keep everyone alive while you set up the massive elemental reaction.

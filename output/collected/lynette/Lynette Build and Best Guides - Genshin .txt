Anemo

as well as Lynette build as a reaction support only. However, note

4.
Lynette Combos and How to Play
As an off-field DPS or support, Lynette's playstyle revolves mostly around her Elemental Burst,
Magic Trick: Astonishing Shift
. This skill offers a decent
amount of AoE Anemo DMG as well as a taunting ability to keep nearby
opponents off of your team. Note that this does not replace shielders or healers. 
However, it also absorbs and spreads elements. Thank to her passive, the more elements you have in your team, 
the higher the ATK buff for the whole party.
As on off-field DPS or support, you will typically swap Lynette in to use her Skill 
and then her Burst, before swapping back to your Main DPS.
If Lynette has her C6, it adds
Anemo
into her Normal Attacks, which allows her to be 
played on-field. You will then use your supports first, before swapping to Lynette to 
use her skill, burst, and spam her normal attack.

Genshin Impact Yaoyao Guide : Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
January 29, 2024
Tips
Share:
Yaoyao in Genshin Impact
is a
4-star Dendro character
, the first
Dendro healer
offered by HoYoverse. She will be able to heal her allies very effectively with her adorable stuffed animals. This character offers more flexibility for Dendro teams. In this
guide
dedicated to
Genshin Impact Yaoyao
we explain which artifacts and weapons to equip her with in order to have the
best build
, but also in which teams to play her. With our tips and tricks, you will be able to take advantage of her full potential.
How to get Yaoyao in Genshin Impact ?
Yaoyao in Genshin Impact is a
4 star character
, so you have a small chance to get it when you make wishes on the
Standard Wish
(as of 3.5 update) or on a
Character Event Wish
.
You can also
unlock Yaoyao for free in Genshin Impact
in the
3.4 update
. During the
Under The Exquisite Night Chimes
event, you have the opportunity to pick up a 4-star Liyue character of your choice.
Banner of Yaoyao
Yaoyao was released on Wednesday, January 18, 2023 in Genshin Impact. It is highlighted on the banners of
Alhaitham
and
Xiao
until Tuesday, February 7, 2023 5:00 pm.
Yaoyao's constellations
Yaoyao is one of the few 4-star characters that is already very effective without any of her constellations. So it requires very
little investment
. But of course, her constellations make her even more efficient. Especially with her C1
Adeptus’ Tutelage
, which allows her to grant a 15% Dendro damage bonus to her team when her radishes explode.
As for the other constellations, C2 makes him recover energy every time a
White Jade Radish
explodes, C4 increases Yaoyao's elemental mastery to 0.3% of his max HP after using an elemental skill (max 120 points). And thanks to C6, every 3 radishes sent, Yaoyao's Yuegui will launch a
Mega Radish
that will generate more damage and more healing.
How to play Yaoyao in Genshin Impact ?
Yaoyao is mainly played as a
healer
and
support
in Genshin Impact. She will be able to apply the Dendro element in order to promote the elemental reactions linked to this element and heal your characters. She has the advantage of providing quick and efficient healings.
With her elemental skill
Raphanus Sky Cluster
, Yaoyao calls upon a
Yuegui: Throwing Mode
, an adorable stuffed animal that will throw
White Jade Radishes
every second for 10 seconds (10 radishes in total). If all your characters have more than 70% of their HP, it will target the enemies to do damage according to its ATK max.
On the other hand, if your characters have
70% HP or less
, the Yuegui will target the character with the least HP. It will heal him according to Yaoyao's maximum HP. Once the ability is activated, you can switch to another character. Yaoyao will only stay on the field for a short time. Radishes that don't hit anyone stay on the field for 5 seconds, then explode. Once the
In Others’ Shoes
passive is unlocked, radishes create a healing zone for 5 seconds when they explode.
To get more healing, you can cast
Moonjade Descent
elemental rampage. Yaoyao will then cast Yuigui continuously for 5 seconds. However, there can only be 3 at the same time.
During this time, Yaoyao must stay on the battlefield or it will make the Yuigui disappear. Her movement speed is increased by 15% and her Dendro resistance by 50%. Thanks to the passive
Starscatter
, if Yaoyao runs, sprints or jumps in this state, she will also throw white jade radishes at enemies (every 0.6 seconds). In addition, the radishes generate more damage and healing than during the elemental skill.
This ability will be ideal if your entire team needs
extensive care
. But unfortunately it takes up field time. The trick is to use the
elemental skill
. The elemental rampage can be used more if you play it in a team around
Nilou
. Finally, we advise you to use Yaoyao's elemental skill first, then her elemental rampage. You will not use her normal attack.
What is the best equipment for Yaoyao?
In Genshin Impact, Yaoyao has a HP awakening and
heals according to his max HP
. So it can be tempting to raise her HP. However, she already provides high healing and can easily heal your team with about 20,000 health. So, to do more damage with Yaoyao (especially if you play her in
Bloom
,
Quicken
or
Exuberance
teams), our advice is to raise her elemental mastery to the max and her energy recharge a bit.
For this purpose we reveal the
best artifacts and weapons to equip on Yaoyao in Genshin Impact
for a good support build.
The best artifacts for Yaoyao
Deepwood Memories
For a good
support build
, we recommend equipping Yaoyao with the 4-piece
Deepwood Memories
artifact set. The 2-piece set grants a 15% Dendro damage bonus. With 4 pieces, after using an elemental skill, you will lower the Dendro resistance of enemies by 30%. This will be ideal to accompany Dendro DPS like
Alhaitham
and
Tighnari
for example.
Flower of Paradise Lost
But if you play Yaoyao in a Blossom team with
Nilou
, we recommend the
Flower of Paradise Lost
artifact set instead. With 2 pieces, you increase elemental mastery by 80 points and with 4, the damage caused by the reactions of Bloom, Exuberance and Burgeon will be greatly increased.
Gilded Dreams
You can also opt for the
Gilded Dreams
artifact set which will increase elemental mastery and ATK.
Tenacity of the Millelith
However, if you're worried about running out of heals, the
Tenacity of the Millelith
set is a good choice. With it, you will get a 20% HP bonus and boost the ATK of your other characters after using an elemental ability.
Maiden Beloved
It is also possible to opt for only 2 pieces of equipment to combine with 2 pieces of the set
Maiden Beloved
, which increases healing by 15%.
If in doubt, prefer artifacts with the best statistics:
Hourglass: elemental mastery, energy recharge or HP%;
Cut: Elemental mastery or damage bonus Dendro;
Crown: elemental mastery, critical rate or critical damage.
For secondary statistics:
Elemental Mastery;
Energy Recharge;
HP;
ATK;
Critical rate / critical damage.
The best weapons to equip on Yaoyao in Genshin Impact
To perfect your equipment, the trick is to opt for weapons that increase elemental mastery or energy recharge. If you want to bet on the
elemental mastery
, the best weapons to equip on Yaoyao in Genshin Impact will be :
Dragon's Bane (4★)
Kitain Cross Spear (4★)
Moonpiercer (4★)
Dragon's Bane
: The bonus of this weapon increases the damage inflicted on enemies affected by Pyro or Hydro elements by 20%;
Kitain Cross Spear
: this spear is made by the blacksmith and in addition to increasing elemental mastery, it will also boost damage and energy recharge;
Moonpiercer
: by performing elemental reactions related to the Dendro element, you will obtain a leaf of renewal which will increase by 16% the ATK of the character who picks it up and this for 12 seconds.
Favonius Lance (4★)
To increase Yaoyao's
energy recharge
and make it easier to perform his elemental rampage, we recommend the
Favonius Lance
. This hast weapon can also generate energy particles when you perform critical strikes.
Black Tassel (3★)
Staff of Homa (5★)
Finally, to increase Yaoyao's HP, the
Black Tassel
will be optimal, as well as the
Staff of Homa
, which will increase the critical rate and the ATK.
Yaoyao's ascension and skill materials in Genshin Impact
To
raise Genshin Impact Yaoyao to level 90,
you will need:
18
Slime Condensate
, 30
Slime Secretions
and 36
Slime Concentrate
;
168
Jueyun Chili
: you can harvest them in Liyue around Qingce village. Locate them directly with the
interactive map of Teyvat
. You can also grow them in your
Serenitea Pot
;
1 Nagadus Emerald Sliver, 9 Nagadus Emerald Fragment, 9 Nagadus Emerald Chunk and 6 Nagadus Emerald Gemstone as well as 46
Quelled Creeper
to loot on the
Dendro Hypostases
.
And to increase Yaoyao's skills:
Teachings, Guides and Philosphie of Diligence
. These items are available on Tuesdays, Fridays and Sundays in the
Taishan Mansion
dungeon;
Daka’s Bell
(from level 7): You will get it when you fight
Shouki no Kami
.

best healersin Genshin Impact

Dendro element
and thus promote elemental reactions.

How old and tall is Yaoyao from Genshin impact ?
Yaoyao is a resident of Liyue and a disciple of Madame Ping. Although she is about
ten years
old, she already loves to take care of her surroundings. She is also one of the smallest characters in Genshin Impact, being approximately
1m38
tall.
That's it for our tips and tricks in this
guide dedicated to Genshin Impact Yaoyao
and his build. In summary, this is
one of the best 4-star characters in Sumeru
at the moment. She will be able to heal your team's characters efficiently (even with a low number of HP and without constellations), while applying the Dendro element.
Yaoyao will work great with excellent characters like
Nilou
and
Alhaitham
. However, her elemental skill is expensive and requires keeping her on the field. But that's not really a problem since her elemental ability alone already provides decent healing.

4. Bloom Team
In this team, earlier, 2 Dendro characters, a hydro damage dealer, and a healer. Earlier
Kokomi
was used as the healer. But now, with the introduction of
Yaoyao
, the team becomes very easy. And also, the role of the healer is confirmed.
Now, players can use damage-dealing characters in place of the healer. With the introduction of Yaoyao, players would be able to form new team comps, and as she is a dendro character who can heal.

Also, Yaoyao’s ultimate skill provides dendro resistance which would be fit for hyperbloom teams. As there are chances that players themselves are damaged by the reaction. Also getting Yaoyao’s artifacts is easy considering she is a supporting character and just needs the main stats and energy recharge. Considering she can also heal off-field characters, is a huge advantage. So, players should try to get Yaoyao from the
Lantern Rite Festival

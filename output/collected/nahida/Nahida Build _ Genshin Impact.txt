Nahida Overview
This Page provides the Best Nahida Build information.
Nahida is an up coming and also first 5-Star Rarity Dendro Character in Genshin Impact
Nahida is a DPS in Genshin Impact. Nahida DPS Build is mainly used. Nahida mainly deal DMG by her Elemental Skill, which connects 8 enemies at maximum and deals Dendro DMG to them while triggering elemental reactions. The Elemental Burst of Nahida buffs her Elemental Skill depends on the Element of teammates. As a Dendro Character, the DMG of Nahida can be maximized by triggering Dendro-related reactions – Hyper Bloom, Catalyze or Quicken.
In Genshin Impact, Nahida is a 5 Stars rarity Dendro character currently in SS tier.

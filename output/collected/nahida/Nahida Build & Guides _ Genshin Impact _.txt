Dendro Catalyst in Genshin Impact. Whether you’re using her in an off-field
support role or as an on-field DPS, this guide will explore her best artifacts,
weapons, talents, and team compositions to amplify her impact in any lineup.

Nahida's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Extremely powerful Dendro application both on-field and off-field.
Provides large Elemental Mastery bonuses to the party.
Low Elemental Burst cooldown and Energy costs.
Incredibly powerful C2 and C6.
Weaknesses
Low HP and Defense meaning damage can be more impactful both to knock-back and overall survivability.
Elemental Skill's aim mode can be more difficult to use on Mobile and Console.

3.
Nahida’s Combos and Playstyle Guide
You often want to start with her Elemental Skill,
All Schemes to Know
, to tether as many enemies as possible so
that subsequent reactions can deal more damage using Tri-Karma Purification
(from
Seed of Skandha
). Tri-Karma does have a limit of up to
8 characters at a single time, however.
Apply your Elemental Burst and start swapping to the other members of your
party who will now be buffed due to the burst. Periodically swap back to Nahida
to reuse
All Schemes to Know
again either for new waves of
enemies or due to its powerful
Dendro
application to maintain
any ongoing reactions.
Using
Illusory Heart
is a huge buff to your team, dealing increased
damage from
All Schemes to Know
as well as buffing the entire parties
Elemental Mastery up to a maximum of 250.
3.1.
Out of Combat Gameplay Information
Nahida has 2 very unique gameplay benefits that take place outside of combat
surrounding
All Schemes to Know
.
Nahida can collect up to 4 nearby items from the open world at a time using
her Elemental Skill such as berries and flowers. This does not work for
animals/collectables that move such as Scarabs in the desert.
Nahida can also use her Elemental Skill on NPCs in Sumeru to read their
thoughts and gain additional dialogue. This is mainly for normal NPCs out
in the world but at times can be used on other playable characters for
example during an event.

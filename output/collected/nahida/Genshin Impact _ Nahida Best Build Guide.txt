Rate Nahida!
In battle, Nahida is the Dendro Sub-DPS that applies Dendro Off-Field. She can also buff the Elemental Mastery of the active character and be the On-Field Reaction Driver that applies Dendro.
Strengths & Weakness Of Nahida
Strengths
- Consistent Dendro infliction through Elemental Skill.
- Short Cooldown for both Skill and Elemental burst.
- Can fit in many kinds of teams.
- Elemental Mastery increases its CRIT Rate and DMG making her easier to build.
Weakness
- Burst does not deal damage.
- Very reliant on reactions and other element to function.
▼ Details about Nahida below.

Nahida Skill Guide & How To Use Nahida
How To Use Nahida?
How To Use?
▼ Dendro Sub-DPS for Dendro Reaction Teams
▼ Use Elemental Skill to Start Applying Dendro Off-Field
▼ Elemental Skill (Hold) can be used to Collect Plants and Read the Minds of Sumeru NPCs
▼ Elemental Burst buffs Nahida's Elemental Skill Attacks
▼ Passives Provides Buffs for the Active Teammate and Nahida
Dendro Sub-DPS For Dendro Reaction Teams
Nahida is a Dendro Sub-DPS that works amazingly on Elemental Application. Her Elemental Skill allows her to mark enemies that she can deal Off-Field Attacks on when Reactions are triggered on them.
Use Elemental Skill To Start Applying Dendro Off-Field
Nahida's Elemental Skill is used to mark enemies that she will deal Off-Field Attacks on. It has two versions. The Tap version has a shorter CD but only applies the mark to nearby enemies while the Hold version can target farther enemies. When an Elemental Reaction is triggered on marked enemies, she will deal an attack called Tri-Karma Purification on them.
Elemental Skill (Hold) Can Be Used To Collect Plants And Read The Minds Of Sumeru NPCs
Nahida's Elemental Skill has an Overworld interaction mechanic. It can be used to harvest plants from far away and also read the minds of Sumeru NPCs that were connected to the Akasha.
Elemental Burst Buffs Nahida's Elemental Skill Attacks
Nahida's Elemental Burst is used to give buffs on Nahida's Tri-Karma Purification Attack. Pyro, Hydro and Electro teammates can affect what type of buff and how potent the buff will be.
Element
Effect
Increases the DMG of Tri-Karma Purification
Increase the duration of Illusory Heart
Decrease the interval between each Tri-Karma Purification
Passives Provides Buffs For The Active Teammate And Nahida
Nahida's Passive allows her to buff the active character's Elemental Mastery based on the character with the highest EM. Her other Passive buffs the CRIT stats of her Tri-Karma Purification attack.
Should You Pull for Nahida's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Prioritize Nahida's Constellations
Signature VS Constellations
C2 > C1 or
A Thousand Floating Dreams
C2 is a High Value Constellations
Nahida's C2 is a constellation that most Dendro reaction-oriented teams would greatly appreciate.
If you like playing with Dendro reactions or have a Dendro-focused DPS you want to upgrade, then going for her C2 is going to give you some of the best possible results in terms of their damage.
Her Signature Weapon, in contrast, can easily be substituted by other 4-star alternatives.
Recommendation if You Have
Kagura's Verity
C2 > C1 >
A Thousand Floating Dreams
Other 4-star and 5-star Weapons are Competitive
Kagura's Verity significantly decreases the need for Nahida's Signature Weapon in your account if you have it.
However, since she can use any of her weapon alternatives with no noticeable drop in performance, pulling for A Thousand Floating Dreams is going to be lower on the priority list regardless.

All Schemes To Know (Elemental Skill)
Effect
Sends forth karmic bonds of wood and tree from her side, dealing AoE Dendro DMG and marking up to 8 opponents hit with the Seed of Skandha. When held, this skill will trigger differently.
Hold
Effect
Enters Aiming Mode, which will allow you to select a limited number of opponents within a limited area. During this time, Nahida's resistance to interruption will be increased. When released, this skill deals Dendro DMG to these opponents and marks them with the Seed of Skandha. Aiming Mode will last up to 5s and can select a maximum of 8 opponents.
Seed Of Skandha
Effect
Opponents who have been marked by the Seed of Skandha will be linked to one another up till a certain distance. You can trigger at most 1 Tri-Karma Purification within a short period of time.

Genshin Impact Nahida Teams: Best Comps
Heizo
Updated
February 29, 2024
Tips
Share:
With her remarkable
on and off-field damage
, 100% uptime of her
Dendro application
, and
buff
, it's no longer a surprise that she is one of the
most highly sought-after characters
in
Genshin Impact
.
Nahida
, the God of Wisdom is a
5-star Dendro Catalyst
character from Sumeru who seamlessly slots well in any
Dendro-based team compositions
. But if you're still wondering which among the long list of
Genshin Impact characters
synergize perfectly with the Dendro Archon, then look no further as we have all the right answers for you in this Genshin Impact
Nahida
teams guide.

One of the best Genshin Impact Nahida Teams: Bloom
Nilou
, by far is the most ideal component for the
Bloom team
due to her
unmatched hydro application
and ability to produce
bountiful cores
.
Bountiful Cores essentially work similarly to Dendro cores, but
they explode faster
and
have larger AoE effects
than the latter. In tandem with Nahida and
Collei
, the team can generate as many cores as possible to deal more damage over time. Take note though that since the
Bloom damage can also take a huge chunk off your health
, a
strong healer
like
Kokomi
is required to balance it out.
You can also check out the
Nahida materials farming guide
to learn more about this Dendro character.
Hyperbloom Nahida Team
Hyperbloom
is one of the
strongest reactions
in the game and also
belongs to the best Genshin Impact Nahida Teams
.
This comp can be used even by beginners due to the large number of available characters, both 4-stars and 5-stars that can form this particular team, and how easy it is to build each member.
For a hyperbloom team,
Nahida
can be the
solo dendro member
and
driver
of the team thanks to her exceptional
dendro application
enough to consistently generate
Dendro Cores
.
Xingqiu
and
Yelan
on the other hand will serve as the
off-field hydro applicator
. A Hyperbloom team is then completed by an electro character like
Kuki Shinobu
who can trigger dendro cores through her elemental skill and burst, spawn projectiles that will target enemies and at the same time provide healing.
Genshin Impact Nahida Spread Team
In terms of Quicken team, this
Alhaitham DPS Spread Team
is recommended.
Alhaitham
belongs to the
top-tier Genshin Impact characters
even without his constellations and his strength is amplified by Nahida who can buff his
EM-scaling kit
through her burst. To further improve his damage output, taking advantage of the
Quicken or Spread reaction
capable of
boosting Dendro attacks
is suggested. This reaction is going to be made possible with the help of
off-field Electro sub-DPS
,
Fischl
and
off-field healer and applicator
, Kuki Shinobu.
Burgeon, among the best Genshin Impact Nahida Teams
With the rise of
Dendro reactions
,
Thoma
seemed to have found another purpose for his kit other than being a shielder.
Thoma's elemental skill inflicts a
single instance of AoE Pyro damage
as he puts up a shield. Meanwhile, his burst summons another fiery shield and applies
AoE Pyro damage
in coordination with the active character's normal attacks. This makes him an ideal
trigger
for
Burgeon
, provided
he's at level 90 and uses the EM build
. But before the Burgeon reaction, Dendro cores must first be created. For this, characters who can maintain Hydro application such as Xingqiu and Kokomi are needed alongside Nahida to produce dendro cores.
Don't forget to check out our
Genshin Impact Nahida build guide
!
Genshin Impact Nahida Teams- Burning Melt
As we know,
Cryo does not react with Dendro
, but it does not mean that you cannot form a fully functional team with these elements. In fact, you can use one of the few Dendro reactions to
boost the damage of any Cryo DPS
such as Wriothesley.
Wriothesley
is a
5-star Cryo DPS
who throws flurries of punches to his opponents to deal intense damage to any enemies in his range. Given that he's a
Cryo damage dealer
, he can use strong reactions such as
Reverse Melt
to double the damage that he deals. And that's where the
Burnmelt
teams come into play.
Nahida
alongside Thoma and
Bennett
can trigger the
burning reaction
consistently with their
off-field application
, applying continuous
Pyro aura
to the enemies. Consequently, this will allow Wriothesley to take advantage of the reverse melt reaction from it.
Bennett
on the last slot of this team
doubles
as a healer and another source of Pyro damage in this team.
Congratulations on reaching the end of this
Genshin Impact Nahida teams guide
! Fortunately, there are
plenty of characters
that can work well with Nahida and her talents. Having that said, you can rest assured that you will never run out of options when it comes to building a team with the Dendro archon be it for Bloom, Burgeon, Hyperbloom and more.
What's next? You can head over to our
Genshin Impact tips and tricks
section to find more helpful insights like this, including the best
teams for Xianyun
and
Gaming
. Enjoy!

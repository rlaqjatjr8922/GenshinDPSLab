Baizhu
is a 5-star Dendro Catalyst character that is both a healer and a shielder. He works very well in most Dendro reaction teams.
You can find some popular team compositions and builds below, as well as recommended team rotations!

Baizhu
is a very useful addition to any Aggravate team due to his relatively consistent Dendro application. His 4th Ascension Passive increases the Aggravate reaction DMG dealt by the teammates he has healed through his Elemental Burst. Below is an example of an Aggravate team that includes
Raiden Shogun
as the Main DPS,
Yae Miko
as the Sub DPS,
Kazuha
as the Support, and
Baizhu

as the Healer.

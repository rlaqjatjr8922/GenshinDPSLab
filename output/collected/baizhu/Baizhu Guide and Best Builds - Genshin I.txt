Baizhu's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Fulfills multiple roles and has flexible team options.
Can easily sustain an entire team by himself.
Good on-field Dendro application due to being a catalyst character.
Weaknesses
Requires a log of Energy Recharge to regularly burst.
Struggles to apply Dendro against multiple enemies.
His shield is weak compared to other shielders.

3.
Baizhu Combos and How to Play
Baizhu will mainly use his
Universal Diagnosis
and
Holistic Revivification
.
Since his heal is on his Elemental Skill, it allows for a regular healing rotation, while you
can swap to Baizhu when his Burst is ready and proc his shield, which will stay on the active character.
You will not use Baizhu's
The Classics of Acupuncture
in
a support build, unless you need to apply a bit more
Dendro
.
The heal from
Universal Diagnosis
will affect the entire team.
However, it is a short-ranged skill, meaning that if you want the skill to hit more than one enemy,
they need to be close together before you send it out. It will heal you whether it hits or not, but if it does not hit,
it will not apply any
Dendro
.
Baizhu's rotation is fairly simple:
Use his
Universal Diagnosis
to apply
Dendro
,
which will also heal you if needed
Use his
Holistic Revivification
to apply the shield
Swap through your other teammates
Swap back to him when his
Universal Diagnosis
is ready again (it has 10s cooldown)

Baizhu's Constellations
Baizhu has very good constellations, however, he is already excellent at C0. His heal
and buff are plenty sufficient for most situations at C0.
If you want to invest, his C1 is worth it as it gives you an additional charge on
Universal Diagnosis
, increasing his healing and
Dendro
application. His C4 will boost the teams Elemental Mastery which can be a huge boost.

Cyno is a 5-star Electro character that wields a Polearm in Genshin Impact. His gameplay focuses on being an On-Field DPS. He can deal massive Electro damage during his Elemental Burst while utilizing his Elemental Skill during the Endseer state for higher damage output. Lore-wise, Cyno serves as the General Mahamatra and has ties with the Akademiya.

What Kind of Character is Cyno?
Cyno is an on-field Electro DPS who enters Pactsworn Pathclearer after using his Elemental Burst, where he deals most of his damage.
He synergizes well with
Nahida
for her EM buffs and benefits from EM-scaling reactions like Aggravate, Hyperbloom, and Lunar-charged.
Cyno’s meta standing at base investment has been unstable due to glaring kit issues.
His Burst state lasts long when played optimally, causing most supports’ abilities to expire mid-rotation. This limits support choices, teambuilding, and DPS potential, as Travelers often swap early to refresh support abilities.
Ineffa
is a strong addition to Cyno’s teams, contributing high personal damage, enabling Lunar-charged, and providing both a shield and an Elemental Mastery buff. Together, these significantly boost his Hyperbloom team DPS, making Cyno more competitive and viable in harder end-game content even at low investment.

Roles & Playstyle of Cyno and His Strengths and Weakness
On-Field DPS Cyno
Cyno is known to have problems with his supports' abilities running out while he's in his Elemental Burst state. His old team DPS at low investment was also underwhelming, but
Ineffa
and the Lunar-charged reaction does help to make him more competitive.
Pros and Cons of On-Field DPS Cyno
Pros
- Core gameplay is easy to play after some practice.
- Benefits from the new Lunar-charged reaction, which is likely going to be part of the new meta.
- Decent into both single-target and multi-target content.
Cons
- Long field-time limits his support options since most abilities don't last as long as his Elemental Burst state.
- Despite having increased interruption resistance in his Burst state, he can still get knocked around a lot.
- Almost always requires a second electro to reliably use his Burst every rotation due to the 80 Energy cost.
- His team's DPS output can be on the lower end without Ineffa or Lunar-charged.

Prioritize Cyno's Energy Requirement
Even when Cyno is with another Electro teammate, he still needs to have around a decent amount of Energy Recharge to be able to use his Elemental Burst reliably, since it's his main source of damage.
The exception to this rule is when he's paired with
Ineffa
, whose Energy generation is generous enough for Cyno to run a very small amount of Energy Recharge.
After ensuring he can charge his Burst up every rotation, finding a balance between Elemental Mastery and CRIT becomes the secondary focus. Always try to have a 1:2 ratio of CRIT Rate to DMG for the reliable DPS, and try to hit as close to his recommended Elemental Mastery number as possible.

Team Rotation of Cyno and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for On-Field DPS Cyno
Basic Rotation For On-Field DPS Cyno
1. Start with supports with longer ability durations.
2. Then switch to your supports with shorter abilities.
3. Swap to Cyno then execute his damage combo.
4 Return to Step 1.
Cyno's team rotation, as stated, is very straightforward since you're generally going to be deploying your support abilities first before swapping into Cyno.
Cyno has one optimal combo with his current best artifact set, 4-piece
Thundering Fury
, and another combo when he's equipped with a different set.
Optimal Cyno Combo
Thundering Fury Combo
1. Use Elemental Skill.
2. Normal Attack once.
3. Use Elemental Burst into Elemental Skill.
└ If Cyno doesn't have the Thundering Fury set, you use Normal Attack twice first before using his Skill.
4. Normal Attack at least 4 times, or until you see the eye icon.
5. Use Elemental Skill when the eye icon flashes yellow.
└ It's important to remember to Skill when the icon glows or flashes, not when it's still purple, to ensure you trigger the Duskstalker Bolts.
6. Repeat steps 4-5 up to 6 times or until Elemental Burst state expires.
This combo assumes you have the 4-piece Thundering Fury equipped,
since the Skill reset from the full set piece helps to fit as much Skill uses into his Burst state as possible, which helps to increase his personal damage. This is the optimal and most recommended combo overall.
If your Cyno isn't equipped with the Thundering Fury set, the above combo should still apply. But you will have less overall Skill usages due to the lack of the Skill reset.
You can Normall attacks to fill in the gap while waiting for the Skill to come off cooldown.
Basics Of Using Cyno
Cyno is an On-Field Electro DPS who uses his Elementall Burst to enter a special state called Pactsworn Pathclearer to deal most of his damage.
While in this state, Cyno's Normal, Charged and Plunge Attacks are infused with Electro, and his Elemental Mastery, interruption resistance and immunity to Electro-charged DMG also increases.
Elemental Burst State Is Cyno's Primary DPS Source
The bulk of Cyno's damage is condensed into his Burst state.
This Burst has a relatively long duration and support abilities will often expire before his Pactsworn Pathclearer ends. This is one of Cyno's known gameplay issues, but it can be worked around by swapping out of Cyno's Burst early. Meeting his Energy Recharge requirements should be your first priority because of how important his Elemental Burst is.
Elemental Skill Extends Burst State Duration
When Cyno uses his Elemental Skill while in his Pactsworn Pathclearer state, Cyno instead releases an attack that deals AoE Electro DMG while also extending the duration of his Elemental Burst state by 4 seconds.
Because using his Skill is important for his overall DPS (since it also triggers his Featherfall Judgment passive), the Pactsworn Pathclearer state will naturally last longer.
Favors Reactions that Scale with Elemental Mastery
Cyno is naturally built with Elemental Mastery because of his Ascension 4 Passive, Authority Over the Nine Bows.
This passive increases the damage of his Normal Attacks in his Pactsworn Pathclearer and the damage of the Duststalker Bolts that are released whenever you successfully trigger his Ascension 1 Passive, Featherfall Judgment. This make it so that teams that focus around reactions that take into account Elemental Mastery are usually Cyno's best teams.
Lunar-Charged Is Very Good for Cyno's DPS
The
Lunar-charged
reaction introduced with
Ineffa
greatly boosted Cyno’s damage in his best team, which blends Quicken (Dendro + Electro) and Hyperbloom (Dendro + Hydro, then triggered by Electro).
Lunar-charged scales with the whole team’s stats, can CRIT, and uses Elemental Mastery (EM) in its formula, which is a stat that Cyno has a surplus of by default. Additionally, Ineffa also provides a shield and buffs EM for herself and the active character.
It's easy to see that Cyno gains significant value from Ineffa, which makes this an excellent pairing as a boost for Cyno's team DPS.

Electro DPS That Enters a Special State After Using Elemental Burst
Basic Rotation Of Cyno
1. Start with the supports use their Elemental Skills or Bursts. You want to prioritize activating the abilities with longer durations first, then the shorter durations after.
2. Swap to Cyno to execute his full damage combo.
3. Repeat from Step 1.
Cyno's team rotation is straightforward considering how he wants to have as much on-field time as possible.
This makes it so that he'll always be last in the team rotation. His basic combo also involves using his Elemental Skill in his Pactsworn Pathclearer state at specific timings, which is fully explained below.
Pactsworn Pathclearer State Is His Primary Damage Source
Cyno's Elemental Burst puts him into a special state or stance called Pactsworn Pathclearer.
This state enhances his Normal, Charged and Plunge attacks by infusing them with Electro, while also increasing his Elemental Mastery and resistance to interruption. This Burst state also works in tandem with both of his passives. Because his Elemental Burst is his strongest and most important ability, having enough Energy Recharge to use it every rotation should be a priority.
Elemental Skill Extends Pactsworn Pathclearer Duration
Secret Rite: Chasmic Soulfarer
When Cyno is under the Pactsworn Pathclearer state triggered by Sacred Rite: Wolf's Swiftness, he will instead unleash a Mortuary Rite that deals thunderous AoE Electro DMG and extends the duration of Pactsworn Pathclearer.
When in his Burst state, using Cyno's Skill's extends the duration of the state by 4 seconds.
His Ascension 1 Passive, Featherfall Judgment, makes it so that you also need to time the usage of his Skill while in the Burst state at specific timings for more damage. More importantly, since you will always want to use Cyno's Skill while Pactsworn Pathclearer is active for DPS purposes, its duration is naturally going to be extended.
Ascension 1 Passive Requires You to Time Usage of His Skill
Cyno's A1 Passive Mechanics
(Ascension 2) Featherfall Judgment
- While in Pactsworn Pathclearer state, Cyno periodically enters Endseer stance (which is indicated by a purple eye icon).
- Using Elemental Skill during the Endseer stance activates the Jurisdiction effect:
└  Increases the damage of his Elemental Skill by 35%.
└ Fires 3 Duststalker Bolts, each dealing 100% ATK as Electro DMG. Duststalker Bolts count as Skill DMG.
While in the Burst state, an eye icon will occasionally flash on your screen.
This means Cyno has entered his Endseer Stance, and it requires you have to activate his Skill whenever the eye lights up.
A successful Skill trigger whenever Endseer Stance happens increases the damage dealt by the Skill while also firing 3 additional bolts of Electro damage. This is a good part of his personal DPS, so ensuring you activate his Skill at the correct time is part of his optimal gameplay.
Good With Electro-Related Reactions That Scale With Elemental Mastery
(Ascension 4) Authority Over the Nine Bows
Cyno's DMG values will be increased based on his Elemental Mastery as follows:
- Pactsworn Pathclearer's Normal Attack DMG is increased by 150% of his Elemental Mastery.
- Duststalker Bolt DMG from his Passive Talent Featherfall Judgment is increased by 250% of his Elemental Mastery.
Cyno typically wants to build Elemental Mastery to take advantage of his Authority Over the Nine Bows passive.
This essentially buffs all of the important damage sources in his Burst state, which naturally makes him more synergistic with Electro reactions that scale well with more Elemental Mastery, such as Aggravate, Hyperbloom and Electro or Lunar-charged. Cyno's strongest teams will usually consist of either or all reactions working together.
Having Another Electro Teammate Helps With Energy
Playing Cyno with another Electro teammate to generate Energy particles is highly recommended,
since it also activates Electro Resonance that increases Energy gain. This includes supports like
Fischl
and
Ineffa
that can act as potent batteries to charge Cyno's high cost Elemental Burst. Without another Electro teammate, Cyno's Energy requirement becomes too high, which can be hard to balance alongside his best offensive stats like CRIT and Elemental Mastery.
Pairing Him With Shield Supports Is Recommended
Having a shield makes playing Cyno more comfortable despite his increased resistance to interruption in his Burst state.
This is because getting interrupted while Pactsworn Pathclearer is active is going to be a lot less forgiving because of his Endseer Stance timing.
Missing one properly timed Skill use leads to lower DPS, which is something we want to avoid, especially in harder endgame content. A shield character that synergizes well with Cyno, such as
Baizhu
,
Kirara
and
Ineffa
help to avoid this problem entirely.
Should You Pull for Cyno's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Prioritize Cyno's Constellations
Signature VS Constellations
C2 > C1 or
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
C2 is a Good Stopping Point
Cyno's early constellations, like most DPS characters, are designed in a way to increase his damage.
His best early constellations that are likely to provide a noticeable increase to his damage without going to C6, which is his top constellation overall, will be his C1 and C2.
The attack speed buff (from C1) helps you perform specific ability rotations easier and the sizeable Electro DMG bonus (from C2) is easily maintained throughout combat, making both nice to have if you want to vertically invest in Cyno directly.
If you do not have a 5-star Crit spear, you can still consider getting Staff of the Scarlet Sands since it's one of the decent 5-star stat sticks for other spear characters that would appreciate the Elemental Mastery or Crit Rate it provides.
Recommendation if You Have
Staff of Homa
or
Ballad of the Fjords
C2 > C1 >
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
Other Crit Spears are Competitive
Staff of Homa, Ballad of the Fjords (both at base refinement and max refinement) and the
Primordial Jade Winged-Spear
will all provide similar results with minimal differences between each other and versus his Signature Weapon.
The Staff of the Scarlet Sands starts losing its pull value if you have any of his good alternative Crit spears,
so you're likely better off pulling for Cyno's constellations or his best supports' constellations instead.

Featherfall Judgment
Effect
When Cyno is in the Pactsworn Pathclearer state activated by Sacred Rite: Wolf's Swiftness, Cyno will enter the Endseer stance at intervals. If he activates Secret Rite: Chasmic Soulfarer while affected by this stance, he will activate the Judication effect, increasing the DMG of this Secret Rite: Chasmic Soulfarer by 35%, and firing off 3 Duststalker Bolts that deal 100% of Cyno's ATK as Electro DMG. Duststalker Bolt DMG is considered Elemental Skill DMG.
Authority Over The Nine Bows
Effect
Cyno's DMG values will be increased based on his Elemental Mastery as follows:
・Pactsworn Pathclearer's Normal Attack DMG is increased by 150% of his Elemental Mastery.
・Duststalker Bolt DMG from his Passive Talent Featherfall Judgment is increased by 250% of his Elemental Mastery.
The Gift Of Silence
Effect
Gains 25% more rewards when dispatched on a Sumeru Expedition for 20 hours.
Who Is Cyno?
Sumeru's General Mahamatra
Although young, Cyno is Sumeru's General Mahamatra. He uses his authority to arrest any scholars who violate the rules and regulations of the Akdemiya. Any researcher guilty of this can expect the swift intervention of the General Mahamatra.
Known For His Unfunny Jokes
As much as Cyno's known for being the General Mahamatra, he's also known for telling jokes that are not funny for most people. It might be due to his delivery or the fact that he explains them in so much detail.
Lover Of Genius Invokation TCG
Cyno is a known lover of the Genius Invokation TCG. He loves collecting cards and playing against his friends and any person willing enough to battle against him.

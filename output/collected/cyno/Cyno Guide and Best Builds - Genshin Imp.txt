Cyno is a 5-Star Electro Polearm user in Genshin Impact who deals
powerful damage using their alternate form from
his Elemental Burst. This guide will focus on Cyno's best builds,
artifacts, weapons and best team compositions.

The sands Cyno should use depends on his team and weapon. If using 
Staff of Scarlet Sands, and/or playing a team where Cyno triggers Hyperbloom, 
Elemental Mastery should be used. In Lunar-Charged teams, ATK % and 
Elemental Mastery are equal. In Overload, ATK % should be used.
For the Goblet, Electro is always best, but Elemental Mastery is close behind in 
Hyperbloom teams, especially if using Staff of the Scarlet Sands.

Invoker's Spear
Cyno’s Normal Attacks in his Burst are a much larger portion of his total 
damage output than his Skill, so it should be leveled first. The Skill is still 
important, so it should be leveled too. Cyno’s Normal Attack talent can be left 
unleveled, as it is not used.
How to Play Cyno
Cyno’s playstyle involves using him as an on-field DPS in his Burst, 
performing Normal Attacks and casting his Skill when he enters his 
Endseer stance, or off cooldown if using Thundering Fury. Ideally, you will go 
up to the 4th hit in his Normal Attack chain and do a dash cancel if his Skill 
is on cooldown, and use the Skill if it isn’t, as this will fit into the 
Endseer interval. If using Thundering Fury, the dash cancels are replaced with 
his Skill, as the set allows him to fit in those extra Skill casts without
 missing his Endseer intervals, if your timing is right.

Cyno Team Overview
This Page provides the best Cyno Team information. Cyno is a Main DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Cyno and Raiden Shogun to make your own best team.
From SS tier to A tier, Cyno best team can be formed in different ways including Thunder, Superconduct, Electro-Charged, Overload, Spread, Catalyze, Aggravate, Hyperbloom, Burgeon team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

Genshin Impact Cyno Guide: Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
January 29, 2024
Tips
Share:
Cyno
is a
5-star
Electro
hero
who fights with a hast weapon. He is played as the
main DPS
and takes up a lot of field time. He offers an atypical gameplay that essentially revolves around his elemental rampage. We explain
how to play Cyno in Genshin Impact with this complete guide
. You will know which artifacts and weapons to equip him with to give him the
best build
, but also in which teams to play him. We also give many
tips and advice for beginners
.
How to get Cyno in Genshin Impact ?
Cyno is a
5-star character
that you can only summon when he is featured in an
Event Banner
. He was released with the 3.1 update of Genshin Impact and got his first rerun with the 3.5 update.
Cyno's banner
You have a small chance to
unlock Cyno in Genshin Impact
by wishing on his
Twilight Arbiter
banner from March 1 to 22, 2023.
Cyno constellations
Cyno already has a good offensive potential, so you can perfectly do without his constellations. However, if you want to make the character even stronger, we recommend his first two constellations.
C1
Ordinance: Unceasing Vigil
increases the speed of Cyno's normal attacks by 20% for 10 seconds during his elemental rampage.
Ceremony: Homecoming of Spirits
, his C2, boosts Cyno's Electro damage bonus by 10% for 4 seconds when one of his normal attacks hits an enemy. This effect can be accumulated up to 5 times.
How to play Cyno in Genshin Impact ?
Cyno is played as the main DPS. His
gameplay
mainly revolves around his elemental rampage
Sacred Rite: Wolf's Swiftness
. It allows him to transform into a
Pactsworn Pathclearer
. In this form, his normal, charged, and plunging attacks do Electro damage. His elemental mastery is also increased by 100 points. This skill has a base duration of 10 seconds and takes 20 seconds to recharge. It costs 80 points of elemental energy.
With the
Featherfall Judgment
passive, you'll be able to do even more damage. To do this, simply cast Cyno's elemental skill
Secret Rite: Chasmic Soulfarer
when you see an eye appear while under the effect of Oathfinder. Cyno will generate 3
Duststalker Bolt
that deal 100% damage at ATK. This attack will extend the elemental rampage by 4 seconds.
However, Cyno will be easily interrupted and will lose DPS. To avoid this, we advise you to play him with a hero able to summon shields such as
Zhongli
or to increase his resistance to interruption like
Xingqiu
.
For
optimal rotation
, start by casting all of your supports' skills and eventually Cyno's elemental attack to charge his elemental rampage. When the ability is available, activate
Sacred Rite: Wolf's Swiftness
and chain normal and charged attacks together, followed by the elemental attack QTEs until the skill ends. Since it lasts a long time, be sure to choose support characters whose abilities stay on the field long enough to boost Cyno's damage and create reactions. Among the best options are
Fischl
,
Xingqiu
,
Nahida
, etc.
Cyno can easily hit several enemies at the same time and can be used as a single target or as a multi-target.
Especially raise Cyno's elemental rampage and elemental ability. You can leave the normal attack at level 1 as these stats are not taken into account when Cyno enters the Pathfinder form.
What is the best equipment for Cyno?
Mostly increase Cyno's offensive stats: ATK, critical rate and critical damage, but also his elemental control so that he does more damage from elemental reactions. Also, his
Authority Over the Nine Bows
passive increases the damage of Cyno's normal attacks when he unleashes his elemental and his sandblasts based on his elemental mastery. Also aim for at least 140% energy recharge.
We reveal
the best artifacts and weapons to equip on Cyno in Genshin Impact
.
The best artifacts for Cyno
Gilded Dreams
The best artifact set to equip on Cyno in Genshin Impact is the 4-piece
Golden Dream
set. It increases his elemental mastery by 80 points, but alsoATK by 14% for every character of the same elemental type as Cyno and his elemental mastery by 50 points for all characters of different elemental types. This effect is triggered when you perform an elemental reaction and lasts 8 seconds.
You can also go to :
Thundering Fury
Gladiator's Finale
Thundering Fury
: With 2 coins, you increase Electro damage by 15%, and with 4 coins, you increase the damage of Elemental reactions related to the Electro element. In addition, elemental reactions reduce the cooldown of the elemental skill. This is the set that allows you to do the most damage with Cyno in theory, but you will lose DPS if you get interrupted often. So we don't recommend it for beginners;
Gladiator's Finale
: This set increases the damage ofATK by 18% and the damage of normal attacks by 35% if the equipped character carries a hast weapon, one-handed sword or two-handed sword. This is the easiest set for beginners to obtain as it can be looted from RPG bosses.
If you hesitate between several artifact sets, choose the sets with better statistics:
Hourglass: elementary mastery or ATK%;
Cut: bonus to Electro damage
Crown: critical rate or critical damage.
For secondary statistics:
CRIT Rate;
Critical damage ;
Elemental Mastery;
Energy Recharge;
ATK.
The best weapons for Cyno
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
The best hast weapon to equip on Cyno in Genshin Impact is definitely his signature weapon: the
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
. It boosts his critical rate considerably, but also his ATK depending on his elemental control.
You can also bet on :
Primordial Jade Winged-Spear
Staff of Homa
Deathmatch
Blackcliff Pole
Primordial Jade Winged-Spear
: This hast weapon increases critical rate and gives a significant bonus ofATK% when you hit enemies;
Staff of Homa
: with it, you will increase the critical damage, HP andATK of Cyno ;
Deathmatch
: the weapon of the battle pass boosts critical rate,ATK and DEF depending on the number of enemies present;
Blackcliff Pole
: This hast weapon can be bought in the game's store and boosts the critical damage rate and theATK after defeating an enemy.
The best Cyno equipment for beginners and free-to-play players
You are a
beginner player in Genshin Impact
and want to stay on free-to-play content for the build of your Cyno?
It's a good thing, because one of the best weapons to equip on him is a 3-star hast weapon: The
White Tassel
. It increases critical damage and damage from normal attacks. This 3-star weapon is one of the best options for Cyno's equipment in Genshin Impact. You can find them in Liyue's chests.
White Tassel
You can also use the
Kitain Cross Spear
, which can be made by the blacksmith. This weapon increases the elemental mastery and the damage of elemental skills.
Kitain Cross Spear
As for the artifacts, if you are not yet at the 45 level of adventure, we recommend :
Martial artist
Berserker
Martial Artist
: This set increases the damage of normal and charged attacks;
Berserker
: With two coins, you will increase Cyno's critical rate by 12% and with 4 coins by 24% when his HP are below 70%.
Cyno's climbing materials in Genshin Impact
In order to
raise Cyno to level 90 in Genshin Impact
, you will need to collect the following resources:
1 Sliver, 9 Fragments, 9 Chunk and 6
Gemstone of Vajrada Amethyst
as well as 46
Thunderclap Fruitcore
, to be looted from the
Electro Regisvines
;
168
Scarabs
that you will find in the desert of Sumeru
.
Locate them with the
interactive map of Teyvat
.
18
Divinatory Scrolls
, 30
Signed Scrolls
and 36
Cursed Scrolls
to collect from
Shamans Hilichurl.
Which team should Cyno play in?
To be able to perform his elemental rampage as often as possible, Cyno will need a battery and therefore an Electro character to assist him like
Fishl
or
Shinobu
who are the best options.
Also, playing with a
Dendro character
like
Nahida
or the
Dendro Traveler
will boost their damage with
Aggravate
reactions.
Among the best teams to play Cyno in Genshin Impact, we advise you :
Cyno /
Nahida
/
Xingqiu
/
Shinobu
or
Beidou
: This team is based on the
Hyperbloom
reaction. In addition to boosting Cyno's damage with the Dendro element, you will explode
Dendro Cores
on the enemies. You can also use the team Cyno /
Fishl
/
Nahida
/
Kokomi
.
Cyno / Nahida /
Xingqiu
/
Thomas
: it's the same principle, but this time, it's based on the
Burgeon
reaction (Dendro + Hydro +Pyro) ;
Cyno / Fishl / Nahida or Traveler Dendro /
Zhongli
or
Kazuha
: The character Dendro will boost Cyno's damage thanks to the
Aggravate
effect, Fishl will allow Cyno to recharge his energy faster, while Zhongli will protect him and avoid him being interrupted. You can also choose Kazuha to gather enemies and boost Cyno's Electro damage.
If you are just starting out and have few characters, one of the best teams for Cyno will be : Cyno / Fishl /
Dendro Traveler
/
Barbara
How old and how tall is Cyno in Genshin Impact ?
Cyno is an important character of Sumeru since he is the general mahamatra; he supervises the researchers of the academy. He is about 1m61 tall and would be about 18/20 years old.
That's it for this
guide dedicated to Cyno from Genshin Impact.
He is an easy character to play, even for beginners, but also fun and dynamic. With a good build, he will do a lot of damage in the Abyss. To maximize his potential, we recommend playing him with a Dendro character and making sure that the effects of other characters stay on long enough while he does his elemental rampage. If you are not skilled in the art of dodging, opt for characters that can increase his resistance to interruption.

Popular Teams For Cyno

Description
▼ Hyperbloom Cyno
★★★★★
- Cyno will deal Hyperbloom with Furina and Baizhu.
- This is a high damage team that also gives Interruption Resist to Cyno through Baizhu.
- The Personal DMG of Cyno and additional DMG from Hyperbloom reaction is very high in this team comp.
▼ Aggravate Cyno
★★★★
- Cyno can deal a ton of damage with an Aggravate team comp
- With Fischl, you can get enough energy to continuously use Cyno's Burst
- Nahida will ensure continuous use & spread of Aggravate
- Yaoyao will provide additional Dendro application & team healing
▼ Nuke Cyno
★★★
- Cyno can deal a ton of damage with the correct setup between Bennett & Lisa
- Nahida will ensure continuous application of Aggravate & Spread
▼ Electro-Charged Cyno
★★★
- Cyno can take advantage of Yelan's Burst during his own Burst to continuously proc Electro-charged
- Kokomi will keep the team's health up while also being able to apply Hydro to enemies
- Fischl will provide energy to Cyno and proc Electro-Charged

This team will deal pure Electro DMG and several Hyperblooms that are devastating on both single target and multiple enemies.
Pros & Cons Of Hyperbloom Cyno Team
Pros
- High damage potential team comp since attacks come from all characters
- Cyno as the Hyperbloom driver can deal massive damage as well when procing Hyperbloom & Aggravate
- Easy rotation that does not reduce the damage even when mistakes happen.
Cons
- Will require a bit of energy management for Cyno.
- Being mindful of HP is required since Furina drains the team's HP quickly.
Rotation Of Hyperbloom Cyno Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida's Burst
2
Either Hold Nahida's Skill to apply the mark on all enemies (if they are further apart) or quick E if they're all near you
3
Switch to Furina and use Elemental Skill (3 Salon Members)
4
Use Elemental Burst of Furina.
5
Switch to Baizhu and use Elemental Skill and Burst
6
Switch to Cyno, use Elemental Skill then Burst.
7
Deal damage with Elemental Skill and Normal attacks until the buffs of other off-field expires.
8
Repeat from No. 1
The most important part of this setup is keeping the buffs from other characters active. If the buffs ran out while the Burst is still active, it is okay to stop the Elemental Burst then proceed to setting the buffs once again.

This team build is made with Aggravate and Spread in mind. You can deal a ton of damage across all characters since Cyno is not the only one that can benefit from the reactions.
Pros & Cons Of Aggravate Cyno Team
Pros
- Good damage across all characters due to Spread/Aggravate
- Cyno will really shine with Aggravate since his EM build will increase the damage
Cons
- Since it only has two elements in the team, it can be hard to deal against Electro-resistant and Dendro-resistant enemies
Rotation Of Aggravate Cyno Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida's Burst
2
Either Hold Nahida's Skill to apply the mark on all enemies (if they are further apart) or quick E if they're all near you
3
Switch to Cyno and use his Burst. Use your Normal Attack and Skill until Cyno's Burst expires
4
Switch to Fischl and use her skill
5
Switch to Cyno to get as much energy as you can
6
Switch to Yaoyao when you need healing
The rotation for this build is fairly simple and is not as restricted as other characters. Make sure to always keep up Nahida's Elemental Skill to ensure that Dendro can always proc for Spread/Aggravate.

This is a Nuke build, meaning all of the damage are meant to come from a single instance. It's not that recommended against multiple enemies, but it will work well against Bosses.
Pros & Cons Of Nuke Cyno Team
Pros
- Big damage numbers from Cyno
- Can defeat most bosses when setup is done right
Cons
- Requires a good understanding of their rotation and cooldowns
- Does not work quite as well when against multiple enemies since the damage is focused on a single instance
Rotation Of Nuke Cyno Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida's Burst
2
Either Hold Nahida's Skill to apply the mark on all enemies (if they are further apart) or quick E if they're all near you
3
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst
4
Switch to Lisa and use her Burst
5
Switch to Cyno and use his Burst. Keep using his Normal attack and Elemental Skill when it prompts
The rotation for this build is fairly simple since you will be using all of the Bursts almost at the same time. Since it's very restricted like this, this is again not recommended for multiple instances of spawning enemies.

This team build is made to continuously apply Hydro and Electro to deal Tazer. Cyno will act as the Main DPS, while the rest of the team work with him to deal electro-charged.
Pros & Cons Of Electro-Charged Cyno Team
Pros
- Good damage across all characters due to Electro-charge damage
Cons
- Since it only has two elements in the team, it can be hard to deal against Electro-resistant and Hydro-resistant enemies
Rotation Of Electro-Charged Cyno Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Yelan's Burst
2
Switch to Cyno and use his Burst. Continue to attack with your normal attacks and Elemental Skill until it expires
3
Switch to Kokomi and use her skill
4
Switch to Fischl and use her skill
5
Switch to Cyno and let him be charged with the particles from Fischl's Skill
6
Switch to Yelan and use her skill
7
Repeat Step 1
The rotation for this build is fairly simple since you need to pair Yelan's Burst with Cyno's Burst most of the time. Kokomi's skill can be used while Yelan's Burst is done. While Fischl's Skill and Burst should be used to give energy to Cyno.

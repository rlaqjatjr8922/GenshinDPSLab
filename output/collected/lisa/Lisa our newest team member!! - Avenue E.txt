Lisa our newest team member!!
Lisa is our newest member, and the baby of the team. Lisa just recently finished her two year diploma at Douglas College, to become an Optician. During the short time she has been here, Lisa is settling in and getting comfortable in the new environment. She is currently under training to become an educational, and inspirational Optician at Avenue Eyecare, to provide the best care to every patient. Lisa enjoys helping everyone, but is striving to learn more about our products to further educate our patients.
Apart from work and school, Lisa likes to go to the gym to stay fit and healthy (especially when she has to run for the bus or skytrain). Lisa also enjoys playing different kinds of sports, but badminton and volleyball are her most favourite. In the future, she would like to start playing in tournaments more often to stay connected with her friends as well as make new ones.
Lisa will be working more often at Avenue Eyecare, so stop by to say hi to her!

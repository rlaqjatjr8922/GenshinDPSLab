Lisa Skill Guide & How To Use Lisa
Recommended Rotation For Lisa
Recommended Rotation
1
Start with Elemental Skill to build stacks
2
Activate it using your charged attack and spam normal attacks before then
3
Release Elemental Burst then switch to another character to cause Elemental Reactions
Uses Of Lisa's Elemental Skill
Applies Stacks For Amplified Damage
Lisa's Elemental Skill can apply a stack on enemies that it hits that deals additional damage when triggered. Focus on building stacks first to be able to increase your damage, especially when against multiple enemies.
Trigger The Applied Stacks
By holding the Elemental Skill, Lisa can trigger the applied stacks on the enemies that cause additional damage. Be careful when doing it as it requires channeling and makes Lisa vulnerable.
Uses Of Lisa's Elemental Burst
Can Deal Continuous Electro Damage
So long as Lisa's Burst is activated, it will zap an enemy to apply Electro to them. Since it's an on-field ability, maximize it by switching to either a Hydro or Pyro character to get Elemental Reactions.
Decrease Enemies' DEF
One of her passive talents enables Lisa to decrease the DEF of enemies by 15% for 10 seconds when it gets hit by her Elemental Burst.
Should You Pull for Lisa's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Constellations Are Ideal, But Hard to Acquire
Constellation Priority
C4 > C6
C4 Is Her Best Constellation
Lisa with her base kit is a little lacking in terms of performance, and has very few teams where she's more than an okay option.
Getting more constellation levels on her at least elevates her to a decent alternative in teams that can flex an off-field Electro sub-dps.
C4 will be the ideal stopping point since her Burst will now release 1-3 lightning bolts whenever it attacks, thereby increasing her personal damage and Electro application overall, which is nice to have on Quicken and other reaction oriented teams that involve Electro.
C6 provides a decent Quality of Life change to Lisa's gameplay.
Swapping to her in combat will immediately affect nearby enemies with 3 stacks of Conductive status, allowing her to use Skill (Hold) immediately instead of having to accumulate it first with Skill (Press) before using Skill (Hold), which makes her less clunky to use.
It is worth mentioning that while her constellations make her a lot more usable, Lisa is one among the three 4-stars characters who are given for free during the first few chapters of the Monstadt Archon Quest and have also not been featured in any rate-up banner since the game's release.
It isn't realistic to get her to any constellation level apart from the off-chance you get her from a Standard Banner Pull or if you diligently save enough Starglitter to get Lisa every time she appears in
Paimon's Bargain
, which totals to twice a year.
It will be significantly easier to invest into a better catalyst or into her premium supports options, but if you are dedicated enough, getting Lisa's constellations will only be a matter of time.

Who Is Lisa?
Head Librarian of the Knights of Favonius
Lisa Minci is a powerful Electro user who works as the Head Librarian of the Knights of Favonius in Mondstadt.  Lisa’s deep understanding of arcane lore and her meticulous nature ensure that the library remains a vital resource, offering guidance on everything from elemental magic theory to local history.
A Genius Mage with Boundless Knowledge
Lisa is widely recognized as one of the most brilliant mages in Mondstadt. Her mastery over Electro magic is second only to her intellectual curiosity. Her deep understanding of the arcane arts, elemental manipulation, and ancient texts makes her a formidable figure among scholars and adventurers alike.
Former Akademia Scholar
Lisa is also a distinguished graduate of Sumeru Academia, renowned for being one of the most gifted mages the institution has produced in centuries. Despite the prestige and opportunities that awaited her in Sumeru, Lisa chose to return to Mondstadt.

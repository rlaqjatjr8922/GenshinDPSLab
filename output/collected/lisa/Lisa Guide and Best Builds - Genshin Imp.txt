Lisa Guide and Best Builds
Lisa is one of the original 4-star characters of Genshin Impact.  She has
long been a puzzling character, as her toolkit was confusing and did not fit
many teams. However, the addition of
Dendro
has made Lisa a
fantastic options as an on-field driver or an off-field reaction support. She
offers an exceptional
Electro Aura
on the field and
can shred enemy defenses as well as being a potentially strong Electro on-field
DPSer with the right investment.

1.
Lisa Off-field DPS Build

DPS role, it requires a lot less investment and does not require any of her
constellations.

2.
Combos and How to Play Lisa
The first thing any new Lisa player needs to understand is how her
Conductive stacks work. This component of her game play is quite confusing and
the in-game explanations do not help. Lisa's Elemental Skill,
Violet Arc
, once tapped will travel to an enemy and the initial
impact will create a small radius. Enemies within that radius, including the
first one you hit, will gain a single stack of Conductive.  Now this what the
game fails to explain, each of the mobs that were hit in the initial strike
will also create their own small radius for a single Conductive stack. This is
why a well placed skill shot can create 3 stacks of Conductive on multiple
enemies. This effect is also applied if a reaction,
Overload
or
Superconduct
is
triggered against an enemy, granting another stack of Conductive.
This leaves us with the question of how to best play Lisa on the field. There are three 
methods to work with.
First, tap her Elemental Skill and then tap it again.  This strings
together her Elemental Skills, allowing for a faster and more effective
Conductive application.
Second, weave her Elemental Skill in between your standard attacks. This
again is an effective way to build different radius' for her Conductive stacks
to apply to multiple targets, getting to 3 stacks faster.
Lastly, you hold her Elemental Skill for the second application of it.
While this does take time to ‘charge' up, this application of Conductive works
best when you already have reactions on the field to create a larger area for
her to connect her skill to.
Her Elemental Burst
Lightning Rose
is a large AoE 
ring with a lantern in the center of it. This lantern or "rose" strikes enemies 
constantly within the area with a blast of lightning. This is effect is 
continuous regardless of if Lisa on or off the field. So using it for 
reactionary drivers or triggers is best.

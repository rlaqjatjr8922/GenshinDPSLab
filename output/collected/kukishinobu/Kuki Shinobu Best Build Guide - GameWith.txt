Kuki Shinobu is a 4-star Electro character that wields a Sword in Genshin Impact. Her gameplay focuses on being a hybrid healer and support, providing consistent healing through her Elemental Skill while applying Electro to enemies for elemental reactions. She excels in supporting DPS units by keeping the team sustained and enabling Electro-based combos.

Roles & Playstyle Of Escoffier and Its Strengths and Weakness
Healer Shinobu
Shinobu's best role is as a healer who can be built with HP or EM. In both HP healer and EM Hyperbloom Sub-DPS build, she has similar levels of healing.
Pros and Cons of Healer Shinobu
Pros
- Only needs Elemental Skill for healing and HP or EM stat to boost her healing.
- Can be used as Healer and Hyperbloom Sub-DPS if built on full Elemental Mastery.
Cons
- Before C6, Shinobu can be prone to getting knocked out since casting her Elemental Skill drains her HP by 30% and enemies are able to attack her since her Elemental Skill doesn't have an invisibility frame like her Elemental Burst.

Team Rotation of Kuki Shinobu and Combos
Basic Team Rotation with Healer/Sub-DPS Shinobu
Basic Rotation with Healer/Sub-DPS Shinobu
1. Use other Supports/Sub-DPS abilities.
2. Switch to Shinobu and use her Elemental Skill.
3. Switch to the On-Field Character.
Shinobu only uses her Elemental Skill in team rotations since it's the source of both her healing and Off-Field Electro Application. And since she's often used with a Dendro DPS, she goes before the On-Field character to provide healing right away.
Basics Of Using Kuki Shinobu
Shinobu is easy to use since she only needs her Elemental Skill to be activated. The only difficult part that comes with using her is what stat to focus on since it depends on which role you want for her.
Use Elemental Skill to Heal & Apply Electro
Shinobu's Elemental Skill creates an Electro Ring around the active character and reduces her HP by 30%. This Ring can heal the active character and deal damage to surrounding enemies.
Elemental Mastery Build or HP Build?
Shinobu's healing from her Elemental Skill scales on both her HP and Elemental Mastery
. If you want to use her as a pure Healing Support, building her on HP can work but if you want to use her as a Hyperbloom Sub-DPS and healer, going full Elemental Mastery works better.
Elemental Burst Is Not Needed for Shinobu
Shinobu's Elemental Burst is a Damaging Skill that won't deal much damage since Shinobu is primarily built as a Healer with no CRIT Stats. This means that
Energy Recharge isn't needed on her
and that she's a very good healer to bring for Stygian Onslaught if you need one.
Elemental Skill, Burst and Abilities Guide for Kuki Shinobu
How To Use Kuki Shinobu's Skills And Passives?
How To Use?
▼ Electro Sub-DPS for Hyperbloom & Electro Healer
▼ Scales on both HP and Elemental Mastery for her Healing
▼ Only needs Elemental Skill to deal Damage Off-Field and Heal
▼ Energy Recharge isn't needed since her Elemental Burst is optional
Electro Sub-DPS For Hyperbloom & Electro Healer
Kuki Shinobu is an Electro Hyperbloom Sub-DPS that can also work as a healer. Her Off-Field Damage scales on her Elemental Mastery and HP, making her ideal to build as Hyperbloom Enabler. It also comes with healing which allows her to take on the healer role as well.
Scales On Both HP And Elemental Mastery For Her Healing
Shinobu's Elemental Skill's damage scale on her HP but her Passive boosts it with her Elemental Mastery. This makes building both good but it's best to focus on one stat depending on her Build.
Only Needs Elemental Skill To Deal Damage Off-Field And Heal
When building Shinobu, getting her to max level is important for both builds then the only Skill needed is her Elemental Skill. Her Elemental Skill creates an Electro Ring that deals Electro Damage periodically while also healing the active character.
Energy Recharge Isn't Needed Since Her Elemental Burst Is Optional
Shinobu's Elemental Burst is good for dealing Damage but the damage potential is rather low compared to other characters which make it ignored when building. It also takes up time when in rotations.
Should You Pull for Kuki Shinobu's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Any Constellation Level is Good to Have
Constellation Priority
C2 > C4 or C6
C2 is a Good Stopping Point
Shinobu's use cases will highlight her healing, Electro application and to an extent her personal damage, which her constellations steadily build up on. Although C6 is going to be the best increase to her damage overall,
stopping at her C2, where her skill duration is increased, will already upgrade Shinobu's performance as an electro healer for her specific teams,
since most of them heavily depend on her "Grass Ring of Sanctification". Anything beyond C1 and C2 are welcome bonuses to have, and you wouldn't need to fully commit to getting C6 if you aren't able to. However, the extra Electro application from C4, the "Thundergrass Mark", is good for maintaining a Quicken aura for just a little longer, so it's something to consider.
Of course, Shinobu at C0 isn't too much an issue, and you can continue using her at this constellation level without too much of a deficit versus a Shinobu with C2.

Who Is Kuki Shinobu?
Deputy Leader of the Arataki Gang
Kuki Shinobu is the deputy leader of Arataki Itto's Arataki Gang. Her role in the gang is to keep their troublemakers in line and to keep the gang's activities from potential infractions.
Former Shrine Maiden of the Grand Narukami Shrine
In one of Shinobu's Hangout endings, Yae Miko gives the Traveler a photo of Shinobu in her Shrine Maiden days. It was also revealed that the majority of her family worked as Shrine Maidens and she was forced to become a Shrine Maiden due to it. She eventually quit and joined the Arataki Gang.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Hyperbloom Kuki Shinobu Team
Character
Detail
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of Hyperblooms.
Xingqiu
The consistent Hydro application can produce Dendro cores, especially when the active character is beside the enemy marked with Nahida's Skill.
Yaoyao
With Deepwood Memories x4, Yuegui can reduce the Dendro RES of the enemy and backup healer when Kuki's HP is low. Also, the Dendro resonance is enabled with Nahida.
The objective of this team is to constantly produce bloom so Kuki's Skill can transform it into Hyperbloom projectiles.
Yaoyao Vs Baizhu For Dendro Support
Theoretically, Baizhu is better than Yaoyao due to his shield & heal. However, it would be best if you consider the effects of the constellation. Since Yaoyao is a 4-star character, you can likely acquire her constellation more than Baizhu. In this case,
Hyperbloom team can benefit more from Yaoyao with C2 & C4 than Baizhu at C0
.
Pros & Cons Of Hyperbloom Kuki Shinobu Team
Pros
- Hyperbloom projectiles can chase enemies
- Relatively easy to build since you only need to focus on raising EM as high as possible
Cons
- Could have a hard time dealing with enemies that are immune or resistant to Dendro
- Kuki cannot sustain Grass Ring unless C2
Rotation Of Hyperbloom Kuki Shinobu Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Elemental Burst for EM buff
2
Use Nahida's Elemental Skill for mark
3
Switch to Xingqiu
4
Use Xingqiu's Elemental Burst & Skill for Hydro application & coordinated attack
5
Switch to Kuki
6
Use Kuki's Elemental Skill for persistent Electro application
7
Switch to Yaoyao
8
Use Yaoyao's Elemental Skill to either reduce the enemy's Dendro RES or heal the active character
From time to time, you can
switch to Yaoyao for her Burst if you need to recover much HP
. Not only she can heal faster but she can also apply Dendro as you move with Yaoyao.
When To Use Kuki's Burst
Kuki's Burst
does not have an internal cooldown
so it can consecutively apply Electro and trigger any possible reactions when the enemy inside the AoE. It is recommended to
use when both Nahida's Burst & Xingqiu's barrier are active
.

Dendro Cores Can Be Pulled By Sucrose
If you already have a healer, Sucrose is a good choice for the 4th slot. She has an EM buff and her crowd control can also compress the Dendro cores. When all of the Dendro cores are bundled together, Kuki can trigger them all at once.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Electro Support Kuki Shinobu Team
Character
Detail
Eula
Eula fights at close range and needs Superconduct to deal damage making Kuki Shinobu a good pair with it.
Rosaria
Rosaria's CRIT Rate share and Physical Debuff makes her an amazing support for Eula.
Mika
Mika helps in healing Kuki Shinobu and dealing speed buff for Eula.
For Kuki Shinobu's support team, she acts as a buffer and a healer instead of a sub-DPS. Kuki Shinobu works well with Melee characters + characters that work well with Electro reactions such as Eula. Kuki Shinobu + Main DPS is good enough of a team so the other 2 slots should be supports that is based on needs of the main DPS.
Pros & Cons Of Electro Support Kuki Shinobu Team
Pros
- Team members are very flexible.
Cons
- Most of the time, Kuki is not the best pick for those teams.
Rotation Of Electro Support Kuki Shinobu Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Eula's Elemental Skill.
2
Switch to Rosaria and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
3
Switch to Mika and use Elemental Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Kuki Shinobu and use Elemental Skill.
5
Switch to Eula and use Elemental Skill → Elemental Burst → Elemental Skill (Hold)
6
Deal Normal Attack until Elemental Burst explodes.
7
Return to No. 1
When To Use Kuki's Burst?
You can use Kuki's Burst if you want to heal Kuki Shinobu a bit because this gives Kuki quite a long invincible time.

Genshin Impact Linnea Build Guide: Best Weapons, Artifacts, and Teams

Linnea is a Geo support in Genshin Impact built around Lunar-Crystallize reactions. This guide covers her best weapons, artifacts, stats, and team setups to maximize her damage, buffs, and overall team value.
Genshin Impact introduces Linnea as a Geo support who focuses on boosting reaction-based teams through Lunar-Crystallize mechanics. She provides resistance shred, healing, and off-field damage, making her a strong enabler rather than a main DPS.
To use her effectively, you need the right build setup. Weapons, artifacts, and team composition all play a major role in unlocking her full potential, especially in Hydro + Geo teams.
Best weapons for Linnea
Choosing the right weapon is important because Linnea scales with DEF and benefits from CRIT stats.
Top weapon options:
Frostbound Oath (Signature weapon)
High CRIT DMG stat
Increases DEF and team damage
5-star CRIT bows
Strong stat scaling for support damage
Slingshot (F2P option)
Easy to obtain and still effective early game
Her signature weapon stands out due to its strong CRIT DMG scaling and DEF bonus, which directly supports her kit.
Best artifacts for Linnea
Artifacts define Linnea’s performance as a support. She benefits most from DEF-based builds with CRIT focus.
Main stat priority:
Sands → DEF%
Goblet → DEF%
Circlet → CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG
Substats priority:
CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG
DEF%
Elemental Mastery
DEF scaling is key for her abilities, while CRIT helps improve her off-field damage.
You can also use mixed sets if you do not have optimal gear, but focus on maximizing DEF and CRIT balance.
Best artifact set combinations
While exact sets may vary, these combinations work best:
4-piece support-focused sets → Boost team damage and utility
2-piece DEF + 2-piece CRIT setups → Balanced damage and scaling
Reaction-based sets → Improve Lunar-Crystallize output
The goal is to enhance both her support value and reaction amplification.
Best Linnea teams
Linnea performs best in teams that use Hydro to trigger Lunar-Crystallize reactions.
Top meta team:
Zibai (Main DPS)
Linnea (Support)
Columbina (Hydro Sub-DPS)
Illuga (Buffer)
This setup maximizes reaction damage and overall team synergy.
Navia burst team:
Navia (Main DPS)
Linnea (Support)
Hydro unit
Flex support
This team focuses on burst damage and quick rotations.
F2P-friendly team:
Noelle (Main DPS)
Linnea (Support)
Hydro support
Flex unit
Linnea helps solve energy and damage issues in low-investment teams.
Best way to build Linnea (quick setup)
If you want a simple setup, follow this structure:
Build DEF-focused stats
Add CRIT for damage scaling
Use Hydro + Geo team synergy
Focus on reaction-based gameplay
This ensures you get both utility and damage from her kit.
Common mistakes to avoid
Many players misuse Linnea due to misunderstanding her role:
Building her as a main DPS
Ignoring DEF scaling
Using her without Hydro teammates
Over-investing in Energy Recharge
Fixing these will significantly improve performance.
TL;DR
Build DEF + CRIT → use signature or CRIT bow → equip DEF-based artifacts → pair with Hydro units → trigger Lunar-Crystallize → boost team damage → use as support, not DPS.
Final Thoughts
Linnea is one of the most impactful Geo supports introduced in recent updates, but only when built correctly. She depends heavily on team synergy and proper stat scaling.
If you focus on DEF, CRIT, and reaction-based teams, she can significantly increase your overall team damage and make Geo compositions far more effective than before.

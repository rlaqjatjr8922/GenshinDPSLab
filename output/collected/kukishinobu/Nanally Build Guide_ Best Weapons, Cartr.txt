Character Overview, Playstyle Mechanics & Skill Leveling Priority
Nanally is an S-Rank Anima Main DPS utilizing a Plasma Arc. Her entire kit revolves around entering an enhanced state and overwhelming the enemy with coordinated strikes.
Core Mechanic 1:
Ichi-daime's Authority (Skill) Casting her Skill (Colucci Howling Technique) grants her a 12-second buff that significantly increases her Critical Damage (+30% CRT DMG).
Core Mechanic 2:
Underboss (Ultimate) Her Ultimate (Colucci Ultimate Technique) summons her "Underboss" to fight alongside her for 6 seconds (extendable via Awakenings). While active, the Underboss coordinates with all of Nanally's attacks, dealing massive Anima DMG that counts as Follow-up Attack damage.
Combat Rotation: Trigger her Skill to gain the CRT DMG buff -> immediately cast her Ultimate to summon the Underboss -> continuously attack to trigger her Passives (like Fair Duel and Call Me the Boss), which rain down additional follow-up attacks.
Skill Leveling Priority:
To optimize your resources, upgrade Nanally's skills in this order:
Colucci Ultimate Technique (Ultimate): Highest priority. Upgrading this directly increases the scaling of all your Underboss's follow-up attacks.
Colucci Howling Technique (Skill): Increases the initial area damage and secures your CRT DMG buff.
Basic Attack: Level this last. While she constantly uses Basic Attacks on-field, the vast majority of her actual damage output stems from the Underboss coordinating with those attacks, not the basic multipliers themselves.
Awakening (Constellations) Depth Analysis
Nanally is perfectly viable and incredibly strong at base level (A0), meaning F2P players do not need to feel pressured to pull duplicates. However, if you are planning to invest further, here are her most impactful Awakening milestones:
A0 (Base Form): A complete and powerful kit. Ready to carry your team out of the box.
A1 (Gang Formation): High Value. Fixes her energy issues. Grants 2.5 Ultimate Energy for each follow-up attack she performs.
A3 (Call Me the Boss): Massive DPS Spike. Applies an extra follow-up attack (50% ATK) every second while she is in her enhanced state.
A4 (Not a Troublemaker): Grants a flat 30% DMG increase specifically for her Underboss.
A6 (Because We're Family): The Whale Goal. Extends her Ichi-daime's Authority buff duration from 12s to 15s (and 20s when out of combat), drastically increasing her overall DPS window.
Best Weapons (Arc) for Nanally
Nanally uses Plasma type Arcs. Here is the breakdown of her best-in-slot signature weapon versus the best free-to-play alternative.
T0 (Best): Ready-Ready (Signature)
The absolute best choice. Provides a massive ATK% boost (up to 30% at Rank 5). Casting her Ultimate or Redirect Skill grants stacks that buff her Basic Attack and Critical Riposte damage by up to 30%. Managing the Tiger Talismans grants a direct DMG boost against Bosses.
T1 (F2P): Song of the Whale (Map Boss Drop)
An incredible free alternative. At max refinement (Rank 5), it performs at roughly 84% the efficiency of her signature weapon. It grants 20% ATK, increases DMG to Broken enemies by 20%, and provides excellent sustain by restoring 30% HP upon defeating a Broken enemy.
Best Cartridge (Artifact) Configuration
To maximize Nanally's damage, you need a set that rewards her multi-hit, elemental playstyle.
Best Set: Fireflies and the Forest (Liquid Set)
2-Piece Bonus: Anima DMG +10%.
4-Piece Bonus: Increases the wearer's CRIT DMG by 8% when a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from the Team, stacking up to 7 times (Max +56% CRIT DMG).
Because Nanally's Underboss and passives deal rapid instances of Anima DMG, she maxes out the 7 stacks almost instantly.
Stat Priorities
Because the "Fireflies and the Forest" set and her built-in skills already provide a massive amount of Damage Boost, managing your stats carefully is key to avoiding diminishing returns.
Main Stat Selection: Prioritize Anima DMG Bonus for your primary cartridge slot. Establishing this elemental baseline is crucial for maximizing her burst potential.
Sub-Stat Priorities: Once your Main Stat is secured, scaling her base ATK% yields higher overall damage than blindly stacking more DMG increases.
CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG: The absolute core. Keep a 1:2 ratio (e.g., 70% CRIT Rate / 140% CRIT DMG).
ATK %: Because her follow-up multipliers are so high, increasing her base ATK gives those multipliers (and her built-in DMG buffs) a much larger number to scale off of.
DMG Increase: Still a great sub-stat, but lower priority than ATK% to avoid stat dilution.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Q1: Can Nanally work as a Sub-DPS?
No. Nanally's Ichi-daime's Authority buff disappears the moment she leaves the battlefield. She is a hyper-carry Main DPS who demands field time to deal her damage.
Q2: Does she need a healer?
If you are using her F2P weapon (Song of the Whale), she has built-in self-sustain upon defeating Broken enemies. However, in high-difficulty boss fights (where adds are scarce), running a dedicated shielder or healer is recommended to keep her safe during her combos.
Q3: Is she good for open-world exploration?
Yes! Her Grand Entrance! (Plunge Attack) has a massive damage multiplier based on fall height, and her combat mobility makes traversing the urban environments of NTE very fluid.

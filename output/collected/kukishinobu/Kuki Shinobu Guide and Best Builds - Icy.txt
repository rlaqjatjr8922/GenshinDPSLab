Kuki Shinobu Guide and Best Builds
Kuki Shinobu is a 4-star Electro Sword character in Genshin Impact. She
functions best as a Healer and as a Sub-DPS for Dendro reaction-based team
compositions.

Kuki Shinobu's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Consistent powerful Healing.
Incredibly powerful in Hyperbloom teams.
Great Electro application.
Weaknesses
Healing is only single-target.
Low energy generation.
Frequent use of Elemental Skill keeps her HP low.

4.
Combos and How to Play Kuki Shinobu
Kuki is easy to play with her Elemental Skill,
Sanctifying Ring
using a portion of her HP to heal whoever is currently on the field as well
as dealing Electro damage to nearby enemies.
Gyoei Narukami Kariyama Rite
deals Electro damage in a circle and
lasts longer if Kuki's HP is below 50%. Both of these are important to her
Electro
Application.
Often, you only want to quickly swap Kuki in to apply
Sanctifying Ring
before swapping back to other characters to both maintain healing while trying
to ensure her HP stays below 50%.

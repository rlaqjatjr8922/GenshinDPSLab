Kuki Shinobu Team Overview
This Page provides the best Kuki Shinobu Team information. Kuki Shinobu will be Healer in most of the situation. Kuki Shinobu is the first Electro Healer.
From SS tier to A tier, Kuki Shinobu best team can be formed in different ways including Thunder, Superconduct, Electro-Charged and Overload team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

Nahida mainly deals DMG by her Elemental Skill, which connects 8 enemies at maximum and deals Dendro DMG to them while triggering elemental reactions. The Elemental Burst of Nahida buffs her Elemental Skill depends on the Element of her teammates.
With aid of Xingqiu and Yelan which can provide Hydro Element even off-field, Blooming can be triggered to produce Dendro cores. Then switch to Kuki of high Elemental Mastery and use her Electro skills to activate the Dendro cores, which finally trigger Hyperbloom.
As there are two Hydro Characters and one Electro Character in this team, the Tri-Karma Purification trigger interval is reduced and the duration of the Elemental Burst is increased.

Genshin Impact Snare Hook weapon guide
Discuss Now
Guide on crafting and leveling up Snare Hook (Image via Sportskeeda Gaming/HoYoverse)
Snare Hook is a new 4-star Bow added to the game in Genshin Impact Luna I. Belonging to the Nod-Krai craftable weapon series, it can be forged after obtaining its blueprint from the Nasha Town smithy. This weapon boosts both Energy Recharge and Elemental Mastery, making it a great free-to-play weapon for characters who scale off of these stats.
Ad
This article provides a guide on obtaining the sketch for the Snare Hook and crafting it, as well as its stats and level-up materials, along with a list of characters that you can equip this Bow on.

Genshin Impact Snare Hook: How to craft
Lyulka's location in Nasha town (Image via Sportskeeda Gaming/HoYoverse)
To craft Snare Hook, you must first obtain its blueprint from Lyulka at Rossum Workshop in Nasha Town. To get to her, teleport to the main Nasha Town Teleport Waypoint and make your way southeast to reach the smithy.
Ad
Purchase the Snare Hook blueprint (Image via Sportskeeda Gaming/HoYoverse)
Talk to Lyulka and choose the "I'd like to buy a weapon" option. Then, purchase the
Diagram: Snare Hook
from her using these materials:
Ad
Nocturnal Blossom x10
Midsommar Berry x10
Rainbowdrop Crystal x10
After having bought the blueprint from Lyulka, you can forge Snare Hook with the following materials:
Borderland Bow Billet x1
Rainbowdrop Crystals x50
White Iron Chunks x50

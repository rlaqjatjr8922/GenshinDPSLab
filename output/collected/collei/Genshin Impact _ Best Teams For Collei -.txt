Best Teams for Collei
Collei Works Best As Dendro Sub-DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Catalyze Team
- Collei's many sources of Dendro application is great for Catalyze teams.
- Dendro resonance can lessen ER requirements for Collei and buff EM for the team.
2nd
▼ Superbloom Team
- Sprout effect for Dendro damage over time is easily triggered due to the abundance of Bloom reactions happening.
- Collei's Burst can be easily extended in this team.
3rd
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- C4 Collei can buff EM for the Hyperbloom driver.
- Her passive allows the active character to trigger Dendro damage over time on enemies.
4th
▼ Burgeon Team
- C4 Collei's EM buff to the team can increase the Burgeon damage.
- Dendro resonance can further increase the Burgeon damage as well as reduce Collei's ER requirements.
Collei is able to inflict Dendro easily with her Skills and Passive effects. She is best used in Dendro reaction teams though she is often paired with another Dendro character to battery her Burst.

Rotation Of Catalyze Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Clorinde and use her Burst to apply Electro
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies with Normal Attacks twice
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Fischl and use her Skill to summon Oz
5
Switch to Collei and use her Burst then Skill
6
Switch back to Clorinde and use her Skill twice
7
Deal damage with Normal Attacks and use Clorinde's Skill to perform a lunging attack
8
Repeat from 1

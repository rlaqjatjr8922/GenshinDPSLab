Post-Buff Razor Works Best As Electro DPS
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Overload Razor
★★★★★
- Easy to use team featuring overload reaction with Chevreuse as the main support and C6 Bennett.
- Requires C6 Bennett and Chevreuse to be the best but is very straight forward and easy to use.
- Provides a huge amount of Electro and Pyro DMG to enemies.
▼ Lunar Charged Team
★★★★・
- Razor has consistent Electro application and can be used with Hydro sub-DPS and Ineffa, you can produce an amazing team
- With Durin or C6 Bennett, you can also produce Overload furthering the damage of this team.
▼ Physical DPS Razor
★★★・・
- The team that uses the Physical Bonus Ascension of Razor.
- Greatly increases the Physical DMG of Razor with Mika and Furina.
- For consistency, Witch character like Albedo and Fischl is added.
After Buffs, Razor does not only deal Physical Damage but also a frequent amount of Electro DMG making him amazing even outside Physical Teams. Unlike other Witches, his damage is not completely reliant on the Hexenzirkel (Hexerei) Faction Buff and can work even outside those teams. However,  he still works best when paired with other Hexerei.

This team uses Razor and C6 Bennett to allow Razor to trigger Overload while being buffed by Chevreuse. Durin is there for the useful resistance shred and personal damage in his white mode.
Rotation Of Overload Reaction Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Razor and use Elemental Skill to inflict Electro.
2
Switch to Durin and use Elemental Skill twice then Elemental Burst.
* You should trigger Overload in this step.
3
Switch to Chevreuse and use her Hold Skill then Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Bennett and use Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
5
Switch to Razor and use his Burst then continuously attack with Normal Attack and Skill while staying inside Bennett's Circle.
6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire
To activate all of Chevreuse's Buff, make sure to trigger an Overload reaction and use her Hold Skill.

While Physical teams are not very strong at the current Meta, this is the team that makes use of all sections of Razor's Kit. Furina provides team damage bonus while providing powerful off-field without disturbing Super-conduct for Physical Resistance Shred. Mika increases the movement speed of Razor and provides sustain for the whole team. Fischl activates the Witch Resonance buff with Razor and also provides decent personal damage herself off-field.
Rotation Of Physical DPS Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Elemental Skill and Burst.
2
Switch to Fischl and use her Skill.
*If the Skill is on cooldown, use Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Mika and use his hold Skill to hit enemies
4
Use Mika's Burst before switching him out
5
Switch to Razor and use his tap Skill then Burst
6
Deal damage with Normal Attacks and use his Skill when it is available
7
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire.

Razor is a 4-star Electro character that wields a Claymore in Genshin Impact. His gameplay focuses on being a physical and Electro on-field DPS, excelling at fast melee attacks and powerful burst damage. Razor thrives in close combat, using his Electro abilities to enhance his physical attacks and quickly overwhelm enemies.

Razor is an Electro and Physical DPS who utilizes his Electro Sigils to fast charge his Elemental Burst. Razor's Elemental Burst is his main source of damage as it increases his Normal Attack speed and has Electro coordinated attacks. Razor's Constellations help enhance his performance as a DPS.

Electro DPS

Electro DPS Razor
Razor's best role is Electro DPS since his Burst deals a lot more Electro damage if Secret Rite effect is active. However, the Skills leveled will now include his Elemental Skill if you want to maximize on his damage.
Pros and Cons of Electro DPS Razor
Pros
- Has decent damage and can be used in endgame content.
- Becomes better with Constellations but works fine as C0.
Cons
- Razor's CRIT self buffs and additional Sigil generation is locked in his C6 which newer players might not have.
Physical DPS Razor
Razor can still be used as a Physical DPS but note that Physical teams are still weaker compared to reaction teams. In terms of his buffs, only his Witch's Homework buff for his C6 is good for his Physical DPS role because his Secret Rite passive focuses on the Electro damage.
Pros and Cons of Physical DPS Razor
Pros
- Decent DPS at C0 but better as C4.
- Secret Rite passive allows him to use his Burst faster.
Cons
- CRIT buffs being locked at C6 can lower his damage potential if he's not C6 yet.
Best Build for Razor
Razor's build depends on the role you want to use him on. While he's better as an Electro DPS, it depends on if you have a good Hexerei character to support him. Otherwise, you can use him as a Physical DPS if you don't have a good Hexerei support.

Team Rotation of Razor and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for DPS Razor
Basic Rotation for DPS Razor
1. Use Sub-DPS/Support characters abilities.
2. Switch to Razor and use his Elemental Skill (Tap) then use his Burst.
3. Use Razor's Skill (Hold) then deal damage with Normal Attacks while waiting for his Skill CD to end.
4. Alternate between using his Tap or Held Skill in between Normal Attacks.
5. Repeat from Step 1.
Razor's rotation differs a bit during his Burst duration since spamming Skill (Tap) after the first Hold cast would let him gain Energy faster. Skill (Hold) can be used if you want to deal more damage but otherwise, it's best to focus on letting Razor have enough Energy for the next rotation.
Basics Of Using Razor
Great Electro DPS for Early Game and End Game
Razor was a decent DPS for early game content since he's an easily obtained 4-Star but with the new Hexerei buff, his viability for endgame content has increased. Especially if his Constellation 6 is unlocked since it greatly boosts his CRIT stats.
Hexerei Buff Boosts Damage & Makes It Easier to Use His Burst
One drawback to Razor before his buff was his high Energy cost on his Burst. Since it was his main source of damage, building a decent amount of Energy Recharge was needed but his Skill mechanic allowed you to charge his Burst despite the high cost.
Razor Buff Summary
- Buffs Razor's Wolf silhouette damage by 70% of his ATK.
- If Electro Sigils overflow during Razor's Burst duration, the Wolf silhouette will deal AoE Electro DMG and restore 7 Energy to Razor. Can be triggered once every 1s.
Razor's Hexerei buff allows his Wolf silhouette to deal more instances of damage if his Sigils overflow. It will also regenerate Energy for him.
Newly Buffed C6 is the Best Constellation for Razor
Razor's new buff for his C6 makes him better as a either Physical and Electro DPS since it makes him gain 3 Electro Sigils and gives a 10% CRIT Rate and 50% CRIT DMG buff. It lowers his Energy Recharge and CRIT stat requirements.
Razor C6 Changes Summary
Before
- Every 10s, Razor's next Normal Attack will release lightning that deals Electro DMG based on his ATK.
- When Razor is not using his Burst, the lightning released from his Normal Attack will grant one (1) Electro Sigil.
After
- Every 10s, Razor's next Normal Attack will release lightning that deals Electro DMG based on his ATK.
- When Razor is not using his Burst, the lightning released from his Normal Attack will grant three (3) Electro Sigils.
- Razor's Elemental Skill use (Hold and Tap) will give him 3 Sigils instead.
- After using Razor's Skill (Hold) to consume Electro Sigils, his CRIT Rate is increased by 10% and CRIT DMG by 50%.
Elemental Skill, Burst and Abilities Guide for Razor
How To Use Razor's Skills & Passives?
How To Use?
▼ Electro or Physical DPS Based on Team Comp
▼ Use Elemental Skill (Tap) to Boost Energy Recharge
▼ Elemental Skill (Hold) for Energy
▼ Elemental Burst Summons Coordinated Electro Attacks
▼ Hexerei Buffs Make Him Better as an Electro DPS
Electro Or Physical DPS Based On Team Comp
Razor is a flexible DPS character due to how his Burst works. He can be used as an Electro DPS or Physical DPS since he does not have Elemental Infusion to his attacks. If Secret Rite is active, Razor is better as Electro DPS while if it's inactive, he's better as a Physical DPS.
Use Elemental Skill (Tap) To Boost Energy Recharge
When tapped, Razor's Elemental Skill creates an Electro Sigil (max 3) that boosts his Energy Recharge by 20% per Sigil. These Sigils are used primarily to increase his ER and then consumed to give him Energy so he can use his Burst.
Elemental Skill (Hold) For Energy
Razor can consume all the Sigils he has and gain 5 Energy per absorbed Sigil. While it's a bit lackluster in terms of Energy given, his Constellation 6 makes using his Skill (Hold) more worthwhile.
Elemental Burst Summons Coordinated Electro Attacks
Razor's Elemental Burst summons a wolf silhouette that shadows him during his Burst and does coordinated Electro attacks with Razor's Normal Attacks. His Burst is important for both roles he can do since it also buffs his Normal Attack speed.
Hexerei Buffs Make Him Better As An Electro DPS
Razor's Hexerei and Secret Rite buffs makes him better as an Electro DPS, to the point that he can outdamage older Electro DPS characters with C6. His Burst's coordinated attacks deal more damage and regenerate Energy for him while his C6 is a CRIT stat buff that's useful for both roles.
Razor Hexerei Buffs
Needs Secret Rite
(Requires at least 2 Hexerei in the team)
- New Hexerei passive that allows his Burst to deal more damage and regenerate Energy for him.
Only Needs Witch's Homework Clear
- Improved C6 effect that boosts his CRIT Rate by 10% and CRIT DMG by 50% when he consumes Electro Sigils.
- C6 makes him create 3 Electro Sigils instead of 1 when his Burst is not active.
Should You Pull for Razor's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim for C6 if You Are Able To
Constellation Priority
C6 > C4 > C1
With the new Hexerei buff, Razor's Constellation 6 is now a very good Constellation for him. It allows him to obtain 3 Electro Sigils using his Skill and get a 15% CRIT Rate and 50% CRIT DMG buff after he consumes Electro Sigils.
C1 is a Good Stopping Point
If you are unable to get Razor's C6, stopping at his C1 is fine too. It's able to boost his damage a bit.
Razor will still be functional at C0 if you do not have the means to get his C6

Who Is Razor?
A Boy Raised By Wolves
Having been abandoned by his parents as a child, Razor was found and brought up by the Wolf of the North Boreas. He was brought up as part of the pack and sees them as his family.
Awkward Due To Lack Of Human Contact
Prior to being found by Varka, the Grand Master of the Knights of Favonius, Razor had no interactions with other humans. He has since started to integrate with the citizens of Mondstadt with Lisa acting as his teacher. He still speaks in short sentences though.
Friends With Bennett
Because of his situation, Razor is not yet great with making friends. He does get along well with Bennett as they share the same tastes in food.

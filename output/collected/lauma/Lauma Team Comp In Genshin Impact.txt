Lauma Team Comp In Genshin Impact
Here are the best teams for Lauma in
Genshin Impact
:
Hyperbloom Lauma team:
Lauma + Nahida + Ineffa + Neuvillette
Nod-Krai team:
Lauma + Aino + Ineffa + Baizhu
Nilou Bloom team:
Lauma + Nahida + Nilou + Kokomi
F2P Lauma team:
Lauma + Xingqiu + Dendro MC + Kuki
Lauma + Nahida + Ineffa + Neuvillette
Since Lauma buffs any reaction related to Bloom in
Genshin Impact
, you can build a Hyperbloom team around her. For this, you need to bring a Hydro and Electro unit.
Considering Lauma doesn't need much field time, players can bring a Hydro DPS like
Neuvillette
or
Sangonomiya Kokomi
. If the Hydro is an off-field support like Aino, then players can drive the team with a Dendro user like
Nahida
or
Baizhu
.
Lauma and Nahida will
provide the off-field Dendro application
needed to create all the Blooms around Neuvillette's ​​​Charged Attacks. This is where Ineffa comes to play. Ineffa will trigger all the Lunar Blooms around enemies into Hyperbloom DMG while also triggering Lunar-Charged when her Electro reacts with Neuvillette's Hydro. This Lauma team is filled with characters that buff Lunar reactions and can smoothly trigger them on and off the field.
There are many variations to play this Lauma Hyperbloom team, like replacing Neuvillette with Aino for a full off-field Sub-DPS team.
Baizhu can replace Nahida
to add a bit of healing to the team, which ensures Lauma and her team can stay close when all the Dendro Cores explode.
Team Roles
Lauma:
Support, Buffer, Dendro Enabler
Nahida:
Support, Sub-DPS, Buffer, Debuffer, Dendro Enabler
Ineffa:
Support, Sub-DPS, Buffer, Shielder, Electro Enabler
Neuvillette:
Main DPS, Hydro Enabler
Lauma + Nahida + Nilou + Kokomi
Having a character like Lauma who can greatly buff Bloom's reactions is another reason to build a new
Nilou
team in
Genshin Impact
.
Nahida
and Lauma will provide insane off-field Dendro reactions, while Kokomi will be the main DPS who heals the team while providing all the Hydro enabling they need. Not to mention, Lauma adds a Crit effect to all the Bloom reactions, which further adds to their DMG. C2 Nahida can also add a Crit effect, and that will skyrocket the team's rank into a
top-tier team in
Genshin Impact
.

As an F2P-friendly character to build, you can also go full Lauma
F2P team in
Genshin Impact
. Add
Xingqiu
or
Aino
as off-field Hydro Enablers and Dendro MC as an off-field Dendro Enabler.

Kuki Shinobu

Hyperbloom characters in

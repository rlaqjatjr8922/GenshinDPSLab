Lauma's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Lauma is a
Dendro
support who works in almost every
Dendro-based team thanks to her multiple buffs for Dendro reactions across
different archetypes. In this guide, we will cover her best teams with premium
5-Star characters as well as strong 4-Star options.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Lauma
Nefer
Nefer
Nefer is the best
Dendro
DPS to pair with Lauma, as they synergize
      extremely well together. Lauma provides additional Lunar Bloom damage
      and powerful resistance shred, both of which are incredibly valuable
      for Nefer’s overall damage output. Since both Nefer and Lauma are
      Nod-Krai characters, they also unlock Ascendant Gleam, making their
      synergy even stronger.
Columbina
Columbina
Columbina is one of the best characters to pair with Lauma. She provides
      consistent
Hydro
application for Lunar Bloom
      and further buffs Lunar Bloom damage. Columbina can also function as an
      on-field DPS alongside Lauma, consuming Verdant Dew stacks through her
      Charged Attacks for additional damage.
Alhaitham
Kinich
Tighnari
Other Dendro DPS Options
Lauma, being one of the strongest Dendro supports in the game,
      synergizes well with many Dendro DPS characters.
Alhaitham
benefits greatly from Lauma’s Lunar Bloom
      access, Dendro RES shred, and additional Crit-related reaction bonuses.
Kinich
also gains strong value from Lauma, especially
      when she holds
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
and the
Deepwood Memories
set, allowing her to provide 25% Dendro RES
      shred and strong team buffs.
Tighnari
is another solid option who can pair with
      Lauma in Hyperbloom or Burgeon teams, benefiting from her off-field
      Dendro application and resistance shred.
Neuvillette
Kamisato Ayato
Nilou
Hydro DPS Options
Since Lauma can shred
Hydro
resistance by 25%,
      she pairs extremely well with Hydro DPS characters.
Nilou
can utilize Lauma as a Lunar Bloom support
      alongside other units like
Nahida
and
Baizhu
to create very strong Bloom-based teams.
Neuvillette
benefits from Lauma in Hyperbloom and
      Burgeon teams thanks to Hydro RES shred and consistent off-field Dendro
      application.
Kamisato Ayato
is another strong Hydro DPS who can make
      excellent use of Lauma’s off-field Dendro application in both
      Hyperbloom and Burgeon compositions.

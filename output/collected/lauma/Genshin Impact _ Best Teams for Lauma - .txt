Rating

▼ Premium Lunar-Bloom Support Lauma
★★★★★
- Lauma is considered
Nefer's
best teammate due to all her Lunar-Bloom related buffs that were fully intended to be maximized by Nefer.
- One of the higher DPS ceiling teams, especially when paired with
Columbina
.
▼ Hyperbloom with Lauma
★★★☆☆
- You can pair Lauma with Hydro and Electro teammates to create a Hyperbloom team.
- The On-Field character of this setup can range from a Hydro or Electro character, another Dendro character, or Lauma herself.
▼ Nilou Bloom with Lauma
★★★☆☆
-
Nilou
appreciates Lauma's Dendro RES shred and her ability to enable the special Bountiful Cores to CRIT, which slightly raises the DPS potential of the team.
- The team composition is rigid, as substitutes result in a noticeable downgrade.
▼Burning Support Lauma
★★☆☆☆
- Apart from Lunar-Bloom teams, Lauma can also perform decently well as a support for Burning teams who need consistent Dendro application, preferably Dendro DPSes.
Lauma is a character that greatly increases the damage of Bloom-related reactions and also produces a special bloom reaction called "Lunar-Bloom". While she finds the best success in dedicated Lunar-Bloom teams, you'll be able to get some mileage out of her in most Dendro-focused comps.

Nefer's premium Lunar-Bloom team has high DPS potential due to the inherent synergy between Lauma, Nefer, and Columbina. The core pairing of Nefer and Lauma is already quite strong on its own.
This team typically prefers the last slot to be another offensive, buff-providing support rather than a healer. This can make surviving difficult if you have not mastered Nefer's optimal combos.
Rotation Of Premium Lunar-Bloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Lauma. Use her Skill (hold), then Burst.
└
If this is the very first rotation, you can use Lauma's tap Skill.
2
Switch to Columbina. Use her Skill, followed by her Burst.
3
Swap to Sucrose, then use her Skill or Normal Attack (x1) into Burst.
5
Switch to Nefer to execute her Charged Attack combos.
└
Her ideal combo will be:
Skill -> [ Charged Attack -> Dash ] (x3) -> Skill -> [ Charged Attack -> Dash ] (x2) -> Charged Attack -> Burst or swap to Columbina to do Charged Attack (x1)
6
Swap back into Sucrose. Use her Skill.
7
Repeat from Step 1.

Rotation Of Hyperbloom Lauma Team
No.
Procedure
1

2
Switch to Furina. Use Skill, followed by her Burst. You can dash after using her Skill to cancel the long animation.

4

8

9
Do Charged Attack (x2) on Neuvillette again.
10
Repeat from step 1.

Emilie
- Any character with decent Off-Field application are going to be decent substitutes, though you'd be losing out on Nahida's Elemental Mastery buff.
- The purpose of the secondary Dendro is to hold

Barbara

Candace
- Kokomi is the primary Hydro applicator and should be built with full Elemental Mastery. This is because Nilou's Bountiful Bloom damage is calculated based on the stats of the character who triggers the reaction, which can be any team member.

Nilou
Kaveh
- Replacing Nilou is a significant decrease in DPS since she enables the Bountiful Blooms with her presence in the party. Without her, regular Bloom DMG is often underwhelming.
- Kaveh can be a viable option since he can quickly detonate Dendro Cores with his Skill, but the DPS will still be lower than what Nilou can achieve.
Nilou's Bountiful Bloom passive is enabled when the team consists of Dendro and Hydro characters only. This makes the Nilou-Lauma team restrictive in options since maintaining the passive is key for this team's DPS.

Lauma's Dendro application through her Skill can be enough to sustain the Burning reaction for Kinich teams. Her Dendro RES shred also sees use here. She essentially functions as a substitute for
Emilie
.
Rotation Of Burning Support Lauma Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Lauma. Use her Skill (tap), into Burst.
2
Switch to Durin. Use Skill, then Skill again to enter the Confirmation of Purity state. Use Burst.
3
Switch to Iansan and use her Skill. Do Normal Attack (x1) before using her Burst.
4
Swap to Kinich to do his optimal damage combo. You can briefly hold, aim, then release his Skill to cancel the animation, which can result in more Cannon shots overall. Use his Elemental Burst to refresh his Nightsoul points for another Cannon shot.
5
Repeat from step 1 after you're done with Kinich's combo.

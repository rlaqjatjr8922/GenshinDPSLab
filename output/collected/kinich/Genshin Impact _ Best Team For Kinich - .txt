Recommended Teams For Kinich
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Burning Team
★★★★★
- Strongest team for Kinich.
- Uses Burning to boost the damage of Kinich and Emilie.
- Bennett for ATK buff and healing.
- Xiangling for off-field Pyro.
- With Burning, you can easily shoot 3~4 cannons per Elemental Skill.
▼ Burgeon-Vaporize Team
★★★★・
- Uses Kinich and Furina with Burning to produce constant Burgeon and Vaporize.
- Because Burning is present, Kinich will also have powerful damage.
- Burgeon helps with Kinich's lack of good AoE but is less consistent compared to Burning.
▼ Over-Burn Team
★★★・・
- Uses an Off-field Electro with Burning reaction to produce overload while maintaining Burning aura.
- Flexible team options but you may have an issue with crowd control since light enemies gets blasted off with overload.
- Has a better AoE than other teams.
▼ Burn-Melt Team
★★★・・
- Uses an Off-field Cryo to deal powerful Melt DMG.
- There are few powerful Cryo characters that can serve as an off-field Melt in this team.
Teams of Kinich is generally composed of Kinich + an off-field Pyro and flexible members. So long as Burning or Burgeon reaction is active, it will produce enough Nightsoul for Kinich to continuously shoot his Skill DMG.

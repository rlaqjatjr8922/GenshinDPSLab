Genshin Impact: Best Team Compositions For Kinich

Kinich, the second limited five-star character from Natlan in
Genshin Impact
, stands out not only for his rarity but also for his exceptional abilities, particularly his Elemental Skill, which ranks as one of the best for mobility in the game’s vast open world.
As a Dendro Claymore user and a powerful on-field DPS, Kinich excels when integrated into team compositions that leverage the Burning or Burgeon reactions, essential for maximizing his damage output. Crafting the perfect team around Kinich involves understanding the synergy between his abilities and those of other characters.
In this guide, we’ll delve into the top team setups for Kinich, ensuring you can harness his full potential and dominate your
Genshin Impact
battles. Let’s explore the most effective combinations to make Kinich a formidable leader in your lineup.
Kinich's Best Overall Team: Kinich And Emilie
Kinich shines as a main DPS in
Genshin Impact
, particularly when paired with Emilie, a stellar off-field damage dealer who thrives alongside Kinich’s Dendro abilities to trigger potent Burning
reactions
. This duo forms the core of a team designed to efficiently dismantle formidable adversaries through their synergistic damage output.
Ideal Team Composition:
•
Kinich
-
Role:
Main DPS (On-field)
•	Kinich serves as the primary damage dealer, leveraging his Dendro Claymore to initiate and sustain
combat
engagements with powerful on-field presence.
•
Emilie
-
Role:
DPS (Off-field)
•	As Kinich’s tactical counterpart, Emilie excels in amplifying the team’s damage from the sidelines, particularly enhancing the Burning effect triggered by Kinich.
•
Xiangling/Dehya
-
Role:
DPS (Off-field)
•	Xiangling can significantly contribute to off-field damage and, along with Bennett, facilitates the Burning reaction, essential for maximizing DPS. Alternatively, Dehya offers not only damage but also utility benefits like resistance to interruption, making her a versatile choice depending on the encounter’s demands.
•
Bennett
-
Role:
Support (Attack Buffer and Healer)
•	Bennett is pivotal for boosting the team’s attack through his Elemental Skill and providing healing via his Elemental Burst, which also enhances the abilities of Xiangling or Dehya when used in tandem.
Strategic Play:
1.
Bennett:
Initiate with Bennett’s Elemental Skill to buff the team, followed by his Elemental Burst for sustained attack enhancement and healing.
2.
Xiangling/Dehya:
Quickly switch to Xiangling to deploy her abilities within the range of Bennett’s Elemental Burst to maximize damage output. If using Dehya, leverage her skills for additional utility and damage.
3.
Emilie:
Activate Emilie’s Elemental Burst to escalate the off-field damage, capitalizing on the
reactions
set up by the rest of the team.
4.
Kinich:
With the stage set by his teammates, Kinich can dominate the battlefield, exploiting the full potential of his DPS capabilities.
This team setup not only harnesses each character’s strengths but also ensures a dynamic and fluid combat strategy, allowing Kinich to excel as a formidable on-field DPS in
Genshin Impact
.
Kinich’s Four-Star Team Composition
Kinich’s versatility and potent raw damage make him an excellent choice for players looking for a free-to-play-friendly main DPS. A well-rounded team consisting of Bennett, Xiangling, Collei, and Kinich can smoothly carry you through the endgame content of
Genshin Impact
.
Team Roles:
•
Kinich:
Main DPS (On-field) - Kinich dominates the battlefield with his robust Dendro attacks, staying on-field to continuously dish out damage.
•
Collei:
Support DPS (Off-field) - Collei aids the team by setting up Dendro reactions with her skills, enhancing the overall damage output.
•
Xiangling:
Support DPS (Off-field) - Xiangling complements Dendro with her Pyro skills to trigger powerful Burning
reactions
, boosting the team’s damage significantly.
•
Bennett:
Support (Attack Buffer and Healer) - Bennett is crucial for his dual role, providing both healing and attack boosts with his Elemental Burst, which amplifies the team’s overall effectiveness.
Alternative Option:
•
Thoma
can replace Xiangling if additional defense is needed. Thoma offers off-field damage and creates protective shields, though this switch may slightly reduce the team’s overall damage output.
Strategic Gameplay:
1.
Collei:
Initiate
combat
by deploying Collei’s Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst to set the stage with Dendro energy.
2.
Bennett:
Follow up quickly with Bennett’s Elemental Skill for an immediate attack boost, then activate his Elemental Burst for sustained
heal
ing and enhanced attack power.
3.
Xiangling/Thoma:
Use Xiangling’s Elemental Skill and Burst to intensify the field with Pyro, maximizing reaction damage. If using Thoma, deploy his skills strategically to balance offense with defense.
4.
Kinich:
With the groundwork laid by his teammates, Kinich can capitalize on the setup, employing his potent on-field attacks to control and conquer the battlefield.
This team structure not only maximizes Kinich’s DPS capabilities but also ensures a balanced approach to combat, allowing for both aggressive offense and stable defense in
Genshin Impact
’s challenging scenarios.
Kinich Burgeon Team Composition
Kinich’s adaptability shines in various team setups, including the Burgeon team composition. Burgeon is a Dendro reaction that occurs when Bloom cores, created by Dendro and Hydro
reactions
, interact with Pyro. This reaction type is particularly powerful for its area-of-effect damage, making it ideal for handling groups of enemies efficiently.
Team Roles:
•
Kinich:
Main DPS (On-field) - Kinich leads the charge, consistently applying Dendro to trigger reactions.
•
Yelan:
Support DPS (Off-field) - Yelan provides Hydro application from off-field, essential for forming the initial Bloom cores.
•
Thoma:
Support DPS (Off-field) - Thoma adds Pyro into the mix, crucial for triggering the Burgeon reaction with his off-field abilities.
•
Baizhu:
Support (Shielder and
Heal
er) - Baizhu offers both
heal
ing and shielding, ensuring the team’s sustainability while enhancing reaction damage with his Dendro application.
Investment and Artifacts:
The team is relatively low-maintenance in terms of investment. Characters other than Kinich mainly need Elemental Mastery on their artifacts to maximize the reaction damage, simplifying the gearing process.
Flexible Gameplay Strategy:
1.
Baizhu:
Start by deploying Baizhu’s Elemental Burst for healing and his Elemental Skill for Dendro application, setting up for reactions.
2.
Yelan:
Quickly switch to Yelan to use her Elemental Skill and Burst, ensuring Hydro is present to create Bloom cores.
3.
Thoma:
Follow with Thoma’s Elemental Burst and Skill to introduce Pyro, which will interact with the Bloom cores to trigger the Burgeon reaction.
4.
Kinich:
With the stage set, Kinich takes over as the primary damage dealer, exploiting the chaotic environment created by the continuous reactions.
This rotation strategy doesn’t adhere to a strict sequence but focuses on maintaining a flow where each character contributes their elemental abilities as needed. The key is to keep the elements cycling through Dendro, Hydro, and Pyro to sustain the reactions, with Kinich capitalizing on the resultant damage to control and decimate enemy ranks. This Burgeon team not only leverages Kinich’s strengths but also creates a dynamic and explosive battlefield conducive to his aggressive playstyle.
Kinich Hyper Carry
For those aiming to harness the full potential of Kinich’s Elemental Skill in dealing damage, a team composition centered around enhancing his capabilities makes him an unparalleled hyper carry. Pairing Kinich with Furina, Siegwinne, and Bennett creates a powerhouse team that not only boosts Kinich’s damage output but also ensures sustained healing and support.
Team Roles and Contributions:
•
Kinich:
Main DPS (On-field) - Kinich remains at the forefront, utilizing his potent Dendro abilities to consistently deal damage.
•
Furina:
Support DPS (Off-field), Damage Buffer, and
Heal
er - Furina excels in boosting Kinich’s damage while providing healing, which is crucial for maintaining durability in prolonged encounters.
•
Siegwinne:
Damage Buffer and
Heal
er - Siegwinne offers additional buffs and healing, reinforcing Kinich’s role as the primary attacker.
•
Bennett:
Damage Buffer and Healer - Bennett is renowned for his ability to significantly enhance damage output and heal, making him an essential part of any hyper carry setup.
Dynamic Combat Strategy:
1.
Start with Bennett:
Activate Bennett’s Elemental Burst to lay down an area that boosts attack and provides healing. Follow up with his Elemental Skill to maximize the damage enhancement.
2.
Switch to Furina:
Quickly transition to Furina, utilizing her Elemental Skill and Burst to further amplify damage and ensure Kinich is continuously supported with buffs and
heal
ing.
3.
Incorporate Siegwinne:
Use Siegwinne’s Elemental Skill and Burst to add another layer of damage enhancement and healing, fortifying Kinich’s presence on the battlefield.
4.
Unleash Kinich:
With the buffs and healing in full swing, deploy Kinich’s Elemental Burst to unleash his full damage potential, capitalizing on the accumulated boosts from his teammates.
5.
Adapt as Needed:
Depending on the flow of battle, continue to cycle through the skills of Furina, Siegwinne, and Bennett, keeping their buffs active and adjusting your strategy to the demands of the combat situation.
This team setup for Kinich not only maximizes his damage output but also ensures he is well-supported with continuous
heal
ing and buffs, allowing him to excel as a hyper carry in challenging scenarios. The synergy between these characters leverages the best aspects of each, making Kinich an unstoppable force in any encounter.
Conclusion
Kinich’s versatility in Genshin Impact allows for a range of dynamic team compositions that can maximize his effectiveness as a main DPS. Whether you’re harnessing the explosive potential of Burgeon reactions, focusing on enhancing his raw damage output in a hyper carry setup, or utilizing his mobility for strategic play, there’s a composition to suit every playstyle. By strategically pairing Kinich with characters like Emilie, Yelan, and Bennett, and tailoring your approach to incorporate essential reactions and buffs, you can unlock the full potential of this powerful Dendro Claymore wielder. Each recommended team setup is designed to enhance Kinich’s
unique abilities
, ensuring that players can enjoy both high performance and engaging gameplay experiences.

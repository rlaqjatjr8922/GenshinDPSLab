Kinich is the newest and shiniest 5-star to join the Genshin Impact roster in version 5.0.
He is a 5-star Dendro DPS character who uses a Claymore and swings around the battlefield to shoot down his enemies, be they flying or grounded. Kinich is a powerful DPS who relies a lot on the Burning reaction to be present, so now is a good time to dust off those Pyro units.

Kinich Best Weapons

[Fang of the Mountain King]

Additionally, the weapon’s effect also increases Kinich’s Skill and Ult dmg by up to 60%, making it ideal for him. That being said, Kinich also has a great F2P alternative in the new craftable
[Earth Shaker]
. This weapon gives him a good boost to ATK while also increasing his skill DMG when used with Pyro, so Kinich can benefit from it fully.

Kinich Best Artifacts
Artifacts might be a bit tricky when it comes to Kinich, as his best-in-slot Artifact set is the newly added [Obsidian Codex]. It is the best Artifact set for any Natlan DPS, and that is true for Kinich as well. However, due to being quite new, you might not yet have all the pieces needed to whip up a full 4-piece set from it. That’s where the Unfinished Reverie and Marechaussee Hunter come in. Although not ideal, they can serve as a good replacement for the Obsidian Codex until you manage to get enough pieces.

Skill Priority
Kinich’s skill priority is quite simple. Almost all of his damage comes from his Elemental Skill, so that is the one you should max out first, followed by his Elemental Burst. His Normal Attack is completely useless, so you can either fully disregard it or level it if you want to have your Kinich fully maxed out.
Skill Priority
Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst > Normal Attack

Best Artifacts for Kinich
Obsidian Codex
Obsidian Codex
2-Piece Set
: While the equipping character is in Nightsoul's Blessing and is on the field, their DMG dealt is increased by 15%.
4-Piece Set
: After the equipping character consumes 1 Nightsoul point while on the field, CRIT Rate increases by 40% for 6s. This effect can trigger once every second.
Obsidian Codex is currently Kinich’s best-in-slot artifact set and is practically
designed around his kit. Since Kinich frequently enters Nightsoul’s Blessing,
he can consistently activate the 40% CRIT Rate bonus, making this set by far
his strongest and most consistent option for maximizing personal damage.

How to Play Kinich
Kinich is a Nightsoul-based character whose entire kit revolves around
generating Nightsoul points and consuming them to deal
Dendro
damage.
When you press his Elemental Skill, he enters the Nightsoul’s Blessing
state. During this state, triggering Burning generates Nightsoul points
for Kinich.
Once you accumulate enough Nightsoul points, his Skill will begin to glow,
allowing you to cast an enhanced version that deals significantly higher
damage. While in Nightsoul’s Blessing, a Blind Spot appears near the
opponent. When Kinich moves into this Blind Spot, it disappears and
grants him 4 additional Nightsoul points, helping him reach his
enhanced Skill faster.
His rotation is very simple and intuitive. Press Elemental Skill to
enter Nightsoul’s Blessing, move toward the Blind Spot whenever it
appears to gain extra Nightsoul points, and use the enhanced Skill
whenever it becomes available. Repeat this loop while maintaining
Burning uptime for maximum consistency and damage.

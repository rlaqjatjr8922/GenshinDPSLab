Varesa is a 5-star Electro unit and one of the two new characters that were added to Genshin Impact during the Version 5.5 update. She is a strong DPS unit that relies on Plunge Attacks to deal the majority of her damage and can be used in a ton of teams that bring out the best in her. In this guide, we’ll go over some of Varesa’s best teams so that you can start using her as soon as you get her.

Varesa Best Teams
As we’ve already touched on this, Varesa is a unit that primarily deals Plunge Attack DMG. This means that she does great with units that boost said damage, such as, for example, Xianyun. However, as of Version 5.5, Varesa’s best teammate actually happens to be the new 4-star Electro unit, Iansan. Iansan works extremely well with Varesa, and they are clearly made to be used in conjunction; as such, you will be seeing Iansan in all of Varesa’s teams.

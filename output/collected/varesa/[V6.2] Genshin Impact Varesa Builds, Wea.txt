Varesa Gameplay
Varesa
is a
limited 5-star Electro Catalyst
user who specializes in dealing damage through
Plunging Attacks
. When she reaches the maximum Nightsoul points, she enters the
Fiery Passion state
, enhancing her damage output. Upon casting her Elemental Burst, Varesa's Nightsoul points are fully restored, and she immediately enters the Fiery Passion state. During this time, her interruption resistance is increased, allowing her to unleash especially powerful Plunging Attacks that deal massive damage to enemies.

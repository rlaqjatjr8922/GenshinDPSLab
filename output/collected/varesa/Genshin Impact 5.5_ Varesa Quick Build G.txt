Varesa’s Talents
Normal Attack
:
By the Horns
Varesa unleashes 3 strikes that deal Electro DMG.
Charged Attack
Consumes stamina to headbutt opponents and deals Nightsoul-aligned Electro DMG, with momentum allowing her to leap into the air.
Plunge Attack
Deals AoE Electro DMG as she plunges to the ground. Striking opponents grants her Nightsoul points, helping her enter the Fiery Passion state when she reaches max points.
Elemental Skill
:
Riding the Night-Rainbow
Varesa rushes forward and deals Electro DMG, entering the Nightsoul’s Blessing state. She gains 20 Nightsoul points and can trigger a Follow-Up Strike effect, boosting her attack speed and adding convenience to her Plunging Attacks.
Elemental Burst
:
Guardian Vent!
A powerful flying kick that deals AoE Electro DMG, followed by the restoration of Nightsoul points. Increases her interruption resistance and allows her to perform enhanced Plunge Attacks in the Fiery Passion state.
Passive Talents
Tag-Team Triple Jump!
: Boosts ground impact DMG from Plunge Attacks.
The Hero Twice-Returned!
: Provides an ATK buff when a Nightsoul Burst is triggered.
Night Realm’s Gift
: Allows Varesa to interact with Phlogiston and enter Sudden Onrush mode, useful for terrain traversal.
Blazing Heart, Singular Advance!
: Boosts sprint speed and restores Phlogiston when consuming food in specific areas.
So, as you can see, utilizing her elemental skill to build up her secondary burst is where her main source of damage lies. You should focus on building that up while gradually going to your actual elemental burst.
Varesa Best Builds
Electro Plunge CRIT DPS
Focus:
Your Varesa Build should prioritize CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, and ATK for maximum damage output. Optionally, consider Elemental Mastery and Energy Recharge for synergy with Electro Hypercarry, Dendro team,s and Burst usage.
Playstyle:
As a Plunge DPS, her damage comes mainly from her Plunge Attacks, which scale significantly with her stats.
Best Weapons
Vivid Notions
– The ideal weapon for Varesa, as it likely synergizes with her Plunge DPS playstyle.
Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds
– A solid alternative for CRIT Rate and Elemental DMG.
The Widsith
– Offers CRIT DMG and a random buff, making it a versatile choice.
Other Options:
Kagura’s Verity, Cashflow Supervision, Skyward Atlas.
Best Artifacts
Long Night’s Oath (4-piece)
– The best set for boosting Plunge Attack DMG. The set increases damage by up to 100% when five stacks of “Radiance Everlasting” are triggered.
Obsidian Codex (4-piece)
– A good alternative if you focus more on boosting CRIT DMG or ATK rather than CRIT Rate.
Artifact Main Stats:
ATK% (Sands), Electro DMG Bonus (Goblet), CRIT Rate/CRIT DMG (Circlet).
Artifact Substats:
Prioritize CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Energy Recharge, and Elemental Mastery.

Key Playstyle Notes
Fiery Passion State:
Entered via Plunging Attacks or her Elemental Burst, boosting all of Varesa’s attack types (Normal, Charged, and Plunge). Her Elemental Burst also enables an enhanced Plunging Attack.
Mobility and Interrupt Resistance:
Her Elemental Skill boosts mobility and reduces interruption, which is crucial for her on-field DPS role.

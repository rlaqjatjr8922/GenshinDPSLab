Nilou's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Nilou brings a more powerful version of Dendro Cores that explode faster in a bigger radius.
Amazing for AoE Hydro application in combat.
Incredibly tanky often having 60-70k HP when built.
Weaknesses
Bountiful Cores can not be used in Hyperbloom or Burgeon reactions.
Limited team building primarily focusing on Bloom.
Requires a Dendro or Hydro healer to recover from Bloom self damage.

Nilou's best weapons in this build are
Key of Khaj-Nisut
,
The Dockhand's Assistant
and
Serenity's Call
. These weapon options for Nilou are significantly 
better than any other option such as
Iron Sting
,
Favonius Sword
etc 
due to the listed weapons being the only HP-Scaling options.
With regards to artifacts, build Nilou with 2-Piece HP% artifacts as your main 
goal is to just increase Nilou's HP to as high as possible.
Energy Recharge requirements entirely depend on whether you
plan to use your Elemental Burst,
Dance of Abzendegi: Distant Dreams, Listening Spring
every rotation or
every other. Elemental Mastery can be a nice added bonus in teams where 
Nilou is contributing towards some of the cores themselves but it can be tricky 
to tell how much of a contribution your Nilou is doing towards the actual bloom 
damage.
Nilou's talent levels do not require heavy investing due to the fact
that they do not increase Nilou's buff to your parties Bloom damage.

Nilou is incredibly easy to build, only really requiring you build HP up to 74,444 which caps
her 4th ascension,
Dreamy Dance of Aeons
. Just stack as much as you can and enough ER 
and you're good for Nilou's build.
3.
Combos and How to Play Nilou
Nilou's Main mechanic centres around the use of her Elemental Skill,
Dance of Haftkarsvar
. You want to use
Dance of Abzendegi: Distant Dreams, Listening Spring
every other
character rotation but this can vary depending on your
Energy Recharge and team compositions.
3.1.
Dance of Haftkarsvar
Nilou's
Dance of Haftkarsvar
has 2 separate forms,
Sword
Dance Form
and
Whirling Steps Form
. Ending Nilou's Elemental Skill
with a Normal Attack will activate Sword Dance Form, while ending with an
Elemental Skill will activate Whirling Steps Form.
Use Whirling Steps to create more
Bloom reactions
, for example in parties
with 2
Hydro
characters. Use Sword Dance when they want to minimize Nilou's
personal Bloom ownership, such as in parties with 3
Hydro
characters.
4.
Nilou's Best Team Compositions
Nilou excels in Bloom compositions due to her
niche kit, making her incredibly powerful in that niche but less powerful
outside of it. Due to Nilou's Chalice's Bounty, created by
Court of Dancing Petals
, the entire party should consist of only
Dendro
and
Hydro
characters. This means you
can either run 2 Hydro and 2 Dendro characters or 3 Hydro and 1 Dendro
characters. 1 Hydro would not be enough Elemental Application.

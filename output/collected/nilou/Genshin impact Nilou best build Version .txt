Best builds for Nilou
1.
Sub-DPS Build for Off-Field
This Nilou build is crafted to highlight her optimal role as a Sub-DPS, with a focus on generating Bountiful Cores. To maximize her potential, prioritize HP% as the main stat and equip her with a combination of two pieces from the Tenacity of the Millelith set and two pieces from the Vourukasha's Glow set, which synergizes to enhance the damage output of her Bountiful Cores.
2.
Main DPS Build for On-Field
Nilou can be deployed as a frontline combatant capable of generating Bountiful Cores through her Hydro-infused Sword Dance. The Sacrificial Sword is an excellent choice for her, as it allows for the utilization of both forms of her Elemental Skill, thereby optimizing her Hydro coverage on foes.
While Nilou can benefit from the full 4-piece Vourukasha's Glow set bonus due to the self-damage inflicted by her Bountiful Cores, you may opt for the more accessible 2-piece HP%/HP% artifact combination to streamline your farming process and reduce the resin expenditure.

Nilou excels when equipped with weapons that have HP% Bonus Stats, as these significantly enhance the damage of her Bountiful Cores. This makes HP-based weapons the optimal choice for her, ensuring that her unique combat mechanic is as effective as possible in battle.
While HP% weapons are ideal, those with Elemental Mastery (EM) or Energy Recharge (ER) as a secondary stat are also valuable for Nilou. Elemental Mastery weapons are particularly beneficial in scenarios where her Bountiful Cores trigger elemental reactions, boosting the overall damage output. Energy Recharge weapons, on the other hand, are advantageous if you need Nilou to utilize her Elemental Burst more frequently, reducing the reliance on cooldowns.
Among the free-to-play (F2P) craftable weapons, the Iron Sting stands out as an excellent option for Nilou. Its passive ability and Elemental Mastery substat synergize well with her playstyle, amplifying her damage across the board, especially when she successfully triggers a Bountiful Core reaction.
The Sapwood Blade is another weapon to consider, particularly for its supportive capabilities and Energy Recharge substat. It's a viable choice if you're looking to bolster Nilou's utility in a team composition that requires more frequent use of her abilities. However, it's important to note that, like Elemental Mastery, the Sapwood Blade's passive ability is most effective when Nilou is able to trigger a Bloom reaction, which may limit its consistent performance in all combat situations.
Best team comps for Nilou
1.
Double Hydro Team
A team composition that features two Hydro and two Dendro characters can potentially generate a higher number of Bountiful Cores than a team with three Hydro characters. However, this diversity in elemental representation can lead to complications in managing the ownership of the Bountiful Cores. As a result, it becomes essential for each team member to allocate some resources to boosting their Elemental Mastery. This investment will ensure that the team can effectively capitalize on the increased Bountiful Core generation, maintaining control and strategic advantage in combat scenarios.
2.
Nilou Triple Hydro Team
Nilou thrives in a team setup that consists of three Hydro elements complemented by a single Dendro damage dealer. This configuration ensures that the generation of Bountiful Cores is attributed solely to the Dendro character, simplifying the process of enhancing their impact. By focusing on equipping the Dendro unit with a full set of Elemental Mastery (EM) Artifacts, the team can effortlessly amplify the damage potential of the Bountiful Cores. This streamlined approach allows for a more targeted and efficient build, maximizing the elemental synergy and leveraging Nilou's abilities to their fullest extent.
3.
Nilou On-Field
When Nilou assumes the role of Main DPS, she becomes the central figure in applying Hydro to enemies. In this scenario, it is most effective for Nilou to wield the Sacrificial Sword while on the field, allowing her to utilize both of her Dance Forms to consistently apply Hydro to opponents.
Upon the conclusion of her Sword Dance form's duration, the team can transition to Barbara, who can continue the Hydro application. This switch maintains the elemental advantage and ensures that enemies remain vulnerable to Hydro-related reactions, thereby supporting the team's overall damage output and strategic positioning.
Summary
In conclusion, Nilou emerges as a formidable character in the realm of Genshin Impact, with a unique set of strengths that can significantly bolster a team's combat capabilities. Her ability to transform Dendro Cores into the more potent Bountiful Cores is a game-changer, providing a strategic edge in battles against multiple foes. Furthermore, her second constellation bestows powerful debuffs upon enemies, enhancing her team's overall damage potential.
However, Nilou's effectiveness is contingent upon a specific team composition, primarily consisting of Hydro and Dendro elements, to fully harness her talents. This requirement can limit flexibility in team building and strategy. Additionally, her reliance on a healer is crucial to counteract the friendly fire damage caused by Dendro Core explosions, ensuring that her team remains in fighting shape throughout the engagement.
Despite these considerations, Nilou's strengths shine through, making her a valuable asset for players seeking to optimize their elemental strategies and maximize damage output. With careful team composition and strategic play, Nilou can be a key component in overcoming even the most challenging of adversaries in Genshin Impact.

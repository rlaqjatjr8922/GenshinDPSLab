Lyney's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Lyney team composition guide and character synergy 
overview for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams 
for Lyney, alongside recommended character pairings that help 
maximize his Charged Attack damage.
Lyney's kit focuses mainly on Mono-Pyro teams, leveraging his unique passive 
that buffs damage based on the number of
Pyro
characters 
in the party. However, thanks to the sheer strength of Vaporize and Melt reactions, 
Lyney also has incredibly competitive teams within those archetypes.

Pyro Sub-DPS & Utility
Since Lyney's main kit is designed for Mono-Pyro, adding Pyro sub-DPS or buffer 
      characters is the best way to increase overall team damage.
Mavuika
and
Durin
are premium off-field supports that provide both buffs 
      and excellent off-field damage. Defensive options like
Dehya
and
Thoma
can also be used to provide much-needed interruption resistance 
      while aiming Charged Attacks.
Furina
Vaporize Supports
Furina
is one of the very few Hydro supports who works perfectly 
      with Lyney. Because Lyney is a Charged Attack damage dealer, he does not trigger the 
      coordinated attacks of characters like
Yelan
or
Xingqiu
. Furina not only applies the necessary off-field Hydro 
      for Vaporize, but also provides a massive DMG bonus buff from her Burst to push 
      his damage even higher.

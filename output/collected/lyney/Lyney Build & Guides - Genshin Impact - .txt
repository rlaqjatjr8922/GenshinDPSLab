Best Artifacts for Lyney
Marechaussee Hunter
Marechaussee Hunter
2-Piece Set
: Normal and Charged Attack DMG +15%
4-Piece Set
: When current HP increases or decreases, CRIT Rate will be increased by 12% for 5s. Max 3 stacks.
Marechaussee Hunter is by far Lyney's best artifact set. Because his Charged Attacks 
inherently consume his HP and he heals himself using his Elemental Skill, he easily 
maintains the 3-stack buff. This provides a massive 36% CRIT Rate, allowing you to focus 
heavily on CRIT DMG and ATK % on your artifact pieces.
Lavawalker
Lavawalker
2-Piece Set
: Pyro RES increased by 40%.
4-Piece Set
: Increases DMG against opponents affected by Pyro by 35%.
Lavawalker is a viable alternative if you are playing Lyney in his standard Mono-Pyro 
team composition. Since enemies will constantly be affected by
Pyro
, 
Lyney will maintain 100% uptime on the 35% DMG bonus provided by the 4-piece set.

Lyney's Normal Attack talent is by far the most important to level, as it scales the 
damage of his Prop Arrows and Grin-Malkin Hats. His Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst 
deal solid damage and are roughly similar in value, so level them equally after maxing 
his Normal Attacks.
How to Play Lyney
Lyney's gameplay is centered heavily around his Charged Attacks. You want to hold his 
attack to the second charge level to fire a "Prop Arrow". This will summon a
Grin-Malkin Hat
on the field and consume a portion of Lyney's HP (if he 
is above 60% HP), perfectly stacking his
Marechaussee Hunter
set.
The Grin-Malkin Hat is incredibly useful, as it taunts nearby enemies to keep their 
attention away from you, making it much easier to safely charge your shots! When destroyed 
or when its duration expires, the hat deals AoE
Pyro
DMG. This 
can even occur when Lyney is off-field.
His Elemental Skill,
Bewildering Lights
, deals front-loaded AoE 
Pyro damage based on how many "Prop Surplus" stacks he generated from consuming HP. It 
also detonates any active Grin-Malkin Hats and heals Lyney based on the stacks consumed.
Finally, Lyney's Elemental Burst,
Wondrous Trick: Miracle Parade
, turns 
him into a Grin-Malkin Cat that moves rapidly around the field, dealing Pyro DMG to 
enemies he passes through. You can end the state early by pressing the Elemental Skill 
button, which will deal a final instance of AoE Pyro DMG and summon another Grin-Malkin Hat.
Lyney's Best Constellations
Lyney's constellations offer strong damage increases, with C2 being an incredible 
stopping point for low spenders.
Investment Priority:
Signature Weapon > Constellation 1 > Constellation 2
Whimsical Wonders
(C1) allows Lyney to have two Grin-Malkin Hats 
on the field simultaneously. Additionally, his Prop Arrows will summon two hats instead 
of one and grant an extra Prop Surplus stack, significantly speeding up his rotation and burst potential.
Loquacious Cajoling
(C2) is arguably his best early constellation. 
It provides a massive, unconditional CRIT DMG increase (up to 60%) simply for keeping 
Lyney on the field for 6 seconds, heavily rewarding his standard Main DPS playstyle.

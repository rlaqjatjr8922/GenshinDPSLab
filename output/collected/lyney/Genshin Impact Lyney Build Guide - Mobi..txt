Genshin Impact Lyney Constellations
The constellations of
Lyney
are extraordinary, as they provide a lot of additional damage to his overall kit. First of all, let us take a look at his first constellation,
Whimsical Wonders
. This constellation is less remarkable than his other constellations. However, it can still be essential when dealing with many enemies, as two Grin-Malkin Hats can taunt many enemies.
His second constellation,
Loquacious Cajoling
, adds to his overall damage, providing a certain amount of Critical Damage as long as Lyney is on the field. And as a main DPS, its max stacks are easily achievable. Moving on to his third Constellation,
Prestidigitation
, it also adds to his overall damage as it increases the level of his normal attacks and gives him a chance to level it beyond level 10.
His normal attack level increase is significant because most of Lyney’s damage comes from doing a lot of Normal attacks. His fourth Constellation decreases enemies’ resistance against Pyro when hit by Lyney’s charged attacks, which further increases his Pyro damage to the enemies. Lastly, his
C6
further increases his overall damage by having additional damage when Lyney fires off his charged attack.
Genshin Impact Lyney Build Guide: The Best Equipment
To know the
best build for Lyney
, we need to analyze the essential stats and talents to consider when putting on his Artifact. So, as a main Pyro DPS, Lyney needs a
build
that compliments well on his kit.
In this section of our
Genshin Impact Lyney guide
, we gathered some of the
best Artifacts
and
weapons
to build Lyney to maximize his abilities.
The Best Artifacts for Lyney
By far, this brand-new
artifact set
is the best for Lyney. The
Marechaussee Hunter
boosts his Charged attack, but more importantly, it provides a stackable Critical Rate bonus, which is an essential stat for a DPS character.
The 4-piece
Lavawalker artifact set
is one of the good alternatives in case you haven’t gotten the new artifact set. When an enemy is under the influence of
Pyro
, the Lavawalker set increases
Lyney's Pyro damage
, which complements Lyney's abilities.
The
Retracing Bolide
does not only provide a strong shield for Lyney; it also boosts Lyney’s charged attacks. Although it is not as beneficial as the Marechaussee Hunter set, it is a good alternative if you haven’t gotten a good
artifact
stat yet. Also, when building
Retracing Bolide
, you must have a support character that provides a shield to use its 4-piece talent.

The Best Weapons for Lyney
In our
Genshin Impact Lyney guide
, we’ll also tackle his best weapons to figure out what to build for Lyney. Here are the
5-star weapon
options
that we recommend using for Lyney:
The First Great Magic
is his signature and overall
best-in-slot weapon
for Lyney. You’ll get a significant stacking attack percentage bonus in a Pyro team. Also, this weapon has a substat of
Critical damage
, which is also why it is his
best weapon overall
.
Aqua Simulacra
is also great for Lyney if you haven’t gotten his signature weapon. It has a high
Critical Damage substat
and a damage bonus whenever an opponent is nearby.
Prototype Crescent
is situationally his
best weapon
, as you need to hit an enemy's weak point. An enemy's weak point also depends on its type; sometimes, it might have or haven’t.
Song of Stillness
is his
second-best F2P weapon
because of its attack percentage substat. Also, note that you need to get healed to get the benefits of the weapon.

How to play Lyney in Genshin ?
The
character
Lyney is a main DPS, relying on charged attacks while scaling on Attack to deal huge Pyro damage.
Utilizing Lyney's talent passive,
Prop Surplus
, is essential, as the more Prop Surplus stacks you have, the greater the damage of the Elemental Skill when you use it. We can quickly achieve this by firing multiple charged attacks, regardless of whether or not they hit an enemy. Also, Lyney will decrease his HP after firing a charged attack and will not gain an additional Prop Surplus stack if his current HP% is less than 60%. So, having him healed when doing charged attacks is necessary to achieve maximum stacks.
Before reaching the maximum stacks of
Prop Surplus
, Lyney must use his Elemental Burst,
Wondrous Trick: Miracle Parade
, as it not only deals a decent amount of Pyro damage but also adds one stack of Prop Surplus. Lastly, after reaching the Prop Surplus’ maximum stacks, we can now cast his Elemental Skill at total damage, decimating enemies left alive.
On the other hand, if you’re aiming for a melt team comp with Lyney, few Cryo support characters fit Lyney’s playstyle, which includes a
5-star Cryo bow character
, Ganyu, and a 4-star Cryo bow Support, Diona.
That said, optimal rotation and better energy generation are necessary to deal Vaporize and Melt damage consistently, primarily if your support character relies on their hydro or cryo application in their burst.
Guide of the best teams for Lyney in Genshin Impact
As we’ve said earlier,
Lyney
is best paired with characters that synergize well in a mono Pyro team. Additionally, alternative Lyney teams play primarily with Vaporize and Melt.
For a
Mono Pyro team
,
Kazuha
is a must because of his debuffing capabilities and providing Elemental Damage Bonus.
Xiangling
for more Pyro damage and application, while also shredding even more Pyro resistance when an enemy comes in contact with gouba.
Bennett
is there for more attack bonuses and healing capabilities, which Lyney needs to accumulate stacks.
In this alternate team composition,
Lyney
is focused on doing
melt damage
with
Diona
’s Elemental burst. In this situation, Diona must receive a good amount of energy recharge to maximize her Elemental burst.
Kazuha
and
Bennett
are also on this team for support capabilities.
How to Get Lyney in Genshin Impact?
If you’re wondering
how to unlock
Lyney
, you're in luck. The character Lyney makes his grand entrance in Genshin Impact's 4.0 update, and he's quite the catch. To obtain this
5-star character
, you'll want to keep an eye on the
Character Event Banners
. These banners shine a spotlight on a 5-star character for roughly 20 days. But here's the kicker: if you're gunning for the ultimate weapon to pair with
Lyney
, you're in for a treat. His top-tier weapon is conveniently featured on the same
banner as Lyney
himself. Talk about a double win!
Banner of Genshin Impact Lyney
Lyney
is the featured 5-star character in the first phase of the
Character Event Wish Banner
of
Genshin Impact version 4.0
. It was officially released on
August 16, 2023
, with the release of the new region, Fontaine.
How old is Lyney in Genshin Impact ?
In Genshin Impact, the exact age of
Lyney
is
unknown
. However, he is confirmed to be older than his sister Lynette. Based on his appearance, he is estimated to be around
16-21 years old
. He is also described as a "born performer," suggesting that he has been in the entertainment industry for a while, making him 18 years old. Additionally, his
birthdate
is
February 2nd
.
Lyney
and his sister
Lynette
were orphaned young and forced to live on the streets. They learned magic from a beggar, Cesar, who taught them how to use their magic to survive. Lyney and Lynette eventually became famous magicians, and they now use their magic to entertain and help others.

S-tier Pyro character
. His semi-fast-paced gameplay makes him fun to use. And speaking of his sister, check out our Lynette guide to determine her best build.

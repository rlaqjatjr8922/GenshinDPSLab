Escoffier is the savior of Cryo and is an amazing support unit for those who want to brush off the dust from their Ayaka or Wriothesley. With her release, Escoffier has given new life to Cryo and Hydro users with her ridiculous buffs, which can sometimes even increase the team’s damage by up to 30+%. And if you’ve already pulled for her, then this guide will be the perfect place to look at some teams that you can slot her into!

Escoffier Best Teams
Escoffier thrives when used in Cryo and Hydro only teams, as that is also the only way to get the most of her passive, which reduces Cryo and Hydro RES based on the number of different Hydro and Cryo units you have in your team. To get the most out of this passive, you will want to have 4 different Hydro/Cryo users in your team. This makes Escoffier somewhat limited when it comes to team building but also allows her to buff only those two elements, providing great buffs for only them rather than mediocre buffs for the whole roster. So while she’s somewhat limited in her team building, we believe that this is good, as Cryo can finally be usable again!

This team manages to bring out the most of Ayaka’s damage by pairing her with three amazing supports. There is no need to talk about the impact that Shenhe has on Cryo DPS units, and Furina is also a powerhouse when it comes to buffing the team’s damage. Escoffier’s place here is to further boost the Cryo DMG that Ayaka deals while also providing just enough healing to counteract the HP loss that comes as a result of Furina.

This team squeezes out what it can from the F2P Cryo and Hydro units to make something that is viable but isn’t exactly the best. While Kaeya can deal some damage, he falls quite short of all other Cryo DPS units like Ayaka and Wriothesley, and even with the support of the rest of the cast, this team still struggles to reach the potential of the above-mentioned teams. Having said that, if you’re lacking in units, this is a good starting point, and you can always replace Kaeya whenever you get a new Cryo DPS unit to instantly make this team way stronger.

Escoffier Guide & Best Builds
Welcome to our Escoffier build guide for Genshin Impact.
Escoffier
is a powerful 5-star
Cryo
support character,
dealing high off-field damage, large amounts of RES Shred to
Cryo

Hydro

and providing great healing.

:

:
Cryo DMG
or ATK %
:
CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG > ATK %
Substats:
Energy Recharge > CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG > ATK %
If an Energy Recharge Sands is needed to reach Escoffier's Energy
requirements, then it should be used. Otherwise, always use ATK%.
Cryo DMG
Goblet
is better than ATK%, but it is a small difference if using
Golden Troupe
and ATK% is much better for her healing, so either Goblet is fine.
CRIT circlets are better than ATK%, but ATK% is close enough to be used
until you get a good Crit circlet.

Kitchen Skills
The overwhelming majority of Escoffier's damage comes from her Elemental 
Skill, so this talent should always be prioritized. Her Elemental Burst doesn't
deal too much damage, but her healing scales off this talent, so you do want to
level it. Her Normal Attack talent can be ignored entirely.
How to Play Escoffier
Escoffier's playstyle focuses on being used in a team consisting
entirely of
Hydro
and
Cryo
characters,
providing great buffing via 55% RES Shred to both Elements, teamwide healing,
and great off-field damage of her own. She will typically swap in and cast her 
Elemental Skill to get her damage and RES Shred going, then her Elemental Burst
to heal the team. After casting both abilities, she will swap off to the next
character in the party, continuing to do great damage from off-field the whole
rotation.
Escoffier's Best Constellations
Escoffier has an extremely powerful C1, providing 60 Cryo Crit DMG% to both
herself and the active character. This Constellation is a fantastic investment
to make if you are using Escoffier with a Cryo DPS. However, if you are using a DPS
of any other Element, this Constellation is not worth it, and getting her weapon
Symphonist of Scents
will be a better investment. Once you
have Escoffier's C1 and/or weapon, this is a good investment stopping point, as
her remaining Constellations aren't particularly great.

Best weapon for Escoffier
Her signature Symphonist of Scents is without doubt the best choice out there. If you plan to invest in her, then picking up the weapon before her constellations will be ideal. This is because it is one of the few weapons that actually buffs your other party members.
For F2P options, you can’t go wrong with the Favonious lance as it offers high base ATK and Energy recharge.
💡
Pro tip: Symphonist outperforms even Staff of Homa if you’re focusing on reaction-based Freeze comps.
Best Artifact Sets for Escoffier
There are two ways you can build Escoffier, either Sub-DPS or Support, but I’d recommend Sub-DPS as her kit provides massive support if you just build her right.
Best Artifacts for Escoffier

Golden Troupe (4-pc)
Increases Elemental Skill DMG while off-field. Synergizes with Cryo application from her kit.
Support:

Adds ATK buff after Burst; easy fit for support role. (I’d only recommend this if your best stats on GT is on Furina)
The Exile (4★)
Energy regen for F2P / early-game players.

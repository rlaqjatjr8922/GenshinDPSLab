Escoffier is one of the upcoming characters of Genshin Impact Version 5.6. She is a 5-star Cryo healer who uses a polearm. In this guide, we’ll go over the needed materials to ascend Escoffier to level 90, as well as level her skills to level 10. Most of the materials needed to level up Escoffier are currently available, with the only exception being her boss mats, which will become available after the Version 5.6 update goes live.

How to Get Beryl Conch
Beryl Conches are a Local Specialty found in Fontaine and can be found underwater in the Source of All Waters: The Great Fontaine Lake or purchased from Hinterman and Xana. There are a total of 105 Beryl Conches available on the map.

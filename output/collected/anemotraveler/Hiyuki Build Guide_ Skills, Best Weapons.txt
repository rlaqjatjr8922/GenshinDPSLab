Dual Forms (Present Self & Foreclaimed Self)
Present Self (Default Form):
Hiyuki starts in this state. By using Basic Attack Stage 3 and her Intro Skill, she builds up the Forte Gauge
[Dedication]
. Once [Dedication] reaches 300, she can cast her Enhanced Heavy Attack (Frost Splinter), unlocking her Resonance Liberation (Foreclaiming: Inward Vision) to switch to her burst form.
Foreclaimed Self (Burst Form):
In this state, Hiyuki's Basic Attacks are considered Resonance Liberation DMG. Attacking builds a new Forte Gauge,
[Frostheart]
. Consuming 100 [Frostheart] allows her to perform a special
[Iai]
strike.
Core Nuke (Blade Liberation)
While in Foreclaimed Self, casting [Iai] grants stacks of [Whiteout Bitterfrost]. At 3 stacks, she can cast a special Heavy Attack to gain up to 3 points of
[Snowforged Blade]
. Finally, she can consume these points to unleash her ultimate nuke:
Foreclaiming: Blade Liberation
. The more points consumed, the higher the damage multiplier.
Glacio Chafe & Glacio Bite
Hiyuki applies "Glacio Chafe" to enemies with her attacks. When she is in the team, any Glacio Chafe applied by nearby Resonators converts into "Glacio Bite," which triggers additional burst damage based on the enemy's stack limit. This makes her both a selfish hypercarry and an excellent synergistic piece for Glacio teams.
Best Weapons for Hiyuki
For Hiyuki, you want to prioritize
Crit Rate, ATK%, and Resonance Liberation DMG bonuses
.
Best in Slot: Self No More (Signature / 5★)
Perfect Fit.
Provides 24.3% Crit Rate and 12% ATK. Applying Glacio Chafe amplifies Glacio DMG by 28% and makes Resonance Liberation DMG ignore 10% of the target's DEF. It also buffs Glacio Chafe DMG for the team when she is active.
Best Alternative: Emerald of Genesis (5★)
Provides 24.3% Crit Rate and 12.8% Energy Regen. Casting Resonance Skills stacks ATK% (up to 12%). This is the absolute best generalist 5-star option if you skip her signature.
Best 4-Star: Oath of Tide Hunters (4★)
Provides 20.25% Crit Rate. Casting Resonance Liberation increases ATK by 7.2% and grants a 10.8% Resonance Liberation DMG Bonus, syncing perfectly with Hiyuki's burst rotation.

Resonance Chain (Constellations) Analysis
Hiyuki is a complete and powerful character at Resonance Chain 0 (S0). However, if you are looking to invest further, S2 and S6 are her massive power spikes.
Chain 1 (Springless):
Increases Foreclaimed Self multipliers, adds grouping to her basic attacks, and provides interruption resistance. Great quality-of-life upgrade.
Chain 2 (To Burn Cold in Silence) - Best Value:
Increases [Iai] DMG multiplier by 125%. Out of combat, she instantly restores [Snowforged Blade] and [Frostheart], massively speeding up her burst rotation upon entering a fight.
Chain 3 (No Self, No Bound):
Significantly buffs her coordinated [Glacio Bite] damage multipliers.
Chain 4 (Like Reeds on Tides):
Casting specific skills increases teamwide damage by 20% and restores a large chunk of HP, turning her into a potent sub-DPS/healer hybrid.
Chain 5 (Vessel of Thousand Wishes):
Standard skill multiplier increases (+80%).
Chain 6 (Into a Night Without End) - Ultimate Nuke:
Increases the Crit. DMG of her Foreclaiming abilities by an absurd 500%. Furthermore, any Resonator applying Glacio Chafe triggers fixed-multiplier Glacio Bite DMG, making her an unstoppable force.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Q: Is Hiyuki difficult to play?
A: She has a moderate learning curve. The core of her gameplay is "Dual Gauge Management": max out [Dedication] to enter burst form, max out [Frostheart] for Iai strikes, and stack [Snowforged Blade] for the final nuke. Once you learn the rhythm, her rotation flows very smoothly.
Q: What is the best F2P weapon alternative?
A: If you don't pull her signature, the Standard 5-star sword,
Emerald of Genesis
, is your best bet. Its Crit Rate and ATK% buffs ensure her damage floor remains very high.
Q: Does Hiyuki need Energy Regen (ER%)?
A: Yes. Because her massive nuke is tied to her Resonance Liberation (which costs 125 Energy), you should aim for approximately
120% - 130% Energy Regen
from your Echo substats to guarantee a smooth rotation in endgame content.

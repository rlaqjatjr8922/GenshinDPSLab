General information
Traveler is a 5-star and also the main hero of the game who can additionally choose his element. In this variant, we describe Traveller of Anemo element. When it comes to this variant, the best possible choice for a role is Sub DPS gaining immense benefits from Swirl reaction.
Best team compositions
Anemo Sub DPS - Xiao/Traveler/Bennet/Zhongli
Anemo Sub DPS
In this build, Traveler's main role will be to use Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst to create Swirl reactions that will apply damage from various elements.
Artifacts -
4x Viridescent Venerer
Weapons -
Skyward Blade/Favonius Sword/Sacrificial Sword
The only viable artifact set for Traveler of Anemo element is
4x Viridescent Venerer
. Not only
Anemo DMG is increased by 15%
-
Swirl reaction will deal 60% more damage
and the resistance to the element that was used as a base to Swirl will be lowered for all enemies to 40% for 10 seconds. Your Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst will bring cascades of Swirl reactions very easily.
When it comes to weapons for Anemo Traveler, especially consider
Skyward Blue
. Mainly because aside from extra Energy Recharge and increased critical hit chance by 4%, after using Elemental Burst,
movement speed and attack speed increase by 10% and ATK of normal and charged attacks by 20% for 12 seconds
.
If you don't have the means to obtain this 5-star weapon, a equally good choice will be
Favonius Sword
. Aside from extra Energy Recharge, with this weapon critical hits will have
a 60% chance of spawning 1 Elemental Orb
, which after being picked up,
gives 6 Energy
. This effect may occur once every 12 seconds.
Second possible pick for a weapon is a 4-star
Sacrificial Sword
. With it equipped,
when hitting the opponent with Elemental Skill you'll have 40% chance of lowering the cooldown on this ability completely
. This effect may occur once every 30 seconds.
Talents
Talents are special abilities divided into Combat and Passive categories. In case of Geo Traveler, you should focus on these talents in this order:
Anemo Sub DPS -
Palm Vortex, Gust Surge, Foreign Ironwind
Constellations
In case of Traveler, you can unlock all Constellations very quickly and practically for free, so the faster you get them all, the more formidable and useful in the team he will be.

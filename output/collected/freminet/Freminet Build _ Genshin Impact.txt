Freminet Overview
This Page provides the Best Freminet Build information.
Freminet is widely recognized for his exceptional professionalism, impressive ability to maintain focus, and extensive knowledge of the sea. It’s unfortunate that he chooses not to accept assignments from others, as his skills would be highly valuable to many.
As the younger sibling of Fontaine’s notable magical duo Lyney and Lynette, Freminet prefers to keep a low profile. He feels uneasy when surrounded by people and their gazes, words, and comments disrupt his equilibrium. Instead, he retreats to the ocean’s embrace, where he finds peace and solace in the company of a Romaritime Flower.
Although he may appear detached, Freminet’s dedication runs deep; his passion is unwavering and unrelenting, much like the tides themselves.

What Kind Of Character Is Freminet?
Freminet Is A Physical & Cryo DPS
Freminet is a character that is able to do decent damage with all reactions of Cryo, including Superconduct (Physical). If you like playing with Cryo reactions like Melt, Shatter, and Superconduct, Freminet is a great character to pick for you.
Strengths & Weakness Of Freminet
Strengths
- Can use any Cryo reactions well.
- Great at Shatter Comps since it increase its DMG.
Weakness
- Easily overshadowed by other characters in his role as a Physical or Cryo DPS.
- Slow  movement since the weapon used is Claymore.
- Prone to interruption.
▼ Details about Freminet below.

Freminet Skill Guide & How To Use Freminet
Recommended Rotation For Freminet
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Support characters or off-field DPS.
2
Switch to Freminet and use Elemental Skill and attack based on the type of Freminet you use.
▼Jump to Combos
3
Switch to Freminet and use Elemental Burst.
4
Use Elemental Skill and deal damage with Normal Attacks.
5
Repeat 1st step.
Uses Of Freminet's Elemental Skill
Summons Pers And Provides Damage Depending On The Level Of Shattering Pressure
Freminet will summon Pers to charge. Using Elemental Skill once again will deal damage depending on the level of charge that you collected. You can increase the level of Shattering Pressure by using Normal Attacks.
How To See Level Of Shattering Pressure?
Level
Damage
(Assuming 100% is Highest DMG)
Level 0
Deals 100% Cryo DMG
Level 1
Deals around 50% Cryo and 20% Physical DMG
Level 2
Deals around 35% Cryo and 35% Physical DMG
Level 3
Deals around 20% Cryo and 50% Physical DMG
Level 4
Deals 100% Physical DMG
Check the meter above Pers to see the Shattering DMG Level that you accumulated.
Combos Using Freminet
Depending on the type of comp and artifact that your Freminet uses, the combo after using this skill changes.
Cryo-Focused Freminet
Cryo Freminet
Elemental Skill → Elemental Skill
For Cryo Freminet, you do not need to charge Shattering Pressure damage since the highest Cryo DMG is from Level 0.
Physical-Focused Freminet
Physical Freminet
Elemental Skill → Normal Attack (4x) → Elemental Skill
For Physical Freminet, you need to charge it to max and use Pers to hit the enemies.
Uses Of Freminet's Elemental Burst
Powerups Normal Attacks & Reset The Elemental Skill Of Freminet
The main point of Elemental Burst is to reset and increase the DMG that Freminet does when he is at Elemental Skill form.
Provide One Hit Of Huge DMG
At the start of the Elemental Burst, a small AoE of Cryo DMG will happen first that deals decent DMG.
Will Enter "Subnautical Hunter" Mode
After using Elemental Burst, Freminet will enter a "Subnautical Hunter" mode which will give buffs to Freminet. Changing characters will cancel the buffs provided by Elemental Burst.
Buffs During Subnautical Hunter Mode
- Increase of Resistance to Interruption
- Decrease the Cooldown of Elemental Skill by 70%
- Additional 200% Normal Attack Damage
- Normal Attacks will increase Pressure Level by 1 more.
Should You Pull for Freminet's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
h3: Freminet's Constellations are Good to Have
Constellation Priority
C2 > C4 > C6
C2 is a Good Stopping Point
Freminet's C2 notably makes it easier for him to burst consistently, increasing his damage output by a noticeable margin, while C6 is where Freminet will output his best possible damage.  Note that while constellations can his gameplay smoother and damage higher, they aren't strictly required.
Freminet will perform as intended even at C0.

Effect

Shattering Pressure
Effect
Executes different sorts of attacks based on the Pressure Level of Pers Timer, and then cancels Pers Timer.
・Level 0: Unleashes a vertical cut, dealing Cryo DMG.
・Levels 1 to 3: Unleashes a vertical cut alongside Pers, dealing Cryo DMG and Physical DMG. DMG dealt scales based on Pressure Level.
・Level 4: Borrows the power of a fully-pressurized Pers to deal Physical DMG. Meanwhile, Normal Attack: Flowing Eddies will be replaced by Shattering Pressure.

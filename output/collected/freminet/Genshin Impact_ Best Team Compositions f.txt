Best Team Compositions for Freminet in Genshin Impact
Physical DPS
A Physical DPS Freminet team needs an Electro unit to help trigger Superconduct for the Physical RES shred. There are multiple variations of this team, one of the key differences is the presence of a Hydro unit. A Hydro unit would enable Freeze+Shatter which grants Freminet several buffs based on his constellations.

This team has many options for the other slots, try to mix and match what you need. For example, you do not need 2 healers nor do you need both Rosaria and Zhongli’s Physical RES shred at once. Bennett’s ATK buff is always welcome.

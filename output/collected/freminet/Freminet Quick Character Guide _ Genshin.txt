Freminet is a flexible character that is capable of dealing both Cryo DMG and Physical DMG.
Freminet's Elemental Skill will generate a Pers Timer, which will indicate the current Pressure Level. While Pers Timer is active, Freminet's Normal Attacks will be infused with Cryo and the Pressure Level will increase. When unleashing his Elemental Skill again during this time, Freminet will perform different sorts of attacks based on the Pressure Level of the Pers Timer.
Freminet's Elemental Burst can reset the CD of his Elemental Skill. While his Elemental Burst is active, the CD of his Elemental Skill will be decreased.

Skills Overview
Normal Attack: Flowing Eddies
Normal Attack
Performs up to 4 consecutive strikes.
Charged Attack
Drains Stamina over time to perform continuous spinning attacks against all nearby opponents.
At the end of the sequence, performs a more powerful slash.
Plunging Attack
Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Elemental Skill: Pressurized Floe
Performs an upward thrust that deals
Cryo DMG
and causes Freminet to enter Pers Timer for 10s.
While Pers Timer is active, his Elemental Skill will turn into Shattering Pressure.
Pers Timer
When Freminet uses Normal Attacks, he will also unleash waves of frost that deal
Cryo DMG
and increase Pers's Pressure Level.
The accompanying
Cryo DMG
dealt this way is considered Elemental Skill DMG.
Cooldown
: 10 seconds
Shattering Pressure
Executes different sorts of attacks based on the Pressure Level of Pers Timer, and then cancels Pers Timer.
Level 0
: Unleashes a vertical cut, dealing Cryo DMG.
Levels 1 to 3
: Unleashes a vertical cut alongside Pers, dealing Cryo DMG and Physical DMG. DMG dealt scales based on Pressure Level.
Level 4
: Borrows the power of a fully-pressurized Pers to deal Physical DMG. Meanwhile, Normal Attack: Flowing Eddies will be replaced by Shattering Pressure.
Arkhe: Pneuma
At certain intervals, after using the upward thrust, a Spiritbreath Thorn in the form of another upward thrust will be created, dealing Pneuma-aligned Cryo DMG.
Spiritbreath Thorn Interval
: 9 seconds
Elemental Burst: Shadowhunter's Ambush
Unleashes a wave of untouchable cold, dealing
AoE Cryo DMG
, resetting the CD of the Elemental Skill "Pressurized Floe," and causing Freminet to enter the Subnautical Hunter mode for 10s.
While in Subnautical Hunter mode, Freminet's resistance to interruption will increase, and his Elemental Skill "Pressurized Floe" will obtain the following buffs:
CD is decreased by 70%.
Normal Attacks will increase the Pers Timer by 1 additional Pressure Level, and the frost released by his Normal Attacks deal 200% of their original DMG.
These effects will be canceled when Freminet leaves the field.

TLDR / Rotation
Freminet have 2 possible roles: a
Physical DPS
or
Cryo DPS
. These 2 roles make use of his Elemental Skill in different ways.
If you're using him as a
Physical DPS
, what you need to focus on is making sure he
reaches at least Level 2 Shattering Pressure
(deals both Cryo and Physical DMG) by using Normal Attacks after his Skill.
His general rotation would be:
Burst > [Skill > 2 Normal Attacks > Skill > 1 Normal Attack] x2 times. By the times you finished the 2 [rotation], his Elemental Burst will run out soon.
If you're using him as a
Cryo DPS
, you need to
spam his Skill
as much as possible to dish out Level 1 Shattering Pressure (deals Cryo DMG only)
during his Elemental Burst
.
His general rotation would be:
Burst > [Skill > Skill > Normal Attack] x4 times. By the times you finished the 4 [rotation], his Burst will run out soon.

Team Compositions
Complete Superconduct/Physical Team
If you're playing a Physical team, you can bring a Sub-DPS Electro character like Fischl to deal extra damage. The last slot is really flexible so you bring along anyone you want and completely built such as:
1. Shenhe
2. Beidou
3. Yelan
4. Mika
5. Bennett
Freeze + Shatter Team
As the name suggests, you need a combination of Hydro and Cryo to Freeze enemies so bring along at least one Hydro character. You can then bring along another Cryo character for the extra Energy who has a decent AoE DMG like Chongyun/Kaeya/Rosaria/Shenhe/Layla.
Lastly, an Anemo character gathers enemies and reduces Elemental RES. Since Freminet is a Claymore character, he can also trigger Shatter after Freezing enemies with this team. Replace someone with a healer if you're having trouble with survivability.
Other similar team: Freminet + Shenhe + Ayaka + Jean
Shatter + Hyperbloom Team
A Hydro character (Xingqiu/Yelan/Kokomi) is a must to Freeze and Shatter enemies while an Electro character is to trigger Superconduct and reduce enemies' Physical RES. In this team, you can proc Shatter and Superconduct for Freminet and get passive Hyperbloom with the help of the Dendro character in the last slot.

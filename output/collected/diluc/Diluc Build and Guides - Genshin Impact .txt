Diluc Guide & Best Builds
Welcome to our Diluc build guide for Genshin Impact.
Diluc
is a 5-Star
Pyro
Main DPS Character who 
focuses on consistent on-field damage through Elemental Skill chaining and 
reaction-based gameplay such as Vaporize and Melt. In our guide, we'll cover everything you need to 
know to get the most out of your Diluc, from building the best Artifacts 
and Weapons to important stat priorities and rotations.

Diluc primarily scales with ATK and CRIT stats. Elemental Mastery becomes more valuable

allowing you to prioritize CRIT DMG and ATK more heavily.

Diluc's Talent Priority
Talent Priority:
Normal Attack > Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst
Diluc’s Normal Attacks should be prioritized as they make up the majority of his damage,
followed by his Elemental Skill and then his Elemental Burst.
How to Play Diluc
Diluc is a straightforward on-field DPS whose gameplay revolves around chaining his
Elemental Skill between Normal Attacks to maximize damage output.
His Elemental Skill can be cast three times in quick succession, allowing him to weave
Normal Attacks between each cast. This creates a smooth rotation that maintains
consistent Pyro application.
Diluc performs best in reaction-based teams, especially Vaporize, where he can
consistently trigger reactions with characters like
Xingqiu
or
Yelan
.
His Elemental Burst provides a large AoE Pyro attack and infuses his weapon with Pyro,
allowing all subsequent Normal Attacks to deal Pyro damage. This should be used early
in the rotation to maximize uptime.

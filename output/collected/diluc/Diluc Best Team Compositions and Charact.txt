Diluc's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Diluc team composition guide and character synergy overview for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams for Diluc, including Melt, Vaporize, Overload, and Mono Pyro variations, alongside recommended character pairings that help maximize his damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Diluc
Diluc is primarily played in reaction-based teams such as Melt and Vaporize due to his consistent
Pyro
application. His Elemental Skill allows him to chain attacks while maintaining Pyro uptime, making him highly effective with off-field Cryo or Hydro enablers.
Diluc benefits greatly from teammates that provide ATK buffs, Elemental RES Shred, or enable consistent reactions. Buffers such as
Bennett
and Anemo supports with
Viridescent Venerer
are especially valuable for maximizing his overall damage output.

ATK Buffers / Sustain
Bennett
is Diluc’s best support, providing both strong ATK buffs and healing while enabling Pyro Resonance.
Iansan
offers a comparable ATK buff but has less reliable healing.
Dehya
provides damage mitigation and enables Pyro Resonance, though her overall buffing capability is lower.

Xilonen

Sucrose
and

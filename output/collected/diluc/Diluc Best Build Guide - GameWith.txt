Diluc is a 5-star Pyro character who wields a Claymore. He comes from the wealthy Ragnvindr family and is the current owner of the Dawn Winery in Mondstadt. He is also known for his persona as the "Darknight Hero". As an on-field Pyro DPS, he excels at dealing consistent, high damage with his Elemental Skill and Burst, making him an impactful carry. He remains a formidable option as a main DPS for both beginners and veteran players alike.

What Kind of Character is Diluc?
In battle,
Diluc is a claymore-wielding 5-Star
On-Field
Pyro
DPS
whose Elemental Skill can be used three times within a rotation, with each usage having a distinct animation and damage scaling.
His Elemental Burst creates a wide AoE attack that sweeps forwards in the shape of an eagle, dragging enemies in its path.
As a standard banner character, he requires significant investment in his supports to compete with the current meta teams in endgame content.

Diluc's Role, Strengths & Weaknesses
On-Field Pyro DPS
Diluc's abilities make him an on-field DPS who requires as much field time as possible. He largely prefers supports that can amplify his damage, but since his kit has no major restrictions, he can also be paired with strong Sub-DPS characters to boost the team's total DPS.
Pros and Cons of On-Field DPS Diluc
Pros
- Easy to play.
- Flexible teambuilding options.
- Scales with ATK%, allowing him to take full advantage of the teamwide ATK buff from Pyro Resonance.
Cons
- As a standard character, his performance is generally lower than that of limited characters.
- Competes with stronger DPS characters for their supports.
- One of his passives reduces the stamina cost and increases the duration of his Charged Attack. However, he rarely uses his Charged Attack in optimal rotations, making this passive less impactful.

Find a Balance Between Offensive Stats
Diluc's stat priority is straightforward most of the time, regardless of his team. He generally wants high Attack and a 1:2 CRIT Rate to CRIT DMG ratio for consistent CRIT hits. Diluc's Team Rotations and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for On-Field DPS Diluc
Basic Rotation For On-Field DPS Diluc
1. Use your supports' abilities, ordering them by duration from longest to shortest.
2. Swap to Diluc. Start with his Burst, then use his Skill followed by a Normal Attack. Repeat this for all three Skill charges. For Plunge teams, use Plunging Attacks instead of Normal Attacks between each Skill.
3. Repeat from Step 1.
Diluc's basic team rotation follows the typical DPS pattern of using support abilities before switching to the main damage dealer. This applies to most of his team comps, with minor variations depending on the specific supports on the team.
Basics Of Using Diluc
Diluc is an
On-Field
Pyro
DPS
who utilizes all three charges of his Skill to deal the bulk of his damage.
His abilities are simple and lack strict teambuilding requirements, but he performs best in teams that can amplify his damage through reactions like Melt or Vaporize.
Skill Has Three Distinct Attacks
Diluc's Elemental Skill is his defining feature, as it can be used up to three times, with each cast having a different animation and damage multiplier.
While the transition between each attack is fine on its own, it's recommended to weave in two Normal Attacks between each Skill cast to cancel the animation. This saves time per rotation and makes his gameplay feel smoother.
Burst Is a Wide Pyro Attack
His Elemental Burst unleashes a phoenix that deals AoE Pyro damage as it rushes forward. This ability can also drag smaller enemies along its path.
Has a Pyro Infusion
After using his Elemental Burst, Diluc's claymore becomes infused with Pyro. The importance of this infusion varies depending on your team composition and playstyle.

Flexible Pyro DPS With Three Skill Usages
Basic Rotation Of Diluc
1. Deploy support abilities, starting with those that have longer durations.
2. Swap to Diluc. Start the combo with his Burst, then weave his Skill with Normal Attacks (e.g., Skill > NA > Skill > NA > Skill > NA).
3. Repeat the rotation once your support abilities expire.
Diluc's playstyle is relatively straightforward, but it allows for simple optimization depending on his team composition.

Best Played in Melt or Vaporize Teams
Vaporize & Melt
While Diluc's base damage is moderate, amplifying his attacks with Melt or Vaporize reactions can significantly boost his overall DPS.
Should You Pull for Diluc's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Constellations Are Nice But Not Necessary
Constellation Priority
C6 > C3
C6 Makes Attack Combos Easier
To improve his performance, Diluc's constellations as a Standard Banner character are typically not a priority in terms of pulling, especially if you have limited funds. However,
if you're willing to invest that much into Diluc directly instead of upgrading his weapons or his off-field supports
, then the only way to guarantee his constellations would be through the
Chronicled Wish
banner. In this case,
C6 is his strongest constellation because it allows you to perform his Normal Attack combo much easier while also providing a decent Attack Speed buff.
Aiming for C6 on any character is an unrealistic goal for most players unless you have the funds to do so. With this in mind,
it's recommended to either invest your funds elsewhere, or consider pulling for Diluc's C6 only if you already have him at C4 or C5. Otherwise, C3 can be a viable stopping point due to the additional Skill levels, which makes up the bulk of Diluc's damage.
In general, his constellations are nice to have but not something you would need to actively pull for, especially since he's on the Standard Banner. Although he is outclassed by others with the same role,
Diluc can still be a viable on-field Pyro DPS character at C0, especially if you have all the pieces for his best team
.
Recommended Pull Priority
A Thousand Blazing Suns
> C6 > C3

Who Is Diluc?
Owner Of The Dawn Winery In Mondstadt
Diluc is the owner of the Dawn Winery, giving him a great monopoly on Mondstadt's alcohol industry. This makes him a prominent figure in Mondstadt, more so since he's from the Ragnvindr Clan.
A Former Knight Of Favonius
Diluc was once part of the Knights of Favonius, but something happened to make him part ways with the organization. He still protects Mondstadt, but by his own means now.
Kaeya's Adoptive Brother
Kaeya was adopted by Diluc's father and was raised alongside him as his brother. However, they currently have a strained relationship due to an incident that happened between them.

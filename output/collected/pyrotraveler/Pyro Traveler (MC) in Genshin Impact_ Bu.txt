Pyro Traveler in
Genshin Impact
is the main character belonging to the Pyro element, able to take the position of either a sub- or a main-DPS in their team. He applies Pyro status during normal attacks of allies, and when having some consts, he can deal significantly more damage or receive infusion for his attacks.
In this guide, we will take a look at how important the constellations for the Pyro MC are and how they change his possible positions in a team. One of the key features of the hero is his Pyro status, which is applied relatively often compared to other characters of the same element, so he can be used as an applicator.
Having arrived to a new territory, the Traveler fails to create resonance with the fire element in Natlan. On his way, he meets a girl from the Children of the Echoes tribe, Kachina, who tells him about the future pilgrimage and shares information on what this event actually is. The Traveler is again drawn into a whirlwind of events, including countless battles, secrets of the fallen empire and solution of the Abyss problem.

The Best Pyro Traveler Builds in Genshin Impact
Pyro Traveler is a hero who provides Pyro status and gives buffs to the squad with his constellation effects. During the Nightsoul Blessing, the character should find the time to provide his squad with buffs and apply Pyro to the target using his elemental skill. To deal good damage to enemies, it is necessary to balance the critical mass (critical damage and hit chance) with artifacts, weapons and passive set effects.
In a team, Pyro MC at C6 is able to take the slot of the main damage dealer and use his normal attacks, but, regardless of buffs and equipment set, he will be significantly inferior to the similar heroes of epic rarity. In the following paragraphs, we will tell you how to build Pyro Traveler, what artifacts and weapons to chhose for him, and also indicate the stats he needs.

Teams
Pyro MC is better assigned to be a support, providing buffs and Pyro application for the main damage dealer. His Nightsoul mechanics makes it easier to deal with opponents from Natlan, who require having such a character in their squad. Can play well in many teams that need Pyro damage from the pocket to trigger subsequent reactions with other damage dealers.
The main damage dealer Pyro Traveler is more demanding and dependent on allies. He needs strong buffers and supports that can fully support the hero during damage and protect him from crushing attacks of opponents.

Skills
Pyro Traveler's skills invite to playing his as a secondary damage dealer and applicator, capable of performing joint attacks with an ally to cause reactions for targets. Similar to the heroes of Natlan, Pyro MC has the mechanic of
Nightsoul
, and is able to use Blessing and Burst, but not Transmission. Thus, his skillset lacks skills that make it easier to explore areas with phlogiston, but in combat he remains as effective as other heroes of the Pyro region.
Nightsoul Blessing is activated when you press the elemental skill and ends when all NS points are spent. To maintain stacks, you need to use elemental burstn and resort to activating your passive ability, which is very easy to do in any squad.

How to Play Pyro Traveler
The Pyro Traveler can be played in two ways, taking the role of both the main damage dealer and the supporting character. The style of play practically does not change, except that at a certain stage, regular infused attacks are added. To do this, it is necessary to activate the sixth constellation of the Pyro MC, which can be obtained for completing missions and exploring the world of Natlan. Activating the Nightsoul Blessing with an elemental skill is a key mechanic for both the sub-DD and the main damage dealer.
Rotation
Skill Order:
Elemental skill (E) for Pyro Traveler > going on with further team rotation > Elemental burst (Q) > switching to a damage dealer or going on with further team rotation.
Here's a detailed sample rotation for Pyro Traveler:
In elemental squads, it is important to start the rotation with Pyro MC. This way, in addition to extra attacks perfomed by Blazing or Scorching Threshold, valuable energy for the ultimate will be accumulated. With the first two constellations, it will be possible to get more benefit from the activation and a buff for the attacking ally.
Perform any reaction over an already applied Pyro status using attacks by sub-damage dealers and supports, according to the established team rotation.
Use Traveler's Elemental Burst to inflict a new Fire status to enemies and gain a chance to restore 28 Nightsoul points to maintain the Blessing state.
Go on with the team rotation until the Pyro MC's skill is restored (18 seconds). If the hero is playing as the team's main damage dealer, provided that C6 is available, use normal attacks with Pyro Infusion.
Character Summary
Pyro Traveler in Genshin Impact is a sub-damage dealer and an applicator, who performs joint attacks with the main DPS of the team. Having obtained his constellations, the hero can act as a buffer and increase combat potential of allies. One noticeable advantage over other characters is his availability as a combat unit, because to get the MC you do not need to spend wishes in the event banner, but just finish story chapters. The Traveler has the mechanics of Nightsoul, which allows you to enter into favorable battles with opponents from Natlan, obliging you to have at least one hero in the team with such a status.
Thanks to the abundance of reactions in his passive skill, Pyro MC can be a good addition to any team as a combat unit. Despite the high cooldown of abilities, Pyro Traveler effectively closes reactions and carries out additional attacks together with the main damage dealer. The only real disadvantage is his low damage even at C6. The hero can play in the open world, but will feel the lack of DPS on targets in the Twisted Abyss, the Imaginarium Theater or in battles with local legends, sometimes losing even to epic heroes in the same position.

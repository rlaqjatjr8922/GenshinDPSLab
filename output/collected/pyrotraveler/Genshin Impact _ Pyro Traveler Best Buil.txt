Rate Pyro Traveler!
What Kind Of Character Is The Pyro Traveler?
The Pyro Traveler is a Pyro character that can use Nightsoul Blessing state. They can be the DPS or Support in a team. The Elemental Skill can deal off-field Pyro damage and their Elemental Burst can infuse their Attacks with Pyro upon unlocking C6.
Strengths & Weakness Of Pyro Traveler
Strengths
- Off-field Pyro sub-DPS with the ability to buff the active character.
- Decent Pyro DPS if C6 is unlocked.
- Can hold Scroll set to buff teammates.
Weakness
- DMG-boosting buff has weaker effect for non-Natlan characters
- Somewhat slow Pyro application compared to top sub-DPS

Pyro Traveler Skill Guide & How To Use Pyro Traveler
How To Use Pyro Traveler?
How To Use?
▼ Pyro DPS that can be a Support
▼ Use tap E. Skill to summon a fire ring that deals Pyro DMG
▼ Use E. Burst to deal damage & restore Nightsoul points
▼ C6 Pyro Traveler can be on-field DPS
Pyro DPS That Can Be a Support
The Pyro Traveler is a DPS that can deal off-field Pyro damage and also hold Scrolls set. Their support capabilities is further enhanced with C1.
Use Tap E. Skill To Summon a Fire Ring That Deals Pyro DMG
The Elemental Skill has two (2) versions: Tap or Hold. Both will summon a Fire Ring that deals damage. Tapped makes it deal damage by itself, Hold makes it do coordinated attacks with the active character.
Use E. Burst To Deal Damage & Restore Nightsoul Points
Pyro Traveler's Elemental Burst deals damage and charges the Traveler's Nightsoul points for 4 seconds.
C6 Pyro Traveler Can Be On-field DPS
If C6 has been unlocked, the Traveler gains Pyro infusion and increased CRIT DMG on infused attacks
Should You Pull for Pyro Traveler's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
No Need To Pull For Pyro Traveler
The Pyro Traveler is a free character that cannot be obtained via wishes. You can unlock the character by completing the sub-quest, "Where All Hopes Lie," in the
Archon Quest Chapter 5 Act 5
.

Flowfire Blade (Elemental Skill)
Effect
Wields a searing flame that can incinerate the very earth. Has different effects when Tapped or Held.
Press
Effect
Call forth flames in a raging blaze. When used, the Traveler gains 42 Nightsoul point and enters the Nightsoul's Blessing state, summoning a Blazing Threshold that follows the current active character. When the Blazing Threshold gets close to opponents, it will deal Nightsoul-aligned Pyro DMG to nearby opponents at intervals.
Hold
Effect
Unleash fire in a composed manner, dealing Nightsoul-aligned AoE Pyro DMG to nearby opponents. When used, the Traveler gains 42 Nightsoul points and enters the Nightsoul's Blessing state, summoning a Scorching Threshold that follows the current active character. When the current active character within a Scorching Threshold deals DMG to opponents, the Threshold will launch a Coordinated Attack that deals Nightsoul-aligned Pyro DMG based on the Traveler's ATK. This effect can occur once every 3s.
Nigthsoul's Blessing: Traveler
Effect
Continuously consumes Nightsoul points. The Traveler's Nightsoul's Blessing state lasts 12s at maximum, and when their Nightsoul points are exhausted, or if the skill is used again, this state will end. Blazing and Scorching Thresholds will disappear once the Traveler's Nightsoul's Blessing state ends, and only 1 Blazing or Scorching Threshold summoned by the Traveler themselves can exist at any one time.

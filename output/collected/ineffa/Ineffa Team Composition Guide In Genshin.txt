Genshin Impact

Ineffa’s best teams in

The list below is not in any particular order.
Ineffa Team Comp In Genshin Impact
Here are some examples of Ineffa’s teams in
Genshin Impact
:
Ineffa + Furina + Varesa + Xilonen
Ineffa + Chasca + Furina + Bennett
Ineffa + Yelan + Alhaitham + Nahida
Ineffa + Ororon + Ifa + Xingqiu
Since Ineffa primarily boosts Lunar-charged, she needs to have a Hydro character in her team. You'll be fine running a dedicated Lunar-charged team, too, since the reaction takes into consideration the entire team's Lunar-charged DMG as the final DMG count.
Ineffa + Furina + Varesa + Xilonen
Lunar-Charged With Reliable Electro DPS
As Ineffa wants Lunar-charged reactions, you can build Ineffa’s team with strong Electro DPS characters like
Varesa in
Genshin Impact
. She will keep hitting her Plunge attacks while Ineffa and Furina do the off-field Electro and Hydro application.

Since Varesa is a
powerful DPS in
Genshin Impact
, having both Furina and Xilonen notably increases her DPS without interfering with the team’s DMG. Ineffa gives the team enough Elemental Mastery to generate good Lunar-charged numbers, while Xilonen shreds enemies’ resistance to Electro to further improve the Lunar-charged reaction DMG.
Team Roles
Ineffa:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler, Lunar-charged Enabler
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Varesa:
Main DPS, Electro Enabler
Xilonen:
Healer, Buffer, Debuffer
Ineffa + Chasca + Furina + Bennett
Chaos With Chasca
Chasca is a character who takes all the team’s elements and blends them into one attack. She then adds her Swirl reactions to trigger them again into different kinds of reactions. This is a perfect setup for an Ineffa team as
Chasca drives all the reactions
and participates alongside Furina in Ineffa’s Lunar-charged reactions.
You can replace Bennett with
Iansan to focus the team’s reactions
only on Lunar-charged. Iansan pairs nicely with Chasca, since Chasca can move easily while Iansan rocks the Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City set, which increases the team’s DMG by up to 40%.
Team Roles
Ineffa:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler, Lunar-charged Enabler
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Chasca:
Main DPS
Bennett:
Support, Healer, Buffer
Ineffa + Yelan + Alhaitham + Nahida
Hyperbloom Is Viable
Although she focuses more on Lunar-charged, adding Ineffa to
Hyperbloom teams in
Genshin Impact
is not a bad idea. This team is rich in Elemental Mastery (EM) because of Nahida’s EM buff, Dendro Resonance’s EM buff, and Ineffa’s EM buff. All of these factors create a team that mainly focuses on triggering and maximizing every Hyperbloom and Lunar-charged reaction in
Genshin Impact
. Nahida also participates in another step to boost the Hyperbloom reaction by shredding enemies’ resistance to Dendro by having the Deepwood Memories set.
With Yelan and Ineffa’s strong off-field Hydro and Electro application, Alhaitham can comfortably take the main DPS role and keep enabling Dendro for both characters to create Bloom and trigger it into Hyperbloom DMG. Yelan and Ineffa are the only characters who can trigger the Lunar-charged reactions, but that's no issue as they have a strong Crit value alongside the team’s EM buffs.
Team Roles
Ineffa:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler, Lunar-charged Enabler
Yelan:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Alhaitham:
Main DPS, Dendro Enabler
Nahida:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Debuffer, Dendro Enabler
Ineffa + Ororon + Ifa + Xingqiu
F2P Lunar-Charged Team
Ineffa works nicely with F2P team comps as well, especially when teaming her with characters that love the Electro-charged reaction, like Ororon and
Ifa in
Genshin Impact
. On the other hand, Xingqiu enjoys a massive off-field Hydro application that works well with Ifa’s Elemental Skill duration. Both Ifa and Xingqiu will offer the team decent sustainability, while Ororon will buff everyone’s DMG with his Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City set.
This Ineffa F2P team is good and has a lot of variations by simply switching a character with another that performs almost the same, if not better. You can switch Ifa with Sucrose or Heizou, as both characters can successfully do what Ifa does on the field. As for Ororon, you can switch him with characters like Fischl or Beidou. Both Electro characters have some of the best off-field Electro-Enabling abilities. In conclusion, it’s easy to build an Ineffa
F2P team in
Genshin Impact
.
Team Roles
Ineffa:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler, Lunar-charged Enabler
Ororon:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler
Ifa:
Main DPS, Buffer, Healer
Xingqiu:
Sub-DPS, Hydro Enabler

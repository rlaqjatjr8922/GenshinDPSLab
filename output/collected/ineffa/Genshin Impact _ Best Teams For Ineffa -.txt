Ineffa Works Best As a Lunar-Charged Sub-DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Lunar-Charged Team
- In a Lunar-Charged team, Ineffa acts as the Sub-DPS who uses the new artifact set for Moonsign Sub-DPS characters.
- Flins is the DPS and Columbina or Aino is used as the Hydro applier.
- Since Elemental RES shred can help boost Lunar-Charged DMG, a VV holder like Sucrose is used. Her EM scaling helps with Ascendant Gleam effect and she can also buff EM for the team.
2nd
▼ Moonsign Quickbloom Team
- With the addition of Nefer, a Catalyze team can be used to buff Nefer's Burst DMG.
- Ineffa won't trigger Hyperbloom since Nefer converts all Dendro Cores and prevents it from being affected by Elements.
- Columbina provides more Verdant Dew for Nefer so she can use her Special Charged Attack even if Ineffa's Electro overwrites Hydro or Dendro for Bloom.
3rd
▼ Lunar-Overvape Team
- Ineffa can be used as a Shielder and Sub-DPS for an triple reaction team that can make use of her EM buff as well.
- An Anemo character can be used as On-Fielder to chain trigger more reactions.
4th
▼ Overload Team
- Overload teams can use Ineffa as a much-needed Shielder since she is one of the few Shielders that is Electro or Pyro and has a tanky shield if built right.
- Chevreuse can be used with Durin to provide more Electro shred.
5th
▼ F2P Lunar-Charged Team
- With Aino as a free character for Nod-Krai's release, Ascendant Gleam can be easily achieved.
- The Natlan Electro-Charged duo can be used for their synergy to the reaction but you can use other Electro, Hydro or Anemo characters as well.
Ineffa is an Electro Sub-DPS who can enable Lunar-Charged reactions by converting Electro-Charged
. With the addition of new Moonsign characters and artifact sets from Nod-Krai, Ineffa can be built as a Sub-DPS in Moonsign teams. Outside of Moonsign teams, she's a great shield support and her off-field application helps for reactions.

Flins
Neuvillette
Cyno
Clorinde
- Reserve this spot for the On-Field DPS. Any Hydro or Electro character can work.
- Since Ascendant Gleam allows non-Moonsign characters to buff Lunar Reactions, HP-scaling characters are preferred for Hydro while ATK-scaling are preferred for Electro.
- Cyno scales on both ATK and EM, allowing him to contribute to the team EM needed while also boosting Lunar-Charged with the Ascendant Gleam effect.
Columbina
Aino
Yelan
Xingqiu
- Aino is the best Moonsign alternative to Columbina since she can activate Ascendant Gleam effect.
- Yelan is the best non-Moonsign alternative since she scales on HP and can buff Lunar-Charged via the Ascendant Gleam effect.
- Xingqiu is an F2P alternative for Yelan but won't be able to buff using Ascendant Gleam effect.
Sucrose
Jahoda
Kazuha
Xilonen
Bennett
- Reserve this spot for an Elemental Shred support or ATK buffer.
- Jahoda can be used as VV holder and Moonsign character for activating Ascendant Gleam effect. She can provide healing as well.
- Xilonen works great as an Elemental Shred support and healer and her DEF scaling allows her to buff Lunar-Charged via Ascendant Gleam effect.
- Kazuha works as a VV holder but his Elemental Damage buff doesn't affect Lunar-Charged DMG. His EM scaling makes him great for buffing Lunar-Charged via Ascendant Gleam.
- Bennett is a great ATK buffer and can buff Flins' damage while also providing healing.
Ascendant Gleam effect works great for Lunar-Charged damage so having at least one other Moonsign character works the best. When choosing for the sole non-Moonsign character, it's best to pick based on the Element and stat that can buff the reaction damage.

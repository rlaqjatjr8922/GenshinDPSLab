Hydro Traveler
's personal DPS is not high enough to clear the
Abyss
, unless used in an extremely expensive and invested team.
To build a team around
Hydro Traveler
, consider the following:
Invest in a high-damage dealing main DPS character to carry the team.
Use a support character with high
Energy Recharge
to enable
Hydro Traveler
's elemental bursts.
Choose an
Electro
-based character to trigger
Electro-Charged
reactions and increase overall team damage.
Keep in mind that even with an invested team,
Hydro Traveler
's personal damage output will still be relatively low.
In terms of artifacts, focus on sets that provide high
Elemental Mastery
and
Attack%
bonuses to maximize
Hydro Traveler
's damage potential.
Remember to farm materials and resources accordingly to support your team's growth and development.
Hydro Traveler
Playstyle and Build Guide
IMPORTANT NOTICE: At the time of writing this,
Hydro Traveler
provides no utility to a team that cannot be better filled by another unit, including
Barbara
which all players can obtain at AR 18.
SUPPORT
Since
Hydro Traveler
lacks any utility, this playstyle focuses on using their Skill and Burst to trigger various support-based weapon and artifact passives along with dealing
Pneuma
damage with their Skill. This is only recommended if a source of
Pneuma
is required and
Traveler
is your only source of
Pneuma
aligned damage. It should be noted that the guaranteeable 4-Star "
Sword of Narzissenkreuz
" can also provide an
Arkhe
attack for other characters, meaning
Hydro Traveler
's usability is further diminished if this weapon has been obtained.
Artifact Sets:
Tenacity of the Millelith
: Only used if you use the hold version of
Hydro Traveler
's Skill.
ON FIELD DPS
Hydro Traveler
can be used as an on-field DPS. Typically this is only worth using in teams with
Furina
,
Bennett
, and a flexible unit. Their DPS is not extremely high, however.
Weapon Choices:
Uraku Misugiri
: This weapon is
Hydro Traveler
's Best Weapon in teams with a
Geo
character.
Summit Shaper
: This is ranked assuming
Hydro Traveler
is C4 which provides a shield for him.
Wolf Fang
: At R5 this weapon is about equal to
Light of Foliar Incision
.
Favonius Sword
: This weapon's utility may make it better than above options.

▼ Shatter Bloom Team
★★★★★

★★★・・

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Shatter-Bloom Team
Character
Detail
Diona
Diona will provide healing, off-field Cryo Application and Elemental Mastery Boost if you have her at C6.
Xingqiu
Xingqiu provides off-field Hydro enabling Kaveh to walk around and deal damage to produce bloom and freezing.
Nahida
Nahida boosts the Elemental Mastery of the team for more DMG and more consistent Dendro Infliction.
The objective of the team is to deal both Shatter by attacking frozen enemies with Blunt Attacks and damage them with Dendro Blooms. Both Bloom and Shatter scales from Elemental Mastery and Dendro does not react with Cryo making this very easy to do when you have Kaveh in the team.
Pros & Cons Of Freeze Bloom Team
Pros
- Amazing against mobs especially if there are multiple enemies.
- Fairly easy to build and the rotation is not strict.
- Characters are easily replaceable.
Cons
- Difficulty to use against enemies that are not affected freeze and immune to Dendro.
Rotation Of Freeze-Bloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Skill Of Nahida.
2
Switch to Xingqiu Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Diona and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Nahida and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
5
Switch to Kaveh and use Elemental Skill to make Blooms explode then use Elemental Burst.
6
Deal damage with Normal Attack and Elemental Skill.
7
Return to No. 1

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Bloom Team
Character
Detail
Nilou
Nilou will boost the EM of the team + make the Bloom Bountiful Cores greatly increasing the damage. This cores will further be buffed by Kaveh's Bloom Buff.
Kokomi
Kokomi is there for Hydro infliction and healing.
Nahida
Nahida boosts the Elemental Mastery of the team for more DMG and more consistent Dendro Infliction.
This team focuses on producing Blooms and increasing their damage. Since Kaveh is healed by Bloom explosions, he works amazing as an on-fielder for bloom teams.
Pros & Cons Of Bloom Team
Pros
- Amazing against mobs especially if there are multiple enemies.
- Fairly easy to build and the rotation is not strict.
- Characters are easily replaceable.
Cons
- Other Dendro characters like Tighnari and Kirara works better than Kaveh.
Rotation Of Bloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Skill Of Nahida.
2
Switch to Nilou and use her Elemental Skill 4 times..
3
Switch to Kokomi and use Elemental Skill.
4
Switch to Kaveh and use Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
5
Deal damage with Normal Attack and Elemental Skill.
6
Return to No. 1

Character
Detail
Raiden Shogun

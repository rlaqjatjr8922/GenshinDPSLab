“Empyrean Reflection” Kaveh
is a 4* claymore-wielding Dendro character to be released in the second half of
Genshin Impact
version 3.6
. He is the first Dendro Claymore character. He will be available as a rate-up 4* character on the debut banner of
“Beyond Mortality” Baizhu
as well as the third rerun banner of
“Plenilune Gaze” Ganyu
. In this Genshin Impact guide, we will discuss all the pre-farm materials required to level up the character of Kaveh to its max potential.
Ascension Materials for Kaveh in Genshin Impact
Image via HoYoverse
In this section of the guide, we will discuss the different materials required for the character ascension of Kaveh in Genshin Impact. The materials needed to ascend Kaveh are as follows :
Ascension 2
3x Nagadus Emerald Fragment, 10x Mourning Flower, 15x Fungal Spores, 2x Quelled Creeper, 40K Mora
Ascension 3
6x Nagadus Emerald Fragment, 20x Mourning Flower, 12x Luminescent Pollen, 4x Quelled Creeper, 60K Mora
Ascension 4
3x Nagadus Emerald Chunk, 30x Mourning Flower, 18x Luminescent Pollen, 8x Quelled Creeper, 80K Mora
Ascension 5
6x Nagadus Emerald Chunk, 45x Mourning Flower, 12x Crystalline Cyst Dust, 12x Quelled Creeper, 100K Mora
Ascension 6
6x Nagadus Emerald Gemstone, 60x Mourning Flower, 24x Crystalline Cyst Dust, 20x Quelled Creeper, 120K Mora
Total
1x Nagadus Emerald Silver, 9x Nagadus Emerald Fragment, 9x Nagadus Emerald Chunk, 6x Nagadus Emerald Gemstone, 168x Mourning Flower, 18x Fungal Spores, 30x Luminescent Pollen, 36x Crystalline Cyst Dust, 46x Quelled Creeper, 420K Mora

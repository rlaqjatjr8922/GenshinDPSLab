Furina possesses a high damage-boosting support capability and a certain backend damage output. However, it is so hard and strict to achieve the high damage. And the performance of the team is not particularly satisfying at C0. This team build aims to prioritize the practicality of the team while considering Furina's performance in real battle scenarios.
The characters in the team are all considered at C0, I will mention something specifically if higher constellations are required.
I analyzed the top 10 teams that most players used in Genshin Impact. And how to use their skills.
Team 1: Neuvellet, Furina, Kazuha, Baizhu
Neuviilet
Furina
Kazuha
Baizhu
Furina and Neuvillette (or Mona) double Hydro team, all at C0. However, Furina's talent's 3-level damage increase depends on the environment. Although there is a loss in talent damage, the double Hydro resonance and the damage boost provided by Furnina are still decent.
This team is also good to use if you have higher Constellations of the other characters.
How to use:
Furina EQ - Kazuha EQ - Baizhu EQ - Neuvillette EQ and charge attack

Neuvillette
Furina
Zhongli
Baizhu

How to use :
Furnina EQ - Zhongli long E - Baizhu EQ - Neuvillette EQ charged attack
Team 3: Ayaka, Furina, Kazuha, Diona (To replace the old team)
Ayaka
Furina
Kazuha
Diona
Furnina replaces Mona in the original lineup. Compared to Mona, Ayaka's instantaneous burst may be slightly lower, but her sustained output is more stable, and she has higher water coverage capability and flexibility. Players without Mona can choose this team.
How to use:
Diona long EQ - Kazuha EAQ - Furnina EQ - Ayaka dodge AQEA charged attack

Team 8: Hutao, Furina, Zhongli, Xingqiu
Hutao
Furina
Zhongli
Xingqiu
Hutao is in a double Hydro team. You can choose Furina if you don't have Yelan and Mona. The team has certain requirements for HP. It can quickly reach a maximum damage bonus above half HP, but it is difficult to achieve a damage bonus below half HP.
Compared to C0 Mona, there is not much overall difference, with a slightly lower burst but higher sustained damage.
How to use:
Zhongli's Elemental Skill (Long Press E) - Furina's Elemental Burst (EQ) - Xingqiu's Elemental Skill + Elemental Burst (QE) - Hutao's Elemental Skill + Charged Attack (EA)

Zhongli's Elemental Skill (Long Press E) - Furina's Elemental Burst (EQ) - Yelan's Elemental Burst (EQ) - Hu Tao's Elemental Skill + Charged Attack (EA)
Team 10: Arataki Itto or Noelle in C6, Furina, Baizhu, and Gorou

Furina

Arataki Itto and Furina have good synergy, with Furina compensating for the lack of damage in Arataki Itto's Hydro range, and there's no need to worry about damage loss.

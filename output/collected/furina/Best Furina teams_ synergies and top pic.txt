Furina Bloom teams
The Bloom reaction between Dendro and Hydro is still one of the strongest reactions in Genshin Impact. Furina’s Salon Solitaire skill can be very strong in terms of additional damage and creating Dendro Cores alongside Baizhu’s ability, for example. One of the strongest and most consistent teams is Neuvillette, Furina, Kazuha, and Baizhu. In this case, Neuvillette performs as the main DPS, with Furina as sub-DPS, Kazuha as a buffer, and Baizhu as a healer.
Neuvillette, Furina, Kazuha, and Baizhu (Images via HoYoverse, edit by esports.gg)
Another very consistent Bloom team is Furina, Nilou, Nahida, and Baizhu. However, in this team, Furina performs as a main DPS, still providing Hydro for triggering the Dendro Cores off-field, while Nahida provides Dendro and buffs the team. Nilou will be a sub-DPS, and Baizhu will heal the team effectively. Nilou’s Talent also enhances all Dendro Cores, making the Bloom reaction even stronger with this team.
Furina, Nilou, Nahida, and Baizhu (Images via HoYoverse, edit by esports.gg)

Hyperbloom teams for Furina
Hyperbloom teams are also very powerful in Genshin Impact. Electro with Hydro and Dendro has proven to be a super damage dealer composition of elements. In this case, some teams might get you to do incredible numbers and eliminate enemies faster. Either with an electro DPS such as
Clorinde
or Shogun or with a dendro DPS like
Alhaitham
, Furina will make miracles with her strong damage power and Hydro application in any fight.

Vaporize teams for Furina
If you still like an old-fashioned reaction like Vaporize, we also have your back. Furina is a very versatile character that can fit pretty much every team you want a hydro application or a Hydro sub-DPS.
Hu Tao
is still one of the strongest pyro main-DPS, and she will do incredible damage with the constant hydro application of Furina’s Elemental skill. Her full potential comes from Vaporize reactions, which can be accomplished easily in this composition.
Hu Tao, Furina, Yelan, and Zhongli (Images via HoYoverse, edit via esports.gg)
Yelan will be the sub-DPS since she deals very good damage and synergizes well with Hu Tao due to her passive. In this composition, Zhongli will be the shielder, granting that to Hu Tao, who needs to be low HP to deal more damage, so she doesn’t die in the middle of the fight. Even if you can use a healer, it won’t be as good as a shielder since Hu Tao’s requirement to be low HP.

Free to play Furina team
Not everyone has an inventory full of five stars to play alongside Furina. However, don’t be sad because there is a solid solution for her. Inside the Vaporize reaction, there’s a team that works very well with a main DPS Furina. In this case,
Xiangling
is going to apply Pyro with her Elemental Skill while giving constant damage with her Ultimate. Sucrose will do Kazuha’s normal job by assembling the enemies in the same place and buffing everyone’s elemental mastery. Bennett will heal and buff the team’s damage at the same time.
Furina, Xiangling, Sucrose, and Bennett (Images via HoYoverse, edit via esports.gg)

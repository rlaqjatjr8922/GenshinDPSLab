Furina is a 5-star Hydro character that wields a Sword in Genshin Impact. Her gameplay focuses on being an off-field DPS and support, utilizing her Elemental Skill (Ousia) summons and buffs characters with her Elemental Burst. She can also heal allies with her Pneuma Elemental Skill. Lore-wise, Furina was the acting Hydro Archon and the human form of Focalors.

Roles & Playstyle Of Furina and Its Strengths and Weakness
Hydro Sub-DPS and Buffer Furina
Furina's best role is to be the Sub-DPS and buffer for her teammates. While she can be used as a healer, that playstyle doesn't work well with her Elemental Burst's buff. Moreover,
her ability to apply Hydro without needing Normal Attacks from the On-Field character make her great to use for certain characters.
Pros and Cons of Sub-DPS Furina
Pros
- Deals powerful Sub-DPS damage due to Elemental Skill scaling and Passive's buff.
- Only needs to be built as a Damage Dealer since her team buff doesn't scale on her stats.
- Does not need Normal Attacks to trigger her Off-Field Attacks like
Xingqiu
and Yelan.
- Low Energy cost on Elemental Burst makes Furina need less Energy Recharge compared to other Burst buffers.
- Enables certain DPS characters to use
Marechaussee set
to buff their Normal/Charged Attacks and CRIT Rate.
Cons
- HP drain effect from her Elemental Skill can be hard to mitigate without a good healer.

Team Rotation of Furina and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for Furina
Basic Rotation for Furina
1. Use Elemental Skill to start draining team HP.
2. Use Elemental Burst and start stacking up Fanfare stacks for buffing the DPS.
3. Switch to other characters.
Furina's buff mechanic requires her to be the first character in the rotation. This is so she can stack up Fanfare stacks faster while the rotation is ongoing and letting the On-Field character have a higher damage buff.
Basics Of Using Furina
Built As a Damage Dealer
Furina is both a Sub-DPS and Support but she can be built as a damage dealer since her buff doesn't scale on any of her stats. You will only need good Energy Recharge on her to consistently use her Elemental Burst for her buff.
Uses Elemental Skill's Damage Mode More
Despite having two modes in her Elemental Skill, Furina's Off-Field damage mode is used more since it can drain HP for her Burst's buff.
It's easier to fulfill the HP change condition with HP drain instead of healing since it does not record overflow.
Elemental Skill Damage and Healing is Increased by Furina's Max HP
Furina's Skill multipliers for the damage and healing is scaled on her Max HP. Her 2nd Passive also boosts the damage and healing per 1k HP she has, capping the boost when she has 40k Max HP in total.
First Passive Has Great Synergy to Single-Target Healers
Furina's 1st Passive allows Healing Overflow from the On-Field character to heal characters who are not on the field.

allowing you to quickly gain Fanfare stacks as well as receive slightly more DMG% bonus if you go over the maximum amount of Fanfare. C2 will also boost Furina's damage output, which makes it a good stopping point if you aren't going all the way to C6.

Who Is Furina?
The Hydro Archon Of Fontaine
Furina is known by all as the Hydro Archon, ruler of Fontaine. When the Traveler first met her, she was at the very spotlight of Fontaine. Some would even say she is the nation's biggest celebrity.
The Human Self Of The Hydro Archon
In reality, Furina is not the Hydro Archon. She's actually the human self of Focalors, the real Hydro Archon, that she separated from herself. She cursed Furina to be undying so the latter could play the role of the Archon while she prepared for her plan.
A Master Of Theater
Once freed from her "role", Furina found herself back to what she loved: being on stage. She is a talented actress and a great director, making her a master of the theater. She even gained a Hydro Vision when she decided to go back on the stage.

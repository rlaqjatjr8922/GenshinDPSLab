Genshin Impact Furina Guide: Introduction
Genshin Impact
Furina, also known as Focalors, is the Hydro Archon of Fontaine, introduced in  4.2 update. As a 5-star Hydro Sword user, she brings a unique playstyle to the game, combining offensive capabilities with supportive roles. Her abilities are centered around the Arkhe system, allowing her to switch between two alignments: Pneuma and Ousia. This duality enables her to adapt to various combat situations, making her a versatile addition to any team.
In the game’s lore, Genshin Impact Furina is depicted as a passionate and theatrical character, embodying the essence of Fontaine’s culture. Her role as the Hydro Archon ties her to the element of water, granting her abilities that focus on Hydro damage and elemental reactions. This connection not only enhances her combat effectiveness but also deepens her narrative significance within the game’s world.
Players can utilize Genshin Impact Furina’s unique abilities to trigger powerful elemental reactions, such as Vaporize and Electro-Charged, by pairing her with characters of different elements. Her support capabilities, including healing and buffing, make her a valuable asset in various team compositions. Mastering her Arkhe system and understanding the synergy between her abilities and those of other characters can lead to highly effective strategies in both single-player and co-op modes.
Genshin Impact Furina Guide: How to Obtain Furina
Genshin Impact Furina is a 5-star character available through Genshin Impact’s “wish” system, appearing in limited-time character banners. During these events, players can increase their chances of obtaining her by using Primogems to make wishes. It’s important to note that her availability is tied to specific banners, meaning she may not always be featured. As with all 5-star characters, her drop rate is relatively low, so patience and persistence are key if you’re aiming to obtain her.
If you miss the banner, you’ll have to wait until Genshin Impact Furina’s next re-run to try again. Keep an eye on official announcements from miHoYo, as they often reveal when popular characters like Genshin Impact Furina will be featured again. Additionally, participating in events and completing certain quests may provide opportunities to obtain her or her signature weapon, further enhancing her potential in your roster.
Weapons
Genshin Impact Furina’s Weapons are key to unlocking her full potential, enhancing her abilities to deal Hydro damage and provide support through healing. As a 5-star character, Furina benefits from weapons that increase her HP, boost her Elemental Mastery, or enhance her Hydro DMG. Selecting the right weapon can amplify her healing output and overall performance, ensuring she thrives in both offensive and defensive roles. In this guide, we’ll dive into the best weapon options for Genshin Impact Furina, helping you choose the perfect weapon to complement her playstyle and elevate her abilities.
Genshin Impact Furina benefits from weapons that boost her Hydro damage, energy regeneration, or CRIT Rate. Here are some top picks for weapons that synergize well with her abilities:
Mistsplitter Reforged
An excellent 5-star sword for Furina in Genshin Impact, providing a substantial boost to her damage potential. Its primary stat is CRIT DMG, which greatly enhances Furina’s ability to deal damage. The weapon’s passive effect, Mistsplitter’s Emblem, increases her Elemental DMG based on the number of stacks of the emblem, making it ideal for maximizing her overall damage output. When Furina’s Elemental abilities trigger, she can accumulate stacks, which further amplify her Elemental DMG. This sword is particularly suited for maximizing her Hydro abilities, making it one of her best-in-slot weapons.
Splendor of Tranquil Waters
This 5-star sword is Furina’s signature weapon, offering a significant boost to her CRIT Damage and providing an HP bonus. Its passive effect increases her damage output based on her HP, making it ideal for maximizing her potential.
Aquila Favonia
This 5-star sword increases her ATK and boosts her damage potential significantly, especially when paired with her Hydro abilities. It also has a healing effect when HP drops below a certain threshold, which can keep her and her teammates in the fight longer.
The Black Sword
A great option for players looking to increase her CRIT Rate. This 4-star sword enhances Furina’s CRIT Rate and provides a healing effect on CRIT hits, making her more sustainable in battle.
Favonius Sword
This weapon is particularly useful for its Energy regeneration potential. When Furina triggers a CRIT hit, it generates additional Energy particles, helping her quickly recover Energy and use her Elemental Burst more frequently.
Each of these swords helps maximize Furina’s potential, depending on whether you want to focus more on damage or Energy management.
Genshin Impact Furina Guide: Talents
Genshin Impact Furina, the Hydro Archon of Fontaine, is a powerful and dynamic character. With her unique abilities, she brings a blend of damage-dealing potential and support capabilities to the team. As a Hydro character, Genshin Impact Furina excels at manipulating water-based attacks and healing, providing both offensive and defensive benefits. Her abilities revolve around her Arkhe alignment, allowing her to switch between different playstyles for varied effects. Whether you’re looking to boost your team’s damage or provide consistent healing, Furina is a versatile addition to any roster.
Normal Attack – Soloist’s Solicitation
Genshin Impact Furina performs up to four normal strikes, and her charged attack deals Physical DMG and alters her Arkhe alignment. If her summons are active, they change form accordingly.
Elemental Skill – Salon Solitaire
Depending on Furina’s Arkhe alignment, this skill summons different entities.
Ousia
summons Salon Members that deal AoE Hydro DMG based on Furina’s Max HP, while
Pneuma
summons the Singer of Many Waters, healing nearby characters based on her HP.
Elemental Burst – Let the People Rejoice
This ability deals AoE Hydro DMG and grants nearby characters the Universal Revelry state, enhancing damage and healing for the team based on accumulated Fanfare points.
Passive Talents
Endless Waltz (1st Ascension): When another character receives healing that overflows, Furina heals a nearby character for a portion of their Max HP.
Unheard Confession (4th Ascension): Increases the damage of Salon Members and decreases the healing interval of the Singer of Many Waters based on Furina’s Max HP.
Talent Materials
To level up Furina’s talents, you’ll need materials like Whopperflower Nectar, Guide to Justice, and Lightless Mass, along with Mora. Talent leveling requires various amounts of materials depending on the level.
Genshin Impact Furina Guide: Constellations
Genshin Impact Furina, The Hydro Archon of Fontaine, is a highly anticipated character in Genshin Impact, bringing unique mechanics that make her a versatile and powerful addition to any team. As the god of justice in Fontaine, Genshin Impact Furina wields the power of Hydro and possesses abilities that can deal both damage and provide substantial healing. With her unique mechanics surrounding Fanfare points, she enhances her team’s abilities to heal or dish out damage, depending on her constellation level and playstyle.
Genshin Impact Furina’s constellations enhance her abilities, providing significant boosts to her effectiveness in combat. Here’s a breakdown of what each constellation does:
C1: Love Is a Rebellious Bird That None Can Tame
Upon using her Elemental Burst, Let the People Rejoice, Furina gains 150 Fanfare points, and her Fanfare limit increases by 100. This enhancement allows her to accumulate more Fanfare, boosting her team’s damage and healing bonuses more effectively.
C2: A Woman Adapts Like Duckweed in Water
While Let the People Rejoice is active, Furina’s Fanfare gain from increases or decreases in nearby characters’ HP is increased by 250%. Additionally, each point of Fanfare above the limit increases Furina’s Max HP by 0.35%, up to a maximum of 140%. This constellation significantly accelerates Fanfare accumulation, allowing Furina to reach maximum stacks more quickly and enhancing her HP for increased healing and damage.
C3: My Secret Is Hidden Within Me, No One Will Know My Name
Increases the level of Furina’s Elemental Burst, Let the People Rejoice, by 3, enhancing its damage and effectiveness. This improvement directly boosts the burst’s output, making it a more potent tool in combat.
C4: They Know Not Life, Who Dwelt in the Netherworld Not!
When Furina heals or deals damage with her Elemental Skill, Salon Solitaire, she regenerates 4 Energy every 5 seconds. This passive energy regeneration reduces her Energy Recharge requirements, allowing for more offensive sub-stats and enabling her to use her Elemental Burst more frequently.
C5: His Name I Now Know, It Is…!
Increases the level of Furina’s Elemental Skill, Salon Solitaire, by 3, enhancing its damage and utility. This upgrade makes her Elemental Skill more effective, contributing to her overall damage output and support capabilities.
C6: Hear Me – Let Us Raise the Chalice of Love!
After using Salon Solitaire, Furina gains the “Center of Attention” effect for 10 seconds. During this period, her Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and Plunging Attacks are converted into Hydro DMG, which cannot be overridden by any other elemental infusion. Additionally, her damage is increased by an amount equivalent to 18% of her Max HP. Depending on her Arkhe alignment, different effects occur:
Arkhe: Ousia
: Every 1 second, all nearby characters in the party are healed by 4% of Furina’s Max HP for 2.9 seconds. Triggering this effect again extends its duration.
Arkhe: Pneuma
: The ground impact damage of her attacks is further increased by 25% of her Max HP. When these attacks hit an opponent, all nearby characters consume 1% of their current HP. This effect can trigger up to 6 times during the duration.
Constellations can significantly improve Furina’s utility, but they are not necessary to enjoy her gameplay. They simply provide more flexibility and higher damage potential, making her even more formidable.
Genshin Impact Furina Guide: Artifacts
Genshin Impact Furina’s Artifacts play a crucial role in maximizing her potential as both a damage dealer and support character in Genshin Impact. With her unique Hydro abilities and reliance on her Max HP for scaling damage and healing, selecting the right artifact sets can significantly enhance her performance. Furina benefits from artifact sets that boost her Hydro DMG, HP, and healing effects, allowing her to fully utilize her talents and provide strong utility to her team. In this guide, we’ll explore the best artifact sets for Furina and how to optimize her build for various playstyles.
Genshin Impact Furina’s performance is greatly affected by the Artifacts she equips. To maximize her potential, players should focus on Artifacts that boost her Hydro damage and overall support abilities. Here are some recommended sets:
Golden Troupe
This 4-piece set boosts Furina’s Elemental Skill damage and increases her Elemental Skill crit rate. After using her Elemental Skill, it also boosts her Elemental Burst damage by 20% of her Elemental Skill’s damage for 8 seconds. It’s a great choice for enhancing Furina’s overall damage output, especially when focusing on her Elemental Skill and Burst synergy.
Heart of Depth
This 4-piece set increases her Hydro damage and provides an ATK bonus after using her Elemental Skill. It’s a solid choice for enhancing Furina’s ability to deal damage, especially when triggering Hydro-based reactions.
Noblesse Oblige
This set boosts her Elemental Burst damage and increases the ATK of all nearby teammates after using her Elemental Burst. It’s a great choice if you want to increase her support role in addition to her damage output.
For sub-stats, prioritize CRIT Rate, CRIT Damage, ATK%, and Hydro Damage Bonus to increase Furina’s overall effectiveness.
By carefully selecting the right weapons and artifacts, you can tailor Furina’s build to suit your playstyle and team composition, maximizing her effectiveness
Genshin Impact Furina Guide: Best Team
Genshin Impact Furina excels in teams that benefit from her Hydro abilities and support role. Here are a couple of team compositions that work particularly well with her:
Furina
Xiangling
Beidou
Xingqiu
This team focuses on triggering powerful Vaporize and Electro-Charged reactions. Xiangling’s Pyronado, Beidou’s Electro abilities, and Xingqiu’s Elemental Skill and Burst all work well with Furina’s Hydro capabilities, creating a synergy that maximizes elemental damage output.
Furina
Albedo
Zhongli
Fischl
This team benefits from the Geo resonance, which provides extra shields for the team. Zhongli’s shields keep the team safe while Albedo and Fischl provide extra damage with their Geo and Electro abilities, respectively. Genshin Impact Furina’s Hydro abilities can trigger additional elemental reactions, giving the team a solid balance of defense and offense.
Both of these team compositions utilize Furina’s abilities in different ways, making her an excellent support and damage dealer.

Materials Breakdown
Varunada Lazurite is dropped by the Hydro Hypostasis, which can be found in Mondstadt. Dandelion Seeds can be gathered from the Dandelions that grow around Mondstadt, often in the wild areas. Whopperflower Nectar is obtained from defeating Whopperflowers, which can be found in various locations across Mondstadt and Liyue. Lastly, the Hoarfrost Core is dropped by the Cryo Regisvine, a boss located in Liyue. These materials are essential for ascending Furina and enhancing her abilities.
Genshin Impact Furina Guide: Conclusion
Genshin Impact’s Furina is a highly versatile character, excelling in both damage dealing and support roles. Her distinctive Arkhe system allows her to shift between two alignments—Pneuma and Ousia—giving her a unique edge in battle. This flexibility enables players to customize her abilities based on their team composition and combat needs. When aligned with Ousia, Furina can deal AoE Hydro damage, scaling off her Max HP, while Pneuma boosts her healing capabilities, allowing her to support the team with substantial HP regeneration. This ability to adapt between offense and support makes her incredibly versatile, allowing her to function effectively in a wide range of team setups, from elemental reaction-focused squads to teams that prioritize survivability.
Furina’s weapons, artifacts, and constellations all synergize seamlessly to elevate her performance. As a 5-star character, Furina benefits from weapons that increase her HP or boost Hydro-related stats, further enhancing her role as a Hydro buffer and damage dealer. Artifact sets that enhance Hydro DMG and Max HP are key to optimizing her skill scaling, ensuring she can either dish out significant damage or provide robust healing to her team. While her constellations do provide noticeable boosts—such as reducing cooldowns or enhancing her Hydro damage—Furina can still perform admirably without them, making her a viable option for players who prefer to build her without heavy investment in her constellations.
In conclusion, Furina’s flexibility and power make her an exceptional choice for both new and experienced players. Her ability to juggle between support and damage-dealing roles ensures she can be a central figure in many team compositions, whether you’re aiming for powerful elemental reactions or maintaining high team sustainability. Furina’s diverse skill set and solid synergy with weapons, constellations, and artifacts solidify her position as a top-tier character for any player looking to enhance their roster. Whether you choose to focus on maximizing her potential through constellations or prefer to keep her as a reliable character with a more casual build, Furina remains a valuable and dynamic addition to any team.

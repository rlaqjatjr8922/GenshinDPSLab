Furina, the Hydro Archon of Fontaine, is a versatile character in Genshin Impact, serving as both a Support and Off-Field Hydro DPS. Her Elemental Burst, "Let the People Rejoice," provides up to a 75% DMG bonus to all party members, scaling with her Fanfare points, which are accumulated through HP fluctuations within the team. To maximize this buff, incorporating a team-wide healer is essential, as it ensures consistent HP changes that contribute to Furina's Fanfare accumulation.
Below is an in-depth analysis of specialized team compositions centered around Furina as of version 5.4, detailing each member's role and synergy within the team.
AD
1. Neuvillette Hypercarry
Neuvillette (Main DPS):
As the primary damage dealer, Neuvillette benefits significantly from Furina's Hydro application and DMG bonus, enhancing his overall damage output.
Furina (Sub-DPS/Support):
Provides continuous Hydro application and a substantial DMG bonus through her Elemental Burst, supporting Neuvillette's offensive capabilities.
Xilonen (Sub-DPS):
Offers additional damage and elemental reactions, complementing the team's offensive strategy.
Kaedehara Kazuha (Anemo Support):
Utilizes Anemo abilities to group enemies and amplify elemental damage through Swirl reactions, enhancing the team's overall damage.
2. Kinich Burgeon
Kinich (Main DPS):
Focuses on triggering Burgeon reactions by interacting with Dendro Cores generated through Hydro and Pyro applications.
Furina (Sub-DPS/Support):
Consistently applies Hydro to facilitate Dendro Core generation, essential for Burgeon reactions.
Mavuika (Dendro Support):
Provides consistent Dendro application, enabling continuous Dendro Core formation for Burgeon reactions.
Bennett (Healer/ATK Buffer):
Offers healing and an attack buff through his Elemental Burst, ensuring team survivability and enhanced damage.
3. Alhaitham Quickbloom
Alhaitham (Main DPS):
Utilizes Dendro abilities to trigger Quicken reactions, increasing damage potential.
Furina (Sub-DPS/Support):
Applies Hydro to enable Bloom reactions, which can lead to Hyperbloom when interacting with Electro.
Nahida (Dendro Support):
Provides consistent Dendro application and enhances Elemental Mastery, boosting reaction damage.
Kuki Shinobu (Electro Healer):
Applies Electro to trigger Hyperbloom reactions and offers healing, maintaining team health and maximizing damage output.

Furina (Sub-DPS/Support):

Xilonen (Sub-DPS):
Offers additional damage and elemental reactions, complementing the team's offensive strategy.
Bennett (Healer/ATK Buffer):
Provides healing and an attack buff, ensuring team survivability and increased damage output.
5. Clorinde Electro-Charged
Clorinde (Main DPS):
Focuses on Electro attacks to trigger Electro-Charged reactions when combined with Hydro.
Furina (Sub-DPS/Support):
Applies Hydro consistently, facilitating Electro-Charged reactions and providing a DMG bonus.
Jean (Anemo Healer):
Offers team-wide healing and can utilize Anemo abilities to Swirl elements, increasing overall damage.
Ororon (Electro Support):
Provides additional Electro application, enhancing the frequency of Electro-Charged reactions.
6. Wriothesley Furinational

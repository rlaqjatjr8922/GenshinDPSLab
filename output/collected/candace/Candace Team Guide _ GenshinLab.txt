Candace Team Overview
This Page provides the best Candace Team information. Candace will be Support DPS in most of the situation.
Candace best team can be formed in different ways including Permafrost, Vaporize or Electro-Charged, Bloom and Hyperbloom team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

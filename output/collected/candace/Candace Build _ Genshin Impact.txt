Candace Overview
This Page provides the Best Candace Build information.

Candace is a the guardian of Aaru Village in Sumeru.

Candace is a Sub DPS with supportive function in Genshin Impact. Candace DPS Build and Support Build are generally accepted by most travelers. Her elemental burst infuses active character with Hydro Elements.

In Genshin Impact, Candace is a 4 Stars rarity Hydro character currently in  tier.

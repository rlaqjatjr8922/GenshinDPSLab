Candace Works Best as Normal Attack DPS Support
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Normal Attack DPS Support
★★★★★
- Candace is great with DPSes that use Normal Attacks since she can buff the damage dealt based on the amount of HP she has.
- Damage dealers whose Element is the same as Candace's or can react with Hydro are best to use with her since she can hold Cinder City set to buff the Elemental Damage dealt by the team.
▼ Lunar-Charged DPS Support
★★★★☆
- At C6, Candace can be viable Hydro teammate option for
Flins
or
Ineffa
thanks to her AoE Hydro application.
- In Flins' case, it's best to only use Candace in this team if Ineffa is on the team already due to his reliance on Ascendant Gleam for his personal DPS.
▼ Hyperbloom Team
★★★☆☆
- Candace can be flexed into a Hyperbloom team that consists of an Electro or Dendro On-Field DPS.
- With the Hydro infusion with her Burst, Candace can enable characters like Shinobu or Yaoyao to be On-Field reaction drivers.
- Her C6 allows her to be the On-Field driver instead.
▼ Nilou Bloom Team
★★☆☆☆
- Nilou Bloom team with Yaoyao as On-Field driver.
- Yaoyao's Burst needs her to be On-Field so that it can attack enemies with Dendro.
- Hydro infusion from Candace's Burst allows Yaoyao to generate Blooms with Hydro-infused Normal Attacks.
Candace can buff Elemental Damage of Normal Attacks as well as the damage dealt. When equipped with a support set, she can be used in various teams but is best at supporting Normal Attack damage dealers.

6

Rotation Of Lunar-Charged DPS Support (Flins & Ineffa) Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Ineffa and use her Skill. Burst is optional.
2
Switch to Candace. Skill into Burst.
3
Switch to Sucrose and use her Skill or Normal Attack (x1) into Burst.
└
If you're using
Wandering Evenstar
, do Normal Attack into Burst instead so you can use her Skill at the end of the rotation.
4
Switch to Flins. Use his Skill twice and then his Special Burst whenever it's up.
└
His ideal combo will be:
Skill (x2) -> Normal Attack (x3), dash -> Normal Attack (x4) -> Special Burst -> Normal Attack (x4) -> Skill -> Special Burst -> Normal Attack (x3)
5
Swap back into Sucrose to re-use her Skill.
6
Repeat from Step 1.

Candace is another addition to a list of niche support characters in
Genshin Impact
. She should be paired with characters that deal Normal Attack DMG. Other than that, she also has a utility that no one can use as of now: Hydro Infusion. While Hydro Infusion sounds appealing, there’s currently no character that can utilize it. The only Hydro units that can get the infusion are Xingqiu and
Genshin Impact
Ayato
, but Xingqiu doesn’t fit as the main DPS while Ayato already infuses his own sword.
Nevertheless, Candace’s Normal Attack DMG buff is still viable on the battlefield, and she can also use it for herself with the following Candace builds in
Genshin Impact
.

Candace Best Build In Genshin Impact
Candace is a rather unique unit in
Genshin Impact

best

For now, players can build Candace as a support, sub-DPS, or main DPS.
Best Build For Support Candace In Genshin Impact
Candace can be a great support for characters that rely on their Normal Attack. She enhances her team’s Normal Attack DMG, scaling with her HP.
Artifact For Support Candace
Artifact Set
4pc Noblesse Oblige
2pc Tenacity of the Millelith + 2pc Emblem of Severed Fate
The best artifact set for support Candace in
Genshin Impact
is easily
Noblesse Oblige
. Not only will this enhance her own Elemental Burst DMG (which is most prominent at C6), but she will also grant an ATK buff to her entire team.
Other than Noblesse, people can also opt for
Tenacity of the Millelith
to increase Candace’s HP. Pair it with
Emblem of Severed Fate
for easy access to Energy Recharge.

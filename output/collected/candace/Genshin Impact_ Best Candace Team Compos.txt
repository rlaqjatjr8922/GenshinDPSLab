Candace in
Genshin Impact
currently doesn’t have a pair that she works best with. The buff she offers isn’t bad at all, but she doesn’t support better than older support characters. This, plus the fact that people will need to work on raising her, easily makes Candace a unit that sits on the bench.
Related:
Genshin Impact: Best Spiral Abyss Team Compositions
However, for those who have prepared for Candace or simply like her enough to use her without caring about maximizing a team’s performance, then here are some examples of the best Candace team composition in
Genshin Impact
.
C6 Candace + Cyno + Yun Jin + Zhongli
Cyno in
Genshin Impact
is a selfish DPS
that will dominate most of the field time. That’s why his teammates should consist of those who don’t need much time on the field and can be swapped out easily. In this Candace team comp, she will buff Cyno’s Normal Attack along with Yun Jin. Meanwhile, Zhongli exists to protect the main DPS, trigger Geo Resonance, and reduce the enemy’s RES.
Team Roles
Candace
: Buffer (4pc Noblesse Oblige), Hydro enabler (C6)
Yun Jin
: Damage buffer
Cyno
: Main DPS
Zhongli
: Shielder, debuffer, buffer (4pc Tenacity of the Millelith)
C1 Candace + Kamisato Ayato + Yun Jin + Kazuha
This Candace team comp is one where she supports Hydro units like Kamisato Ayato. Like last time, the combination of Yun Jin and Candace will enhance Ayato’s damage to a large number. The last slot can be filled with another damage-enhancing support. People can use units like Kaedehara Kazuha, Beidou, Fischl, or even
Genshin Impact
Venti
.

C1 Candace + Sangonomiya Kokomi + Dendro MC + Dori
To focus less on Yun Jin, this Candace team comp will instead highlight the
Dendro Elemental Reaction in
Genshin Impact
. Players can use C6 Candace here also for extra Hydro application.
The main DPS in this team would be any Hydro damage dealer like Sangonomiya Kokomi, Kamisato Ayato, or Tartaglia. Use either Dendro MC or Collei for the Dendro application, and lastly, Beidou, Dori, or Kuki Shinobu to trigger Hyperbloom.

C1 Candace + Yun Jin + Kazuha + Bennett
If Travelers wish to have fun with Candace’s Hydro Infusion and play her as a carry, then she can be paired with Yun Jin, C6 Thoma, and a buffer like Bennett or Kaedehara Kazuha. Since Candace will work as the main DPS, Bennett’s ATK buff will come in handy, along with the Pyro Resonance triggered by Thoma and Bennett. Thoma also enhances Normal Attack DMG if players own his C6.

: Buffer (4pc Noblesse Oblige), healer
C1 Candace + Yoimiya + Bennett + Yun Jin
To put Hydro aside, players can also use Candace with ranged Normal Attack damage dealers like Yoimiya. Bring Yun Jin for more damage buff, and the last slot can be filled by any
Genshin Impact

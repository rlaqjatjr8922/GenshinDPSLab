Candace Gameplay
Candace’s gameplay revolves around
enhancing Normal Attacks through Hydro infusion and team buffs
. Her kit focuses on providing utility rather than consistent elemental application, making her a niche but valuable support in teams that rely on Normal Attack–focused playstyles. While she is not designed to be a primary DPS, her ability to empower teammates allows her to fit into specific compositions effectively.
Her Elemental Skill, Sacred Rite: Heron’s Sanctum, allows Candace to
block incoming attacks and release a Hydro strike
, dealing damage while generating energy for her Elemental Burst. This skill also provides minor defensive utility, helping her sustain herself in combat.
When Candace uses her Elemental Burst, Sacred Rite: Wagtail’s Tide, she grants the party
Hydro infusion to melee characters’ Normal Attacks and increases their damage
. This effect enables teammates to trigger reactions like Vaporize or Bloom in certain setups, though her Hydro application remains limited compared to dedicated Hydro supports.
Candace’s passive talents further enhance her role by
scaling her buffs with Max HP
, making HP stacking essential for maximizing her effectiveness. With proper Energy Recharge and HP investment, she can consistently provide supportive buffs while enabling situational elemental reactions.

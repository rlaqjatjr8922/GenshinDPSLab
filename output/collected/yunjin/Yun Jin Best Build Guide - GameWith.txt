Yun Jin is a 4-star Geo character that wields a Polearm in Genshin Impact. Her gameplay focuses on being a supportive off-field enabler and burst shielder, enhancing Normal and Charged Attack effectiveness for her team. Her Elemental Skill, "Opening Flourish," blocks damage when combined with a Normal Attack and deals AoE Geo damage, generating shielded transit. Her Elemental Burst, "Cliffbreaker Chant," creates an energized shield that absorbs damage based on Yun Jin's DEF and converts Normal and Charged Attacks into Geo damage, granting increased AoE power to allies' attack strikes nearby. Lore-wise, Yun Jin is the graceful troupe director of the Yun-Han Opera Troupe in Liyue, renowned for her refined performances, strong sense of aesthetics, and caring mentorship of upcoming young performers.

Roles & Playstyle Of Yun Jin and Its Strengths and Weakness
Normal Attack Support Yun Jin
Yun Jin's main role in a team is to be the Normal Attack buffer for the On-Field character. While she's Elemental Burst-reliant, she has a relatively lower Energy cost compared to other Burst-reliant Supports.
Pros and Cons of Support Yun Jin
Pros
- Requires only 2 stats to be built (Defense and Energy Recharge).
- Has 0 Elemental Application so she can't interfere with Elemental Reactions.
Cons
- Her lack of sustain ability makes her less used since other supports have sustain and damage buffing abilities.
Best Build for Yun Jin
Yun Jin is a Burst-reliant support whose buff scales on her DEF. Since there's few Defense weapons for Yun Jin, her best artifact set is Husk set since it can increase her Defense while her weapon is Favonius Lance to generate Energy for the whole team.

Team Rotation of Yun Jin & Combos
Basic Rotation for Support Yun Jin
Basic Rotation of Support Yun Jin
1. Use the abilities of other Supports/Sub-DPS characters with longer buff duration.
2. Switch to Yun Jin and use her Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
└
If the DPS character has a high Energy cost on their Burst and needs to use it, Yun Jin should use her Skill after her Burst to funnel Energy.
3. Switch to the On-Field character.
Yun Jin's buff duration is a bit shorter compared to most supports so she should be used last before the On-Field DPS. Additionally, her buff has a trigger quota so if the DPS attacks multiple enemies, it can run out fast.
Basics Of Using Yun Jin
Elemental Burst Reliant Support for Normal Attack DPS
Yun Jin is a support that can increase the Normal Attack damage dealt by the active character. Her buff is based on her Defense and comes from her Elemental Burst. This means she needs both Defense and Energy Recharge when building her.
Use Favonius Lance for Energy, Footprint Of The Rainbow for Buff
Favonius Lance is a good all-purpose weapon that can be used by Yun Jin. However, you can use the
Natlan craftable polearm
for her Burst's buff.
Weapon Effects
Favonius Lance
- Energy Recharge 30.6%
- CRIT hits has a chance to generate Energy Particles.
Footprint Of The Rainbow
- Increases Defense by 51.7% and 32% (from using Elemental Skill) for 15s.
Has a Counterattack Elemental Skill with Tap and Hold Version
▲ Video showing Yun Jin's Counterattack being triggered (left side) and the normal version of the Skill on the right.
Yun Jin's Elemental Skill is a counterattack that activates when hit while Holding it. She can gain more Particles when successfully counterattacking or holding her Skill long enough for Charge Level 2 but it's not recommended for fast rotations.
It is somewhat difficult to time her Skill use to immediately use Charge Level 2 attack for Particles since the margin of error is very small.
2nd Passive Increases Buffs Based on Team Elements
Yun Jin's 2nd Passive can increase the buff she gives to her teammates depending on the team composition. If there's 4 different elements, it is increased by 11.5%. See below for the full list of her buff increase:

Elemental Skill, Burst and Abilities Guide for Yun Jin
How To Use Yun Jin's Skills & Passives?
How To Use?
▼ DMG Support for Normal Attack DPS
▼ Elemental Burst Reliant for Buff
▼ Elemental Skill is a Counterattack
▼ Uses Charge Level 2 Skill Attack if hit while using Skill
▼ Gains more DEF if there's 4 Elements in the team
DMG Support For Normal Attack DPS
Yun Jin is a buff support for Normal Attack DPS characters like Arlecchino and Hu Tao. She's best with a second Geo character if the DPS scales on their ATK so that Geo resonance can be used to buff their ATK as well.
Elemental Burst Reliant For Buff
Elemental Burst Effect
- Deals AoE Geo DMG and grants all nearby party members a
Flying Cloud Flag Formation
.
Flying Cloud Flag Formation:
- When Normal Attack DMG is dealt to opponents, Bonus DMG will be dealt based on Yun Jin's current DEF.
(58% of Yun Jin's DEF at Skill Level 10)
- The effects of this skill will be cleared after a set duration or after being triggered a specific number of times.
(12s Duration and Trigger Quota of 30 at any Skill Level)
- When one Normal Attack hits multiple opponents, the effect is triggered multiple times according to the number of opponents hit. The number of times that the effect is triggered is counted independently for each member of the party with Flying Cloud Flag Formation.
Burst Energy Cost
: 60
Burst Cooldown
: 15s
Yun Jin's buff comes from her Elemental Burst, meaning she will need decent Energy Recharge to ensure her Burst uptime. This makes Favonius Lance her best weapon.
Elemental Skill Is A Counterattack
Yun Jin's Elemental Skill can be tapped to deal damage or held to hold up a shield and then deal a counterattack if hit. While holding her Skill, she can increase its Charge Level and gain more Energy Particles if you reach Charge Level 2.
Uses Charge Level 2 Skill Attack If Hit While Using Skill
Yun Jin's Passive makes her immediately use Charge Level 2 attack if she gets hit when you use her Skill (including Charging it). This can help shorten her field time but most of the time, tapping her Skill is fine for Energy purposes.
Gains More Def If There's 4 Elements In The Team
Yun Jin is able to gain 11.5% more Defense if there's 4 Elements in the team. However this can be disregarded if you have other sources of damage buffing.
Should You Pull for Yun Jin's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim for C6 if You're Able To
Constellation Priority
C6 > C4 > C2
C6 Is Ideal But Not Necessary
At C0, Yun Jin will be functional in her niche as a Normal Attack DMG buffer, but the buffs she contributes to her teams can be further enhanced by obtaining her constellations, with C6 being her best possible performance without considering weapons, artifacts and future content.
Each level of her constellations boost the buffs she can give by a marginal amount, so getting her to C6, apart from giving a decent Attack Speed bonus, combines all these small increases into a significant upgrade from C0 overall.
But while it is ideal to try for Yun Jin's C6 if you have the means to do so, she will still be usable at any constellation level regardless.

Effect

The number of times that the effect is triggered is counted independently for each member of the party with Flying Cloud Flag Formation.

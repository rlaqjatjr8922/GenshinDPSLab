Mizuki, the 6th Anemo Catalyst user, has finally made her way to Genshin Impact. She is the newest featured character and also a unit that will become available in the standard banner after the end of Version 5.4. Similar to other Anemo Catalyst users, Mizuki relies on the Swirl effect to deal damage. If you want to compare Mizuki to any other Anemo Catalyst user, then the closest comparison would be Sucrose. Both of these units are great at Swirling and almost any team that works on Sucrose will work for Mizuki as well!

Mizuki Best Teams
Mizuki is honestly an interesting unit. While both her Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst deal almost no damage even with Swirl, she still requires a ton of field time for the Elemental Skill to take effect. In addition, her Elemental Skill prevents Mizuki from attacking, which greatly locks her team options, as both Yelan and Xingqiu are no longer a viable option. Neither is Beidou, and Fischl’s C6 also becomes completely useless. This in itself greatly reduces Mizuki’s usefulness and stops her from being a decent swirl driver. Having said that, some teams can still use her. You can check them out below.

This team relies on the Taser effect to allow Mizuki to reliably swirl both Hydro and Electro to deal damage. While this is not the most optimal taser team, it is what works for Mizuki and her Elemental Skill. In this team, you will generally want to go through all of the three other characters’ skills and then swap back to Mizuki and activate her Elemental Skill, and then just float around.

Honorable Mention
Lastly, it is also worth mentioning that Venti is a decent team option for Mizuki. Since Mizuki’s kit allows her to boost all swirl damage, and not only her own, she can also boost the swirl reactions that Venti creates. This can make for some pretty fun teams and is great against multiple enemies, though it will inevitably feel weak against a single target.

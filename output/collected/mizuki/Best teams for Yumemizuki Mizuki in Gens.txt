Best teams for Yumemizuki Mizuki in Genshin Impact
Who gets along best with Mizuki?
Christine Choi
| Feb 10, 2025 4:51 am
Screenshot via Genshin Impact YouTube
Yumemizuki Mizuki is an on-field Swirl DPS in
Genshin Impact
, and her unique kit means there are some quirks to her team setup. Here are the best teammates for Mizuki in
Genshin Impact
, sorted by element.
Recommended Videos
Mizuki’s teambuilding requirements
There are a couple of things to keep in mind when choosing teammates for Mizuki. Firstly, she
needs something to trigger Swirl with
, so
Pyro, Hydro, Electro, and Cryo
teammates are essential. Secondly,
Mizuki is
unable to use Normal Attacks
while in her Dreamdrifter state, so teammate abilities can’t rely on Normal Attacks to function. Let’s look at the best characters in
Genshin Impact
who meet these requirements.
Best teammates for Mizuki
In general, you can mix and match teammates as you wish, as long as they deal some form of off-field Pyro, Hydro, Electro, or Cryo damage, However, a few specific characters stand out as great partners for Mizuki.
Screenshot by Destructoid
Electro-Charged Mizuki with Furina
The first notable mention is
Furina
, who deals significant off-field damage and buffs the entire team. Mizuki’s healing also helps to offset Furina’s health-drain mechanics. Combine Furina with Electro characters like
Fischl
or
Yae Miko
for a powerful Electro-Charged comp.
Mizuki’s Pyro teams
Xiangling
(paired with Bennett) makes for a great Pyro option. Make sure to stand in Bennett’s Burst circle while casting Xiangling’s Burst, and her Pyronado will keep his buff for its entire duration.
Mavuika
is also a strong option. Add Furina or any other elements you desire to get some reactions going.
Non-Swirlable teammates for Mizuki
Mizuki can’t Swirl Dendro, but
Emilie
, Nahida, or Dendro Traveler can be interesting additions to a Burning-based team.
Xilonen
can help provide buffs and additional RES shred. I recommend only having one teammate with a non-Swirlable element (Anemo, Dendro, Geo) so that Mizuki can still consistently trigger Swirl with her other teammates.
Image via HoYoverse

The “other teammates” column has characters who can technically work with Mizuki, but have significant caveats like low damage or redundant roles. My personal favorites are in bold. Mizuki’s teams are quite flexible, so feel free to experiment and have fun!

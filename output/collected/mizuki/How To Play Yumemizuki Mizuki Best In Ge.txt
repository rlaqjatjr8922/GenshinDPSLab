Yumemizuki Mizuki is a 5-star Anemo unit that was added to the game with
Genshin Impact
version 5.4. She is an interesting unit as she primarily functions as a Swirl support, but she also has the ability to heal allies and deal damage as an on-field driver.

However, her base kit has a few limitations, which, in turn, are fixed by her constellations. Since she will be added to the standard banner, players can get her constellations later on. If you are interested in pulling for Yumemizuki Mizuki, here's a detailed guide that will help you build her appropriately.
Best Yumemizuki Mizuki Weapons
Yumemizuki Mizuki is a Catalyst user, and she
primarily relies on the Elemental Mastery stat
to boost her damage and healing. This means any Catalyst that can provide a lot of Elemental Mastery, either through substats or passive will work.
However, you should also look for some Energy Recharge so that you can use her ultimate ability much more frequently.

A Thousand Floating Dreams
Five star
This weapon also

the elements of the rest of the party, the former
receives a massive boost
to Elemental Mastery up to a maximum of three times.
This means you will need to have multiple Anemo characters in the party
to make the most out of this weapon. However, even in the base state,
it is incredibly good for Yumemizuki Mizuki.
Sacrificial Fragments

If you are looking for an affordable optional, Sacrificial Fragments is a good
4-star choice for Yumemizuki Mizuki.
It provides Elemental Mastery
through

refunding the cooldown of her Elemental Skill.
Mappa Mare
Four star
This is a completely free-to-play option for Yumemizuki Mizuki. You receive
Elemental Mastery from the secondary stat
and a boost to her Elemental DMG.
This weapon is decent, but you shouldn't go for it unless you have no other
option.
Yumemizuki Mizuki feels quite clunky as a unit at Constellation zero and starts getting better at higher Constellations. This means if you are planning to build her, going for Constellation 2 or Constellation 4 will be worth it.
Best Yumemizuki Mizuki Artifacts
Since
Yumemizuki Mizuki is an Anemo unit that relies on Swirl and Elemental Mastery
, the choice of her artifact is quite simple. However, if you do not want to go for her best 4-pc set, you can also look for a 2-pc set and combine it with something else. Regardless of what you go for, make sure to keep a look at her substats.
Here are the best artifact choices for Yumemizuki Mizuki:
Viridescent Venerer (4-pc)
Being an Anemo unit, this artifact set is the best one for Yumemizuki Mizuki.
It
provides Anemo DMG Bonus
and also boosts Swirl DMG which synergizes
with Mizuki's kit. Additionally, it
also reduces the RES of enemies
, which comes
in handy, especially in the Spiral abyss.
Noblesse Oblige (4-pc)
Suppose you want to
build Mizuki as an all-purpose support
while using a second Anemo
character who already uses the Viridescent Venerer. In that case, Noblesse Oblige is a good
secondary choice. It provides a
boost to Elemental Burst DMG
while also increasing
the ATK of the entire party.
Wanderer's Troupe (2-pc)
and Gilded Dreams (2-pc)
If you don't have a good 4-pc Viridescent Venerer set, you can use a
combination of
Wanderer's Troupe and Gilded Dreams for the time being. Both of these artifact sets
provide Elemental Mastery as a 2-pc bonus and if you have good substats on them, they
can be used until you farm the 4-pc set.

When it comes to stats, everything about Yumemizuki Mizuki
revolves around Elemental Mastery,
including both her damage and her heals. This is why, regardless of the artifact set you go for,
try to push her Elemental Mastery as much as possible
to maximize her kit.

Best Yumemizuki Mizuki Team Composition
When building teams for Yumemizuki Mizuki,
you will be looking for units that can benefit from Swirl
. Additionally, Mizuki performs best when used as an on-field driver, at least in Constellation zero. As such, any team where the characters can swap out after using their abilities and help Mizuki deal damage by Swirling all the elements will work.

Yumemizuki Mizuki Talent Priority
Since Yumemizuki Mizuki is a hybrid unit, you have to rely on both her ultimate ability as well as her Elemental Skill. This is because the
Elemental Skill deals damage
, while the
ultimate provides heals or damage
depending on whether the HP is below or above 70%.
However, in terms of priority,
you should first level up her Elemental Skill
and then
follow it up with her Elemental Burst
. You don't need to level up her Basic ATK unless you really like the character.

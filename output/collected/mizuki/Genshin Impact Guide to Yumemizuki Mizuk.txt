Best Build for Yumemizuki Mizuki
Best Weapons
You have multiple options for choosing the right weapon for Mizuki, depending on whether you want to focus on her Elemental Mastery, Energy Recharge, or both.
Best 5-Star Weapons
Sunny Morning Sleep-In:
This is Mizuki's
signature weapon
. It increases her Elemental Mastery and provides an ATK boost when triggering Swirl. It is probably your best choice as it offers the highest synergy with Mizuki's playstyle, which is highly dependent on Swirl.
A Thousand Floating Dreams:
It increases Elemental Mastery and provides team buffs based on elemental reactions. This weapon is a very good alternative to "Sunny Morning Sleep-in" because it maximizes Elemental Mastery while also providing significant buffs to teammates.
Best 4-Star Weapons
Sacrificial Fragments (Best F2P Choice)
Effect
: High Elemental Mastery and a chance to reset Elemental Skill cooldown.
Why It's Great
: Ensures continuous uptime for her Elemental Skill, making her a better Swirl enabler.
Mappa Mare
Effect
: Increases Elemental Mastery and grants an elemental damage bonus upon triggering reactions.
Why It's Great
: A solid choice for maximizing Swirl damage.
Favonius Codex
Effect
: High Energy Recharge with a chance to generate particles upon CRIT hits.
Why It's Great
: Helps with Burst uptime if Energy Recharge is an issue.
Best Artifacts
Mizuki's performance largely depends on the artifact set used. The best artifact choices enhance her Swirl damage and provide team-wide utility.
Best Artifact Sets
Viridescent Venerer (4-piece) – Best Overall
2-piece: +15% Anemo DMG Bonus.
4-piece: Increases Swirl DMG by 60% and reduces enemy resistance to the Swirled element by 40%.
This is the best overall set for Mizuki as it maximizes her Swirl damage while boosting the entire team's elemental damage.
Instructor (4-piece) - Best for Team Support
2-piece
: +80 Elemental Mastery.
4-piece
: Increases the team's Elemental Mastery by 120 when triggering an Elemental Reaction.
If Mizuki is built purely as a reaction enabler, this set significantly enhances team synergy.
Noblesse Oblige (4-piece) - Hybrid Support Option
2-piece
: +20% Elemental Burst DMG.
4-piece
: Increases all party members' ATK by 20% for 12s after using an Elemental Burst.
This set is an effective one if you want Mizuki to provide healing and ATK buff to the whole team.
Artifact Stat Priority
To optimize Mizuki's effectiveness, prioritize the following stats:
Sands
: Elemental Mastery (Best) or Energy Recharge (if Burst uptime is low)
Goblet
: Anemo DMG Bonus
Circlet
: Elemental Mastery or CRIT Rate/DMG (if focusing on Swirl damage)
Substats Priority
: Elemental Mastery > Energy Recharge > CRIT Rate/DMG > ATK%
Best Team Compositions
Mizuki shines in teams that benefit from Swirl reactions. Here are some of the best team setups:
Swirl Driver Team
Mizuki (Anemo – Swirl Driver)
Xingqiu or Yelan (Hydro – Off-field DPS)
Bennett (Pyro – Attack buff & healing)
Fischl or Raiden Shogun (Electro – Off-field Electro application)
This is an excellent team since Mizuki can use Swirl on the elements applied by other characters, maximizing team-wide damage. This team is the best suited for AoE damage.
Electro-Charged Swirl Team
Mizuki (Anemo – Swirl enabler)
Furina (Hydro – Off-field Hydro application)
Fischl or Yae Miko (Electro – Off-field Electro application)
Kazuha or Sucrose (Additional Anemo support for Swirl damage boost)
This team is a highly effective one as you can apply constant Hydro and Electro, allowing Mizuki to trigger Electro-Charged and Swirl continuously.
Freeze Swirl Team
Mizuki (Anemo – Swirl enabler)
Ganyu or Ayaka (Cryo – Main DPS)
Furina or Xingqiu (Hydro – Freeze enabler)
Kazuha or Sucrose (Additional Anemo support)
With the help of this team, Mizuki can amplify Cryo and Hydro applications, keeping enemies frozen while dealing extra Swirl damage.
F2P-Friendly Team
Mizuki (Anemo – Swirl driver)
Xiangling (Pyro – Off-field Pyro application)
Barbara (Hydro – Healer and Hydro enabler)
Lisa (Electro – Free Electro application)
This team, although uses free characters, still maximises Swirl reactions.

Best Playstyle & Rotation
Use Mizuki's Elemental Skill to start applying Anemo and generate Swirl reactions.
Switch to Elemental Reaction Characters (Hydro/Electro/Pyro/Cryo) to set up elemental applications.
Use Mizuki's Elemental Burst to enhance Swirl damage and provide healing.
Trigger more Swirls and reactions by maintaining her Elemental Skill and swapping between team members.
Repeat Rotation and ensure consistent uptime on Swirl effects.

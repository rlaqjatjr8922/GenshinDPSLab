Yumemizuki Mizuki's Strengths & Weaknesses

Has their own self sustain that can also deal damage or heal other party members.

Weaknesses

4.
How to Play Yumemizuki Mizuki
Yumemizuki Mizuki is incredibly easy to play, her Elemental Skill
Aisa Utamakura Pilgrimage
allows Mizuki to enter the Dreamdrifter state. This allows Mizuki
to drift around and regularly deal
Anemo
every 0.75s while
swirling every other hit.
This also increases the
Swirl
based on Mizuki's
Elemental mastery. Mizuki is also unable to attack while in the Dreamdrifter state.
All damage from Mizuki comes in the form of damage from her Elemental Skill. This
state is extended when Swirls are triggered up to a total of 10 seconds in
the state.
Yumemizuki Mizuki's Elemental Burst
Anraku Secret Spring Therapy
drops snacks on the ground near Mizuki.
Anyone in the party can collect these snacks which have different effects based
on their HP.
HP > 70% - Deals AoE
Anemo
DMG
HP < 70% - Provide healing based on Mizuki's EM
Snack will also explode, dealing AoE
Anemo
DMG if ignored
If Mizuki picks up the snack, the healing effect is doubled. This provides Mizuki
with a form of self-sustain while on-field.
To play Mizuki, her rotation would be the following:
Use supports and swap to Mizuki
Use
Aisa Utamakura Pilgrimage
Use
Anraku Secret Spring Therapy
and float around while picking up snacks

Mizuki's best teams are generally based around
Aggravate
and
Electro-Charged
(taser) reactions. Mizuki has great
synergy with characters such as
Furina
because Mizuki can
generate Fanfare. At the same time Mizuki should not be played with
characters who attack when Mizuki attacks such as
Xingqiu
because Mizuki is unable to attack in the Dreamdrifter state.

6.3.
Yumemizuki Mizuki's Constellations
With Yumemizuki Mizuki being a Standard Banner character starting in
patch 5.5 i would not advise aiming for many constellations as you can gain
these over time. If you do want to invest into Mizuki consider starting
with her weapon first as that may be harder to obtain in the future.
Mizuki's best constellation is generally her C2 which provides a
significant DMG increase for PHEC units while in her Dreamdrifter state.

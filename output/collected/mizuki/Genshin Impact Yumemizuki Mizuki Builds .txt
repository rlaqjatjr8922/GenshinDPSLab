Yumemizuki Mizuki Gameplay
Mizuki is a healer who can also deal decent damage to opponents. Her Elemental Skill can make her
enter a
Dreamdrifter
state where she floats above the ground and
deal AoE Anemo DMG to nearby opponents at regular intervals. At the same time, the
Swirl DMG
that nearby party members deal will be increased based on Yumemizuki Mizuki's Elemental Mastery. Her Elemental Burst can
pull in nearby objects and opponents, dealing AoE Anemo DMG. And at regular intervals,
Yumemikaze Specialty Snack
will be created. Depending on the picker's HP, The Specialty Snack can explode and deal AoE Anemo DMG or heal the one who picked it up.

Yumemizuki Mizuki fulfills two key roles in battle: amplifying

Prioritizing her Elemental Skill

Best Weapons
Whether you're looking to boost Mizuki's Swirl damage or enhance Mizuki's healing, Elemental Mastery is the most important stats. Therefore, prioritize equipping her with a
EM weapon
. Additionally, since her healing relies on her Elemental Burst, you can also consider a
ER weapon
to help her unleash it more frequently.

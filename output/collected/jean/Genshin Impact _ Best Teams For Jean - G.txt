Jean Works Best As Anemo Support
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Anemo Support Jean
- Jean's ability to apply Anemo to trigger Swirl makes her a viable holder for VV to support PHEC damage dealers.
- Her powerful healing makes her great to pair with Furina who needs healing to offset her teamwide HP drain and Fanfare stacking.
- Jean can be a pure healer instead and hold another set if someone else is using the Scroll set in the team.
2nd
▼ Sunfire Melt/Vaporize
- Makes use of the Swirl interaction of Jean and Bennett's Burst that continuously applies Pyro to enemies near the active character within the Burst range.
- Any Hydro or Cryo DPS can be used to trigger Vaporize or Melt from the Pyro applied.
3rd
▼ Anemo DPS Support Jean
- Requires C4 Jean to shred Anemo RES.
- Since getting Jean's C4 can be difficult for newer players, this team is ranked lower than her other two teams
Jean is an Anemo healer with amazing healing abilities and can hold VV to shred Elemental RES
since she can apply Anemo with her Skill and Burst. She's best used as a VV holder for supporting a PHEC DPS, but she can also be used for applying Pyro via the "Sunfire" interaction of her Burst and Bennett's Burst.
C4 Jean Has Another Team
Summary of Jean's Constellation 4 Effect
All enemies within Jean's Elemental Burst's range will have their Anemo RES reduced by 40%.
Jean's Constellation 4 effect allows her Burst to shred 40% Anemo RES on enemies within its field. This enables her to act as an alternative to Faruzan as an Anemo support, though Jean's support ability is weaker than Faruzan's.

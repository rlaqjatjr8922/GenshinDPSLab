– Flins is a 5-star Electro Polearm character from Nod-Krai, debuting in Genshin Impact Version Luna I Phase 2. Known for her unique Lunar-Charged mechanic, Flins can trigger a stronger version of Electro-Charged that allows CRIT damage. This ability gives her exceptional potential as a main DPS in Electro reaction teams. Additionally, part of Flins’ ATK converts into Elemental Mastery, making her build flexible and efficient.
Below is a detailed guide on the best Flins build, including her top weapons, artifacts, and team compositions to help you optimize her potential.
Best Weapons for Flins
Choosing the right weapon is crucial to maximizing Flins’ performance. Here are the best options:
Bloodsoaked Ruins
After using an Elemental Burst, Flins’ Lunar-Charged reaction DMG increases by 36% for 3.5 seconds. Each time she triggers a Lunar-Charged reaction, she gains +28% CRIT DMG for 6 seconds and restores 12 Energy. This effect activates every 14 seconds, ensuring both consistency and power.
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
Provides an ATK Bonus based on 52% of Flins’ Elemental Mastery. When her Elemental Skill hits an enemy, it grants an additional 28% ATK Bonus, stacking up to three times. Perfect for hybrid builds focused on both EM and raw damage.

Primordial Jade Winged-Spear
Increases ATK by 3.2% when hitting enemies, stacking up to seven times for a 12% total DMG boost. It’s reliable and pairs well with her consistent attack frequency.
Prospector’s Shovel
Boosts Electro-Charged DMG by 48% and Lunar-Charged DMG by 12%, with an additional 12% boost from the Moonsign: Ascendant Gleam effect. A solid 4-star option for players who lack premium weapons.
Fractured Halo
After using Elemental Skill or Burst, ATK increases by 24% for 20 seconds. When generating a shield during this effect, all nearby party members gain +40% Lunar-Charged DMG, making it great for team synergy.
Deathmatch
Boosts ATK and DEF by 16% if surrounded by two or more enemies, or +24% ATK if fewer. Excellent for consistent stat scaling in combat-heavy scenarios.
Best Artifacts for Flins
There are a few recommended artifact sets depending on your playstyle and availability.
4-Piece Night of the Sky’s Unveiling
When allies trigger a Lunar reaction, Flins gains Gleaming Moon: Intent for 4 seconds. Depending on the Moonsign state, she gains +15% to 30% CRIT Rate. Each unique Moon effect among party members increases the team’s Lunar reaction DMG by 10%, making it her best-in-slot set.
4-Piece Thundering Fury
Increases Electro-Charged, Overloaded, Superconduct, and Hyperbloom DMG by 40%, and Aggravate DMG by 20%. It also reduces Elemental Skill cooldown by 1 second whenever triggering reactions, providing a more aggressive rotation.
4-Piece Marechausse Hunter
Boosts CRIT Rate by 12% when HP changes, stacking up to three times. Excellent for Flins builds that rely on HP fluctuation or sustained combat.
Recommended Main Stats:
Sands: ATK%
Goblet: ATK%
Circlet: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG
Recommended Sub-stats:
Energy Recharge, ATK%, CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, and Elemental Mastery.
Best Team Compositions for Flins
To unleash Flins’ full potential, she should be the Main DPS supported by characters who can trigger consistent reactions and provide off-field damage:
Main DPS: Flins
Sub DPS: Yelan / Xingqiu / Aino
Reaction Support: Kuki Shinobu / Ineffa
Support / Crowd Control: Sucrose
Flins works best when her team focuses on Lunar-Charged and Electro-related reactions. Ensure she has enough Energy Recharge to use her Burst consistently, as her main damage output scales from it.
Optimizing Flins’ Stats and Playstyle
As a Lunar-Charge DPS, Flins thrives with high ATK and Energy Recharge. Her kit doesn’t require full reliance on Electro DMG Bonus, as most of her output comes from reaction multipliers. Prioritize ATK% and CRIT stats, then allocate leftover sub-stats toward Elemental Mastery for extra reaction damage.
With the Night of the Sky’s Unveiling set, her CRIT Rate requirement drops by up to 30%, allowing you to focus more on CRIT DMG and Energy efficiency.

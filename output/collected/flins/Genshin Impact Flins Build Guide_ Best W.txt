In this Flins guide, we’ll cover the best Flins builds, weapons, artifacts, and teams. Of course, we’ll also look at his stat priorities and general tips to help you get the most out of one of the best characters in the meta right now.
Thanks to his strong Elemental Burst damage and flexible weapon choices, Flins is a top-tier on-field damage dealer in Lunar-Charged teams. Of course, it helps that he’s very beginner-friendly in terms of gameplay.
Genshin Impact
6.4: Flins Overview and Playstyle
Image Credit: HoYoverse
Flins is an on-field Electro DPS, and his gameplay is all about triggering Lunar-Charged reactions. This reaction is a special variation of Electro-Charged introduced with Nod-Krai characters, and one of its most important features is that it can crit, which increases its damage.

He can enter a special state through his Elemental Skill, which converts his attacks into Electro and unlocks a special version of his Elemental Burst. During this state, Flins deals repeated Lunar-Charged damage while reacting with Hydro teammates.
Most of his damage comes from:
Elemental Burst damage
Direct Lunar-Charged damage
Reaction damage triggered through Hydro application
Because of this design, Flins works best when used with teammates who can maintain Hydro application, generate Energy, and buff reaction damage.
As for his personal rotation, that’s pretty simple:
Activate Elemental Skill to enter his special combat state.
Use the enhanced Skill to deal AoE Electro damage.
Trigger the special Burst version for Lunar-Charged damage.
Continue attacking to trigger additional reactions.
The majority of his DPS is during these short on-field windows, so you’ll want good off-field support.
Flins Talent Priority
Image Credit: HoYoverse
When upgrading Flins’ talents, you’ll want to focus on the ones that increase his damage first.
Talent Priority
Elemental Burst – Highest Priority
Elemental Skill – Second Priority
Normal Attack – Lowest Priority
Flins’ Elemental Burst is the largest portion of his total damage output. His enhanced Burst versions deal multiple instances of Lunar-Charged damage, which scale extremely well with proper buffs and stats.
The Elemental Skill is also important because it enables his special combat state and allows him to access his short Burst. Leveling this talent increases the damage of those abilities and improves his overall rotation.
Normal Attacks are the least important because their multipliers are relatively low and are mainly used to fill downtime and generate energy particles rather than deal damage.
Best Weapons for Flins
Image Credit: HoYoverse
As is the case with most DPS characters in Genshin Impact, Flins wants weapons with CRIT and ATK. And of course, due to his reaction-based kit, he wants weapons with Elemental Mastery, or Lunar-Charged damage bonuses as well.
Best-in-Slot Weapon
Bloodsoaked Ruins (5-Star)
Flins’ signature weapon is his strongest option because it directly boosts Lunar-Charged damage while also solving some of his Energy issues.
It provides:
High base ATK
CRIT Rate
Bonus Lunar-Charged damage after casting his Burst
Energy restoration after triggering reactions
These bonuses perfectly complement his Burst-focused playstyle.
Other 5-Star Weapons
Many of the other 5-Star Polearms work on Flins if you don’t have his signature weapon.
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
A strong alternative that converts Elemental Mastery into ATK, improving both reaction damage and direct damage.
Primordial Jade Winged-Spear
Provides CRIT Rate and stacking ATK bonuses, making it a reliable general DPS option.
Fractured Halo
Has strong stats and an ATK buff after using Skills or Burst, but its shield-related passive is not always fully utilized.
Staff of Homa
This weapon has always been a good stat stick for any Polearm character, and Flins is no different.
Best 4-Star Weapons
If you’re playing free-to-play or don’t have 5-star weapons, these options still perform very well.
Deathmatch
One of the best 4-star weapons for Flins, thanks to its high CRIT Rate and ATK bonus.
Prospector’s Shovel
A craftable Nod-Krai weapon that increases both Electro-Charged and Lunar-Charged damage.
Ballad of the Fjords
Provides Elemental Mastery when multiple elements are present in the party, which is easy to achieve in reaction teams.
Missive Windspear
Offers ATK and Elemental Mastery bonuses after triggering reactions, making it a solid budget choice.
Best Artifacts for Flins
Image Credit: HoYoverse
With the right Artifact set, you can maximize Flins’ Lunar-Charged damage.
Best Artifact Set
Night of the Sky’s Unveiling (4-Piece)
This set is currently Flins’ best artifact set.
Set bonuses include:
Elemental Mastery increase
Up to
30% CRIT Rate
Bonus Lunar reaction damage for the team
Since Flins’ kit is all about Lunar-Charged reactions, this set is overall the best.
Alternative Artifact Sets
If you don’t have the best set yet, these can work temporarily:
Thundering Fury
Provides Electro damage and reaction bonuses, but it’s less effective for Lunar-Charged damage.
Marechaussee Hunter
Works well in certain teams that have HP drain, which is mainly with Furina.
2-piece sets
You can also use a combination of ATK%, EM, or Energy Recharge sets if you have nothing else to work with.
Best Artifact Main Stats
Sands:
ATK%
Goblet:
ATK%
Circlet:
CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG
Note:
ATK Goblets perform better than Electro DMG Goblets because Lunar-Charged damage does not scale with Electro DMG bonuses.
Substat Priority
Focus on the following stats when farming artifacts:
CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG → Energy Recharge → ATK% → Elemental Mastery
Recommended Stats for Flins
Image Credit: HoYoverse
For optimal performance, try to reach the following stat thresholds.
ATK: 2500+
Energy Recharge: 120–140% (with another Electro teammate)
CRIT Rate: around 50-60% before artifact buffs
CRIT DMG: 175% or higher
Elemental Mastery: 100–200
Flins gains Elemental Mastery from his passive based on his ATK, so building enough ATK helps boost his reaction damage indirectly.
Energy Recharge is also important because his Burst is central to his rotation.
Best Teams for Flins
Image Credit: HoYoverse
Flins works best in Lunar-Charged teams, which require both Electro and Hydro elements.
For most of his teams, your setup will look something like this.
Flins + Electro Support + Hydro Support + Utility/Anemo Support
These team setups have constant reaction triggers, energy generation, and enemy resistance reduction.
Best Overall Team
Flins / Ineffa / Columbina / Sucrose
This team maximizes Lunar-Charged damage.
Flins is the main DPS, while Ineffa provides Electro particles, shielding, and reaction buffs. Columbina applies Hydro and has big buffs for Lunar reactions, and Sucrose boosts Elemental Mastery while shredding enemy resistances.
Alternative Premium Team
Flins / Ineffa / Yelan / Sucrose
Yelan replaces Aino as a Hydro applicator while adding some decent off-field damage.
While her damage buff is less impactful on Lunar-Charged damage, her Hydro application still enables consistent reactions. You can also use Furina or Xinqiu as the Hydro applicator.
F2P-Friendly Team
Flins / Fischl / Aino / Sucrose
This team replaces Ineffa with Fischl.
Fischl has excellent Electro particle generation and off-field damage, and it will lower Flins’ Energy requirements. She also has strong reaction uptime.

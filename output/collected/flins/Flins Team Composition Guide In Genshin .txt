Flins Team Comp In Genshin Impact
Flins is a main DPS character that needs field time in
Genshin Impact
. He also needs teams that continuously trigger the Lunar-Charged reactions while he's on the field. Finally, you need to pair Flins with characters that can buff his personal DMG alongside the Lunar-Charged reactions. So, here are some of Flins' best teams in
Genshin Impact
:
Full Lunar-Charged Flins Team:
Flins + Aino + Ineffa + Yelan
Nod-Krai Flins Team:
Flins + Aino + Ineffa + Lauma
F2P Flins Team:
Flins + Aino + Xingqiu + Sucrose
Flins + Aino + Ineffa + Yelan
There's no doubt that Aino is an essential part of every Flins' team in
Genshin Impact
. One of the reasons is that Aino is a Nod-Krai F2P 4-star character who triggers the
Moonsign: Ascendant Gleam
buff, which greatly takes Flins' DMG to the next level. She can also apply a lot of Hydro while not even on the field. On the other hand, in this Flins team, you can add Ineffa, who can also transform Electro-Charged reactions into Lunar-Charged ones. Only Ineffa greatly buffs those reactions while offering
shields for her team in
Genshin Impact
.
The last spot is a flexible one, as you have already established a strong Flins team in
Genshin Impact
. So, add a character that can add something to the team. Many characters are fit for the spot, like Yelan, who can offer an insane off-field Hydro application and DMG while buffing Flins' DMG. You can also add
Anemo characters like Sucrose
, who can increase the team's Elemental Mastery while lowering enemies' resistance to Electro and Hydro, or Kazuha, for increased Electro and Hydro DMG through his Swirls.
Team Roles
Flins:
Main DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler
Aino:
Support, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Ineffa:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler
Yelan:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Flins + Aino + Ineffa + Lauma
Flins can have a full team from
Nod-Krai in
Genshin Impact
. Aino provides the off-field Hydro enabling while Flins also helps with triggering Lunar-Charged reactions through his kit and Electro attacks. On the other hand, Lauma synergizes with both units to create Lunar-Bloom reactions that will also affect the Hyperbloom reactions that Flins and Ineffa will trigger after.
It's a Lunar engine team where each one of the four Nod-Krai characters will participate with their stats to trigger an endless amount of Quicken, Aggravate, and Spread; but most importantly, Lunar-Bloom, and
Lunar-Charged reactions in
Genshin Impact
.
Team Roles
Flins:
Main DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler
Aino:
Support, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Ineffa:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler
Lauma:
Support, Buffer, Dendro Enabler
Flins + Aino + Xingqiu + Sucrose
Building an F2P Flins team in
Genshin Impact
is easy, as you only need good off-field
Hydro characters to match his on-field time
. When using Flins' Elemental Skill, he'll infuse his Polearm with Electro and will start spreading Electro among enemies. This is where a character like Aino or Xingqiu is essential, since they offer the off-field Hydro application that will continuously trigger the Lunar-Charged reactions. Not to mention, each one of Aino's constellations is a blessing to Flins' teams in
Genshin Impact
.
After establishing the synergy between Flins and Aino, the last two spots are flexible for some
F2P characters in
Genshin Impact
. Including Xingqiu adds another layer of Hydro application that can help the team trigger more Lunar-Charged reactions. As for Sucrose, her Swirl reactions will keep moving the Electro and Hydro auras around enemies, triggering more Lunar-Charged reactions while shredding enemies' resistance to Electro and Hydro. She will also buff the team's Elemental Mastery, which will also increase their Elemental reaction DMG.
Team Roles
Flins:
Main DPS, Buffer, Electro Enabler
Aino:
Support, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Xingqiu:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Sucrose:
Support, Buffer, Debuffer

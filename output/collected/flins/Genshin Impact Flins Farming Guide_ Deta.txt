Location of Flins’s Ascension Materials in Genshin Impact
Mob Drops
The materials required for Flins’s progression include the
Broken Drive Shaft, Reinforced Drive Shaft, and Precision Drive Shaft.
Precision Drive Shaft (Image via HoYoverse)
These items can be obtained from
Landcruisers
enemies scattered across
Nod Krai
. You’ll need these materials for Flins’s Ascension and Talent upgrades, so it’s a good idea to farm extra for future use.
Local Specialty
Genshin Impact Frostlamp Flower Location (Image via HoYoverse)
To
Ascend Flins
, you’ll also need to gather material called
Frostlamp Flower
, a Local Specialty from
Nod Krai.
Like other Local specialists, Frostlamp Flower respawns every
48 real-life hours
, so it could take up to a week to collect enough to ascend Flins fully.
Boss Drops
Genshin Impact Knuckle Duckle Boss Location (Image via HoYoverse)
You will need
46
total boss drops to ascend Flins to level 90. The boss she needs is called “
Knuckle Duckle
,” which is located in Nod Krai.
Mora
In addition, you’ll need
420,000 Mora
to complete all of
Flin’s Ascension phases
, plus an additional
1,637,400 Mora
to level him up using
Character EXP Materials
fully. Be sure to challenge
Blossoms of Wealth
to gather more Mora. If possible, take on these challenges in
Nod Krai
, as they often feature the Mob bosses required to ascend the character.

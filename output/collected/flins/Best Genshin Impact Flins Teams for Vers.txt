Best Genshin Impact Flins Teams for Version 6.0
Jennifer Davis
1. October 2025
Summary:
Genshin Impact version 6.0 has introduced Flins, a new 5-star Electro polearm character hailing from the Nod-Krai region.
Flins has several gameplay mechanics and passive abilities that require specific characters as his teammates, like his Moonsign mechanic and the Lunar-Charged reaction.
Flin’s premium team includes the most recent five-star support/sub-DPS Ineffa, while his F2P options include Aino, Sucrose, and Ororon.
Thinking about pulling for or building Flins in
Genshin Impact
? This new 5-star Electro polearm character from Nod-Krai debuts in Version 6.0 on a limited banner. With high damage and the Lunar-Charged reaction mechanic, he’s already seen as one of the strongest DPS characters in the game. To get the most out of him, here’s a guide to the best Genshin Impact Flins teams that can help you clear tough endgame content more easily.
Why Flins Needs the Right Team
Flins’ whole playstyle is built around the Lunar-Charged reaction
, which deals Electro damage but works differently from normal reactions. Instead of scaling with Electro Damage Bonus, his damage relies more on ATK and CRIT stats. This makes support characters who lower enemy resistance or boost Elemental Mastery very useful.
Since Flins is meant to stay on the field as a main DPS, he needs strong off-field supports that let him maximize his uptime. To unlock his best effects, he also needs
at least one other Nod-Krai character
for the new Moonsign mechanic, which gives him extra buffs when stacked.
Premium Lunar-Charged Team
The Premium Lunar-Charged Team is the strongest option for
Flins
and best suited for players with access to limited 5-star units.
Composition:
Flins (Main DPS)
– Core damage dealer.
Ineffa (Sub-DPS/Support)
– Provides shields, Energy regeneration, and Elemental Mastery buffs.
Sucrose (Support)
– Best with Viridescent Venerer and Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers for resistance shredding and EM and ATK buffs.
Aino (Hydro Support)
– Enables consistent Hydro application and activates Moonsign buffs at C6.
In this team, Ineffa and Sucrose boost Flins’ damage, while Aino adds Hydro to trigger constant reactions. This combo lets Flins hit safely and deal massive damage at the same time.
Free-to-Play Lunar-Charged Team
Not everyone has access to premium characters, but there are also Genshin Impact Flins teams that are free-to-play. Here’s the strongest composition with 4-star characters.
Composition:
Flins (Main DPS)
– Stays on-field to trigger reactions.
Aino (Free Hydro Support)
– Provides Hydro application and Moonsign buffs.
Anemo VV Holder (Sucrose/Lan Yan)
– Either option is best with Viridescent Venerer and Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers. Sucrose is the more offensive option while lan Yan can sustain with her shield.
Electro or Hydro Sub-DPS (Ororon, Fischl, Xingqiu, or Shinobu)
– Adds extra damage and helps maintain reactions. Ororon is the best Electro option while Xingiu is the best for a Hydro option. If you need more sustain, Shinobu also works.
Even without Ineffa, this team is solid because Aino is free, and the remaining two slots can be occupied by characters who are fairly easy to build.
Hyperbloom Team Option
If you don’t have Ineffa but got Lauma, you can try a
Hyperbloom-focused Flins team
. While not as strong as Lunar-Charged comps, it still delivers consistent damage.
Composition:
Flins (Electro Main DPS)
Dendro Sub-DPS (Lauma, Nahida, Baizhu, or Collei)
– Applies Dendro for Dendro reactions.
Hydro Support (Aino, Yelan, or Xingqiu)
– Provides Hydro application.
Flex Support (Sucrose, Fischl, or Kuki Shinobu)
– Adds buffs, healing, or extra Electro damage.
This version mixes Hyperbloom with Lunar-Charged, giving Flins more versatility if you don’t have Ineffa. What’s important is that either Aino or Lauma needs to be in the team for Moonsign to work.
How to Maximize Flins’ Teams
Here are some tips to get the most out of your
Genshin Impact Flins Teams
:
Always pair Flins with at least one
Nod-Krai character
(like Ineffa or Aino) to activate
Moonsign buffs
.
Prioritize supports who boost
Elemental Mastery
and shred
enemy resistances
.
Meet Flins’ stat goals: around
2,000 ATK
,
130–140% Energy Recharge
, and balanced
CRIT Rate/DMG
.
Use weapons like
Bloodsoaked Ruins
or
Staff of the Scarlet Sands
to further amplify his Lunar-Charged damage.
Conclusion
Flins works best in teams that can keep up with his Lunar-Charged reactions. The strongest
Genshin Impact Flins
teams usually focus on a Premium Lunar-Charged lineup with Ineffa, but you can also build budget-friendly versions using Aino and other common supports. If you don’t have Nod-Krai characters, Hyperbloom setups are another good option.
With the right teammates, Flins can match the power of top-tier DPS characters in Version 6.0. So, which team are you planning to build for Genshin Impact’s newest Electro 5-star?

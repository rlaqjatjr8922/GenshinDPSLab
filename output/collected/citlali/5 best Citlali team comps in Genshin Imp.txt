5 best Citlali team comps in Genshin Impact
Discuss Now
This article lists the best teams for Citlali in Genshin Impact (Image via HoYoverse)
Citlali is a new Cryo character released in
Genshin Impact 5.3
. Her main role in any team is that of a shielder, but she also has decent damage potential, if built well. Citlali is a Catalyst user who works best as an off-field Cryo applicator and shielder.
Ad
Since Citlali's A1 passive provides Pyro and Hydro RES shred (for opponents) specifically for teams that can trigger Freeze or Melt reactions, there are a few teams where she shines particularly well. This article takes a look at Citlali's best teams in Genshin Impact.

Top 5 Citlali team compositions in Genshin Impact
1) Citlali, Mavuika, Xilonen, Bennett
Ad
Trending
Citlali, Mavuika, Xilonen, Bennett (Image via HoYoverse)
This is Citlali's best Melt team, wherein
Pyro Archon Mavuika
functions as the main DPS and the other three as supports. Citlali's strong Cryo application can ensure that Mavuika will constantly be triggering powerful Melt reactions.
Ad
Furthermore, Citlali synergizes well with Mavuika, as she can not only buff the latter but also generate Nightsoul points pretty fast for her Elemental Burst. Mavuika is further buffed by Xilonen and Bennett in this setup, making this a really strong team for both overworld and Abyss usage in Genshin Impact.
2) Citlali, Arlecchino, Rosaria, Bennett
Citlali, Arlecchino, Rosaria, Bennett (Image via HoYoverse)
Since Citlali buffs and provides RES Shred for Pyro DPS characters and teams, a team with
Arlecchino as the main DPS
is also a great option. Arlecchino's fast attack speed combined with Citlali's Cryo application will ensure that Melt reactions are constantly being triggered.
Ad
This, in turn, will reduce opponents' Pyro RES, giving Arlecchino an even better opportunity to wipe out all enemies. Rosaria plays the role of a Cryo sub-DPS in this team, while Bennett can greatly boost Mavuika's overall damage.
3) Citlali, Yelan, Yoimiya, Bennett
Citlali, Yelan, Yoimiya, Bennett (Image via HoYoverse)
Yoimiya functions as the main DPS in this team. Being a Pyro unit with relatively fast attacks, she can comfortably trigger Melt reactions while also taking advantage of the Pyro RES shred and buff provided by Citlali.
Ad
Yelan's role in this team is that of a sub-DPS, who can also help trigger Vaporize reactions in conjunction with Yoimiya. Overall, if you want to play a VapeMelt team with Citlali, then this lineup is your best option. Make sure you equip the 4-piece Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City artifact set on Citlali.
4) Citlali, Chasca, Furina, Bennett
Citlali, Chasca, Furina, Bennett (Image via HoYoverse)
Although Chasca is neither a Pyro nor a Hydro DPS, she can work very well when paired with Citlali. Despite being an Anemo character, the former is capable of dealing multi-element damage.
Ad
Due to Furina and Bennett's presence in the team, Chasca's damage will already be greatly enhanced. Moreover, since Citlali can provide both Pyro and Hydro RES shred, Chasca's bullet damage will be increased even further.
5) Citlali, Neuvillette, Furina, Kazuha
Citlali, Neuvillette, Furina, Kazuha (Image via HoYoverse)
Citlali is also a strong support option for a Neuvillette hypercarry team. Neuvillette is often paired with Zhongli due to his shielding and RES Shred. Citlali can now replace Zhongli in that role in this particular team.
Ad
Apart from providing Neuvillette with a shield, Citlali can also buff him quite well, provided she is equipped with a 4-piece Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City artifact set. If you want to play Neuvillette with a shielder, pairing him with Citlali in this team (with Furina and Kazuha as supports) is a good choice.

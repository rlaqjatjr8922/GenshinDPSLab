Citlali is a 5-star Cryo character that wields a Catalyst in Genshin Impact. She is a Support Character who provides a Shield to protect her allies, she uses Nightshoul points to charge Itzpapa that will start dealing Cryo damage, has a unique debuff on enemies that can increase Hydro and Pyro damage dealt. Lore-wise, Citlali is a legendary shaman from the Masters of the Night-Wind, known in Natlan as the eminent "Granny Itztli".

What Kind of Character is Citlali?
Citlali is an Off-Field Cryo Support that provides a Shield and Cryo Application in an area around the active character.
She's a Natlan character that enters a Nightsoul's Blessing State after using her Skill. She can also gain and consume Nightsoul Points from her abilities and passives. Her summon, Itzpapa requires these points to be able to generate the Cryo field that applies the element to nearby enemies.
Citlali's place in the current meta is very much in the upper half of the best teams because she's Mavuika's ideal partner, and together they make-up  the strongest team in terms of damage as of the current version.
She's also very versatile and can be paired with other Pyro DPSes that want to trigger Melt, Hydro DPSes that can benefit from Freeze, or Cryo DPSes that need the fourth slot in the team to be filled.

Roles & Playstyle of Citlali and Her Strengths and Weakness
Off-Field Support Citlali
Citlali performs at her best in teams that appreciate her wide Cryo application, specifically in Melt or Freeze comps.
Pros and Cons of Off-Field Support Citlali
Pros
- Easy to play.
- Provides additional sustain and interruption resistance through her shield.
- Cryo Application from Itzpapa follows the active character instead of being stationary.
Cons
- Her shield isn't strong enough to be the main sustain option.
- Sometimes her Cryo application can be too slow to maintain consistent Melts, which requires you to wait a bit between attacks.
- Isn't as good with Cryo carries due to her Ascension 2 passive only shredding Pyro and Hydro RES.

Basic Team Rotation for Off-Field Support Citlali
Basic Rotation For Off-Field Support Citlali
1. Start with supports with longer ability durations. If the DPS requires you to start the rotation with their Skill (like
Arlecchino
), then feel free to do so.
2. Swap to Citlali then use her Elemental Skill into Burst.
3. Swap to your On-Field DPS to do their full damage combo.
4 Return to step 1.
Citlali's rotations are very simple and easy to follow.
You want to ensure she can use her Skill and Burst at the same time since this gives her around 50 Nightsoul Points, allowing Itzpapa to generate the Cryo storms that apply Cryo to enemies.
Basics Of Using Citlali
Citlali is an Off-Field Cryo Support that applies Cryo, shreds Hydro and Pyro Elemental Resistance while also providing a Cryo Shield as a small bonus.
Her Elemental Skill summons Itzpapa, who will continuously generate a Frostfall Storm that deals AoE Cryo Damage provided Itzpapa maintains its Opal Fire state by having 50 Nightsoul points. Citlali's Elemental Burst calls upon her allies Citlalin and Itzpapa (again), dealing more AoE Cryo Damage and creates Spiritvessel Skulls for up to 3 nearby opponents. These skulls will eventually explode and deal more Cryo Damage.
Citlali Has a Nightsoul's Blessing State
Like all Natlan characters, Citlali has a Nightsoul's Blessing State, which she enters after activating her Elemental Skill.
Every Natlan character receives Nightsoul Points after entering the state. Citlali receives 24 Nightsoul Points after using her Skill.
Itzpapa needs 50 Nightsoul points to enter the Opal Fire State in order to create Citlali's signature Frostfall Storms that apply Cryo to enemies in an AoE around the active character. The Frostfall Storms consume up to 8 Nightsoul Points a second, which means you must keep generating points for Citlali to apply Cryo.
Citlali's Elemental Burst Restores Nightsoul Points
Itzpapa wants 50 Nightsoul points to apply Cryo, but Citlali's Elemental Skill only generates 24 on cast.
This is why it's important to be able to use her Elemental Burst per rotation, since it also helps her generate more points. The initial cast of Citlali's Burst does AoE Cryo damage while generating 24 points. The Spiritvessel Skulls also not only deal Cryo damage to a maximum of 3 nearby enemies, but also restores 3 Nightsoul Points for every Skull.
Citlali's Passives Help Build More Points
Citlali generates extra Nightsoul Points through her passive abilities.
These unlock as she ascends or levels up. Her first passive (unlocked at Ascension 2) grants 16 points every 8 seconds whenever a party member triggers a Frozen or Melt reaction.
Her second passive (unlocked at Ascension 4) restores 4 points each time a Natlan character activates a
Nightsoul Burst
, which happens when Elemental Damage is dealt by a party that consists of at least 1 Natlan character.
Citlali Is Best with a Pyro or Hydro On-Field DPS
Citlali's Ascension 2 Passive, Mamaloaco's Frigid Rain, allows her to shred enemy Hydro and Pyro Resistance so long as anyone in the party (including herself) trigger a Melt or Freeze reaction. This makes her synergistic with Pyro and Hydro On-Field DPSes, She can still be used with Cryo DPSes like
Skirk
, but not to the same effectiveness.

Effect

Holding Aiming Mode will cause Citlali to enter the Spiritspeaker state, which will allow her to interact with Nightspirit Graffiti and Nightspirit Sigils, extracting information and power from them. Additionally, holding the jump button will cause Citlali to consume 75 Stamina and leap. In areas within Natlan where Phlogiston mechanics are present, Citlali will prioritize consuming 5 Phlogiston to leap to a height higher still.
When in the air, Citlali can Hold her Normal Attack to consume Phlogiston or Stamina and enter Aiming Mode and trigger Charged Attacks. When initiating Charged Attacks in mid-air, Citlali will prioritize consuming 20 Phlogiston.

Who Is Citlali?
A Renowned Shaman from Natlan
Citlali is a well-known and highly respected member of the Masters of the Night-Wind. Considered as a "living legend" due to how long she has lived, her expertise and knowledge as a Shaman is unmatched within the tribe.
Rendered Immortal for Reasons Unknown
It was revealed that Citlali has lived a longer life than most. Mavuika, the Pyro Archon, mentions that she has known Citlali for at least 200 years and did not expect her to stay cooped up in her house for so long.
The Revered Granny Itztli
Many of the young generations from the Masters of the Night-Wind revere and fear Citlali, giving her the moniker of "Granny Itztli." Ororon, who Citlali raised herself when he was abandoned by his parents, calls her fondly as Granny.

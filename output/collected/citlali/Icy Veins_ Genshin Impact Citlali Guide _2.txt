Citlali Guide & Best Builds
Welcome to our Citlali build guide for Genshin Impact.
Citlali
is a 5-Star
Cryo
dedicated support character who
focuses on
Melt
and
Freeze
reactions. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to
know to get the most out of Citlali, from the best artifacts and weapons
to important stat priorities and rotations.
Best Weapons for Citlali
Listed below are Citlali's best weapon options. As a support character,
Citlali focuses primarily on providing shielding,
Cryo
application, and team
buffs rather than personal damage. Because of this, weapons that provide
Elemental Mastery, Energy Recharge, or team-wide buffs tend to perform the
best for her overall utility.
Starcaller's Watch
Offering Unto Wind and Sun
Base ATK:
542
Elemental Mastery:
265
Increases Elemental Mastery by
100 / 125 / 150 / 175 / 200
. Gain the "Mirror of Night" effect within 15s after the equipping character creates a shield: The current active party member deals
28% / 35% / 42% / 49% / 56%
increased DMG to nearby opponents. You can gain the "Mirror of Night" effect once every 14s.
Starcaller's Watch
is Citlali's signature weapon and her best-in-slot option.
It provides a massive amount of Elemental Mastery, which strengthens her shield,
while also granting the active character up to 28% additional damage when they
are protected by Citlali's shield. This makes it an extremely strong option for
buffing your main DPS while also allowing Citlali to focus on Energy Recharge
through her artifacts.
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
Heritage
Base ATK:
401
HP:
35.2%
When switching characters, the new character taking the field has their ATK increased by
24% / 30% / 36% / 42% / 48%
for 10s. This effect can only occur once every 20s.
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
is the best F2P option for Citlali. It grants
a massive 48% ATK buff to the next character you swap into after Citlali,
making it an excellent choice when paired with ATK-scaling DPS characters such
as
Mavuika
or
Arlecchino
. While the buff does not have full uptime every
rotation, it remains one of the strongest support catalysts available.

.
Etherlight Spindlelute
is another great option for Citlali, providing Energy
Recharge through its main stat and Elemental Mastery through its passive.
This combination allows Citlali to easily burst every rotation while also
strengthening her shield, making it a very comfortable and consistent option.
Wandering Evenstar
Wildling Nightstar

Wandering Evenstar
is a strong supportive option, especially at higher
refinements. It provides Elemental Mastery for Citlali, which increases her
shield strength, while also granting an ATK buff to nearby teammates based on
her Elemental Mastery. This makes it a great hybrid option when you want both
shield strength and additional team buffs.

A Thousand Floating Dreams

as much as it benefits reaction-based characters, but the raw Elemental Mastery
still helps increase the strength of her shield.

Best Artifacts for Citlali
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
2-Piece Set
: When a nearby party member triggers a Nightsoul Burst, the equipping character regenerates 6 Elemental Energy.
4-Piece Set
: After the equipping character triggers a reaction related to their Elemental Type, all nearby party members gain a 12% Elemental DMG Bonus for the Elemental Types involved in the elemental reaction for 15s. If the equipping character is in the Nightsoul's Blessing state when triggering this effect, all nearby party members gain an additional 28% Elemental DMG Bonus for the Elemental Types involved in the elemental reaction for 20s. The equipping character can trigger this effect while off-field, and the DMG bonus from Artifact Sets with the same name do not stack.
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
is Citlali's best-in-slot artifact set. It
generates additional Energy and provides up to a 40% damage bonus to the team
based on the reactions triggered by Citlali. This makes it the go-to option
when playing her alongside DPS characters that benefit heavily from damage
bonus buffs.
Tenacity of the Millelith
Tenacity of the Millelith
2-Piece Set
: HP +20%
4-Piece Set
: When an Elemental Skill hits an opponent, the ATK of all nearby party members is increased by 20% and their Shield Strength is increased by 30% for 3s. This effect can be triggered once every 0.5s. This effect can still be triggered even when the character who is using this artifact set is not on the field.
Tenacity of the Millelith
is another strong option for Citlali, especially if
another character in the team is already using Scroll of the Hero of Cinder
City. It provides a 20% ATK buff to the team whenever Citlali's Elemental Skill
hits an enemy. To maintain consistent uptime on this buff, you generally want
to burst every rotation so her Skill continues applying damage reliably.
Noblesse Oblige
Noblesse Oblige
2-Piece Set
: Elemental Burst DMG +20%
4-Piece Set
: Using an Elemental Burst increases all party members' ATK by 20% for 12s. This effect cannot stack.
Noblesse Oblige
is another solid support option. It provides a 20% ATK buff to
the entire team after Citlali uses her Elemental Burst. If other teammates are
already using Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City or Tenacity of the Millelith,
this set can be a strong alternative. To fully benefit from this set, Citlali
should aim to use her Burst every rotation.
Instructor
Instructor
2-Piece Set
: Increases Elemental Mastery by 80.
4-Piece Set
: Upon triggering an Elemental Reaction, increases all party members' Elemental Mastery by 120 for 8s.
Instructor
can also be used when you cannot run any of the above sets,
especially in
Melt
teams. This set provides Elemental Mastery to the team,
which can significantly increase reaction damage. Because Instructor is a
4-star set with lower base stats, it is recommended to run an Energy Recharge
Sands to ensure Citlali can still burst consistently each rotation.

Citlali’s most important stats are Energy Recharge and Elemental Mastery.

How to Play Citlali
Citlali is a dedicated support mainly used with
Pyro
and
Hydro
DPS characters.
She provides
Pyro
RES Shred when
Melt
reactions are triggered and
Hydro
RES
Shred when
Freeze
reactions are triggered, making her especially strong in
Melt and Freeze team compositions.
Her rotation is extremely simple. Start by using her Elemental Skill to
generate her shield and summon Itzpapa, then use her Elemental Burst and
immediately swap into your main DPS. Ideally Citlali should be used right
before your DPS so that all of her buffs remain active for the full duration
of the damage window.
When using
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
, make sure you swap
directly into your main DPS after Citlali to ensure the ATK buff applies to
the correct character. Citlali provides reliable off-field
Cryo
application
for
Melt
teams and can also act as a defensive shielder while holding several
support artifact sets to provide additional team buffs.

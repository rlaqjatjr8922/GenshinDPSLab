Best Teams for Citlali
Citlali Is A Support For Teams With Hydro Or Pyro Damage Dealers
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Melt Team
- Citlali is an amazing support for Melt because of her Pyro resistance shred and off-field Cryo allowing Melt to happen.
- Mavuika is the best Pyro DPS to use in this team because of her synergy with Citlali but any Pyro damage dealer will do.
- Powerful team that is one of the best team in-game.
2nd
▼ Vapemelt Team
- This team allows your character to produce either Vaporize and Melt reaction constantly.
- This team is powerful and supports Citlali's ability fairly poor Cryo infliction off-field.
3rd
▼ Freeze Team
- Make use of Hydro resistance shred and off-field Cryo of Citlali to freeze opponents.
- Less damage than Melt teams but still an amazing option especially against non-boss opponents.
Citlali has the ability to shred the Hydro and Pyro RES of enemies when teammates trigger Frozen or Melt reactions. She can also hold Cinder City set and use it to buff her teammates' Elemental damage. This makes Citlali an excellent support for such teams.
She is mainly an offensive support but also provides defensive options with her shields.

Citlali and Xilonen's Nightsoul point consumption can be used to charge Mavuika's Fighting Spirit. Xilonen and Bennett can both heal and buff the team. Mavuika is the on-field Pyro DPS.
Rotation of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mavuika and use her tap Skill.
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then Normal Attack twice to gain Nightsoul Points.
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out.
4
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst.
5
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst to apply Cryo.
6
Switch to Mavuika and use her Burst to consume Fighting Spirit.
7
Use Normal/Charged Attacks as Mavuika to deal damage.
8
Repeat from 1.
To avoid Mavuika's fast Pyro application overtaking Citlali's Cryo, you want to dash after every Charged Attack finisher move that Mavuika does. The finisher happens after Mavuika third spin on her motorbike, where she attacks an enemy with her Claymore directly. That animation is your signal to press Dash to cancel it, giving some time for Citlali to apply Cryo again while also saving a few seconds of clear time.

This team makes use of Citlali's ability to increase the DMG dealt by Pyro and Hydro. Arlecchino and Yelan are both good damage dealers. Kazuha can Swirl all elements and increase Cryo application if his Burst Swirls Cryo.
Rotation of Vapemelt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino and use her Skill to apply Pyro
2
Switch to Kazuha and use his tap Skill to Swirl Pyro
3
Switch to Yelan and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst to gain Nightsoul points
5
Switch to Kazuha and use his Burst
6
Switch back to Arlecchino and use her Charged Attack to gain Pyro infusion
7
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage. If her Pyro infusion ends, use Burst (to reset Skill cooldown) or Skill to mark enemies again
8
Repeat from 2 after buffs expire
Arlecchino's rotation can be forgiving due to her Pyro infusion remaining even after she swaps out. So while the general idea of Arlecchino's rotations should be easy, there shouldn't be any issue if you accidentally swap out of her, unlike in most teams.

Hydro and Cryo resonance can benefit HP-scaling DPS characters like Neuvillette and Furina.
Citlali's passive can decrease the Hydro RES of enemies and with Cinder City, she can also buff Cryo and Hydro damage.
Escoffier shreds Cryo RES while also contributing her own personal DPS, which can ramp up quickly alongside Furina's. Escoffier's teamwide healing also helps to stack Furina's Fanfare faster.
Rotation of Freeze Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Neuvillette's Skill
2
Switch to Furina and use her Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill into Burst
4
Switch to Escoffier to use her Skill into Burst
5
Switch to Furina then use her Burst
6
Swap into Neuvillette then use Charged Attack x1, into his Burst, then his Skill, then finally end with more Charged Attacks
7
Repeat from 1
Escoffier's Cryo application will often be enough to main the Freeze reaction, but because her Skill is purely single-target, it's nice to have Citlali's AoE Cryo application complementing it.
When facing multi-target endgame content, using Citlali's Burst is ideal, but in single-target, resorting to simply using her Skill and then normal attacking as Citlali can maybe help save a few seconds since her Burst animation is very long.

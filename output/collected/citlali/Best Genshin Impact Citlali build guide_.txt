Discuss Now
Best Citlali build guide (Image via HoYoverse)
Citlali in Genshin Impact is a new 5-star Cryo character from the Masters of Night-Wind tribe in Natlan. She is a pretty good support character who can provide a shield to protect her party members. Furthermore, Citlali can shred the enemy's Pyro and Hydro resistance, which makes her very good in the Melt and Freeze teams. Building Citlali is also quite easy since most of her kit scales from Elemental Mastery.
Ad
Without further delay, this article will list the best artifacts, weapons, talent priority, and team comps for the best Citlali build in Genshin Impact.
Best artifacts for Citlali in Genshin Impact
1) Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City is Citlali's best choice (Image via HoYoverse)

A 4-pc set of Tenacity of the Millelith is also a decent option until you can get a full set of the Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City. The 4-pc set can increase Citlali's shield strength and also allow her to buff her party member's ATK.

Best weapons for Citlali in Genshin Impact
1) Starcaller's Watch
Starcaller's Watch is Citlali's signature weapon (Image via HoYoverse)
Starcaller's Watch
is a 5-star Catalyst and Citlali's signature weapon, making it her best choice as well. The weapon is pretty amazing since it provides a ton of Elemental Mastery bonuses. Furthermore, its passive allows the wielder to buff the active character's damage.
Ad
Also read:
Best characters for Starcaller's Watch in Genshin Impact
2) A Thousand Floating Dreams
A Thousand Floating Dreams is also amazing for Citlali (Image via HoYoverse)
A Thousand Floating Dreams is an amazing 5-star alternative for Citlali. Its second stat also provides a large amount of Elemental Mastery bonus to the wielder. In addition, depending on the party members' elements, the character receives an extra Elemental Mastery bonus, or their Elemental DMG bonus is increased.
Ad
The weapon's passive also increases the entire team's Elemental Mastery, making it amazing for Citlali's Freeze and Melt team comps.
3) Sacrificial Fragments
Sacrificial Fragments is good for its Elemental Mastery bonus (Image via HoYoverse)
Sacrificial Fragments is an amazing 4-star weapon for Citlali. Not only does it provide an Elemental Mastery bonus, but it also lowers the wielder's energy requirements.
Ad
4) Magic Guide
Magic Guide is a good 3-star weapon (Image via HoYoverse)
Magic Guide is a 3-star weapon and a very good F2P weapon for Citlali. It has a pretty nice Elemental Bonus and increases the wielder's DMG against enemies affected by Hydro and Electro.
Ad
5) Mappa Mare
Mappa Mare is a decent F2P choice (Image via HoYoverse)
Mappa Mare is another decent F2P option. It has a pretty low Elemental Mastery but is more accessible than most other weapons, so you can use it until you find a better one.
Ad

Citlali's talent priorities
Citlali's talent priority (Image via HoYoverse)
You can level up Citlali's talents in the following order:
Ad
Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst > Normal Attack
Citlali's Elemental Skill is the most important part of her kit since it summons a shield and allows her to deal off-field DMG. You can level up Burst for some extra damage and ignore the Normal Attack talent since you won't need it.
Best teams for Citlali in Genshin Impact
Mavuika, Citlali, Xilonen, and Bennett (Image via HoYoverse)
Here are some of the best teams for Citlali in Genshin Impact:
Ad
Mavuika + Citlali + Xilonen + Bennett
Mavuika + Citlali + Xilonen + Rosaria/Kaeya
Neuvillette + Citlali + Furina + Xilonen
Hu Tao + Citlali + Yelan + Xingqiu
Arlecchino + Citlali + Yelan + Bennett
Because of how Citlali's kit works, she is best played in Melt and Freeze teams.

Barbara is a 4-star Hydro character that wields a Catalyst weapon. Barbara is one of the first characters that you will meet in their first journey in Teyvat. She is a beloved idol in Mondstadt, the Deaconess of the Church of Favonius, and also
Jean's
younger sister. Known for her cheerful personality and healing abilities, Barbara is also one of the top-tier support characters for both beginner and veteran players since you can build her easily.

Barbara is a Hydro Support character who works great as a healer and On-Field Reaction Driver for Hydro Reactions. Both of her Active Skills are used for healing while her Hydro Normal Attacks are the main focus for Reaction Driver Barbara. Barbara is given for free in the first few Archon Quests and makes for a great healer for beginners in the game.

Roles & Playstyle of Barbara and Her Strengths and Weakness
Healer
Bloom Driver
Healer Barbara
Barbara's whole kit is geared to being the team healer with both of her Skills being healing skills. Building her as a pure healer is a lot easier since you need only one stat while Bloom Driver build needs balancing between EM and HP.
Pros and Cons of Healer Barbara
Pros
- Easier to build as healer and needs only HP/Healing focused equipment.
- Can work entirely Off-Field and swaps in only for healing.
Cons
- Elemental Skill CD can affect healing uptime while her Elemental Burst's high cost makes it difficult to use consistently.
Bloom Driver Barbara
Barbara's Hydro-infused Normal Attacks and Elemental Skill's Hydro aura makes her a good On-Fielder for Bloom teams since she can apply Hydro easily. With the release of Nod-Krai's new weapons, Bloom Driver Barbara is a lot more viable now.
Pros and Cons of Bloom Driver Barbara
Pros
- Can heal when taking damage from Bloom damage.
- Elemental Skill aura also applies Hydro on enemies, allowing for more Dendro Core creation.
- On-Field Barbara can ensure she gets more Energy to consistently use her Elemental Burst for team healing.
Cons
- Needs both EM and HP to balance the Bloom damage and healing.

Team Rotation of Barbara and Combos
Barbara's rotation differ greatly based on the role she plays. Her Healer playstyle is mostly Off-Field while her Bloom Driver playstyle is On-Field.
Healer
Bloom Driver
Basic Team Rotation for Healer Barbara
Basic Rotation For Healer Barbara
1. Use other Support/Sub-DPS abilities.
2. Switch to Barbara and use her Elemental Skill.
3. Switch to On-Field character.
4. (Optional) Switch to Barbara and use her Elemental Burst for healing.
Barbara's team rotations have her switch in before the On-Field character. This is because
she's often used to hold Thrilling Tales and buff the ATK-scaling DPS character who is switched in after her
. But even if she's not used as the Thrilling Tales holder, her Elemental Skill's Hydro application still needs to come before the On-Field character.
Basic Team Rotation for Bloom Driver Barbara
Basic Rotation For Bloom Barbara
1. Use Support/Sub-DPS abilities.
2. Switch to Barbara and use her Elemental Skill.
3. Spam Normal or Charged Attacks to apply Hydro.
As a Bloom Driver, Barbara is the On-Fielder that applies Hydro with her Normal Attacks. She can use Charged Attacks if you need more healing per hit since Barbara's Normal/Charged Attacks allow for healing when her Elemental Skill is active.
Basics Of Using Barbara
Easy To Build Healer for Beginners
Barbara is a free character given away after progressing in Mondstadt's Archon quest. As a healer, she works very well early game and can still be used late game. Her best weapon is a 3-star Weapon that can be easily obtained from Wishing.
Use Elemental Skill for Continuous Healing and Applying Hydro
Barbara's Elemental Skill is a continuous healing aura that surrounds the active character and applies Hydro to them and the enemies that come in contact with it. If Barbara is the active character, her Normal/Charged Attacks will also heal her teammates.
Charged Attack While Her E. Skill is Active Gives More Healing
Barbara's Charged Attacks while her Elemental Skill is active generates x4 healing to her teammates. While it can consume a lot of stamina, Barbara's Passive reduces Stamina consumed during her Elemental Skill's duration.
Elemental Burst is Instant Healing
Barbara's Elemental Burst doesn't deal damage but it heals the team instantly. It has a massive amount of healing given and can overflow easily, making her compatible with Ocean-Hued Clam and Song of Days Past sets.
Has a High Energy Cost
While Barbara's E. Burst healing is very good, it comes with a very high Energy cost of 80. This requires a 2nd Hydro to be used with her if you want consistent healing from Barbara.
Needs Only HP for Healing
Barbara's healing all scales on her Max HP and Healing Bonus. Her best weapon is Thrilling Tales that can increase her HP and allow her to buff ATK for the next character who gets swapped in after Barbara.
Elemental Skill, Burst and Abilities Guide for Barbara
How To Use Barbara's Skills & Passives?
How To Use?
▼ Healer Support or Bloom Driver
▼ Elemental Skill applies Hydro and Continuously Heals the Active Character
▼ Elemental Burst is an Instant Healing
▼ Both Builds' Best Weapon is 4-Star or Lower
Healer Support Or Bloom Driver
Barbara is a Hydro Catalyst user who is great as a pure Healing support or Bloom Driver. Both builds are viable but her Bloom Driver build can only be used if you have Nilou.
Elemental Skill Applies Hydro And Continuously Heals The Active Character
Barbara's Elemental Skill provides Healing and Hydro Application. It has a lengthy CD so it's best used before the On-Field character's turn. The Skill applies Hydro to both you and the enemies. While her Skill is active, Barbara's Normal/Charged Attack has an extra effect of healing her teammates.
Elemental Burst Is An Instant Healing
Barbara's Elemental Burst is an instant Healing that can easily cap out HP bars and overflow. This makes using Song Of Days Past or Ocean-Hued Clam very nice with her. However it has a very high Energy Cost and will require good ER to use it regularly.
Both Builds' Best Weapon Is 4-Star Or Lower
Best Healer Weapon
Best Bloom Driver Weapon
Thrilling Tales Of Dragon Slayers
- HP substat and buffs ATK for the next character.
Blackmarrow Lantern

Aside from being a versatile character, Barbara is an easy character to build and doesn't require premium weapons for both of her builds. For healer build, Thrilling Tales is easy to obtain from Wishes. For Bloom Driver build, Blackmarrow Lantern is craftable and can easily be refined.
Should You Pull for Barbara's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Constellations Aren't Necessary
Constellation Priority
C2 > C3 or C5
C2 Is Decent for More Healing
Barbara doesn't gain a ton of value from her constellations in total, but
her C2 does reduce the cooldown of her Skill by a decent amount while also providing the on-field Hydro character additional an Hydro DMG Bonus while her skill persists, and it can be a good stopping point if you are looking to grab a few of her constellations.
Beyond that, either C3 and C5, which give extra levels to her Burst and Skill respectively, are also viable pickups, notably if you are equipped the
Ocean-Hued Clam
set on her. Her C6 offers a limited-use party revive, but isn't very practical due to the long cooldown.
Barbara's role as an off-field Hydro healer isn't unique, but she is still decent at it even without further investment.
Because her constellations only provide marginal improvements to a fully functional C0 kit, getting more copies of her isn't an ideal pull priority, and should instead be treated as nice bonuses to have if you happen to get them.

Who Is Barbara?
Deaconess Of The Church Of Favonius
As the Deaconess of the Church of Favonius, Barbara serves as a respected figure in Mondstadt. She leads the people in worshiping Barbatos and takes care of the church's religious artifacts.
An "Idol" From Mondstadt
Barbara is considered a star in Mondstadt. She's very popular and happy that her song can bring happiness to the people of Mondstadt. Barbara mentions that
Alice
is the one who introduced her to the concept of idols.
The Younger Sister Of Jean
Barbara is the young of
Jean
, the current acting Grand Master of the Knights of Favonius. She looks up to her sister, wanting to do something great just like her. They currently live apart as their parents are divorced.

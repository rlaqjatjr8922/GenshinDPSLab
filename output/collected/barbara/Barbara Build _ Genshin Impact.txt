Barbara Overview
This Page provides the Best Barbara Build information.
Barbara is a sister of Jean. Every denizen of Mondstadt adores Barbara.
Barbara is a healer in Genshin Impact. Barbara Healer Build is used by most of the traveler. Her elemental burst will heal all the team member even they are off field.
Barbara has different ways to help the party. Not only can she trigger the element reaction easily by normal attack, but also  she can also keep the team nice and healthy by using the Healer Build.
In Genshin Impact, Barbara is a 4 Stars rarity Hydro character currently in C tier.

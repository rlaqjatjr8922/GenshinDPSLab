Chiori is the Geo character in Genshin. Her release banner is in Version 4.6. In this post, you can learn the best advice to pull Chiori.
Chiori Strong and Powerful?
If we check her state, she is a powerful support in the team. Compared with Albedo, Chiori is the new Geo support and she can deal better damage than Albedo.
Chiori is strong if you have some good weapons and artifacts. Otherwise, she is just normal in C0.

For the Free to Play Players
If you are f2p, and planning to save some primogems for the future characters, and you have some support in the team, such as Bennett, Xingqiu, Xiangling, Sucrose, Diona, etc., saving is better than pulling.
Chiori is not the only one support, and she is bound with the Geo team. However, the Geo team is not the strong and popular team in the Spiral Abyss. So saving some primos will be better.
For the Experienced Players
You have some strong teams and some strong characters, and you also have some good artifacts or weapons. As far as I am concerned, keeping your primogems is a nice choice. Using good artifacts or weapons in the future better character could be better.
However, if you want to try something new, then pulling Chiori in C0 is enough.
For the Navia Players
If you want to build Navia as a stronger DPS in your team, you can try to pull Chiori. C0 is ok, C2 is better, C4 is the best, and C6 is overlove.

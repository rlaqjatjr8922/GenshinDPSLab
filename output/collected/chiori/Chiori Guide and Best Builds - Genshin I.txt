Chiori Guide and Best Builds
Chiori is a 5-Star Geo Sword user in Genshin Impact who deals Geo damage
from off-field via her Elemental Skill. This guide will focus on Chiori's best 
builds, artifacts, weapons and best team compositions.

The vast majority, if not all, of Chiori's damage comes from her Elemental
Skill, so it should always be prioritized. Her Burst is often skipped entirely
as it doesn't deal enough damage to warrant the field time, so it can be left
unleveled.
However, her Burst is needed to activate her C2, so at C2+ it's worth 
leveling. If using C6+ Chiori as an on-field DPS, her Normal Attacks become her
second highest source of damage, but the Defense scaling damage they gain at C6
does not scale with the talent level.
How to Play Chiori
Chiori's playstyle is focused on dealing
Geo DMG
from off-field with her
Elemental Skill, spending as little time on-field as possible. Pressing her 
Skill twice in a row will cause her to instantly swap to the next character in 
the party, allowing her to take less than a second of field time.
If Chiori isn't C1+, she does best when paired with characters that can create
Geo
constructs or enable Lunar-Crystallize, as this will grant her a second 
Tamoto doll from her Elemental Skill, greatly increasing her damage. Chiori fits 
well into teams with another
Geo
DPS unit, as they will both be able to benefit 
from
Geo
buffs together.
Chiori's Best Constellations
Chiori's C1 is a tremendous damage increase if using her without a Geo 
Construct. However, with the addition of Lunar-Crystallize, most of Chiori's
teams will already have a Geo Construct at C0, and her C1 won't increase her
damage at all. If you don't have a Construct on your team, C1 is Chiori's
biggest investment priority. If you do have a Construct, then getting her weapon
is better.
Once you have her weapon (and C1 if no Construct), this is a good
investment stopping point unless you are going all the way to C6. Her C6 is
primarily meant to turn her into a powerful on-field DPS, but it still increases
her damage as an off-field DPS by reducing her Skill cooldown, allowing it to be
cast multiple times in a rotation.

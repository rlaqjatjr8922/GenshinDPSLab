Genshin Impact Chiori Teams: Best Comps
Heizo
March 12, 2024
Tips
Share:
Genshin Impact
Chiori
, has joined the character roster, ushering in a new set of team compositions.
Chiori
is a
5-star Geo Sword Character
whose specialty lies in her
elemental skill
—unleashing an
automaton doll
capable of
delivering turret-like attacks
whether she's
on-field or off-field
. Due to her element and kit design, her team compositions are somewhat limited, but she seamlessly integrates into any Geo compositions without issue. In this article, we will explore the
best Genshin Impact Chiori teams
, aiming to help you maximize the potential of this Geo Sub DPS!

Genshin Impact Chiori Teams - Navia Double Geo
Navia
is a
5-star Geo DPS
in Genshin Impact known for dealing
frontloaded damage
to the enemies, and this makes her an ideal pair with Chiori.
While Navia is landing attacks through her
elemental skill,
and
burst
,
Chiori's automaton doll
and Xiangling's
Guoba and Pyronado
can contribute to the overall damage of the team. Additionally, the presence of
Xiangling
and
Bennett
allows the team to trigger the
Crystallization
reaction, which is required to gain the most out of Navia's kit. Finally, Bennett can take the role of a
healer and ATK buffer
.
However, a notable drawback of this team composition is the
lack of a Geo construct
. Chiori summons an additional doll, only when at least one Geo construct is nearby. Hence, you will either have to resort to
Chiori with one doll
, or try to
aim for her first constellation
. You can also find out more about her in our
Chiori Genshin Impact build
!
Itto Hypercarry - Genshin Impact Chiori Teams
One of the most effective teams for
Itto
comprises Itto himself,
Zhongli
,
Gorou
, and Albedo. However, if you require more overall damage, you can substitute Albedo with Chiori.
While Itto deals
sustained damage with his normal and charged attacks
, Chiori's elemental skill enables
coordinated
and
off-field attacks
.
It's important to note that this
mono-Geo team lacks a dedicated healer
. But,
Zhongli's shield
can provide
sustain and survivability
, preventing interruptions. As for the final slot, it's allocated to another
Geo buffer
, Gorou. Alongside the
Geo resonance buff
, which increases damage and reduces enemy Geo resistance, having three other Geo characters in the team grants them
DEF bonuses
,
increased resistance to interruption
, and
damage bonuses
from Gorou.
Genshin Impact Noelle - Chiori DPS Team
Noelle
may be a
4-star character
, but she is more than capable of bringing devastating damage to her enemies especially with the aid of Chiori,
Furina
and Gorou.
For players with
C6 Noelle
, this team composition can perform very well thanks to
Noelle's Geo infused normal attacks
during burst, Chiori and Furina's
off-field damage
. Noelle's ability to
simultaneously heal the entire team
through her shield and burst facilitates quick access to the maximum buff provided by Furina's burst. Additionally, Gorou enhances the team by
boosting Noelle and Chiori's damage
, and provides the necessary
Geo construct
for Chiori to
summon an additional Sode
. Overall, this team has a superb synergy with each other with minimal drawbacks and limitations.
Free-to-Play Genshin Impact Chiori Teams
For a
Free-to-Play Chiori Team
, consider pairing her with
Ningguang
, Gorou, and Bennett, which capitalizes on both ATK and DEF attributes. Ningguang's normal attacks and bursts, combined with Chiori's elemental skill and burst, serve as the team's
main sources of damage
. Meanwhile, Bennett and Gorou provide
damage buffs and healing support
. While Ningguang may not benefit significantly from
Gorou's DEF buff
, his
Geo damage boost
complements
both DPS characters
. On the other hand, Bennett not only
heals the team
but also provides an
ATK bonus for Ningguang and Chiori
.
Chiori Main DPS - Genshin Impact Chiori Teams
If you're aiming to build a team centered around Chiori as the main DPS, consider this
Chiori Geo composition
comprising her, Bennett,
Zhongli
, and Gorou.
Due to
Chiori's 100% uptime
on her
elemental skill
and only a
brief 5-second Geo normal attack infusion
after its activation, utilizing Chiori as a sub-DPS proves more effective than using her as the primary on-field DPS. However, this dynamic shifts with the acquisition of her
sixth constellation
.
Chiori's C6
enables the
casting of her skill again after the infusion ends
, allowing her to
prolong her on-field presence
. Moreover, her normal attack damage increases based on her DEF.
To bolster Chiori's
ATK and DEF-scaling kit
, slot in
Gorou
and
Bennett
for their
DEF buff, Geo damage bonus, ATK bonus, and healing capabilities
. Finally, Zhongli can be incorporated for
additional protection
and
resistance shred
.
That concludes our guide on the
Best Genshin Impact Chiori Teams
in the game. If you're a fan of Geo characters and enjoy the synergy within Geo teams,
Chiori
is a fantastic addition to your roster. Despite limited elemental reactions, her captivating design and flamboyant animations make her a standout choice for any team composition.

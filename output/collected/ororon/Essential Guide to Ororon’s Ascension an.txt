Upgrading Ororon’s Talents: Enhancing His Skill Set
With Ororon’s Ascension complete, players can turn their attention to advancing his Talents. Each upgrade adds incremental strength to his skills, making him a dependable asset in elemental battles. Tyrant’s Fang, a particularly elusive drop from advanced Saurians, is critical here. Building a surplus of these items can help ensure that Ororon’s attacks are fully optimized.
Each Talent level demands more investment, but the payoff in combat effectiveness is undeniable. Whether you’re aiming to strengthen his normal attacks or enhance his ultimate skill, dedicating resources to Talent development is essential to make Ororon a versatile teammate who can perform consistently under high-stakes conditions.

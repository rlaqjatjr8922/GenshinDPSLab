Genshin Impact
, Ororon might be better than what players may expect. He’s somewhat
comparable to Fischl
from a Sub DPS POV and is extremely versatile in a lot of teams.
Like most Natlan characters, activating his Nightsoul Blessing can be extremely beneficial, so it would help
Genshin Impact
players familiarize themselves better with the new Natlan mechanics and how Ororon’s Nightsoul points can be accumulated during battle.

Using Random Rotation
One thing
Genshin Impact
players should be aware of is that
Ororon shines as a Sub DPS off-field attack unit
. His kit is built to target multiple enemies and work hand-in-hand with your Main DPS. If you’re using a Hydro unit in the team (which is a must with Ororon), a rotation would go like this:
Main DPS Burst > Support Skill > Ororon Skill > Ororon Burst > Switch back to Main DPS Skill and attack
4
Not Using A Hydro Component
As free to build as any character in
Genshin Impact
should be, most characters in the game shine properly when they are used to their maximum capability. For Ororon, his specialty lies in Electro-Charged attacks and the amazing Electro DMG output they can create. This means you
need to have a Hydro character
in your team should you plan to use him.

3
Using Wrong Artifacts
Quite frankly, because of Ororon’s Sub DPS skills, players might think it’s safe to stack either ATK or Crit on him. But, because you want to keep his skill as active for as long as possible,
Ororon will need an artifact set that helps him regenerate Energy
. Using something like Thundering Fury might be alright, but
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
is
perfect for any Natlan character
. The 4-pc works wonders on the massive Elemental DMG Bonus that party members receive from the Elemental reactions that are created.
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City is also catered toward Ororon’s kit, since these effects work for off-field DMG as well. Any elemental reaction that is of the same type as the equipped character is what you’re looking for — plus, the Electro-Charged reactions further trigger his Nightsoul Blessing, creating even more damage to surrounding enemies.
2
Not Using Ororon In Exploration
Genshin Impact
players should know by now that all Natlan characters are tailor-made for exploration across the land. For Ororon, his
Elemental Skill
allows him to perform a
Double Jump and fly up into the air
. He consumes Phlogiston points when doing so, but the difference between Ororon and other Natlan characters is that when the Phlogiston bar runs out, he consumes stamina to fly instead. This makes for
longer and easier exploration across Natlan
.
1
Not Taking Advantage Of Ororon’s Nightsoul Blessing
Unlike other Natlan characters, Ororon has a rather
unique Nightsoul Blessing state
. It can be easily triggered off-field, and once he has accumulated enough Nightsoul points, Ororon’s Hypersense Effect will continuously deal Electro DMG based on his ATK to any nearby enemies. There are multiple ways in which Ororon
can obtain the Nightsoul points
and use the buffed effects:
When another party member deals Hydro or Electro DMG while Ororon’s Skill is active
When another party member triggers his Nightsoul Burst state

Genshin Impact Best Chongyun Team Comp
Here is a Genshin Impact team guide article on how to build Chongyun team comp including details of the team members and free-to-play team.
Chongyun Breakdown
In this team build, Chongyun is a sub-DPS that deals with Cryo DMG on both of his skills which create explosion AoE Cryo DMG.
Here is some team build that is suitable for him:
Chongyun Best F2P - Frost Team Comp
This free-to-play team is a great option for a player who doesn't want to spend Primogem or a newbie who doesn't have any 5-star characters. Even though, this is a F2P team you still can maximize her potential as well. You can use Chongyun for Main-DPS that can output Cryo DMG to react with Xingqiu which can attach hydro element on enemies to create a freeze reaction. You can switch to Chongyun if Chongyun is in CD and use Sucrose to group enemies so you can apply EM more easily.
Chongyun
His skill can create a Cryo explosion and summons 3 giant that can deal with AoE Cryo DMG.
Xingqiu
Can apply hydro element to attach enemies which can react with Cryo to create a freeze and provide some DMG and protection using his EM burst.
Sucrose
Apply CC DMG and group enemies together which makes it easier for DPS to deal more Burst DMG.
Chongyun Best Permafrost Team Comp
This team comp focuses on maximizing the Freezing reaction DMG by using Ayaka and Chongyun to react with Kokomi and Kazuha can group the enemies together and buff teammate DMG as well as lower enemies' RES.
Ayaka
Apply Cryo DMG by using Charge ATK or using skill or ultimate to react with Hydro attachment from Kokomi to trigger Freezing.
Kazuha
Apply CC DMG and group enemies together which makes it easier for DPS to deal more Burst DMG.
Kokomi
Deal hydro on her EM Burst and apply hydro on enemies to trigger Ayaka or Shenhe to create a freeze reaction.
Chongyun Best F2P Melt Team Comp
This team comp focuses on maximizing DMG from Xiangling pyro element to create Melt reaction from Chongyun and Xingqiu.
Xiangling
Both of her elemental skills can apply Pyro AoE DMG even if the character is off the field. Her Pyronado will move with active character making it easier to combo with Xingqiu.
Chongyun Best Superconduct Team Comp
This team comp focuses on creating superconduct from Chongyun and Diona's reaction to trigger electro from Raiden Shogun to create superconduct reaction.
Raiden Shogun
Apply electro element attached with enemies to trigger superconduct with Eula or Chongyun.
She also can boost ER and DMG by using EM burst.
Diona
Create a shield that absorbs DMG and Apply Cryo DMG on enemies.
End of Best Chongyun Team Comp.

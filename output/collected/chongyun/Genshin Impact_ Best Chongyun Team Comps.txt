Chongyun is a 4 star Cryo Claymore character
in Genshin Impact. With a lackluster kit, Chongyun is a B-tier character that rarely gets invested in. However, with the right team comp, players can definitely make him work.
In this article, Gurugamer is going to showcase the best Chongyun team comps to maximize his DPS potential.

Chongyun is a decent sub-DPS in Genshin Impact.
1. High-End Chongyun Team Comps
Chongyun's biggest strength is his low cooldown on both skill and burst, which results in very consistent cryo procs. This also makes him very easy to gear and play. Furthermore, he also has a low energy cost, near 100% uptime passives and decent damage scaling.
The biggest weakness of Chongyun is his conflict with superconduct, lack of AoE, low HP and reliance on constellations.

Chongyun is the main combo enabler of this team comp, applying Cryo first so that Klee can trigger Melt. Klee works fairly well with Chongyun in a Melt comp, as she does not have any physical damage attack and is therefore not affected by Chongyun's passive.
Venti can group enemies up for elemental combos. With his relatively fast Energy Recharge, players can swirl either Cryo or Pyro to reduce enemy resistance with the Venerer set.
Klee can contribute pretty nicely in a melt comp alongside Chongyun.
Fischl's Oz is a good source of damage when other skills are on cooldown. It can cause either Overload or Superconduct, with the latter allowing Chongyun to deal more physical damage.

Xingqiu is the main enabler of this team comp, continuously applying Hydro to opponents with his elemental burst. This reacts with Ayaka and Chongyun's Cryo application to freeze enemies.
Ayaka is the main damage dealer of the team, who deals damage with her burst. Chongyun's elemental skill (on C2) can reduce the cooldown time of Ayaka's burst. He also enables Cryo resonance for a boost to crit rate.
Ayaka can combo with Chongyun pretty well in a Freeze team comp.
Lastly, Jean is the main healer of the team, swirling Cryo with the Venerer set to reduce enemies' resistance.

This team revolves around Keqing dealing damage with her Charged attack, maximized by superconduct. Chongyun serves as a Cryo spambot alongside Diona. His Elemental Skill (requires C2) lower the CDs of Keqing's Elemental Burst.
Diona provides the team with shield and Cryo resonance. This crit boost allow Keqing to boost her Physical damage even further.
Jean is the last member of the team, who provides occasional healing with her burst. Furthermore, she can also swirl Cryo to crowd control and allow Keqing to deal more damage.
Keqing is a great Electro DPS that can be the core of a Superconduct team.

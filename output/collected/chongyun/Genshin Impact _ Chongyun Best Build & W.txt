What Kind Of Character Is Chongyun?
Chongyun is an early game Burst DPS character who can use his Skill to infuse melee weapons with Cryo. His Burst is great for oneshotting enemies if you build him right and with the correct setup. In late game, he can be used for National Team along with Xiangling and Xingqiu.
Strengths & Weakness Of Chongyun
Strengths
- Can be either main DPS or support
- Low energy cost for Elemental Burst
- Can increase Normal ATK SPD & imbue Cryo to all melee characters within his Elemental Skill range
↳ Can also decrease Elemental Skill & Burst cooldown simultaneously at constellation 2
Weakness
- When acting as main DPS, some claymore attack movements are prone to interruption
- His Elemental Skill is disruptive to other melee characters that do not need Cryo infusion
- Limited options for the main DPS that can benefit from his Skill

Chongyun Skill Guide & How To Use Chongyun

Uses Of Chongyun's Elemental Skill
Can Buff Melee Characters With Infusion
Those wielding melee weapons such as Sword, Claymore, or Polearm get a Cryo infusion when staying inside Chongyun's Elemental Skill. In addition, one of his passive talents enables his Skill to increase the Normal ATK SPD of melee characters while inside the field.
Inflict Debuff To Enemies
One of Chongyun's passive talents enables his Skill to decrease Cryo RES of the enemy if it gets hit by the falling sword at the end of its duration.
Uses Of Chongyun's Elemental Burst
Quick Burst Of Cryo Damage
Chongyun's Elemental Burst doesn't necessarily have other effects besides damaging enemies with Cryo from a distance. It is great to use whenever you need to set up Cryo-related reaction combos.
Should You Pull for Chongyun's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Any Constellation Level Is Nice to Have
Constellation Priority
C2 or C3 > C6
C2 or C3 are Good But Not Necessary
Chongyun's use-cases are very limited, but he can range from usable to decent in his own niche. His C2 is a rare constellation that can reduce Skill and Burst cooldowns within the Frost Field created by Spirit Blade: Chonghua’s Layered Frost. The exact amount of damage increase you can get from lowered cooldown is variable, but it can come in handy in specific situations. A more noticeable upgrade would be his C3, since it adds levels to his Burst, thus improving his personal damage. C6 is a flat increase to his burst damage, and is more so a nice add-on rather than a final goal.
Any constellation is a good enough stopping point, but the above mentioned are going to be the milestones.
In general, Chongyun is a character whose function and gameplay does not change with his constellations. You don't really need to actively pull more copies of him to get some usage out of his kit.
He will perform as intended at C0, and that's perfectly serviceable in the limited amount of teams that he can flex into.

Who Is Chongyun?
An Exorcist From Liyue
Chongyun comes from a family of exorcists from Liyue. What makes him such an amazing exorcist is the fact that he was born with pure yang energy. This makes spirits flee from him almost immediately.
Prone To Overheating
Due to his innate pure yang energy, Chongyun is prone to overheating. This is why he often eats cold food and wears light clothing.
Friends With Xingqiu
As an exorcist, Chongyun finds it hard to befriend others as his interests solely lie in the art. He is friends with Xingqiu who likes to play pranks to him much to his dismay.

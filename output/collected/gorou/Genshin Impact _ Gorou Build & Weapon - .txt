Gorou is a 4-star Geo character who wields a Bow in Genshin Impact. His gameplay focuses on being a dedicated support for Geo teams, especially those that scale with DEF. His Elemental Skill, "Inuzaka All-Round Defense," creates a field that provides bonuses based on the number of Geo characters in the party, boosting DEF and Geo damage. His Elemental Burst, "Juuga: Forward Unto Victory," deals AoE Geo damage and creates a "General's Glory" field that continuously buffs allies and provides "Crystal Collapse" damage bursts when characters are nearby. Lore-wise, Gorou is the loyal general of the Watatsumi Army, known for his tactical brilliance and unwavering courage, as well as his deep bond with his comrades, especially Sangonomiya Kokomi.

In battle, Gorou is a support for Geo damage dealers whose damage scales with DEF, such as Itto and Albedo. He can buff their Geo DMG, Interruption Resistance, and DEF. Since his buffs don't scale with his own stats, he can focus on building Energy Recharge to ensure consistent Burst uptime.

Roles & Playstyle of Gorou and His Strengths and Weaknesses
Support Gorou
Gorou's only role is to be a buff support for Geo DEF-scaling damage dealers. While his Constellations enable him to heal, it's still a lot weaker compared to a dedicated healer. Gorou is best built with a lot of Energy Recharge and CRIT Rate for Favonius Warbow.
Pros and Cons of Support Gorou
Pros
- His buffs don't scale with his stats, allowing you to focus only on his Energy Recharge.
- His ease of use and ability to wield the Favonius Warbow make him a great team support.
Cons
- Constellation 6 is required for his most important stat buff (CRIT DMG), though he is still usable without it.

Gorou's Team Rotations and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for Gorou
Basic Rotation For Gorou
1. Activate other Support and Sub-DPS abilities.
2. Use Gorou's Elemental Skill to gain Energy Particles.
3. Use his Elemental Burst, then switch to the DPS character.
Gorou works similarly to other support characters, but he should be used last since his buffs have a short duration. Additionally, if he's supporting Itto, he needs to funnel Particles to him, so Gorou's Elemental Skill should be used last to proc the Favonius Warbow's effect for Itto.
Basics of Using Gorou (Beginner Guide)
Buff Support for DEF-Scaling Geo Damage Dealers
Gorou is a support character for DEF-scaling Geo damage dealers like Arataki Itto and Noelle. He provides the party with DEF, Geo DMG Bonus, and Interruption Resistance buffs. At Constellation 6, he can also buff Geo CRIT DMG.
Also a Great Holder for Favonius Warbow
Since Gorou's buffs don't scale with his own stats, he can be built with high Energy Recharge and CRIT Rate to consistently trigger the Favonius Warbow's effect. This makes him an excellent battery, especially for DEF-scaling Geo DPS characters like Itto who rely on their Elemental Burst for damage.
Elemental Skill Gives DEF Increase, Geo Buff and Interruption RES
Gorou's Elemental Skill creates a static field that provides a DEF buff, Geo DMG Bonus, and Interruption Resistance. It also deals AoE Geo damage upon activation, so it's best to use it before his Burst to generate Energy Particles.

Gorou's DEF buff from his Skill scales with its talent level. At Constellation 3, the value is more than double the amount at Level 1, so it's highly recommended to level up this talent.
Note that the Geo DMG Bonus and Interruption Resistance buffs do not scale with his Skill's level.
Elemental Burst Has Similar Effects To His Skill
Gorou's Elemental Burst creates a moving field that provides the same buffs as his Elemental Skill. Since the field follows the active character, it's a more versatile and reliable way to apply his buffs.
Burst Also Buffs 25% DEF
Gorou's first Passive gives a 25% DEF buff to the team when his Elemental Burst is used. It has a longer duration than his field buff and better value.
Requires At Least 3 Geo Characters For Buffs
The number of buffs Gorou provides depends on the number of Geo characters in the party. To unlock all of his buffs (DEF Bonus, Interruption Resistance, and Geo DMG Bonus), you need at least 3 Geo characters on the team. The buffs he provides are detailed below:
Buff Provided
1 Geo Character
- DEF Bonus only.
2 Geo Characters
- DEF Bonus and Interruption RES.
3 Geo Characters
- DEF Bonus, Interruption RES, and Geo DMG Bonus.
Notable Constellations
Notable Constellations for Gorou
Constellation 4
- When Gorou's Burst is used with 2 or more Geo characters, characters will be healed by 50% of Gorou's DEF every 1.5s.
Constellation 6
- After using his Elemental Skill or Burst, increases the Geo CRIT DMG of all party members. The buff scales with the number of Geo characters in the party:
- 1 Geo Character: +10%
- 2 Geo Characters: +20%
- 3 Geo Characters: +40%
Gorou becomes a significantly better support at Constellation 6. While he is perfectly viable without it, the 40% Geo CRIT DMG buff provides a massive damage boost to the Geo DPS he supports.

DEF & Geo Buffer For Geo DEF Damage Dealers
Gorou is a dedicated buffer for Geo damage dealers that scale with DEF. His buffs primarily come from his Elemental Skill, making it a priority to level up. While he can be built with Energy Recharge and CRIT Rate, you can also focus on DEF to maximize the healing effect from his Constellation 4.
Elemental Skill Creates A Static Buff Field
Gorou's Elemental Skill creates a static circular field that provides a DEF increase, Geo DMG Bonus, and Interruption Resistance for the active character. Although it has a short 10-second duration, it also deals damage, making it a useful tool for generating Energy Particles.
Elemental Burst Creates A Buff Field That Follows The Character
▲ Video showcasing the buff field moving with the character, instead of staying still.
Gorou's Elemental Burst is his primary support ability, as it creates a field similar to his Elemental Skill that follows the active character. It provides the same buffs, with the added effect of pulling in nearby Crystallize shards. Note that the Burst has a slightly shorter duration of 9 seconds.
Gorou's Passive Has A Longer Buff Duration
Gorou's first Passive Talent also provides a party-wide buff with a longer duration of 12 seconds. It increases the DEF of all party members by 25%, making him an excellent buffer for both the main DPS and Sub-DPS.
Buffs Need At Least 3 Geo Characters In Party
Buff Provided
1 Geo Character
- DEF Bonus only.
2 Geo Characters
- DEF Bonus and Interruption RES.
3 Geo Characters
- DEF Bonus, Interruption RES, and Geo DMG Bonus.
The buffs from Gorou's Elemental Skill rely on the number of Geo characters in the party. To activate all of his buffs, you need at least 3 Geo characters. Therefore, it's highly recommended to use him in a Mono Geo team to fully benefit from his abilities.
Buffs Rely On Skill Level And Not Gorou's Stats
Unlike many other support characters, Gorou's buffs don't rely on his own stats. Instead, they scale with his Elemental Skill's level. Only the DEF increase scales with leveling; the Interruption RES and Geo DMG Bonus are fixed values.
C6 Greatly Improves His Buffing Ability
Gorou's C6 is his most important Constellation, as it adds a valuable Geo CRIT DMG buff to his kit. However, similar to his other buffs, it requires at least 3 Geo characters in the party to increase the bonus from a base of 10% up to 40%.
Should You Pull for Gorou's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim For C6 If Possible
Constellation Priority
C6 > C1-C5
C6 Is A Huge Upgrade For Geo Teams
Gorou's C6 is as crucial for Geo teams as
Faruzan
's C6 is for Anemo teams. It provides a significant Geo Crit DMG buff, which is a massive damage increase for his on-field partners. However, Gorou's constellations before C6 offer only minor improvements and are generally not as impactful. His C4, notably, offers a small amount of healing, but it is too unreliable to be a primary source of sustain. Therefore, while he is functional at C0,
it is highly recommended to get Gorou to C6 if possible to maximize his potential as a dedicated off-field Geo buffer.

Effect
Displaying his valor as a general, Gorou deals Aoe Geo DMG and creates a field known as General's Glory to embolden his comrades.
General's Glory
Effect
This field has the following properties:
• Like the General's War Banner created by Inuzaka All-Round Defense, provides buffs to active characters within the skill's AoE based on the number of Geo characters in the party. Also moves together with your active character.
• Generates 1 Crystal Collapse every 1.5s that deals AoE Geo DMG to 1 opponent within the skill's AoE.
• Pulls 1 elemental shard in the skill's AoE to your active character's position every 1.5s (elemental shards are created by Crystallize reactions).
If a General's War Banner created by Gorou currently exists on the field when his ability is used, it will be destroyed. In addition, for the duration of General's Glory, Gorou's Elemental Skill Inuzaka All-Round Defense will not create the General's War Banner. If Gorou falls, the effects of General's Glory will be cleared.

Who Is Gorou?
General of the Watatsumi Army
Gorou is a general of the Watatsumi Army, serving under the leadership of
Kokomi
during the Inazuma Archon Quest storyline. He is known by his men for being loyal and reliable.
The Basis for Miss Hina
Yae Miko used Gorou's likeness as the basis for Miss Hina, and even used his own writing for the replies from "Miss Hina." Despite being a huge fan of Miss Hina, Itto is unable to realize that Gorou is the person behind the persona.

A character equipped with this Artifact set will obtain the Curiosity effect in the following conditions:
When on the field, the character gains 1 stack after hitting an opponent with a Geo attack, triggering a maximum of once every 0.3s. When off the field, the character gains 1 stack every 3s.

Best Weapons for Albedo in Genshin Impact
Cinnabar Spindle is definitely his best free-to-play weapon (Image via HoYoverse)
Albedo’s signature weapon is not a five-star weapon. Instead, his ‘signature’ weapon is the four-star Cinnabar Spindle, which is an old event-exclusive weapon. However, now, he has a lot of good five-star and four-star weapon choices.

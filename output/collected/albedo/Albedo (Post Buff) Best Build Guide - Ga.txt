Albedo is a 5-star Geo character that wields a Sword in Genshin Impact. His gameplay focuses on being an off-field sub-DPS, generating consistent Geo damage with his Elemental Skill, "Abiogenesis: Solar Isotoma", which creates a Geo construct that triggers "Transient Blossoms" when enemies are hit. These Blossoms scale off his DEF, making him a strong source of passive damage. His Elemental Burst, "Rite of Progeniture: Tectonic Tide", unleashes a Geo shockwave that creates "Fatal Blossoms", dealing additional AoE Geo damage when "Solar Isotoma" is present. Lore-wise, Albedo is the Chief Alchemist of the Knights of Favonius and a synthetic human created by the legendary alchemist Rhinedottir, with a mysterious connection to Khaenri’ah and the pursuit of forbidden knowledge.

Roles & Playstyle Of Albedo and His Strengths and Weaknesses
Hexerei Damage Support and Sub-DPS
With the new buffs for Albedo, his new role in Hexerei teams is to be the Damage Support mostly while also dealing Sub-DPS damage with his Skill. He can still use his previous BiS artifact set since his buff scales on defense but he can use support sets like Noblesse Oblige.
Pros and Cons of Support Albedo
Pros
- Easy to use. Use his Elemental Skill (and Burst if you need his EM buff) then switch him out.
- Can be easy to build since he only needs defense and CRIT stats.
- When Secret Rite is active, his flower breaking won't be too much of a liability anymore.
Cons
- Elemental Skill is stationary, requiring re-placing if the enemy is out of range.
- 42% Damage buff only applies to Hexerei characters making him niche to use with non-Hexerei.
- Geo application can often remove weaker Elemental Auras.
Best Build for Albedo
A support build for Albedo requires a lot of DEF, but it's better to prioritize his support capabilities over his Sub-DPS damage, even though his damage potential is still good.

Team Rotation of Albedo and Combos
Basic Team Rotation for Albedo
Basic Rotation for C0 Support Albedo
1. Use other Sub-DPS/Support abilities.
2. Switch to Albedo and use his Skill to create a Solar Isotoma.
3. (Optional) Use Albedo's Burst to buff Elemental Mastery.
4. Switch to the main DPS character.
Albedo's Elemental Mastery buff has a short duration, so it's best to use him right before your main DPS. This is recommended even though his Skill's buff has a very long uptime. However, if your team doesn't rely on Elemental Mastery for damage, he can be used at the start of the rotation.
C2 Negates the Need to Use Albedo's Burst
Albedo's Constellation 2 has been buffed to allow maxed Fatal Reckoning stacks to automatically be consumed to do Elemental Burst DMG based on his DEF and buff Elemental Mastery to the active character. This makes it easier to build him since he will only need DEF and CRIT stats.
Basics of Using Albedo (Beginner Guide)
DEF-Scaling Buffer for Hexerei Characters
With the new Hexerei character status, Albedo can buff 42% damage increase to Hexerei characters (12% for non-Hexerei). However, this buff will only be active if there's a 2nd Hexerei character with him to activate the Secret Rite effect.
Albedo Hexerei Buff Summary
- After a Solar Isotoma is created, active characters' gains a 4% (12% max) damage buff for every 1,000 DEF Albedo has.
- After a Silver Isotoma is created, active Hexerei characters gains a 10% (30% max) damage buff for every 1,000 DEF Albedo has.
▲ Silver Isotomas are created when a 2nd Hexerei character is in the team and activates the Secret Rite effect.
Only Needs Defense (and Energy Recharge) When Building Him
Unless you are using Albedo as a support in a reaction-based team, he will only need Defense and Energy Recharge for his Skill DMG and buff. He can also be built as a Sub-DPS if you can easily reach 3,000 Defense on him.
Off-Field Playstyle As a Support and Sub-DPS
Albedo is relatively simple to use. Simply deploy his Solar Isotoma with his Skill, then switch him out. His buffs will become active once a Silver Isotoma is generated after his Solar Isotoma deals damage.
Silver Isotomas cannot be destroyed by enemies, making his Skill have an infinite duration as long as it gets triggered to attack.
Burst is Optional Even More So When C2
Albedo's Elemental Burst deals more damage when a Solar Isotoma is on the field and provides a 125 EM buff. If his C2 is unlocked, using his Burst is no longer necessary since his Skill will automatically provide the EM buff while he is off-field.

Sub-DPS And Support For Hexerei Teams
Albedo is a Geo Sub-DPS and damage support for Hexerei teams. Before his buff, his role was to be a Geo Sub-DPS and battery but now he can be used to provide buffs and deal more damage.
Use Elemental Skill For Damage And Buff
Albedo's general playstyle still remains the same. Place down his Solar Isotoma and then he can leave the field right away. With the Hexerei buff,
he no longer needs to refresh the flower if it breaks
since Silver Isotomas cannot be broken and only expires if it runs out when enemies are no longer getting hit.
Summary of Albedo's Skill Changes
Without Hexerei Buff
- Solar Isotoma can be broken and needs to be placed down again if it breaks.
With Hexerei Buff
- Up to two Silver Isotomas are created when his Solar and Silver Isotomas deal damage and cannot be destroyed.
- Solar Isotoma will do more damage when Silver Isotomas exist on the field.
- Solar Isotoma will buff up to 12% damage to active characters and Silver Isotoma will buff up to 30% damage for active Hexerei characters.
Only Needs DEF And CRIT Stats
Albedo's stat requirements remain the same. He still needs only DEF and CRIT Stats but his Energy Recharge can be on a lower priority especially if you have his C2. Since his buffs and damage scale on his DEF, it's best to focus on that and his CRIT stats since his Skill damage now scale heavily on his DEF.
Elemental Burst Is Optional But Buffs EM
If using a DPS that doesn't need EM for their damage (i.e Mono Geo teams), you can skip using his Elemental Burst and focus on Albedo's damage and buff. But his EM buff from his Burst is amazing when used in reaction teams.

6.2 Update

Albedo Character Buffs & Changes (6.2 Update)
Should You Pull for Albedo's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
C2 Greatly Improves Albedo's Abilities
Weapon VS Constellation
C3 or C2 + C1 > Premium CRIT Swords
C2 is Albedo's best Constellation since it greatly improves his gameplay.
He can deal more damage with his Skill, lower his DEF requirement, and completely remove the need to use his Elemental Burst for buffing
. Fatal Reckoning is a buff that Albedo stacks once he's C2. If there are 4 stacks of it, it will trigger a weaker version of his Elemental Burst that still gives the 125 EM buff, but it requires him to be off-field.
C4 and C6 Provide Additional Damage Buffs But Are Not Necessary
While Albedo's C4 gives his Skill a similar effect to
Xianyun
's Burst, it's only available if he has the Secret Rite effect, which requires a second Hexerei character on the team. His C6 gives a damage buff to characters shielded by Crystallize, but it also incentivizes using his Burst instead of keeping him completely off-field.
Banner Weapon May Vary
Albedo was released with no true 5-star Signature Weapon, so the weapon banner whenever he has a rerun will usually feature a random 5-star sword. Historically, it has been either
Summit Shaper
,
Freedom-Sworn
, or
Primordial Jade Cutter
.
Jade Cutter is a generalist CRIT sword, while Freedom-Sworn and Summit Shaper are more niche options for Albedo.
Albedo's BiS Doesn't Rerun With Him
As of Version 6.2, Albedo's BiS is still Chiori's signature weapon, which doesn't rerun with him. It's better to prioritize his Constellations over premium weapons, since event-limited and craftable weapons work well on him.

Who Is Albedo?
Chief Alchemist Of The Knights Of Favonius
Albedo is the Chief Alchemist of the Knights of Favonius. He also serves as the Captain of the Investigation Team. He's a genius known throughout the city for his prowess in alchemy and is Sucrose's mentor.
A Synthetic Human Made By Rhinedottir
Albedo in truth is a synthetic human created by one of the Hexenzirkel Witches, Rhinedottir. She left him to the care of Alice before vanishing. Albedo seeks to reunite with her again.
Big Brother To Klee
Alice, another of the Hexenzirkel Witches, helped Albedo to settle in Mondstadt. She once told him that they will be a family now and to treat Klee, her daughter, as his little sister. He has done so wholeheartedly ever since.

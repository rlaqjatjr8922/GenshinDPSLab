Best teams for Sigewinne in Genshin Impact
Who wants some herbal tea?
Christine Choi
| Jun 24, 2024 12:54 pm
Image via HoYoverse
If your team is looking for a healer, why not bring nurse
Sigewinne
on board? Sigewinne is a flexible healer in
Genshin Impact
, and though she can slot into many teams, here’s where she shines the brightest.
Recommended Videos
Sigewinne in Furina teams
Sigewinne
offsets Furina’s HP drain
and enables more Fanfare stacks, boosting your whole team’s damage while keeping them alive. As Hydro characters, the two can also provide Energy for one another, reducing the need to build Energy Recharge. Sigewinne will also provide a small buff to her teammates’ Skill damage.

Nilou Bloom with Sigewinne
Sigewinne’s team healing is also valuable in Nilou Bloom teams. Start with Nilou and Sigewinne, then add any two Dendro units of your choice, making sure at least one of them can apply Dendro from off-field. Sigewinne won’t trigger as many Blooms as Kokomi or Yelan, but she is able to heal your entire team without hogging too much field time.

Sigewinne in overworld teams
Sigewinne may not be great for high-level Abyss teams, but she makes for a great overworld healer in the open world of Teyvat. Her healing comes from her Skill, so it’s limited only by her cooldown. Since Sigewinne doesn’t need to use her Burst, you don’t need to worry about Energy or Energy Recharge. This also makes Sigewinne easier to build as a pure healer, since you can just focus on her max HP stat.
Your overworld team should consist of whatever characters you need to make traversal easier for yourself. Kazuha and
Venti
are great for gaining vertical height, while
Yelan
,
Sayu
, and
Lynette
help cover ground more quickly. Make sure you have at least one DPS unit, and then slot in Sigewinne as your healer. Even if you need to change your team to tackle specific enemies or puzzles, Sigewinne will be able to support anyone she’s paired with.

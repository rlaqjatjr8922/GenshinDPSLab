Rate Sigewinne!
What Kind Of Character Is Sigewinne?
Sigewinne is a healer that can be used as a sub-DPS or a Skill DMG Buffer. Sigewinne, while having buffs for Elemental Skill, mainly contributes to the team with her powerful heals. She can also deal damage with Elemental Burst making her a decent support, healer and Burst DPS.
Strengths & Weakness Of Sigewinne
Strengths
- AoE healing makes her an amazing character for sustain.
- Decent damage from Elemental Burst.
- Ability to increase DMG of E. Skill makes her unique.
Weakness
- E. Skill DMG buff is limited to small amount of hits.
- Poor Hydro off-field application.
- High Elemental Burst Cost despite having low DMG and no support capabilities. .
- Elemental Skill takes time to charge if you want to reach the max level.

Sigewinne Skill Guide & How To Use Sigewinne
How To Use Sigewinne?
Sigewinne Abilities & Skills
▼A Support character that heals the whole team with Elemental Skill.
▼Using Hold Elemental Skill does not provide much benefit.
▼Boost the damage of off-field characters 10 times.
▼Recommended To Reach 65,000 HP.
▼You can also opt to not use Elemental Burst.
▼Sourcewater Droplet effect is just a bonus.
A Support Character That Heals The Whole Team With Elemental Skill
Sigewinne is a team supporter that heals the team using Elemental Skill. After shooting Elemental Skill, it will release a bubble called "Boistering Bubblebalm" that heals every team member but Sigewinne. It will only heal Sigewinne at the final bounce but
it is more than enough to keep the whole party alive.
Elemental Skill Effects
- Summons a Bubblebalm that bounces 5 times.
- The bubble deals Hydro DMG to enemies when hit.
- Upon bouncing, it heals team members
other than Sigewinne
.
- After 5 bounces, Sigewinne will be healed.
- Spawns 2 Sourcewater Droplet. (
▼Click for details
)
Hold Skill:
- Increases bubble size (2 Levels)
- Per level, the healing and damage is increased by +5%
- Every bounce, the size gets smaller to the previous level (Up to 2 Levels)
- The big bubble traps smaller enemies.
Using Hold Elemental Skill Does Not Provide Much Benefit
When pressing and holding Elemental Skill of Sigewinne the bubble will become bigger, with it the amount of healing will increase and it also allows you to trap smaller enemies. However, it takes time to fully charge the attack and
the healing does not increase much and the number of trappable enemies are limited.
so there isn't much benefit from using it when fighting higher difficulty contents.

You Can Opt To Not Use Elemental Burst
Elemental Burst is mainly a damage dealing skill. However, for a support focused build Sigewinne, leaving a room for CRIT Rate and DMG is difficult. Because of it,
it can be difficult to deal significant damage with Sigewinne's Elemental Burst.
Supporting with Sourcewater Droplet is also not needed so just using E. Skill then switching the character is normally enough.
If you want to use Elemental Burst for activation of buffs like Noblesse Oblige, you can press the jump button to cancel it.
Sourcewater Droplet Effect Is Mainly Just A Bonus
▲After using E. Skill, 2 droplets will be produced.
Picking up Sourcewater Droplets will give Sigewinne Bond of Life  and clearing the bond of life will give energy to Sigewinne.
The energy given is just 5 energy
and is for the Burst that is not needed for support Sigewinne. Taking the droplets is mainly optional.

Should You Pull for Sigewinne's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Sigewinne's Constellations are a Decent Upgrade
Signature VS Constellation
C2 > C1 >
Silvershower Heartstrings
C2 is a Good Stopping Point
Her early constellations increase her utility and damage output by buffing her damage and healing, with C2 also allowing her to shred Hydro RES, which is a good bonus to have in specific teams that appreciate it. While Silvershower Heartstrings can give her burst a significant +28% additional crit rate,
because her damage at C0 is unremarkable, it would probably be more ideal to obtain her C1 and even her C2 to upgrade her baseline before you consider pulling for her Signature Weapon.

Rebound Hydrotherapy (Elemental Skill)
Effect
Blows a Bolstering Bubblebalm that can stimulate blood flow and help patients drift off to sleep using a bubblegun specially made in the Fortress of Meropide. Bolstering Bubblebalms will bounce between nearby opponents, dealing Hydro DMG based on Sigewinne's max HP to opponents it hits. When it bounces, it will restore HP to all nearby party members except Sigewinne herself. The amount healed is based on Sigewinne's max HP. After bouncing 5 times, the Bolstering Bubblebalm will disappear and restore HP to Sigewinne based on her max HP value. When no opponents are present, the Bolstering Bubblebalm will bounce nearby. Only one Bolstering Bubblebalm created by a Sigewinne may exist at once. When Held, the Bolstering Bubblebalm can be boosted to an even bigger size.
Hold
Effect
Enter Aiming Mode to begin blowing an even bigger, even more breathtaking Bolstering Bubblebalm! The Bolstering Bubblebalm will grow bigger the longer the skill is Held, until it grows up to two tiers in size. Each tier it grows increases its DMG by 5% and healing by 5%. When a Bolstering Bubblebalm bounces, it will drop down a tier in size, until it returns to being a normal Bolstering Bubblebalm. After weaker opponents are hit by big Bolstering Bubblebalms, they will be imprisoned and will be unable to move..
In addition, 2 Sourcewater Droplets will be created near Sigewinne when she uses this Skill. Each Sourcewater Droplet Sigewinne collects will grant her a Bond of Life worth 10% of her Max HP. When Sigewinne's Bond of Life is cleared, she regains 1 Elemental Energy for every 2,000 HP worth of the Bond of Life that was cleared. Sigewinne can regain up to 5 Elemental Energy in this way.
Arkhe: Ousia

Effect

Effect

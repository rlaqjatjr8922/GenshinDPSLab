Sigewinne & Furina Enables Most DPS
The combination of Sigewinne's AoE Healing and Skill DMG Buff with Furina's DMG Buff and HP Drain has the ability to buff most of the DPS and Sub-DPS. Characters that make use of Hydro reaction works especially with this duo.
Rotation Of Double Hydro Sigewinne & Furina
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina's Skill (Attack Summon) and use Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to Navia and use Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Xilonen and use Elemental Skill then 2 Normal Attacks.
4
Switch to Sigewinne and use Elemental Skill.
5
Switch to Navia and use Hold Elemental Skill → Normal Attacks → Elemental Skill.
6
Start from no. 1
Make sure to start with Furina before Sigewinne so you can get Fanfare stacks quicker.

Strong Healer For Bloom Comp
Bloom with Nilou is one of the strongest teams around due to the high DPS Nilou's special Bloom generates. With Sigewinne, you can have a strong healer that will ensure your team survives enemy attacks and the Bloom self-damage.
For this team comp, you will shift between Nahida and Sigewinne as the on-field character. Nahida more than Sigewinne. Nilou and Collei can be quick swaps.
Rotation Of Bloom Sigewinne
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida's Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Nilou and use her Elemental Skill. Tap 3 times, then use her Burst
3
Bring out Collei and use her Burst
4
Switch to Sigewinne and use her Burst, before ending on her Skill
For this rotation, it's imperative to have Nahida's Burst and Skill up as much as possible. Nilou's Skill is also needed to be up most of the time. Sigewinne can stay on the field longer so she can have the best out of her Burst.

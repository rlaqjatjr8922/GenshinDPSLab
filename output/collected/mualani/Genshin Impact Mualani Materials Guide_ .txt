Genshin Impact Mualani Mats Guide
Before Mualani’s release in Genshin Impact 5.0, players should start gathering the following materials:
Ascension Materials
Varunada Lazurite Series
: These materials can be obtained by defeating Hydro bosses like Oceanid and Hydro Hypostasis. Weekly bosses such as Childe (Tartaglia) and Azhdaha also drop them. Additionally, you can convert other elemental gems into Varunada Lazurite using Dust of Azoth at the crafting bench.
Sprayfeather Gill
: A new material that will be released in version 5.0.
Mark of the Binding Blessing
: Typically obtained from specific events or quests. Keep an eye on limited-time events and special quests offering these as rewards.
Sentry’s Wooden Whistle
: Collected by defeating specific enemy types, such as Hilichurl Sentries, found in Hilichurl camps scattered throughout Teyvat.
Warrior’s Metal Whistle
: Dropped by elite enemies and bosses. Focus on defeating high-level enemies and participating in boss fights to gather these.
Saurian-Crowned Warrior’s Golden Whistle
: Rare drops from high-level bosses and elite enemies. Participate in weekly boss fights and high-level domains to increase your chances of obtaining them.
Talent Materials
Contention Talent Book
: These talent books can be farmed from the Taishan Mansion domain on specific days (Tuesday, Friday, and Sunday). Check the domain schedule and plan your farming accordingly.
Lightless Mass
: Typically obtained from defeating specific high-level bosses or through special events. Keep an eye on event announcements and boss drops.
Crown of Insight
: Rare items usually obtained from major events, limited-time quests, and the Frostbearing Tree in Dragonspine. Participate in events and complete quests to collect these.
Although some materials are not available in version 4.8, there is still time before Natlan’s release. And the guide will be updated with all the necessary materials once version 5.0 goes live.

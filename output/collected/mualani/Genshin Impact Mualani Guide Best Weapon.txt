Welcome to the ultimate guide for Mualani, the latest five-star character in Genshin Impact! This guide is designed to provide you with an in-depth look at Mualani’s abilities, optimal weapons, artifacts, team compositions, and more. Whether you’re a seasoned player or new to Mualani, this guide will help you maximize her potential as a forward Vape DPS character.
Mualani’s Kit and Ability Genshin Impact
Basic Attacks and Skills
Basic Attacks
: Mualani’s basic attacks deal minimal damage due to her HP-scaling nature. However, they are useful for applying Hydro status to enemies and finishing off low-health foes.
Elemental Skill – Surfshark Wavebreaker
: Activating her Elemental Skill causes Mualani to ride her Sharky surfboard, transforming her attacks into Hydro-infused Sharky Bites. These bites scale with her HP and have a cooldown of 1.8 seconds each. Bumping into enemies builds
Wave Momentum Stacks
, which, when fully stacked (3), trigger an enhanced Sharky Surging Bite, dealing significant damage.
Elemental Burst – Stormburst Shot
a simple single hit shark missle that will deal damage based on Mualani Max HP!
Night Soul Burst
: Mualani’s Elemental Burst deals massive damage based on her Night Soul stacks, which are influenced by the number of Natlan characters in your party. Each stack boosts the damage of her Burst, making it more effective in well-coordinated teams.
Mualani Genshin Impact Optimal Artifacts and Stats
Best Artifacts
Obsidian Codex Set
: The four-piece Obsidian Codex set is Mualani’s best-in-slot option, offering a substantial 40% Crit Rate and a 15% damage bonus while in Night Soul state. This set maximizes her damage potential and is highly recommended.
Alternative Sets
: If you’re still farming for Obsidian Codex, the four-piece Marechaussee set is a solid alternative, providing around 94% of the performance compared to Obsidian Codex. Other viable sets include Gilded Dreams and Heart of Depth, but they are generally less optimal.
Main Stats and Sub Stats
Main Stats
: Focus on HP% or Elemental Mastery for the Timepiece, Hydro Damage Goblet, and Crit Circlet (preferably Crit Damage due to her Ascension stat).
Sub Stats
: Prioritize Crit Rate, Crit Damage, HP%, Elemental Mastery, and Energy Recharge. Although Energy Recharge is less critical, it helps manage her Elemental Burst cooldown effectively.
Recommended Weapons Mualani Genshin Impact
Ring of Yaxche
: This craftable Catalyst is excellent for increasing Mualani’s HP and basic attack damage. It is a top choice for a free-to-play weapon.
The Widsith
: At refinement 5, The Widsith offers impressive damage boosts through its buffs, although its utility can be inconsistent due to its RNG nature.
Mappa Mare
: Another craftable option, the Mappa Mare is effective in quick vaping scenarios, especially if you’re aiming for a balance between HP scaling and damage output.
Team Compositions for Mualani
Mualani thrives in teams that enhance her Vape damage. Here are some effective team setups:
Standard Vape Team
: Mualani, Xiangling, Kazuha, and a flexible fourth slot (e.g., Sucrose, Barbara). This setup provides a good balance of Hydro application and damage amplification.
Burning Shark Team
: Mualani, Eula, a Pyro character (e.g., Dehya), and a fourth slot (e.g., Nahida, Baal). This team is energy-efficient and does not rely on burst abilities.
Rockfish Team
: Mualani, a Pyro character (e.g., Xiangling), and two Geo characters (e.g., Zhongli, Albedo). This setup provides strong Hydro and Pyro synergy with good damage potential.
Fabulous Fishy Team
: Mualani, Xiangling, Furina, and a healer (e.g., Barbara). While less optimal due to potential energy issues, it’s still a solid option.
Mualani is a versatile and powerful forward Vape DPS character with a lot of potential. While she currently faces some challenges with team compatibility and energy management, her damage output and exploration capabilities make her a valuable addition to any roster. As always, adjustments may be necessary based on future updates and new character releases.

Genshin Impact
. One of the main reasons why Hydro is so good is because of Hydro’s ability to trigger good reactions with almost all other elements. Bloom, Frozen, Electro-charged, and most importantly Vaporize, one of the best reactions in
Genshin Impact
.
This is good news for Mualani since she’s a Hydro
Catalyst user in
Genshin Impact
. She excels as a Hydro unit, especially if her build allows her to deal massive amount of DMG with Vaporize.

Mualani Best Artifact In Genshin Impact
Mualani is a burst-type character who needs to stack as much DMG as possible in a short period to let it all go in one hit. She mostly
deals that burst DMG through her Elemental Burst
and Shark Bite. However, there are a few important notes to take before building Mualani in
Genshin Impact
.
First, Mualani’s hits scale with her max HP, which means giving her an
HP% Timepiece
is a must. She also inflicts a great amount of Hydro DMG, especially if she triggers Crit hits, which prioritize
building Mualani on Vaporize reactions
on top of giving her
Hydro and Crit DMG on Goblet and Circlet respectively
.
The best artifacts for Mualani in
Genshin Impact
are the
4pc of Obsidian Codex
since they increase her DMG while she’s on her Nightsoul Blessing during her Elemental Skill time. On top of that, Obsidian Codex grants Mualani a whopping 40% Crit Rate, which is insane for her build. You can also build Mualani with
4pc of Heart of Depth
since her Sharky hits are considered Normal Attack DMG.
If you don’t have 4pc of Obsidian Codex or Heart of Depth, you can build Mualani with
2pc of HP% or Hydro DMG%
by giving her many options like:
2pc of Obsidian Codex
2pc of Heart of Depth
2pc of Nymph's Dream
2pc of Vourukasha's Glow
2pc of Tenacity of the Millelith
Mualani Best Weapon In Genshin Impact
There’s no doubt that
Surf's Up is the best Catalyst for Mualani
in
Genshin Impact
. As her Signature weapon, Surf's Up grants Mualani a great amount of Crit DMG while buffing her HP% and Normal Attack DMG.
Since Mualani gets a 40% Crit Rate from her
artifacts in
Genshin Impact
, finding her weapons that increase her Crit DMG is necessary. 5-star Catalysts like
Tome of the Eternal Flow
or 4-star
The Widsith
are perfect as stat sticks even if Mualani didn’t fully benefit from their passives.
Sacrificial Jade
grants her a huge amount of Crit Rate, which is unnecessary unless you’re not building Mualani with Obsidian Codex.
Since the 4-star Crit Catalysts are limited, you can choose those with HP% as a Secondary Stat, like
Ring of Yaxche
and
Ash-Graven Drinking Horn
. Both weapons increase Mualani’s HP% by 41% while having a passive that scales well with her Max HP. You can also go with
Mappa Mare
and
Prototype Amber
as decent 4-star alternatives. Prototype Amber has HP% as a Secondary Stat while it regenerates Mualani’s Energy and offers her team a slight amount of healing. As for Mappa Mare, it’s a good F2P
Forgeable weapon in
Genshin Impact
since it improves Mualani’s Elemental reactions while increasing her Hydro DMG.

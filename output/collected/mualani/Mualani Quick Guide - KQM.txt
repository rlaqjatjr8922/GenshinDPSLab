Playstyles
On-Field DPS
Mualani is played as an on-field DPS who uses the Nightsoul mechanic that’s characteristic of Natlan characters. She surfs around on-field during her Skill state and collides with enemies to get stacks that increase her damage, before launching a massive Sharky’s Surging Bite nuke with her Normal Attack.
Talents
Level and Talent Priority
Character Level = Skill > Burst
Mualani scales with HP and heavily benefits from Level 90 to increase her damage. Although Mualani’s Sharky Bite is considered Normal Attack DMG, its multiplier scales with her Skill Talent, so her Normal Attack doesn’t need to be leveled. Her Burst is a nice source of damage and can be leveled afterwards.

Elemental Skill
Elemental Skill
|
Surfshark Wavebreaker
Talent Description
Combining her mastery of speed and the waves and water, Mualani can surf anytime, anywhere.
After using this skill, Mualani will gain 60 Nightsoul points and enter the Nightsoul’s Blessing state.
Nightsoul’s Blessing: Mualani
Continuously consume Nightsoul points. When they are depleted or when the skill is used again, Mualani’s Nightsoul’s Blessing will end. The state itself has the following traits:
• Mualani mounts her Sharky Surfboard, increasing Mualani’s Movement SPD and interruption resistance. Mualani can move on water and liquid Phlogiston while this state is active, and she will take no DMG from the latter.
• Normal Attacks will be converted to Sharky’s Bites, dealing Nightsoul-aligned
Hydro DMG
based on Mualani’s Max HP. Sharky’s Bites can be used in mid-air. DMG dealt this way is considered Normal Attack DMG.
• When she makes contact with opponents in this state, Mualani applies “Marked as Prey” to them and gains 1 Wave Momentum stack. Max 3 stacks. 1 stack can be gained from the same opponent every 0.7s.
Wave Momentum and Marked as Prey
When Mualani uses Sharky’s Bite, her DMG dealt increases based on Wave Momentum stacks. When she has 3 stacks, a Normal Attack will use Sharky’s Surging Bite instead, further increasing her DMG and removing all her stacks when hitting an opponent.
When Sharky’s Bites hit opponents Marked as Prey, that mark will be removed, and she will fire Shark Missiles at up to 5 nearby opponents Marked as Prey, dealing DMG to them equal to this Sharky’s Bite instance and clearing their Marks. If more than 1 opponent is the target of Sharky’s Bite and Shark Missiles, the DMG dealt will decrease, decreasing to 72% of the original DMG when at least 3 opponents are targeted.
Mualani’s Skill grants her 60 Nightsoul points and lets her enter her Nightsoul’s Blessing state, during which she surfs around on her pet shark while consuming Nightsoul points. While in the Nightsoul’s Blessing state, Mualani’s Normal Attack is transformed into Sharky’s Bite. This state lasts 6s at base, and it can be extended to last 10s with her A1 Passive.
While surfing in her Skill state, Mualani can collide with enemies to tag them with a mark and gain stacks that add to the damage scaling of Sharky’s Bite; this does not deal damage to the enemy. The number of stacks is visible with a bar on the screen, and she can gain up to 3 stacks total — obtaining the third stack upgrades Sharky’s Bite to the more powerful Sharky’s Surging Bite.
Mualani can perform a Normal Attack at any time in her Skill state to consume the stacks she currently possesses, launch a Sharky’s Bite, and fire shark-shaped missiles at up to 5 nearby enemies she has previously marked. Sharky’s Bite and Shark Missiles have no ICD, which lets Mualani Vaporize each of them for some impressive damage-per-screenshot hits. Notably, Mualani’s Sharky’s Bite and missiles deal reduced damage if they hit more than 1 target, down to 72% of the original damage at 3+ targets.
Note that you should always try to use fully stacked Sharky’s Bites; they both maximize Mualani’s damage and trigger her A1 Passive to extend her Nightsoul’s Blessing state.

Boomsharka-laka
Talent Description
Fires a Super Shark Missile that can track opponents, dealing Nightsoul-aligned AoE Hydro DMG based on Mualani’s Max HP.
A powerful single-hit nuke that scales with Mualani’s HP and deals Nightsoul-aligned damage. Although Mualani’s Burst deals high damage, especially when Vaporized, it’s still a much smaller share of her damage compared to her Sharky’s Bites. As such, it is not worth building the ER to cast her Burst every rotation.
Ascension 1 Passive
Ascension 1 Passive
|
Heat-Resistant Freshwater Floater
Talent Description
When Sharky’s Surging Bite hits an opponent, a Puffer will be generated nearby. Mualani will restore 20 Nightsoul points when she picks up a Puffer. Only 2 such Puffers can be created in this way during a single instance of being in Nightsoul’s Blessing.
When Mualani hits an enemy with Sharky’s Surging Bite, she spawns a floating pufferfish (Puffer) near her. She can generate a maximum of two Puffers per Skill, and picking one up restores 20 Nightsoul points to Mualani. This lets Mualani extend her Skill duration from 6s up to 10s.
Ascension 4 Passive
Ascension 4 Passive

Natlan’s Greatest Guide
Talent Description
After a nearby party member triggers a Nightsoul Burst, Mualani will gain 1 stack of Wavechaser’s Exploits, which lasts for 20s. Max 3 stacks. When she uses Boomsharka-laka, Mualani will clear all Wavechaser’s Exploits stacks and increase the DMG of this Boomsharka-laka by 15%/30%/45% of her Max HP based on the number of stacks cleared.
When Mualani’s teammates (including non-Natlan characters) trigger Nightsoul Burst by dealing Elemental damage, the next use of her Burst deals increased damage. Each stack lasts for 20s and increases the damage scaling of Mualani’s Burst by 15% HP per stack (additively), up to a maximum of 3 stacks.
Nightsoul Burst can trigger more often in teams with multiple Natlan characters, generating more stacks for Mualani. Xilonen notably provides Mualani with an additional stack via her A4 Passive’s “fake” Nightsoul Burst.
Nightrealm’s Gift
Nightrealm’s Gift
|
Crests and Troughs
Talent Description
After her Nightsoul points are depleted, Mualani will switch to consuming Phlogiston to maintain her Nightsoul’s Blessing.
While in an area with Phlogiston Mechanics within Natlan, she can use Nightsoul Transmission: Mualani. When the active character is currently sprinting, swimming, in a movement mode caused by certain Talents, or at a certain height in the air, the following will trigger when switching to Mualani: Mualani will enter the Nightsoul’s Blessing state and gain 40 Nightsoul points. Nightsoul Transmission can be triggered once every 10s by your own team.
Additionally, Mualani’s Nightsoul point or Phlogiston consumption while moving on water and Liquid Phlogiston during Nightsoul’s Blessing is decreased by 75% while in Natlan, and her Nightsoul point consumption is decreased by 35% while doing the same outside Natlan.
Night Realm’s Gift is a special passive for Natlan characters that increases their quality of life in overworld exploration. After consuming all her Nightsoul points, Mualani starts consuming Phlogiston instead of exiting her surfing state, which essentially gives her a special Stamina bar while in Natlan.
Mualani’s Nightsoul or Phlogiston consumption is also reduced by 75% while surfing on water or liquid Phlogiston in Natlan, and by 35% outside of Natlan, which allows her to explore in her surfing state for longer. Additionally, when swapping to Mualani in an area with Phlogiston mechanics, she gains 40 Nightsoul Points and immediately enters her Nightsoul state. This can happen at most every 10s.

The Leisurely “Meztli”…
Constellation Description
The DMG dealt by the first Sharky’s Surging Bite after Mualani enters Nightsoul’s Blessing and the Shark Missiles it triggers is increased by 66% of Mualani’s Max HP. This instance of increased DMG is subject to the diminishing DMG rules of Surfshark Wavebreaker.
In addition, while out of combat, Mualani’s Nightsoul’s Blessing state consumes 30% less Phlogiston and Nightsoul point.
Significantly increases the damage of Mualani’s first Surging Bite every rotation. Mualani’s C1 is a noticeable increase to her main source of damage, and it makes for a good stopping point for free-to-play players. As an added bonus, Mualani’s Nightsoul Blessing state now consumes 30% less Nightsoul and Phlogiston, so she can surf around for even longer in the overworld.

Stat Priority:
CRIT > HP% > EM (Vaporize)
Mualani’s CRIT Rate Ascension Stat, combined with 4pc Obsidian Codex’s 40% CRIT Rate buff, makes it very easy to overcap on CRIT Rate and increases the value of stats like HP% and EM. As such, an HP% Circlet can perform similarly to a CRIT DMG Circlet, or even better in the case Mualani equips a weapon with a CRIT secondary stat like her signature. Likewise, a Goblet with an HP% main stat often performs close to one with Hydro DMG Bonus.
You can check with
Genshin Optimizer
which main stat combination is best for the artifacts you own.

Easily Mualani’s Best-in-Slot and highly recommended to farm for her. The set provides her with both an unconditional 15% Universal DMG Bonus and a hefty 40% CRIT Rate buff with full uptime.

Teambuilding
Mualani deals the majority of her teams’ damage and wants teammates that can support her.
However, her HP scaling limits her options since she is unable to take advantage of ATK buffers (like Bennett) and ATK% buffs.
A Pyro unit with sufficient off-field application is also necessary to enable Forward Vaporize.
Mualani’s Hydro application is slow enough that she can run weaker Pyro appliers with no issue, as long as she is solo Hydro or with a second Hydro that does not apply Hydro during Mualani’s combo.
Mualani’s field time requirements are long, so her teammates should be able to support her from off-field.
Running a shielder or healer is recommended but not mandatory since Mualani can move freely during her Skill to avoid getting hit. Try it out for yourself in-game for a more accurate assessment.
Forward Vaporize
Mualani — Pyro — Xilonen / Flex — Flex
Mualani’s kit is tailor-made for Forward Vaporize and this archetype is by far her strongest playstyle — you could even say it’s her bread and butter. Her Sharky’s Bites have no ICD, which lets her Vaporize every hit for powerful nuke damage.
Notable Teammates
Mualani’s most recommended teammates are marked with a star (★).
However, characters without a star are also good options.
★
Xilonen
Xilonen’s long-lasting buffs make her the uncontested best support for Mualani. Her A4 Passive’s “fake” Nightsoul Burst also gives Mualani an additional A4 Passive stack.
Additionally, Xilonen enables other units to hold 4pc Archaic Petra and pick up the shard she creates, further buffing Mualani.
Xilonen’s Burst is ideally never used, but it can be used in between rotations if healing is needed.
Pyro
A Pyro unit with sufficient off-field application is required to enable Forward Vaporize.
★
Mavuika
Mavuika is a great option for the Pyro slot, offering Pyro application, DMG Bonus via her A4 Passive, and a nuke which she can Vape (or Melt with Citlali).
It is worth noting that Mavuika’s Constellation 1 lengthening her Skill can cause issues when it comes to triggering a Hydro reaction for 4pc VV, 4pc Scroll, or 4pc AP past the first rotation.
★
Xiangling
Out of the few off-field Pyro appliers available, Xiangling is a great option for Mualani (to no one’s surprise). Xiangling lets Mualani trigger Vaporize on all hits.
Mualani’s Hydro application is slow enough that Guoba alone can enable her. This allows Xiangling to Burst every other rotation to shorten the rotation length, and also lets her equip supportive sets such as 4pc Instructor or 4pc Archaic Petra.
Since Mualani being a Natlan unit enables Nightsoul Burst, Xiangling can equip 2pc Scroll to lower her ER requirements if Bursting every rotation.
Pyro Traveler
Pyro Traveler is an accessible option, offering just enough off-field Pyro application in addition to holding 4pc Scroll, and providing a minor DMG Bonus buff with their Constellation 1. However, Pyro Traveler’s Pyro application falls short in multi-target scenarios.
Due to Pyro Traveler’s Skill’s 18s cooldown being longer than a typical Mualani rotation of ~16s, R5 Sacrificial Sword is strongly recommended.

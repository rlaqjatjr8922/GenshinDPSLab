Mualani is a 5-star Hydro character that wields a Catalyst in Genshin Impact. Her gameplay focuses on being an on-field DPS, utilizing her Elemental Skill where you ride a surfboard and marks enemies. When enemies are marked, use Normal Attacks to use her Enhanced Normal Attacks against marked enemies. Lore-wise, Mualani is a surfer from the People of the Springs tribe bearing the UMJ (Umoja) Ancient Name.

Roles & Playstyle Of Mualani and Its Strengths and Weakness
On-Field Hydro DPS Mualani
Mualani's intended role is to be the On-Field DPS that deals powerful oneshot Hydro attacks. Her playstyle is perfect for slow Off-Field sub-DPS that automatically attacks enemies.
Pros and Cons of DPS Mualani
Pros
- Powerful oneshot DPS that can be used with most Pyro Off-Field sub-DPS.
- Pyro Traveler, a free character, is compatible with her as support and Pyro applier for Vaporize procs.
Cons
- Controlling Mualani during her Nightsoul state can be somewhat difficult and needing to go through enemies can be harder if they move around a lot.
- Missing an attack during Nightsoul state can be difficult to recover from since her Nightsoul points will still drain overtime.

Team Rotation of Mualani and Combos
Basic Mualani Team Rotation
Basic Rotation For Mualani
1. (Optional) Use a Normal Attack to apply Hydro for VV or Scroll set holders.
2. Switch to supports and use their abilities.
3. Switch back to Mualani and use her Elemental Skill.
4. Gain 3 stacks from passing through enemies.
5. Use Mualani's Normal Attack once she gains 3 stacks.
6. Repeat Steps 4 to 5 until you run out of Nightsoul points.
7. (Optional) Use Mualani's Elemental Burst.
Since Mualani is able to apply Hydro easily as a Catalyst character, she can use her Normal Attack to apply for VV or Scroll set supports to trigger their buffs. After activating all the support skills,
Mualani uses her Elemental Skill to enter Nightsoul state and start dealing damage with her enhanced Normal Attacks every time she gains 3 stacks.
Mualani's gameplay is unique that her basic combo is also her optimal combo. She's able to lengthen her Nightsoul state but maneuvering to pick up the Puffers spawned is not recommended. Multiple enemies spawning can allow her to do 4 enhanced Normal Attacks.
Basics of Using Mualani (Beginner Guide)
As a character from Natlan, Mualani has access to the Nightsoul mechanic. For Mualani, the Nightsoul state allows her to ride her surfboard and deal powerful "oneshot" Hydro attacks.
What Is Nightsoul State / Nightsoul Points for Mualani?
Mualani enters Nightsoul state when you use her Elemental Skill. She'll gain a glowing outline and tattoos in this state and a gauge will appear beside her. The gauge displays the amount of Nightsoul points she has and it decreases continuously. Reaching 0 Nightsoul points will end her Nightsoul state.
▼ In-Game Description of Nightsoul State of Mualani
Nightsoul Burst for Mualani
Nightsoul Burst is an effect that activates when Elemental Damage is dealt when there's a Natlan character in the team. This effect has different buffs for the Natlan character.
In Mualani's case, it gives her Wavechaser's Exploits that increases the damage of her Elemental Burst. She can gain up to 3 stacks of this.

How To Use Mualani?
How To Use?
▼Mualani is a Hydro DPS that deals damage with Elemental Skill
▼Ride a shark to mark enemies 3 times and use Normal Attack to deal a huge amount of damage.
▼After using Normal Attack, an AoE follow-up attack will be launched.
▼With Passive, the amount of attack is increased.
▼Elemental Burst is Amazing After using Nightsoul's Burst.
▼Amazing for traveling and exploration at Natlan.
Mualani Is A Hydro DPS Deals Damage With Elemental Skill
Mualani is a Hydro DPS that rides a shark and surf around while marking enemies. While she does not deal damage frequently,
each of her attacks deals powerful damage
. Playing Mualani with an off-field Pyro and dealing Vaporize will effectively double her damage.
Get 3 Stacks Of Wave Charge
While riding the shark, hitting the enemies will give you one Wave Momentum Stack and accumulating 3 stacks will greatly increase the damage.
The amount of damage between 3 and 2 stacks differs greatly
so generally you would use 3 stacks before using Normal Attack.
Effects Of Elemental Skill
Nightsoul's Blessing
- Ride shark and increase speed and resistance to interruption.
- Consumes 10 Nightsoul's Blessing every seconds. (Initial Value: 60)
- Effect ends when Nightsoul or Phlogiston
-
When enemy is hit they are marked and Mualani gain stat.
(Max 3 Stacks)
- Normal Attack is buffed to Shark's Bite.
Sharky's Bite
- Hydro DMG that scales from HP.
- Increase damage depending on the no. of Charge.
- No. of charges reset after using Normal Attack.
- After attacking, Marked enemies will receive a follow-up missile attack. (Max. 5)
└  Follow-Up attack damage launches after Normal Attack.
└ The more enemies, the damage lessens. (Max 28%/ 3 Enemies)
- Enemies marked will have their mark remove when hit by Missile or Normal Attack.
You Can Deal Damage To Multiple Enemies With Follow-Up Missiles
When you marked multiple enemies, after using Normal Attack,
an additional missile attack will hit the marked enemies
. The follow-up damage is also powerful enough to serve as an AoE option for Mualani. However, take note that if there are more than 2 marked enemies, you will deal less damage.
With Passive, Heat-Resistant Freshwater Floater, Mualani Will Attack More
Passive Heat-Resistant Freshwater Floater
- When buffed Normal Attack hits an enemy, summon one Puffer.
- Getting one Puffer will give you 20 Nightsoul Points.
- During Nightsoul's Blessing is active, only 2 Puffer can be summoned.
After unlocking Heat-Resistant Freshwater Floater Passive, when Normal Attack is done, a puffer will appear allowing you to gain a max of 40 Nightsoul.
With 40 points, your skill duration increases by around 4 seconds.
allowing you to increase the number of hits you can do during Elemental Skill.  If you are in Natlan, with Phlogiston, you can further increase the duration of Skill.
Elemental Burst Is More Powerful After Using Nightsoul's Burst
What Is Nightsoul's Burst?
- Happens when A Natlan Character deals Elemental DMG.
- The Burst itself does not have damage.
- Activates faster when there are more Natlan Characters
└1/2/3 charas : 18/12/9 seconds
Elemental Burst is a one-shot attack that has high damage. When the Natlan Characters's special ability, Nightsoul's Burst, is activated it gives an increase Burst DMG so using Elemental Burst. With more Natlan characters, the cooldown of Nightsoul's Burst is also lessen so using Natlan characters in the team is also recommended.
Amazing For Traveling And Exploration
Above Water
Natlan's Gimmick
The Elemental Skill, Shark Surfboard, not only allows you to travel fast and above water, it also allows you to ride special gimmicks at Natlan. With Phlogiston, Elemental Skill last for a long time.
Should You Pull for Mualani's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Both Her Constellations and Signature Will Yield Good Results
Signature VS Constellation
C3+ > C2 > C1 or
Surf's Up
Mualani's C1 and Signature Weapon are relatively tied when it comes to increasing her damage, but can produce a notable boost in performance when both are present.
In general, the more you invest into her Constellations and her Signature Weapon refinements, the more significant the increase in damage will be.
It is recommended to pull for whichever is more accessible for you, taking into account the Character and Weapon Banner lineup as well as available funds.
Recommendation if You Have
Sacrificial Jade
C1 > C2
Sacrificial Jade is a great 4-star option for Mualani, especially at higher refinement levels. You can opt to start pulling for her constellations in this case instead.

Effect
Combining her mastery of speed and the waves and water, Mualani can surf anytime, anywhere. After using this skill, Mualani will gain 60 Nightsoul points and enter the Nightsoul's Blessing state.
Nightsoul's Blessing: Mualani
Effect
Continuously consume Nightsoul points. When they are depleted or when the skill is used again, Mualani's Nightsoul's Blessing will end. The state itself has the following traits:
・Mualani mounts her Sharky Surfboard, increasing Mualani's Movement SPD and interruption resistance. Mualani can move on water and Liquid Phlogiston while this state is active, and she will take no DMG from the latter.
・Normal Attacks will be converted to Sharky's Bites, dealing Nightsoul-aligned Hydro DMG based on Mualani's Max HP. Sharky's Bites can be used in mid-air. DMG dealt this way is considered Normal Attack DMG.
・When she makes contact with opponents in this state, Mualani applies "Marked as Prey" to them and gains 1 Wave Momentum stack. Max 3 stacks. 1 stack can be gained from the same opponent every 0.7s.

Effect

If more than 1 opponent is the target of Sharky's Bite and Shark Missiles, the DMG dealt will decrease, with 28% being the maximum decrease when at least 3 opponents are targeted.

Crests And Troughs
Effect
After her Nightsoul points are depleted, Mualani will switch to consuming Phlogiston to maintain her Nightsoul's Blessing. While in an area with Phlogiston Mechanics within Natlan, she can use Nightsoul Transmission: Mualani. When the active character is currently sprinting, swimming, in a movement mode caused by certain Talents, or at a certain height in the air, the following will trigger when switching to Mualani: Mualani will enter the Nightsoul's Blessing state and gain 40 Nightsoul points. Nightsoul Transmission can be triggered once every 10s by your own team. Additionally, Mualani's Nightsoul point or Phlogiston consumption while moving on water and Liquid Phlogiston during Nightsoul's Blessing is decreased by 75% while in Natlan, and her Nightsoul point consumption is decreased by 35% while doing the same outside Natlan.

Effect

Who Is Mualani?
Guide and Watersports Shop Owner from the People of the Springs
Mualani is known as a very friendly and cheerful person from the People of the Springs tribe in Natlan. She owns a watersports shop and also works as a guide, helping people traverse the varying dangers and wonders of Natlan.
Holder of an Ancient Name belonging to the Heroes of Natlan
Mualani is one of the Six Heroes of Natlan, bearing the Ancient Name of Umoja. Her Ancient Name means Unity. While modern day Natlan is no longer war torn like before, Mualani's positivity and friendliness allows her to easily befriend the other Ancient Name bearers.
Best Friends with Kachina
Kachina is one of Mualani's dearest friends. During the Natlan Archon Quest, their strong friendship is evident and it was also revealed that Mualani would go all-out for saving a friend.

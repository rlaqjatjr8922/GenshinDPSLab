Combos and How to Play Mualani
Mualani is a Hypercarry DPS who attacks slowly but each hit
deals massive DMG. Because of her slow attack frequency, other
characters in Mualani's teams can setup reactions easily without her
Hydro
getting in
the way. Because of this she is best used in
Vaporize
teams.
Mualani's Rotation is:
Surfshark Wavebreaker
Normal Attack immediately
Build 3 Wave Momentum Stacks
Normal Attack (Sharky Surging)
Build 3 Wave Momentum Stacks
Normal Attack (Sharky Surging)
Build 3 Wave Momentum Stacks
Normal Attack (Sharky Surging)
Boomsharka-laka
In AoE situations you simply spam Sharky Surging but this
will heavily depend on the situation. Most grouped situations use
weaker enemies which can make it harder to keep the enemies grouped close to Mualani
because they die too easily.
3.1.
Surfshark Wavebreaker and Wave Momentum Stacks
Mualani's core mechanic is based around her Elemental skill
Surfshark Wavebreaker
.
This allows her to surf on her shark and consumes Nightsoul points. While on the
wavebreaker, mualani can make contact with enemies and applies the "Marked as Prey"
marker to them. This also grants a Wave Momentum stack up to a maximum of 3 stacks.
This also has a 0.7s cooldown on the same enemy, meaning after almost
a second if you are in contact with the same enemy you will
gain an additional Wave Momentum stack. This is useful especially in larger
boss fights.
Using Normal Attacks in this state will convert them into
Sharky's Bites. This damage scales based on how many
Wave Momentum Stacks Mualani has.
Always do this on 3 stacks
as 3 stacks
is a significant damage upgrade over 1/2 stacks. Up to 5 nearby marked enemies will also
take damage from Shark Missiles which provides Mualani with AoE coverage.

Elemental Skill
Surfshark Wavebreaker
Combining her mastery of speed and the waves and water, Mualani can
    surf anytime, anywhere. After using this skill, Mualani will gain 60
    Nightsoul points and enter the Nightsoul's Blessing state.
Nightsoul's Blessing: Mualani
Continuously consume Nightsoul points. When they are depleted or when
      the skill is used again, Mualani's Nightsoul's Blessing will end. The
      state itself has the following traits:
Mualani mounts her Sharky Surfboard, increasing Mualani's Movement SPD and interruption resistance. Mualani can move on water and liquid Phlogiston while this state is active, and she will take no DMG from the latter.
Normal Attacks will be converted to Sharky's Bites, dealing Nightsoul-aligned Hydro DMG based on Mualani's Max HP. Sharky's Bites can be used in mid-air. DMG dealt this way is considered Normal Attack DMG.
When she makes contact with opponents in this state, Mualani applies "Marked as Prey" to them and gains 1 Wave Momentum stack. Max 3 stacks. 1 stack can be gained from the same opponent every 0.7s.
Wave Momentum and Marked as Prey
When Mualani uses Sharky's Bite, her DMG dealt increases based on Wave
      Momentum stacks. When she has 3 stacks, Sharky's Bite will be converted to
      Sharky's Surging Bite, further increasing her DMG
      and removing all her stacks when hitting an opponent.
When Shark Bite hits opponents Marked as Prey, that mark is removed,
      and she will fire Shark Missiles at up to 5 nearby opponents Marked as
      Prey, dealing DMG to them equal to this Sharky's Bite instance and clearing
      their Marks. If more than 1 opponent is the target of Sharky's Bite and
      Shark Missiles, the DMG dealt will decrease, decreasing at most
      to 72% of the original DMG when 3 opponents are targeted.
Elemental Burst
Boomsharka-laka
Fires a Super Shark Missile that can track opponents, dealing AoE
Hydro
DMG based on Mualani's Max HP.

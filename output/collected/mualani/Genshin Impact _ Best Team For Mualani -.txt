▼Classic Vape Team

Mualani is a character that produces high damage in one hit. Increasing that one hit damage with Vaporize reaction is very important to Mualani. Kazuha will buff the damage while also spreading Pyro while Zhongli prevents interruption of combos while providing debuff to the enemies.
Rotation For Classic Vape Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Dehya's Elemental Skill.
2
Switch to Kazuha and use Elemental Skill then Burst.
3
Switch to Zhongli and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
4
Switch to Mualani and use Elemental Skill to hit enemies 3 times and deal Normal Attack Combo.
5
Use Elemental Burst as a finish.
6
Repeat from no. 1

This team functions similarly to the Vape team but uses Dendro character instead of Anemo. You can also use Anemo for damage boost.  In this team, Thoma provides Off-field Pyro application while giving shields. Emilie's skills will provide off-field Dendro and produce strong damage because Burning is constantly happening in this comp. Sigewinne is the healer that also provides Elemental Skill Buff.
Rotation For Burn-Vape Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Emilie's Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to Thoma and use his Elemental Burst then his Elemental Skill
3
Switch to Sigewinne and use Elemental Skill.
4
Switch then to Mualani and use her Elemental Skill → Normal Attack.
6
Finish with Elemental Burst.
5
Repeat from no. 1

- Nahida greatly increases Elemental Mastery for powerful Vapes.

This comp focuses on raw Hydro DMG with no reaction. This has a flexible rotation but mainly relies on Furina for increasing damage.
Rotation For Mono Hydro Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina's Elemental Skill then her Elemental Burst
2
Switch then to Kazuha and use his Elemental Skill → Elemental Burst.

5
Finish with Elemental Burst.

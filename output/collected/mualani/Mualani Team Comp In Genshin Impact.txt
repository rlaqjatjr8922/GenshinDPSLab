As
Genshin Impact
transitions into the Natlan region with the 5.0 update, one of the characters players will be able to obtain is
Mualani
, an impressive
new 5-star Hydro DPS character
, with a power matching of
Neuvillette
's. Her unique kit, featuring new mechanics for combat and exploration specific to Natlan characters, allows her to deal consistent and powerful Hydro damage, making her a versatile addition to any team.
Considering her role as a main DPS, when building a team around Mualani, it’s crucial to maximize her Hydro DPS potential by pairing her with characters who can support and enhance her damage. Mualani excels in teams where she can consistently trigger Hydro-based reactions, in particular Vaporize. Additionally, Burn-Vape and Mono-Hydro team compositions are also viable options for her. Here's the best team compositions for Mualani in
Genshin Impact
, while showcasing as many different character options as possible.
Mualani + Xiangling + Kazuha + Zhongli
A standard Vaporize team composition for Mualani focuses on amplifying her slower but powerful Hydro attacks with consistent Pyro application, enabling the Vaporize reaction for massive damage. Unlike Neuvillette, whose fast-paced attacks make it challenging to set up Vaporize reactions effectively, Mualani’s slower and stronger hits are perfectly suited for this reaction, making it a highly effective choice for maximizing her DPS output.
In this team composition, Mualani serves as the main DPS, dealing heavy Hydro damage and triggering Vaporize reactions with
Xiangling
’s Pyro application through her Elemental Skill and Burst,
while Kazuha as the Anemo support
, offering crowd control and elemental resistance shredding through his Swirl reactions, while also helping with Elemental application on an AoE setting. Lastly,
Zhongli
further decreases the enemies RES, while also providing a shield. Alternative options like
Dehya
can replace
Xiangling for Pyro application
, or
Sucrose
can replace Kazuha if players don't have access to him.

Mualani

Mualani + Emilie + Thoma + Sigewinne
A Burn-Vape team composition built around Mualani
leverages the Burning reaction
, followed by powerful Vaporize attacks, creating a synergy that enhances Mualani's Hydro damage output. This composition takes advantage of the unique interaction between Dendro and Pyro to set up continuous burning, which then applies consistent Pyro, which Mualani can capitalize on with her consistent Hydro attacks.
In this team setup, Mualani remains the main DPS,
Emilie
, the Dendro applicator, who is also highly suitable for Burning reaction when combined with a suitable Pyro applier, which in this case will be
Thoma
. To add on to the Hydro Resonance, as well as a healer spot,
Sigewinne
works very well, especially considering that she can even work as a sub-DPS if build in a specific way. For those looking to adjust the team,
Nahida can replace Emilie as the Dendro enabler
. Similarly, Xiangling or Dehya can replace Thoma for a more aggressive Pyro application. If more healing or a buffer is needed, Sigewinne can be swapped for Kokomi for reliable Hydro application and healing, or
Bennett
, who provides healing and an attack boost but sacrifices some of the reaction consistency due to the lack of continuous Hydro application. However, this essentially should not be a problem, since Mualani can apply Hydro consistently on her own. In fact, this approach could prevent other Hydro Characters in the team to steal some of the Vape reactions from Mualani, as it would be the case with some options like
Furina
.

Mualani + Furina + Kazuha + Kokomi
A Mono Hydro team composition centered around Mualani focuses on amplifying Hydro damage through synergy and support from other Hydro characters, bypassing the need for Vaporize reactions. This setup, while more 5-star heavy and requiring higher investment to match the raw power of a Vape team, still offers a formidable and consistent damage output.
In this team, Mualani once again serves as the primary DPS, while Furina acts as a support and sub-DPS, enhancing the team's overall Hydro damage through her Elemental Skill and Burst while also providing buffs, or potentially healer. Kazuha plays a crucial role as an Anemo support, grouping enemies with his crowd control abilities and shredding their Hydro resistance with his Elemental Skill and Burst, further boosting Mualani's damage. Kokomi takes on the role of healer and another Hydro applier, while ensuring that characters remain over 50% to get the most benefit out of Furina's kit. For those looking for a less premium options, Furina can be replaced by
Xingqiu
, who offers excellent Hydro application and defensive utility with his Elemental Burst. Kazuha can be swapped out for
Jean
or
Sucrose
. Kokomi on the other hand can be replaced by other Hydro users like
Sigewinne
,
Mona
or
Candace
if necessary.

SUBSCRIPTION
DIGITAL
Step into Teyvat, a vast world teeming with life and flowing with elemental energy.
You and your sibling arrived here from another world. Separated by an unknown god, stripped of your powers, and cast into a deep slumber, you now awake to a world very different from when you first arrived.
Thus begins your journey across Teyvat to seek answers from The Seven — the gods of each element. Along the way, prepare to explore every inch of this wondrous world, join forces with a diverse range of characters, and unravel the countless mysteries that Teyvat holds...
MASSIVE OPEN WORLD
Climb any mountain, swim across any river, and glide over the world below, taking in the jaw-dropping scenery each step of the way. And if you stop to investigate a wandering Seelie or strange mechanism, who knows what you might discover?
ELEMENTAL COMBAT SYSTEM
Harness the seven elements to unleash elemental reactions. Anemo, Electro, Hydro, Pyro, Cryo, Dendro, and Geo interact in all sorts of ways, and Vision wielders have the power to turn this to their advantage.
Will you vaporize Hydro with Pyro, electro-charge it with Electro, or freeze it with Cryo? Your mastery of the elements will give you the upper hand in battle and exploration.
BEAUTIFUL VISUALS
Feast your eyes on the world around you, with a stunning art style, real-time rendering, and finely tuned character animations delivering you a truly immersive visual experience. Lighting and weather all change naturally over time, bringing every detail of this world to life.
SOOTHING SOUNDTRACK
Let the beautiful sounds of Teyvat draw you in as you explore the expansive world around you. Performed by the London Philharmonic Orchestra, the soundtrack changes seamlessly with the time and gameplay to match the mood.
BUILD YOUR DREAM TEAM
Team up with a diverse cast of characters in Teyvat, each with their own unique personalities, stories, and abilities. Discover your favorite party combinations and level up your characters to help you conquer even the most daunting of enemies and domains.
JOURNEY WITH FRIENDS
Team up with friends across various platforms to trigger more elemental action, tackle tricky boss fights, and conquer challenging domains together to reap rich rewards.
As you stand atop the peaks of Jueyun Karst and take in the rolling clouds and vast terrain stretching out before you, you may wish to stay in Teyvat a little longer... But until you are reunited with your lost sibling, how can you rest? Go forth, Traveler, and begin your adventure!

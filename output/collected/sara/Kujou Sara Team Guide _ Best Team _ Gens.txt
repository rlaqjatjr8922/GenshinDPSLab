Kujou Sara Team Overview
This Page provides the best Kujou Sara Team information. Kujou Sara will be Sub DPS in most of the situation. You can also swap the position of Kujou Sara and Keqing to make your own best team.
From SS tier to A tier, Kujou Sara best team can be formed in different ways including Thunder, Overload and Electro-Charged team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

Artifacts For Kujou Sara:
As for the artifacts, there are different sets you can work with. If you want to fully focus on her
elemental aspect
, you can go for the
Thundering Fury 4-piece set
. For getting good
burst damage and electro damage
, you can go for
2-piece Noblesse Oblige and 2-piece Thundering Fury.
If you’re only gonna use Sara for her
elemental burst
, having a
4-piece set of Noblesse Oblige or a 4-piece set of Emblem of Severed Fate
would give you massive damage. Constellation 3 Sara is also perfect for big burst damage since it increases the talent level of the Elemental Burst by 3, max up to 15.
Thundering Fury:
2-piece effect:
Electro DMG +15%
4-piece set:
Overload, Electro-Charged, and Superconduct damage +40%. Triggering those 3 reactions reduces Elemental Burst cooldown by 1 second. Can only activate once every 0.8 seconds
Noblesse Oblige:
2-piece effect:
Elemental Burst damage +20%.
4-piece effect:
Using an Elemental Burst increases all party members’ attack by 20% for 12s. This effect cannot stack.
Emblem Of Severed Fate:
2-piece effect:
Energy Recharge +20%
4-piece effect:
Increases Elemental Burst DMG by 25% of Energy Recharge. But, only a maximum of 75% bonus DMG can be obtained in this way.
The ideal stats for her artifacts would be Crit Rate, Crit Dmg, Atk%, Energy Recharge, and Electro Dmg.
Best Team Composition for Kujou Sara:
Kujou Sara’s Elemental Skill buffs the squad and restores teammates’ energy, making her excellent support. Her skill buff, however, only lasts 6 seconds, limiting the number of teams she may support. Genshin Impact Sara is great for characters that are snapshots or one-shot comps that don’t require a long buff duration. Pairing Sara up with an
Electro Dps
and a
Hydro character
would make a great team.
If you have these characters, this would be a great team comp for you:
DPS –
Beidou or Raiden Shogun
Sub-DPS –
Xingqui or Barbara
Support –
Kujou Sara
Enabler –
Childe or Mona
If you don’t have the above characters, here’s a F2P friendly team:
DPS –
Kujou Sara
Reaction –
Kaeya
Support –
Traveler Anemo
Healer –
Barbara
Overall, Genshin Impact Sara seems to be a
great Support or Sub DPS
, depends on how you build her, which is the beauty of this game. If you’re planning to summon for her,
good luck with summoning and farming!

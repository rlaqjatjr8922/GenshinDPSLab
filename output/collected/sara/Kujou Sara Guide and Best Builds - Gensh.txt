In order to understand Kujou Sara's build choices regarding her weapons, artifacts, and stats, 
you first need to understand how her buff works. Similar to
Bennett
's Elemental 
Burst mechanic, Sara's ATK buff is also based on her Total Base ATK. This means that it 
will only take into account Kujou Sara's Base ATk + her weapon's Base ATK. Stacking ATK on her artifacts 
will not give any bonus to her buff. If you want to maximize Kujou Sara's ATK buff, you need to 
wear the weapon with the highest Base ATK, and level her own level up to 90.
Knowing this, Kujou Sara's support build best weapon is the
Skyward Harp
with its 
Base 674 ATK. This weapon also has CRIT Rate as main stat, which makes it a great 
weapon for both Sara's personal DMG, and her buff capacity. Kujou Sara's support build best in slot F2P weapon is 
the
Fading Twilight
with high base ATK, energy recharge, and a good passive.
When building artifacts, for a Support Kujou Sara's build we recommand the
Emblem of Severed Fate
Artifact set for the Energy Recharge and Elemental Burs DMG including a 75% bonus DMG. 
If no one else wears a
Noblesse Oblige
set in your party you can also equip
Kujou Sara with this one.

3.
Kujou Sara Combos and How to Play
The goal with Kujou Sara is to be able to Burst and use her skill at least once every rotation
in order to get the buffs up for your team. Her gameplay changes slightly depending on 
if you have her at C0 or C2+.
At C0 she is a bit clunky to play. Every time you do her Elemental Skill, you have to 
follow up with a Charged Attack. This leaves her extremely vulnerable to interruption, 
and if you get interrupted you get no buff.
Once you get her C2, the ATK buff basically pops up automatically when you use her Elemental Skill.
This allows you to skip the Charged Attack.
Once you have that in mind, her rotation mostly revolves around quickly swapping her in, 
to her Burst when available, her Skill each rotation, follow up with a Charged Attack 
if she's C0, otherwise swap back to your Main DPS and use the buff.

a powerful

Kujou Sara Skill Guide & How To Use Kujou Sara
Recommended Rotation For Kujou Sara?
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Kujou Sara's Skill, shoot a Charge Attack and quickly switch to your DPS.
2
Deal as much damage as you can with your DPS while the Buff is up.
*
You can use Elemental Burst to deal bigger AoE Damage to enemies and provide the same buff for the team.
Uses Of Elemental Skill
Provides ATK Buff For The Team Through Charged Attack
Elemental Skill of Kujou Sara changes the Charged Attack of Kujou Sara to a special one that drops an Electro mine that explodes after a few seconds dealing AoE Electro DMG.
Upon explosion, it gives you ATK Bonus that scales from the Based ATK of Kujou Sara that last 6 seconds from the explosion.
Uses Of Elemental Burst
Provides Same ATK Buff As Elemental Skill
Elemental Burst provides the same buff as Elemental Skill with the same duration. The buff does not stack but it can be used to lengthen the duration of the Buff.
Powerful DMG That Spreads
Elemental Burst is a great finisher because it deals a powerful one shot of lightning. From the center of the lightning, Stormclusters will appear around it.
Should You Pull for Kujou Sara's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Aim for C6 if You're Able To
Constellation Priority
C6 > C2 > C4
C6 Is Ideal for Electro Damage Dealers
Kujou Sara's kit wants her to be the dedicated Electro buffer in a team with an Electro Hypercarry, and shares a similar C6 design concept with
Gorou
and
Faruzan
. Her C6 gives a sizeable Electro Crit DMG bonus, and when considered in tandem with the rest of her constellations, makes her function a lot better in her intended role in comparison to her C0 version.
C2 is an okay stopping point because it lets Sara activate her Attack buff without using Charged Attacks. But
if you would like to upgrade Sara's performance in these teams, such as with
Raiden Shogun
, then aiming for C6 if you have the means to do so is highly recommended.

Who Is Kujou Sara?
The General Of The Tenryou Commission
Kujou Sara is the swift and resolute general of the Tenryou Commission, the arm that handles the security of Inazuma. She leads the Inazuman Army and is the chief enforcer of any laws created by the Shogunate.
Ever Loyal To The Electro Archon
Kujou Sara's loyalty to the Electro Archon runs deep. She believes in the Raiden Shogun wholeheartedly and will do anything and everything to ensure that the Shogun's will is enforced.

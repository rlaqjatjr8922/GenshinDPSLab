Kujou Sara Is An ATK Buff Support For Electro
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Electro DPS Support
- At C6, Kujou Sara is an Electro DPS buffer.
- Any Electro DPS can work but ATK-scaling DPS are better.
- Other supports buff other DMG-boosting buff type to not overlap with Kujou Sara.
2nd
▼ ATK Buff Support
- Outside C6, Kujou Sara can be used as an ATK buff for any DPS.
- ATK-scaling DPS like Arlecchino is preferred.
Kujou Sara is an ATK buff support with a mechanic similar to Bennett. Her ATK buff scales on her base ATK which means she needs max Character Level and a high base ATK bow to maximize her buff. At C6, she can now buff Electro character's CRIT DMG. C2 allows for less clunky gameplay as support.

C6 Sara is preferred for this team but any Constellation level can be used. Clorinde is an ATK-scaling Electro DPS who can benefit the most from Sara's ATK buff. Nahida and Xilonen additionally boost the damage dealt.
Rotation Of Electro DPS Support
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Clorinde and use her Burst
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with Normal Attacks
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Nahida and use her Skill to mark enemies then use her Burst
5
Switch to Sara and use her Burst
6
Switch to Clorinde and use her Skill twice for a Lunging Attack
7
Use Clorinde's Normal Attacks to deal damage
8
When her Bond of Life is full bar, use her Skill again
9
Repeat from 1
If Sara's Burst is not ready yet, you can use her Skill then Aimed Shot for the ATK buff.

Outside of Electro DPS teams, Sara can be used to buff the ATK of characters. She will be having a higher ER requirement depending on your team composition.
Rotation Of ATK Buff Support
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Arlecchino and use her Skill to mark the enemy
2
Switch to Raiden Shogun and use her Skill to summon an Eye of Judgement
3
Switch to Chevreuse and use her Burst then held Skill to shoot an Overcharged Ball
4
Switch to Sara and use her Burst to buff the next character
5
Switch back to Arlecchino and use her Charged Attack to gain Pyro infusion
6
Use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage
7
Use Arlecchino's Burst when buffs are about to expire
8
Repeat from 1
If Sara's Burst is not ready yet, you can use her Skill then Aimed Shot for the ATK buff.

Bennett's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Bennett team composition guide and character synergy 
overview for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams 
for Bennett, alongside recommended character pairings that help 
maximize the value of his massive ATK buffs and healing.
Bennett is a premier healer and ATK buffer, meaning he can be paired with almost 
any DPS who benefits from ATK stats. His Constellation 6 also provides a
Pyro
DMG Bonus, making him exceptionally powerful for 
Pyro DPS characters compared to other options. Additionally, pairing Bennett with 
another Pyro character unlocks Pyro Resonance, providing an additional team-wide ATK boost.

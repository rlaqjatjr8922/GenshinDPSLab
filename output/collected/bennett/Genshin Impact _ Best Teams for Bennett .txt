Bennett Is an All-Rounder ATK Buff Support and Healer
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Pyro DPS Support Bennett
★★★★★
- Bennett synergizes well with nearly all of the available Pyro On-Field DPSes because most of them scale with ATK%.
- Adding him to the team with a Pyro DPS also activates Pyro Resonance, which gives another teamwide ATK% buff.
▼ Anemo DPS Support Bennett
★★★★・
- All of the Anemo On-Field DPSes appreciate Bennett's generous ATK buff with his Burst since they all have abilities that scale off of this stat, and nearly all of them have Bennett included in their best team setups.
▼Electro DPS Support Bennett
★★★・・
- Bennett has good synergy with a few Electro characters, and is usable with the rest of them.
- Notably, he can synergize well with these Electro DPSes in an Overload team, alongside
Chevreuse
.
▼ Dendro DPS Support Bennett (Burning)
★★★・・
-
Kinich
and
Emilie
are the only real Dendro DPSes that work well with Bennett, but it's easy to consider Bennett one of the best supports for either Burning-focused characters.
▼ Geo DPS Support Bennett
★★★・・
- While there are DEF-scaling Geo DPSes,
Noelle
and
Navia
can make use of Bennett's ATK buff thanks to their ability mechanics and scalings.
▼ Cryo DPS Support Bennett
★★★・・
- While most Cryo On-Field DPSes nowadays would prefer being run in a freeze team with
Escoffier
, some Cryo DPSes like
Wriothesley
can be run in a Melt team with Bennett.
▼ Hydro DPS Support Bennett
★★・・・
- Since a majority of the Hydro DPSes primarily scale with HP%, there are very few Hydro DPS teams that want or can use Bennett's ATK% buff, with the only notable ones being
Childe
and
Ayato
.
Bennett is well known for his ATK% increase from his stationary Elemental Burst. His buffing and healing are usable in a lot of teams, which speaks a lot about his versatility as an Off-Field Support.

Bennett's ATK% increase and healing get a lot of mileage in Anemo DPS teams, especially since a good majority of the available Anemo DPSes (at C0) scale with attack or have teammates that really appreciate Bennett's buffs.
Rotation of Anemo DPS Support Bennett
No.
Procedure
1
Start the rotation with Furina's Skill. You can dash to cancel the long animation. Use her Burst immediately afterwards.
2
Make use of Bennett's Skill (Tap) into Burst combo.
3
Switch to Iansan. Deploy her Skill, then Normal Attack (x1), followed by her Burst.
4
Swap to Chasca. Use her Skill, then hold her Normal Attack button to continuously fire her special Charged Attacks. She can use up to 5 Charged Attacks before her Nightsoul Blessing state ends. Use her Burst after 4 Charged Attacks if it's available.
5
Use Bennett's Skill (tap) again.
6
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire.

7
Repeat Step 5 on Navia.
8
Repeat from Step 1.

Rotation of Hydro DPS Support Bennett
No.
Procedure
1
Start the rotation with Childe Charged Shot (x1). Aim to hit an enemy to apply Hydro. Alternatively, you can press his Skill then swap out immediately to simply apply Hydro instead.
2
Swap to Bennett, then use his Burst.
3
Deploy Kazuha's Burst, then his Skill (tap).
4
Swap to Xiangling. Use her Burst first, then her Skill.
5
Switch back to Childe. Use his Burst (ranged mode) followed by his Skill.
└ Best combo after entering his Skill state: [ Normal Attack →  Charged Attack ] x6, you can jump after the Charged Attacks to conserve stamina.
6
Use Bennett's Skill (tap), then let Xiangling catch the Energy particles generated. Repeat this process until Xiangling's Burst is ready.
7
Do Charged Shot (x1) on Childe.
8
Redeploy Xiangling's Skill.
9
Use Kazuha's Skill (hold), then Burst.
10
Repeat from Step 5.

Genshin Impact Bennett Guide : Build, Weapons and Artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
November 14, 2023
Tips
Share:
Are you looking to put together the most optimal team on
Genshin Impact
? You can count on
Bennett
, a 4-star Pyro character, to help you achieve this. He is without a doubt one of the best support characters in the game. In addition to healing your heroes, he boosts their attacks! But for him to perform well, you need to choose his
equipment
carefully. You will find many tips and tricks in this comprehensive and strategic
guide on Bennett from Genshin Impact
. You'll know which
build
is best for him:
weapons
,
artefacts
,
skills
to mount and above all how to play him!
How to get Bennett on Genshin Impact ?
First of all, Bennett must be unblocked. If you haven't already done so, don't worry. There are several possibilities. First of all, you can rely on
wishes
. He can fall into the
Standard Wish
or the
Character Event Wish
at any time. But, given the large number of characters available, your chances of getting Bennett in the draw are quite low.
However, you can buy it directly in exchange for 34
asteria
until 30 November 2021. These can be obtained by performing
summons
.
And, if you already have Bennett, increasing his
constellations
can make him even stronger. We reassure you, it is not essential to mount him in C6. It is not even recommended, depending on the team you are playing with. On the other hand, we can only recommend C1. The character's main interest lies in its ultimate ability:
Wonderful Journey
, which, in addition to healing your heroes, boosts their attack. The C1 Journey
Perspective
lifts the HP restriction on the attack bonus (which basically works on characters with over 70% health) and increases Bennett'sATK .
Want to try the banners, but don't have enough gems? Get some for free with the
code
trick. We've put together a
list of codes
for you
Genshin Impact. Maybe you can unlock Bennett with extra loot!
What is the best equipment for Bennett?
Now that you know how to get Bennett, it's time to level him up. To level up Bennett in Genshin Impact, you will need to collect several resources:
Fragments, chunks and gemstones of
agnidus agate
as well as
fire seeds
while facing the
flaming tree
;
Paddle Chrysanthemums
that grow mainly in
Mondstadt
around the statue of Ventlevé and in the Stormterror lair;
Treasure Hoarder Insignia
, silver and gold raven insignia, collectable from treasure hoarders.
But also a lot of gold and XP scrolls. The journey will be long. However, it will allow you to take care of Bennett's build on the way.
The best artifacts for Bennett
Bennett is mainly a support character for his ultimate Wonderful Journey. Choose the stats of his artefacts carefully in his equipment: try to increase his
energy recharge
as much as possible (so that he can use the attack as often as possible). We also advise you to take into account his
health points
, in order to reinforce his healing abilities. The trick is to remember that the virtuous field it generates heals in proportion to its maximum HP. It is only by taking into account all the specificities of a character that you can achieve a
synergy worthy of his best equipment
.
The best set to improve your stats is the
Noblesse oblige
in 5 stars. You can get it in the
Clear Pool and Mountain Cavern
dungeon, located in Liyue at Mount Aoang. The Bennett build with the 4-piece set increases the attack of all characters by 20% for 12 seconds after Bennett performs Fantastic Voyage.
Until you get your hands on them, you can opt for the 2-piece
Scholar's Bookmark
and
The Exile
sets with 4-stars. These sets both increase energy recharge by 20%, giving a total improvement of 40%.
As for the main stats, focus on VP on the flower, VP % on the cup, healing on the crown, VP or energy on the hourglass and attack on the feather.
The best weapons for Bennett
Unfortunately, sets are not everything! To be successful, Bennett's choice of weapon in Genshin Impact is crucial. The
Skyward Blade
is the best equipment option. But it is a one-handed 5-star weapon, so it is rather difficult to obtain.
If you don't have it, my advice is to go for the
Favonius Sword
as an alternative for energy recharge. If you prefer a more DPS-oriented build, the
Aquila Favonia
or the
Prototype Rancour
(which you can craft yourself) are worth considering. And, if you don't have any, you can equip Bennett with the
Skyrider Sword
(3 stars) in the meantime. The latter increases ATK and movement speed.
How to play Bennett in Genshin Impact ?
Like the other characters, Bennett has 3 abilities. His normal
Lucky Sword
attack allows him to chain up to 5 sword strikes. The charged attack, on the other hand, uses up stamina to deliver two quick strikes.
The
elemental attack Surpassion
triggers Pyro damage. The
evaporation effect
is activated if a hydro attack then hits the target. The damage is then doubled.
But Bennett's ultimate skill is his most interesting ability. You can charge it faster by using Passion Overload several times in a row.
In short, our advice for
talent upgrade
is to focus mainly on Fantastic Voyage, then Passion Overload. If you play Bennett in support, you will hardly use his normal attack.
Which team should Bennett play in?
Bennett is an excellent support: he looks after and boosts his partners at the same time. He fits perfectly into almost any team. By playing another Pyro hero, you can benefit from the elemental resonance
Flames of Fervour
, which reduces Cryo effects by 40% and increasesATK by 25%.
If you are lucky enough to have 5 star characters such as
Tartaglia
and
Kazuha
(which you can replace with
Sucrose
a very efficient 4 star anemo), you can build one of the best teams of the moment by adding
Bennett
and
Xiangling
(4 stars). Kazuha allows you to gather all the enemies, which you can then hit with all the power of Tartaglia, whose Bennett increases the damage considerably and the
Pyronado
Xiangling, which is a fire tornado that attacks all nearby enemies.
You now know how to
equip and play Bennett in Genshin Impact
! All you have to do is try him out with the sets we've recommended. We have no doubt that with this guide and our advice, he will quickly become an indispensable asset for your team: enough to make all your enemies tremble, whether in dungeons or in the spiral depths.

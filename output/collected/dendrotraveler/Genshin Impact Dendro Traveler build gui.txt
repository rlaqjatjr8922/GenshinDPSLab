Dendro Traveler’s abilities in Genshin Impact explained
Elemental Skill
With a flourish of your blade, you unleash a spray of razor-sharp leaves that go before you and deal Dendro DMG.
This is a straightforward skill where your character will perform a dash and apply Dendro DMG to all the enemies in range.
Elemental Burst
Calling upon the might of the flora all around you, you create a Lea Lotus Lamp. This Lamp will deal continuous Dendro DMG to opponents within its AoE.

This skill allows you to place a Dendro lamp in the field with a fixed radius. It deals Dendro DMG to opponents in this range and applies Dendro five times. You can increase it by unlocking the second constellation for the Traveler which increases the skill duration by 3 seconds.

When on the field, the Dendro lamp reacts differently to elements:
Hydro
– The lamp’s AoE and damage increase.
Electro –
The lamp’s ATK speed increases.
Pyro
– The lamp explodes in a few seconds and deals AoE Dendro DMG.
These reactions can happen only once which is why you must be careful during rotations.

How to play as the Dendro Traveler in Genshin Impact
Dendro reactions have turned out to be quite powerful in Genshin Impact, and the Dendro Traveler is great for applying the element to groups of enemies. Characters like Xingqiu, Fischl, and Ayaka are popular for the same reason.
Unlike many other Dendro characters released so far, the Dendro Traveler can fulfill their role perfectly even when off-field. The Elemental Burst is excellent for off-field Dendro application and you can quickly switch to your main DPS and trigger reactions.

Best artifacts for the Dendro Traveler in Genshin Impact
Four-piece Deepwood Memories
The four-piece Deepwood Memories set in Genshin Impact is perfect for Dendro characters and the Traveler is no exception. It increases the Dendro DMG bonus and reduces an opponent’s Dendro RES after a Skill or Burst hits them.
Four-piece Noblesse Oblige
The four-piece Noblesse Oblige grants increased Element Burst DMG in Genshin Impact. After you use an Elemental Burst, the ATK of party members also increases briefly.
The Noblesse Oblige is tailor-made for support characters. Dendro application will still be consistent and anyone who feels that the Traveler deals less damage should use this set.

As for artifact stats, you should focus on getting Energy Recharge that’ll allow you to spam your Elemental Burst on cooldown. Overall, you’ll need over 200 Energy Recharge that can be obtained from Artifacts as well as weapons.

For the best damage output, target a Dendro DMG Bonus Goblet and a Crit Rate/ Crit DMG circlet.
Best weapons for Dendro Traveler in Genshin Impact
Favonius Sword
The Favonius Sword in Genshin Impact grants a ton of Energy Recharge through the sub-stat and Energy Regeneration through the passive. As aforementioned, Energy Recharge is desirable for the Dendro Traveler and it is fairly easy to obtain the Favonius Sword than other five-star swords.
Article continues after ad
Freedom Sworn
The five-star Freedom Sworn in Genshin Impact grants
Elemental Mastery
and buffs your team’s Normal, Charged, and Plunging attack DMG. Because you’ll be relying a lot on Dendro with the Traveler, Elemental Mastery is a great stat.

Sapwood Blade
The Sapwood Blade is a free-to-play option for Genshin Impact players who do not have a Favonius Sword. It has a high base ATK and the 30.6% Energy Recharge will help you use your Elemental Burst more frequently.

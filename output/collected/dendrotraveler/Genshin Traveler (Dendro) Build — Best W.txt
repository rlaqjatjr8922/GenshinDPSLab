“Millennial Movement: Song of Resistance” increases Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attack DMG by
16% (20%, 24%, 28%, 32%)
and increases ATK by
20% (25%, 30%, 35%, 40%)
. Once this effect is triggered, you will not gain Sigils of Rebellion for 20s.
Of the many effects of the “Millennial Movement,” buffs of the same type will not stack.

Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler Guide : Build, weapons and artifacts
Hazulia
Updated
January 29, 2024
Tips
Share:
The
3.0 update
is a very good opportunity to play the Traveler again. He is currently the
best Dendro applicator
and is a very good
support
. The character will be able to trigger elemental reactions related to this element very easily. In this
complete guide
we explain how to equip and play the
Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler
. With our tips and tricks, you will know which artifacts and weapons to equip him with.
How to get the Dendro Traveler in Genshin Impact ?
To
unlock the Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler,
nothing could be easier. All you have to do is interact with a statue of the Seven in Sumeru. This item is available since the
Genshin Impact 3.0 update
. You will find a Dendro statue right next to the Abyss.
The Dendro Traveler constellations
To activate the Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler constellations, you will need
Memory of Flourishing Green
. You can get them by following the main quest in Sumeru.
With it, you will increase the Traveler's energy recharge (C1) and extend the duration of his elemental rampage by 3 seconds (C2). The C4 will allow the Dendro Traveler to boost his team's elemental mastery more easily.
How to play the Dendro Traveler in Genshin Impact ?
As with all his versions, the Dendro Traveler is mostly played as a support / sub-DPS in Genshin Impact. If his personal damage is not extraordinary, he will be able to trigger elemental reactions very easily thanks to his Dendro application.
With the elemental attack
Razorgrass Blade
, the Traveler executes a blade strike that applies the Dendro element, while charging particles of energy.
This ability will allow you to cast
Surgent Manifestation
, the elemental rampage of the Dendro Traveler, more quickly. The ability summons a
glowing lotus
that remains on the battlefield for 12 seconds. It attacks enemies and deals Dendro damage. You can modify it by contacting Hydro, Electro, or Pyro. Hydro increases the lotus' range and area of effect, Electro increases its attack speed, and Pyro causes it to explode for high area damage.
With the
Verdant Overgrowth
passive, the Luminous Lotus gains 1 stack of
Overflowing Lotuslight
every second. Each accumulation increases the elemental mastery of characters deployed in the area of effect by 6 points (up to a maximum of 10 times).
As you can see, Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler gameplay is essentially based on his
elemental rampage
. Throw this skill as often as possible to then attack your main DPS, his elemental mastery will then be boosted.
If you play the Dendro Traveler as a support, you may not mount his skills. It's mostly his
Dendro application
that matters. But if you want to increase his personal damage, focus only on his elemental rampage and elemental ability.
What is the best equipment for the Dendro traveler?
The main goal of the
Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler
is to launch his elemental rampage, but it is expensive: 80 energy points. It is recommended that you increase his energy recharge as much as possible.
Find out in this guide which are the
best artifacts and weapons to equip on Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler
.
The best artifacts for the Dendro Traveler
Deepwood Memories
The best artifact set to equip on the Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler is definitely the 4-piece
Deepwood Memories
set. With 4 pieces, you increase Dendro damage by 15% and with 4, you lower the Dendro resistance of enemies by 30% for 8 seconds after attacking them.
Gilded Dreams
If another character on your team is wearing this set, you can start on the
Gilded Dreams
set. It increases elemental mastery by 80 points and triggering an elemental reaction grants you a 14% damage bonus for each member of the same element as the equipped character. You'll get a 50 point elemental mastery bonus for each member of a different elemental type.
Emblem of Severed Fate
The
Emblem of Severed Fate
set is also available, either as a 4-piece set or as a 2-piece set to pair with 2 pieces of the Deepwood Memories set.
If you're just starting out, you can opt for one of these 4-star sets:
The Exile
Instructor
The Exile
: The 4-piece set increases the Traveler's energy recharge by 20% and restores your entire team's energy recharge by 2 points every 2 seconds for 6 seconds after an elemental rampage;
Instructor
: You increase your elemental mastery by 80 points and the mastery of the whole team by 120 points after an elemental rampage.
As for statistics, we advise you to use the energy recharge or the ATK% on the hourglass, the Dendro damage bonus on the cup and the critical rate or critical damage on the crown.
For secondary statistics, prefer :
Energy recharge;
From ATK%;
Critical Rate;
Critical damage;
From basic mastery.
The best weapons for the Dendro Traveler
To complete the
equipment of the Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler
, choose a one-handed sword providing elemental mastery or energy recharge like :
Freedom-Sworn (5★)
Skyward Blade (5★)
Sacrificial Sword (4★)
Freedom-Sworn
: This is the best weapon to equip on the traveler. It gives you a lot of
elemental control
and its passive increases the damage of the whole team after an elemental reaction;
Skyward Blade
: This sword increases your
energy recharge
and critical rate. The Elemental Unleash will also boost your attacks;
Sacrificial Sword
: This is one of the best options if you don't have a 5-star weapon. In addition to increasing the
energy recharge
, you will have a chance to reset the recharge time of the elemental attack;
Favonius Sword (4★)
Iron sting (4★)
Sapwood blade (4★)
Favonius Sword
: this weapon excels in providing
energy recharge
;
Iron Sting
: you increase your
elemental mastery
and the elemental damage inflicted on enemies;
Sapwood Blade
: This is a new weapon in 3.0 update to be crafted by the blacksmith. The sword increases the
energy recharge
and after triggering a reaction related to the element Dendro, the equipped character gains a 60 point elemental mastery bonus for 12 seconds every 20 seconds.
What are the elevation and skill materials of the Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler?
To
raise the Traveler to level 90 in Genshin Impact,
you will need:
1 Sliver, 9 Fragments, 9 Chunks and 6
Gemstones of Brilliant Diamond
, you will get them by increasing your adventure level;
168
Windwheel Asters
: you will find these flowers in Mondstadt. Locate them on the
interactive map of Teyvat
;
18
Damaged masks
, 30
Dirty masks
and 36
Sinister masks
to be collected at Hilichurl.
To increase the Traveler's skills, you will need :
Teaching of Ingenuity
,
Guide to Praxis
, and
Philisophies of Ingenuity
. These items are available on Tuesdays, Fridays and Sundays in the
Steeple of Ignorance
dungeon;
From
Fungal Spores
,
Luminescent Pollen
and
Crystalline Cyst Dust
to Loot on
Fungus
;
Tears of the Calamitous God
, which you will obtain by confronting
Magatsu Mitake Narukami no Mikoto
.
In which teams do you play the Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler ?
The Dendro Traveler can be played in any team based on Dendro reactions. All you have to do is choose the type of Dendro reactions you want to perform.
For example, you can increase the damage of your Electro characters by performing
Aggravate
(Dendro + Electro) in a team such as: the Dendro Traveler /
Raiden
/
Yae Miko
/ a healer (
Jean
Kokomi
,
Barbara
, etc.).
Aggravate increases Electro damage. By choosing a Hydro character, you will also perform
Sprawling Shots
(a Dendro core will be projected on the enemy). This also works with the Dendro Traveler /
Xingqiu
/
Fischl
/
Shinobu
.
You can also focus on
Burning
reactions (Pyro + Dendro): the Dendro Traveler / Collei /
Xiangling
/
Bennett
or
Thomas
.
That's it for this guide dedicated to the
Genshin Impact Dendro Traveler Guide
This is the best version of the Traveler. You can easily create Dendro reactions with the elemental rampage and have fun with the new reactions. To understand them better, please see our guide to the Dendro element.
You can also take a look at the
complete Traveler's Guide
to discover the build of its Anemo, Geo and Electro versions.

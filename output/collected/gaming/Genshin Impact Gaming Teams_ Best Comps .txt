Genshin Impact Gaming Teams: Best Comps
Heizo
February 1, 2024
Tips
Share:
Hailing from Liyue, Gaming in
Genshin Impact
is a 4-star Pyro damage dealer who uses his Wushou dance alongside Man Chai to burn and obliterate his opponents through plunging attacks. And despite being a 4-star character,
Gaming
may be able to
compete with stronger DPS characters in the meta
, provided that you pair him with the right characters to get the most out of his kit. Provided below is a list of
the best Genshin Impact Gaming Teams
you will ever find, and the best support characters for each type of team comp.

Genshin Impact Gaming Teams - Gaming Furina Vaporize
One of, if not the
best Genshin Impact gaming teams
in the current meta consists of Gaming,
Xianyun
,
Furina
, and
Bennett
.
These are among the best
Genshin Impact Characters
! With
Xianyun’s talents
revolving around
buffing plunging attack damage
while also providing
solid teamwide healing
, she is considered the
best support for Gaming
.
Furina
, on the other hand, is an excellent support that contributes
strong Fanfare buffs
and enables Gaming to benefit from the
reverse vaporize reaction
.
Finally,
Bennett
can slot himself in for
additional healing
, necessary to reset the cooldown of his skill during his elemental burst, while also providing
attack buffs
to the Wushou Dancer. Overall, the team centers around providing as many buffs as possible to Gaming while maintaining his health above 50%.
Here you can learn about the best
Genshin Impact Gaming Build
.
Gaming Genshin Impact National Team
Once again, another variant of the
Genshin Impact National Team
has arrived, and luckily, it works really well with our Wushou Dancer.
If you are a new player with only a few characters to pair with Gaming, then the
Gaming National Team
is a great choice. This
F2P-friendly
team includes
Xiangling
, Xingqiu, and Bennett.
Xingqiu
can provide
off-field damage
and
Hydro application
, enabling a
vaporize reaction
during Gaming's burst. Similarly,
Xiangling
also offers another source of
off-field Pyro damage
, which can be paired with Gaming's abilities.
Bennett is set to become the main support in this team and can provide
attack buffs and healing
. The only issue with Bennett, being the only healer of this team, is that
Gaming must stay inside Bennett's elemental burst
to receive buffs and healing, which can be tough when facing mobile enemies.
To go even further with this pyro 4-star character, check out our
Genshin Impact Gaming Materials tips
.
Mono-Pyro, on of the best Genshin Impact Gaming Teams
A
mono-pyro team
consisting of Gaming, Xianyun, Xiangling and Bennett is also recommended in this Genshin Impact Gaming teams article.
While this particular composition does not make use of any strong reactions other than
Pyro swirls
, it can be just as potent as the other teams. Alongside Gaming, whose elemental burst is amplified with the help of
Xianyun
and
Bennett
, Xiangling can contribute her personal damage to quickly finish off the opponent.
In this team composition,
Xianyun
is the
ideal Anemo unit
for Gaming to increase the plunging attack damage he deals. However, you can still replace Cloud Retainer with other characters such as
Kazuha
and
Sucrose
, provided they are utilizing the
Viridescent Venerer
artifact set.
Overload Genshin Impact Gaming Teams
A team consisting of Gaming,
Chevreuse
,
Fischl
, and Bennett can form a solid group that capitalizes on the overload composition in the game.
Bennett takes on the role of a
buffer and support
, while
Fischl
becomes the
sub-DPS
who is also responsible for the
electro application
necessary to trigger
overload
. The last slot, on the other hand, goes to
Chevreuse
, who is the perfect addition to the team.
Chevreuse serves as
one of the best supports for Pyro and Electro characters
in an
overload-focused composition
, providing
ATK attributes
and
reducing the resistance
of the enemies to both elements. With these buffs and debuffs, Gaming will be able to deal higher amounts of damage.
Burgeon Genshin Impact Gaming Teams
The final place in this recommended Genshin Impact Gaming Teams goes to the
Gaming Burgeon team
, which includes Gaming paired with any
Dendro and Hydro characters.
Burgeon requires three elements to occur;
Pyro, Dendro and Hydro
. Upon creating
Dendro Cores
from the reaction between Dendro and Hydro, you can trigger the second stage,
Burgeon
, by
hitting the cores with any pyro ability
. After doing so, the dendro core will explode and will deal AOE dendro damage to the enemies.
In this case,
Gaming
is responsible for triggering the Dendro cores. But, it is worth noting that since Gaming is an
on-field Pyro DPS
, the other components of the team should be able to
apply their respective elements off-field
. For Dendro, we recommend adding in the Dendro Archon,
Nahida
, as she shines best at
applying the Dendro aura
consistently with her elemental skill. Her burst on the other hand
supplies lots of EM
.
Meanwhile, the two Hydro units,
Kokomi
and
Xingqiu
both offers
hydro application
. In terms of build, one must know that since Gaming is the one triggering the Burgeon reaction, he should be able to stack as many
Elemental Mastery
as he can, instead of Pyro damage and Crit.
This concludes our recommended Genshin Impact Gaming Teams. Being a 4-star DPS, one may argue that there are characters that are better than him. However, this doesn't mean that he no longer deserves any love and investment as
he can perform well both in the overworld and in the abyss
especially when placed in the best Genshin Impact Gaming teams. If this ranking helped you, you can also find some of our best Genshin Impact recommended teams for
Lynette
, Nahida, Xianyun and more in our
Genshin Impact tips
section.

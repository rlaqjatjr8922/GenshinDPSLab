Gaming Overview
Gaming
is a Claymore Pyro user who specializes in Plunging ATK to deal
Pyro DMG
by pouncing forward and leaping to the air and using Plunging ATK.
Best Weapons For Gaming
Here is a different rarity of Claymores that are great to maximize his potential.
Best 3-Star Claymore Weapons
In the early game, it's difficult to find a 4 or 5-star weapon. Therefore, some 3-star weapons are the best for Gaming that can provide some additional
EM Reaction DMG
which can help you to clear monsters faster in the early game. Here is the best-recommended weapon list.
Bloodtainted Greatsword (\Reaction DMG)
Increase EM
When against opponents affected by Pyro, increase DMG by 12%
Quartz (Reaction DMG)
Increase EM stats
Increase ATK when Pyro triggers with another reaction.

4-Stars

5-star

EM

CRIT Rate

Serpent Spine (Main DPS)

Tidal Shadow (Main DPS)

Best 5-Star Claymore
If you want to maximize his potential, you can some of this 5 Stars Claymore to get better DMG. Here is the best-recommended weapon list.
Redhorn Stonethresher (Main DPS)
Increase CRIT DMG% stats
Increase his Normal/Charge ATK base on his DEF.
Beacon of the Reed Sea (Main DPS)
Increase CRIT Rate Stats

Best Artifacts for Gaming Main DPS
Crimson Witch of Flames
Marechaussee Hunter
Marechaussee Hunter
2PC:
Normal and Charged Attack DMG +15%
4PC:
When current HP increases or decreases, CRIT Rate will be increased by 12% for 5s. Max 3 stacks.
Crimson Witch of Flames
2PC:
Pyro DMG Bonus +15%
4PC:
Increases Overloaded, Burning, and Burgeon DMG by 40%. Increases Vaporize and Melt DMG by 15%. Using Elemental Skill Increases the 2-Piece Set Bonus by 50% of its starting value for 10s. Max 3 stacks.
Best Artifacts for Elemental Reaction DPS
Flower of Paradise Lost
Glided Dreams
Flower of Paradise Lost
2PC:
Increase Elemental Mastery by 80.
4PC:
The equipping character's Bloom, Hyperbloom, and Burgeon reaction DMG are increased by 40%. Additionally, after the equipping character triggers Bloom, Hyperbloom, or Burgeon, they will gain another 25% bonus to the effect mentioned prior. Each stack of this lasts 10s. Max 4 stacks simultaneously. This effect can only be triggered once per second. The character who equips this can still trigger its effects when not on the field.
Glided Dreams
2PC:
Increases Elemental Mastery by 80.
4PC:
After Elemental Skills or Burst hit opponents, the targets Dendro RES will be decreased by 30% for 8s. This effect can be triggered even if the equipping character is not on the field.

Gaming Works Best in Pyro Reaction Teams
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Melt Team
- With the release of Citlali, her ability to apply Cryo consistently and shred Pyro RES makes her a great support for Gaming in a Melt team.
- Xianyun and Bennett are healers and also provide their own buffs for Gaming.
- Gaming can be built with more EM for more Melt DMG since Bennett is on the team. Pyro Resonance also gives him an ATK buff as well.
2nd
▼ Vaporize Team
- Vaporize team with Furina is Gaming's former best team but it can still deal high enough damage.
- Furina can easily gain Fanfare stacks with all the teammates having HP effects so Gaming can get a max DMG buff easily.
3rd
▼ Overload Team
- Both Durin and Chevreuse Overload teams can be used.
- Ineffa's shielding provides additional comfort but if Gaming is in the air more, Durin's team would be better depending on your preference.
4th
▼ F2P Melt Team
- F2P Melt team for Gaming to maximize the reaction's multiplier to boost his damage.
- Can be changed to a Vaporize team if you don't have some of the alternative characters.
As a Plunging Attack DPS, Gaming's Pyro element enables him to amplify the damage he deals using Elemental Reactions. Notably, Melt and Vaporize work well by multiplying his damage, while Overload is a mono-Pyro team type that greatly boosts Pyro damage.

Gaming's best partners are Xianyun and Bennett as Plunge Attack and ATK buffers to boost Gaming's damage. Citlali replaces the 3rd member, who is usually a Hydro Sub-DPS, since her Cryo application is good enough for Gaming and has a long duration.
Rotation Of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Xianyun, use her Skill once, then perform a Plunge Attack.
2
Use Xianyun's Burst before switching her out
3
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Gaming and use his Burst
6
Use Gaming's Plunge Attack combo (Skill then Plunging Attack) to deal damage
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
The rotation is slightly different from Gaming's Vaporize team since Xianyun no longer needs to hold VV since Scroll set is easier to trigger.

Gaming's classic premium team. Xianyun buffs his Plunging Attacks, Bennett buffs his ATK and Furina buffs overall DMG. Similar to his Melt team, Gaming can be built with more Elemental Mastery since Bennett buffs ATK a lot.
Rotation Of Vaporize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Xianyun, use her Skill once, then perform a Plunging Attack.
2
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch back to Xianyun and use her Burst to trigger Swirl for VV
4
Switch to Furina and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Gaming and use his Burst
6
Use Gaming's Plunge Attack combo (Skill then Plunging Attack) to deal damage
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
Compared to other teams with Furina, she is used last in the support rotation since Gaming, Xianyun and Bennett's HP effects will trigger Furina's Fanfare stacks easily.

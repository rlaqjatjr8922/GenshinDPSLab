Xiao is a 5-star Anemo Polearm character
in Genshin Impact. He specializes in lightning-fast attacks and has an acrobatic combat style. However, as Anemo is a bad element for DPS in general, building a team around him is a lot less straightforward. In this article, we are going to showcase the best Xiao team comp for high DPS.

Xiao is probably one of the few Anemo characters who can deal damage without high constellations.

Being Anemo,
Xiao is suitable to team up
with a lot of characters, with these 5 being the best.

In this team comp, Xiao is the main DPS, with teammates maximizing the damage of his elemental burst, Bane of all Evil. It allows Xiao to do consecutive Plunge Attacks that deal wide AOE Anemo DMG. Overall, the best weapon for Xiao in all builds is the Primordial Jade Winged-Spear, which increases a lot of ATK and damage dealt. The Deathmatch is the best 4 star alternative.
Zhongli's shield is useful in a lot of lineups, including best Xiao team comps.
Jean is a Healer and battery for Xiao to spam his burst. Furthermore, her C4 can also lower enemy Anemo resistance, which increases Xiao's damage.
Albedo pairs with Zhongli for elemental resonance. His Elemental Skill DMG is maximized by the full Husk of Opulent Dreams set.
Zhongli protects the team with his shield and increases their ATK with the full Tenacity of the Millelith set.

This team is a Double Core lineup with Xiao being the sub DPS character. Overall, Raiden would be the main DPS in the team, who need to be switched in every time Xiao's burst is on cooldown. Raiden Shogun is the key piece of this lineup, as she can boost Xiao's Energy Recharge by her burst while increasing all teammates' burst damage with her elemental skill.
Raiden Shogun is the reason this comp is one of the most effective Xiao lineups.
Bennett's Elemental Burst provides a huge ATK buff and healing to other members.
Zhongli protects the team with his shield and increases their ATK with the full Tenacity of the Millelith set.

This is a lineup geared toward dolphin or F2p players, as all other characters except for Xiao are 4 stars. Similar to the first Xiao team comp, he is the main DPS in this lineup, and the Pyro element from Xiangling is Swirled by Xiao.
Having Xiao spam his plunging attacks is probably the most effective way to use him.

Xiangling's and Bennett are burst support characters, providing damage, attack buff and healing.

Xiao's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Xiao team composition guide and character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we will explore the best teams to build for Xiao, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and Team Compositions to help Xiao 
reach his full damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Xiao
Xiao is a Main DPS that will mainly deal
Anemo DMG
through Plunge DMG. This means that Xiao's teams 
revolve around boosting his Anemo and Plunge DMG, and/or provide at least more 
survivability through shields or healing since he can be quite vulnerable.
Xianyun
Xianyun
Xianyun is Xiao's best support. If you have to invest in one character for your 
	Xiao, she is the best choice. Xianyun requires little field time and increases 
	massively Xiao's plunge DMG. She increases PLunging Attack CRIT Rate, increases 
	plunging DMG by up to 9000, increases his jump height, and also grants some healing.
Faruzan
Faruzan
Faruzan is the second best support for Xiao. She offers
Anemo
RES Shred,
	and raises Xiao's DMG. However, she ideally needs to be C6. Before that, she will lose a lot of 
	value.
Lan Yan
Sucrose
Jean
Other
Anemo DMG
options
If you do not have
Xianyun
or
Faruzan
, these options 
	are perfectly viable as well. They all provide enough particle generation for Xiao, while 
	also granting some buffs, healing, or shields.
Furina
Furina
Furina is great in Xiao's teams. She provides a lot of off-field DMG 
	alongside buffs. However, you will need to run a 
  healer like
Xianyun
or
Jean
with Furina. Furina 
  needs a healer to generate Fanfare stacks for her DMG buff, but also as she manipulates your HP, 
  Xiao can become very fragile.
Zhongli
Zhongli
Zhongli is a good option if you want a strong shield that can remain up for the entire 
	time during Xiao's Burst. His shield not only protects Xiao efficiently, but also
	provides RES Shred, Geo Resonance, and some Crowd Control. Using Zhongli allows you 
	to build a team with one more Anemo, and a Geo off-field DPS such as
Chiori
or
Albedo
.
Pyro
Pyro
Options
If you want to go for more team DMG and less support focused on Xiao, you can
	go for a double Anemo/Pyro team. You will need
Bennett
and team 
	him with another Pyro DPS for the additional 25% Attack from Pyro resonance
	as well as any other support they provide. You can use DPS such as
Mavuika
or
Xiangling

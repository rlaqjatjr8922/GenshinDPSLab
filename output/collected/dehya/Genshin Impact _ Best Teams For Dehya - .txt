▼Burn-Melt Ganyu With Dehya
★★★★★
- Dehya can reduce the DMG that Ganyu will receive and stops interruption from happening as well.
- With Nahida, Dehya can produce burning that allows Ganyu to trigger Melt consistently because of lasting Pyro Aura.
-Team can fully use Dehya's abilities.
▼Overload Dehya
★★★★・
- Dehya with Chevreuse and 1 Electro character provides Overload.
- Gives a decent damage.
▼Mono-Pyro Dehya
★★★★・
- Best team composition when you want to use Dehya as Main DPS.
- Maxes out the power / personal damage of Dehya.
- Requires characters like Bennett and Xiangling that is frequent to other teams.
▼Burgeon Dehya
★★★・・
- Because of Dehya's slow application, there are characters that are a better choice than Dehya in this comp.

The goal of this team is to keep the Elemental Mastery and ATK of Ganyu at the highest so she can trigger powerful Melt shots against enemies inflicted by Burn. Nahida will buff Elemental Mastery and is going to work with Dehya to inflict consistent Burning to enemies.
Bennett will be in charge of healing + ATK Buff through Artifacts and Elemental Burst. This ATK is further increased by Dehya who can battery the team with Favonius Greatsword and boost ATK with Tenacity of Millelith. Dehya's Skill will also prevent interruption allowing Ganyu to safely shoot her charged shots.
Rotation Of Melt Ganyu With Dehya Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Elemental Skill on Enemies.
2
Use Nahida's Elemental Burst then switch to Bennett.
3
Use Bennet's Elemental Burst then Switch to Dehya.
4
Use Dehya's Elemental Skill.
5
Switch to Ganyu, use Elemental Skill and proceed to deal damage using Charged Attack.
No need to use Elemental Burst of Dehya in this rotation for it will cause DMG loss instead.

For this Dehya overload team, Dehya and Raiden Shogun will be the main damage dealers. Raiden Shogun provides a good electro application with her skill and burst. Bennett's burst and Chevreuse skill (hold) will provide a buff to the team activating the overload reaction. Chevreuse will also be the key character in activating the overload reaction.
Rotation Of Overload With Dehya Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Dehya's Elemental Skill
2
Switch to Raiden and use her Elemental Skill
3
Switch to Chevreuse and use her ES then Burst
4
Switch to Bennett Elemental Skill → Burst
5
Switch to Raiden then use her Burst then → NA
6
Use Dehya Burst (optional)
7
Repeat from no.1
This rotation will focus on utilizing the overload reaction. Start it with Dehya's ES to activate her passives, then switch to Raiden Shogun with her ES providing continuous electro application. Then switch to Chevreuse using her ES (hold) and then her Burst. Switch next to Bennett to use his ES and Burst for buff & heal. Then Switch back to Raiden Shogun using her Burst and then use her Normal Attacks.

This team comp does not use reactions but instead focuses on increasing the firepower of Dehya and Pyro DMG towards enemies. Xiangling and Bennett are great combo to deal consistent and powerful off-field DMG. Kazuha can buff the team's DMG, provide consistent crowd control and reduce Pyro resistance of enemies. This team has Pyro resonance, ATK Buff that is amazing when using Dehya as a main DPS.
For artifacts, you can also use 2-piece Crimson Witch Of Flames and 2-piece ATK artifact if you do not have a complete 4pc Emblem of Severed Fate but since Dehya is Energy heavy, rotations might not be as stable.
Rotation Of Mono-Pyro Dehya
No.
Procedure
1
Use Xiangling's Elemental Skill.
2
Switch to Dehya and use Dehya's Elemental Skill.
3
Switch to Kazuha and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
*Make sure to swirl Pyro.
4
Use Bennett and use Elemental Burst.
5
Use Xiangling's Elemental Burst.
6
Use Dehya's Elemental Burst.
The goal of the rotation is to get most Buff from Bennett and Kazuha to Xiangling and Dehya. Make sure to swirl Pyro and use Burst of Bennett before activating Xiangling's Elemental Burst. Getting the chili that is dropped by Guoba can also boost your ATK but no need to focus on it.

The goal of this team is to create produce Dendro Seeds and let Dehya Trigger the seeds causing Burgeon to explode dealing powerful damage. The damage mainly scales from Elemental Mastery so maximizing it is the key for bigger damage. Nahida and Kokomi is there to create Dendro Seeds while providing extra Elemental Mastery (from Nahida) and healing (from Kokomi). Kazuha will further the reaction DMG with his skills and give crowd control as well.
Rotation Of Burgeon Dehya
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Skill to inflict Dendro
2
Use Kokomi's Skill to produce cores.
3
Use Dehya Skill to inflict Pyro to enemies simultaneously making the cores explode.
2
Swirl Pyro using Kazuha's Skill or Elemental Burst.
4
Use Nahida's Elemental Burst.
5
Use Kokomi's Elemental Burst and attack to continue producing cores.
6
Use Dehya's Elemental Burst to trigger some of the cores if Elemental Skill is close to ending.
The goal of the rotation is to make Dehya trigger the Dendro Seeds while she has buffed Elemental Mastery.

Sub

Sucrose is an excellent substitute since she increases EM for Dehya easily. She has less consistent crowd control, however.

Hydro healers are welcome in this comp. Barbara has smaller AoE for hydro infliction and lesser damage however.
Nahida
Dendro Traveler
Dendro Traveler is a decent replacement for Nahida. You need to be extra careful with rotation to not make the Elemental Burst explode with Pyro.

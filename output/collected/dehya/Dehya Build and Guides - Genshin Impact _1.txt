Dehya's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Extremely good damage mitigation leading to high survivability.
Can be played On or Off-field.
Has a good amount of Team composition possibilities.
Weaknesses
Slightly underwhelming at C0.
Despite her high survivability, you will most likely still require a healer in her team.
Dehya's Pyro application is rather slow, making it hard to trigger reactions.
1.
Dehya Build Guide, Best Artifacts, Weapons and Talent Priority
Dehya can be built in three different ways: as a Main DPS, Off-field DPS, or Off-field Support.
All three will require you to build Dehya with different artifacts and weapons, and prioritize different stats.
While we do recommend you go for the Support build, we will still go over the other two
options in case you wish to build Dehya as an off-field DPS or Main DPS. Know
that as a Main DPS she needs way more investment than as a support.

For Dehya's Support build, her best-in-slot weapon would be
Favonius Greatsword
.
This weapon will allows her to lower other teammate's energy requirements. You will need
to build up some CRIT Rate for this weapon.
Portable Power Saw
is another great option for
support Dehya, as it provides some HP with its base stat, and some Energy recharge and Elemental Mastery.
Dehya's best artifact set for a Support build is the
Tenacity of the Millelith
.
For a support Dehya build you need to build up her HP, as she will have to tank.
Your
Molten Inferno
skill will mitigate the damage taken from others towards
Dehya, so she will have to be able to take it. You should aim for around 50,000 HP.
As a Support, Dehya's build will mostly require her Elemental Skill to be upgraded.
You can invest a bit in the Burst if you wish to use it, but you should rarely use anything
else than her Elemental Skill as a support.

3.
Dehya Combos and How to Play
Dehya's Elemental Skill
Molten Inferno
will summon a Pyro field
called Fiery Sanctum. When enemies who are inside of the field take damage, it will
launch a coordinated attack and deal AoE
Pyro
DMG. This DMG
is based on Dehya's ATK and Max HP.
When your active character is inside the field and takes damage, Dehya
will mitigate and take a portion of that damage instead. The active character inside the
field also has increased resistance to interruption.
During her Burst,
Leonine Bite
, Dehya enters a Blazing Lioness state
and continuously unleashes
Pyro
attacks. Her Burst is extremely expensive
in terms of energy, and for Off-field Dehya and Support Dehya builds, you will rarely use it.
Dehya's rotation and playstyle slightly varies whether she is played as a Main DPS,
off-field DPS, or Support.
As a Main DPS you will:
Use
Molten Inferno
Swap to teammates and use their buffs/abilities
Swap back to Dehya and re-use
Molten Inferno
Use
Leonine Bite
When playing as an Off-field DPS and Support the rotation is fairly similar, but you will just not
necessarily use her Burst
Leonine Bite
. As a Support you will
really only switch her in to apply her Fiery Sanctum field so you can mitigate the
incoming damage
As a Sub-DPS you will use Dehya's skill
Molten Inferno
to mainly trigger
Burgeon
. You will have to coordinate with
your
Dendro
character to apply Dehya's skill when
Dendro
Cores
are present. Since Dehya's skill takes a long time to apply
Pyro
,
you can accumulate new
Dendro
cores until the
Pyro
field procs a second time.
4.
Dehya's Best Team Comps
Dehya functions the best as a Support. In order for her to do proper damage as main DPS, she
needs an extremely good build, with extremely well built supports as well. The investment
you have to put into Dehya to main her may be too much. Hence why we recommend you
build her as a support. This allows you to use her in more various teams, as she will
do a very good job, with a bit less investment. She functions well to support characters
that are more sensible to interruptions, or that need
Pyro
application.

5.3.
Dehya's Constellations
At C0, Dehya is rather behind compared to other characters. She will benefit
from some of her contellations. Her C1 is already a very good boost in DMG and HP.
Her C4 is the second best contellation as it will add to her survivability and
reduce her needs in energy recharge. Her C6 is another good boost to Dehya's damage,
but is not necessarly worth the investement. Since she is a standard banner character,
obtaining her C6 can be complicated, so we do not recommend you invest specifically into it.

Best Dehya Build in Genshin Impact
Best Dehya Build in Genshin Impact. (Picture: HoYoverse)
HoYoverse has revealed all the details about Dehya, and
based on her abilities
, she is best suited for the DPS role in the party, and here are the best weapon and artifacts you can use to build her.
Best Weapon
The signature weapon of Dehya, Beacon of the Reed Sea, will be best suited for her; however, if you can’t get your hands on it, you can also go with Redhorn Stonethresher.
Beacon of the Reed Sea
After an Elemental Skill hits an opponent, your ATK will be increased by 20%/25%/30%/35%/40% for 8s. After you take DMG, your ATK will be increased by 20%/25%/30%/35%/40% for 8s. The 2 aforementioned effects can be triggered even when the character is not on the field. Additionally, when not protected by a shield, your character’s Max HP will be increased by 32%/40%/48%/56%/64%.
Redhorn Stonethresher
DEF is increased by 28/35/42/49/56%. Normal and Charged Attack DMG is increased by 40/50/60/70/80% of DEF.
Best Artifact Set
Crimson Witch Of Flames (4-piece) is the best artifact set for Dehya, but if you don’t have it, you can go with Shimenawa’s Reminiscence (4-piece).
Crimson Witch Of Flames
2-Piece Bonus:
Pyro DMG Bonus +15%
4-Piece Bonus:
Increases Overloaded, Burning, and Burgeon DMG by 40%. Increases Vaporize and Melt DMG by 15%. Using Elemental Skill increases the 2-Piece Set Bonus by 50% of its starting value for 10s. Max 3 stacks.
Shimenawa’s Reminiscence
2-Piece Bonus:
ATK +18%
4-Piece Bonus:
When casting an Elemental Skill, if the character has 15 or more Energy, they lose 15 Energy and Normal/Charged/Plunging Attack DMG is increased by 50% for 10s. This effect will not trigger again during that duration.

Best Dehya Team Composition in Genshin Impact
In order to get the most out of Dehya, we recommend you include Hydro and Cyro characters in the team to trigger Vaporize and Melt elemental reactions that will increase the damage dealt to enemies. In terms of the Hydro character, you can either pick Kamisato Ayato or Kokomi, and to fill up the space of a Cyro character, Ganyu or Kamisato Ayaka will be good choices.
The final spot can be given to a Dendro character like Nahida to increase the damage multiple and duration even more by triggering a bunch of reactions, including Burning, Bloom, and more.
Thus, the best team composition for Dehya in Genshin Impact will be Kamisato Ayato/Kokomi, Ganyu/Kamisato Ayaka, and Nahida.

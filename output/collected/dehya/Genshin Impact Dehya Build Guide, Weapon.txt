Gameplay
Dehya’s gameplay revolves around providing
damage mitigation
and limited
off-field Pyro application
while supporting her team’s survivability. Her kit focuses on maintaining her
Fiery Sanctum field
, allowing her to absorb a portion of the damage taken by teammates through
Redmane’s Blood
and grant
resistance to interruption
, making her a reliable defensive option in certain team compositions.
Her Elemental Skill,
Molten Inferno
, creates a Fiery Sanctum that deals
coordinated Pyro damage
at intervals and transfers part of the damage taken by active characters to Dehya. This ability is the core of her kit, enabling both defensive utility and situational off-field contribution.
When Dehya uses her Elemental Burst,
Leonine Bite
, she enters a short
on-field state
where she automatically unleashes a series of
Pyro attacks
, dealing damage in a limited window. While not her main strength, it provides additional damage during rotations.
Dehya’s passive talents further improve her survivability by reducing incoming damage and allowing her to
self-heal
over time. While her personal damage is moderate, she performs best when built around
HP scaling
and team synergy to maximize her overall utility.

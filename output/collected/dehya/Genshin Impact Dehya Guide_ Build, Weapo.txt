Updated

Dehya
is a
5 star Pyro character
who fights with a claymore. She has the advantage of being quite flexible and of being able to play several roles: DPS, sub-DPS or team tank support. We explain how to play it well and equip it. With this
complete guide
dedicated to
Dehya from Genshin Impact
, you will know which artifacts and weapons to assign to her in order to have a good
build
, but also in which teams to play her. We take this opportunity to give you some
tips and tricks for beginners
.

How to get Dehya in Genshin Impact ?
Dehya is a
5-star character
released during the
3.5 update of Genshin Impact.
There are several ways
to get Dehya
via the Character Wishes for summons.
Dehya Wish Banner
You can try to
unlock Dehya in Genshin Impact
from March 1 until March 22, 2023 by performing summons on her
Auric Blaze
banner.
However, as of the
3.6 update
, it will join the
Standard Wish
. You will then have a chance to get Dehya by summoning her on Standard Wish or by losing a 50/50.
Dehya Constellations
It must be said that Dehya's performance is quite average. Her
constellations
can thus help to improve the character. However, we advise you to wait to see her fall in the Standard Wish or after a 50/50 loss rather than spend all your Primogems on her.
Her C1
The Flame Incandescent
increases Dehya's max HP and will provide a damage boost to her elemental skills based on her max HP.
The Sand-Blades Glittering
increases the area duration of Dehya's elemental attack.
How to play Dehya in Genshin Impact ?
Dehya can play several roles in Genshin Impact :
DPS
, sub-DPS or support tank. She has the particularity of having a
large number of HP
, even if we regret that she doesn't really take advantage of them to take damage. Her elemental attack and her elemental rampage are mainly based on her ATK.
The support and sub-DPS aspect of Dehya
With her elemental skill
Molten Inferno
, Dehya creates a
Fiery Sanctum
that stays on the field for 12 seconds. You can reposition it by casting it again. Dehya will then re-damage the field, but this will not affect the original duration.
When an enemy in
this area takes damage
, a coordinated attack is also triggered (once every 2.5 seconds), which makes it
easy to perform elemental reactions
. But that's not all: characters in this area will have their resistance to interruption increased. In addition, they will receive less damage; about 50% is delivered to Dehya instead up to a certain threshold.
With this ability, Dehya is now able
to apply the Pyro element off-screen
, but only once every 2.5 seconds for 12 seconds. Also, the active character will receive less damage and will not be interrupted anymore. This is convenient for fragile characters like Yoimiya or Klee. Dehya replaces the role of a shield here.
However, its zone remains only 12 seconds and takes 20 seconds to recharge. So she won't be able to provide enough protection (unless you have her C2). And even if
Dehya absorbs damage
from her allies, she should be able to hold on thanks to her
Stalwart and True
passive, which allows her to recover 20% of her max HP when they are below 40%, then 6% of her max HP every 2 seconds for 10 seconds.
The burst DPS aspect of Dehya
Dehya's elemental rampage
Leonine Bite
instead destines her to stay on the battlefield. Dehya enters a state of
Blazing Lioness
for 4 seconds. She automatically attacks enemies continuously and deals Pyro damage based on her ATK and max HP.
These are not normal attacks, so increasing their level will not help in this case. Also, Dehya will not benefit from the skills of characters like
Yelan
and
Xinqiu
. On the other hand, by pressing the normal attack button, you can speed up Dehya's combos.
In addition, if the
Fiery Sanctum
is present when Dehya activates her elemental rampage, she will retrieve it and put it back on after the duration of Blazing Lioness expires.
With the
Unstinting Succor
passive, when Dehya recovers her Fiery Sanctum (either with her elemental ability or her elemental rampage) the damage Dehya takes is reduced for 6 seconds and for 9 seconds the resistance to interruption of characters in the Fiery Sanctum area is greatly increased.
What is the best equipment for Dehya?
The
choice of Dehya's equipment in Genshin Impact
depends mostly on the build you want to give her: support, sub-DPS or DPS? As a support build, you'll want to focus on her HP%, while for a more DPS oriented build, you'll need offensive stats, but also a fair amount of energy recharge.
We detail the
best weapons and artifacts to equip on Dehya
according to the build you want to give him.
The best artifacts for Dehya
Emblem of Severed Fate
To
play Dehya as a DPS / Burst DPS
, you will need a build with not only offensive stats : ATK, critical rate and critical damage, but also a lot of energy recharge. That's why we recommend the 4-piece
Emblem of Severed Fate
set. With two pieces you increase the energy recharge by 20% and with 4 the damage of the elemental rampage is increased by 25% of the energy recharge.
Crimson Witch of Flames
You can also use the
Crimson Witch of Flames
set to increase Pyro damage by 15% with 2 coins, then elemental reaction damage related to the Pyro element.
Tenacity of the Millelith
For a
tank support build
, go for the
Toughness of the Millelite
artifact set. 2 pieces increase HP by 20% and 4 pieces boostATK of your team members by 20% after an elemental skill hits an enemy.
Gilded Dreams
Flower of Paradise Lost
If you want to play Dehya with Dendro characters to do
Burn
or
Burgeon
, we advise you to use elemental mastery with
Gilded Dreams
or
Flower of Paradise Lost
.
Statistics to focus on:
Hourglass: ATK% or PV% (for build tank or if C1) ;
Cup : bonus of damage Pyro ;
Crown: critical rate or critical damage.
Secondary statistics :
ATK% ;
Critical rate ;
Critical damage ;
Energy Recharge;
PV %.
The best artifacts for Dehya for beginners
For beginners who are not yet at the 45 level of adventure, we recommend :
The Exile
Instructor
Exiled
: to increase the search for energy and allow it to be restored more easily
Instructor
: to increase the elemental mastery if you play Dehya with Dendro characters.
The best weapons for Dehya in Genshin Impact
To complete Dehya's equipment in Genshin Impact, we recommend the following two-handed weapons:
Beacon of the Reed Sea (5★)
Wolf's Gravestone (5★)
Skyward Pride (5★)
Beacon of the Reed Sea
: This is the best two-handed weapon to equip on Dehya in Genshin Impact. It increases his critical hit rate, but also his ATK after hitting an enemy. And if the equipped character is not protected by a shield, his HP increases by 32%;
Wolf's Gravestone
: The weapon greatly increasesATK and the passive grants an additional 20% boost when enemies have less than 30% of their HP. The ATK% of all characters is then boosted by 40% for 12 seconds;
Skyward Pride
: with this, you increase the energy recharge, but also all the damage inflicted;
The best free-to-play weapons for Dehya in Genshin Impact
These weapons are ideal for beginner players of Genshin Impact, but also for free-to-play players:
Favonius Greatsword (4★)
Sacrificial Greatsword (4★)
Prototype Archaic (4★)
Favonius Greatsword
: with this weapon, Dehya will have no worries about recharging his energy and his critical hits can even generate elemental particles;
Sacrificial Greatsword
: to get energy recharge and to use Dehya's elemental skill more often;
Prototype Archaic
: this weapon to be made by the blacksmith confers ATK and normal and charged attacks have a 50% chance of inflicting an additional 240% damage ATK (for a DPS build);
Luxurious Sea-Lord (4★)
Bloodtainted Greatsword (3★)
Luxurious Sea-Lord
: This weapon increases theATK and the damage of elemental rampages.
Bloodtainted Greatsword
: to increase the elemental mastery if you play Dehya in a Burgeon team.

versatile character
in Genshin Impact, so you can play her in many teams, whether it's to protect your characters and increase their resistance to interruption, to apply the Pyro element off-screen or as a DPS. Unfortunately, she does not excel in any of these areas.

How old and how tall is Dehya in Genshin Impact ?
Dehya is a member of the Eremites, a group of mercenaries operating in the Sumerian desert. She was born on April 7 and is said to be in her early twenties. She is approximately 1m68 tall.
That's it for this
guide dedicated to Dehya from Genshin Impact.
While the character enjoys a great deal of versatility, it is regrettable that she does not shine more in one of these roles. There will often be a better option. On the other hand, she is still a fun character to play, especially for her elemental rampage.
If you like its character design, gameplay and are not looking for a
meta team
, you can easily play it with a proper build and in one of the recommended teams. In addition,
new equipment
and characters can be added to the game later on.

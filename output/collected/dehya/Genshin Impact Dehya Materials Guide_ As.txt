Where to Get Dehya’s Ascension and Talent Materials
Below we have shared the details on how you can get Dehya’s Ascension and Talent level-up materials in
Genshin Impact
:
Agnidus Agate Sliver:
You can get it from the drop of Pyro Regisvine and Pyro Hypostases.
Agnidus Agate Fragment:
You can get it through crafting using Agnidus Agate Sliver or from the drop of Pyro Regisvine and Pyro Hypostases.
Sand Grease Pupas:
You can get it in Wenut Tunnels in Sumeru’s desert area.
Faded Red Satin, Trimmed Red Silk, and Rich Red Brocade
: You can get these three materials from the drop of Eremites.
Light Guiding Tetrahedron:
You can get it from the drop of Semi-Intransient Matrices.
Agnidus Agate Chunk:
You can get it either from crafting using Agnidus Agate Fragment or from the drop of Pyro Regisvine and Pyro Hypostases.
Agnidus Agate Gemstone:
You can get it either from crafting using Agnidus Agate Chunk or from the drop of Pyro Regisvine and Pyro Hypostases.
Teachings of Praxis:
You can get it from Steeple of Ignorance every Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday.
Guide to Praxis:
You can get it from Steeple of Ignorance on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday, or you can craft it using Teachings of Ingenuity.
Philosophies of Praxis:
You can get it from Steeple of Ignorance on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday, or you can craft it using Guide to Ingenuity.
Puppet Strings:
You can get it from the drop of Scaramouche.
Crown of Insight
: You can get it from a limited-duration event reward.
Mora
: You can obtain Mora by performing several activities in the game, such as Blossom of Wealth, Missions, Events, Daily commission, Souvenir Shop, Domain, etc.

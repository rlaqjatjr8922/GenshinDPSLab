If you've added the leader of the Temple of Silence to your team in
Genshin Impact
, then you may be wondering which characters are best to join him. Fair warning,
Sethos
works best on
Aggravate
and
Hyperbloom
teams, so expect to see a fair few Hydro and Dendro characters in this list.

Sethos is a four
-star electro bow
character who hails from
Sumeru
. Within the story, he's the former vessel of
Hermanubis
- whose power now resides in
Cyno
. Sethos was added to the game in the
4.7 update
.
Sethos works best as a
main-DPS
, meaning you'll want to set him up with
sub-DPS
and
support
characters in order to deal the most damage. He scales with
Elemental Mastery
, so single element teams won't utilize him to his full potential, and you'll do a lot of your damage through elemental reactions.
He is also able to deal damage to
multiple targets at once
, something that bow characters don't always excel at doing. However, his full-charged shots cause him to be
unable to move,
and newer players may opt to have shielders or healers to keep him safe.
Aggravate Team 1: Gacha
Main DPS:
Sethos
Sub DPS:
Raiden Shogun
Sub DPS:
Nahida
Support:
Kaedehara Kazuha
As your main damage dealer, Sethos needs to spend the
most amount of time
on the field out of everyone in your party. The
aggravate
reaction happens when an enemy who is hit with
Electro
and
Dendro
is hit by
Electro
again. Therefore, you need characters who have strong
elemental application
. Both
Nahida
and
Raiden Shogun
have strong elemental application with their elemental skill and burst respectively.
Nahida
also scales off of
Elemental Mastery
, meaning even more damage dealt from your elemental reactions.
Kazuha
is the finishing touch for your team, with the addition of
Anemo
allowing for the
Swirl
reaction to do even more damage. In addition to this, Sethos consumes a lot of elemental energy, so if you have Raiden's
C6 constellation
, you can get Sethos'
burst cooldown reduced
.
With two electro characters on your team, you'll benefit from additional
elemental particles
whenever you execute a
Superconduct
,
Overloaded
,
Electro-Charged
,
Quicken
,
Aggravate
, or
Hyperbloom
reaction - which works well as
Sethos uses a lot of energy
.
To make the best use of Sethos, it's advised to have his
C6
in order to improve his energy usage. In addition, his
C4
will give all party members more
Elemental Mastery
, so this will up your damage too.
Character
Action
Raiden Shogun
Start by using Raiden's Elemental skill, as this will give all of your attacks an electro follow-up.
Nahida
Immediately use Nahida's Elemental skill, holding the button down in order to target as many enemies as possible.
Kaedehara Kazuha
Follow up with Kazuha's elemental skill, plunging attack, and then his Elemental Burst.
Sethos
Upon switching to Sethos, immediately use his burst. Follow up with his skill to retreat away from the enemy and then spam empowered normal attacks.
If the enemies you're fighting are spread out, you can swap Nahida and Kazuha in this rotation in order to use Kazuha's skill to pull them closer together.
Aggravate Team 2 : Free-To-Play
Main DPS:
Sethos
Sub DPS:
Collei
Sub DPS:
Fischl
Support:
Yaoyao
A good option for
F2P
players is utilizing one of Genshin's best
electro
supports,
Fischl
. Using her at C6, her elemental skill allows all of your normal attacks to do additional electro damage - similar to Raiden Shogun. In addition to this, her
C5
means that whenever you trigger an electro reaction, enemies will be hit with an attack equal to
80 percent of Fischl's ATK.
Collei makes a good addition to your team, with her kit providing a good amount of
Dendro application
- which is needed for an
aggravate
team. In addition to this, her elemental burst has a wide area of effect - meaning you won't miss out by not having a crowd control character on your team. Her
C4
will be hugely beneficial for this team, as it will increase everyone's
Elemental Mastery
whenever she uses
Trump-Card Kitty
.
Having Collei's
C2
is a must, as it improves on all of her qualities as a sub-dps - namely by introducing the
sprout
effect. This deals
40 percent of Collei's attack as Dendro damage
to nearby opponents and this effect is extended whenever you create an
aggravate
reaction.
The addition of
Yaoyao
not only improves the team's
Dendro application
, but also acts as a F2P friendly healer to ensure your team has a better chance of surviving.
Yaoyao needs to remain on-field after using her Burst, or the additional Yuegui's she summons will disappear.
You should have both
C2
and
C4
activated for
Yaoyao
, as these will aid in her
Energy Recharge
and Dendro application respectively. It's also recommended to have her with a weapon that scales off of energy recharge, like the
Favonius Lance.
Character
Action
Collei
Start by using Collei's Elemental Ekill and then immediately use her Elemental Burst.
Fischl
Summon Oz using Fischl's Elemental Skill.
Yaoyao
Switch to Yaoyao and throw out Yuegui using her Elemental Skill.
Sethos
Finish with Sethos' burst. Follow up with his skill to retreat away from the enemy and then spam empowered normal attacks.
Hyperbloom Team 1: Gacha
Main DPS:
Sethos
Sub DPS:
Raiden Shogun
Sub DPS:
Furina
Support:
Baizhu
Furina
is an incredibly diverse character who can deal huge amounts of
hydro damage
and apply the element to the enemy without being on the field. Additionally, she can also step in as a healer in a pinch. If you're lucky enough to have her
C4
, then she allows for extra healing, and you could probably swap out
Baizhu
for a sub-DPS such as
Nahida
.

Furina works best with another healer, due to her Elemental Skill increasing in damage if she's able to steal HP from all of your team. Since she stops doing this when everyone is at 50% health, she required a second healer to make the most of her kit.
Baizhu
works well with Sethos as he provides a bit more stability in the form of both a
shield and healing,
particularly if you use one of Sethos'
charged shots
and find yourself unable to move. To make the most use out of Baizhu, it's recommended to have his C4 in order to increase the team's elemental mastery whenever he uses
Holistic Revivification
.
Character
Action
Raiden Shogun
Begin your rotation by using Raiden Shogun's elemental skill.
Furina
Switch to Furina and summon her Salon Solitaire using her elemental skill.
Baizhu
Next, move to Baizhu and use his Elemental Burst to make a shield, quickly followed by his Elemental Skill.
Sethos
Finish with Sethos' burst. Follow up with his skill and then spam empowered normal attacks. When Baizhu's Elemental Skill runs out, you can switch back to him to use it again.
Hyperbloom Team 2: Free-To-Play
Main DPS:
Sethos
Sub DPS:
Traveler (Dendro)
Sub DPS:
Xingqiu
Support:
Dori
This team is built all around supporting Sethos to the max. Beginning with
Dendro Traveler
, who acts as a
strong F2P off-field DPS
. Although this character more than any on the team benefits from having constellations, it's the
C4
that is the best to have. This will give your team additional Elemental Mastery whenever you trigger a
Lotus Lamp
configuration.
Dendro Traveler can be obtained by resonating with a Statue of the Seven in Sumeru.
Xingqiu is a strong addition to any team, thanks to his
stellar hydro application
from his Elemental Burst. If you've got his C6, this will give you more
Energy Recharge
- something Xingqiu needs a lot of in order to have easy access to his Burst.
Dori is essentially a battery for the team, with her kit being built around
supplying strong energy recharge
to teammates. With her C4 unlocked, you unlock even more support abilities including
increasing healing received and energy recharge.
Character
Action
Dori
Use Dori's Elemental Burst to summon the Jinni.
Traveler
Quickly switch to Traveler and use their Elemental Skill followed by their Burst.
Xingqiu
Immediately use Xingqiu's burst.
Sethos
Finish with Sethos' burst. Follow up with his skill and then spam empowered normal attacks.

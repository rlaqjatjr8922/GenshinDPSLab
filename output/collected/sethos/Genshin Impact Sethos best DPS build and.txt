Best DPS build for Sethos in Genshin Impact
This 4-Star bow user can deal Empowered Shots to deal extra Electro DMG, while abilities like his Elemental Skill can pull him away from danger while still dealing damage. There’s a lot of potential with Sethos, but only with the right DPS build. Let’s go over the best Artifacts and weapons for Sethos.
Image: HoYoverse
Best Artifacts
By far, the best Artifact set for Sethos is
Wanderer’s Troupe
. This is because his attacks scale with Elemental Mastery and he can dish out instant Charged Attacks, leaving him with better damage results while having a 4-piece Wanderer’s Troupe set equipped. This set is attainable from the majority of bosses or Domain Reliquaries in the Spiral Abyss.
2-piece: +80 Elemental Mastery.
4-piece: Increases Charged Attack DMG by 35% if the character uses a Catalyst or a Bow.
Besides Wanderer’s Troupe, you could always go for a set like
Thundering Fury
to deal extra Electro DMG or
Gilded Dreams
which also improves Sethos’ Elemental Mastery. While you can obtain Thundering Fury Artifacts from the Midsummer Courtyard Domain, the Gilded Dreams set is from the Spire of Solitary Enlightenment Domain.
Thundering Fury
2-piece: +15% Electro DMG Bonus
4-piece: Increases damage caused by Overloaded, Electro-Charged, Superconduct, and Hyperbloom by 40%, and the DMG Bonus conferred by Aggravate is increased by 20%. When Quicked or the aforementioned Elemental Reactions are triggered, Elemental Skill CD is decreased by one second. Can only occur once every 0.8 seconds.
Gilded Dreams
2-piece: +80 Elemental Mastery.
4-piece: Within eight seconds of triggering an Elemental Reaction, the character equipping this will obtain buffs based on the Elemental Type of the other party members. ATK is increased by 14% for each member whose Elemental Type is the same as the equipping character, and EM is increased by 50 for every member with a different Elemental Type. Each of the buffs will count up to three characters. This effect can be triggered once every eight seconds and even when not on the field.
Best weapons
Sethos can use a range of bows to his advantage, but only a few have skills and stats that can improve his build to the best effect.
Hunter’s Path
is probably the best out of the choices below since he not only gains a boost in Elemental DMG, but his Charged Attack DMG increases by a whopping 160% of his EM.
Sethos’ kit is built around Charged Attacks and EM, making this a perfect bow to wield. Of course, if you can’t get your hands on Hunter’s Path there are always the three other choices listed below that can improve his build.

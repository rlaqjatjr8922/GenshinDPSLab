Lisa Works Best As Reaction Driver
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Aggravate Team
- Lisa's Conductive stacking mechanic can allow her to make use of Aggravate to increase the damage of her hold Skill.
- Her Burst's DEF shred can allow her to deal more damage.
2nd
▼ Electrocharged Team
- Electrocharged teams allow Lisa to benefit from her EM ascension stat and provide some Crowd Control that Lisa needs for her hold Skill.
- This team allows Lisa to be paired with other Electro characters that can funnel Energy to her.
3rd
▼ Sub-DPS Lisa
- Lisa's Burst is still active while she is off-field, allowing her to be a sub-DPS.
- The range of her Burst is small and it also can push away smaller enemies once they get staggered.
- Her Burst has a high Energy cost and rotations will require her to use hold Skill to gain Energy.
Lisa is a character whose Electro-infused attacks make her great for applying Electro on-field. Her Elemental Burst is able to shred the Defense of enemies, allowing the team to deal more damage. This also allows her to be a sub-DPS though it will require higher Energy Recharge.

Lisa is the on-field DPS for this team. With Nahida applying consistent Dendro, Xilonen provides healing and damage boost with Scroll set and her debuffs. Fischl can easily generate Energy for Lisa and apply Electro as well.
Rotation Of Aggravate Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Fischl and use her Skill to start applying Electro
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies with Normal Attacks twice
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Nahida and use her Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Lisa and use her Burst
6
Stack Conductive on enemies with her tap Skill or Charged Attack then use her hold Skill to deal damage
7
Repeat the stacking of Conductive and her hold Skill until buffs expire
8
Repeat from 1

Taser Lisa doesn't strictly need her hold Skill to deal damage since Electrocharged relies on Elemental Mastery. While Electrocharged also doesn't need DEF shred for damage, Lisa's Burst can still allow her teammates to deal greater damage.
Rotation Of Electrocharged Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Kokomi and use her Skill
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Lisa and use her Burst
5
Alternate between Lisa's Charged Attacks and tap Skill to apply Conductive on enemies
6
Once two or three Conductive stacks have been placed, use Lisa's hold Skill to deal damage
7
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

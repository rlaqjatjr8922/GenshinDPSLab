Best Teams for Lauma
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Bloom With Lauma
- Lauma as a solo-Dendro is amazing at driving Bloom reactions off-field.
- With Nilou, Bloom's explosions will trigger faster.
- This is especially amazing against multiple enemies.
2nd
▼ Hyperbloom With Lauma
- Make use of Lauma's Buff to Bloom reactions to produce powerful damage.
- In Single Target situations this is the best team to use.
- Can also fight against multiple enemies but it is dependent on the rest of the characters used outside Lauma.
- Another advantage of this team is having flexible members from Premium to F2P selections.
3rd
▼ Burgeon Team
- Burgeon can also be buffed by Lauma.
- While inconsistent, it is still an amazing option to use with Lauma.
Lauma is a character that greatly increases the damage of Bloom related reactions and also produces a special bloom reaction called "Lunar Bloom".  She is an amazing character to put as a Dendro option in Bloom reaction related teams.

Lauma allows Nilou to fight smoothly even against waves of enemies. The goal of this team is to create Blooms with Kokomi as on-field to heal the damages from multiple blooms.
Rotation Of Bloom Team with Lauma
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida and use Elemental Skill to mark enemies and Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to Nilou and use Elemental Skill three times.
3
Switch to Lauma and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
4
Switch to Kokomi and use Elemental Burst then Skill.
5
Proceed to use Kokomi near enemies and use Normal Attacks.
6
Use Lauma's Hold Skill to deal powerful Lunar Bloom Damage.
7
Return to step 1

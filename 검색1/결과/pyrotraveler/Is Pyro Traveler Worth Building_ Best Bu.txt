Pyro Traveler Kit Overview & Strengths
The
core mechanic
of Pyro Traveler is the
Nightsoul state
, which deals
constant Pyro damage
even when they are
off-field
.
Elemental Skill – Nightsoul State
Tap Skill:
Enters
Nightsoul state
, dealing
small AoE Pyro damage over time
.
Hold Skill:
Releases a
coordinated attack every 3 seconds
, but this mode is largely
underwhelming
.
Nightsoul Points:
These points determine the
duration of Pyro damage
, with additional points generated by the
Elemental Burst
.
Elemental Burst – Pyro Explosion
Deals an initial burst of Pyro damage.
Generates additional Nightsoul points
over the next 4 seconds.
Best part of the kit
, as it allows for
big burst damage
.
Passive Talents
Passive 1:
Increases skill range
when
Nightsoul points are above 20
(but the range is still small).
Passive 2:
Generates 5 energy every 12s
when a Pyro reaction is triggered and
4 extra energy from Nightsoul Burst reactions
.
Key Takeaways on Pyro Traveler’s Kit
✔
Best feature:
Consistent
off-field Pyro application
for teams like
Burgeon
or
Pyro reaction teams
.
✔
Biggest problem:
Very small AoE range
on the skill, making it
hard to consistently trigger reactions
.
✔
Needs a
Natlan
unit
in the team to
fully benefit from Cinder City bonuses and energy passives
.
Constellations – Are They Worth It?
Pyro Traveler’s
initial 6 constellations
are unlocked by
completing the Archon Quest
. However, these
only make them stronger against the latest weekly boss
, which is
very niche
.
The
real constellations
come from
maxing out Tribe Reputations
, currently allowing them to go up to
C5
.
Notable Constellations
C1:
Grants a 15% DMG boost
to the active character when Nightsoul is active.
C2:
Generates additional Nightsoul points
from Pyro-related reactions.
C4:
Provides a 20% Pyro DMG Bonus
after using Burst.
C6 (Speculated):
Could potentially
infuse Normal Attacks with Pyro
, making
Traveler a viable on-field DPS
.
Verdict
:
If
C6 grants Pyro Infusion
,
Pyro Traveler
could become
a much stronger DPS option
in the future.

Best Pyro Traveler Teams
Despite its
flaws
, Pyro Traveler works
in several team comps
, primarily in
reaction-based setups
.
1. Burgeon Team (Best Team)
✔
Nahida
+
Xingqiu
+ Kokomi + Pyro Traveler
Why It Works:
Consistent Pyro application for Burgeon reactions.
Kokomi provides healing & Hydro application.
Nahida ensures fast Dendro cores.
Xingqiu adds extra Hydro & damage reduction.
🔹
Energy Recharge is needed for optimal burst uptime.
2. Burning Team (Kinich & Emilie)
✔
Kinich
+
Emilie
+ Anemo Support + Pyro Traveler
Why It Works:
Traveler’s skill provides consistent Pyro application for Burning reactions.
Kinich and Emilie trigger powerful Pyro-based reactions.
Synergy is strong, but Cinder City’s buff sometimes doesn’t refresh properly.
3. Hyper Raiden (Chevreuse Team)
✔
Raiden Shogun
+
Kujou Sara
+
Chevreuse
+ Pyro Traveler
Why It Works:
Traveler can provide off-field Pyro for Overload reactions.
Chevreuse buffs the team’s Electro damage.
Raiden Shogun remains the main DPS.
🔹
Rotation issues with Kujou Sara exist, so be mindful.
4. Main DPS Pyro Traveler (For Fun)
✔
Bennett
(C6) + Anemo Support + Xingqiu + Pyro Traveler
Why It Works:
Bennett’s C6 gives Pyro Traveler Pyro Infusion.
Works as a fun playstyle but not optimal.
If C6 grants Pyro infusion, this could become a legitimate DPS build.
Pyro Traveler Review: Should You Invest in This Free Unit?
✔
Yes, if:
✅ You need a
Burgeon reaction enabler
.
✅ You want a
free unit with decent support potential
.
✅ You enjoy
Traveler’s playstyle and want to use them in Natlan-themed teams
.
❌
No, if:
❌ You already have
better Pyro characters (like
Mavuika
or Kinich)
.
❌ You dislike
low AoE range & unreliable energy generation
.
❌ You don’t want to rely on
a Natlan unit for full energy benefits
.
Should You Invest in Pyro Traveler?
They’re easy to build
since
only the burst needs investment
.
They work well in Burgeon & Burning teams
but struggle in other roles.
If C6 provides Pyro Infusion, their value could increase significantly.
Overall,
Pyro Traveler is surprisingly good
, but
their biggest issue is the low skill range and reliance on a Natlan teammate
. If those issues don’t bother you,
they’re worth trying out
.

Frequently Asked Questions about Pyro Traveler
Is Pyro Traveler worth building?
Yes, if you need a Burgeon enabler or a free Pyro support. Their Burst is their best ability.
What is the best team for Pyro Traveler?
A Burgeon team with Nahida, Xingqiu, and Kokomi provides strong synergy and consistent Pyro application.
What are the best artifacts for Pyro Traveler?
Cinder City (4-piece) is best for reaction teams. Gilded Dreams (4-piece) works for Burgeon builds.
What are the best weapons for Pyro Traveler?
Sacrificial Sword
is the best option to reset their skill cooldown and maintain Nightsoul uptime.
Will Pyro Traveler get Pyro infusion at C6?
It’s rumored that C6 may grant Pyro infusion, making them a viable DPS option in the future.

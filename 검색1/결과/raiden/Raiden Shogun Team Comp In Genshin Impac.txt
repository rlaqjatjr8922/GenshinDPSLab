Raiden Shogun’s ability to take many roles and excel in them always puts her in the highest ranks of
Genshin Impact
’s tier lists. Her Eternal Excellency is a strong Sub-DPS who can continuously apply her Electro Element through her Elemental Skill. She also has one of the hardest-hitting Ultimates, followed by a few seconds of Electro Burst DMG.
However, despite the goddess' insistence on doing things independently, even she must rely on her teammates for players to utilize her to her full potential. The best characters to pair with Raiden Shogun all depend on her role, so this guide will give players a variety of top picks for

Updated May 28, 2025, by Nahda Nabiilah:
With every update, Genshin Impact expands on its roster and items, which creates an opportunity to revamp a character’s build or team. In this case, Raiden Shogun has been through many new artifact sets and characters, especially the Dendro units. For that, Raiden Shogun’s most optimal team will change to fit more into the current meta, and more pairs will be added to enhance her gameplay, keeping in mind all the roles she can play in a team.
4
Raiden Shogun + Xilonen + Kujou Sara + Kazuha
Playing Ei as a hyper carry is totally
viable and justifiable because her Elemental Burst
can deal a lot of DMG, especially if it’s C2 Raiden Shogun. There’s an army of supports that can greatly enhance Raiden’s burst DMG, such as Xilonen, Kujou Sara, Furina, Kazuha, and Bennett. In this Raiden Shogun team, there are no serious reactions besides the Electro Swirl reaction triggered by Kazuha to reduce enemies’ resistance to Electro DMG. Xilonen further shreds enemies’ resistance with her Elemental Skill, and can also buff Raiden if she has her Signature Weapon.
On the other hand, Kazuha, Sara, and Xilonen all possess buffs that would take Raiden’s Elemental Burst’s DMG to the next level.
The best part is that the Electro Archon
will refill all their Elemental Burst when she casts hers. This notably improves the team’s rotation and synergy.
Team Roles
Raiden Shogun:
Main DPS, Buffer, Battery, Electro Enabler
Xilonen:
Healer, Buffer, Debuffer
Kujou Sara:
Buffer, Sub-DPS
Kazuha:
Crowd Control, Buffer, Debuffer
3
Raiden Shogun + Zhongli + Nahida + Kujou Sara
Using buffers to enhance the Electro Archon’s DMG is good, but players can also use
Dendro reactions in
Genshin Impact
. Nahida is an amazing Dendro enabler who can match Raiden’s Electro application. This allows both units to endlessly trigger the Quicken reaction. After that, both Nahida and Raiden Shogun will take turns to trigger their Spread and Aggravate reactions, respectively.

Raiden Shogun + Yelan + Nahida + Xilonen

Dendro units in

1
Raiden Shogun + Neuvillette + Nahida + Furina
For this team, Neuvillette is the main DPS while the Electro Archon takes the Sub-DPS role. Every character in this team is a strong enabler who can continuously apply their Elements, which creates a chance to assemble a strong
Hyperbloom team in
Genshin Impact
. Additionally, Nahida will greatly buff the DMG of each Hyperbloom while dealing a lot of Spread DMG herself.
Since Neuvillette and
Furina’s Hydro will keep taking
the Quicken effect off enemies, it’s better to build Raiden Shogun on Flower of Paradise Lost and maximize her Elemental Mastery to fully take advantage of Hyperbloom DMG. Do the same for Nahida and focus her utility to debuff enemies to Dendro DMG by building her with the Deepwood Memories set.

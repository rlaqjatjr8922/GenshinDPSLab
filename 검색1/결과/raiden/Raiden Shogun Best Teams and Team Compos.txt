Raiden Shogun's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Raiden Shogun team composition guide and character synergy overview for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams for Raiden Shogun, including Hypercarry and reaction-based teams such as Hyperbloom and Electro-Charged, alongside recommended character pairings that help maximize her overall value.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Raiden Shogun
Raiden Shogun can be played in multiple roles depending on the team composition. She has two primary roles: a Main DPS Hypercarry and a reaction-based support in teams such as Hyperbloom or Electro-Charged.
In Hypercarry teams, Raiden Shogun focuses on maximizing her Burst damage while being supported by ATK buffers, CRIT buffers, and Elemental RES Shred. In reaction-based teams, she acts as a consistent off-field Electro applicator, enabling powerful reactions such as Hyperbloom.

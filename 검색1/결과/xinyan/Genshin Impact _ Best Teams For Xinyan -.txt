Xinyan Works Best As Shield Support
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Shield Support
- Makes use of Xinyan's shield from her Skill.
- Level 3 shield can deal Pyro off-field damage.
2nd
▼ Physical Support
- Focuses on the Physical DMG buff in Xinyan's passive.
- C4 additionally allows her to reduce Physical DMG RES as well.
3rd
▼ Physical DPS
- Full constellations Xinyan can focus on building DEF for damage and shield HP.
- Requires consistent application of both Cryo and Electro for Superconduct.
Xinyan is a Pyro character who can create a Shield that deals Pyro damage depending on how many enemies were hit by her Skill. She can also buff Physical DMG when her shield is active.

Pyro Resonance can help lessen Xinyan's Energy costs and also increase the amount of Pyro applied. Shield Support Xinyan can be used in reaction teams, and she can equip a support artifact or Husk/Bolide, where DEF% and Shield Strength are used for her Shield's HP.
Rotation Of Shield Support
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Ayato and use his Burst to apply Hydro on enemies
2
Switch to Kazuha and use his tap Skill to Swirl Hydro
3
Switch to Pyro Traveler and use their Skill, then Burst to gain Nightsoul points
4
Switch to Xinyan and use her Skill to hit three (3) enemies (use her Burst instead if she is C2)
5
Switch back to Ayato and use his Skill, then use Normal Attacks to deal damage
6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

Xinyan's Shield uptime is needed in order to maintain the Physical DMG buff. C2 Xinyan can use Energy Recharge sands instead since her Burst will be able to create a Shield upon cast.
Rotation Of Physical Support
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Raiden Shogun and use her Skill
2
Switch to Xinyan and use her Skill if she is C2, you can use her Burst instead
3
Switch to Mika and use his hold Skill to activate his buff then use his Burst
4
Switch to Eula and use her Burst
5
Charge her Burst's stacks by using her Skill and Normal Attacks
6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire
Raiden Shogun's Eye of Storym Judgement has good uptime and if her Skill hasn't expired yet, you can repeat the rotation from the shielders' turn instead.

Xinyan as Physical DPS will need sub-DPS characters to trigger Superconduct. Her Shield being able to apply Pyro on enemies will need characters that can easily clear it out. DPS Xinyan can be built as a Burst one-shot DPS as well.
Rotation Of Physical DPS
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Raiden Shogun and use her Skill
2
Switch to Zhongli and use his held Skill for shield and RES shred
3
Switch to Mika and use his held Skill to hit enemies
4
Use Mika's Burst before switching him out
5
Switch to Xinyan and use her Burst then Skill
6
Use Normal Attacks to deal damage
7
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire
If Xinyan is C6, you can use her Charged Attacks to deal damage instead of Normal Attacks.

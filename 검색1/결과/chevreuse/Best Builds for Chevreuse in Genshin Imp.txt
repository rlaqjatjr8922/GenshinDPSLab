💪 Chevreuse’s Strengths & Weaknesses
Even though Chevreuse is considered a Pyro character, she can actually deal a mix of Pyro and Electro buffs. She can specifically deal Resistance Shred on enemies which makes her the perfect fit for Shred and Elemental teams. Combine that with basic Attack buffs and she can boost even the simplest teams as powerhouses. Chevreuse can also heal teammates while generating Energy Orbs for those in need. With Chevreuse being a 4-star character, you lose out on the easy signature weapon match. However, she has a lot more versatility on weapons depending on how you play. For Support, you’ll want weapons that increase her survivability and Energy Recharge to perform Skills. For Sub-DPS, anything that deals damage with extra heals is a major plus.

🗿 The Best Artifacts for Chevreuse
Once again, Chevreuse’s best part of her builds depend on whether she’s your Support or Sub-DPS on the team. As a Support, any Artifact that gives a healing bonus is always beneficial. Anything that also increases Energy Recharges for more Skill usage is another plus. Meanwhile, as a Sub-DPS, you’ll want more Elemental Burst and damage coinciding with the Elements.

Kirara Team Overview
This Page provides the best Kirara Team information. Kirara is a Shield/Support in most of the situation.
From SS tier to A tier, Kirarabest team can be formed in different ways including Catalyze, Bloom, Spread, Aggravate, Burgeon and Hyperbloom team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

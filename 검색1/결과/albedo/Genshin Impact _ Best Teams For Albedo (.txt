Best Teams for Albedo
Albedo Works Best As a Support to Hexerei DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Hexerei DPS Team
- Albedo's current best team as a Hexerei character.
- Since his kit changes to a more supportive role, Albedo is built with less focus on his damage compared to before.
- Any Hexerei DPS can be used but Klee and Razor are recommended for the reactions that benefit from Elemental Mastery.
2nd
▼ Overvape Hexerei Team
- Hexerei DPS team but with Pyro, Hydro, and Electro triggering multiple reactions to deal damage.
- Since Lunar-Charge is considered an improved Electrocharged, this team can have two (2) Party Enhancements.
- Albedo's EM buff work great for Lunar-Charge damage but his damage buff doesn't increase it.
3rd
▼ Crystallize Team
- Aside from Hexerei DPS characters, Albedo can be a Sub-DPS for Navia that generates a lot of Crystallize shards.
- With Durin as the Sub-DPS and 2nd Hexerei character, Secret Rite effect is activated allowing Albedo's flower to no longer need frequent replacing.
- Durin also shreds both Pyro and Geo RES of enemies.
4th
▼ F2P Hexerei DPS Team
- F2P Hexerei DPS team consisting of only 4-Star characters aside from Albedo.
- Since Razor is considered the only 4-Star Hexerei DPS, this team focuses on Aggravate but other 4-Star Hexerei characters can be used as DPS.
With Albedo's new Secret Rite buffs, he works better as a support for a Hexerei DPS character. While he can still be used outside of non-Hexerei DPS teams, he's better built as a Sub-DPS there.

Rotation Of Hexerei DPS Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out
4
Switch to Albedo and use his Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Klee and use her Burst then Skill twice to stack up on her buffs
6
Deal damage with Normal Attacks or Charged Attacks
7
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
With the changes to Klee's kit when Secret Rite is active, using her Active Skills right away can enhance her Charged Attacks. Furthermore, her 3rd Normal Attack in her NA string automatically triggers a coordinated Charged Attack.

No.

Best Teams for Hydro Traveler
Hydro Traveler Works Best As On-field Reaction Driver
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Vaporize Team
- Utilizes Hydro Traveler's Hold Skill's fast application of Hydro.
- Xiangling's Burst can Vaporize often with on-field Hydro Traveler.
2nd
▼ Superbloom Team
- Hydro Traveler's Hold Skill can be used as Bloom driver.
- The short duration and CD of the Skill is good for Bloom rotations.
- A healer is needed to sustain the self HP drain that the Hydro Traveler does.
3rd
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- Hydro Traveler's Skill is used for Bloom generation.
- Sacrificial Sword is needed since the Skill cooldown is long.
- Sigewinne is used for Hydro resonance and buffing the Skill DMG of everyone else.
Hydro Traveler has rapid Hydro application that can be used to increase the damage of reactions triggered. Sacrificial Sword is one of the best weapons for the Hydro Traveler to reset their Skill's cooldown.

Xiangling will be the main source of damage in this team. Bennett and Xilonen will be buffing her damage while the Hydro Traveler will apply Hydro at the enemies using their hold Skill.
Rotation Of Vaporize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett and use his Burst to apply Pyro
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attack
3
Switch back to Bennett and use his Skill to generate Energy Particles
4
Switch to Xiangling and use her Burst before the Particles are absorbed
5
Use her Skill before switching her out
6
Switch to Hydro Traveler and use their Burst then hold Skill
7
Apply Hydro on enemies to allow Xiangling's Burst to Vaporize on each hit.
8
Repeat from 1 once Xiangling's Burst expires

Since this team relies on Xiangling's top tier Pyro DMG and application, it can be difficult to replace her. You can use a Hydro sub-DPS instead of Xilonen for more Hydro application.

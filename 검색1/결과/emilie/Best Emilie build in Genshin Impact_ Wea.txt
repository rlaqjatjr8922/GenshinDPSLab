For players who don’t know much about Emilie, she is a 5-star off-field Dendro DPS unit and a Polearm wielder in Genshin Impact. She makes her debut as a playable character in the second half of the version 4.8 update.
For players looking forward to performing gacha pulls on her Character Event Wish banner, bookmark this guide so you know how to build her right.
Genshin Impact Emilie best build
Credit:
HoYoverse
The best build for Emilie focuses on improving her ATK%, as her abilities scale with this attribute, while maintaining a good CRIT Rate to CRIT DMG ratio and Dendro DMG Bonus%.
That being said, for players who don’t know, Emilie’s playstyle revolves around her Lumidouce Case ability, which you can trigger using her Elemental Skill, Fragrance Extraction, and her Elemental Burst, Aromatic Explication.
The ability has three levels — the higher the level, the higher its damage. But that’s the outset of Emilie’s damage-dealing capability.
Credit: ONE Esports,
HoYoverse

The real damage comes from her passive skills, Lingering Fragrance, where each time it collects 2 Scents, the Level 2 Lumidouce Case will consume Scents and release Cleardew Cologne that deals AoE Dendro DMG equal to 600% of her ATK to opponents.
Combined with the other two passives, you’ll get a better idea of what sort of weapons, artifacts, and team comps you must be using with Emilie:
Headspace Capture
: When the Lumidouce Case created by Emilie is on the field, all party members gain 85% Pyro RES against Burning DMG.
Rectification
: Emilie deals increased DMG to Burning opponents based on her ATK, with every 1,000 ATK increasing DMG dealt by 15%. The maximum DMG bonus that can be gained this way is 36%.

For the Genshin Impact veterans who might wonder whether Emilie’s Skills can snapshot, it does not, but on the bright side, her abilities have a high uptime due to the meager cooldown time.
With all that out of the way, let’s look at the best weapons, artifacts, and team comps you will need to attain the best build for Emilie in Genshin Impact.
Best Emilie build: Best weapons
Credit:
HoYoverse
The best weapon for Emilie in Genshin Impact is none other than her signature Polearm weapon, the Lumidouce Elegy, which offers a CRIT Rate% sub stat and a weapon skill called Bright Dawn Overture tailored towards Emilie’s character kit.
Screenshot by Hritwik Raj/ONE Esports
However, if you are not willing to perform gacha pulls on Emilie’s weapon, you can use any other Polearm that offers a sub stat that raises CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, or ATK% and a weapon skill that increases ATK%, such as Shenhe’s signature Polearm, the Calamity Queller, Hu Tao’s Staff of Homa, Xiao’s Primordial Jade Winged-Spear or any other.
Craftable weapons like the Kitain Cross Spear or event weapons like the Missive Windspear are excellent choices for free-to-play players. For slight spenders, we recommend the Deathmatch Polearm, which you can get from the Shop or battle pass. It is one of the best, if not the best, 4-star weapon choice for Emilie.

Best Emilie build: Best artifacts
Screenshot by Hritwik Raj/ONE Esports
The best artifact for Emilie in Genshin Impact is the Unfinished Reverie set, which was introduced in the game with the version 4.6 update. The Unfinished Reverie not only significantly increases ATK% but also improves Emilie’s off-field DPS capabilities and benefits her Pyro team comps.
So how does that work? When you equip four pieces of the Unfinished Reverie artifact set, you get an 18% ATK bonus, and upon leaving combat for 3 seconds, the DMG dealt increases by 50%, with an additional 10%, stacking up to 50% if there are burning enemies.

Another great artifact for Emilie is the Deepwood Memories that you can use to focus on improving Emilie’s Dendro DMG, as it increases the Dendro DMG Bonus by +15% while reducing targets’ Dendro RES by 30% for 8 seconds.
However, if you don’t have good pieces of the above two artifact sets, you can also use an old set like the Golden Troupe to significantly increase the DPS output of Emilie’s Elemental Skill.

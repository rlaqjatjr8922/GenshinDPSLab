Emilie’s Background and Role
Emilie is a newly introduced Dendro (grass element) character in Genshin Impact. She is renowned as the most famous perfumer in Fontaine. Despite not being employed by Fontaine's Cabriere Trading House or affiliated with any brand, her perfumes are highly coveted by the high society ladies, with some even willing to trade a gem for a single drop.
Currently we don't have a good five-star Dendro Support. Emilie could be the Dendro support in the team, and a good match with the other new Fontaine five stars characters, such as Clorinde, Arlecchino, and Neuvillette, etc.
From the story part to analyze her role, she could be a bridge character that connects Fontaine and Natlan. We will learn more about Natlan's stories and characters from her.
Character Interactions and Background
Character Interactions:
Emilie interacts richly with various characters in the game, establishing her as a distinct and charming persona.
Chevreuse:
She has a good relationship with Chevreuse and has frequently assisted her in analyzing evidence and tracking down criminals.
Other Characters:
Emilie’s perfumes receive varied feedback from characters like Lyney, Lynette, Leoslee, Charlotte, and Neuvilette, highlighting her influence and connections in Fontaine.
Design and Community Reaction
Despite Emilie’s intriguing background and role, her design has received mixed reactions from the community. Many players have expressed dissatisfaction with her appearance, feeling that it doesn’t align with the elegant image of a renowned perfumer.
Element and Abilities

Role in Gameplay:
As a five-star character, Emilie is expected to bring significant strength and unique abilities to the team, making her a valuable addition for players focusing on Dendro reactions.

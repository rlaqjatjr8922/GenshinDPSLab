Emilie CONTER
Emilie Conter
, the youngest daughter of
Stephan Conter
, founder of
Stephex Stables
, has been immersed in the equestrian world from a young age. She began her riding career in pony competitions before swiftly advancing to horse events. Today, Emilie competes successfully in international showjumping events ranging from
CSI2
* to
CSI5
* levels.
Emilie’s contributions to the
Belgian National Team
have been significant. In
2014
, she represented Belgium at the
European Championships for Children
, securing a
team silver medal
. Her upward trajectory continued in
2016
when she was part of the
team gold
at the
European Championships for Juniors
in Millstreet, Ireland. Culminating her youth career, Emilie achieved
team gold
and
individual bronze
in the
Young Riders division
at the
2021 European Championships
in Vilamoura, Portugal.
Transitioning seamlessly into senior-level competitions, Emilie has demonstrated her progress on the international circuit. In
February 2023
, she clinched victory in the CSI2* Griffis Residential 1.45m at the Winter Equestrian Festival in Wellington, Florida, riding
Clochard
. Continuing her success, in
February 2024
, Emilie secured first and second places in the
1.40m Speed CSI3
* at the World Equestrian Center in Ocala, Florida, with
Portobella Van De Fruitkorf
and
Cilanto XC Z
.
Emilie’s dedication to the sport is further exemplified by her active participation in the business side of equestrianism, involving herself in the buying and selling of horses for Stephex Stables. Her commitment to continuous improvement and her strategic approach to both competition and business underscore her status as a rising talent in international showjumping.
«Be stubborn about your goals and flexible about your methods.»

In Short
Emilie is a Dendro off-field Sub-DPS with a focus on Burning reactions as she does considerably more damage when enemies are Burning.
The best teams of Emilie will trigger the Burning reaction and maximize her off-field damage output.
The best Emilie teams are Mono Pyro, Vape, Melt, and Aggravate teams.

Emilie is a 5-star Dendro Sub-DPS who wields a Polearm and her kit is
strong in Burning teams
. Emilie’s kit enables her to output continuous Dendro off-field damage. Her damage considerably increases when the Burning reaction is triggered by nearby allies. The best teams of Emilie will need to have characters that can trigger her Burning reaction with consistency to maximize her damage. Here I have ranked the 5 best teams for Emilie in Genshin Impact.
5. Clorinde-Fischl-Emilie-Thoma (Aggravate)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact X (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
The
Clorinde
Aggravate team is one of the best Emilie teams in Genshin Impact. This team has a high damage output but you must ensure that the Electro application on the team is pretty significant compared to the Pyro application.
Double-electro teams work the best in this combo
, especially with Clorinde and Fischl being part of the party.
4. Wriothesly-Xiangling-Emilie-Bennet (Melt)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact X (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Emilie is also a good option for
Wriothesly
Melt teams, dealing a considerable damage output when played correctly. Xiangling and Bennet become the Pyro Applier for the Melt reaction,
and also triggers Burning
which buffs Emilie’s attacks significantly. This team is also easier to play compared to Aggravate teams.

3. Neuvillette-Xiangling-Emilie-Zhongli (Vape)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact X (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Neuvillette
Vape teams
are also a great pick for Emilie, as she can capitalize on the elemental reaction as long as you trigger Burning. Xiangling is one of the best Pyro applier for this team, as her Ultimate can both trigger the Vaporize reaction and cause the Burning for the extra damage from Emilie.
2. Lyney-Kazuha-Emilie-Bennet (Mono Pyro)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact X (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
The
mono Pyro teams are the best teams for Emilie
in Genshin Impact. Emilie can easily replace Xiangling in single-target situations for Lyney teams, dealing comparatively a lot more damage. Emilie is also easiest to play in Mono pyro teams, as you don’t need to constantly worry about the elemental reactions.
1. Arlecchino-Bennet-Emilie-Zhongli (Mono Pyro)
Image Courtesy: Genshin Impact X (Edited by Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Arlecchino’s mono Pyro team is the best Emilie team in the game.
This team perfectly capitalizes on Emilie’s strengths, allowing her to deal constant off-field damage while
Arlecchino
wreaks havoc on the field.
Zhongli
acts as a shielder for Arlecchino, but you can also replace him for Kazuha to get better Crowd Control.

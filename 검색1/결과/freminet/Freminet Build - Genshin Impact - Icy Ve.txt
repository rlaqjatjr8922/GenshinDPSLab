Freminet's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
One of the only characters able to effectively utilise the Shatter reaction.
Reduced stamina consumption underwater aiding underwater exploration.
Capable of being built as either a Physical DPS or Cryo DPS depending on your gameplay preferences.
Weaknesses
As a character who focuses on Shatter and Physical DMG, Freminet lacks the supports to be able to fully utilise him.
Low skill multipliers generally causing low damage.
High energy Recharge requirements.
1.
Freminet Build Guide, Best Artifacts, Weapons and Talent Priority
Freminet has 2 different build options. You can either focus on building 
Freminet as a Physical DPS, or as a Cryo DPS. Both are valid playstyles with 
the Physical playstyle focusing on weaving Normal Attacks and hitting Level 4 
Shattering Pressure and the Cryo Playstyle focusing on more repeated skill spamming.

5.
Combos and How to Play Freminet
Freminet's gameplay mainly revolves around using Normal Attacks
while under the effects of his Elemental Skill
Pressurized Floe
.
Upon use of the skill, it activates his
Pers Timer
.
This causes Normal Attacks to deal additional
Cryo
DMG as
well as increase the Pressure Level by 1 up to a maximum of 4. These change from
blue to yellow as they increase. Pressing Freminet's Elemental Skill
a second time will activate
Shattering Pressure
which does different things depending on the Pressure level:
Level 0 - An attack dealing Cryo DMG.
Level 1 to 3 - Attacks dealing both Cryo and Physical DMG.
Level 4 - Simply deals Physical DMG. Will automatically trigger if you use
a Normal Attack at level 4.
Once Shattering Pressure is used, the Pers Timer will end.
As the Pressure levels increase, more damage will be dealt however as
you can see, Level 4 only deals Physical DMG.
Freminet's Elemental Burst
Shadowhunter's Ambush
helps to support this gameplay loop by resetting the CD of
Pressurized Floe
, decreasing the CD by 70%
while also making Normal Attacks add 2 per hit to the Pressure Level.
You therefore want to use the following combos depending on a Physical
or Cryo build.

Freminet Best Team Comps
Freminet's best teams tend to include another
Cryo
character to help reduce Energy Recharge requirements, provide
Cryo Resonance
and also
provide off-field Elemental Application. A
Hydro
character is then needed to trigger
Shatter
to
activate
Parallel Condensers
. From there, it
depends on if you are playing Cryo or Physical.

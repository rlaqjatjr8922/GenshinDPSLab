Synergy Details
Synergy Of Hyperconduct Freminet Team
Character
Detail
Kuki Shinobu
Kuki Shinobu will allow Hyperbloom and Superconduct to happen in combo with the other characters.
- She also provides healing and extra Energy Recharge if you are using Xiphos' Moonlight.
Nahida
Nahida acts as a Dendro Support and sub-DPS allowing Blooms to happen and boosting Hyperbloom DMG.
Xingqiu
Xingqiu will increase resistance to interruption while providing excellent Hydro infliction to enemies allowing constant bloom to happen while producing significant damage.
The objective of this team is to trigger Superconduct & Hyperbloom simultaneously.
Pros & Cons Of Hyperconduct Freminet Team
Pros
- Since Dendro and Cryo does not react, 2 sets of reaction can be triggered without disruption.
- Very high damage competitive in late game.
Cons
- Freminet is susceptible to interruption if not protected by Xingqiu's swords or if the enemy is not frozen
- Team requires multiple reaction so it does not have space for a crowd control.
Rotation Of Hyperconduct Freminet Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Elemental Skill to mark the enemies
2
Switch to Xingqiu
3
Use Xingqiu's Elemental Burst & Skill for Hydro application
4
Switch to Kuki Shinobu
5
Use Kuki Shinobu's Elemental Skill for Electro application & healing
6
Switch to Freminet and use Elemental Burst.
7
Use Elemental Skill and proceed dealing damage while spamming Normal Attacks and Elemental Skill.
8
Repeat no.1

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Shatter Freminet Team
Character
Detail
Shenhe
With Shenhe's Burst, the enemy's Physical RES can be reduced while inside the Aoe. Shenhe is also a great Cryo applicator and buffer; the additional ATK from Noblesse Oblige is useful for Freminet's DMG
Sucrose
Sucrose has a passive that increases the team's EM and crowd control that can absorb either Cryo & Hydro which is useful for sustaining Freeze.
Kokomi
She is a great healer & Hydro applicator. With Ocean-Hued Clam, Kokomi can periodically deal additional Physical DMG that does not Shatter a frozen enemy.
The objective of this team is to trigger Freeze to the enemy so Freminet can enable Shatter. Elemental Mastery is also useful since it increases the Shatter DMG.
Pros & Cons Of Shatter Freminet Team
Pros
- Make use of constant Cryo and Physical DMG done by Freminet.
- Allows constant activation of Passive of Freminet.
Cons
- Combat handicap when enemies are scattered
- Not all enemies can be frozen, especially bosses and those who are immune to Cryo
Rotation Of Shatter Freminet Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Kokomi's Elemental Skill for Hydro application
2
Switch to Shenhe
3
Use Shenhe's Elemental Burst for triggering Freeze and to reduce the Physical RES of enemies within the range
4
Use Shenhe's Elemental Skill for buff: Press for boosting Skill & Burst & Hold for boosting NA/CA/PA
5
Switch to Sucrose
6
Use Sucrose's Elemental Burst for crowd control and absorbing Hydro
7
Use Sucrose's Elemental Skill for slight crowd control, Anemo particles, & EM buff
8
Switch to Freminet
9
Switch to Freminet and use Elemental Burst.
10
Use Elemental Skill and proceed dealing damage while spamming Normal Attacks and Elemental Skill.
11
Return to no.1
From time to time, you can
switch to Kokomi for Hydro application & healing
. You can also use Kokomi's Burst in case the HP of the team are low.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Mono-Cryo Freminet Team
Character
Detail
Shenhe
She can provide buffs for boosting normal & charged attacks, Skill, Burst, and Cryo DMG which is beneficial for Freminet.
Kazuha
He can shred the enemies' resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 while providing decent crowd control, and an elemental damage bonus with his Burst.
Diona
A great shielder, healer, & battery. She can increase the team's ATK with Noblesse Oblige x4 and EM at C6 which is beneficial for Kazuha.
Pros & Cons Of Mono-Cryo Freminet Team
Pros
- Easy to use comp that has great damage.
- Easy Rotation.
Cons
- Not effective against enemies immuned to Cryo.
Rotation Of Mono-Cryo Freminet Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Diona's Elemental Skill for increased movement speed, shield, & Cryo application to enemies
2
Switch to Kazuha
3
Use Kazuha's Elemental Burst for Cryo-Swirl then Elemental Skill for crowd control
4
Switch to Shenhe
5
Use Shenhe's Elemental Burst for Cryo DMG Bonus
6
Use Shenhe's Elemental Skill for buff: Press for boosting Skill & Burst & Hold for boosting NA/CA/PA
7
Switch to Freminet
8
Use Elemental Burst, Elemental Skill then Normal Attacks.
9
Return to no. 1
From time to time, you can
switch to Kazuha if you need crowd control
. If he triggers Cryo-Swirl, the resistance shred debuff is refreshed. Also, you can
switch to Diona if you need a shield or heal
. Since this team produces a lot of Cryo particles, Diona's Burst can be used more often.

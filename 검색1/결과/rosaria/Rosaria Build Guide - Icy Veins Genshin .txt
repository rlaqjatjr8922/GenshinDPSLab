Cryo
Polearm Character in Genshin Impact who
can be built as a Main DPS through
Reverse Melt
or
built as an Off-Field DPS/Support with offensive buffs and Cryo application. She
is particularly strong with reverse melts and supporting other Cryo characters. This guide will focus on Rosaria's best
weapons, builds, artifacts, teambuilding and more!

Rosaria's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Provides a CRIT Rate buff to your party.
Low skill cooldown with decent particle generation.
Works great both on field and off field.
Weaknesses
Competes with other Cryo characters for the same roles.
CRIT buff is based off Rosaria's CRIT Rate, meaning it does very little when undergeared.
Highly punished for fights where targets run out of her burst AoE, as it is a circle on the ground.

When it comes to Rosaria's stat priorities, we are going to focus more on CRIT Rate
than CRIT DMG. This is due to how her 4th Ascension Perk makes her able to share part
of her own CRIT Rate to the rest of the Party. Aim for around 85% CRIT Rate if you can.
You can read more about this under Combos and How to Play Rosaria.

Due to how Support roles work, Rosaria's weapon selection is mainly focuses around their
effects and their stats, as actual weapon performance will matter less.
Deathmatch

2.
Combos and How to Play Rosaria
Rosaria is straightforward. You want to keep her Elemental Burst on the field as much as possible
and you want to cast her Elemental Skill as often as possible to generate energy
particles.
Ravaging Confession
has a 6s CD and also functions
as a gap closer.
Rites of Termination
lasts for 8s ,12s with
Land Without Promise
(C2),
with a 15s CD.
Regina Probationum
increases her CRIT Rate by 12% for 5s, but
only after you hit an enemy from behind, so make sure to use it at the right position,
when fighting larger targets (Ruin Guards etc.), since it won't dash you behind them.
Shadow Samaritan
gives the rest of your party
(Rosaria excluded!) 15% of
Rosaria's
CRIT Rate. This means you will not be
getting 15% unless Rosaria has 100% CRIT rate. This means you should focus more
on CRIT Rate than CRIT DMG due to this. The general consensus is to have a
1:2 ratio from Rate to DMG, usually 74%/149% CRIT Rate/DMG. The damage loss
when deviating from this is not massive. For Rosaria, this also
means that you can sub out some CRIT DMG for more CRIT Rate. This lowers Rosaria's
personal damage, but increases the damage your team does overall. This does not
mean you want to aim for 100% CRIT Rate, but 85% is about where the CRIT Rate
contribution starts to drop off

Rosaria's Best Team Comps
Rosaria's main team compositions are a bit mixed, as she is both able to be a
Cryo
Driver for
Reverse Melt
,
Cryo Off-field DPS and
Physical
DPS Support. Examples of each
are provided but the list is not exhaustive. Genshin can be pretty flexible,
and Rosaria can fit in well with many teams due to her CRIT buff as long as her
Cryo Application will not mess up the rest of the teams reactions.

5.
Rosaria's Constellations
Rosaria's constellations are pretty straight forward. C2 is her biggest DMG increase,
as the pulsing damage makes up 2/3rds of the damage of her burst. Some of the constellations
ranges from nice to have to not relevant at all, with C6 being the most prominent,
as it allows her to support Physical DMG.

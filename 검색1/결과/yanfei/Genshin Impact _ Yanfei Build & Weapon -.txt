Yanfei Skill Guide & How To Use Yanfei
Recommended Rotation For Yanfei
Recommended Yanfei Rotation
1
Use Off-field skills of supports.
2
Use Normal attacks until you get full Scarlet Seals and finish with Charge Attack
3
Use her Elemental Skill and then Charge Attack.
4
Repeat from number 1
*
Use Elemental Burst when you have it to gain Scarlet Seals Faster.
Uses Of Yanfei's Elemental Skill
Elemental Skill Will Generate Scarlet Seals
Yanfei's Elemental Skill deals with a burst of AoE pyro damage to nearby opponents. If an opponent was successfully hit, Yanfei will gain three (3) Scarlet Seals that will buff her Charge Attack.
Uses Of Yanfei's Elemental Burst
Provides Periodic Gain Of Scarlet Seals
Yanfei's Elemental Burst deals with a rapid intense flame towards the enemies that deals with AoE Pyro DMG. Her burst also gives Yanfei a Scarlet Seals per second even without normal attack making it easier to gain full Scarlet Seals easier.
Normal Attack + Charged Attack Is The Main Source Of Damage
Yanfei's main source of DMG is the combination of Normal Attacks then a finisher Charged Attack. You gain one Scarlet seal every one Normal Attack that will increase the DMG of Charge Attack and reduce the stamina needed for it.
Should You Pull for Yanfei's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Constellation Priority
C1 > C4 > C6
Good at C1 But Has Additional functions at C4
Yanfei is a usable DPS at early game even without constellation but is a lot easier to use with C1. Most of her other constellations just increases her damage.
At C4, she can become a shielder
and being a Pyro Catalyst, she can provide an outstanding amount of ATK through Thrilling Tales of the Dragon Slayer and Pyro Resonance while being a "sustain" of some sort.

Proviso
Effect
When Yanfei consumes Scarlet Seals by using a Charged Attack, each Scarlet Seal will increase Yanfei's Pyro DMG Bonus by 5%. This effect lasts for 6s. When a Charged Attack is used again during the effect's duration, it will dispel the previous effect.
Blazing Eye
Effect
When Yanfei's Charged Attack deals a CRIT Hit to opponents, she will deal an additional instance of AoE Pyro DMG equal to 80% of her ATK. This DMG counts as Charged Attack DMG.
Encyclopedic Expertise
Effect
Displays the location of nearby resources unique to Liyue on the mini-map.
Who Is Yanfei?
A Legal Adviser In Liyue
Yanfei is a well-known and highly regarded legal counsel in Liyue. She's known for her incredible memory as she knows not only Liyue's laws by heart, but also the law of the other nations.
Has Adeptus Blood
Yan Fei is among the few people with Adeptus blood that has yet to form a contract with Rex Lapis. Madame Ping knows her father, an Adeptus.

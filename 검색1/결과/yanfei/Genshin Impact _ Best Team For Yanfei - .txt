Synergy Of Melt Nuke Yanfei Team
Character
Detail
Kazuha
Kazuha will buff the damage of the team by applying Elemental shred and increase the Elemental damage of the team itself
Diona
At C6, Gives additional 200 Elemental Mastery during her Burst, which works well with Yanfei in this Melt Nuke comp
Bennett
With a full Noblesse set, Bennett can increase the Bursts of other members with his own Burst. His Burst will increase the active character's ATK and heal them. This snapshots Xiangling's Burst
- Pyro resonance with Yanfei
In this Melt Nuke Comp, 3 Supports work together to increase Yanfei's Melt damage across all of her attacks. Diona can periodically apply Cryo but make sure that Kazuha swirl both Pyro and Cryo for consistent damage.
Pros & Cons Of Melt Nuke Yanfei Team
Pros
- High damage potential with continuous proc of Melt
- Characters work well together in increasing the damage output
Cons
- Requires 3 of the best Supports in the game
- Requires C6 Diona to get the additional 200 EM in her Burst
- Very strict rotation if you want high damage.
Rotation Of Melt Nuke Yanfei Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Use Bennett's Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst
2
Switch to Diona and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Kazuha and swirl both Pyro and Cryo with Elemental Skill and Burst.
4
Switch to Yanfei and use Elemental Burst then Skill.
5
Use Charged Attack and then continue attacking with Normal attack → Charged Attack combo.
6
Return to no. 1
This comp has a very strict rotation due to Kazuha's Burst's infusion. Since you want it to swirl Cryo, you have to use it against enemies affected by Cryo, not Pyro. Remember that between Cryo and Pyro, the Infusion will always prioritize Pyro.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Vaporize Yanfei
Character
Detail
Xingqiu
Xingqiu will provide additional damage, slight healing and hydro infliction.
Yelan
Yelan will produce Hydro resonance with Xingqiu increasing her personal damage and the shields of Yanfei at C4.
Bennett
With a full Noblesse set, Bennett can increase the Bursts of other members with his own Burst. His Burst will increase the active character's ATK and heal them.
- Pyro resonance with Yanfei
This comp focuses on dealing vaporize damage to group of enemies. This is a powerful team comp great for both solo and AoE DMg. The characters with Yanfei either boost the DMG of Yanfei or provides additional attack to create an overall huge damage.
Pros & Cons Of Vaporize Yanfei Team
Pros
- High damage from the team with Vaporize.
- Great at both single and multiple targets.
Cons
- Requires multiple characters that is used commonly on other comps.
Rotation Of Vaporize Yanfei Team
* This rotation presumes that all characters have their Burst up similar to how they are at the start of a Spiral Abyss floor
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Burst of Xingqiu and then Elemental Skill.
2
Switch to Yelan use Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Bennett use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Yanfei, use Elemental Burst then Elemental Skill.
5
Use Charged Attack and then continue attacking with Normal attack → Charged Attack combo.
6
Return to no. 1
For the rotation, you only need to have the off-field Burst of Xingqiu and Yelan to be active while using Yanfei. Use Yanfei on top of Elemental Burst of Bennett for higher damage.

Synergy Of Shielder Yanfei
Character
Detail
Wanderer
- Yanfei can increase the ATK of Wanderer by providing Pyro + Thrilling Tales of Dragonslayers.
- Yanfei's shield is also great Wanderer who is prone to vulnerability.
Faruzan
- Faruzan provides crowd control and buff for Wanderer.
Yelan
-Yelan works as a sub-DPS that increases the DMG of the main DPS while providing powerful DMG off-field to enemies.
This comp focuses on using Yanfei as a shielder and ATK booster. Yanfei is a flexible ATK booster because of Thrilling Tales and the shield from the 4th Constellation of Yanfei.
Pros & Cons Of Shield Support Yanfei  (Tankfei )Team
Pros
- Allows you to use Yanfei in various teams.
- Significant buff from Yanfei alone.
Cons
- Requires you to have C4 or you cannot use this team or build for Yanfei.

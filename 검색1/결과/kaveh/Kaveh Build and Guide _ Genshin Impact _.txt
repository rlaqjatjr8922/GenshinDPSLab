Kaveh's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Able to manually detonate Dendro Cores using their Elemental Skill or Burst.
Elemental Burst provides a Dendro infusion allowing him to drive Dendro reactions.
Taking damage from Dendro Cores activates Kaveh's powerful self healing abilities.
Weaknesses
Unable to provide anything of value to the team while off-field.
High Energy Recharge requirements and has no Constellation to reduce or solve it.
Low damage while having an on-field role.

3.
Combos and How to Play Kaveh
Kaveh's gameplay revolves around his ability to burst Dendro Cores
automatically in several ways. These are:
Upon use of
Artistic Ingenuity
which will cause all
Dendro Cores to explode if they are in the AoE.
Upon use of
Painted Dome
which also causes Dendro Cores
to explode withing the AoE.
When Kaveh has the Elemental Conversion of
Painted Dome
while having
Pairidaeza's Dreams
(C6).
Do not forget that Dendro Cores burst by Kaveh does not necessarily
mean they have been
triggered
by Kaveh. If he was not the character
who specifically made the Cores they do not gain the effect of
Feast of Apadana
(C4) and do not scale on the Elemental Mastery
that Kaveh has been built with. Instead, they scale based off of the stats of the
character who did trigger the reaction.
It is also worth noting, Kaveh's Dendro Core triggers do in fact
trigger with
Nilou
's Bountiful Cores as a result of
being counted as Dendro Cores. That being said, the explode quickly regardless
creating anti-synergy.
Kaveh's Elemental Burst
Painted Dome
not only
causes Dendro Cores to explode, it also converts your DMG into
Dendro
DMG, increases your Interruption RES and
increases the DMG of all Dendro Cores that the party generates.
This Dendro conversion goes away when Kaveh leaves the field so you
want to make the most of it while he is on-field. After using your
Elemental Burst, the Attack combo is 3 Normal Attacks and then a dash cancel
before the 4th Attack which you then repeat while dealing Dendro DMG.
4.
Kaveh Best Team Compositions
Kaveh's best team compositions revolve around
Bloom
,
Hyperbloom
or
Burgeon
. Because
Kaveh's
Dendro
application comes while on-field,
his party needs to revolve around off-field characters with ideally a
second Dendro character to ease Energy Recharge requirements.
Kaveh has
the ability to self heal meaning another healer may not necessarily be
required or characters with less healing such as
Xingqiu
can suffice.

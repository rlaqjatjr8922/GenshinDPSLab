In Short
Clorinde is a Electro hyper carry with a focus on Normal attacks, and her kit is also focused on Bond of Life.
The best teams of Clorinde will help trigger her passives quickly and maximize her damage output.
The best Clorinde teams are Overload, Electro-charged, Quickbloom, and Aggravate teams.

Clorinde is a 5-star Electro DPS who wields a Sword and her kit is very strong and continues the Bond of Life mechanic trend.
Clorinde’s kit
allows her to build her Bond of Life when using her Normal attacks or receiving healing. Having Bond of Life increases her damage output and allows her Skill to do significant damage, especially if it’s more than 100%. The best teams of Clorinde will need to have characters that can trigger her Passive ability with consistency while maximizing her damage. Here I have ranked the 5 best teams for Clorinde in Genshin Impact.
5. Clorinde-Chevreuse-Fischl-Xiangling (Overload)
Image Courtesy: Hoyoverse/Genshin Impact (Edited by: Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Overload teams are always a good pick for Electro characters
, especially if you have Chevreuse in the roster. However, one of the big issues of Overload teams has always been crowd control (even with Chevreuse), which is why this team synergy is put at 5th. Clorinde’s best Overload team will be Clorinde, Chevreuse, Fischl, and Xiangling. You can also replace Xiangling with Bennet.
4. Clorinde-Fischl-Yelan-Xingqiu (Electro-Charged)
Image Courtesy: Hoyoverse/Genshin Impact (Edited by: Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Electro-charged teams are going to be a solid pick for Clorinde teams
, doing a fair amount of damage while getting the best out of Clorinde’s kit. Pair Clorinde with two Hydro appliers like Yelan and Xinqiu and add another electro applier like Fischl or Beido to get create the best electro-charged teams for Clorinde. Her best electro-charged team would be Clorinde, Fischl, Yelan, and Xingqiu.
3. Clorinde-Furina-Nahida-Fischl
Image Courtesy: Hoyoverse/Genshin Impact (Edited by: Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Furina
teams can also work great with Clorinde.
Furina can use Clorinde’s HP drop and regain mechanic
due to her Bond of Life to further boost her damage. Although this team is basically a version of the Quickbool teams given below, it’s a bit weaker compared to it as survivability can become a big factor. The best Furina team with Clorinde will be Clorinde, Furina, Nahida, and Fischle.

2. Clorinde-Nahida-Fischl-Yelan (Quickbloom)
Image Courtesy: Hoyoverse/Genshin Impact (Edited by: Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Quickbloom is another great choice for Clorinde teams and can be very close to the first spot in terms of damage output. Because of Clorinde’s kit, Hyperbloom becomes kind-of difficult for her, whereas
Quickbloom becomes the perfect reaction
. Pair Clorinde with Nahida, Fischl, and Yelan to get the best out of her Quickbloom teams. You can also swap in Xingqiu or another Hydro-applier for Yelan in this team.
1. Clorinde-Nahida-Fischl-Kazuha (Aggravate)
Image Courtesy: Hoyoverse/Genshin Impact (Edited by: Sanmay Chakrabarti)
Aggravate teams appear to be the best for Clorinde, as both Aggravate and Quicken will register as two electro reactions, stacking Clorinde’s passive quickly. Also, Clorinde has to attack so much due to her kit,
Aggravate teams make the most sense for her
at the moment. Pairing her with Nahida, Fischle and a crowd-controller like Kazuha seems the best team for Clorinde. You can also swap in Baizhu for Nahida or Kuki Shinobu for Fischl to get the best out of her aggravated teams.

Gaming is a 4-star Pyro character who launched alongside
Genshin Impact
version update 4.4. Here are our recommended Artifacts, weapons, and more to build him.
On December 18, 2023, HoYoverse officially revealed two units who made their first appearance in the
game
during the Lantern Rite 2024 festival. One of them was
Xianyun
, whom players know as Cloud Retainer.
The second one was Gaming, a Pyro 4-star character who wields a Claymore. Gaming will be featured as one of the available 4-star characters on Furina’s rerun and Sigewinne’s debut banner. This will run from
June 25, 2024, to July 16, 2024.

Gaming abilities in Genshin Impact
Normal Attack: Stellar Rend
Normal Attack: Performs up to 4 consecutive strikes.
Charged Attack: Drains Stamina over time to perform continuous spinning attacks against all nearby opponents. At the end of the sequence, performs a more powerful slash.
Plunging Attack: Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Elemental Skill: Bestial Ascent
Pounces forward using the Wushou arts, leaping high into the air after hitting.
After Gaming has used Bestial Ascent to rise into the air, he will use the especially powerful Plunging Attack: Charmed Cloudstrider when performing a Plunging Attack.

Plunging Attack: Charmed Cloudstrider
The DMG from Plunging Attacks caused by Bestial Ascent is converted to
Pyro DMG
that cannot be overridden by other elemental infusions. Upon landing, Gaming will consume a fixed amount of HP. Gaming’s HP cannot be reduced below 10% by this method.
Charmed Cloudstrider DMG is considered Plunging Attack DMG.
Article continues after ad
Elemental Burst: Ring of Bursting Grenades
Gaming enters a “Wushou Stance” and hurls forward his companion, Lion Man Chai. It’ll deal AoE Pyro DMG and Man Chai will return to Gaming after rolling near the area and smashing the targets. The moment Man Chai returns, it’ll reset Gaming’s Elemental Skill. Man Chai can be summoned again if Gaming performs his plunging attack again with 50%+ HP.

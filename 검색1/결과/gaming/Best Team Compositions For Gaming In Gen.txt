Gaming is a four-star-on-field DPS character that mainly uses plunge attacks for damage and to cook the best Gaming teams you must understand the recipe to make the best Gaming team.
Gaming must need at least one healer to be his party member so that he can continue to do plunge attacks with his elemental skill and all other party members can be his damage buffers. As for Gaming's best party members, they are Xianyun, Bennett, and Chevreuse.
Gaming’s Best Overall Team: Gaming Xianyun Vaporize
Hoyoverse@Twitter
Xianyun being a
plunge attack buffer
and a character that
allows all characters to plunge attack
, it's no surprise that a
Gaming team with Xianyun is one of his best teams
. While taking advantage of Gaming element Pyro and its powerful reaction, Vaporize, and with the added support of Xianyun, you can certainly expect to easily clear any hard content Genshin Impact has to offer.
To effectively play this team,
make sure to use one normal attack before every plunge
with Gaming and make sure the enemy always has hydro applied. If you do not follow this combo, you will be doing considerably less damage.
Gaming
: Main DPS(On-field)
Bennett
: Attack Buffer and Healer
Xianyun
: Plunge Damage Buffer, Healer and Support
Xingqiu
: Hydro application for enabling reactions
Character
Action
Xianyun
Start by
swirling pyro
(from Bennett's elemental skill) with Xianyun elemental skill and use Xianyun's Elemental Burst.
Xianyun's buff from her burst enables Gaming to do plunge attacks faster
, and he will get a great damage boost in plunge damage thanks to Xianyun's passive skills.
Bennett
Immediately use Bennett's Burst
after switching from Xianyun for Bennett's attack buff and healing.
Xingqiu
Use
Xingqiu's elemental skill and then his Elemental burst
. Now with all the buffs and Xingqiu's rain swords, you are ready to deal damage with Gaming.
Gaming
Upon switching to Gaming,
immediately use his Elemental Skill, do a normal attack
, and then you can start spamming plunge attacks. Make sure to do a normal attack before trying to jump and plunge attack.
Gaming Chevreuse Overloaded
Hoyoverse@Twitter
Considering a vaporize or melt team with Gaming would seem like the go-to team, but
Gaming Chevreuse’s Overloaded is considered to be Gaming's best consistent team
. Thanks to her
high buffs such as attack buff, resistance shred, and healing
, she is one of the best contenders for Gaming’s best teammates.
With consistent damage output,
this team is also a good choice if you do not have Xianyun and are looking to go to the deepest floors of Spiral Abyss
with Gaming while staying on a budget.
Using this team comes with the added benefit of being able to do great damage on enemies that can't be vaporized, and there is no need to manage your attack combos for doing reactions.
Constellations on characters
like C6 Chevreuse and C6 Fishcl will make a huge difference
in this team composition's overall damage output.

Gaming Vaporize Team
Hoyoverse@Twitter
A Vaporize team with Gaming and no Xianyun is certainly viable. With some
attention to how your Xingqiu is applying Hydro onto the enemy
, you can make a Gaming team that is going to dish out big numbers on every hit.
Sucrose and Kazuha can be a great replacement for Xianyun
in this team and Sucrose can be considered even better than C0 Kazuha in this team.
Vaporize Gaming does suffer from problems like
not being able to Vaporize consistently and performing poorly on enemies or bosses that can't be vaporized
. Make sure to follow the normal attack first before the jump and plunge rule as well.
Gaming
: Main DPS(On-field)
Bennett
: Attack Buffer and Healer
Sucrose/Kazuha
: Damage Buffer
Xingqiu
: Hydro application for enabling reactions
The team rotations for playing this team are;
Character
Action
Sucrose/Kazuha
Swirl Pyro
with your anemo character's elemental skill and
do not use their burst
.
Bennett
Use Bennett's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst like usual.
Xingqiu
Start by using Xingqiu's Elemental Skill first, and then his Elemental Burst. Now use your anemo character's elemental burst for a
hydro infused burst and extra hydro application
for Gaming to vaporize his damage.
Gaming
Use Gaming's Skills like normal while making sure you are
keeping the enemy applied with hydro
.

Gaming Melt Team
Hoyoverse@Twitter
This is another team that takes advantage of Pyro’s powerful reactions and Gaming’s high multipliers.
With constant Cyro application from Kaeya
you can cook a fun team to play that is going to hit big numbers every jump, if you can keep Kaeya hitting the enemy all the time.
This team composition can be
somewhat inconsistent depending on what kind of enemy you are fighting
. Kaeya's Elemental Burst radius is low and since his burst has to hit at least 2 to 3 times before Gaming's plunge for a proper Cyro application, using this team can be tricky.
This team is especially bad against short enemies like treasure hoarders and is
too hard to use on enemies that can fly or go underground
, so if you are looking to hit the highest numbers possible with Gaming, this team is your best choice.
Gaming
: Main DPS(On-field)
Bennett
: Attack Buffer and Healer
Kaeya
: Cyro application for enabling reactions
Sucrose/Kazuha/Xianyun
: Damage Buffer
The rotations for playing this team are;
Character
Action
Sucrose/Kazuha/Xianyun
Use these characters' Elemental Skills to swirl Pyro from any convenient source and
only use Elemental Burst if you are using Xianyun
.
Bennett
Use
Bennett's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst
for a powerful attack buff and healing.
Kaeya
Start by using his
Elemental Skill and then use his Elemental Burst
within Bennett's burst.
Gaming
Use your Gaming Elemental skill first and then proceed to spam his plunge attacks while
making sure you are very close to the enemy
so that Kaeya's Elemental Burst can hit.

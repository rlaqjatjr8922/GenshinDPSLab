Ineffa is the
first character from Nod-Krai
and will work as a guide for the traveler in the new region. This means that she will likely become better as more artifacts, weapons, and characters are released in Nod-Krai. So,

Ineffa has
mostly pros and is highly recommended
for Genshin Impact players. She can deal insane amounts of damage while staying off-field. Ineffa also enables new Lunar-charged reactions, which are basically electro-charged reactions that can deal CRIT damage. This makes her invaluable and probably the strongest Electro Sub-DPS in the game.
Should You Get Ineffa’s Signature Weapon?
Image Credit: Hoyoverse (via YouTube/Genshin Impact)
Ineffa’s signature polearm, Fractured Halo, is strong for
increasing both her and the team’s damage
. The Fractured Halo increases Attack by 24% and when the equipping character makes a shield, it increases all allies’ Lunar Charged damage by 40%. Equipping Ineffa’s signature weapon will greatly increase her damage output.
However, Ineffa also has other good weapon options as suggested below. She strictly doesn’t need her signature weapon, but I would recommend you get it over her C1.

When it comes to stats, we suggest you simply
maximize the Crit Rate on Ineffa
first. As Lunar-Charged reactions can CRIT, you do not want to miss a CRIT chance. Next, make sure to get at least 150% Crit Damage; the more the better.
Follow up with the ATK% stat, as it scales Ineffa’s entire damage. The more attacks you can have on Ineffa, the better. Finally, you can put EM and ERR on Ineffa, but these are not her priorities.

Genshin Impact Ineffa Kit: Talents, Constellations, and Rotations
Genshin Impact Ineffa Rotation
Skill > Burst > Swap > Repeat
When it comes to Ineffa’s rotation in Genshin Impact, start with Skill and then use her Elemental Burst. Next, swap to your other characters, based on the team comp you have chosen to apply the elements correctly on the enemies.
Once you have procced the elemental reaction, repeat the same thing again to restore your shield and Ineffa’s summon. You can completely ignore Ineffa’s basic attacks as they don’t provide any additions to her abilities.

Cyclonic Duster
(Normal Attack)
– Normal Attack: Performs up to 4 consecutive spear strikes.
– Charged Attack: Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to perform a spinning attack.
– Plunging Attack: Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Cleaning Mode: Carrier Frequency
(Elemental Skill)
– Ineffa engages her Enhanced Cleaning Module, dealing a single instance of AoE Electro DMG to nearby opponents, activating her Optical Flow Shield Barrier, and summoning the Multipurpose Smart Assistance Unit, Birgitta.
– The Optical Flow Shield Barrier absorbs DMG based on Ineffa’s ATK, and it will absorb Electro DMG with 250% effectiveness.
Supreme Instruction: Cyclonic Exterminator
(Elemental Burst)
– The Multipurpose Smart Assistance Unit, Birgitta, can use Rocket Punches to quickly clean up opponents! Ineffa shoots Birgitta into the fray, dealing AoE Electro DMG and allowing Birgitta to remain on the field.
– If Birgitta is already on the field, having been summoned by Ineffa, this Skill will re-summon Birgitta at the target’s location and reset her duration.
Moonsign Benediction: Assemblage Hub
(Passive 1)
When a party member triggers an Electro-Charged reaction, it will be converted into the Lunar-Charged reaction, with every 100 ATK that Ineffa has increasing Lunar-Charged’s Base DMG by 0.7%, up to a maximum of 14%.
Overclocking Circuit
(Passive 2)
If there are thunderclouds created by Lunar-Charged reactions nearby when Birgitta unleashes Discharge attacks, she will initiate an additional attack, dealing AoE Electro DMG equal to 65% of Ineffa’s ATK. This DMG is considered Lunar-Charged DMG.
Panoramic Permutation Protocol
(Passive 3)
When using the Elemental Burst Supreme Instruction: Cyclonic Exterminator, all your party members will gain the Parameter Permutation effect: Increases Ineffa and your own active party member’s Elemental Mastery by 6% of Ineffa’s ATK for 20s.
Flavor Synthesis Unit
(Passive 4)
When Ineffa uses food, there is a 30% chance of gaining a seasoning ingredient.
Additionally, in Nod-Krai, it seems you’ll be able to change Birgitta’s appearance through certain means…
Rectifying Processor
(C1)
When Ineffa activates her Optical Flow Shield Barrier, all nearby party members will gain the Carrier Flow Composite effect for 20s: Increases Lunar-Charged DMG by 2.5% for every 100 ATK that Ineffa has, up to a maximum of 50%.
Support Cleaning Module
(C2)
After the Elemental Burst Supreme Instruction: Cyclonic Exterminator hits opponents, it will inflict the Punishment Edict status on one of those hit opponents: After a brief delay, or when this opponent is attacked, nearby opponents will take AoE Electro DMG equal to 300% of Ineffa’s ATK. This DMG is considered Lunar-Charged DMG. Additionally, when the Elemental Burst Supreme Instruction: Cyclonic Exterminator is unleashed, nearby party members will gain Optical Flow Shield Barriers.
Enhanced Emotion Emulator
(C3)
Increases Elemental Skill Level by 3
The Edictless Path
(C4)

Mirror’s Dream Transcension
(C5)

A Dawning Morn for You
(C6)
When Ineffa is affected by the Carrier Flow Composite effect, she will deal AoE Electro DMG equal to 135% of her ATK to opponents in the current active character’s vicinity after nearby thunderclouds release bursts of lightning at opponents. This DMG is considered Lunar-Charged DMG. The aforementioned effect can trigger once every 3.5s.

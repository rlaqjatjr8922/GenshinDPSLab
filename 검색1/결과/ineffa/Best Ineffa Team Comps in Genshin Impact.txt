Ineffa, the first 5-star character from Nod-Krai, brings a unique twist to Genshin Impact’s combat with Lunar-Charged reactions, a variant of Electro-Charged that can critically hit. As an off-field Sub-DPS and buffer, she can fit into multiple team setups, but her best teams revolve around Hydro characters to trigger Lunar-Charged consistently.

How Ineffa Team Comps Work
Ineffa’s strength lies in:
Consistent off-field Electro application
Boosting Lunar-Charged reactions with Crit potential
Providing shields and team-wide Elemental Mastery buffs
To maximize her potential:
Pair her with at least one Hydro character to reliably trigger Lunar-Charged.
Include buffers or drivers like Anemo characters to spread reactions.
Consider dual Electro or dual Hydro teams for stronger resonance and reaction uptime.

Pro Tips for Building Ineffa Teams
Always run at least one Hydro teammate for consistent Lunar-Charged triggers.
Dual Hydro or Dual Electro teams enhance reaction uptime and energy generation.
Anemo drivers like Kazuha, Sucrose, or Ifa spread elements in AoE and boost EM for higher reaction damage.
Prioritize Crit Rate and Crit DMG for Ineffa, as Lunar-Charged can CRIT; EM is a secondary stat for reaction scaling.
Recommended Rotation: Skill → Burst → Swap to Main DPS → Trigger Reactions → Repeat when Burst is ready.

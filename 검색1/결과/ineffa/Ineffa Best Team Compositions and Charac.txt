Ineffa's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Ineffa team composition guide and 
character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we'll explore the best teams to build for Ineffa, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and team compositions to help Ineffa 
reach her full potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Ineffa
Ineffa works best on teams that can utilize the Lunar-Charged reaction and
maximize its damage. This entails pairing her with a
Hydro
unit, and
characters of other elements that are not disruptive to keeping both
Hydro
and
Electro
auras on enemies consistently.
Columbina
Columbina
Columbina is Ineffa's greatest teammate, providing the Hydro needed for
	Lunar-Charged, providing fantastic buffs to the reaction, and even
	dealing excellent Lunar-Charged damage of her own. Pairing Columbina
		and Ineffa together results in a powerful duo that can fit into many
		different teams, dealing tremendous Lunar-Charged damage from off-field.
Hydro
Hydro
If not using Columbina, you still need a Hydro unit on the team.
		For off-field Hydro options,
Aino
works well as she provides Moonsign
		to enable Ascendant Gleam, and can buff the active character's EM and 
		Lunar DMG% with her C1 and C6 respectively.
Furina
works well if there 
		is a healer on the team, but keep in mind her DMG% buff does not apply to
			Lunar damage. If not using Aino or Furina, the next best options will
			generally be
Yelan
and/or
Xingqiu
.
Sucrose
Anemo
Anemo
Anemo characters are fantastic on Lunar-Charged teams, lowering enemy
	resistance by 40% via 4pc Viridescent Venerer.
Sucrose
will usually be the
best Anemo unit, thanks to providing great Elemental Mastery buffs while also 
being able to buff your active character's ATK% via Thrilling Tales.
Jahoda
is also a good option, as she can enable Ascendant Gleam, while providing
some Elemental Mastery and even some CRIT DMG buffing at C6. Jahoda also 
provides healing, which can allow
Furina
to be used as the Hydro slot if not
using Columbina.
Ifa
also works well here, directly buffing Lunar-Charged DMG%. However, he needs
to be used on-field, so it's not recommended to use him in teams with another
on-fielder.
		On-field Hydro appliers can also be good to use, such as
Neuvillette
,
Kamisato Ayato
,
		or
Tartaglia
.
Flins
Electro
Electro
Flins
is a very powerful DPS to use with Ineffa, as he deals excellent
		Lunar-Charged damage and further buffs the reaction's damage, allowing
		him and Ineffa to deal incredible damage together. 
			Although not as strong as Flins, other Electro DPS can also work well 
			with Ineffa, especially if they have Elemental Mastery scaling. For example,
Cyno
,
Sethos
, and
Yae Miko
.
Geo
Geo
Geo can be used on Lunar-Charged teams, enabling the Lunar Crystalize
	reaction to be used as well. This can decrease the amount of damage procs
	from the Lunar-Charged Thundercloud, but there is still enough uptime for
	Ineffa to get all of her direct Lunar-Charged damage procs. As such, this is 
	a powerful team core for many Geo DPS, such as
Navia
,
Arataki Itto
,
Noelle
, and
Ningguang
.
	If using Ineffa with a Geo DPS, then
Xilonen
also becomes a strong 
			support option instead of Anemo, able to provide RES Shred to both
Electro
and
Geo
.
Durin
Durin
can trigger both Vaporize and Overload on Lunar-Charged enemies,
			allowing him to deal good damage while providing some RES Shred. His long
			uptime makes him a good alternative to Anemo in teams with an on-fielder
			with long field time, such as
Cyno
.
Pyro
Pyro
With sufficient Hydro application, Pyro DPS can be used in Lunar-Charged
	teams, and the Pyro DPS can trigger both Overload and Vaporize. Since the
	Pyro application will continuously remove the Lunar-Charged aura from
	the enemies, this results in less damage procs from the Thundercloud. 
			However, with sufficient Hydro application, there is still enough for 
			Ineffa to get all of her direct Lunar-Charged damage instances.
			Some Pyro DPS that can work well here are
Hu Tao
,
Lyney
, and
Arlecchino
.
Dendro
Dendro
Dendro characters can be used with Ineffa to create Hyperbloom and 
	Quickbloom teams. With fast enough Hydro application, Lunar-Charged can be
	triggered enough to not miss any direct Lunar-Charged damage on Ineffa, 
	allowing her to still deal her own high damage, while either she or another 
	Electro unit also deals Hyperbloom damage. If there is not enough 
	Hydro application, Dendro can still work well with Ineffa but there will be no Lunar-Charged uptime, 
			and Ineffa will be relying mostly on Hyperbloom for damage. Alongside 
			that Hyperbloom damage, she still provides her Shield, Elemental Mastery 
			buff, and can also enable Quicken.
			Some Dendro units that can work well with Ineffa are
Alhaitham
and
Tighnari
as on-field options, and
Lauma
and
Nahida
for off-field options.

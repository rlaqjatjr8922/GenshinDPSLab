Key Of Khaj-Nisut Vs Primordial Jade Cutter
While both weapons can boost the user's max HP, the Key of Khaj-Nisut has a higher HP increase compared to Primordial Jade Cutter. However, choose the weapon that fits your preference.

Rotation Of Shielder Layla Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Layla's Elemental Burst for off-field Cryo DMG
2
Use Layla's Elemental Skill for shield & periodic Cryo DMG
3
Switch to Bennett
4
Use Bennett's Elemental Burst for ATK buff
5
Switch to Xingqiu
6
Use Xingqiu's Elemental Burst & Skill for Hydro application & coordinated attack
7
Switch to Yoimiya
8
Use Yoimiya's Elemental Skill for Pyro infusion and utilize it until the duration ends
When Yoimiya's Pyro infusion has ended, switch to other characters and use their Skill to produce particles for Burst. When everything is ready, repeat the rotation again. It is recommended to set up everything first before using Yoimiya's Skill for optimized damage output.
When To Use Yoimiya's Burst
If you are using Shimenawa's Reminiscence, you can often use her Burst since you need to have at least 15 energy to trigger its x4 effect. However, if you are using Echoes of an Offering, you can use it whenever available.

Variation Of Morgana Team
▼Morgana team
is a team comp that can trigger Freeze consistently which immobilizes the enemies. This team is widely known since it offers a safe yet effective combat environment.

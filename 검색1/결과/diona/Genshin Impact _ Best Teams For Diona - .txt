Diona Works Best as Shield Support
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Shield Support Diona
- Best team for Diona's Cryo Off-Field application from her Burst is Melt.
- Her ability to buff EM at C6 allows her to increase the reaction damage.
- Can be used with most DPS characters but the ones that can benefit from Cryo reactions and Elemental Mastery work better with support Diona.
2nd
▼ Premium Freeze Team
- Diona as a 4-star shielder and her great battery potential makes her a good member for Freeze if you don't have some of the other premium members.
- Healing can overlap with Escoffier's role but unless a low HP DPS is used, Diona and Escoffier can be used together.
3rd
▼ Physical Support Diona
- Diona can be a good Battery for Cryo Physical DPS like Eula and Freminet.
- Freminet is able to make use of Diona's EM buff since Shatter scales on it while both Eula and Freminet can benefit from her Shield and healing.
- Diona's Off-Field Cryo application can be used for triggering Frozen to enable Shatter that Freminet needs.
Diona's best role is as a Shielder who can provide healing and an EM buff at Constellation Level 6. She also works great as Energy battery for Cryo characters because of her multi-hit Skill that can easily trigger Favonius Warbow's effect.

3

8
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire

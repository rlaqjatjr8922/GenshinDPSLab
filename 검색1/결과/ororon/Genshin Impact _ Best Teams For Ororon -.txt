Recommended Teams For Ororon
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Taser Team
★★★★★
- Ororon's strongest team.
- Increased chance to trigger his passive's follow up attack.
▼ Chasca Rainbow Team
★★★★・
- Fully maximizes Chasca's converted Shell DMG multipliers
- Allows for additional application of Hydro and Electro to trigger Electro-Charged for Ororon's passive
- Ororon passive synergy with Chasca's Nightsoul-aligned DMG
▼ Hyperbloom Team
★★★・・
- Variation of the Hyperbloom team
- If Electro-Charged can be guaranteed, Ororon can use his follow up attack to have consistent Electro applied on the enemies
Ororon’s role is an Electro sub DPS with passives leaning towards Electro-charged teams. His elemental Burst's summon taunts enemies, leading to a better DMG window for main DPS characters. With C6, he can buff the on-field character’s ATK when triggering his passive.

In Taser teams, Ororon is the latest best Electro sub DPS character because of his passives.
Rotation Of Taser Team
No.
Procedure
1
Enter with Neuvillette and use his Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Kazuha and use his Elemental skill. Be sure to not hold the Skill button
3
Switch to Xilonen and use her Burst
4
Hit enemies with 2 of Xilonen's Normal Attack then use her Skill
5
Switch to Ororon and his Skill then Burst
6
Switch to Neuvillette and start using Charged Attacks to deal DMG
7
Use Neuvillette's Skill or Burst when you run out of Sourcewater Droplets.
8
Once Ororon's summon disappears, start from No. 2

- Any Hydro main DPS work for this team.

Xilonen

- Furina can boost Neuvillette's DMG greatly and deal off-field Hydro DMG to trigger Ororon's Nightsoul points passive generation.
Taser is a flexible team, needing at least 1 Hydro and 1 Electro character. Anemo or Electro character can be used as reaction driver but you will have to adjust the team comp and will limit the number of non-Hydro/Electro support characters.

In the classic “soup” or Rainbow team, Ororon replaces the Electro sub DPS. Chasca is the on-field reaction driver, even allowing Bennett to be used due to her ability to deal Pyro DMG. Chasca is able to deal Nightsoul-aligned DMG that can trigger Ororon’s follow up attack without needing Electro-Charged reactions.
Rotation Of Chasca Rainbow Team
No.
Procedure
1
Enter with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Chasca and use her Skill
5
Use Charge Attacks to aim and begin loading Shells to deal DMG
6
Repeat from 1

Sucrose
- Any Anemo Catalyst characters can work as the reaction driver substitute for Chasca
- But without Chasca's Shell conversion mechanic, it will be difficult for Pyro application to happen unless an off-field Pyro DPS instead like Xiangling.
- Changing to a Taser team comp might work if needing to substitute Chasca
Furina
Kokomi
Ayato
Hydro
- Kokomi and Ayato can both do off-field Hydro DMG. Kokomi can also heal the team if in her Burst mode.
- If using Chasca as driver, any Hydro characters work due to her Shell conversion mechanic. But those with the ability to buff the team or deal off-field Hydro DMG are preferred
- Note that Chasca's main source of DMG is her Shells that deal Charged Attack DMG, off-field sub DPSes that require Normal Attacks will be harder to use with Chasca
Bennett
Dehya
Pyro
- Dehya can provide DMG mitigation and off-field Pyro DMG.
- Any Pyro character can be used as substitute due to how Chasca's Shell conversion works.
- Note that, without Bennett's healing, Furina's off-field DMG will be lessened if the team's HP is below 50%

Substitute Characters
Character
Sub
Details
Neuvillette
Mualani
Ayato
Childe
- Mualani can deal Nightsoul-aligned DMG to trigger Ororon's follow up attack. However, her Hydro application is lacking as such you will need a Hydro sub DPS to make up for it
- Ayato is a good alternative as a Normal Attack DMG dealer. His Burst range and scaling are good but not as strong as Neuvillette's unique DMG scaling.
- Childe's Skill cooldown will be tricky to use in rotations but he can be used as alternative Hydro driver for Hyperbloom
- Any Hydro Catalyst character can work as reaction driver but they may perform not as well as the 3 mentioned characters.
Baizhu
Nahida
Collei
Yaoyao
- Neuvillette's self healing suffices most of the time so using a non-healer Dendro substitute is fine.
- Using Nahida allows for EM buff and longer range for off-field Dendro DMG
Furina
Kokomi
Yelan
Xingqiu
- Kokomi can be used as healer along with her summon for off-field Hydro DMG. With the right equipment, she can buff the DMG dealer but you'll have to adjust the rotation accordingly
- Yelan and Xingqiu are good alternatives for off-field Hydro DMG and DMG buffs. The rotation will have to be adjusted to include Normal Attacks from the DMG dealer.

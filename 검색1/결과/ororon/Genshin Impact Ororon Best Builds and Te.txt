Ororon Strengths and Weaknesses
Strengths
● Benefits from the Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City set, which increases team Elemental DMG by up to 28%.
● Dominates in AoE situations with decent damage.
● Delivers consistent off-field Electro damage and passive Electro application.
● The hypersense effect maintains nearly full uptime in well-organized teams and improves overall efficiency.
● Simple to build, as his passive damage scales with ATK.
● Rocket jump ability provides mobility and allows him to bypass vertical obstacles.
Weaknesses
● Relies on Electro-Charged or Nightsoul-aligned damage to activate Hypersense, which limits team flexibility.
● Electro's application weakens outside of his ideal team setups.
● Elemental Skill has limited hits, and each enemy can only be struck once.

The best artifact for
Ororon Genshin

Ororon Best Team Comps

That said, he isn't completely restricted to them. He can perform well in teams that provide consistent Electro and Hydro application, or in teams that include a Nightsoul-aligned ally to trigger his passives.

Ororon Talents and How to Use
Before we conclude our
Ororon build
guide, let's take a closer look at his talents and how to use them.
Ororon Rotations
Ororon's core rotation is straightforward. Simply bring him on-field for a short window to cast his Elemental Skill and Burst, then switch out. His Hypersense effect will continue to deal consistent Electro damage off-field, which maintains his Sub-DPS contribution.
Normal Attack
●
Normal Attack:
Fires up to three consecutive arrows with a bow.
●
Charged Attack:
Performs a precise and aimed shot that deals higher damage. When fully charged, the arrow becomes infused with Electro energy and causes Electro DMG on hit.
●
Plunging Attack:
Shoots a volley of arrows while descending and causes AoE damage upon impact with the ground.
Elemental Skill – Night's Sling
Ororon summons a Spirit Orb of midnight energy and channels an ancient Night-Wind technique to hurl it at his enemies and cause Nightsoul-aligned Electro DMG.
The Spirit Orb can bounce between nearby opponents, but strikes each one only once per use. After up to three bounces, or when no additional targets remain, the orb dissipates and ends the attack sequence.
Elemental Burst – Dark Voices Echo
Ororon performs an ancient ritual that releases a wave of Nightsoul-aligned AoE Electro DMG and summons a Supersonic Oculus:
● Taunts nearby enemies and draws their attacks.
● Rotates continuously and emits sonic waves that deal with Nightsoul-aligned Electro DMG over time.

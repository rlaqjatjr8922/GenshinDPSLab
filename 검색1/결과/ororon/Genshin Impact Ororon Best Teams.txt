Ororon
is finally here, and those of you who have been eager to get your hands on the emo boy will love to learn that he is actually a good addition to many teams even at C0, making him one of the more useful 4-star characters to be released in recent times. Ororon’s ability to buff the whole team if equipped with the Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City makes him a good supporting Electro character, and his passive, which only applies when he’s used with other Electro or Hydro characters, makes him a good choice in almost any Electro or Hydro team. So, let’s not waste any more time and see where you can play Ororon.

Free-to-play players, you’re eating well today. Thanks to his amazing versatility and buffs, Ororon actually has a ton of great free-to-play teams that bring back some old 4-star characters and bring them to a level where they don’t fall behind even some of the better 5-stars. The f2p team we’ve gone with includes:
Sucrose
Ororon
Xingqiu
Beidou
This team uses Sucrose as the driver to proc a ton of Swirl reactions and obliterate the enemy with all of the damage that will be coming from Xingqiu, Beidou, and lastly Ororon. If you don’t have Sucrose, you can also opt to use Heizou as the team’s driver thanks to his high damage potential and Anemo Element.

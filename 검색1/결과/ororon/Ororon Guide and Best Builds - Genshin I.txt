Ororon Guide and Best Builds
Ororon is a 4-star Electro Bow character in Genshin Impact who acts
as a Sub DPS for Electro-Charged reactions and taser teams.
This Ororon guide covers his best builds, artifact sets,
weapons, team compositions, and talents to maximize his role as an AoE-focused
off-field damage dealer.

Ororon's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Excels in AoE situations providing decent Damage.
Elemental Burst provides a taunt to nearby enemies.
Good character to use Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City on, allowing Ororon to act as a buffer too.
Weaknesses
Triggering Electro-Charged is essential to fully utilize Ororon's kit, limiting team-building.
Elemental Skill only hits 3 times normally and 5 with C1 but more importantly, each enemy can only be hit once.

Ororon’s build benefits from versatile weapons that cater to both
supportive and damage-focused roles. His best weapon is
Elegy for the End
,
offering a balance of support and energy generation. Other excellent
choices include
Polar Star
for increased damage output or
Favonius Warbow
for energy regeneration. That being said, Ororon has plenty of build options.
Ororon's best artifact set is generally
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
however if another character is using this set, Noblesse Oblige is your 
nest best option.
Focus on upgrading Ororon’s Elemental Burst and Elemental Skill as they
form the core of his gameplay. His Normal Attack is not necessary for most
builds, making investment in that talent redundant.

Ororon Best Team Comps
Ororon's best teams often revolve around the
Electro-Charged
reaction, also known as Taser teams. This requires at least 1
Hydro
character to be present in the team. Ororon is often compared to
Fischl
who fills a similar role as an
Electro
Sub DPS however Ororon provides significant lower damage and
generates less energy as a trade-off for being more AoE-focused.

Ororon Ascension Passives and Materials
Ascension Passive
Explanation
Nightshade Synesthesia
After a nearby party member triggers Nightsoul Burst, Ororon will gain 40 Nightsoul points.
     Within 15s after using his Elemental Skill, when other party members' Hydro or
     Electro attacks hit opponents, Ororon will gain 5 Nightsoul points, an effect
     that can occur every 0.3s for a maximum of 10 times during this 15s duration.
     Ororon can have a maximum of 80 Nightsoul points.
Also, when nearby opponents take Electro-Charged reaction DMG or
     Nightsoul-aligned DMG dealt by other nearby characters, Ororon will consume
     10 Nightsoul points (provided he has at least that amount), entering the
     Nightsoul's Blessing state and triggering the Hypersense effect: Deal
     Nightsoul-aligned Electro DMG based on 160% of Ororon's ATK to at most
     4 nearby opponents. The aforementioned effect can trigger once every 1.8s.
Nightsoul's Blessing: Ororon
Ororon's Nightsoul's Blessing state lasts 6s.

's Spirit Orb hits
    an opponent, Ororon will gain the Aspect Sigil effect for 15s.
Aspect Sigil
When a nearby active party member hits an opponent with Normal, Charged,
    or Plunging Attacks, that character will restore 3 Energy. If Ororon is
    off-field, Ororon will also restore 3 Energy. This effect can trigger
    once every 1s, and can trigger 3 times per duration.
Night Realm's Gift: Flowing Fog, Spritely Shadows
While in an area with Phlogiston Mechanics within Natlan, he can use Nightsoul Transmission: Ororon. When the active character is currently in the air, the following will trigger when switching to Ororon: Ororon will leap up high. Nightsoul Transmission can be triggered once every 10s by your party.
Holding Aiming Mode will cause Ororon to enter the "Spiritspeaker" state, which will allow him to interact with "Nightspirit Graffiti" and "Nightspirit Sigils," extracting information and power from them. The rules for interacting with these objects follow the same rules as those governing Iktomisaurus interactions.
Additionally, holding the jump button will cause Ororon to consume 75 Stamina and leap. In areas within Natlan where Phlogiston mechanics are present, Ororon will prioritize consuming 5 Phlogiston to leap to a height higher still.
When in the air, Ororon can Hold his Normal Attack to consume Phlogiston or Stamina and enter Aiming Mode.
The Art of Skinchanging
Increases gliding SPD for your own party members by 15%.
Not stackable with Passive Talents that provide the exact same effects.
Ororon gains ATK % as he levels giving him a total of 24% ATK %
at level 90.

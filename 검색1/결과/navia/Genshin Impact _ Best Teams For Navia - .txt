▼ Double Pyro & Geo Navia
★★★★★
-  Greatly boost the personal DMG of Navia through Geo and Pyro Resonance.
- Fully utilizes the kit of Navia and deals powerful damage.
- Restrictive team comp, however.
▼ Navia-Furina Comp
★★★★・
- Furina will boost the DMG of Navia while inflicting enough Hydro for Crystalize.
- Team requires a team wide healer to be consistent.
▼ Sub-DPS Navia
★★★・・
- Team that mainly uses Navia's Elemental Burst and Skill.
- Flexible teamcomp compared to other options but less up-time for Navia.
Navia is a Geo DPS that mainly deals damage from Elemental Skill. She boost damage from Crystalize so pairing her with off-field characters that reacts with Geo is recommended. Forming a team around Navia as a Main DPS or a Quickswap DPS is the best way of using her.

This comp focuses on inflicting Pyro DMG to the enemies and triggering Crystalize using Navia and Albedo while boosting the personal DMG of Navia with Geo and Pyro Resonance.
Rotation Of Double Pyro & Geo Navia Team Comp
No.
Procedure
1
Start by placing Albedo's Elemental Skill on the ground.
2
Use Bennett's Elemental Burst then use Elemental Skill
3
Switch to Xiangling and use Elemental Burst then Skill.
4
Switch to Navia and use Elemental Burst then Elemental Skill.
5
Gather Crystalize shards while dealing Normal Attack then use Elemental Skill.
6
Repeat 1 onwards
*
Use Elemental Burst of Albedo if it is active.
The goal of the rotation is to create crystalize using Pyro and Geo then use those Crystalize to gain stacks for Elemental Skill of Navia. For maximum damage from Xiangling, make sure to use Bennett's Ultimate first then Xiangling's Elemental Burst.

This team focuses on using Furina and Navia together to deal powerful damage. Navia and Furina will be the permanent member while the other two characters are for increasing the DMG and healing for Fanfare stacks.
Rotation Of Furina-Navia Team Comp
No.
Procedure
1
Use Furina and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
2
Switch to Zhongli and use Elemental Skill (Hold) then Burst.
3
Switch to Sigewinne and use E. Skill.
4
Switch to Navia and use Elemental Burst then gather crystalize then use Elemental Skill.
5
Deal normal attacks to produce more Crystalize then Elemental Skill.
6
Once Elemental Burst of Furina ran out, switch to Furina and use Elemental Burst and Skill.
7
Repeat from no. 2 onwards.
The goal is to use Navia when there are a lot of Fanfare stacks from Furina. Make sure to use Elemental Skill of Furina to inflict Hydro and produce Hydro Crystals with Geo. Sigewinne is there mostly for healing but it is also ideal to use her skill before Navia for a decent Skill Buff.

This team uses Navia as a sub-DPS. With Archaic Petra, she can provide DMG buff for the team while providing decent damage.
Rotation Of Sub-DPS Navia Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Albedo and place Elemental Skill.
2
Use Bennett and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Navia and use Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Lyney and use Elemental Burst, Charged Attack then Elemental Skill.
5
Absorb Crystalize and use Elemental Skill.
6
Repeat 1 onwards
For the rotation, just make sure to release all the buff before using your main DPS. Once Navia has max stats, use Elemental Skill.

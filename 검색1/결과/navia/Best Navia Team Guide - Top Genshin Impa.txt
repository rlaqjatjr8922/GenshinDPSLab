The All-Rounder Dream Team 💎
This is the team I consider Navia's absolute best-in-slot, the one that feels tailor-made for her. It's the squad I use when I want to tackle any challenge the game throws at me.
Navia
(Main DPS)
Furina
(Off-field DPS & Buffer)
Zhongli
(Shielder & RES Shred)
Bennett
(Healer & ATK Buffer)
Now, I won't sugarcoat it—this team requires a significant investment. Having three five-star characters, especially ones as sought-after as Furina and Zhongli, can be a tall order for free-to-play players. But if you have them, the synergy is just incredible. Here's why each piece is crucial:
Furina
is the star of the show besides Navia herself. Her Elemental Burst provides a massive, team-wide DMG bonus that scales with HP changes. Since Navia's Skill hits like a truck, this buff multiplies her damage to insane levels. Furina's off-field Hydro application is also perfect for creating consistent Crystallize shards.
Zhongli
is my comfort pick. His shield provides near-invincibility, which is great because it lets Navia focus on her combos without dodging every attack. More importantly, his shield reduces all elemental and physical RES of nearby enemies by 20%. This stacks beautifully with...
Geo Resonance!
Having two Geo characters activates one of the best resonances in the game. It increases shield strength (making both Zhongli's shield and your Crystallize shields tougher), gives a 15% DMG bonus while protected by a shield, and reduces Geo RES of enemies you hit. With Zhongli's shield, you're shredding a whopping 40% Geo RES!
Bennett
is the final piece of the puzzle. His Burst provides a gigantic ATK boost based on his own Base ATK, which is exactly what Navia, who scales heavily on ATK, needs. He also provides crucial healing to help manage Furina's HP-draining mechanic.
My Recommended Rotation:
Start with
Furina
. Use her Skill (I prefer the Salon Solitaire mode for off-field Hydro) and then her Burst to set up her massive DMG bonus field.
Swap to
Zhongli
. Drop his Stone Stele with his Skill (hold for a stronger shield) and use his Burst if it's ready for extra crowd control and damage.
Quickly switch to
Bennett
. Use his Burst inside Furina's field, then tap his Skill. A pro tip I've learned: equipping him with a
Favonius Sword
and triggering its passive here helps funnel energy particles to both him and Furina, ensuring their Bursts are ready for the next rotation.
Finally, bring out
Navia
. Pick up any Crystallize shards on the field, then unleash her Skill for massive damage. Follow up with her Normal Attacks while she has Geo infusion from her Skill, and use her own Burst whenever it's available to maintain off-field Geo damage and generate more shards.
The Frozen Shatter Symphony ❄️💧
Sometimes, you want to try something different, something that capitalizes on Navia's Claymore wielder status. That's where this freeze-and-shatter team comes in. It's a bit more niche but incredibly satisfying when it works.
Navia
(Main DPS / Shatter Trigger)
Charlotte
(Healer & Cryo Applier)
Yelan
(Off-field DPS & Buffer)
Xingqiu
(Off-field DPS & Damage Reduction)
This team is all about control and reaction. Shatter is a transformative reaction triggered when a
Blunt
attack (from Claymores, Geo attacks, etc.) hits a
Frozen
enemy. While its damage isn't the highest, the setup here creates a safe environment for Navia to operate.
Charlotte and the Hydro Duo (Yelan & Xingqiu)
work together to constantly freeze enemies. This not only locks them in place but also sets them up for Shatter.
Navia
is the perfect Shatter trigger. Every time she hits a frozen enemy with her Geo-infused Normal Attacks or her Skill, she'll cause a Shatter reaction. While the focus is still on her raw Skill damage, the constant freeze provides immense utility.
A nice bonus is
Hydro Resonance
from having two Hydro characters, which increases the team's max HP. This directly benefits Yelan's damage and Charlotte's healing.
My Recommended Rotation:
Begin with the Hydro supports. Use
Xingqiu's
Skill and Burst, then immediately switch to
Yelan
to do the same. This layers their powerful off-field Hydro attacks.
Bring in
Charlotte
. Use her held Elemental Skill to apply a strong Cryo aura to multiple enemies. If your team needs healing, use her Burst as well.
Switch to
Navia
. With enemies frozen, you can safely execute her full combo: Skill > Burst > Geo-infused Normal Attacks. The freeze will re-apply constantly thanks to your off-field supports. A fun weapon choice here is the
Sacrificial Greatsword
; if its passive triggers, you can use her Skill twice in quick succession for a huge damage spike!
The Classic Pyro Powerhouse 🔥
Before Furina's release, this was the quintessential Navia team for many players, myself included. It's straightforward, powerful, and uses some of the game's most reliable and accessible four-star supports.
Navia
(Main DPS)
Xiangling
(Off-field Pyro DPS)
Zhongli
(Shielder)
Bennett
(Healer & Buffer)
This team is a masterclass in simplicity and power. It leverages the infamous "National Team" core (Xiangling + Bennett) and adds Geo for resonance and durability.
Pyro Resonance
from having two Pyro characters gives a straight 25% ATK increase, which is a fantastic passive buff for Navia.
Xiangling
is the engine. Her Pyronado, especially when snapshotting Bennett's ATK buff, deals monstrous off-field damage and, most importantly for Navia, creates a
ton
of Pyro Crystallize shards.
Zhongli and Bennett
provide the same incredible utility as in the first team: unbreakable shielding, RES shred, healing, and a massive ATK buff.
The key to this team is speed. Bennett's Burst field doesn't last forever, and you want Xiangling's Pyronado to snapshot that buff.
My Recommended Rotation:
Bennett
first, always. Drop his Burst, then use his Skill to generate energy particles.
Immediately switch to
Xiangling
. Use her Burst and Skill inside Bennett's circle to snapshot the ATK buff.
Swap to
Zhongli
and use his hold Skill for the shield. You can use his Burst if you need the petrification, but it can eat into your buff timers.
Finally,
Navia
takes the field. Gather the Pyro shards created by Xiangling and unleash your cannon barrage. The beauty of this team is its energy economy; with two Pyro characters, Xiangling and Bennett battery each other, making rotations smooth and consistent.
Final Thoughts from a Navia Enthusiast
Playing Navia has been one of my most rewarding experiences in
Genshin Impact
. She successfully modernized the Geo element by making a previously defensive reaction (Crystallize) into an offensive tool. Whether you go for the premium Furina team, the controlled Freeze-Shatter comp, or the classic Pyro powerhouse, the core principle remains: surround her with characters that can consistently apply elements off-field to feed her Crystal Shrapnel stacks. Don't be afraid to experiment with other supports like
Albedo
for extra Geo damage and particles, or
Fischl
for consistent Electro application. The most important thing is to have fun unleashing those satisfying cannon shots. Happy building, and may your Navia always crit!

Best weapons for Navia
The Verdict stands as Navia's ultimate weapon choice, providing a comprehensive boost to her combat prowess. This 5-star claymore not only enhances her Critical (CRIT) stats and Attack (ATK), but also offers Elemental Skill bonuses, all of which are essential for amplifying her initial burst of damage. Its well-rounded stats make it an ideal fit for Navia's aggressive playstyle.
However, if you're looking to experiment with different strategies, consider equipping her with the Serpent Spine or The Unforged. These weapons can offer alternative benefits, particularly when you aim to leverage the Crystallize reaction for creating powerful shields. The Serpent Spine, with its focus on HP and DEF, can be a strategic choice for enhancing survivability, while The Unforged can provide a unique utility in managing elemental reactions.
By selecting the right weapon, you can tailor Navia's role in your team, whether you prioritize raw damage output or strategic shielding. Let's delve into how these weapon options can complement Navia's capabilities and further refine her gameplay.

Team Comps Recommendations
Navia thrives in a team adept at activating the Crystallize reaction efficiently. To maximize her potential, she requires skilled Sub-DPS allies capable of introducing additional elemental effects and delivering off-field damage.
1.
Navia Hypercarry Team
In these team compositions, Navia's role is to capitalize on the elemental diversity applied to enemies, enabling her to execute Crystallize effectively. As a core player on-field, she'll initiate her own Elemental Skills or Bursts following the setup by her teammates.
Characters like Xiangling and Yelan work well in this as they are capable in dealing Off-Field damage as Navia takes the field.
2.
Navia Main DPS in Furina Teams
Navia's compatibility with the Marechaussee Hunter set makes her an excellent partner for Furina, especially in leveraging her off-field capabilities. Integrating a team-wide healer such as Charlotte can further enhance Furina's damage output, but traditional support characters like Bennett are equally viable for boosting the team's overall performance.
3.
Navia Sub DPS Teams
Navia's agility in quickswapping positions her well for a Sub-DPS role within teams featuring other prominent DPS characters. By incorporating Furina alongside Noelle as the Main DPS, you can create a balanced team composition that offers healing, damage, elemental resonance, and damage buffs specifically for Navia.
Additionally, Navia can don the Archaic Petra set to bolster her teammates from the sidelines, switching to her strategically to introduce Geo elements amidst a mix of other elemental influences.
Summary
Firstly, it should be noted that Navia, as a C0 character, is not particularly highly recommended for pulling, because the strength of Neuvillette, Furina, and Arlecchino is clearly much higher than that of Navia. In the case of full constellation, Wriothesley and Clorinthe are also stronger than Navia. Therefore, it is not recommended to pull for Navia if you have other elemental main DPS characters. However, if you enjoy playing with a Geo team and lack a character for single-target combat, then Navia could be a good pull for you.

Kokomi Team Overview
This Page provides the best Kokomi Team information. Kokomi will be DPS/Healer and Kokomi DPS build will be used in most of the situation.
You can also swap the position of Kokomi and Mona to make your own best team.
From SS tier to S tier, Kokomi best team can be formed in different ways including Vapourize, Permafrost and Melt team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

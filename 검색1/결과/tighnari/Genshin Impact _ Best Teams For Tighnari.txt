Synergy Of Spread Aggravate Tighnari Team
Character
Detail
Yae Miko
The attack interval of her turrets can work perfectly with Tighnari's playstyle.
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of Spread & Aggravate reactions. Also, she can decrease the marked enemies' DEF when her C2 is unlocked.
Kuki Shinobu
Her Skill can heal the active character and she can provide an EM bonus to the team thru the Key of Khaj-Nisut.
The objective of this team is to consistently trigger Electro-Dendro reactions in quick succession for catalyze reactions.
Pros & Cons Of Spread Aggravate Tighnari Team
Pros
- Multiple EM bonuses can stack altogether: Dendro resonance, Nahida's Burst, & Key of Khaj-Nisut
- Tighnari's taunt can give Yae Miko a time buffer for setting up turrets
Cons
- Kuki's HP is reduced whenever she uses her Skill so she needs to be on-field when her HP is critical
- Yae Miko needs ample time from time to time to set up all 3 turrets
- The 3 turrets should be connected altogether for maximum damage
- Yae Miko's Burst cost is high so it is often used
Rotation Of Spread Aggravate Tighnari Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Burst for EM buff
2
Use Nahida's Skill for mark
3
Switch to Yae Miko
4
Use Yae Miko's Skill thrice to deploy 3 turrets
5
Switch to Kuki Shinobu
6
Use Kuki Shinobu's Skill for periodic healing & EM bonus
7
Switch to Tighnari
8
Use Tighnari's Elemental Skill for taunt & empowered CA
9
Use Tighnari's Wreath Arrow thru CA x3
10
Use Tighnari's Elemental Burst to consume energy
11
Return to step 1
Occasionally, you can use Yae Miko's Skill to reposition or sustain the turrets and use her Burst whenever available.

Synergy Of Spread Tighnari Team
Character
Detail
Yae Miko
The attack interval of her turrets can work perfectly with Tighnari's playstyle.
Kazuha
He can shred the enemies' Electro resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 when Electro-Swirl has been triggered with his Burst. Freedom-Sworn also increases the damage output of Tighnari's charged ATK & wreath arrows.
Baizhu
An excellent healer & shielder to pair with another Dendro such as Tighnari. Deepwood Memories can reduce enemies' Dendro resistance which can improve Tighnari's damage output.
The objective of this team is to sustain the presence of all of Yae Miko's turrets for periodic Electro DPS. In this way, Tighnari can easily trigger a spread reaction.
Use Hakushin Ring For Buffer Yae
If you do not have Kagura's Verity, the
Hakushin Ring
is a great substitute as it
can provide an Elemental DMG bonus
for Yae Miko and Tighnari when triggering Electro-Dendro reactions.
Pros & Cons Of Spread Tighnari Team
Pros
- Bonus EM & ATK buffs are both beneficial for Tighnari & Yae Miko
- Tighnari can taunt enemies with his Skill
- Active character does not get interrupted while shielded
Cons
- Baizhu needs decent Energy Recharge to sustain the shield
- Yae Miko needs ample time from time to time to set up all 3 turrets
- The 3 turrets should be connected altogether for maximum damage
- Yae Miko's Burst cost is high so it is often used
Rotation Of Spread Tighnari Team
No.

1
Use Yae Miko's Skill thrice to deploy 3 turrets
2
Switch to Kazuha
3
Use Kazuha's Elemental Burst for Elemental DMG bonus & Electro-Swirl
4
Switch to Baizhu
5
Use Baizhu's Elemental Burst for shield
6
Use Baizhu's Elemental Skill for Dendro particles
7
Switch to Tighnari
8
Use Tighnari's Elemental Skill for taunt & empowered CA
9
Use Tighnari's Elemental Burst to consume energy
10
Use Tighnari's Wreath Arrow thru CA x3
11
Return to step 1
Occasionally, you can use Yae Miko's Skill to reposition or sustain the turrets. Also, you can freely use the burst of Yae Miko & Tighnari whenever available.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Hyperbloom Tighnari Team
Character
Detail
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of Hyperblooms.
Xingqiu
The consistent Hydro application can produce Dendro cores, especially when Tighnari is beside the enemy.
Kuki Shinobu
Her Skill can heal the active character and the Electro application makes her high EM significant for boosting the damage out Hyperbloom projectiles. The buff from Freedom-Sworn can empower Tighnari's charged ATK.
The objective of this team is to produce Dendro cores and transform them into hyperbloom projectiles thru Kuki's Skill. The projectiles are homing which chase an opponent even if it is quickly moving or afloat.
Pros & Cons Of Hyperbloom Tighnari Team
Pros
- Tighnari's projectiles can deal damage together with hyperbloom projectiles
- Tighnari's taunt can give Kuki a time buffer whenever she needs to heal herself
- Kuki's Skill is efficient since it heals and transforms Dendro cores simultaneously
Cons
- Kuki consumes HP whenever Skill is used so she needs on-field time to recover
- Could be difficult to utilize when facing an enemy that has a debilitating debuff such as corrosion
Rotation Of Hyperbloom Tighnari Team
No.

9
Use Tighnari's Elemental Burst to consume energy
10
Use Tighnari's Wreath Arrow thru CA x3
11
Return to step 1
Even if Kuki's Burst does not deal much damage, its Electro application does not have a cooldown which is useful for transforming Dendro cores quickly. Also, it is advantageous for decreasing the enemy's DEF with C2 Nahida.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Bloom Tighnari Team
Character
Detail
Nilou
Nilou upgrades Dendro cores into Bountiful cores if the team consists of only Hydro & Dendro characters.
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of Bountiful cores. When C2 is unlocked, Bountiful cores have a chance to CRIT.
Kokomi
An excellent healer who can consistently apply Hydro for faster bloom production. With Wandering Evenstar & Tenacity artifact, she can also provide an ATK buff & slight ER bonus to the team.
The objective of this team is to produce Bountiful cores as fast as possible. Since Bountiful cores can be produced in a Dendro-Hydro team with Nilou, Tighnari can fit in this team comp.
Pros & Cons Of Bloom Tighnari Team
Pros
- Tighnari can deal damage together with Bountiful cores
- Tighnari's taunt can give Nilou a time buffer to free hit with Hydro-infused attacks or finish Skill casting thrice
- Slow from Cryo is not a problem since it does not affect Nilou's Hydro aura & Kokomi's Hydro application from Skill
Cons
- Bountiful cores are less effective against an opponent that moves a lot or afloat most of the time
- Nilou's Bountiful cores could be weak against enemies with Dendro resistance
Rotation Of Bloom Tighnari Team
No.

8

9
Use Tighnari's Elemental Burst to consume energy
10
Use Tighnari's Wreath Arrow thru CA x3
11
Return to step 1
You can juggle between Nilou's Skill and Kokomi's Skill for Hydro application. If you require healing, use Kokomi first instead.

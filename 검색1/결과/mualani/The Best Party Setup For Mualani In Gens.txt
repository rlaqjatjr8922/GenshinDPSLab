Genshin Impact

Mualani

Mualani is a five-star Hydro character using a Catalyst, and you can easily see some big damage numbers from her. However, if you want to take her to Spiral Abyss or Imaginarium Theater, you'll have to build a synergistic team with her.
Best Mualani Team
Mualani
Zhongli
Xiangling
Emilie
/
Nahida
If all the characters mentioned above are available to you, you can easily
put them in a team together and use them without a second thought
. Due to the lack of synergistic Natlan characters on Mualani's release, this is by far the best team you can use with her to not only get the most damage output overall, but also see the highest numbers Mualani can put out.
The main idea of this team is to apply the
Burning effect on the enemies using the combination of Xiangling and Emilie
, which constantly applies the Pyro element on them. This is already the perfect scenario for Emilie to deal a ton of damage, and the constant Pyro application also allows Mualani to
consistently trigger the Vaporize reaction
.
This is especially good if you have Mualani's signature weapon
since it relies on the Vaporize reaction
to give her the most damage.
Finally, the role of Zhongli in this team is to
reduce the enemy's RES to all the elements involved
, which further buffs your damage. Ideally, you'd want to have the highest possible Elemental Mastery on both Emilie and Mualani in this team, and here's what your rotation will look like:
Start with Zhongi's shield and follow it up with Xiangling's Elemental Skill and Burst to apply the Pyro element on the enemies. It's highly
recommended to build Xiangling on Energy Recharge
for this team.
Switch over to Emilie and use her Skill and Burst to trigger the Burning reaction and
you'll already start seeing a bunch of damage
.
Finally, bring Mualani on the field and
use as many fully buffed Normal Attacks as you can
in her Nightsoul state.
Due to the fast cooldowns, the rotation for this team will feel quite refreshing. However, you also
have to pay a lot more attention to be efficient with this team
.
Vaporize-Focused Mualani Team
Mualani
Furina
Dehya
/
Xiangling
Kaedehara Kazuha
This team will feel a lot like the previous one, except it removes the Dendro element from the equation and
focuses more on buffing Mualani's damage as much as possible
. Unfortunately, the lack of off-field Pyro applicators in the game makes Dehya and Xiangling the only choices for this, and you'll also have to Swirl Pyro with
Kazuha's Burst to get the required
Pyro application.
Both Furina and Kazuha are an important part of this team since they can heavily buff Mualani's overall damage. However, this team
loses a ton of damage after a few rotations since there's no healer
and Furina's Burst doesn't buff your damage if your team can't lose any more HP.
To make this team give you a little less but regular damage, you'll
have to replace Kazuha with Xianyun or Jean
.
The rotation for this team is highly important as it can easily make or break your damage:
Start with using a normal attack from Mualani to apply Hydro on the enemies and Swirl it with Kazuha's Skill before
switching to Dehya and using her Skill as well
.
Now, switch back to Kazuha and
use his Burst to Swirl Pyro
and start the constant application.
Finally, you can switch to Furina to use her abilities and
then execute the buffed Mualani attacks
.
Keep in mind that you have to do all of this quite fast since
Kazuha's main buff only lasts for ten seconds
after Swirling Hydro at the start.
Overall, this team is a lot harder to use than Mualani's main team, and you'll also get less value in most situations. However, it can be
good if you want to deal some high damage
for a screenshot.
Mono-Hydro Mualani Team
Mualani
Mona
Sucrose
/
Kaedehara Kazuha
Sangonomiya Kokomi
Whenever a new DPS gets released in Genshin Impact, it's almost a necessity to make a mono-elemental team around them since these can be quite fun to use. Of course, you'll
get a lot less value from this team and it nullifies some of the passive from Mualani's signature weapon
unless you're up against an enemy that can apply Pyro to itself.
The main idea behind this team is to use Mona's Elemental Burst to
buff Mualani's damage alongside the boost from Swirl and potentially Kazuha
. Kokomi is also the perfect sustainer to use here since she can consistently heal the active character.
The rotations for this team aren't specifically complicated since you can use any character at any point as long as
you use Mona's Burst and Swirl before switching
to Mualani.

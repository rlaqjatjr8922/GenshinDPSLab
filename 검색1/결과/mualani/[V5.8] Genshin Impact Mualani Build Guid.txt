Best Team Comps
Mualani’s kit confines her damage potential to teams that make use of the Vaporize reaction. As a result, her team compositions generally fall into two categories: pure
Vaporize teams
, which focus solely on Vaporize, and
Burn-Vape teams
, which combine both Burning and Vaporize reactions.
1. Mualani Vaporize Team
In Vaporize teams, Mualani relies on an off-field Pyro unit to consistently apply Pyro, allowing her to trigger Vaporize effectively. Adding supports that can reduce enemies’ Hydro resistance further amplifies her damage potential, with top choices including Xilonen, Kazuha, Zhongli, and Sucrose.

2. Mualani Burn-Vape Team
The Burning state is effective for protecting Pyro elements, allowing Hydro elements to vaporize more reliably. This synergy is ideal for Mualani's team composition. To maintain the enemy's Burning state, it's essential to include both a Pyro and a Dendro character in the team.

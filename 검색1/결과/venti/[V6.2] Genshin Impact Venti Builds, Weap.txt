Crowd Control Support / Sub-DPS
Venti is the 5-star Anemo bow wielder who possesses the ability to Swirl and massively crowd control smaller enemies with his Burst. His sub-DPS build focuses on stacking Elemental Mastery for his Elemental Absorption damage, which offers the best damage against multiple enemies. His position as a premier crowd control specialist has remained valuable in some endgame content throughout current meta, particularly in content featuring multiple smaller enemies where his grouping capabilities shine.
Venti Gameplay
Venti
's gameplay centers around his Elemental Burst, which summons a massive vortex that continuously pulls enemies inward while dealing Anemo damage over time. His Elemental Skill, summons a Wind Domain that deals AoE Anemo damage and launches enemies into the air, with the Hold version allowing Venti to ride the wind. His Burst provides VV RES Shred, enemy grouping, energy regeneration, and respectable DPS from frequent Swirls.
His capabilities include providing exceptional crowd control that groups smaller enemies into one location for efficient AoE damage, consistent energy regeneration through his Burst's refund mechanic, and universal elemental RES shred via the Viridescent Venerer artifact set. His A4 passive regenerates 15 Energy for Venti after Wind's Grand Ode ends, and if an Elemental Absorption occurred, this also restores 15 Energy to all characters of that corresponding element.
Upcoming Hexerei Buffs (Version 6.2)
In the upcoming
Version 6.2 update
,
Venti
will receive significant buffs through the Hexerei Resonance mechanic after completing Witch's Work: On the Waking of Wind. His Normal Attacks will become Windsunder Arrows that pierce enemies and are infused with Anemo after using his Elemental Burst. During his Elemental Burst Wind's Grand Ode, when the active character triggers a Swirl reaction, that character's damage is increased by 50% for 4 seconds, and the Stormeye deals 135% of its original damage.
These buffs transform Venti from primarily a support character into a more formidable on-field damage dealer option when paired with at least one other Hexerei character (Durin, Albedo, Mona, Klee, Fischl, Sucrose, or Razor). His constellations will also receive enhancements, particularly C1 which will fire two additional Hurricane Arrows that track enemies, and C2 which can reset his Elemental Skill cooldown after his Burst ends with a 300% damage buff.

Elemental Burst - Wind's Grand Ode
Level his Elemental Burst first to boost its damage, duration, and energy absorption capabilities. His Burst is the primary focus as it provides strong grouping and Energy regeneration from frequent Swirls, as well as RES Shred from 4-piece Viridescent Venerer. The vortex can absorb Pyro, Hydro, Electro, or Cryo elements to deal additional elemental damage, making it incredibly versatile in reaction-based teams.
Elemental Skill - Skyward Sonnet
Level his Elemental Skill second, as it provides consistent Anemo application and generates energy particles. The skill has a short cooldown and can be used to trigger Viridescent Venerer's resistance shred effect. The Hold version's ability to launch Venti into the air also provides valuable positioning advantages and exploration utility.
Normal Attack - Divine Marksmanship
The Normal Attack Talent does not hold much value for typical Venti playstyles. Level this last unless you're pursuing the experimental CRIT-focused Anemo DPS build. However, with the upcoming Hexerei buffs in Version 6.2, Normal Attacks may gain more relevance as they transform into Anemo-infused Hurricane Arrows after using his Burst.

Venti Constellations
Venti performs well even at C0, providing excellent crowd control and energy support. However, his constellations do offer notable improvements, particularly for players wanting to maximize his damage potential.
C1 Performance
Venti's C1 fires 2 additional arrows per Aimed Shot, each dealing 33% of the original arrow's damage. This constellation has limited value for typical Venti playstyles since he rarely uses Aimed Shots in combat. However, with the upcoming Version 6.2 Hexerei buffs, C1 will fire two additional Hurricane Arrows that track enemies and can deal infused elemental damage, making it significantly more valuable.
C2 Performance
Skyward Sonnet decreases enemy Anemo RES by 12% for 10s, and enemies launched by Skyward Sonnet suffer an additional 12% Anemo RES and Physical RES decrease while airborne. This constellation provides valuable resistance shred and benefits Anemo DPS builds. With Version 6.2 buffs, C2 will also be able to reset his Elemental Skill cooldown after his Burst ends with a 300% damage increase, making it extremely powerful.
C4 Performance
When Venti picks up an Elemental Orb or Particle, he receives a 25% Anemo DMG Bonus for 10s. This constellation benefits CRIT-focused Anemo DPS builds but requires rotation adjustments to maximize its effectiveness. In Version 6.2, this buff will activate when using his Skill or Burst rather than requiring particle pickup, making it more consistent.
C6 Performance
C6 causes targets who take damage from Wind's Grand Ode to have their Anemo RES decreased by 20%. If an Elemental Absorption occurred, their RES towards the corresponding Element is also decreased by 20%. This RES Shred is a great addition to Venti's buffing capabilities and simultaneously increases his Swirl damage, making it beneficial for any playstyle. In Version 6.2, C6 will also grant enemies 100% increased CRIT DMG taken, dramatically increasing team damage potential.
Conclusion
This comprehensive guide demonstrates Venti's position as one of the most valuable crowd control specialists in Genshin Impact. His unparalleled grouping capabilities, consistent energy regeneration, and flexible build paths make him an exceptional choice for content featuring multiple smaller enemies. While heavier enemies that resist his crowd control have reduced his dominance in some content, his utility remains unmatched in appropriate scenarios.
With the upcoming Version 6.2 Hexerei buffs, Venti is poised to receive significant enhancements that expand his role from pure support into a viable on-field damage dealer, particularly when paired with other Hexerei characters. These updates breathe new life into the Anemo Archon and ensure his continued relevance in the evolving meta.

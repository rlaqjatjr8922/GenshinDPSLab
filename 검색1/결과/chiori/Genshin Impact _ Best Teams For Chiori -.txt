▼ Mono-Geo Team Comp
★★★★★
- Maximizes the power of Chiori with Geo Resonance and Geo Structures.
- In this team, Chiori will deal powerful damage together with Itto and further boosted by Gorou.
▼ Navia-Chiori Team Comp
★★★★・
- Navia and Chiori will deal damage against enemies.
- Chiori make it so that Crystalize is consistently produced.
- C1 is recommended when using this team comp because Navia cannot summon Geo Structures.
▼ Mono Elemental Team With Chiori
★★★・・
- Mainly uses Chiori as a support that uses Archaic Petra.
- Allows Chiori to deal damage while supporting the team.
- Decent option for Mono-Elemental Teams but makes Chiori very Niche.

Mono-Geo with Chiori focuses on dealing powerful Geo damage to enemies. Chiori and Itto will be the main damage dealers in the team and their damage will be supported by Gorou and Zhongli.
Rotation Of Mono-Geo Chiori Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start by using Hold Elemental Skill of Zhongli.
2
Switch to Gorou and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
3
Switch to Chiori and use Elemental Skill twice to switch to Itt.
4
Switch to Itto and use Elemental Burst then use Elemental Skill.
5
Use Normal and Charged Attack for Itto.
6
Switch to Chiori and finish with Elemental Burst.
7
Repeat from no.1
The goal of the rotation is to provide all the buff to Itto and Chiori before activating their skills for them to provide powerful damage. The rotation does not require specific reactions so it can be easily mixed.

Mono-Geo team comps, traditionally, is mostly composed of Geo. However damage buffers, like Furina and Bennett, can also be a good option despite being non-Geo.

This team focuses on using Chiori to produce Crystalize shards and let Navia deal powerful damage. Bennett and Xiangling will mostly deal powerful damage while spreading Pyro and increasing the damage of Chiori and Navia. C1 Chiori is recommended in this team comp.
Rotation Of Furina-Navia Team Comp
No.
Procedure
1
Use Chiori's Elemental Skill twice to switch to the next character.
2
Switch to Bennett and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
3
Switch to Xiangling and use Elemental Skill then Elemental Burst.
4
Switch to Navia and use Elemental Burst.
5
Switch to Chiori and use Elemental Burst.
6
Switch to Navia and use Elemental Skill → Normal Attacks to get more Crystals → Elemental Skill.
7
Repeat from no. 1 onwards.
The goal is to use Navia after producing enough Crystalize to maximize her damage.

Chiori's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Chiori team composition guide and 
character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we'll explore the best teams to build for Chiori, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and team compositions to help Chiori 
reach her full damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Chiori
If Chiori is not at C1+, she performs best when paired with a
Geo
Construct so
that she can get two Tamoto dolls instead of one, allowing her to deal far more
damage. This can be from characters that directly create
Geo
Constructs of their
own, or characters that enable Lunar-Crystallize, as the Moondrifts from the
reaction are considered
Geo
Constructs.
Chiori will generally be used in a team with an on-field
Geo
DPS, as these 
teams will usually have buffs for
Geo DMG
that both she and the on-field
DPS can benefit from together. If you have a C6 Chiori, you can instead use her
as the on-field DPS and shift the focus to maximizing her own damage.
Geo
Geo
DPS Options
Chiori works well with any
Geo
DPS such as
Zibai
,
Navia
and
Arataki Itto
. However, in
Zibai
's case, her
	damage is so high that you'd much rather have a buffer like
Illuga
or
Gorou
instead of Chiori's sub-DPS damage.
Columbina
Columbina
Columbina is one of the best supports to use in a team with Chiori,
	as she enables and buffs Lunar-Crystallize, while providing the necessary 
	Hydro application to trigger the reaction. Columbina also deals high amounts
	of direct Lunar-Crystallize damage of her own, allowing her to benefit from
	buffs to Geo damage alongside Chiori.
Linnea
Linnea
Like Columbina, Linnea enables and buffs Lunar-Crystallize. She provides 
	good buffing via her Geo RES Shred and acts as a powerful Geo sub-DPS
alongside Chiori. Linnea also provides healing, so she can cover the team's
sustain slot.
Xilonen
Xilonen
Similar to Linnea, Xilonen provides RES Shred and healing, while offering
	much more buffing thanks to the
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
set, but she doesn't enable Lunar-Crystallize or deal good damage of her own.
	Using Chiori and Xilonen together can make a decent team core, enabling Geo
	resonance while Xilonen provides strong buffs to both your on-field DPS and
	Chiori. However, Xilonen does not work in teams with more than two Geo units,
	which holds back the synergy, as this means she can't be used together with
	powerful characters like
Linnea
or
Gorou
.
	Xilonen's buffing will work in these teams at C2, but she still won't be 
	able to heal with her Elemental Burst.
Illuga
Illuga
Illuga, especially at C6, provides strong buffs to the whole team,
	increasing their CRIT Rate, CRIT Damage, and Elemental Mastery. Illuga is
	also a Moonsign character, so he can enable Ascendant Gleam when used with
Columbina
or
Linnea
to enable
	more buffs.
Gorou
Gorou
The Crit DMG from Gorou's C6 as well as the DEF% from his Ascension 1
passive talent are team-wide, so Chiori will benefit from them even off-field.
Since Gorou buffs Defense and wants three Geo units to maximize his buffs, he 
will do well when there is another Defense-scaling Geo unit on the team 
alongside Chiori.
Zhongli
Zhongli
Zhongli provides a Geo Construct, a powerful Shield, and RES Shred.
	Similar to Chiori, Zhongli will also benefit greatly from any other 
	Constructs on the team, as the Stone Stele from his Skill gains an 
	additional hit for each Construct. This will make him especially useful in
Lunar-Crystallize teams, since the Moondrifts from the reaction count as four
total Constructs.
Furina
Furina
Furina provides a good teamwide DMG% buff while doing respectable damage
	of her own. She requires healing to get her full value, so it's recommended
	to use her alongside either
Linnea
or
Xilonen
.
	Since Furina is Hydro, if using
Linnea
, Furina can be
	used as an alternative to Columbina if you don't have her available. She can
	also be used in a double Hydro core alongside another Hydro unit, allowing both units to
	deal more damage thanks to lower Energy requirements and Hydro Resonance.
Yelan
Xingqiu
Mona
If using
Linnea
and not using
Columbina
or
Furina
, you will need a Hydro unit in their place.
	Yelan and Xingqiu are generally the best alternatives, but if you are using
Albedo
, Mona can also work well because she is a Hexerei 
	character. If using Mona, you will want to give her a good amount of field 
	time to apply Hydro with her Normal/Charged Attacks, which will also extend 
	the duration of her Omen, allowing Chiori and Albedo to deal much more 
	damage. At C1 and C2, Mona can also provide decent buffs to Lunar-Crystallize 
	DMG% and Elemental Mastery.
Durin
Durin
Durin deals good Pyro damage from off-field while also providing RES
	Shred to both Geo and Pyro. This is enhanced further if using a second
	Hexerei character, resulting in great synergy if also using
Albedo
.

Chiori Overview
Chiori
is a
5-star Geo Sword
user who holds a Twin blade that can slide enemies dealing Geo DMG. She is the owner of Chioriya Boutique. A tailor renowned throughout Fontaine.
Best Weapons For Chiori
Here is a different rarity of Swords that are great for maximizing her Geo DMG. Since her skill is based on ATK and DEF, we prioritize these stats to rank these weapons.
Best 3-Star Sword Weapons
In the early game, it's difficult to find a 4 or 5-star weapon. Therefore, some 3-star weapons can be good for her kit.
Traveler's Handy Sword
Provide a good amount of
Base ATK (448.22)
and
DEF (29.28%)
at Lv 90 which is pretty decent for newbie players who want to maximize their DMG to deal with enemies in the early game.

4-Stars
Sword if you don't have a
5-star
Sword for her. All you do is look for DEF%
, EM, or CRIT DMG/Rate with passive that can increase her EM skill/burst DMG.
Cinnabar Spindle
This might be the best 4 stars for Chiori since it has
DEF%
as Sub stats and
EM Skill DMG
boost based on DEF which fit well with her kits and playstyle.
Wolf-Fang
Increase her
EM skill/burst
and boost
CRIT Rate
which increases her overall DPS DMG which can be stackable.
Festering Desire
Increase skill uptime as well as boost
EM Skill DMG
and
EM skill CRIT Rate
.

Best Artifacts For Chiori
Here is the best artifacts list for Chiori that can be used in Sub-DPS build.
Best DPS/Sub DPS Artifacts for Chiori
Husk of Opulent
2 Pieces:
DEF +30%
4 Pieces:
A character equipped with this artifact set will obtain the Curiosity effect in the following conditions: When on the field, the character gains 1 stack after hitting an opponent with Geo attack, triggering a maximum of once every 0.3s. When off the field, the character gains 1 stack every 3s. Curiosity can stack up to 4 times, each providing 6% DEF and a 6% Geo DMG Bonus. When 6 seconds pass without gaining a Curiosity stack, 1 stack is lost.
Archaic Petra
2 Pieces:
Gain a 15% Geo DMG Bonus
4 Pieces:
Upon obtaining a crystal created through a Geo Elemental Reaction, all party members gain 35% Elemental DMG Bonus to that particular element for 10s. Onlt once form of Elemental DMG Bonus to that particular element for 10s. Only one form of Elemental DMG Bonus can be gained in this manner at any one time.
Nighttime Whispers
2PC:
ATK +18%
4PC:
After using an Elemental Skill, gains a 20% Geo DMG Bonus for 10s. While under a shield granted by the Crystallize reaction, the above effect will be increased by 150%, and this additional increase disappears 1s after the shield is lost.
Golden Troupe
2PC:
Increases Elemental Skill DMG by 20%.
4PC:
Increases Elemental Skill DMG by 25%. Additionally, when not on the field, Elemental Skill DMG will be further increased by 25%. This effect will be cleared 2s after taking the field.

Chiori Sub DPS/DPS Build

will be the best for her since this is her signature weapon which provides everything she needs to output more DMG.
Cinnabar Spindle

Husk of Opulent Dreams

Golden Troupe
will be a great option which provides an elemental Skill DMG bonus.

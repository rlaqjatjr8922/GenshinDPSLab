Master the stylish and powerful Geo sub-DPS Chiori in Genshin Impact with this definitive guide to her best weapons, artifacts, and team compositions. Unlock her true potential by balancing her unique ATK and DEF scaling to maximize damage from her automaton doll, Tamoto.
The arrival of Chiori in Genshin Impact's Version 4.5 brought a stylish and powerful new Geo sub-DPS to the roster. She's the kind of character who makes a team look effortlessly cool while dishing out serious damage from the sidelines. Her secret weapon? Tamoto, the automaton doll created by her Elemental Skill, which sticks around to pelt enemies with Geo damage even after Chiori has left the field. Building her right is key, as she dances between scaling off both ATK and DEF—a bit of a balancing act, but oh-so-rewarding when you get it just right. Think of her as a DPS, but instead of going all-in on ATK, you're swapping that out for DEF as your main squeeze, with ATK playing a solid supporting role. It's a unique playstyle that truly lets her shine.
Best Weapons For Chiori: Finding Her Perfect Sword
Choosing a sword for Chiori is all about boosting those CRIT stats and giving her Elemental Skill a major power-up. DEF is the star of the show, but don't sweat it if your sword doesn't have it—Artifacts will handle the heavy lifting there. Her signature weapon, the 5-star
Uraku Misugiri
, is basically her soulmate in sword form. It's got everything she could ever want:
High CRIT DMG sub-stat
(88.2% at max level) for those satisfying big numbers.
Boosts Normal Attack DMG by 16% and Elemental Skill DMG by 24%
—hello, Tamoto!
A killer passive: when the active character deals Geo DMG, those bonuses double for 15 seconds. Talk about teamwork!
And as the cherry on top, it grants a
20% DEF increase
. It's the complete package.
But let's be real, snagging a limited 5-star weapon isn't always in the cards. No worries! The best alternative is actually a 4-star sword:
Cinnabar Spindle
. This old event weapon is a hidden gem (pun intended) for Chiori. Its ATK stat might be on the lower side, but it makes up for it with a massive
DEF sub-stat (69% at max level)
and a skill that increases Elemental Skill DMG based on her DEF. Since her Skill is her bread and butter, this weapon is a match made in heaven... if you were lucky enough to get it back in Version 2.3.
If you don't have either, here are some other solid picks:
5-star options:
Summit Shaper, Primordial Jade Cutter. They boost ATK and CRIT, enhancing her overall damage.
4-star options:
Wolf-Fang, Festering Desire. These are great for boosting her Elemental Skill damage even without a DEF bonus.
Building The Dream Team For Chiori
Chiori isn't a solo act; she thrives in the right company. Her main gig is dealing off-field Geo damage and resonating with other Geo characters. She's usually at her best in
mono Geo teams
, where she can really let loose. Pair her up with a strong Geo DPS like Navia, Arataki Itto, or even an underrated powerhouse like C6 Ningguang. Round out the team with supports like Gorou or Yun Jin to buff the whole squad, and a reliable shielder like Zhongli or Noelle to keep everyone safe. That's the classic recipe for success.
But teams don't have to be one-note! You can mix in other elements for some fun reactions. If you're running a more varied team and using Chiori more for support (though it's not her strongest role), the Archaic Petra artifact set can help boost your other characters' damage through Crystallize shards. For F2P players, a team of Ningguang (DPS), Chiori (sub-DPS), Gorou (support), and a flexible fourth slot (like a healer) can work wonders without breaking the bank.
Artifacts, Stats & Skill Priority: Fine-Tuning Her Build
When it comes to Artifacts, the
Husk of Opulent Dreams
set is her best-in-slot, no question. The 2-piece bonus gives a sweet
30% DEF increase
, and the 4-piece Curiosity effect is perfect for her playstyle. She gains stacks both on-field and off-field, with each stack boosting her DEF and Geo DMG by 6%. This means her doll, Tamoto, is getting stronger even while she's chilling on the bench. It's the set that fully unlocks her potential as a persistent off-field threat.
As a strong alternative, the
Golden Troupe
set is all about amplifying her Elemental Skill damage. It's a great choice if you want to maximize Tamoto's punches, though it doesn't do much for her Burst. For stat priorities on your Artifacts, focus on this order:
DEF%
(Sands of Eon)
Geo DMG Bonus
(Goblet of Eonothem) - though a DEF% Goblet can also work!
CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG
(Circlet of Logos)
Always look for
DEF%, CRIT Rate, and CRIT DMG
in the sub-stats. ATK% is still useful, but it takes a backseat to DEF and CRIT.
When leveling her Talents, resources might be tight, so here's the priority:
Top Priority: Elemental Skill.
This is her core damage ability. Level it up first!
Second Priority: Elemental Burst.
It packs a punch when she jumps in.
Last Priority: Normal Attack.
It's nice to have, but she spends most of her time off-field.
Building Chiori might seem tricky with her dual scaling, but once you know the targets—DEF, CRIT, and that glorious Elemental Skill—it all falls into place. She's a character that rewards thoughtful building with consistent, powerful performance, making any team she's on just that much more formidable.

Gameplay
Wriothesley
’s gameplay revolves around dealing consistent on-field damage through his
Enhanced Normal Attacks
and sustained Cryo application. His kit focuses on maintaining his enhanced state from
Icefang Rush
, making him a strong and reliable
main DPS
in teams built around
Freeze
and
Melt
reactions.
His Elemental Skill,
Icefang Rush
, allows Wriothesley to enter an enhanced state that increases his
Normal Attack damage
while consuming HP when attacking. This state is the core of his kit, enabling him to deal continuous Cryo damage and maintain strong pressure against enemies.
When Wriothesley uses his Elemental Burst,
Darkgold Wolfbite
, he unleashes a powerful AoE Cryo attack that deals solid damage. While not his primary damage source, it adds extra burst and helps smooth his overall DPS during rotations.
Wriothesley’s passive talents further enhance his performance by rewarding proper HP management, allowing him to recover HP through his
Charged Attack
under certain conditions. With the right balance of
CRIT stats
and
ATK
, he can consistently deliver high and reliable damage throughout combat.

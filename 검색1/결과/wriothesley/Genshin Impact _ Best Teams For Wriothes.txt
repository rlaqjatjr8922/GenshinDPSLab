Best Teams for Wriothesley
Wriothesley Is A Great Cryo DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Melt Team
- Mavuika applies Pyro for Wriothesley to trigger Melt on.
- Mavuika is able to reach high Fighting Spirit count with Xilonen and buff Wriothesley's damage.
- Xilonen & Bennett buffs can also increase the damage dealt and provide healing.
2nd
▼ Burn-Melt Team
- Burning reaction can help maintain the Pyro aura for Wriothesley to trigger Melt on.
- Emilie is a good Burning sub-DPS that can deal powerful DMG.
- Pyro Traveler or Mavuika can be used for Pyro application and hold Scroll set to buff Dendro/Cryo/Pyro DMG.
3rd
▼ Freeze Team
- Frozen reaction is a good defensive option for Wriothesley.
- Cryo resonance can be activated with Citlali, who can buff the Hydro sub-DPS for additional DMG.
- This team is weak against enemies that cannot be Frozen like bosses and Hydro Phantasms.
4th
▼ Mono Cryo
- This team focuses on buffing Wriothesley's own damage without reactions.
- Shenhe and Xilonen can buff Cryo DMG greatly.
- Bennett buffs Wriothesley's ATK stat.
Wriothesley is a Melt/Freeze DPS that deals damage using his Cryo-infused Normal & Charged Attacks. He has self HP drain which makes Marechaussee set his BiS but it also means he needs a sustain support even though he has self healing as well.

Rotation Of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Wriothesley and use his Normal Attack to apply Cryo
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then use Normal Attack twice
3
Get the Cryo Elemental Shard before using Xilonen's Burst
4
Switch to Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
5
Switch to Mavuika and use her Burst then Skill
6
Switch to Wriothesley and use his Skill
7
Deal damage using Normal Attacks then Charged Attacks once he gains Gracious Rebuke
8
Use Wriothesley's Burst to end his turn
9
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

Rotation Of Burn-Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Wriothesley and use Normal Attack once to apply Cryo
2
Switch to Pyro Traveler and use their Skill then Burst to gain Nightsoul points

8
Repeat from 1
It may be tricky to refresh Scroll set buff for Cryo DMG since Burning reaction happens. You can refresh Emilie's off-field DMG with her Skill if her Burst is not available in the next rotation.

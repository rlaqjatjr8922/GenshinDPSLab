Thoma Gameplay
Thoma’s gameplay revolves around providing reliable shielding while applying Pyro from off-field to support reaction-based teams. His kit focuses on maintaining defensive utility while enabling consistent elemental reactions through his
Elemental Burst
. While he is not designed to be a primary damage dealer, his ability to protect the team and trigger reactions makes him a valuable support option in many compositions.
His
Elemental Skill
, Blazing Blessing, deals Pyro damage to nearby enemies while generating a protective shield called the Blazing Barrier. This shield scales based on Thoma’s
Max HP
and provides solid protection for the active character. The Skill also generates Pyro particles, helping him recharge his
Elemental Burst
more consistently during combat.
When Thoma uses his
Elemental Burst
, Crimson Ooyoroi, he creates a fiery armor that continuously generates Fiery Collapse attacks whenever the active character performs Normal Attacks. These attacks deal Pyro damage and refresh the Blazing Barrier, allowing Thoma to maintain layered shields while applying Pyro from off-field. This mechanic makes him especially useful in reaction-based teams that rely on frequent Normal Attacks.
Thoma’s passive talents
further enhance his defensive role by increasing shield strength and improving the durability of his Blazing Barrier. With proper energy management and HP investment, he can provide consistent protection while enabling powerful elemental reactions for the rest of the team.
Thoma Talent Priority
Thoma’s talent priority focuses primarily on his Elemental Burst and Elemental Skill, since both abilities are responsible for maintaining his shields and applying Pyro from off-field. These talents directly impact his defensive value and overall team utility.

Elemental Burst – Crimson Ooyoroi
This is the core of Thoma’s support role. His Burst creates a fiery armor that triggers Fiery Collapse attacks whenever the active character performs Normal Attacks. These attacks deal Pyro damage and refresh the Blazing Barrier shield, allowing Thoma to maintain continuous protection while applying Pyro from off-field. Because this ability defines his entire playstyle, it should be prioritized first.
Elemental Skill – Blazing Blessing
Thoma’s Elemental Skill deals Pyro damage and generates a Blazing Barrier shield that scales with his Max HP. It also produces Pyro particles that help recharge his Burst. While the shield from the Skill alone is not extremely strong, it plays an important role in building and refreshing the layered shields created by his Burst, making it the second priority.
Normal Attack – Swiftshatter Spear
Thoma’s Normal Attacks are rarely used in his typical support role, as he generally stays off-field after activating his abilities. Because of this, leveling his Normal Attack talent provides very little value and should be considered the lowest priority.

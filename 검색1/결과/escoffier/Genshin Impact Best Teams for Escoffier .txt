Escoffier Works Best As Freeze Team Support
Team
Rating
Description
▼ Cryo or Hydro DPS Support Escoffier Team
★★★★★
- Escoffier lowers or shreds the Hydro and Cryo
Resistance
of enemies hit by her Elemental Skill. In a full Cryo and Hydro-only team, this RES shred amounts to a noticeable boost to the team's total DPS.
- Escoffier's strongest team as of the current version is in a Freeze with
Skirk
, a Cryo DPS.
- Some Cryo or Hydro DPSes can opt into having other elemental supports on the team, but the damage potential is expectedly lower.
▼ Anemo or Pyro DPS Support Escoffier Team
★★★・・
- Apart from her RES shred, Escoffier's personal DPS and Cryo application can be employed by Anemo or Pyro DPSes in their respective teams.
- While you lose a significant amount of RES shred in these setups, just having Escoffier's damage or application is enough to allow these teams to deal viable DPS.
Escoffier is a Freeze support/sub-DPS similar to Chevreuse.
Her debuff on enemies is stronger the more Cryo/Hydro characters are in the team
. She can be built as a healer and be used outside Freeze.

As previously stated, Escoffier's RES Shred is fully maximized in Cryo and Hydro-only teams. An ATK% goblet on Escoffier can be considered if she's on a team with Furina, since this can boost her healing, but you'll generally want to use a Cryo DMG Bonus for her damage.
Rotation Of Cryo or Hydro DPS Support Escoffier Team (Skirk)
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill, then Burst. You can dash after her Skill to cancel the long animation.
2
Switch to Escoffier. Deploy her Skill, followed by her Burst.
3
Use Shenhe's Skill (hold), then her Burst.
4
Deploy Skirk as the on-field character. Do a few Normal Attacks before using her Skill (tap).
5
Use Skirk's Elemental Burst to buff her Normal Attacks. This will also absorb nearby Void Rifts.
6
Deal damage with Normal Attacks, and after a few Void Rifts spawn, use her Charged Attack to absorb them.
└ Optimal combo demo can be found
here
.
7
Repeat from Step 1.

Although she isn't being used to her full potential, Escoffier can still be flexed into teams that aren't purely Freeze teams by virtue of her element type. Even the low RES Shred amount is usable due to it being a fairly decent damage boost, regardless.
Rotation Of Anemo or Pyro DPS Support Escoffier Team (Varka)
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Escoffier. Use her Elemental Skill (tap).
2
Use Shenhe's Skill (hold) into Burst combo.
3
Switch to Venti. Use his Skill (Tap), then Elemental Burst. End his combo with Normal Attack (x1).
4
Swap to Varka. Use his Skill. You can then spam Normal Attack (x2) or use the more complex
combo
.
5
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
If you want more Cryo application, you can use Kazuha's Burst to Swirl Cryo, but Escoffier has to go first since Hydro takes priority over Cryo in Swirl.

Skirk & Escoffier Overview
First things first, let's take a quick profile overview of Shirk and Escoffier.
Who Is Shirk?
Skirk is a mysterious swordswoman hailing from the sea of stars. She was Tartaglia's master during her time in the Abyss.
In terms of gameplay, Skirk is a 5-star Cryo Sword character and an on-field Main DPS meant to be used in Freeze teams. Her Elemental Skill allows her to enter Seven-Phase Flash mode for damage when tapped, while holding it makes her move faster in the field. Her Elemental Burst does not rely on Energy but on Serpent's Subtlety that she builds up from her Skill.
What makes Skirk unique is her passive talent system. When nearby Hydro party members deal Hydro DMG or nearby Cryo party members deal Cryo DMG, Skirk gains the "Death's Crossing" effect, which can stack up to 3 times. Each stack significantly increases both her Normal Attack DMG in Seven-Phase Flash mode and her Elemental Burst damage. Simply put, the more Cryo and Hydro allies you run alongside her, the deadlier she becomes.

Who Is Escoffier?
Escoffier is a 5-star Cryo Polearm character from Fontaine who was introduced in
Genshin Impact Version 5.6
. She does not take the center stage as the main DPS. Instead, she shines as a dedicated Cryo support and sub-DPS. She enables Cryo elemental reactions and increases team damage through wide-area Cryo attacks and a summonable Cooking Mek that keeps applying Cryo. This makes it easier to trigger consistent elemental reactions.
Her most impactful tool is her passive talent, which reduces enemy Cryo resistance (up to 55%). However, it requires a full team of Cryo and Hydro allies to be most effective. Her Elemental Burst delivers a powerful blast of Cryo damage to surrounding enemies and also heals the entire party based on her ATK stat. All this gives her a dual role as both a damage amplifier and a sustain option.

How Skirk & Escoffier Synergize in Freeze Teams?
The Skirk and Escoffier duo has become a strong pairing in
Version 6.4 (Luna 5)
because their kits complement each other perfectly. Skirk's passive abilities prefer Cryo reactions, as her passive directly prefers Hydro and Cryo party members to activate its bonuses. This makes Escoffier the best-in-slot support for her.
Some of the other reasons why Skirk and Escoffier synergy works well are:
Escoffier shreds Cryo resistance by up to 55%, which directly multiplies all of Skirk's Cryo damage output.
Escoffier's consistent off-field Cryo application keeps enemies Frozen, which feeds Skirk's passive stacks and maintains her damage bonuses.
Skirk's teams with Escoffier are among the strongest Cryo compositions available.
Simply put, Skirk provides the raw damage, Escoffier amplifies it. Run them together, and you get the most complete Freeze team cores in the current meta.

Passive Talent (Core Mechanic)
Skirk's gameplay centers around Seven-Phase Flash mode, which unlocks through her passive talent. This mode powers most of her damage, so prioritize unlocking all passive talents early.
Elemental Skill – Havoc: Warp (Top Priority)
This skill activates Skirk's main combat stance and builds Serpent's Guile stacks.
Tap:
Triggers Seven-Phase Flash and generates stacks.
Hold:
Grants better mobility and interruption resistance.
Level this first, as it increases her damage to a great extent.
Elemental Burst – Havoc: Ruin
Her Burst consumes Serpent's Subtlety stacks instead of Energy. The damage scales based on the stacks used. In Seven-Phase Flash mode, it upgrades to Havoc: Extinction for more serious damage. Level this after her Skill.
Normal Attack – Havoc: Sunder
Normal Attacks contribute less to her overall damage compared to her Skill and Burst. Upgrade this last if you have extra resources.

Escoffier Build Guide in Genshin Impact 6.4
With Skirk's build covered, it's time to turn our attention to her best-in-slot support, Escoffier.
Escoffier Skill Leveling Priority
The best approach to get the most value from
Escoffier's guide in Genshin Impact 6.4
is to focus on upgrading her Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst. These abilities define her support capabilities.
Escoffier Talent Priority
Unlock all Passive Talents → Elemental Skill → Elemental Burst → Normal Attack
Her Passive Talents enhance her overall support capabilities, so unlocking them early strengthens her team's utility.
Her Elemental Skill should also be prioritized since it improves her Cryo damage and strengthens her off-field contribution. After that, leveling her Elemental Burst increases its damage and the amount of healing it provides to the team.
Since Escoffier is not much used for basic attacks, Normal Attacks can usually be left unupgraded.

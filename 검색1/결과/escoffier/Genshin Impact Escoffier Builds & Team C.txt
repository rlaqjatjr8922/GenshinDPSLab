Escoffier Gameplay
Escoffier
is a
Cryo Polearm
user. Her Elemental Skill allows her to perform off-field coordinated attacks. Her Elemental Burst can restores HP to all nearby party members based on her ATK. Most notably, her passive talent makes her a crucial support in the meta: when the party has 1/2/3/4 Hydro or Cryo characters in the party, Escoffier will decrease the Hydro RES and Cryo RES of any opponents hit by her Elemental Skill or her Elemental Burst by 5%/10%/15%/55%.

Escoffier Best Team Comps
Escoffier’s talent can reduce enemies’ Hydro and Cryo RES based on the number of Hydro and Cryo characters in the party, making her an excellent fit for both Hydro- and Cryo-centric teams. She synergizes especially well with Cryo teams, as her first constellation (C1) further boosts the entire team's Cryo DMG.

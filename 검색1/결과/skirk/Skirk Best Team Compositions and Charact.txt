Best Pairings and Synergies With Skirk
Skirk is primarily played in teams focused on
Cryo
and
Hydro
.
This is because her passive,
Return to Oblivion
, strongly rewards running Hydro/Cryo teammates and
can result in notable damage loss when adding non-Hydro/Cryo characters. This team restriction is very similar to
Escoffier
’s passive requirements, which is why these two characters naturally pair extremely well.
Escoffier
is the single best support you can get for Skirk when aiming to maximize her damage.
In full Hydro/Cryo teams, Escoffier provides powerful resistance reduction for both Hydro and Cryo, increasing the entire
team’s damage significantly. She also synergizes very well with units like
Furina
thanks to her healing,
which helps maintain buffs and keeps the team stable during longer fights.
Escoffier
Escoffier
Escoffier is currently Skirk’s best-in-slot support and one of the strongest Freeze enablers in the game.
      She provides consistent off-field application, healing, and powerful resistance reduction in Hydro/Cryo teams,
      making her the perfect partner for Skirk’s passive requirements and overall damage output.
Furina
Furina
Furina is one of Skirk’s 2nd best
Hydro
partners after Escoffier, enabling
      consistent HP fluctuation for Marechaussee Hunter setups while also providing
      strong teamwide buffs. She pairs especially well with Freeze cores when the
      team has enough healing to maintain stability.
Yelan
Shenhe
Citlali
Skirk 4th Slot Options
Yelan
is one of Skirk’s best premium Hydro partners,
      providing consistent off-field
Hydro
application and
      very high personal damage to push overall team DPS higher.
Shenhe
is a premium
Cryo
buffer that
      can significantly increase Skirk’s damage through Cryo amplification tools
      such as Icy Quill and Cryo RES Shred. She is strongest in higher investment
      Freeze teams focused on maximizing Skirk’s personal damage.
Citlali
is another excellent option, as she is one of the
      few characters that can hold the Cinder City artifact set while also using
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
to provide both a large damage bonus
      and an ATK buff for Skirk. On top of this, she also provides a strong shield,
      improving team survivability.

Skirk Gameplay
Skirk
is a
5-star Cryo Sword
character. Like Mavuika, her Elemental Burst does not rely on Elemental Energy, but instead consumes a special resource known as
Serpent's Subtlety
. Skirk has two main ways to deal damage.
The first focuses on her Normal Attacks. By tapping her Elemental Skill, Skirk enters
Seven-Phase Flash
mode, allowing her to consume Serpent's Subtlety to perform enhanced Normal Attacks.
The second approach emphasizes burst damage. Skirk can consume all available Serpent's Subtlety to unleash her Elemental Burst —
Havoc: Ruin
. The damage of this ability scales with the amount of Serpent's Subtlety consumed.

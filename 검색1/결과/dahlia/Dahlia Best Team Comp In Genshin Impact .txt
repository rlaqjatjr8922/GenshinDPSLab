Dahlia is a playable 4-star Hydro unit in
Genshin Impact
. As a Support, players must pair Dahlia with characters that rely on their Normal ATK to deal DMG. This list ranks the
best teams for Dahlia in
Genshin Impact
.

3
Dahlia + Escoffier + Furina + Skirk
Since Dahlia increases the active character’s Normal ATK SPD in
Genshin Impact
, it only makes sense to pair her with strong Normal ATK DPS like
Skirk
. In this Dahlia team,
Escoffier
and
Furina
work together
to buff Skirk and debuff enemies’ resistance to Cryo, while Skirk enjoys all these buffs, including Dahlia’s Normal ATK SPD boost.
This team’s synergy extends beyond just buffing Skirk. For instance, Escoffier’s healing can fulfill Furina’s Elemental Burst requirement, which allows her to buff the team’s DMG notably. Other than the healing the team can provide, Dahlia can also shield his allies and further improve the team’s survivability.
Team Roles
Dahlia:
Support, Shielder, Buffer
Escoffier:
Support, Sub-DPS, Healer, Cryo Enabler, Debuffer
Furina:
Support, Sub-DPS, Healer, Hydro Enabler, Buffer
Skirk:
Main DPS, Cryo Enabler
2
Dahlia + Xingqiu + Fischl + Wanderer
Since
Wanderer
operates as a useful driver for the team, players can team him up with Dahlia to further increase his Normal ATK SPD. Wanderer already gets Normal ATK SPD from his artifact set and signature Catalyst. So, adding an extra source of Normal ATK SPD will always further help Wanderer drive more Elements during combat.
When it comes to the best off-field Sub-DPS characters that can help Wanderer drive their Elements, C6
Xingqiu
and
C6
Fischl
are one of the best choices
out there. Simply because, unlike other Hydro characters, Xingqiu coordinates his off-field Hydro DMG with Wanderer’s Normal Attacks while sufficiently handling his own Energy and getting his Elemental Burst back on every rotation. Fischl, on the other hand, can easily provide Electro application on every rotation with a strong DPS and Electro-charged reactions.

Dahlia + Mika + Yun Jin + Razor
This is more of a fun team to maximize
Razor
’s Normal ATK SPD by teaming him with Dahlia in
Genshin Impact
. In this setup, players can see Razor go berserk after casting his Elemental Burst. Every character in this team has a
Normal ATK SPD buff for Razor
, which greatly increases the speed of his hits. A character like
Mika
provides Razor with Physical DMG buffs while granting him around 25% extra Normal ATK SPD. On the other hand,
Yun Jin
increases Razor’s Normal ATK DMG, which is where most of his DMG comes from. She also increases his Normal ATK SPD by 12% on her sixth Constellation.
When it comes to Razor and Dahlia team in
Genshin Impact
, Razor gets around 40% extra Normal ATK SPD when casting his Elemental Burst. Dahlia protects Razor with a shield and adds another 20% extra Normal ATK SPD to Razor. All those speedy buffs increase Razor’s Normal ATK SPD to a total of almost 100%, which is a crazy buff to
a DPS in
Genshin Impact
.

Dahlia:
Support, Shielder, Buffer
Mika:
Support, Healer, Cryo Enabler, Buffer
Yun Jin:
Support, Buffer

Main DPS

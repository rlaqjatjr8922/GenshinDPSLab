Elemental Skill
Immersive Ordinance
Time for a fun little trick! Summons the Sacramental Shower in different ways depending on whether you Tap or Hold it.
Tap
After a short prayer, summons the Sacramental Shower in front.
Hold
Engages in continuous prayer, and when done, summons forth a Sacramental Shower in the designated position.
Sacramental Shower
When opponents or nearby party members touch the Sacramental Shower, different effects will occur depending on the one who touches it:
If an opponent touches the Shower, it will explode and deal
AoE Hydro DMG
to nearby opponents.
If a nearby party member touches it, it will bounce the one who touches it high in the air.
Only 1 Sacramental Shower summoned by Dahlia himself can be on the field at once.
Elemental Burst
Radiant Psalter
Appeals to the divine with earnestness and song, dealing
AoE Hydro DMG
to nearby opponents and granting his party members Favonian Favor, summoning a Shield of Sacred Favor.
The Shield of Sacred Favor's DMG absorption is based on Dahlia's Max HP, and it will absorb
Hydro DMG
with 250% effectiveness.
Favonian Favor
While this effect is active, 1 Benison stack may be consumed to summon a new Shield of Sacred Favor when an existing Shield is broken via DMG.
Dahlia can obtain Benison via the following methods:
· He will gain 1 Benison stack when his own active party member hits opponents 4 times with Normal Attacks while affected by Favonian Favor.
· After triggering the effect of his Passive Talent,
"The Wind's Gentle Grace"
(You must first unlock this Passive Talent.)
Dahlia can gain a maximum of 4 Benison stacks per Favonian Favor duration.

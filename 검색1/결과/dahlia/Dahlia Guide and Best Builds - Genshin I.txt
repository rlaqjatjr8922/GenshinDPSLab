1.
Dahlia Build Guide, Best Artifacts, Weapons and Talent Priority
Dahlia is best built as a support unit, providing shielding to the party using 
his Elemental Burst. Dahlia also provides an ATK SPD buff to the party.

Dahlia's best weapon is generally
Favonius Sword
as building this 
weapon on Dahlia allows you to further support your teams ER requirements. 
If this is not needed,
Serenity's Call
both has high ER for Dahlia's personal 
requirements but also provides a 32-64% Max HP increase to Dahlia (32 without Moonsign, 
64 with Moonsign) which is very significant.
Build Dahlia with
Noblesse Oblige
or 2-Piece HP if you need further 
HP buffs. Otherwise build Dahlia with Scroll but be mindful of the fact that 
they need to trigger the correct reactions to provide the right buffs.
Dahlia's Elemental Burst is by far the most important talent to level as 
this is what generates shields. Dahlia's Elemental Skill can also be leveled to 
increase their personal damage but is not required at all.

Recommended Endgame Build Stats for Dahlia
Build recommendations assume a "decently built" level of artifact rolls alongside 
Dahlia's best weapon. Using other weapons may increase or decrease 
certain stats relative to these recommendations.
HP:
40,000+
ATK:
1000+
ER:
200%+
Dahlia should primarily be built with HP % aiming for at least 40,000 to cap 
out their passive
Prayer of Well-Wrought Joy
however further HP 
will continue to increase Dahlia's shield strength.

from the ATK SPD buff.

Dahlia Ascension Passives and Materials
Ascension Passive
Explanation
The Wind's Gentle Grace
When characters affected by the Favonian Favor effect of the Elemental Burst
Radiant Psalter
trigger Frozen reactions on opponents, they will grant Dahlia 2 Benison stacks. This effect can trigger once every 8s.
Prayer of Well-Wrought Joy
When your active party member is affected by the Favonian Favor effect of the Elemental Burst "
Radiant Psalter
," their ATK SPD will increase based on Dahlia's Max HP: Every 1,000 Max HP will cause an increase of 0.5%, up to a maximum of 20%.
Pilgrimage Upon Returning Winds
During the day (06:00 – 18:00), your party members gain the Swift Stride effect: Movement SPD increased by 10%.
Does not take effect in Domains, Trounce Domains, or Spiral Abyss. Swift Stride does not stack.

5.3.
Dahlias Talents
Type
Talent Name
Brief Explanation
Normal
Favonius Bladework - Ritual
Normal Attack
Performs up to 4 rapid strikes.
Charged Attack
Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to unleash 2 rapid sword strikes.
Plunging Attack
Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
Elemental Skill
Immersive Ordinance
Time for a fun little trick! Summons the Sacramental Shower in different ways depending on whether you Tap or Hold it.
Tap
After a short prayer, summons the Sacramental Shower in front.
Hold
Engages in continuous prayer, and when done, summons forth a Sacramental Shower in the designated position.
Sacramental Shower
When opponents or nearby party members touch the Sacramental Shower, different effects will occur depending on the one who touches it:
If an opponent touches the Shower, it will explode and deal AoE Hydro DMG to nearby opponents.
If a nearby party member touches it, it will bounce the one who touches it high in the air.
Only 1 Sacramental Shower summoned by Dahlia himself can be on the field at once.
Elemental Burst
Radiant Psalter
Appeals to the divine with earnestness and song, dealing AoE Hydro DMG to nearby opponents and granting his party members Favonian Favor, summoning a Shield of Sacred Favor.
The Shield of Sacred Favor's DMG absorption is based on Dahlia's Max HP, and it will absorb Hydro DMG with 250% effectiveness.
Favonian Favor
While this effect is active, 1 Benison stack may be consumed to summon a new Shield of Sacred Favor when an existing Shield is broken via DMG.
Dahlia can obtain Benison via the following methods:
He will gain 1 Benison stack when his own active party member hits opponents 4 times with Normal Attacks while affected by Favonian Favor.
After triggering the effect of his Passive Talent, "The Wind's Gentle Grace" (You must first unlock this Passive Talent.)
Dahlia can gain a maximum of 4 Benison stacks per Favonian Favor duration.

5.5.
Dahlias Constellations
Dahlias best constellations are
Revelation of Mercy
(C2) and
You Shall Go Out With Joy
(C6). Dahlias C2 provides 
more shielding while their C6 provides more ATK SPD buffs as well as the ability to 
revive characters every 15 minutes.
#
Name
Explanation

Infallible Procession

Revelation of Mercy
After Dahlia consumes his Elemental Burst Radiant Psalter's Benison stacks to summon a Shield of Sacred Favor, the character protected by said Shield will gain 25% increased Shield Strength for 12s.

This effect can trigger once every 15 minutes.

HSR Dahlia Best Team Comps In HSR
Dahlia can only operate in Break teams due to her insane buffs and debuffs to the Break gameplay. Her best teams should always include characters who deal high Toughness Break DMG, such as Firefly, Rappa, and Lingsha. Here are some team comps for Dahlia in
HSR
.
Mono-Fire Dahlia team:
Dahlia + Firefly + Fugue + Lingsha
Dahlia non-Fire team:
Dahlia + Rappa + Harmony MC + Lingsha
Dahlia's F2P team:
Dahlia + Himeko + Harmony MC + Gallagher
Dahlia + Firefly + Fugue + Lingsha
Although Dahlia adds an entirely new mechanic to
Break teams in
Honkai Star Rail
, she's mainly here to bring back Firefly into the meta. This team is a full Fire team and can take on any content in the game because of Dahlia's ability to put Fire weakness on enemies. She also allows the team to proc Super Break DMG without breaking enemies' Toughness, which will make the Break playstyle work like the Critical one.
Still, Firefly will get the added DMG when she breaks enemies' Toughness, on top of the extra DMG she's getting from the Super Break DMG. That's why
characters like Fugue are still essential for her team
, as she gives enemies a second Toughness bar for Firefly to enjoy breaking again. This team not only brings back Firefly and Fugue to the meta, but it also gives Lingsha a new life to showcase her consistent healing and huge Break Effect buff. As a bonus, Lingsha's frequent healing can constantly refresh Dahlia's second Ascension passive.
Dahlia + Rappa + Harmony MC + Lingsha
Dahlia can also work with Break characters like Rappa and
Harmony MC in
HSR
. Rappa needs the Harmony MC as much as she needs Dahlia, because the MC gives her a large amount of Break Effect. Both Rappa and Harmony MC are Imaginary, which helps the team deal a lot of Toughness DMG when Dahlia puts the Imaginary weakness on enemies. Additionally, since Dahlia is going to always put Fire weakness on enemies, Lingsha will always shine with her skills and follow-up attacks.
Dahlia + Himeko + Harmony MC + Gallagher
Players can also build a Dahlia F2P team in
Honkai Star Rail
. Pairing Dahlia and Harmony MC is already a great combo for any Break team. So, building Himeko on Break DMG and putting her in this Dahlia F2P team will help her perform better than her standard build. This showcases how powerful Dahlia is as a support in
Honkai Star Rail
, as she can take a character like Himeko, who doesn't have any Break gameplay, and turn them into a
powerful Break DPS in
HSR
.
Since Himeko is an
Erudition character in
Honkai Star Rail
,
this F2P team will mostly perform well in AoE content like Pure Fiction
. Dahlia and Himeko's constant AoE attacks will clear wave after wave before reaching the final boss.
Of course, the best 4-star healer for this team would be Gallagher, who not only has AoE Ultimate and good SP generation, but is also relevant for the Break team.

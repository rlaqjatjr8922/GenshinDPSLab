Frozen Wilds
Aloy throws a Freeze Bomb in the targeted direction that explodes on impact, dealing
Cryo DMG

Aloy can gain up to 1 Coil stack every 0.1s.
Coil

·When Aloy has 4 Coil stacks, all stacks of Coil are cleared. She then enters the Rushing Ice state, which further increases the DMG dealt by her Normal Attacks and converts her Normal Attack DMG to
Cryo DMG
.
While in the Rushing Ice state, Aloy cannot obtain new Coil stacks.
Coil effects will be cleared 30s after Aloy leaves combat.

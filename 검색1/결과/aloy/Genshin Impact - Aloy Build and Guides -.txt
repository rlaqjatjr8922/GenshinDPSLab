Aloy's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Great Freeze/Melt potential for teams.
Both buff your teams ATK and lowers the enemies ATK.
Low Energy Costs for Aloy's Elemental Burst.
Able to act as a strong Cryo Battery for teams.
Weaknesses
No constellations.
Coil Stacks depend on her Elemental Skill's bombs hitting enemies.
Unobtainable in-game if you were not here for the collaboration.

3.
Aloy Combos and How to Play
Aloy's playstyle centers around her Elemental Burst,
Prophecies of Dawn
. This burst is a large
Cryo
AoE and can be used as a trigger for powerful
Elemental Reactions
.
This Elemental Burst recharges quite quickly with a low 40 energy cost, allowing
you to setup other Elements while Aloy is off-field then bringing her back
on-field to use her Burst and Skill again. Due to this Aloy is typically played in quick
swap teams.
Aloy also possesses a unique stacking mechanic called 'Coils'. These are
generated from her Elemental Skill,
Frozen Wilds
, which spawns
small Freeze Bombs on the battlefield. When an enemy triggers one of
these, Aloy will gain a single Coil Stack. Once you get 4 stacks, Aloy will enter her
Rushing Ice state which infuses her normal attack with
Cryo
.
A normal rotation with Aloy will be:
Prophecies of Dawn
Frozen Wilds
Swap Aloy out
Use your other character's abilities
Swap back on Aloy for her Burst and repeat
Anemo
characters such as
Kaedehara Kazuha
,
Sucrose
, or
Jean
can group Aloy's Bombs the same way as they group enemies, which allows you to trigger the
bombs more often.
4.
Aloy's Best Team Compositions
Aloy's role is a Burst-DPS, which means she is used to quickly swap in, use her Elemental Burst and skill,
and swap out again. This will let Aloy trigger
Freeze
and
Melt
reactions, or serve as a battery for other
Cryo
characters.
However, while Aloy can be an option, know that she is rarely the most optimal one.

Best Teams for Aloy
Aloy Works Best As Cryo DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Melt Team
- Citlali can activate Cryo resonance and increase the team's CRIT Rate against Cryo-affected enemies.
- Xiangling and Aloy can deal powerful Melt DMG with Bennett's Burst buff.
2nd
▼ Vapemelt Team
- Aloy is used as quickswap DPS in this team.
- Citlali and Mavuika combo is great for this team since Mavuika can deal both on-field and off-field Pyro DMG.
- Yelan is the Hydro sub-DPS that can apply Hydro consistently and buff the active character.
Aloy is a collab-limited Cryo character that can deal powerful DMG with her Burst. She can use her Skill to gain Cryo infusion in her Normal Attacks, allowing for another source of Cryo application.

Rotation Of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett and use his Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst to gain Nightsoul points
3
Switch to Xiangling and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Aloy and use her Burst then Skill
5
Apply Cryo on enemies using her Normal Attacks.
6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire.

Rotation Of Vapemelt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mavuika and tap her Skill
2
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst to gain Nightsoul points
3
Switch to Yelan and use her Skill then Burst
4
Switch to Aloy and use her Burst then Skill
5
Use Aloy's Normal Attack to trigger Frozen on the enemies
6
Switch back to Mavuika and use her Burst then tap her Skill to fully charge her Nightsoul bar
7
Use Normal Attacks to deal damage and trigger Yelan's follow-up attack
8
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire

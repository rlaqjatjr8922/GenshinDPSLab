Citlali is a decent Support in
Genshin Impact
. Unlike other Cryo units in the game, Citlali directly shred enemies’ resistance to Hydro and Pyro, which is not her Elements. This makes her a great option to include in Freeze and
Melt teams in
Genshin Impact
.
Just like any other character in the game,
building Citlali with the best build
allows her to shine as a Support instead of being a mediocre one. While her kit has Cryo applications and debuffs, she still can offer some buffs that greatly help her team in
Genshin Impact
.

Citlali Best Artifact In Genshin Impact
Citlali is a reliable
shielder in
Genshin Impact
. For that, giving her as much Elemental Mastery as possible is the best way to build Citlali in
Genshin Impact
. Starting from her artifact sets, build Citlali with
4pc of Scroll of the Hero of Cinder
City to take advantage of her Nightsoul’s Blessing state. It will grant an enormous amount of DMG to the allies that trigger an Elemental reaction with Citlali. You can also build Citlali with
4pc of Noblesse Oblige
since it’s a universal set for supports and it’s easy for Citlali to cast her Elemental Burst in
Genshin Impact
.
If you want to maximize Citlali’s Elemental Mastery, go for
2pc sets that offer 80 Elemental Mastery
, like
Gilded Dreams
,
Flower of Paradise Lost
, and
Wanderer's Troupe
. You can even build Citlali with
4pc of Gilded Dreams
if her team didn’t have any
Cryo users in
Genshin Impact
. That will increase her Elemental Mastery by an extra 150 every time her team triggers a reaction.
For stats, focus on building Citlali’s Energy Recharge to make sure she can cast her Elemental Burst on every rotation. After that, add as much Elemental Mastery as possible to strengthen her shield and DMG.
Citlali Best Weapon In Genshin Impact
To maximize Citlali’s Elemental Mastery, build Citlali with
Starcaller's Watch
in
Genshin Impact
. Her best-in-slot Catalyst will grant her the ability to continuously buff the active character’s DMG by 28%. This further adds to Citlali’s Supportive role, ranking her as one of the best
Supports in
Genshin Impact
.
For other options, give Citlali Catalysts that give her Elemental Mastery like
A Thousand Floating Dreams
and
Wandering Evenstar
. You can also build Citlali with
Sacrificial Fragments
, which is a great option. The 4-star weapon has a chance to reset Citlali’s cooldown in order to cast her Elemental Skill again. That gives her better shielding, more off-field Cryo application, and more energy to cast her Elemental Burst.
Finally, you can always go with F2P options like Fruit of Fulfillment, Favonius Codex, and Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers. They’re
decent Catalysts that can offer something
for Citlali’s kit, especially Thrilling Tales with its enormous ATK% buff.

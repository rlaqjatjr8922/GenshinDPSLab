Best Teams for Mavuika
Mavuika is Best Used As DPS With Natlan Supports
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Melt Team
- Mavuika's best Melt team with Citlali applying Cryo and shredding RES for Mavuika.
- Xilonen provides additional RES shred and healing while Bennett provides ATK buff and healing as well.
2nd
▼ Vaporize Team
- Vaporize Mavuika with Furina. Xilonen heals the HP loss from Furina's buff and shreds enemy resistances.
- Bennett buffs Mavuika's attacks and activates Pyro Resonance for more attack buffs.
3rd
▼ Overload Team
- With Chevreuse buffing the damage dealt by Pyro and Electro teammates, Mavuika can deal powerful damage.
- Iansan and Ororon provide Mavuika with Fighting Spirit and their own buffs from kit/equipment.
- Ororon can be swapped out with another Electro Off-Field Sub-DPS but Iansan may not be able to max out Mavuika's FS.
4th
▼ Sub-DPS/Support Mavuika
- Mavuika's ability to buff damage increase allows her to be a Sub-DPS/Support hybrid character.
- She'll also be able to buff Elemental Damage since she has access to Nightsoul and can hold Scroll set.
- Will need a 2nd Natlan character as support or DPS to charge her Burst.
Mavuika's best teams are with 2+ Natlan supports.
Citlali or Xilonen can be used as the only other Natlan character but without one, Mavuika can have trouble on activating her Burst each rotation
. Aside from her DPS ability, Mavuika's Elemental Skill allows her to be a Support and Sub-DPS hybrid great for applying Pyro and buffing the active character.

Bennett buffs Mavuika's attack while Citlali and Xilonen both boost her Elemental Damage dealt. Xilonen can use Archaic Petra if you want more Elemental Damage buffs but she's fine with Song of Days Past set.
Rotation Of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mavuika and use her Skill (Tap) to drain Nightsoul points for Obsidian Codex
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with Normal Attacks.
3
Use Xilonen's Burst before switching her out (Optional)
4
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst to gain Nightsoul points
5
Switch to Bennett then use his Burst then Skill
6
Switch back to Mavuika and use her Burst
7
Deal damage with Mavuika's Charged Attacks
8
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
You can skip using Xilonen's Burst if healing is not yet needed.

Another team that Mavuika can be the DPS in is Overload since Iansan and Ororon are great Electro units for it and are Natlanese. Iansan buffs attack and Ororon provides Off-Field Electro damage to trigger Overload for Chevreuse to shred RES and buff attack too.
Rotation Of Overload Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Mavuika and use her Skill (Tap) to apply Pyro
2
Switch to Ororon and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Chevreuse and use her Skill (Hold) to hit enemies
4
Switch to Iansan and use her Skill then hit enemies once with her Normal Attack
5
Use Iansan's Burst before switching her out
6
Switch back to Mavuika and use her Burst
7
Start dealing damage with Mavuika's Charged Attacks
8
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
If Chevreuse is C4 or above, use her Burst before her Skill since it negates the cooldown of her Skill (Hold) once.

Rotation Of Sub-DPS/Support Mavuika
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Xilonen and use her Skill then hit enemies twice with her Normal Attacks

5
Use Mualani's damage combo (make contact with enemies thrice then use her Normal Attack to deal damage) three times.
6
Repeat from Step 1
Mualani can cast her Burst at the end of her turn but most of the buffs would have expired by then.

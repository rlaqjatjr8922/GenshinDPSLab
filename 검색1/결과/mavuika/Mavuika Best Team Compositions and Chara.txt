Mavuika's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our team composition guide for the Pyro Archon Mavuika.
In this guide, we will discuss her best teams with both premium 5-Star
and accessible 4-Star options, covering different synergies and
playstyles depending on whether she is played as a Main DPS,
off-field damage dealer, or burst buffer.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Mavuika
Mavuika is an extremely flexible unit who can function as a Main DPS,
off-field damage dealer, or a powerful burst buffer.
Her strongest Main DPS teams are usually reaction-focused,
such as Melt or Vaporize, to fully maximize her Burst damage.
A major part of Mavuika’s kit revolves around her Elemental Burst,
which does
not
rely on Energy Recharge.
Instead, she consumes
Fighting Spirit
, which is primarily
generated by Natlan characters. Because of this,
it is highly recommended to run at least one Natlan character
alongside her for optimal Burst uptime.
She can also function as a strong support for other DPS units such as
Arlecchino, Wriothesley, Kinich, Mualani, and many more,
thanks to her post-Burst damage bonus that can reach up to 40%.

Mavuika Main DPS Supports
Citlali
is one of the best partners for Mavuika,
      providing Fighting Spirit generation, shielding,
      and Cryo application for consistent Melt reactions.
Xilonen
is another premium option,
      generating Fighting Spirit efficiently while providing
      resistance shred and healing.
Iansan
is an excellent 4-Star alternative.
      At higher constellations (especially C6),
      she can rival or even outperform Xilonen in some setups.
Pyro Traveler
is a fully F2P option,
      offering Fighting Spirit generation and additional damage bonuses,
      particularly useful in Overload teams.

Mavuika with Natlan DPS
Mavuika can also act as a powerful burst buffer and off-field
      damage dealer for Natlan DPS characters.
      After using her Burst, she grants up to 40% damage bonus
      to the active character depending on Fighting Spirit consumed.
Kinich
Burning teams benefit from her Pyro
      application and buffs while she contributes strong personal damage.
Mualani
Vape teams are a natural fit,
      as Mavuika enables consistent Vaporize setups.
Chasca
teams can also utilize Mavuika
      alongside other buffers like Citlali and Furina.
Varesa
Overload is another strong option,
      where Mavuika helps activate Varesa’s passive
The Hero Twice-Returned!
while providing strong buffs and damage.

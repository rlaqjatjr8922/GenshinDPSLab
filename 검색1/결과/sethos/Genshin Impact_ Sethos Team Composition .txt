Sethos
is an Electro Bow user in
Genshin Impact
. His fighting style is close to
Tighnari
by quickly charging his arrows and launching Electro DMG at his enemies. Of course, Sethos is still somewhat unique with his mechanics as he doesn’t rely on his Elemental Skill to improve his charging times, but instead, he consumes Energy when he charges his shots.
As an Electro unit that works well with Aggravate, Hyperbloom, and Overload teams, it’s important to know the best characters to include in every
Sethos team in
Genshin Impact
.

4
Sethos + Furina + Nahida + Kokomi
Since Sethos heavily relies on Elemental Mastery to deal DMG, it’s perfect for him to be included in
Hyperbloom teams in
Genshin Impact
. In this Sethos team, Kokomi and
Nahida
are the Dendro Cores factory who continuously trigger Bloom reactions with the help of Furina. Sethos will shoot the Dendro Cores with his Shadowpiercing Shots to trigger the Hyprbloom reactions. Furina is important since she can notably elevate Sethos and Nahida’s DMG while providing a decent Hydro application on the battlefield.
As the only Dendro unit in this team, building Nahida with Deepwood Memories to shred enemies’ Dendro resistance and enhance the Hyperbloom DMG is essential.
Team Role
Sethos:
Main DPS, Electro enabler
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Hydro enabler, buffer
Nahida:
Sub-DPS, Dendro enabler, buffer, debuffer
Kokomi:
Healer, Hydro enabler
3
Sethos + Dehya + Chevreuse + Yae Miko
Getting away from the Hyperbloom strategy, you can pair Sethos with
Pyro units in
Genshin Impact
to create an Overloaded team. For this Sethos team,
Chevreuse
is essential since she’s the main buffer for all the Overloaded DMG. At the same time,
Dehya
is the character that protects the team while providing a continuous off-field Pyro application.
Yae Miko
is a strong Electro enabler who will always help trigger the Overloaded reaction.
This team doesn’t have big sustain to be in battles continuously but it’s strong enough to participate in hard content like the
Spiral Abyss in
Genshin Impact
.
Team Role
Sethos:
Main DPS, Electro enabler
Dehya:
Support, Pyro enabler
Chevreuse:
Sub-DPS, Pyro enabler, buffer, debuffer
Yae Miko:
Sub-DPS, Electro enabler
2
Sethos + Kazuha + Nahida + Kujou Sara
For a Sethos team that works well with
Dendro reactions in
Genshin Impact
, you can build him with Crit to amplify his Aggravate reactions. To do that, you must pair him with powerful buffers, such as Kazuha, Nahida, and
Kujou Sara
. Kazuha will collect enemies in one spot while debuffing their Electro resistance and increasing Sethos’ Electro DMG. Nahida will notably increase Sethos Elemental Mastery and that will increase his DMG and reaction DMG at the same time. Finally, Sara is an amazing Electro Support that will greatly increase Sethos ATK and Electro Crit DMG.

Sethos:
Main DPS, Electro enabler

1
Sethos + Thoma + Chevreuse + Fischl
Getting out of his comfort zone and relying more on his Twilight Meditation state, you can create a unique Sethos and
Chevreuse team in
Genshin Impact
. Since Sethos has an Elemental Burst that infuses his Bow shots into Electro attacks, you can pair
Thoma
and
Fischl
with him to follow up his attacks with Pyro and Electro off-field DMG. This will create powerful and continuous Overloaded reactions greatly buffed by Chevreuse while she constantly heals the team.
During his Twilight Meditation state, Sethos’ Normal attacks are considered Charged attacks, which makes Thoma’s C6 able to buff all of his attacks. Of course, to make the most of Thoma’s Pyro enabling, you have to keep Sethos relatively close to enemies since Thoma’s Pyro doesn’t travel long distances.
Team Role
Sethos:
Main DPS, Electro enabler
Thoma:
Support, Pyro enabler, shielder, buffer
Chevreuse:
Sub-DPS, Pyro enabler, buffer, debuffer
Fischl:
Sub-DPS, Electro enabler, battery

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Aggravate Keqing Team
Character
Detail
Kazuha
He can shred the enemies' Electro resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 when Electro-Swirl has been triggered with his Burst. Freedom-Sworn also increases the damage output of Keqing's normal & charged ATK.
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of Aggravate reactions. Also, she can decrease the marked enemies' DEF when her C2 is unlocked.
Baizhu
An excellent healer & shielder to pair with another Dendro such as Nahida.
The objective of this team is to consistently trigger catalyze reactions for offense and Electro-Swirl for amplifying Keqing's damage output.
Pros & Cons Of Aggravate Keqing Team
Pros
- Keqing's Skill cooldown can be reduced when triggering Electro-related reactions
- Active character cannot be interrupted while Baizhu's shield is in action
Cons
- Baizhu needs decent Energy Recharge to sustain the shield
- Kazuha's Burst could produce Pyro-Swirl if Pyro is present in the combat area or if Kazuha is affected by it
Rotation Of Aggravate Keqing Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Baizhu's Burst for shield
2
Switch to Nahida
3
Use Nahida's Burst for EM buff
4
Use Nahida's Skill for mark
5
Switch to Keqing
6
Use Keqing's Skill (1st cast) for Electro application
7
Switch to Kazuha
8
Use Kazuha's Burst for Electro-Swirl reaction
9
Use Kazuha's Skill for crowd control & Anemo particles
10
Switch to Keqing
11
Use Keqing's Skill (2nd cast) for Electro infusion
12
Use Keqing's Burst for Electro DMG & CRIT Rate bonus
13
Use Keqing's NA/CA and recast her Skill twice for infusion
14
Utilize Keqing until Baizhu's shield is gone
15
Return to step 1
Nahida's mark can last for a while so you can still trigger catalyze reactions even if Baizhu's shield is gone. However, if you cannot avoid incoming attacks, repeat the rotation when you want to recast Baizhu's shield.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Hyperbloom Keqing Team
Character
Detail
Xingqiu
The consistent Hydro application can produce Dendro cores, especially when Tighnari is beside the enemy.
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of Hyperblooms.
Baizhu
An excellent healer & shielder to pair with another Dendro such as Nahida.
The objective of this team is to produce Dendro cores and transform them into hyperbloom projectiles when they come in contact with Keqing's Electro application. The projectiles are homing which chase an opponent even if it is quickly moving or afloat.
Pros & Cons Of Hyperbloom Keqing Team
Pros
- Keqing's Skill cooldown can be reduced when triggering Electro-related reactions
- Active character cannot be interrupted while Baizhu's shield is in action
- Effective against stationary & mobile enemies
Cons
- Baizhu needs decent Energy Recharge to sustain the shield
- Keqing's Electro application through infused normal ATK can be slowed by Cryo
Rotation Of Hyperbloom Keqing Team
No.

10
Use Keqing's Skill (2nd cast) for Electro infusion
11

12
Utilize Keqing until Baizhu's shield is gone
13
Return to step 1
Nahida's mark can last for a while so you can still produce Dendro cores with Xingqiu's barrier even if Baizhu's shield is gone. However, if you cannot avoid incoming attacks, repeat the rotation when you want to recast Baizhu's shield.

If you already have a healer, Sucrose is a good choice for the 4th slot. She has an EM buff and her crowd control can also compress the Dendro cores. When all of the Dendro cores are bundled together, Keqing can trigger them all at once.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Physical Keqing Team
Character
Detail
Rosaria
Thru her Burst, she is a good Cryo applicator, CRIT Rate buffer, & ATK buff provider from Noblesse Oblige x4.
Mika
Mika is great support for Physical DPS. Mika has buffs for boosting ATK SPD & Physical DMG Bonus which is beneficial for Keqing. With Favonius Lance, Mika can produce some energy particles when landing a CRIT hit.
Zhongli
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' Physical RES and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
The objective of this team is to consistently apply Cryo to the enemy so Keqing can trigger superconduct with her Skill & Burst. Keqing does not use the 2nd cast of her Skill in order to not get the Electro infusion.
Pros & Cons Of Physical Keqing Team
Pros
- Keqing's normal & charged ATK can deal powerful damage
- Several multipliers can significantly improve Keqing's damage output
Cons
- Keqing cannot maximize her constellations since her constellations are leaning towards Electro build
- Keqing's attack rotation can be slowed by Cryo

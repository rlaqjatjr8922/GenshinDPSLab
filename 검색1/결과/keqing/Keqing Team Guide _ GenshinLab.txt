Keqing Team Overview
This Page provides the best Keqing Team information. Keqing is DPS in most of the situation.
Keqing is one of the characters with highest Heavy Attack DMG. Keqing can also be formed in different ways including Thunder, Superdconduct, Electro-Charged and Overload team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

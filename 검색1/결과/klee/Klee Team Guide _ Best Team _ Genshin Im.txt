Klee’s Elemental Skill throws a bomb that deals AoE Pyro DMG and eventually splits into mines that explode on contact or after a short delay. Her Elemental Burst continuously summons Sparks ‘n’ Splash to attack nearby enemies.
Klee’s Normal Attacks, Elemental Skill, and Elemental Burst can grant her Explosive Sparks, allowing her Charged Attack to transform into a stamina-free Boom-Boom Strike.
With at least 2 Hexerei characters in the party, during her Burst, Klee’s third Normal Attack can consume an Explosive Spark to release a Boom-Boom Strike. Additionally, each time Klee deals damage with different attack types, she gains Boom Badges, which further strengthen her Boom-Boom Strike based on the number of badges she holds.

Durin gains either the “Confirmation of Purity” or “Denial of Darkness” state through his Elemental Skill, and his Elemental Burst will also take on different forms depending on which state he is in. The “Confirmation of Purity” state focuses on support utility, providing continuous Off-field Pyro DMG while causing nearby party members, when they trigger Burning, Overloaded, Pyro Swirl, or Pyro Crystallize on enemies, or deal Pyro DMG or Dendro DMG to enemies affected by Burning, to reduce those enemies’ Pyro RES and the RES of the corresponding element. The “Denial of Darkness” state focuses on Durin’s own damage output, providing higher-damage Off-field Pyro DMG and further increasing Durin’s reaction damage in Vaporize or Melt teams. Additionally, when Durin is teamed up with a Hexerei character, both of the above effects will be further strengthened.

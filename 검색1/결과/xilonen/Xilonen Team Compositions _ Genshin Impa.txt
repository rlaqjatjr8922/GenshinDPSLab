Xilonen
is a 5-star Geo Sword character that is both a support and a healer. Her role in the team is to enhance survivability and provide Elemental RES reduction through her Source Samples while applying significant DEF-scaling Geo DMG.
How
Xilonen
Helps:
Xilonen
’s
Source Samples
can decrease Elemental RES, allowing DPS units to trigger strong reactions.
During
Nightsoul’s Blessing
, she reduces Elemental RES and adds rapid Geo DMG through her
Blade Roller
kicks, supplementing team DPS without overtaking the main reaction.
When using her Burst
Ocelotlicue Point!
,
Xilonen
can heal teammates if at least two of her Source Samples are non-Geo, keeping DPS characters sustained.
You can find some popular team compositions and builds below, as well as recommended team rotations!

Xilonen

Her DEF-scaling Geo DMG complements Vaporise reactions by lowering Elemental RES, while her Elemental Burst provides additional healing to sustain aggressive gameplay.

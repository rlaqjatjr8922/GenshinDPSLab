Xilonen's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Xilonen team composition guide and character synergy 
overview for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams 
for Xilonen, alongside recommended character pairings that help 
maximize the value of her Elemental RES Shred, healing, and support utility.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Xilonen
Xilonen is primarily used as a support who provides Elemental 
RES Shred for
Geo
,
Electro
,
Hydro
,
Pyro
, and
Cryo
teams. Because of this, her best teams 
are usually built around damage dealers from these elements who can fully 
benefit from her support utility.
Xilonen is especially strong in teams that want both resistance shred 
and a flexible defensive option. Her ability to buff multiple archetypes 
makes her one of the most universal supports for modern elemental teams.

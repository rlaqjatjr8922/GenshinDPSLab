Elemental Mastery Gives More Damage Over ATK%
While ATK% gives higher personal damage when using Anemo Traveler as support, Elemental Mastery can increase overall damage because of how Swirl works. This is especially when dealing with multiple enemies at once.
Swirl Cannot Produce A CRIT Hit
While CRIT stats can boost raw Anemo DMG, it does not affect swirl damage so CRIT is not recommended for Anemo Traveler Support Build.

Anemo Traveler Skill Guide & How To Use Anemo Traveler
Recommended Rotation For Anemo Traveler
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Elemental Skill to gather enemies, spread elements & deal swirl damage, or produce energy for Burst
2
Use Elemental Burst to either deliver the final blow or use as a prelude to a combo
Uses Of Anemo Traveler's Elemental Skill
Crowd Control Small Enemies
Small enemies can be displaced by the Elemental Skill. This can be a quick way to crowd control small mobs and throw them back to either give yourself safe distance or trigger swirl reaction.
Spread Elements To Enemies
All enemies caught in the Anemo Traveler's Elemental Skill gets hit multiple times and inflicts swirl damage to all of them. Use this for a quick way of spreading an element to multiple enemies in range.
Gain Energy & Resistance To Interruption
The Anemo Traveler's Elemental Skill is on a relatively small cooldown. You can either tap or hold it against an enemy to produce energy particles to charge your Elemental Burst. Also, you gain resistance to interruption when holding Elemental Skill.
Use Of Anemo Traveler's Elemental Burst
Displaces Lightweight Enemies & Inflict Damage
Small enemies can be displaced by the Elemental Burst. They are going to be dragged to a fixed distance so you can create a space during combat by using it.
Spread Swirl To Multiple Enemies
The Anemo Traveler's Burst is basically a Tornado that can absorb an element on the field and spread it to enemies it hits. Same as the Elemental Skill, this is a good way of spreading an element against multiple enemies.

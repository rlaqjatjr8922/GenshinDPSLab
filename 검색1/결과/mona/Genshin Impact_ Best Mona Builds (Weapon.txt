Understanding Mona’s Role and Abilities
Mona is a powerful Hydro character in Genshin Impact, primarily serving as a Catalyst user specializing in damage dealing and crowd control. Her unique ability to reveal enemies’ positions with her Elemental Skill and amplify damage with her Elemental Burst makes her a versatile addition to many team compositions.
Her Elemental Skill,
Mirror Reflection of Doom
, creates a phantom clone that taunts enemies and provides a Hydro infusion, enabling quick reactions and damage combos. The clone lasts for a limited time and can be used to trigger various elemental reactions or escape danger.
Mona’s Elemental Burst,
Starry Sky
, summons a large area of effect (AoE) which continuously deals Hydro damage and applies the Wet status to enemies. When enemies are affected by Wet, Mona’s damage output increases significantly, especially if her talents are properly leveled.
Her role in teams is often as a Hydro applicator and damage dealer, maximizing elemental reactions such as Freeze, which pairs well with Cryo characters, or Electro-Charged with Electro characters. Mona’s ability to provide consistent wet status makes her invaluable for reactions that deal additional damage.
Understanding Mona’s abilities also involves managing her stamina and positioning, as she excels when she can stay within optimal range to trigger reactions without exposing herself to danger. Proper timing of her skills and Burst ensures maximum uptime for damage output and enemy control.
In summary, Mona’s role hinges on her capacity to apply Hydro reliably, burst damage potential with her Elemental Burst, and synergize with other elemental characters. Mastering her abilities and understanding her specific strengths allows players to unlock her full potential in diverse team setups.

Optimal Weapon Choices for Mona
Choosing the right weapon for Mona is crucial to maximize her potential as a Hydro DPS or support. The optimal weapons enhance her elemental damage, energy recharge, or both, depending on your team composition and playstyle.
Best-in-Slot Weapons:
Skyward Atlas
: The premier choice for Mona, this 5-star claymore increases Elemental Damage Bonus and provides a substantial boost to her Elemental Skill and Burst damage. The increased damage output makes her a formidable Hydro applicator and damage dealer.
The Widsith
: A 4-star weapon that offers versatile damage boosts based on its random effect. It grants increased ATK and Elemental Damage Bonus, making it a strong option for Mona when you lack 5-star options.
Prototype Ambition
: A 4-star craftable claymore that increases Energy Recharge, allowing Mona to use her Elemental Burst more frequently. Its bonus increases with each shielded or protected party member, making it especially useful in shield-focused team compositions.
Favonius Greatsword
: Although not a claymore, it’s worth mentioning for its Energy Recharge stats, enabling Mona to generate more Energy particles and sustain her Burst uptime.
Other Notable Weapons:
The Blackcliff Slasher
: Offers increased critical damage, suitable for Mona when built as a damage dealer.
Serpent Spine
: Provides increased Crit Rate and Crit Damage at the cost of some HP, effective in aggressive playstyles.
When selecting a weapon, consider your Mona build and team role. Skyward Atlas remains the best for maximizing damage output, while weapons like Prototype Ambition and Favonius Greatsword excel in energy recharge and support roles. Match your weapon choice with artifact sets and team composition for optimal performance.

Ideal Substats for Mona Artifacts
Maximizing Mona’s effectiveness hinges on focusing her artifact substats. Prioritize substats that enhance her primary roles: a Hydro applicator and a catalyst for elemental reactions. The ideal substats include:
Elemental Mastery (EM):
Boosts Mona’s Hydro damage and enhances the damage of elemental reactions like Freeze, Vaporize, and 4-Piece Mantle of Thunders reactions. A high EM stat significantly improves her overall damage output.
Hydro Damage Bonus:
Increases Mona’s Hydro damage directly, making her more potent in applying Hydro effects and triggering reactions.
Critical Rate and Critical Damage:
These substats are crucial for maximizing Mona’s damage. Aim for a balanced Crit Rate/Crit Damage ratio—ideally around 1:2—to ensure consistent and high burst damage.
ATK%:
Slightly increases Mona’s overall damage, but generally less impactful than EM or Crit stats. Use this substat if other slots lack optimal options.
When selecting substats, it’s essential to consider the artifact piece’s primary stat to balance the overall stats efficiently. For example, a Sands of Eon—Electro or Elemental Mastery—paired with substats focusing on Crit and EM will optimize Mona’s performance. Crit substats should be prioritized on Circlet and Goblet pieces, especially if their primary stats align with Crit or Hydro Damage Bonus.
In summary, the best artifact substats for Mona emphasize Elemental Mastery, Hydro Damage Bonus, and Crit Rate/Crit Damage. Proper substat prioritization ensures Mona can consistently apply Hydro, trigger powerful reactions, and deal substantial damage in any team composition.

Gameplay and Stat Prioritization for Mona
Optimal Mona performance hinges on precise stat prioritization that amplifies her strengths as a Hydro catalyst user. Her primary role involves damage dealing through charged attacks, mass hydro application, and elemental reactions. To maximize her potential, focus on the following stats:
Hydro Damage Bonus
: This is Mona’s most significant damage amplifier. Prioritize increasing her Hydro DMG Bonus through artifacts and weapons to maximize her burst and charged attack damage.
ATK%
: Increasing Mona’s overall attack power enhances both her normal and charged attacks. This stat synergizes well with her abilities, boosting her overall damage output.
CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG
: Balancing these two stats is crucial. Aim for a CRIT Rate of around 50-60% to ensure consistent critical hits, and stack CRIT DMG to maximize damage output.
Elemental Mastery (EM)
: Mona’s elemental reactions—especially with Electro and Pyro—are powerful. Prioritize EM to boost the damage of reactions like Electro-Charged and Vaporize.
Energy Recharge
: Crucial for spamming Mona’s Elemental Burst, her primary damage source. Aim for 150-200% Energy Recharge to ensure her burst is available frequently without sacrificing damage stats.
In terms of gameplay, Mona excels when her Elemental Burst is available for continuous hydro application. Build her to sustain high uptime on her burst, freeing her to support reactions and deal consistent damage. Proper stat prioritization ensures Mona remains effective in various team compositions, whether focused on quick reactions or sustained DPS.

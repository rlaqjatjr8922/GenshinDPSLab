With Candace having incredibly high Energy Recharge requirements,
her best build options tend to be those which help to ease this.
Favonius Lance
is therefore her best weapon for most situations.
Alternatively, if you are aiming to increase Candace's personal DMG, you can use
weapons such as
Staff of Homa
or
Engulfing Lightning
instead
in her build.
Build Candace with
Emblem of Severed Fate
or
Noblesse Oblige
. Emblem is generally the best allowing you to
effectively use the 4-piece bonus of the artifact set as well as heavily
benefitting from the +20% Energy Recharge. Noblesse however can be a great
option in teams that want a lot of ATK %.
Candace's most important Talent is her Elemental Burst which is the focus
of most of her supportive utility. Her Elemental Skill can also be useful to
level but it is not worth levelling her Normal Attacks when played off-field.
1.1.
Candace's Energy Recharge Requirements
Candace has incredibly high Energy Recharge requirements with her
Elemental Skill only generating 2-3 particles. As a result of her low
personal DPS you also do not want to be funnelling particles to her either
which only increases her build requirements further.
Your Energy Recharge requirements will depend on how menu
Hydro
characters you have in your team as well as if you use her Elemental Skill once
or twice in a rotation.

2.
Candace Combos and Skills Explained
Candace's Elemental Skill
Sacred Rite: Heron's Sanctum
has 2 different modes with different uses:
Tap - Dashes forward dealing
Hydro
in front of Candace.
Hold - Allows candace to block incoming attacks. The shield is also charged
for a period of time, when released or when the shield is broken she will
deal AoE Hydro DMG in front of herself. This charges instantly with
her A1
Aegis of Crossed Arrows
.
Tapping the Skill generates 2 Energy particles while Holding the skill
generates 3 instead.
Candace's Elemental Burst
Sacred Rite: Wagtail's Tide
is the
main way in which she helps to support your team. This provides the
"Prayer of the Crimson Crown" buff to your active character.
This buff allows your active character to deal increased
Elemental
DMG with their Normal Attacks, deals AoE Hydro DMG periodically and also provides
an
Elemental Infusion
to applicable characters.

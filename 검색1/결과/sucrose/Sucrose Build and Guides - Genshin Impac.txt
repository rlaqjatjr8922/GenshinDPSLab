Sucrose Build
Sucrose Guide and Best Builds
Sucrose is a 4-star Anemo Catalyst character that brings an Elemental Mastery
boost to any team. She excels with Elemental Burst teams, adding a simple 'off-field'
play style to power up other characters and their abilities. Not only can she boost
damage for the rest of the team, but she can use her skills to group enemies up into
packs and help to control the flow of battle.

1.
Elemental Support Sucrose
Sucrose's primary role is buffing other Elemental Damage dealers. She has a
simple and fast rotation, swap her into the field and use her Elemental Skill
to pull enemies together than boost another Element's DMG with her Elemental
Burst. Now your ready to switch to another Sub DPS role and create some
elemental reactions. Lastly, have your Main DPS join the party and watch the
numbers explode. She can also be an On-field Driver, using her skills as the
final trigger for chain reactions to devastate your enemies. Sucrose can repeat
both these processes quite quickly and she is an amazing addition to any Elemental
Burst team.

2.
Combos and How to Play Sucrose
When using Sucrose on your team, your first priority is to understand which
reactions you should be targeting to maximize your damage. Sucrose has
half-a-dozen teams she can be an effectively Off-fielder, and several more as
an on-field driver. Each team has a unique style of targeting a different
Elemental Reaction
all with powerful results so you need to understand the three main
reactions: Transformative, Amplifying and Additive.
Transformative reactions use Elemental Mastery and Character levels to
determine their damage. These reactions are:
Bloom
,
Burgeon
,
Burning
,
Electro-Charged
,
Hyperbloom
,
Overloaded
,
Shatter
,
Superconduct
and
Swirl
.
These reactions typically ignore enemy defense stats and focus solely on
their elemental Resistances which means you can target a given elemental
weakness.
Amplifying reactions scale from offensives stats on your characters
and can CRIT. These hits are then multiplied by a reaction multiplier
when triggered.. These reactions include:
Melt
and
Vaporize
.
Additive reactions are similar to Transformative reactions in that they
scale from Elemental Mastery and Character Level, but can also benefit
from % DMG and CRITs. These reactions include:
Aggravate
and
Spread
.
Every team that uses Sucrose will be driving for these triggered reactions
and its important to develop your rotations, timing and needed stats to
trigger massive damage. Overall, Sucrose is a powerful elemental ally with
a huge versatility and very little talent investment.

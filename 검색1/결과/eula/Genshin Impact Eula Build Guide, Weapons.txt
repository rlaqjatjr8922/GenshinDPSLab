Eula Gameplay
Eula's gameplay centers around the unique
Lightfall Sword
mechanic, which transforms her graceful combat style into a devastating physical "nuke" through her specialized burst window. Her kit primarily scales on her
Physical DMG
and
ATK
, and she enables high-impact hypercarry compositions by providing constant on-field pressure and essential resistance shred for her own strikes.
Her
Elemental Skill
, Icetide Vortex, creates
Grimheart
stacks that increase her defense and resistance to interruption. When Eula utilizes the "Hold" version of the skill, she consumes these stacks to unleash a flurry of ice that shreds nearby enemies'
Physical and Cryo RES
; otherwise, the "Press" version serves as her primary tool for quick Cryo application and stack building. When these stacks are consumed while her Burst is active, it ensures a high-frequency damage spike that perfectly sets the stage for her finale.
When she drops her
Elemental Burst
, Glacial Illumination, she summons a
Lightfall Sword
that basically puts her on a countdown, gaining energy stacks every time her normal attacks, skills, or bursts land on an opponent. It creates a localized power spike that keeps the pressure on enemies as Eula cycles through her optimal attack string to maximize the final explosion's scale. The best part is that her built-in interruption resistance means she rarely misses a beat, allowing her to stand her ground and keep swinging until the blade finally shatters for massive AoE damage.
Her
Passives
are all about making her rotation feel rewarding while scaling her damage off her aggressive playstyle. One of them triggers a "shattered" mini-burst whenever two Grimheart stacks are consumed at once, while the other directly resets her skill cooldown the moment her Burst is cast to ensure she never runs out of steam. It makes her super satisfying to play once you find your rhythm because you’re essentially turning her into a dancing powerhouse who hits harder the more she persists on the battlefield.

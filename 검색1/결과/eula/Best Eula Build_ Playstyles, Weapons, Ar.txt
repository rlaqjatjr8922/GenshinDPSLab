Eula’s Normal and Charged Attacks
Normal attack:
Eula
executes 5 strikes with her claymore.
Charged attack:
Eula
consumes stamina over time to perform continuous slashers. When her stamina ends, or attack button is released, she performs a stronger slash.
Eula’s Elemental Skill
Eula’s Elemental Skill Press and Hold
Her Elemental Skill,
Icetide Vortex
,
can be pressed or held to activate
.
When you tap her Elemental Skill, Eula swiftly slashes her opponents and deals Cryo Dmg.
Simply pressing
her Elemental Skill has a
short cooldown of 4 seconds
, while
holding it has a 10-second
cooldown.
Pressing
Eula’s Skill button gives her max 2 stacks of
Grimheart
.
Holding
Eula’s Skill button consumes
Grimheart
stacks, reducing nearby opponents’ Physical and
Cryo
RES.
Different instances of
Cryo
DMG will be dealt to nearby enemies depending on the number of stacks consumed.
Eula’s Elemental Burst
Eula’s Elemental Burst
Most of
Eula’s
damage comes from her Elemental Burst,
Glacial Illumination
. Once pressed,
Eula
sweeps off her opponent’s feet with a massive wave of Cryo DMG. It also creates a
Lightfall Sword
that increases her resistance to interruption and follows her around for up to 7 seconds.
Eula’s
Lightfall
Sword
charges up whenever her Normal attack, Elemental Skill, and Elemental Burst deal DMG to opponents. The
Lightfall Sword
can gain a charge every 0.1s.
According to
Zy0x’s
updated Eula guide, the maximum number of stacks you can get on her Lightfall Sword at a C0-optimized rotation is 14. Likewise, the maximum number of stacks you can get with constellations is 30. A downside of her Elemental Burst is the high Energy Cost of 80, so pairing her with battery characters like
Diona
,
Rosaria
, and even
Raiden Shogun
is crucial.
A rotation involving Eula as your main DPS can go like this:
Tap Eula’s Elemental Skill (E).
Activate Eula’s Elemental Burst (Q).
Hold Eula’s Elemental Skill (E).
Spam Normal Attacks.

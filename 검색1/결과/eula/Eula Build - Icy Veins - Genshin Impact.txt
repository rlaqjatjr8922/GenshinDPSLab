Eula Guide and Best Builds
Eula is a 5-Star Cryo Claymore user in Genshin Impact who primarily
focuses on dealing Physical damage instead of Elemental damage. This guide
will focus on Eula's best builds, artifacts, weapons and best team compositions.

Eula's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Elemental Skill reduces Physical and Cryo RES on enemies.
Incredibly powerful Physical damage.
Easy to play around Superconduct Elemental Reactions.
Weaknesses
Elemental Burst has high energy requirements often requiring a battery character.
Elemental Burst provides such a high amount of damage that it is a significant DPS loss if it misses or does not CRIT.
Lack of effective and varied units that can support Eula.

3.
Combos and How to Play Eula
Eula's Elemental Skill
Icetide Vortex
can be pressed
or held providing different effects accordingly. Pressing gives
stacks of
Grimheart
while Holding uses the stacks to
deal increased damage and lowering both the
Physical
and
Cryo
RES of enemies.
Eula's Elemental Burst
Glacial Illumination
is your main
source of damage. Upon use, a Lightfall Sword will follow Eula for 7s. Attacks
deals to opponents from Eula during this time will add a stack to the Lightfall
Sword therefore increasing its Physical DMG at the end of the 7-second mark
or when you swap to another character. This makes Eula's damage very backloaded,
only occurring once a full rotation has been completed.
Eula's main combo to gain Lightfall stacks is:
Icetide Vortex
Tap
Glacial Illumination
Favonius Bladework - Edel
x4
Icetide Vortex
Hold
Favonius Bladework - Edel
x4
With Attack SPD buffs, you can do alternate rotations such as a 5th
Normal Attack at the end of the rotation giving you an additional stack.

Chongyun's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Elemental Burst only costs 40 Energy.
Able to infuse Cryo onto other characters.
One of the rare characters that buffs ATK SPD.
Weaknesses
Lacks personal DMG and a lot of investement if you want to play him as a DPS.
Limited team compositions.
Few characters actually benefit from the Cryo Infusion Chongyun provides.

For this Chongyun build the best weapon is
Wolf's Gravestone
, as it not only boost his own
ATK but also the entire team.
Beacon of the Reed Sea
and
Serpent Spine
are good options for more DPS focused Chongyun, as they are better for his personal damage.
Sacrificial Greatsword
is a great option as it can allow you to use Chongyun's skill
twice in a row.
For a Chongyun Burst support build, his best set is
Noblesse Oblige
as it will provide a team wide ATK buff through Chongyun's Burst. If another character in 
your team already uses
Noblesse Oblige
, you should use either
Blizzard Strayer
or
Emblem of Severed Fate
.
Chongyun's Energy Requirements are rather low since his Burst does not cost that much, but you
should still aim towards 130 % ER.
Whether you use Chongyun as a Sub DPS or Burst Support, prioritize his Elemental Burst
and Skill first. His Normal Attack can be upgraded slightly, but you do not need to max it.

3.
Chongyun Combos and How to Play
Chongyun is one of the easiest characters to play since he does not require any 
big rotation, or anything specific to function. He is mainly used as a Burst Support, 
since his
Spirit Blade: Cloud-Parting Star
Burst deals massive damage.
You will swap to Chongyun to put down his
Spirit Blade: Chonghua's Layered Frost
AoE zone that will infuse with
Cryo
any melee weapons of characters 
who are inside the circle, and then use his Burst before swapping back to the main DPS.

5.3.
Chongyun's Constellations
Chongyun's C2 and C4 are the most viable, as they respectively grant him less Cooldown 
and more energy regeneration. However, you do not need to target him specifically, since he is a 4-star 
you will eventually get all his constellations through pulling for other characters.

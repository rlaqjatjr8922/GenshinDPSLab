Dori Guide and Best Builds
Dori is a 4-star Electro healer that oddly uses a claymore as a weapon.
She has quite a flexible toolkit allowing to fill multiple roles such as a
healer, battery and moderate reaction driver. This guide will focus on Dori's
best builds, artifacts, weapons and best team compositions.

2.
Combos and How to Play Dori
Dori's playstyle centers around her Elemental Burst,
Alcazarzaray's Exactitude
, while her elemental skill,
Spirit-Warding Lamp: Troubleshooter Cannon
, provides a mediocre
amount of
Electro
Damage. However, her Elemental Skill can be
boosted from her first constellation, granting her the ability to apply Electro
twice during her skill phase. Typically, Dori is played as a healer with her
ability to apply Electro, usually only taken advantage of when she has
completed her constellations at C6. This can lead many players to feel like her
abilities are sub-par when compared to other Electro healers like
Kuki Shinobu
. However, if you do have Dori at C6 her
Normal Attacks will become infused with Electro allowing her to become a strong
Elemental Reaction driver.
Those without her C6 will have to rely on both her Elemental Skill, which
recharges quite quickly, and her Elemental Burst to maintain any kind of
Electro on the battlefield. As a healer, Dori's Elemental Burst offers constant
healing based upon your position compared with her summoned Jinni. This applies
to the active character only and does not effect any off-field members.

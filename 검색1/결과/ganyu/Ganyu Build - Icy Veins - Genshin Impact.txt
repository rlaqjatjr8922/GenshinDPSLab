Ganyu's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
High base CRIT DMG, which makes her a powerful main DPS even at C0.
Easy to Build and to play.
Powerful AoE attacks.
Good Off-Field damage as well.
Weaknesses
Must use charged shots, and without C6 it takes quite some time to charge, leaving you vulnerable.
Requires specific teammates to support her.

For this build, Ganyu's BiS weapon is the
The First Great Magic
. This is due to 
the fact it heavily buffs her charged attacks as well as the bow's stack mechanic 
which she can benefit from whether you play Ganyu with other
Cryo
characters or not.
When building Ganyu, you can combine
The First Great Magic
with the 
4-piece
Blizzard Strayer
Artifact set which will boost both her ATK and CRIT Rate, 
allowing to aim more on CRIT DMG on the pieces themselves.
If you play Ganyu in a melt-focused team, you will want to use
Wanderer's Troupe
instead, as it will provide you with 80 Elemental Mastery and the 4-piece set constantly applies 
the buff to Charged Shots.
If you play Ganyu with
Furina
you can use
Marechaussee Hunter
.
Stat-wise, you want to aim for around 130% Energy Recharge if you want to be able to 
Burst every rotation. If you use
Blizzard Strayer
you only need around 50/60% CRIT Rate 
as the Artifact set will add a lot of CRIT Rate.

3.
Ganyu Combos and How to Play
Ganyu is an extremely powerful
Cryo
DPS. 
Whether Ganyu is played in a
Freeze
,
Melt
, or 
Mono-Cryo, you will mostly use the same rotation. Place Ganyu's Elemntal Burst 
and use her Charged Attacks. Ganyu's Elemental Skill,
Trail of the Qilin
, is 
mainly used for energy generation and a taunt to keep her free to fire charged attacks at her enemies.
Overall, no matter what team Ganyu is a part of, she will always use her
Elemental Burst,
Celestial Shower
, covering a huge AoE zone with it, 
and being able to trigger Elemental Reactions inside it through the help of her teammates,
While she shoots down all the enemies with her powerful charged shots.

5.3.
Ganyu's Constellations
Ganyu works fine at C0, however, her C1 and C6 are her best constellations. Her C6 
is really useful and increases her damage drastically, since it reduces the charge time of 
her charged shots. But since it does require a lot of investment, stopping at C1 is 
plenty enough, as it reduces enemies' Cryo RES and helps with energy recharge.

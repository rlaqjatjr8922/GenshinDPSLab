Updated May 29, 2025, by Nahda Nabiilah:
Escoffier has changed the game for Cryo-needing teams. Now, Morgana teams have a very strong debuffer who shreds enemies’ resistance by a whopping 55%. Of course, with an ability like that, all the Hydro and Cryo characters are making a comeback. There aren’t many units in Genshin Impact that are excellent at constantly applying Hydro in an AoE. Whether players are seeking to use Ayato as a main DPS or go stray and use him as a Hydro enabler, it’s crucial to know the
best team comp for Ayato in Genshin Impact
.
5
Ayato + Furina + Citlali + Escoffier
Playing Ayato in a Freeze team is far better than it used to be before Natlan, thanks to Citlali and Escoffier. Citlali can reduce enemies’ resistance by 20% while increasing Ayato’s DMG by 40% with her Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City set. On the other hand, Escoffier heavily shreds enemies’ resistance by 55%, which will insanely buff Ayato’s Hydro DMG. Finally, Furina will take Ayato’s DMG to the next level with her Elemental Burst’s buff.
Team Roles
Ayato:
Main DPS, Hydro Enabler
Furina:
Sub-DPS, Healer, Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Citlali:
Shielder, Buffer, Debuffer, Cryo Enabler
Escoffier:
Sub-DPS, Debuffer, Cryo Enabler
4
Ayato + Nahida + Nilou + Baizhu
Since Ayato has a strong Hydro-enabling ability, players can include him in a
Bloom team in
Genshin Impact
. When he finishes his Elemental Skill, players can count on Ayato’s Elemental Burst and Nilou’s Hydro-enabling ability to keep the Hydro application going. From the Dendro units' side, Nahida and Baizhu barely need any field time to keep the field flooding with off-field Dendro attacks.
Team Roles
Ayato:
Main DPS, Hydro Enabler, Driver
Nahida:
Sub-DPS, Buffer, Debuffer, Dendro Enabler
Nilou:
Buffer, Hydro Enabler
Baizhu:
Healer, Shielder, Buffer, Dendro Enabler
3
Ayato + Nahida + Kuki Shinobu + Baizhu
Aside from Bloom teams, Ayato can also work with other
Dendro reactions in
Genshin Impact
. Replacing Nilou with Kuki Shinobu boosts Nahida’s DMG by introducing the Spread reaction to her DPS.

Ayato + Fischl + Ororon + Ifa
For F2P Ayato teams, you can pair Ayato with Electro characters to trigger as many
Electro-charged reactions as possible
. Although Ayato is mostly a main DPS, he has a strong Hydro application when he leaves the field, helping both Fischl and Ororon to trigger more Electro-charged reactions. On top of that, when Fischl gets her C6 passive ability, she will be able to send an Electro attack with every Ayato Normal attack, creating an endless stream of Electro-charged reactions. This Electro-charged atmosphere will allow Ororon to enter his Nightsoul's Blessing state and buff Ayato’s DMG by 40%.

1
Ayato + Kazuha + Xiangling + Bennett
This team adopts the mainstream idea of the national team. Usually, the Hydro unit in this team composition is Xingqiu, but here, Kamisato Ayato takes the lead. The idea behind
having the Pyro characters is to trigger Vaporize
reactions with Ayato and Xiangling. Meanwhile, Kazuha and Bennett will allow both characters to deal massive DMG.
No matter how strong Xiangling’s Energy Recharge stat is, she always needs Bennett to regain her Elemental Burst. And it’s not bad, since Xiangling snapshots Bennett’s buff, which means her Pyronado will spin around the entire duration buffed by Kazuha and Bennett’s buffs. Not to mention, both Pyro characters will trigger the Pyro Resonance, adding 25% extra ATK to the team.
Team Roles
Ayato:
Main DPS, Hydro Enabler
Kazuha:
Crowd Control, Buffer, Debuffer
Xiangling:
Sub-DPS, Pyro Enabler

Support, Healer, Buffer, Battery

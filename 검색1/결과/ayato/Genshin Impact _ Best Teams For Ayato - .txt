▼Hyperbloom Ayato
★★★★★
- Uses Electro, Hydro and Dendro to deal powerful damage to single target.
- Does not need high investments on characters.

▼Burgeon Ayato
★★★★・
- Uses Ayato as a Hydro Enabler for Burgeon Teams.

- Uses Vaporize reactions to deal powerful damage.
- Amazing at both single target and AoE.

The objective of this team is to produce Hyperbloom while dealing powerful damage through attacks of Ayato.
Pros & Cons Of Hyperbloom Ayato Team
Pros
- Powerful against single target and is competitive against top meta teams at minimal investments.
- Easy to use and does not have strict rotations.
Cons
- Can be difficult to use when the enemies have elements that disrupts the reaction.
Rotation Of Hyperbloom Ayato Team
General Attack Rotation
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Burst of Ayato.
2
Switch to Nahida and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
3
Switch to Kuki Shinobu and use Elemental Skill.
4
Switch to Sucrose and swirl Electro using Elemental Skill and Burst.
5
Switch back to Ayato and use Elemental Skill and deal damage using Normal Attack.
6
Repeat from step 1
The rotation itself is flexible so long as you can trigger the bloom with Kuki's Elemental Skill. Try to swirl Electro using Sucrose to increase the Hyperbloom damage.

The objective of this team is to create blooms and trigger them with the Pyro for an AoE DMG.
Pros & Cons Of Burgeon Thoma Team
Pros
- Deals powerful damage at AoE and single target.
Cons
- Strict rotation is required or Burgeon would not trigger.
- Requires high investments of Energy Recharge to Thoma.
- Deals damage to the teams as well so health management is required.
Rotation Of Burgeon Thoma Team
General Attack Rotation
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Burst of Ayato.
2
Switch to Nahida and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
3
Switch to Baizhu and use Elemental Skill and Burst.
4
Switch to Thoma and use Elemental Skill and Burst. Trigger the current blooms created using Elemental Skill.
5
Switch to Ayato and use Elemental Skill then deal damage with Normal Attack.
6
Repeat to no. 1
You need to use Pyro after creating blooms. If not it may cause other reactions like Vaporized and Burning.

- Collei has frequent Dendro infliction but has shorter duration.
It is important to have Dendro and Pyro in the team to create Burgeon reaction. The frequency of infliction is important for more Burgeon to be triggered.

This team mainly deals damage with Ayato and Xiangling. Kazuha gathers the enemies and increases the DMG of the team while Xiangling and Bennett works together in dealing powerful off-field Pyro.
Pros & Cons Of National Ayato Team
Pros
- Powerful against single target and easy to use.
Cons
- Building requires high investments for damage.
- Bennett and Xiangling are irreplaceable units in this team comp.
Rotation Of National Ayato Team
General Attack Rotation
No.
Procedure
1
Use Elemental Burst of Ayato.
2
Switch to Bennett and use Elemental Burst.
3
Switch to Kazuha and use Elemental Burst and Skill.
4
Switch to Xiangling and use Elemental Burst and Skill.
5
Switch back to Ayato and use Elemental Skill and deal damage using Normal Attack.
6
Repeat from step 1
The rotation itself is flexible. Just make sure to use Bennett's Elemental Burst before using Xiangling's Elemental Burst.

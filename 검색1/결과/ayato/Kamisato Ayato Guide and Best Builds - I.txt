Kamisato Ayato's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Extremely good Hydro application.
Solid DPS in both single-target and multi-target combat.
Weaknesses
High Elemental Burst cost.
High Skill Cooldown making it difficult to combine with other characters for optimal rotations.
Works well in many teams, but nothing special compared to other similar Hydro characters, so he isn't essential.

3.
Kamisato Ayato Combos and How to Play
Ayato is not very hard to play. You want to keep in mind that you should always try 
to use his Elemental burst before you trigger his Elemental Skill. This is to maximize his 
Normal Attack DMG. When under his Elemental Skill stance, Ayato also can not use anything 
else than his normal attack, which would keep you from using his burst afterwards.
Switching to another character while Ayato is in his stance will end it early, 
so you should always swap out only once his stance is over.
Ayato's burst will leave a
Hydro
fiel that will constantly 
deal hydro DMG. This field increases the Normal ATK of any character inside the field. This 
means that you may want to use Ayato's skills in a two-time rotation:
Use Ayato's Elemental Burst
Quick-Swap through other teammates: Buffers, Shields, Sub DPS
Swap back to Ayato before his Burst end to take advantage of it and the Normal 
ATK boost. Use his Skill and spam his Normal Attack

Ayato Team Overview
This Page provides the best Ayato Team information. Ayato will be DPS and Ayato DPS build will be used in most of the situation.
You can also swap the position of Ayato and Xingqiu to make your own best team.
From SS tier to S tier, Ayato best team can be formed in different ways including Vapourize, Permafrost and Melt team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

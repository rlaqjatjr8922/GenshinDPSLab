Strengths

- Can provide decent Crowd Control and works well with various elements.
Weakness

- Fairly long cooldown of Elemental Skill making it a bit inconsistent.

Shikanoin Heizou Skill Guide & How To Use Shikanoin Heizou
Recommended Rotation For Heizou
Recommended Rotation
1
Use the off-field skills of other characters in the team.
2
Switch to Heizou and trigger 4 Swirls  or until you maximize the Declension Stack.
3
Use Elemental Skill.
4
Repeat number 1.
*
Use Elemental Burst to gather enemies at the start of battle or everytime it is active.
Uses Of Shikanoin Heizou's Elemental Skill
Great For Quickly Increasing Short-Span Damage
The Elemental Skill is one of the highest damage source in Heizou's Kit. Similar to the Elemental Burst of
Childe
and
Hu Tao
it provides a sudden increase in his DPS for it is a one shot attack that has a high multiplier.
Declension & Conviction Buff Increases Its Power & Casting Speed
One Stack
Full Stack
▲The Amount of Glowing Arrow = Amount of Declension Stack. Maximum of 4 Stacks.
Elemental Skill is powered up by Declension & Conviction Buff. The amount of increase in multiplier is dependent on the Talent level of the Elemental Skill.
How To Gain Declension Stack?
Declension Stack can be gained in two ways: long pressing the skill button and triggering Swirl reaction using Heizou. The latter is recommended because
charging the Elemental Skill leaves you vulnerable from attacks for a long period of time
.
Using Normal attacks can easily trigger swirl if paired with an off-field character. Because of internal cooldown of reactions, Heizou will trigger swirl
roughly after every three hits
.
What Is Conviction DMG Bonus
▲If there are 4 Declension stack, it means that Conviction is activated.
When there are 4 Declension Stacks, Conviction will automatically trigger. This makes the Elemental Skill be casted with wider AoE and more power.
Elemental Skill Also Provides Elemental Mastery For The Team
Once you unlocked Penetrative Reasoning, using Elemental Skill will buff the team's Elemental Mastery. This is great if you are using Heizou as a Sub-DPS. This will also increase the damage from reactions that is not produced by Heizou.
Uses Of Shikanoin Heizou's Elemental Burst
Gathers Small Enemies In One Spot
The main use of Elemental Burst of Shikanoin Heizou is to gather mobs into one location enabling an easier start of a combo. This is also great when using Heizou as Sub-DPS / Support
Deals Powerful Anemo & Other Elemental Damage
The Elemental Burst also deals powerful Anemo Explosion that is followed by another explosion from the Swirled Element. Because of this skill, pairing Heizou with an off-field character will easily double the damage he provides.
Should You Pull for Shikanoin Heizou's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Any Constellation Level is Good to Have
Constellation Priority
C6 > C2 > C1
C2 Is a Good Stopping Point
Heizou's performance as the main on-fielder of his teams is relatively stable at C0, where progressing his constellation levels serve to make his gameplay feel smother while also improving his personal damage.
If you are able, C6 is ideal and is the most significant in terms of damage increase. But if you can't, anywhere between C1 and C3 are viable stopping points,
both adding Quality of Life improvements that will be nice in Heizou's teams, but not so important that it would warrant spending actual pulls for these constellations specifically.
Heizou does not need his constellations to function the way he was designed to do so, and will be a decent carry character as is.

Windmuster Kick (Elemental Burst)
Effect
Leaps into the air and uses the Fudou Style Vacuum Slugger and kicks his opponent. The Vacuum Slugger will explode upon hit and create an Arresting Windtunnel that pulls in nearby objects and opponents, dealing AoE Anemo DMG.When Vacuum Slugger hits opponents affected by Hydro/Pyro/Cryo/Electro, these opponents will be afflicted with Windmuster Iris. This Windmuster Iris will explode after a moment and dissipate, dealing AoE DMG of the corresponding elemental type. Vacuum Slugger can afflict a maximum of 4 opponents with the Windmuster Iris. A single opponent cannot be under the effect of Windmuster Irises of different elements at the same time.

Paradoxical Practice
Effect
When Shikanoin Heizou activates a Swirl reaction while on the field, he will gain 1 Declension stack for Heartstopper Strike. This effect can be triggered every 0.1s.
Penetrative Reasoning
Effect
After Shikanoin Heizou's Heartstopper Strike hits an opponent, increases all party members' (excluding Shikanoin Heizou) Elemental Mastery by 80 for 10s.
Pre-Existing Guilt
Effect
Decreases sprinting Stamina consumption for your own party members by 20%. Not stackable with Passive Talents that provide the exact same effects.

Xingqiu's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Incredible single-target Hydro application.
Elemental Skill provides damage reduction, stagger resistance and healing making Xingqiu have supportive utility.
Weaknesses
Requires a lot of Energy for Xingqiu's Elemental Burst.
Elemental Skill has a long 21 second cooldown limiting use.
Can accidentally freeze themselves due to the self-Hydro application of their Elemental Skill.

2.
Combos and How to Play Xingqiu
Xingqiu's Elemental Skill,
Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen
, and
Burst,
Guhua Sword: Raincutter
are both incredibly useful. When using
Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen
be careful that you do not swap
characters too early to ensure
Hydro
Particles are collected if you need the
energy for your Burst.
At lower constellations, use your Elemental Skill first. However, at C4 you
should use your Elemental Burst
before
your Skill for increased damage. This is
assuming you have the energy to do so.
That is all you need to do for Xingqiu, once your burst is used. Swap to other
supports or your Main DPS and the rain swords will do all the work to facilitate
elemental reactions and deal damage.
Be careful when using either Skill or Burst! They both apply the
Hydro
Wet status to yourself. This can be useful to cleanse
anything such as
Pyro
burning off but does cause
Cryo
to freeze you.
2.1.
How Does Xingqiu Compare to Yelan?
Many people may compare Xingqiu to his 5-star counterpart,
Yelan
. However, Xingqiu offers increased
Hydro
application for reactions and survivability instead of
the damage Yelan provides.
That being said, many team compositions will actually use both Xingqiu and Yelan at the same
time! Combined they have amazing elemental application.

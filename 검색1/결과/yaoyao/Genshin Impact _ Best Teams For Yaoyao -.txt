Yaoyao is a Good Dendro Healer
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- With Furina in the team, Yaoyao's single target healing in Burst can become teamwide healing
- Yaoyao holds Deepwood set to buff Alhaitham's Dendro damage
2nd
▼ Aggravate Team
- Nahida and Kazuha both increase the damage dealt by the DPS
- With Yaoyao on the team, Nahida can be built with a sub DPS set instead
3rd
▼ Superbloom Team
- Nilou bloom team with Yaoyao
- Yaoyao's Burst can be used for healing and rapid application of Dendro
4th
▼ Burgeon Team
- Yaoyao's Burst healing is good for recovering from the damage from Burgeon
Yaoyao is able to do off-field healing but on a lesser scale compared to her Burst's healing which is limited to Yaoyao when it's used. Furina becomes a valuable teammate due to her passive when overflow healing is happening. Yaoyao's teams are centered on Dendro reactions where she can be a support or sub DPS.

Rotation of Hyperbloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Yaoyao and use her Skill

6
Repeat from 1
You can use Yaoyao's Burst for massive healing if it's available.

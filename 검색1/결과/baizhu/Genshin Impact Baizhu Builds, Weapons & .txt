Baizhu Gameplay
Baizhu’s gameplay centers around his mastery of the
Gossamer Sprite
and
Seamless Shield
mechanics, transforming his medical expertise into a relentless defensive anchor for any Dendro-focused team. His kit primarily scales on
Max HP
, and he enables high-comfort elemental compositions by providing consistent team-wide healing, reaction-based damage buffs, and essential protection against the aggressive mechanics found in modern
Snezhnayan
combat.
His
Elemental Skill
, Universal Diagnosis, controls a Gossamer Sprite that cruises through nearby enemies, dealing Dendro DMG before returning to provide an
instant team-wide heal
. This skill serves as his primary tool for rapid energy generation and reactionary healing; because it heals the entire party regardless of who is on the field, it is particularly effective at counteracting the health-drain of
Void-status
enemies or stacking
Furina’s Fanfare
points in a single cast.
When he unleashes his
Elemental Burst
, Holistic Revivification, he enters the Pulsing Clarity state and generates a
Seamless Shield
every 2.5 seconds. Each time a shield is refreshed, shattered, or expires, it triggers a
Spiritvein
attack and heals the active character, effectively placing the team under a perpetual cycle of renewal. The best part is that these shields provide a recurring window of
interruption resistance
, allowing your primary DPS (like
Skirk
or
Cyno
) to maintain their aggressive attack strings without being staggered by heavy enemy strikes.
His
Passives
are all about making his medicinal utility feel rewarding by directly converting his total health into offensive pressure for the entire team. One of them provides dynamic buffs to Baizhu based on the current HP of the active character, prioritizing healing when they are vulnerable and
Dendro DMG
when they are healthy, while the other significantly boosts the damage of all
Dendro-related reactions
for any character under his shield's care. It makes him incredibly satisfying to play in reaction-heavy cores, as he essentially turns his survival kit into a direct damage multiplier for his teammates.

This is Baizhu's "bread and butter." It initiates the

Elemental Skill - Universal Diagnosis
Baizhu’s Skill is his primary tool for
party-wide health management
. He unleashes a
Gossamer Sprite
that attacks up to three times before returning to heal the
entire
party based on Baizhu’s Max HP. This is essential for surviving high-corrosion environments or quickly building stacks for
Furina’s Fanfare
. While it doesn't provide the same buffing utility as his Burst, leveling this talent ensures that a single Skill cast can bring a dying team back to full health.
Normal Attack - The Classics of Acupuncture
As a Support/Healer, Baizhu spends very little time performing basic strikes. His Normal Attack string consists of melee-range Dendro hits with relatively low scaling. Unless you are building him as a dedicated
on-field driver
for Hyperbloom or Aggravate teams, this talent can be left at Level 1 to save on precious resources. His value is almost entirely off-field, making his physical combat presence a low priority for his effectiveness.

Baizhu Best Artifacts and Stats
When choosing suitable artifacts for Baizhu, there are two main aspects to consider: first, enhancing his
support
capabilities through artifact set effects; and second, increasing his
healing
. Below are some recommended artifact options and the scenarios in which they perform best.
Deepwood Memories:
Best-in-slot option in most Dendro teams. The 4-piece effect reduces enemies’ Dendro RES after triggering Elemental Skill or Burst, significantly boosting the team’s overall Dendro damage. If no other teammate is equipped with this set, it is the optimal choice for Baizhu.
Song of Days Past:
This set's 2-piece effect can increase Baizhu's healing, while the 4-piece effect focuses on converting healing into damage bonuses for teammates’ Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, Plunging Attacks, Elemental Skills, and Elemental Bursts. If your Baizhu is not used in a Dendro-focused team, this becomes a strong support-oriented artifact option.
Ocean-Hued Clam:
This set's 2-piece effect can increase Baizhu's healing, while the 4-piece effect can convert excess healing into AoE damage. This set is versatile and works well in most situations, allowing Baizhu to keep the team alive while also contributing additional damage.

off-field Dendro application
to maintain high Quicken uptime in
Aggravate or Spread teams
, granting your Electro and Dendro carries vital interruption resistance while amplifying their raw reaction damage through his unique passive. Alternatively, Baizhu serves as a premier sustain unit in
Dendro-focused compositions
, where his team-wide healing is essential to neutralize the self-inflicted damage and maximize the lethality of
Bloom or Hyperbloom reactions
. Finally, for high-pressure
Burning or Burgeon setups
, he acts as a definitive defensive anchor, utilizing his consistent
Seamless Shields
to stabilize your active character against the relentless health drain of environmental fire and volatile elemental explosions.

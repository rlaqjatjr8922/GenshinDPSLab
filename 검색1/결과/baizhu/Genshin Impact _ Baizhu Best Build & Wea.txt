Strengths & Weakness Of Baizhu
Strengths
- Powerful healing and decent off-field Dendro.
- Ability to buff Dendro reactions makes him an amazing support.
- Decent Resistance to interruption support.
Weakness
- Needs a lot of Energy to be consistent.
- Not enough Dendro application to be the only Dendro in the team.
The resistance that the shield provides is not enough when dealing with multiple hard-hitting enemies.

Baizhu Skill Guide & How To Use Baizhu
Recommended Rotation For Baizhu
Recommended Rotation
1
Use Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
2
Switch to the Main DPS.
3
Deal damage until the Elemental Burst duration is finished.
4
Return to No. 1
Uses Of Baizhu's Elemental Skill
Mainly Used To Produce Energy & Healing
After the Elemental Skill hit the opponents, it will produce 3 Dendro energy orbs making it a good Energy producing skill. Once the Elemental skill returns to you, it will heal the whole party that scales from the HP of Baizhu but this effect is mostly a bonus.
Works Well With Deepwood Memories
Baizhu's Elemental Skill tracks opponents and hit 3 of them. This is great with Deepwood Memories especially for activating the 4 piece effect.
Uses Of Baizhu's Elemental Burst
Off-Field Dendro DMG
This will also produce an off-field homing Dendro attack that will hit the opponent that is being attacked by the on-field character. This has minimal application but is pretty decent as a secondary Dendro for the team.
Useful For Resistance To Interruption
▲Good enough to help with interruption against single target enemies.
While the shield of Baizhu itself is pretty weak compared to other shields, it recovers every 2.5 seconds allowing consistent resistance to interruption during its uptime. It has difficulty against multiple heavy hitting opponents, however.
Exploration Passive Is Very Useful For Beginners
Picking up plants will heal the team every time. For beginners, this is like having your own portable Statue of Seven.
Should You Pull for Baizhu's Constellation Or Best Weapon?
Prioritize Baizhu's Constellations
Signature VS Constellations
C2 > C1 >
Jadefall's Splendor
C2 is a Good Stopping Point
Baizhu's signature weapon is easily substituted by the craftable
Prototype Amber
while also producing similar or even better results. Meanwhile, his C1 helps him get his burst a little quicker thanks to the additional skill proc, while C2 increases his means of applying Dendro while not on-field. Because his ideal teams would find more value in the extra Dendro application,
it's generally better to focus on getting his constellations if you would like to vertically invest in Baizhu's supportive abilities.

Five Fortunes Forever
Effect
Baizhu gains different effects according to the current HP of your current active character:
・When their HP is less than 50%, Baizhu gains 20% Healing Bonus.
・When their HP is equal to or more than 50%, Baizhu gains 25% Dendro DMG Bonus.
All Things Are Of The Earth
Effect
When White Jade Radishes explode, active characters within their AoE will regain HP every 1s based on 0.8% of Yaoyao's Max HP. This effect lasts 5s.
Herbal Nourishment
Effect
When Baizhu is in the party, interacting with certain harvestable items will heal your current active character for 2.5% of Baizhu's Max HP.
Who Is Baizhu?
Owner Of The Bubu Pharmacy
Baizhu is a brilliant physicist and the owner of the Bubu Pharmacy in Liyue. He's known for being kind and mysterious, but does his job well for people to keep coming back to him for help.
Qiqi's Caretaker
Baizhu has become Qiqi's caretaker in the guise of being the pharmacy's herb gatherer. He cares well for her and even makes sure to save Qiqi from Hu Tao's attempts to put her to rest.

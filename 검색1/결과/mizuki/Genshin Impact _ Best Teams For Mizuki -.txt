Mizuki Works Best As Reaction Driver
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Taser Team
- Mizuki will be the Swirl driver for Taser.
- Swirl can chain trigger Electro-Charge.
- Swirl DMG increase based on EM can greatly increase the damage of her own Swirls while in Dreamdrifter state.
2nd
▼ Rainbow Swirl Mizuki
- Mizuki's EM-scaling can be used for Swirl driver.
- Her Snack's continuous healing can offsets Furina's HP drain.
3rd
▼ Burst Healer
- Makes use of Mizuki's Burst for healing.
- Sacrificial Fragments can increase her EM and reset her Skill.
- Mizuki's Skill is used for collecting the Snacks for increased healing.
Mizuki's Dreamdrifter state allows her to deal powerful Swirl DMG. The pickup range for her Burst's healing Snacks also increase and the continuous healing she can receive from it is increased greatly. Both her heal and Swirl DMG buff are scaling on EM, making her a great Swirl driver.

Mizuki's enhanced Swirls are amazing with Taser. She can deal her own damage with Swirls while triggering Electro-Charge. Ororon holding Scroll set can buff the Elemental DMG of the entire party.
Rotation Of Taser Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Yae Miko and use her Skill thrice
3
Switch to Ororon and use his Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Mizuki and use her Burst then Skill
5
Move around the field to evade enemy attacks.
6
Repeat from 1 once buffs expire
This rotation does not need to be strictly followed. The sub-DPS characters can go in any order, just make sure Mizuki goes last in the rotation. Additionally, Yae Miko's Burst isn't highly needed in Taser but you can use it whenever.

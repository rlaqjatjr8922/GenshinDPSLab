Furina Gameplay
Furina
is a character who makes use of the
entire party's HP
to unleash attacks. Furina can use her Charged Attack to change her Arkhe, and at intervals, her Normal Attacks deal the corresponding Pneuma- or Ousia-aligned Hydro DMG. Furina's Elemental Skill summons either the Salon Members or the Singer of Many Waters based on her current Arkhe. The former fight alongside the active character, and upon attacking, consume the character's HP to increase the DMG dealt. The latter continuously heals nearby characters. Her Elemental Burst causes the whole party to enter the Universal Revelry state. In this state, Furina gains
Fanfare
when character HP increases or decreases, increasing the
DMG dealt and Healing Bonus
of all nearby party members.
Furina
Talent Priority
Furina's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst share equal top priority, with Normal Attack significantly behind. This talent priority is based on Furina’s role as a sub-DPS or off-field character whose damage and utility come from her abilities rather than on-field kit.

healers

energy management

Furina Constellations
Furina performs well at C0, and while higher constellations offer noticeable upgrades, they are not essential. For most players, investing in
C1 or C2
provides the optimal balance, as subsequent constellations deliver more specialized benefits tailored to specific playstyles or account setups.
C1 Explanation
C1 provides immediate 150 Fanfare upon Elemental Burst usage and added Fanfare cap to 400 which means starting rotations will give out a significant damage bonus before any HP manipulation occurs. This constellation improves the synergy with single-target healers while also providing personal damage increase. C1 investment is a better choice compared to her signature weapon as it serves as a progression toward C2 which should be the stopping point for most accounts.
C2 Explanation
C2 provides exceptional value through 250% increased Fanfare generation rate plus Max HP scaling above the cap. This constellation improves the slow ramp-up time of Fanfare generation by a significant rate into a near-instant max buff application, reaching maximum stacks after 72% party HP fluctuation instead of the 300% required at C0.
C4 Explanation
C4 addresses the ER problem through 4 Energy restoration every 5 seconds which gives out 16-20 Energy per rotation cycle. This constellation reduces ER requirements by a significant amount, enabling the usage of HP% Sands over ER% Sands and allowing Furina to use damage-focused weapons over energy-management options. Although this gives out a quality of life improvement for most accounts, it is not a mandatory constellation as it requires a lot of investment.
C6 Explanation
C6 enables her on-field DPS playstyle through Hydro infusion plus 18% Max HP added to Normal Attack damage. This constellation provides access to both enhanced damage output and self-sufficient healing. This creates a high increase to her personal damage while also enabling sustainless team compositions.
This comprehensive guide demonstrates Furina’s position as one of the most versatile support characters in Genshin Impact and recommends investment analysis for every level and team archetype preference.

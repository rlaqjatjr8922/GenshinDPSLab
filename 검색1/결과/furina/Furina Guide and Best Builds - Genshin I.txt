Furina Guide and Best Builds
Furina is a 5-star Hydro Sword character in Genshin Impact
that can play the roles of an off-field support and a team buffer who can
switch between Ousia and Pneuma at will to provide
healing or damage to the team. Read our Furina guide to learn more about
Furina's kit, Furina's best Builds and also her Ascension and Talent Materials.

Furina Build Guide Infographic
Furina's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Strong team-wide Damage increases and Healing bonuses.
Consistent uptime on both Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst.
Incredibly powerful Constellations.
Great exploration benefits by walking on water and Healing on demand.
Weaknesses
Requires a Healer in the party to fully utilise her buffs and generate Fanfare.
High Energy requirements before C4.
Low Hydro application for reaction-based team compositions.

3.
Combos and How to Play Furina
To play Furina you normally want to use her at the start of your rotation
so that you can accumulate Fanfare stacks while setting up your team.
Start with
Let the People Rejoice
and then
Salon Solitaire
immediately after before swapping to your Healer of choice such as
Xilonen
,
Baizhu
and
Charlotte
. Party-wide healers
help to generate fanfare faster!
Using
Charged Attack
allows you to change the Salon Solitaire
members from
Ousia
to
Pneuma
.
3.1.
What is Fanfare?
Furina's main mechanics involve you increasing her Fanfare stacks
during
Let the People Rejoice
to increase your teams damage.
Fanfare itself is a stacking mechanics during this Elemental Burst which
increases as HP changes occur throughout
your entire team
. Both
increases and decreases to your parties HP will generate stacks of Fanfare.
Furina supports this with
Salon Solitaire
draining
HP until your team is at 50%. Healing an entire team from 50% - 100% would
generate 200 Fanfare. Overhealing does not contribute to Fanfare stacks
but does trigger
Endless Waltz
.
Because of this, a Healer that can heal your entire team is ideal to
generate Fanfare stacks quickly. You want Fanfare stacks quickly so that
you obtain the maximum possible buff faster, increasing your damage.
At 25%, 50% and 75% Fanfare generated, the amount of people cheering at the bottom of your screen
will increase. When you hit the cap the screen will briefly flash white
for a moment and the bubbles around the edges become more noticeable.
4.
Furina Best Team Comps
Furina can be used in an incredibly wide array of team compositions to support the team
and increase their damage. Without investing into constellations, Furina
will need a Healer on the team. This
can in some situations cause her to not have synergy with some characters but
can also unlock new potential.
A Healer is important because the entire team will drop to 50% HP
causing them to be unable to generate Fanfare or buff the damage of
Salon Solitaire
. You therefore need a healer that
can heal over 50% of your entire teams HP.

5.3.
Furina's Constellations
Furinas Constellations are great all-round with each providing great benefits.
"A Woman Adapts Like Duckweed in Water"
(C2) is great to improve
Furina's Fanfare generation increasing,
"They Know Not Life, Who Dwelt in the Netherworld Not!"
is really good for lowering Energy Recharge requirements and
"Hear Me — Let Us Raise the Chalice of Love!"
makes it possible to convert Furina into a Main DPS.

Explanation

"Love Is a Rebellious Bird That None Can Tame"
The immediate 150 increase to Fanfare generation massively raises the

C2 massively increases the rate at which you generate Fanfare allowing

C4

Furinas C4 removes any Energy Recharge requirements allowing you to

Increases both the Damage of the Salon Members as well as the

      instead have an additional off-field DPS to increase your teams damage
      even further.

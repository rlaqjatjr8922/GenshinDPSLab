Furina is one of the best support units in the game. Here is a complete guide on how to properly build Furina in Genshin Impact.
Best Furina build guide in Genshin Impact (Image via HoYoverse)
Furina, the Hydro Archon of Fontaine, was released in Version 4.2 of
Genshin Impact
, alongside the ending of the Fontaine Archon Quest. Ever since then, she has solidified herself as one of the best, if not the best, support units in the game.
With her ability to
heal, serve as an off-field DPS, and vastly increase her teammates’ damage
, it quickly becomes easy to see why she is one of the best units in the game. And so, here is a build guide on how to maximize your Furina’s potential in Genshin Impact.

Best Artifacts for Furina in Genshin Impact
Golden Troupe (Image via HoYoverse)
The artifact sets to use for Furina depend on whether you want more sub-DPS or more support.

The 2-piece bonus from the Golden Troupe set increases your Elemental Skill damage by 20%. The 4-piece bonus increases it further by 25% and
another
25% if the character is off-field. This set is practically tailor-made for Furina as her primary mode of dealing damage is off-field. This can lead to an overall 70% increase in her Elemental Skill damage.
If you don’t have Golden Troupe,
an alternative for sub-DPS is Nymph’s Dream
. This can boost her Hydro damage by up to 40%. For
support purposes, Tenacity of the Millelith
increases the party’s attack for each hit of her Elemental Skill. Lastly, if you plan to use Furina as
a main DPS (only recommended if you have her sixth Constellation), then using Marechausee Hunter is your best option.

Best Weapons for Furina in Genshin Impact
The Fleuve Cendre Ferryman (Image via HoYoverse)
Furina can utilize a wide range of weapons due to her support and sub-DPS nature. She can deal more damage with more crit stats, while more Energy Recharge will help you use your Elemental Burst easily.

Her signature activates whenever the party’s HP changes, which occurs constantly with Furina. It also increases your maximum HP, thereby increasing the damage done by Furina and other HP-scaling damage dealers.

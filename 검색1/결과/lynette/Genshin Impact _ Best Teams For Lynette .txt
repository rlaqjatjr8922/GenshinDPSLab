National Comp by itself is already a strong team. With Lynette, you can apply Elemental Shred and Swirl for the team with her Burst. You can also group together enemies within Bennett's Burst to take full advantage of it before it expires.
Rotation Of National Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett's Burst
2
Bring out Lynette and use her Burst to swirl Pyro
3
Bring out Xiangling and use her Burst
4
Next, use Childe as the on-field DPS to proc Vaporize with Xiangling
5
Once all Bursts expire, use the party member's Elemental Skills to build for Burst
6
Repeat 1 onwards
For this comp, what's most important is to only use your character's Bursts when Bennett's Burst is active and it must be within it. This will snapshot the damage increase so that even if you move away from the circle, the ATK Buff will continue to be active.

This Aggravate Comp will have Lynette acting as a support character to apply Electro shred for enemies. Cyno with Fischl will deal Electro & Aggravate DMG, while Baizhu's Burst will act as the main Dendro driver for the team.
Rotation Of Aggravate Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Fischl's skill
2
Switch to Lynette and use her Burst, then skill
3
Switch to Baizhu and use his Burst
4
Next, bring out Cyno and use his Burst. Make sure to use his Elemental Skill when prompted to get extra damage in
5
Once his Burst expires, use Baizhu and Lynette's Skills to build for their Bursts
6
Bring out Oz again by using Fischl's Burst
7
Switch to Cyno to build his Burst from Oz and his own Elemental Skill
8
Repeat 1 onwards
For this comp, what's most important is to have Cyno on-field as much as possible while Oz is active. This is to help him build his Burst as quickly as possible. It's also very important to have Baizhu's Burst active before Cyno uses his Burst in order to have Aggravate.

A Mono comp featuring both Lyney & Lynette. Lynette will act as an Elemental Shred driver for the team, increasing the damage further between Xiangling, Bennett, and Lyney.
Generally, Lynette can work well with mono-comps with Swirlable Elements. Her Burst makes it easy to apply shred and will even act as a decoy for Crowd Control.
Rotation Of Mono Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett's Burst
2
Switch to Lynette and use her Burst to swirl Pyro
3
Bring out Xiangling and use her Burst within Bennett's circle
4
Next, bring out Lyney and spam his Charged Attack until the rest of the team's Bursts expires
5
Use his Burst, then Elemental Skill before switching him out
6
Build for Burst between Lynette, Xiangling, and Bennett
7
Repeat 1 onwards
This team comp is highly reliant on the increase in damage provided by Bennett's Burst. Make sure to use it only when the rest of the Bursts are available, especially for Xiangling. Lyney must also stay within it to further increase his Charged Attack damage.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Vaporize Hu Tao Team
Character
Detail
Xingqiu
An excellent off-field Hydro DPS who protects Hu Tao while active. Since Hu Tao is a melee, the Hydro application of the barrier is utilized.
Yelan
An excellent off-field Hydro DPS who is recommended to pair with a DPS character that utilizes normal ATK. Also, Elegy for the End provides additional buffs that are beneficial for the team.
Zhongli
His durable shield can protect Hu Tao from incoming damage and reduces the elemental RES of nearby enemies.
The objective of this team is to consistently trigger Vaporize reaction to multiple opponents simultaneously and sustain Zhongli's shield to protect Hu Tao, especially when her HP is low.
Pros & Cons Of Vaporize Hu Tao Team
Pros
- Hydro resonance is beneficial to all members of the team
- Powerful damage especially when Hu Tao's HP is 50% or below
- Can easily produce Hydro particles which is convenient for the Burst of both Xingqiu & Yelan
Cons
- Hu Tao's infusion disappears when switched out
- Difficult to utilize when facing opponents that can inflict damage that can bypass shield such as corrosion
Rotation Of Vaporize Hu Tao Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Zhongli's Skill for shield and monolith
2
Switch to Xingqiu
3
Use Xingqiu's Burst & Skill for Hydro application & coordinated attack
4
Switch to Yelan
5
Use Yelan's Skill to inflict Hydro DMG and produce Hydro particles
6
Use Yelan's Burst for the coordinated attack
7
Switch to Hu Tao
8
Use Hu Tao's Skill for infusion
9
Utilize Hu Tao's Normal/Charged Attack until infusion is gone
10
Repeat from step 1
Since Hu Tao's infusion disappears when switching to another character, you need to set up first before using Hu Tao's Skill.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Melt Hu Tao Team
Character
Detail
Rosaria
An excellent off-field Cryo DPS who can also provide CRIT Rate buff.
Kazuha
He can shred the enemies' resistance with Viridescent Venerer x4 while providing decent crowd control, and an elemental damage bonus with his Burst.
Diona
With Sacrificial Bow, her shield can be used more often. She also has a heal in case Hu Tao needs emergency healing.
The objective of this team is to consistently trigger Melt reaction to multiple opponents simultaneously.
Pros & Cons Of Melt Hu Tao Team
Pros
- CRIT Rate bonus from Rosaria's Burst & Cryo resonance can significantly increase the damage output of the team
- Diona has a heal that can save the team when facing opponents with a debuff such as corrosion
Cons
- Hu Tao's infusion disappears when switched out
- Rosaria's Cryo lance, which is a disadvantage when facing a mobile opponent
Rotation Of Melt Hu Tao Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Rosaria's Skill & Burst for Cryo application
2
Switch to Kazuha
3
Use Kazuha's Burst to absorb Cryo
4
Use Kazuha's Skill for crowd control
5
Switch to Diona
6
Hold Diona's Skill for shield
7
Switch to Hu Tao
8
Use Hu Tao's Skill for infusion
9
Utilize Hu Tao's Normal/Charged Attack until infusion is gone
10
Repeat from step 1
You can start the rotation with either Diona or Rosaria for Cryo application that Kazuha's Burst can absorb.

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Burgeon Hu Tao Team
Character
Detail
Xingqiu
An excellent off-field Hydro DPS who can provide additional buffs that are beneficial for the team thru Freedom-Sworn.
Nahida
She can sustain the Dendro application and increase the team's EM which boosts the damage of elemental reactions.
Kirara
A great support with a durable shield who also enables Dendro resonance with Nahida. She can also provide EM bonus thru Key of Khaj-Nisut.
The objective of this team is to consistently trigger vaporize, burning, bloom, & burgeon reactions simultaneously. This team highlights the various EM multipliers that can effectively increase the damage output of Hu Tao's attack & elemental reactions.
Pros & Cons Of Burgeon Hu Tao Team
Pros
- Collective damage is advantageous, especially when there are numerous enemies
- Can simultaneously inflict damage to multiple marked opponents even from afar
Cons
- Xingqiu must have at least 160% ER for Burst uptime
Rotation Of Burgeon Hu Tao Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Burst for EM buff
2
Use Nahida's Skill for mark
3
Switch to Xingqiu
4
Use Xingqiu's Burst & Skill for Hydro application & coordinated attack
5
Switch to Kirara
6
Tap Kirara's Skill for shield
7
Switch to Hu Tao
8
Use Hu Tao's Skill for infusion
9
Utilize Hu Tao's Normal/Charged Attack until infusion is gone
10
Repeat from step 1
The cooldown of Kirara's shield depends on how you use it. The shield's duration is 12s regardless of how her Skill was used. Tapping Kirara's Skill will send it to an 8-second cooldown which is great for sustaining the shield.

1.
Ningguang On-field DPS
Ningguang is built for critical damage. Her
Geo
shards can take down a
single target with ease and her ability to stay at ranged sets her apart from
other Geo characters. Where Ningguang struggles is that she has no AoE outside of
her
Jade Screen
which is used more for defensive bonuses than the actual
damage itself. Unfortunately to truly unlock her potential,
Grandeur Be the Seven Stars
(C6)
is absolutely necessary as she isn't reliant on Elemental Reactions and is
built for pure solo DPS. For the purpose of this guide, we will be focusing
on her capabilities as an on-field damage dealer.

Ningguang is built for damage, so the artifact sets above are somewhat equal choices
at boosting her damage output. However, they focus on different aspects of how you
would play Ningguang. First,
Emblem of Severed Fate
focuses more on using
charged attacks, buffing their damage while you weave your normal attacks
in between your skills.
Noblesse Oblige
focuses on a team-wide buff for
Attack power when you use
Starshatter
and offers a
flat elemental buff for your whole team. This set can work really well with
a team that is swapping Sub DPS characters on and off the field to add to
Ningguang's damage. Lastly, Ningguang can stack several two piece bonuses
while searching for those strong CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG stats. Overall,
Ningguang doesn't have a BIS set but rather can use different options
depending upon how you play her and the team she's put into.
2.
Combos and How to Play Ningguang
Ningguang has a very simple playstyle, with very little technical know how required.
This makes it very easy for new players to pick her up and play her correctly.
However, to get the highest DPS out of her you will need to heavily invest in
building CRIT and ATK. She follows a simple rotation of
setting up her
Jade Screen
and then using her charged
attacks to fire through it. This amplifies the attacks and with
Grandeur Be the Seven Stars
(C6) adds extra projectiles.
Some discussion must be had around Ningguang's Energy Recharge as her
requirements will vary depending on her team composition. Typically if you are
running 1 other
Geo
character, you can expect to cast
Starshatter
every 15s with a requirement of 120% Energy Recharge. Her burst
cooldown can be lowered with the addition of other Geo characters, which will
up her Energy Recharge requirement to 140% to maintain a constant burst.
However, our team compositions will be looking at Sub DPS characters
than can help support Ningguang's damage with on-field skills and with only
one other Geo character joining her (
Zhongli
,
Noelle
or
Albedo
), setting up a
12s burst with without a battery for Ningguang requires a very specific build
and isn't recommended.
3.
Ningguang's Best Team Comps
Ningguang is quite versatile for team composition, as she is a
standalone DPS who can be supported through on-field
Elemental Reactions
like
Fischl
's
Midnight Phantasmagoria
or
Bennett
's
Fantastic Voyage
.
For our team sections we will be focusing on how
we can create elemental reaction pairings while still supporting
Ningguang on a 15s burst cycle with
Starshatter
. These
teams have very simple rotations and are very easy to play, allowing
for powerful combinations with Ningguang showcasing her single target
damage. With that being said we have chosen our team builds based upon
character availability (4 stars vs 5 stars), overall damage potential
and best conditions for Ningguang to shine.

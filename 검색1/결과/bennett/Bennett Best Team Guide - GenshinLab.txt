Bennett Team Overview
This Page provides the best Bennett Team information. Bennett is a Healer/Support in most situation. Bennett’s Elemental Burst provide huge ATK bonus and frequent healing to teammates at the same time. This makes Bennett becomes a flexible character and suitable in different teams.
Bennett can be assigned to different teams. This guide shows some of the best teams of Bennett.

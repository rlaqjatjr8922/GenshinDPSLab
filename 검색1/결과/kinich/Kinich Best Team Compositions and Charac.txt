Kinich's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Kinich in-depth team guide. In this guide, we will cover
everything related to
Kinich
’s synergies, best teammates, F2P options,
and optimal team compositions to maximize his Burning-focused playstyle.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Kinich
Kinich is a Burning-focused
Dendro
DPS whose kit
revolves around triggering Burning to fill his Nightsoul bar and access
enhanced Skill damage. Because of this, you always want to pair him with
at least one
Pyro
character to maintain consistent
Burning uptime.
You should also play Kinich with another Nightsoul character to
activate his passive
Flame Spirit Pact
. While his kit
requires Burning to function, Kinich himself does not deal Burning damage.
His damage is purely Dendro, meaning Elemental Mastery has very low value.
Instead, you should prioritize ATK, Dendro DMG Bonus, and Dendro RES Shred
to maximize his personal damage.
Emilie
Lauma
Dendro Options for Kinich
Emilie
is one of the best sub-DPS options
      for Kinich teams. Since Kinich constantly triggers Burning,
      Emilie synergizes perfectly as her damage also scales around
      Burning interactions, significantly increasing overall team DPS.
Lauma
on the other hand functions more as a
      dedicated buffer rather than a sub-DPS. She provides 25% Dendro
      RES Shred, can hold TTDS for additional ATK buffs, and can equip
      Deepwood Memories for further Dendro resistance reduction.
      Compared to Emilie’s damage-focused role, Lauma offers a more
      complete supportive package for Kinich.
Mavuika
Durin
Pyro Options for Kinich
Mavuika
is the strongest Pyro partner for
      Kinich. She provides consistent off-field Pyro application for
      stable Burning uptime, while also granting up to 40% damage bonus
      through her Burst. She can additionally hold Cinder City to further
      amplify Kinich’s damage.
Durin
is another excellent Pyro option,
      providing Dendro resistance reduction and off-field damage for
      Kinich. He becomes especially strong when paired with another
      Hexerei member such as
Klee
holding TTDS,
      further boosting Kinich’s overall team damage.
Dehya
Xiangling
Thoma
Pyro Traveler
Other Pyro Options
Outside of
Mavuika
, Kinich has multiple Pyro alternatives.
Pyro Traveler
is a solid F2P option with off-field Pyro and
      Scroll utility.
Xiangling
is excellent in Burgeon-heavy teams
      due to her strong Pyro application.
Dehya
can hold Tenacity
      for ATK buffs while providing interruption resistance and
      consistent Pyro uptime.
Thoma
is another reliable off-field
      Pyro applicator who can equip Noblesse for additional ATK buffs.
Bennett
Iansan
4-Star ATK Buffers
Since Kinich scales primarily with ATK and gains large amounts
      of damage bonus from Cinder City and his signature weapon,
      additional ATK buffs are extremely valuable. Bennett and Iansan
      are among the best 4-star options, especially when holding
      Noblesse Oblige to further amplify Kinich’s damage output.

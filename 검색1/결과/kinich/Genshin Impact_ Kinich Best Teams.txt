Kinich is the newest 5-star character to join the Genshin Impact, and he takes on the role of a Dendro Main DPS unit. He can be a bit tricky to play and revolves heavily on having Pyro units in the team to proc the Burning reaction. Due to this, you might have to brush off some of those Pyro units and get them ready!
Discover some of the best Kinich teams below!

Best Teams
As we’ve already mentioned, Kinich relies heavily on having Pyro units in his team. This is because he is highly dependent on the Burning reaction to deal the chunk of his damage, and his signature weapon also procs with it. That is why it is highly recommended that you have at least one Pyro unit to pair with Kinich at all times. With that out of the way, let’s check out some of his best teams!

Hypercarry Team
Hypercarry Kinich Team
Kinich
Emilie
Dehya
Bennett
This Kinich hypercarry team consists of:
Kinich
Emilie
Dehya
Bennett
It allows Kinich to get a ton of field time, thanks to Emilie and Dehya doing most of their damage off-field. Additionally, Emilies fits perfectly here due to her affinity with the Burning reaction, allowing for some crazy numbers even as an off-field DPS. Dehya and Bennett, on the other hand, are mainly here to proc the Burning reaction while also providing a nice boost in survivability and DMG boost to Kinich.
That being said, not everyone will have Emilie given that she was only released recently, so you can swap her out for almost any other Dendro unit such as Nahida, Baizhu, or even Collei to make the team more accessible and still maintain its overall power.

Burgeon Team
Burgeon Kinich Team
Kinich
Xiangling
Bennett
Xingqiu
Another alternative that you can do for Kinich is place him in a Burgeon Team consisting of:
Kinich
Xiangling
Bennett
Xingqiu
This team allows Kinich to both proc Burning and Burgeon due to his E’s ability to trigger Xingqiu’s ultimate attacks, allowing for some seamless gameplay loops. Additionally, this team is also quite free-to-play friendly thanks to having 3 other 4-star characters. And you can even think of it as an alternative to the national team.

F2P Kinich Team
Kinich
Xiangling

Collei
This F2P Kinich team consists of:
Kinich
Xiangling
Bennett
Collei
Luckily, due to Kinich only needing a Pyro unit to deal most of his damage, he can greatly benefit from two of the strongest F2P units in Genshin, Bennett and Xiangling. Alongside Collei there to boost Dendro application and provide decent off-field DPS potential, this team is likely to help you clear even the hardest floors in the Abyss.

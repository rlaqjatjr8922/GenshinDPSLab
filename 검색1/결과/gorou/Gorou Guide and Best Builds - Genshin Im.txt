Gorou's best-in-slot weapon for this build is
Favonius Warbow
. Not only do 
you obtain a free Favonius Warbow when you complete the first Archon Quest, but it 
provides Gorou with Energy Recharge that is heavily needed on him. This weapon also allows Gorou 
as well to be a battery for his teammates. If for some reason you can not equip
Favonius Warbow
on your Gorou, any Bow with Energy Recharge as main stat will do the job, just know that their 
passives may be wasted on Gorou.
For this Gorou Build we recommend you use
The Exile
. While it is only a 4-star 
Artifact set, it provides energy recharge, making it a competitive option for Gorou.
Noblesse Oblige
is a good choice as well since it provides a team-wide buff, but 
the ATK on it may be wasted. We recommend you wear it on another teammate instead. 
You can also go for a 2-piece
Husk of Opulent Dreams
and 2-piece
Emblem of Severed Fate
for more DEF % and Energy Recharge.
You do not have to invest in Gorou's Normal Attack, it is fully optional as you will not 
really need it.

Recommended Endgame Build Stats for Gorou

Gorou's best weapon. Using other weapons may increase or decrease certain stats relative to these recommendations.

For this build, Gorou's energy recharge is important so we can always have his Elemental Burst available 
as soon as the cooldown is over. Aim for at least 220% ER with
Favonius Warbow
, and more without it. 
You also need some CRIT Rate for the
Favonius Warbow
passive.
After you reach the energy requirements, you will have to invest in a lot of Defense as Gorou's
A Favor Repaid
increases his Skill DMG based on his DEF. Additionally, if you have his C4
Lapping Hound: Warm as Water
, the healing Gorou provides 
also scales on his DEF.
3.
Gorou Combos and How to Play
Gorou's Elemental Skill
Inuzaka All-Round Defense
is what makes 
him a specific
Geo
support. Depending on the number of 
other
Geo
characters in the team you can get the following buffs:
1
Geo
Character - DEF Bonus
2
Geo
Characters - Increases the resistance to interruption
3
Geo
Characters - Increases
Geo
DMG Bonus
Of course if you have 3
Geo
characters, you gain all 
three bonuses. Gorou counts as well, so you need at least two other
Geo
characters.
When Gorou uses his Elemental skill it puts a Banner on the ground, and you have to stand in 
the Banner's area with your active character to get the previously mentioned bonuses.
Gorou's Elemental Burst
Juuga: Forward Unto Victory
will spawn a small floating dog 
with an AoE zone which will follow the active character. This dog will periodically 
deal
Geo
DMG to opponents in it's AoE area. It also provides 
the same buffs and Gorou's skill.
You will want to use his Burst first when available, and his skill next. Note that 
when the burst is active, no banner is generated, but you still have the same bonuses from 
the burst.

5.3.
Gorou's Constellations
Gorou's constellations are rather good as they offer him even more abilities to buff 
his teammates, on top of adding a bit of healing. However, it is a 4-star character, meaning you 
will most likely get his constellations with time, so we do not recommend you try to 
target-farm for them.

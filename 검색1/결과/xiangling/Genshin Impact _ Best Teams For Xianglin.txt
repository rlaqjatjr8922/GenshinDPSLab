Best Teams for Xiangling
Xiangling Works Best as an Off-Field Pyro DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Melt Team
- Best team for multiplying Xiangling's Burst damage with the Melt reaction.
- Requires using a Cryo catalyst character since other Cryo DPS can be clunky to use or have high Energy requirements, making them unfit to use with Xiangling.
2nd
▼ Vaporize Team
- 2nd best team for multiplying Xiangling's Burst damage through reactions.
- More flexible than Melt since there are a lot of Hydro characters that can be used as the Reaction Driver.
3rd
▼ Overload Team
- Chevreuse can be used as a Pyro character to funnel Energy for Xiangling.
- Raiden Shogun is one of the best On-Field characters for Xiangling since she can charge her Burst.
4th
▼ National Team
- F2P-friendly team that utilizes a full 4-star team.
- While it was very good for Abyss clearing years ago, it can struggle against new character kits that are very backloaded.
- Members are easily replaceable especially if you have a character who can perform much better.
Xiangling's Elemental Burst deals high damage but with a high Energy cost. Melt and Vaporize are her best teams where she can deal more damage and the On-Field driver can also deal powerful damage. Burning Team with Kinich is not listed since her Burst cost can make it difficult to maintain Burning.

Rotation Of Melt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Citlali and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst then Skill (Tap)
3
Switch to Xiangling and use her Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Wriothesley and use his Skill
5
Deal damage using his Normal Attacks and use his Charged Attack when he gains a red aura
6
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
If Xiangling is unable to have her Burst up on time, switch in Bennett to use his Skill (Tap) then switch to Xiangling before repeating the rotation. You can also choose to wait for Guoba to create a few Particles before switching Xiangling out.

An old team from Genshin Impact's early years that relies on three (3) reactions to deal damage. The base team of all 4-star can be replaced with newer characters who can improve the damage dealt by Xiangling and the Hydro Sub-DPS.
Rotation Of National Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Bennett and use his Burst then Skill (Tap)
2
Switch to Xiangling and use her Burst then Skill
3
Switch to Xingqiu and use his Burst then Skill
4
Switch to Chongyun and use his Burst then Skill
5
Switch back to Xiangling and use her Normal Attacks to apply Cryo (Pyro if Bennett is C6)
6
Repeat from Step 1 once buffs expire
Any of the characters can be the on-field driver since all of them are melee.
Substitute Characters
Character
Sub
Details
Chongyun
Citlali
Raiden Shogun
- Citlali can be used as alternative to Chongyun. She won't be able to infuse Cryo to other teammates' melee attacks.
- Raiden Shogun can be used as On-Field DPS, changing the team into the Raiden National or Rational variant.
Xingqiu
Furina
Yelan
- Furina and Yelan are 5-star alternatives to Xingqiu who can work similarly to him.
- Both have a damage buff, with Furina's being higher, but they do not have the interruption resistance and damage mitigation that Xingqiu has.
Bennett
Kazuha
Xilonen
Sucrose
Lan Yan
- Since this is a team reliant on Elemental Reactions, Kazuha is better compared to Xilonen due to his ability to spread Elements when Swirl is triggered.
- Sucrose and Lan Yan can hold VV and also trigger Swirl. For buffs, Sucrose can buff EM while Lan Yan provides shields.
Since this is a team reliant on having 3 or more reactions, any compatible DPS can be used as the On-Field character. Just change the Sub-DPS to get more reactions if needing to do so.

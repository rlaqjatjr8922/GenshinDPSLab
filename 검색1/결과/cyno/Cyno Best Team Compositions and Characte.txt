Cyno's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Cyno team composition guide and 
character synergies overview for Genshin Impact. 
In this guide, we'll explore the best teams to build for Cyno, including 
recommended characters, character synergies, and Team Compositions to help Cyno 
reach his full damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Cyno
Cyno works best when paired with a second Electro character for energy. He 
also works best in teams that focus on Electro reactions, so characters that can 
enable and buff these reactions for him will be ideal.
Ineffa
Ineffa
Ineffa is currently one of Cyno’s most valuable teammate - paired with 
	Hydro, she enables the Lunar-Charged reaction, which deals damage based on 
	each characters CRIT and Elemental Mastery. These are both stats that Cyno 
	builds high amounts of. At the same time, Ineffa will deal tremendous damage 
	of her own from off-field, while providing a high amount of Electro particles 
	for Cyno, buffing his Elemental Mastery via her Burst, and has a decently 
	strong Shield to cover the sustain slot. Furthermore, her abilities all have 
	high uptime for Cyno’s long 
rotations.
Electro
Electro
If Ineffa is unavailable or doesn’t work in your team, a different 
	Electro unit should be used. Generally, the best will be Fischl or Iansan. 
	Fischl deals good damage and can provide some Elemental Mastery and/or ATK %
	buffing if there is a second Hexerei character on the team.
Iansan can provide an Attack buff and 40 DMG% via 4pc Scroll of the Hero of 
Cinder City, which will last for Cyno’s long field time.
Kuki Shinobu can be used if you need a Healer.
Columbina
Columbina
Columbina is also one of Cyno’s most valuable teammates. She enables and 
	buffs Lunar-Charged, while providing the Hydro needed to trigger the 
	reaction. Pairing Columbina and Ineffa together results in great 
	buffing for Lunar-Charged while they both deal excellent damage from 
	off-field. This will also enable Ascendant Gleam, allowing Cyno to use 
	Night of the Sky’s Unveiling, and allow a support to use Silken Moon’s 
	Serenade for more buffing.
Hydro
Hydro
If Columbina is unavailable or you aren't playing a Lunar-Charged team, a 
	differentHydro unit should be used. If using a Healer, Furina will be good, 
	as her  abilities have long durations, and she buffs DMG%, which is a stat 
	Cyno tends to lack. Xingqiu can be a strong option in Hyperbloom teams, as
	he has the next longest uptime and by far the fastest Hydro application, 
	which is especially great in Hyperbloom teams. Yelan is also a good option, 
	but her slower Hydro application and shorter duration makes her less ideal 
	than Xingqiu.
Lauma
Lauma
If using Hyperbloom, Lauma is by far the best Dendro option, providing 
	excellent buffs to all Bloom reactions. She applies Dendro at 2 second 
	intervals, which is enough to still get Lunar-Charged with Hydro appliers 
	like Xingqiu and Furina, but not enough with Columbina. Lauma will also 
	provide Ascendant Gleam when paired with Ineffa. Note that if using Lauma 
	with Furina, it is recommended to have Lauma's Constellation 1 for healing.
Dendro
Dendro
If playing Hyperbloom and Lauma is unavailable, another Dendro should be 
	used instead. Nahida provides the best buffing, but applies too much Dendro 
	for Lunar-Charged. Baizhu is the next best option, particularly if using 
	Furina, as he provides great healing for her Fanfare.
Durin
Durin
In a Lunar-Charged team, Durin is a fantastic option for the last slot, 
	as he can trigger both Vaporize and Overload, allowing him to deal good 
	damage while reducing the enemy’s resistance. His 20 second Burst duration 
	is also good for Cyno’s long rotation length. Durin is also great for Cyno's
	Overload teams, providing the necessary off-field Pyro application to 
	activate Chevreuse’s buffs, while also dealing good damage of his own.
Sucrose
Sucrose
Sucrose is a good option for Lunar-Charged, as she provides teamwide 
	Elemental Mastery buffs and can lower enemy resistance with the 4pc 
	Viridescent Venerer set effect. However, Cyno’s long field time results in 
	Sucrose's buff uptime not being great here, so Durin is superior to her for
	this team slot.
Chevreuse
Chevreuse
If your team consists entirely of Electro and Pyro units, Chevreuse 
	provides excellent buffs with great uptime. This makes Overload a viable 
	team option for Cyno. Due to the team restriction, it's not quite as strong 
	as his Lunar-Charged team at the moment, but is a strong alternative with 
	more of a focus on Cyno's personal damage.
Pyro
Pyro
If playing Overload, you’ll need a second Pyro unit, one with off-field 
	application in order to trigger Overload and activate Chevreuse’s buffs. 
	If not using Durin, Xiangling is currently the next best option for this 
	slot.

Best teams for Alhaitham in Genshin Impact: Quicken and Hyperbloom
Just add Electro.
Christine Choi
| Jun 3, 2024 2:28 pm
Image via HoYoverse
Alhaitham
is a
powerful Dendro DPS character
in
Genshin Impact
, but he relies on having the right teammates to reach his full potential. Here are some of Alhaitham’s best teams to maximize his damage.
Recommended Videos
Alhaitham’s best Quicken teams
As an on-field DPS, Alhaitham will rely on triggering Spread as many times as possible to deal the most damage. This means pairing him with off-field Electro teammates to trigger Quicken, which allows Alhaitham to trigger Spread.
Fischl
,
Beidou
, and
Yae Miko
are good offensive picks, while
Kuki Shinobu
makes for a good healer. Pick one or two Electro teammates to complement Alhaitham.
Then, round out your team with a second Dendro unit.
Nahida
is the best in this slot, but you can also use
Dendro Traveler
.
Baizhu
and
Yaoyao
make for good Dendro healers if you need defensive utility.
If space permits, you can bring an Anemo character with 4pc Viridescent Venerer.
Kazuha
, Sucrose, and
Venti
have good crowd control and will increase your Electro teammate’s damage by applying a RES debuff to enemies. Alternatively,
Zhongli
can apply a similar debuff while granting Alhaitham a shield.
Examples of Alhaitham Quicken teams
Alhaitham – Fischl – Beidou – Dendro Traveler
Alhaitham – Yae Miko – Nahida – Zhongli
Alhaitham – Fischl – Kuki Shinobu – Nahida
Alhaitham – Fischl – Baizhu – Kazuha
Screenshot by Destructoid
Alhaitham’s best Hyperbloom teams
Hyperbloom teams are similar to Quicken teams, but with added Hydro. Dendro and Hydro create Dendro Cores, which look like little green seeds on the ground. Hitting these Dendro Cores with Electro attacks triggers Hyperbloom, which deals significant damage based on your Electro character’s Elemental Mastery.
Bring an Electro character who deals consistent AoE Electro damage. The best Electro characters for Hyperbloom in
Genshin Impact
are
Raiden Shogun
and
Kuki Shinobu.
Choose one or the other; you don’t need more than one Electro unit.
As for Hydro units, Alhaitham can work with most off-field Hydro options. These include
Furina
,
Xingqiu
,
Yelan
, and
Kokomi
.
I recommend only bringing one Hydro character, since too much Hydro can make it harder for Alhaitham to trigger Spread.
The last team slot can be a second Dendro character (
Nahida, Dendro Traveler, Baizhu, or Yaoyao
) or a flex unit of your choice. Anemo characters like Kazuha, Sucrose, or Venti are fine picks, or you can bring Zhongli for his RES debuff and shield.
Examples of Alhaitham Hyperbloom teams
Alhaitham – Kuki Shinobu – Xingqiu – Dendro Traveler
Alhaitham – Raiden – Xingqiu – Yaoyao
Alhaitham – Raiden – Furina – Baizhu
Alhaitham – Kuki Shinobu – Yelan – Zhongli

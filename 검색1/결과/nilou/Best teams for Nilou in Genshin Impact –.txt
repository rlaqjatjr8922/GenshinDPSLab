Best teams for Nilou in Genshin Impact
Make sure you have a healer.
Christine Choi
| Jul 20, 2024 3:36 pm
Image via HoYoverse
Nilou
teams are capable of significant damage, but there’s a catch. Nilou’s ability to turn everyday Dendro Cores into potent bombs only works when her teammates are all Dendro or Hydro. So, given this strict team restriction, what are Nilou’s best teams?
Recommended Videos
Teambuilding advice for Nilou
Nilou’s best teams are usually
double Dendro, double Hydro
. This means there should be a total of two Dendro characters and two Hydro characters, including Nilou herself. Having two of each element means you can trigger Bloom more often, resulting in more Dendro Cores and more explosions. While you can try to increase Bloom damage by building Elemental Mastery on your characters, it’s often better to just maximize the sheer number of Blooms by applying as much Dendro and Hydro as possible.
You should also remember to
bring a
healer
.
When Dendro Cores explode, they deal damage to
everyone
in their blast radius, yourself included. While you take reduced damage from Dendro Cores compared to enemies, it’s easy to accidentally die from self-damage.

Pick two Dendro characters from this list.
Remember that you need a healer in your team, and try to only have one dedicated on-fielder in total. This list is in general order of recommendation.

Can Nilou be used outside of Bloom teams?
Optimally,
Nilou should not be played in anything other than pure Bloom teams.
While it’s possible to play Nilou in Hyperbloom or Vaporize teams, these teams don’t make full use of Nilou’s kit. Vaporize teams, in particular, require a heavily invested Nilou with high Constellations. Nilou is optimized to turn Bloom into a powerful reaction. Take advantage of her unique kit and fill the world with green explosions.
That said, if you’re dying to try out a nontraditional Nilou team, go for it! These guidelines are just based on what’s optimal and are not intended to police your gameplay. However you decide to play your Nilou, I hope you have a great time with her.

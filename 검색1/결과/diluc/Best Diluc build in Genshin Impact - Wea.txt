Genshin Impact has a wide roster of characters to choose from, many of which are rare five stars. Every character has their own element, and while many are supports, there are some strong DPS characters to choose from. Diluc is a pyro character who can put out high damage numbers.

Here is the best build for Diluc:
Wolf's Gravestone:
Diluc gets a 20 percent attack buff. In addition, each time he hits an enemy with less than 30 percent health, the entire party gets a 40 percent attack buff for thirty seconds.
Crimson Witch of Flames Artifact Set:
Diluc gets a 15 percent boost to pyro damage, and any overload or burning reactions he causes do 40 percent more damage. If Diluc triggers a vaporizer or melt reaction, then the attack does 15 percent more damage.

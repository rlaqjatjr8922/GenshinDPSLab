Neuvillette Overview & Strengths
Neuvillette is a 5-star Hydro Catalyst user from Fontaine, best known for his ability to deal continuous AoE Hydro damage. While recent meta shifts have introduced new competition, Neuvillette remains more than capable in nearly all content. His strengths include:
Versatile fit in multiple Hydro-based teams
HP-scaling skills that reward high investment
Exceptional mobility while casting charged attacks, even allowing traversal over water
Powerful AoE Hydro application, perfect for triggering reactions
However, it’s important to note that maintaining his charged attacks uninterrupted can be tricky, making a shielder or interruption resistance highly desirable in his neuvillette genshin impact team.

Neuvillette Team Overview
This Page provides the best Neuvillette Team information. Neuvillette will be DPS and Neuvillette DPS build will be used in most of the situation.
You can also swap the position of Neuvillette and Ayato to make your own best team.
From SS tier to S tier, Neuvillette best team can be formed in different ways including Vapourize, Permafrost and Melt team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

★ Talent Priority ★
Chasca mainly deals damage by
Shining Shadowhunt Shell
, so prioritize upgarding her
Elemental Skill
. Then, upgrading her Elemental Burst could be considered, as it still has a high proportion of overall damage. As for her Normal Attack, you can skip levelling it to save materials. In summary, prioritize Chasca's talents in this order:
Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst > Normal Attack
.
★ Best Weapons ★
Chasca primarily deals damage through Charged Attacks, so weapons that enhance Charged Attack DMG are highly recommended. Additionally, Chasca benefits from a high CRIT Rate due to her Ascension stats, and the Obsidian Codex artifact set can further boost her CRIT Rate by 40%. Therefore, bows with CRIT DMG as a sub-stat are particularly suitable for her. Based on these factors, we recommend the following weapons:
Astral Vulture's Crimson Plumage, The First Great Magic, Aqua Simulacra, Flower-Wreathed Feathers, Scion of the Blazing Sun and Chain Breaker

★ Best Teams ★
Thanks to Chasca's kit, Chasca can trigger Elemental Reactions on her own even her teammates do not apply Elemental Aura to enemies. And her attacks will be enhanced based on the Elemental Types of the other characters in the party: For each Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, or Electro character in the party, one Shadowhunt Shell of Chasca's will randomly be Converted into a "Shining Shadowhunt Shell". So try to include more teammates of Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, or Electro Attribute in your Chasca's teams. Futhermore, Chasca lacks DMG increased ways so try to add Funina in your teams.

Chasca is an Anemo 5-star Bow DPS unit in
Genshin Impact
that can deal a lot of damage while being quite self-sufficient. The key to Chasca is her ability to trigger elemental reactions on her own without the need to switch to other team members. This opens up several options about what you can do with her teams.

However, irrespective of what team members you choose, it has to be between Hydro, Electro, Cryo, or Pyro. Chasca doesn't have any Geo or Dendro syngery in her kit. If you're looking to pull for her, here's a rundown of her best possible teams.
Best Chasca Team
Chasca
Main DPS
Furina
Primary buffer
Xiangling
Sub-DPS
Bennett
Healer
This is the best team composition for Chasca as
it can trigger the Vaporize reaction while also buffing her quite extensively
. In this composition, Chasca will gain Pyro and Hydro bullets in her Elemental Skill alongside Anemo.
Since Pyro and Hydro can be applied in any order to trigger Vaporize, this team is quite easy to use as well.
Make sure you have Chasca's signature Bow Astral Vulture's Crimson Plumage
for maximum output.
Furina is the best support for Chasca, and she can provide massive buffs to the latter through her ultimate
. She also has good personal damage through her Elemental Skill. Bennett can also provide a massive ATK boost through his ultimate ability and heal Chasca. The healing is necessary as
Furina will drain Chasca's HP and Bennett needs to keep the latter healthy
.
Finally,
Xiangling's ultimate deals massive damage as long you build her properly with sufficient Elemental Mastery
. The damage output of this entire team is very high, and you shouldn't have much issue clearing end-game content as long as there aren't Pyro enemies.
If you want even more damage, you can replace Bennett with Ororon. However, that will kill the entire team as Furina's HP drain is lethal without a powerful healer.
Chasca Electro-Charge Team
Chasca
Main DPS
Furina
Buffer
Ororon
Electro Driver
Kokomi
Healer
Chasca functions exceptionally well in an Electro-Charged team as well and the damage output is comparable to her best team. Here
Chasca
can load Hydro and Electro bullets in her Elemental Skill alongside Anemo
. This composition is also very easy to use as there's no particular order in which Electro and Hydro need to be applied.
Furina
plays the role of a buffer in this team, where she can massively boost Chasca's damage. Her Elemental Skill can also deal substantial damage and thereby increase the overall output for the team. Her role here isn't anything different from the vaporize team that is mentioned in the earlier section.
Ororon will apply Electro to trigger the Electro-Charge reaction in this team.
Make sure to use the
Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City artifact set
to provide a 40% Elemental buff to Chasca.
Finally,
Kokomi is the primary healer for the team who will keep Chasca alive from Furina's HP drain
. Additionally, you can use the
Tenacity of Millelith
artifact set alongside the
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers 3-star Catalyst
to massively boost the damage for the entire team. If your rotations are perfect, then Chasca should arrive on-field after Kokomi and the former will receive the 48% ATK buffs from the Catalyst.
Chasca F2P Team
Chasca
Main DPS
Ororon
Electro Driver
Xiangling
Pyro Driver and Sub-DPS
Bennett
Healer
Chasca doesn't have a lot of F2P teams, but this one works quite well in terms of damage and viability. Here,
Chasca can load Electro and Pyro bullets and trigger the Overloaded reaction
. Chasca needs to rely a lot on her damage here, so it is recommended to try the strongest bow that you can find. If you have her signature Bow, that will be the best, but something like Aqua Simulacra or the craftable Chain Breaker will also work.
Ororon
will be the primary Electro driver and applicator for this team.
He functions exactly similar to the Electro-Charge team, where his primary job is to
proc the Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City
buffs for a 40% Elemental damage bonus.
Xiangling needs to deal a lot of damage here so make sure to stack up Elemental Mastery on her build. Overloaded is a pretty strong reaction, and Xiangling is someone who can trigger it quite easily. Once that is coupled with her damage, the team becomes viable for the late game.
Bennett is the primary buffer and healer for the team.
You should consider using Noblesse Oblige on Bennett here as the 20% ATK boost from the 4-piece set
will help out Chasca massively. Additionally, since this team lacks a buffer like Furina,
try to push Bennett's base ATK as much as possible to increase the buff from his ultimate ability
.

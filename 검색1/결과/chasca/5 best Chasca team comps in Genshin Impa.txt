This article lists the best teams for Chasca according to her kit and gameplay (Image via HoYoverse)
Chasca is a new 5-Star character that will be released in
Genshin Impact 5.2
. Chasca is an Anemo Bow user and is a member of the Flower-Feather clan. Chasca has had ample screen time in the Natlan Archon quest so far, as a result of which players already know her personality pretty well by now. When it comes to her gameplay, Chasca works well as a DPS/Sub-DPS unit in a variety of teams.
Ad
Chasca's kit revolves around her floating in the air and converting her Shells into the elements that her teammates have before firing those Shells and attacking the enemy. Her Shells can undergo Elemental conversion as long as her teammates are of the following Elemental types: Pyro, Hydro, Electro, and Cryo.
Keeping this in mind, this article discusses Chasca's best teams after her release in
Genshin Impact 5.2
.

5 best team compositions for Chasca in Genshin Impact
1) Chasca, Furina, Xiangling, Bennett
Ad
Trending
This is one of the best team's with Chasca in it (Image via HoYoverse)
This team comprising Chasca as main DPS, Furina and Xiangling as sub-DPS units, and
Bennett as support
/healer is Chasca's best team currently in the game. This team will not only help Chasca deal a lot of damage thanks to Furina and Bennett's buffs but she will also be aided by off-field damage thanks to Furina and Xiangling.
Ad
2) Chasca, Furina, Fischl, Bennett
Fischl provides off-field Electro application in this team (Image via HoYoverse)
Furina is a must-have in most Chasca teams (or in any Hypercarry team in the game, really) due to her incredible buffs. Running Fischl in this team will make sure that Electro-Charged reactions are triggered, along with Fischl providing some steady off-field damage.
Ad
Bennett works both as a buffer and a healer in this team. If you don't need the extra buffs from Bennett and want to bring in a different healer, you can swap him with Charlotte.
3) Chasca, Ororon, Xiangling, Bennett
You can also play Ororon with Chasca (Image via HoYoverse)
Since Ororon is releasing alongside Chasca's banner, he can be added to Chasca's team. Do keep in mind that this team has only Pyro and Electro elements, meaning that Chasca will have only a 66% chance of her Shells undergoing Elemental conversion. While this is not the most ideal team for Chasca, it is still acceptable and can be used, especially if you want to play Ororon with Chasca.
Ad
4) Chasca, Furina, Xilonen, Bennett
Xilonen can be added to the team to further buff Chasca (Image via HoYoverse)
This is another team that does not fulfill Chasca's Shell Elemental Conversion clause, as Geo does not aid in her shells undergoing Elemental Conversion. Regardless, this is still a good team for Chasca, as it gives her an incredible amount of buffs, thanks to Furina, Xilonen, and Bennett.
Ad
Xilonen can hold the 4-piece Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City artifact set in this team composition, and her RES shred ability will also be extremely useful.
5) Chasca, Yae Miko, Rosaria, Sigewinne
Sigewinne can be used as a healer in Chasca team comps (Image via HoYoverse)
This team composition allows for off-field Electro and Cryo application, thanks to Yae Miko and Rosaria. If you don't have Yae Miko, you can switch her with Fischl. Sigewinne is a pretty good healer who can be used in this team composition, and her Hydro elemental type can also help trigger a variety of Elemental Reactions.
Ad
Of course, if you don't own Sigewinne, you can always switch in Bennett as the fourth-slot healer/support character.

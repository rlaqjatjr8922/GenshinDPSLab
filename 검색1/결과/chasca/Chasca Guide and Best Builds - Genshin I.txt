Chasca Guide and Best Builds
Chasca is a powerful DPS character in Genshin Impact,
specializing in dealing consistent multi-elemental damage through her unique
bullet mechanic. This guide will help you master her playstyle, showcasing
the best builds, artifacts, weapons, talents, and team compositions to optimize
her potential. This comprehensive Chasca build and guide has you covered.

Chasca's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Unique take on Anemo gameplay allowing the DPS to fully take advantage of other Elements.
Does not need to be accurate in aiming for bullets to hit enemies.
Great attack range and can shoot from very far away.
Incredible overworld mobility.
Weaknesses
Bullet order has elements of randomness making elemental reactions inconsistent.
Unable to attack directly below her which creates issues with Bennet who is a great support for damage but encourages you to stay in a fixed position.
Unable to activate the effects of characters such as Yelan and Xingqiu as well as the effects of some weapons such as Amos Bow.
Elemental restrictions currently pigeonhole her into using Furina + Bennett in every ideal team, although this will likely change in the future.

4.1.
Chasca's Bullet Mechanic Explained
Shadowhunt Shells is Chasca's main mechanic and the
core of her rotation. When holding the Normal Attack during Nightsoul's Blessing,
6 bullets will be loaded and automatically fired. Bullets 4/5/6 will be
converted into
Pyro
,
Hydro
,
Cryo
, or
Electro
(often
referred to as PHEC) depending on who is in your
party with 1 bullet for each element. Bullet 3 will also be converted thanks to
Chasca's A1.
Chasca can still move and dodge attacks while loading shells but if the Normal
Attack button is let go, it will immediately fire which you want to avoid.
Bullets fire in a "Last in, First out" order however the order that each element
will be converted is random. There is no priority system meaning you can not
control what reactions will take place or how those reactions will occur.
Soulseeker Shells is the equivalent while Chasca is using her Elemental Burst,
the main difference is that 2 bullets will be converted into an element
for each character instead of 1. The other difference is that bullets fired
from Chasca's Elemental Burst are actual projectiles and can therefore miss.
4.2.
Chasca Rotation Guide: How to Maximize Her DPS
Chasca's actual rotation is very simple. Start by using supports and buffs,
use your Elemental Burst if available, use your Elemental Skill and then perform
4 fully loaded Charged Attacks.
Use supports
Soul Reaper's Fatal Round
(If available)
Spirit Reins, Shadow Hunt
4x Charged Attacks
Repeat

Chasca's Constellations
Every single one of Chasca's constellations has the potential to provide
good power increases.
Cylinder, the Restless Roulette
(C1)
and
Muzzle, the Searing Smoke
(C2) are especially great
for teams that want to use 1 less PHEC character or have a little extra control
of the reactions taking place. For players seeking to maximize her build,
these constellations add flexibility to her rotations and amplify her DPS
output.
C5 however does not provide a meaningful damage
increase due to Chasca's Elemental Burst being used every other rotation most
of the time.
Showdown, the Glory of Battle
(C6) is also a huge power
spike worthy of being a C6.

This page provides the best Jean Team information. Jean is a Support in most situations. You can also swap the position of Sayu and Bennett to make your own best team.
From S tier to A tier, Jean best team can be formed in different ways from the Elemental RES team to the Swirl Team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

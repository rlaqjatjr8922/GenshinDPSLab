Jean's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Very good constant healing.
Her elemental Skill has a short cooldown.
Jean's Burst Heal can keep up even with Furina's fanfare requirements.
Heal is scaling on ATK which makes her usable as Sub-DPS.
Low energy requirements thanks to her ascension passive.
Weaknesses
Often pushes enemies often away from you.
The long Cooldown of her Burst can be annoying.
Low personal damage.

3.
Jean Combos and How to Play
Jean will mostly be off-field, and switched in when you need to use her Burst, Skill, 
or Charged attack.
Her normal charged attack can launch enemies in the air, which can be useful 
to get a bit of crowd control when needed. If you fight larger monsters, you will 
need two consecutive charged attacks for them to launch.
Jean's Elemental skill on the other hand will launch enemies far away. Her
Gale Blade
can be useful if you need to get away from some enemies. Holding it will pull everyone in before you can
throw them in a specific direction. This can be helpful when you are near water for example, as throwing enemies 
into the water will instantly kill them. However, be careful to not use her skill 
before swapping to another character that needs to be close-ranged with enemies.
Jean's most important skill for this build is her Burst,
Dandelion Breeze
.
This burst will create an
Anemo
field, launching enemies, dealing Anemo damage, 
and healing all party members. Any enemies that enters the field will also receive damage, so you 
want to stay inside of it. Do not use Jean's Skill when the field is up, to not risk pushing enemies
out of it.
For the Sunfire specific build, you will want to use
Bennett
's Burst 
first, and then put Jean's burst right on top of it, before switching to your DPS.

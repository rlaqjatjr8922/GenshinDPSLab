Fischl's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Very little field time required to play Fischl.
Fischl can act as a powerful battery for your team's Energy Recharge needs.
Consistent Electro Application due to their 4th Ascension passive.
Weaknesses
Can be unreliable in Hyperbloom teams.
Fischl is not invulnerable when transforming into OZ meaning they can take damage or be defeated while using her Elemental Burst.

2.
Combos and How to Play Fischl
As an off-field Sub DPS, Fischl will often have very little
screen time with the majority of her damage coming from Oz, her summon. Oz can
be summoned using either your Elemental Skill,
Nightrider
and remains
as a result of your Elemental Burst,
Midnight Phantasmagoria
.
Because both
Nightrider
and
Midnight Phantasmagoria
will summon Oz, you are able to have
high uptime by consistently alternating whether you use your Skill or Burst.
Always make sure to keep an eye on cooldown timers and bursts to help maintain
Oz uptime.
When using
Midnight Phantasmagoria
, exercise caution.
Unlike many characters, Fischl has next to no invincibility frames during her
Burst so enemy attacks can still kill you while you have limited control over
your movement.

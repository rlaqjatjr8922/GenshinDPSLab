Dehya Team Overview
This Page provides the best Dehya
Team information. Dehya will be Main DPS in most of the situation.
You can also swap the position of Dehya and Xiangling to make your own best team.
From SS tier to S tier, Dehya best team can be formed in different ways including Melt, Buring and Pure Pyro team. You can choose your own best team according to your build and character.

3.Noblesse Oblige
Noblesse Oblige is an Artifact Set that can be obtained from Clear Pool and Mountain Cavern. Its 2-Piece Bonus increases Elemental Burst DMG by 20% and its 4-Piece Bonus increases all party members’ ATK when you use an Elemental Burst.
Reasons to use this set:
Even if you don’t have Geo DMG in the set, you can obtain it from the Goblet. With this set, her Elemental Burst will deal all of the DMG.
Yunjin’s DMG increases with her DEF, so he’ll do a lot of DMG anyways if you go for that as sub-stats and main stats in your objects.
The best polearm for this set is
Favonious Lance
. CRIT hits have a 60-100% chance to generate a small amount of Elemental Particles.

2. Retracing Bolide
Retracing Bolide is an Artifact Set available at 4-star and 5-star rarities which can be obtained from Domain of Guyun.
Its 2-Piece Bonus increases Shield Strength by 35% and its 4-Piece Bonus, while protected by a shield, increases normal and charged attack DMG by 40%.
Reasons to use this set:
Yunjin or any of her teammates will always be protected by a shield since her Elemental Skill grants them with a shield and her Crystalize reaction will shield them so their DMG will be always increased.
Also, Yunjin’s Shield’s strength will be increased, making them stronger and their last more time.
The perfect weapon for this set is
Prototype Starglitter
. After using an Elemental Skill, increases Normal and Charged Attack DMG by 8-16% for 12s.

1.Husk of Opulent Dreams
Husk of Opulent Dreams is an Artifact Set available at 4-star and 5-star rarities which can be obtained from Slumbering Court.
Its 2-Piece Bonus increases DEF by 30% and its 4-Piece Bonus increases DEF by 6% and Geo DMG by 6%.
Reasons to use this set:
Yunjin’s DMG increases with his DEF, and since this set provides her with DEF, she’ll gain DMG from everywhere.
This set also increases his Geo DMG, which she deals with her Elemental Skill and his Elemental Burst.
The best polearm for this set is
Favonious Lance
. CRIT hits have a 60-100% chance to generate a small amount of Elemental Particles.

Goblet: DEF Bonus
Circlet: DEF%

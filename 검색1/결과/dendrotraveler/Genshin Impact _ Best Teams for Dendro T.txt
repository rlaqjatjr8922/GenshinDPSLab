Best Teams for Dendro Traveler
Dendro Traveler is Best Used as Dendro Sub-DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Hyperbloom Team
- Lotus Lamp can be infused with either Hydro or Electro to fit the situation
- Raiden Shogun is able to alleviate the Energy requirements and guarantee Burst uptime for the team
2nd
▼ Aggravate Team
- Electro infusion on Lotus Lamp can allow for faster Dendro application
- Clorinde is able to deal powerful damage and self heal when needing so
3rd
▼ Spread Team
- Dendro on-field DPS will be able to generate Dendro Energy particles
- Electro infused Lotus Lamp may trigger Spread, requiring a Dendro on-field with faster attacks
4th
▼ Superbloom Team
- Hydro infused Lotus Lamp will have bigger range and deal AoE attacks, making it good for Superbloom
- If paired with good Hydro characters, this team can do powerful damage
The unique mechanic of the Dendro Traveler's Burst makes them a good Dendro sub DPS for Dendro reaction teams. Due to the high Energy cost, Electro resonance or characters with Energy generation abilities are common in their teams.

Raiden Shogun is the best Electro on-field for Dendro Traveler Hyperbloom due to her ability to recover Energy to teammates. Her build is focused on increasing Hyperbloom damage and her Burst damage. Baizhu will provide sustain and additional Dendro application. Yelan will be the off-field Hydro sub DPS and buff the damage of the active character.
Rotation of Hyperbloom Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Yelan and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Baizhu and use his Skill then Burst
3
Switch to Raiden Shogun and use her Skill
4
Switch to Dendro Traveler and use their Burst then Skill
5
Switch back to Raiden Shogun and use her Burst
6
Start attacking with Normal/Charged Attacks
7
Repeat from 1
If the enemy being fought is easily knocked away or in crowds, infusing it with Hydro will allow a wider range of attacks and does AoE attacks. Infusing it with Electro will allow it to have a smaller range but does faster attacks

Rotation of Aggravate Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Nahida and use her Burst and Skill to mark enemies
2
Switch to Fischl and use her Skill
3

4
Get Energy particles with the Traveler before switching to Clorinde
5
Use Clorinde's Burst then Skill twice
6
Start attacking with Normal Attacks and use Skill if Bond of Life is at 100% of HP
7
Repeat from 1 once off-field summons expire

Rotation of Spread Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Fischl and use her Skill
2
Switch to Dendro Traveler then use Burst and Skill
3
Get particles before switching or you can do a Normal Attack once to trigger Lamp infusion
4
Switch to Shinobu then use her Skill
5
Switch to Alhaitham and use his Burst
6
Dodge enemies or use Normal Attacks while waiting for Mirror stacks
7
Once stacks are obtained, use Normal/Charged Attacks to deal damage
8
Use Skill to refresh stacks until off-field damage dealers expire
9
Repeat from 1

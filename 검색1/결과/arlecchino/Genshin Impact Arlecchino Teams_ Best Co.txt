Genshin Impact Arlecchino Teams: Best Comps
Heizo
Updated
April 30, 2024
Tips
Share:
Arlecchino
is a
five-star Pyro Hypercarry
in Genshin Impact boasting a huge capability to dominate the entire meta, pretty much like
Hutao
. To make the most of a hypercarry like her, you need not only great builds but also
solid support characters
—ones that can
shield her
,
give her buffs
, or
debuff enemies
for her. If that's what you're after, you're in the right place! In this
Genshin Impact Arlecchino teams
, we’ll show you the best teams you can put together with her, and why these support characters are worth having.

Genshin Impact Arlecchino Teams - Overload
Let’s kick off this best
Genshin Impact Arlecchino Teams
list with the
free-to-play Overload team
. While Arlecchino is focused on dealing damage with her normal attacks, the
off-field support
and
sub-DPS
roles are covered by
Chevreuse
,
Fischl
, and
Bennett
. This team setup provides a strong balance of damage and support.
Fischl
can deploy
Oz
to deal
consistent Electro damage
over time, which also triggers
Overload
. When it comes to Overload teams, Chevreuse is an excellent choice. She
increases the damage of both Pyro and Electro characters
in the party and
reduces the enemies' resistance
to these elements, making them more vulnerable. Finally, Bennett can be added to the team. Although Arlecchino might not need his healing because of her passive skill, she and the rest of the team will benefit from the
flat ATK boost
provided by his burst.
What makes this team even better is that it's
mostly comprised of four-star characters
, making it a great option for players who don't have many five-star characters.

Mono Pyro Genshin Impact Arlecchino Teams
This
Mono-Pyro team composition
includes
Arlecchino
,
Xiangling
, Bennett, and
Kazuha
. Although this team doesn't rely on strong elemental reactions aside from Swirl, you can still count on its
raw damage
,
powerful buffs
, and the
Pyro resonance
to get the job done.
Arlecchino
and
Xiangling
work together to obliterate opponents, with The Knave as the
on-field DPS
and
Xiangling as the off-field sub-DPS
. Meanwhile,
Kazuha
serves as a
versatile support
, grouping enemies and using his Swirl ability to amplify Pyro damage
. Bennett
, on the other hand, is the
team's healer
and
damage buffer
, providing critical support to keep everyone in fighting shape.
Check out the
best Genshin Impact Arlecchino build
to learn more about this 5-star Pyor character.
Double Hydro Genshin Impact Arlecchino Teams
Pyro DPS characters like Arlecchino can take advantage of powerful reactions such as
Vaporize
, effectively
doubling her damage output
.
Since she's an
on-field hypercarry
, you'll need support or sub-DPS units that can
apply Hydro off-field
. The best choices for this are
Yelan
and
Xingqiu
, who are known for their exceptional
Hydro application with their bursts
. Additionally,
Yelan
can
boost Arlecchino's damage
not only through Vaporize but also through
her passive skill
, as long as her burst is active. For the final support, you can include
Bennett
, who provides a
damage boost
for Arlecchino and Xingqiu, along with his
healing abilities
.
Genshin Impact Arlecchino Teams - Vaporize
Here's another version of the Arlecchino Vape team, featuring
Yelan
,
Kazuha
, and
Zhongli
.
Zhongli
is a valuable addition, especially if your
Arlecchino is at C0
. His shield not only
prevents her from taking damage
but also
reduces interruptions during her attacks
, avoiding DPS loss.
Yelan
's role on the other hand is to
apply Hydro
to trigger
Vaporize
while also boosting Arlecchino's damage with her passive skill.
Kazuha
, the fourth member, is the Anemo unit who
groups enemies
,
swirls and drives elements
, and
reduces enemy resistance
to Pyro through his Swirl effect.
Arlecchino Double Geo Genshin Team
For the final team on this list, consider using
Arlecchino
,
Yunjin
,
Yelan
, and
Zhongli
. This lineup consists entirely of characters from
Liyue
, allowing you to take advantage of the Lithic Spear weapon.
Since Arlecchino relies on
normal attacks for damage
, Yunjin is an excellent support. She can
boost Arlecchino's normal attack damage
, and at
C6, she even increases attack speed
. Including another Geo character also activates the
Geo resonance
,
strengthening shields
and increasing
overall team damage by 15%
. In this team,
Zhongli
plays the role of
shielder
, and his ability to
lower enemy resistance
adds to the team's damage potential. Lastly,
Yelan
serves as the
sub-DPS
and
Hydro applicator
, enabling Vaporize reactions to further amplify damage.

Genshin Impact Arlecchino teams

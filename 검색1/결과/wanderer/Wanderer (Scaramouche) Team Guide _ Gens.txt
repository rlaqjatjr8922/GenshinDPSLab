Wanderer (Scaramouche) Team Overview
This page provides the best Wanderer Team information. Wanderer is a Main DPS in most situations.
You can also swap the position of Wanderer and Xiao to make your own best team.
From SS tier to A tier, Wanderer best team can be formed in different ways to help the party. In fact, his team formation is similar to Xiao’s.

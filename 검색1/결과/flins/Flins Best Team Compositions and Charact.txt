Flins's Best Teams & Character Synergies
Welcome to our Flins team composition guide and character synergy overview 
for Genshin Impact. In this guide, we cover the best teams for Flins, including 
F2P-friendly and premium options, teams without Columbina and Ineffa, and 
recommended character pairings that help maximize her damage potential.
Best Pairings and Synergies With Flins
Flins is mainly played with
Electro
and
Hydro
characters. This is because Flins is Lunar Charge DPS
and deal most of it's damage performing Lunar charge which triggers when electro and 
Hydro reacts in the presence of Flins
Ineffa
and
Columbina
in the team.
Flins being a Lunar-Charged DPS and Lunar-Charged scaling only from Attack 
and Elementary Mastery means that regular damage bonus buffers like
Furina
become 
very bad. Additionally, you always want to play him 
with another Nod-Krai member to unlock his artifact set passives and his own passive talents.
Ineffa
Ineffa
Ineffa is currently the best teammate Flins can have. She provides a strong shield,
      reliable off-field
Electro
application, and additional damage.
      Ineffa also grants Elemental Mastery to Flins through her passive
Panoramic Permutation Protocol
.
      Additionally, her Moonsign Passive,
Moonsign Benediction: Assemblage Hub
, increases the base damage of Lunar-Charge
      reactions, leading to higher overall team DPS. As a Nod-Krai character, Ineffa also
      activates Ascendant Gleam for Flins, giving her a near-perfect synergy.
Columbina
Columbina
Columbina is Flins’ second-best teammate and by far his strongest
Hydro
option. She provides excellent off-field Hydro application
      and massive Lunar-Charge buffs for the entire team, while also serving as an additional
      way to activate Ascendant Gleam. Columbina can also hold
Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
to provide Flins with a powerful ATK buff,
      further increasing his damage output.

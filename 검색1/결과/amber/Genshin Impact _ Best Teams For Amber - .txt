Best Teams for Amber
Amber Is Decent As Burst DPS
Rating
Team
Description
Best
▼ Vapemelt Team
- Utilizes Citlali's passive for shredding Pyro and Hydro DMG RES of enemies.
- Furina's consistent Hydro application can be used for Vaporize with Amber's Burst.
- Bennett's Burst will offset Furina's HP drain while buffing Amber's ATK.
2nd
▼ Vaporize Team
- Hydro sub-DPS characters are used to maintain the Hydro aura on enemies for Vaporize.
- Amber's multi-hit Burst can trigger Vaporize more often.
- Bennett can funnel Energy and boost Amber's DMG.
3rd
▼ Melt Team
- Cryo and Pyro resonance is good for DPS Amber build.
- Scroll set is great for boosting both Elements' DMG Bonus.
- 2nd Cryo applicator can be changed for an Anemo support like Kazuha or Venti.
Amber is a Pyro bow character who can taunt enemies with her Skill and use her Burst to deal rapid Pyro application. Due to the mechanic of her Skill, she needs another Pyro character to funnel Energy Particles to her.

Rotation Of Vapemelt Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Skill then Burst
2
Switch to Citlali and use her Skill then Burst to gain Nightsoul points
3
Switch to Amber and use her Normal Attack then Charged Attack to activate two (2) stacks of Polar Star's ATK buff.
4
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst then Skill
5
Switch back to Amber and use her Burst then Skill
6
Repeat from 1
If Amber's Energy Recharge is lacking, you can use Bennett's Skill to funnel Particles to her.

Kokomi and Furina will maintain the Hydro aura while Amber's Burst is happening. Kokomi and Bennett's healing can offset Furina's HP drain. The three supports can boost Amber's DMG greatly.
Rotation Of Vaporize Team
No.
Procedure
1
Start with Furina and use her Burst then Skill
2
Switch to Amber and use her Normal Attack then Charged Attack to activate two (2) stacks of Polar Star's ATK buff.
3
Switch to Bennett and use his Burst
4
Switch to Kokomi and use her Skill
5
Switch to Amber and use her Burst then Skill
6
Switch back to Bennett then use his Skill to generate Energy Particles
7
Switch back to Amber and take the Energy Particles
8
Repeat from 1
This rotation does not to be strictly followed aside from letting Kokomi go first before Amber's turn since Kokomi holds Thrilling Tales that buffs the swapped in character.

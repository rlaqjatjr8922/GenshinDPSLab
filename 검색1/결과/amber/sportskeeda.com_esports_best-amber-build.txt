Ultimate Amber build in Brawl Stars details (Image via Supercell)
Crafting the best Amber build in Brawl Stars is pivotal, as it directly impacts her performance and capabilities on the battlefield. It requires players to choose the right combination of Gadget, Star Power, and the two Gears. As a Legendary Brawler, Amber is known for her high damage output and long-range attacks. She boasts moderate health of 6400 at the highest level, which is complemented by her average movement speed of 720.

Amber can be unlocked through Starr Road, where she appears in sequence with other Brawlers. There are various ways by which players can obtain her. The first method requires players to unlock all the Brawlers preceding Amber on Starr Road and then collect 3800 credits through various in-game activities.

Secondly, they can also unlock Amber instantly by spending 699 gems in Starr Road, which can be earned through the Brawl Pass or Starr Drops.
Additionally, lucky players might unlock Amber directly through Starr Drops, which can also reward her Gadgets and Star Powers, essential for crafting the ultimate Amber build in Brawl Stars.

Amber is a Controller-class Brawler with a base movement speed of 720, which can be boosted to 820 when using the Fire Starters Gadget. Her primary attack allows her to unleash long-ranged piercing flames from an extended ammo bar, with a slight spread of 15 degrees.
At max level, Amber can fire 10 flames per second, each dealing up to 420 damage. Over four seconds, she can inflict a total of 16,800 damage, making her extremely dangerous for Heist Safe. Her reload speed varies based on the number of flames fired, ranging from 0.22 seconds to 8.8 seconds.
Ad
Each hit from her main attack charges 4.3% of her Super, meaning she can charge approximately 43% of her Super in a single second. Amber’s Super creates a flammable puddle, which can be ignited with her main attack. Enemies caught in the flames will suffer 960 damage per second for four seconds.
Considering all these abilities, it’s important to accumulate the best Amber build in Brawl Stars to optimize her performance.
Best Gadget for Amber: Dancing Flames
Ad
Dancing Flames Gadget (Image via Supercell)
The Dancing Flames is an ideal choice for the ultimate Amber build in Brawl Stars. Upon activation, it envelops her in three orbiting flames for five seconds, each dealing a substantial 1890 damage. This gadget not only amplifies her offensive capabilities but also acts as a shield in close encounters.
Ad
Best Star power for Amber: Wild Flames
Wild Flames Star Power (Image via Supercell)
Wild Flames stands out among the Star Powers in the ultimate Amber build in Brawl Stars. This ability allows her to create two puddles simultaneously instead of one with the help of her Super. It also enables her to charge her Super when she stands near one of these puddles.
Ad
Best Gears for Amber: Reload Speed and Sticky Oil
Sticky Oil Mythic Gear (Image via Supercell)
To complete the optimal Amber build in Brawl Stars, it’s essential to use the combination of Reload Speed and Sticky Oil Mythic Gears. The Reload Speed Gear is essential to increase her ammo reload speed by 15%. This will allow her to play aggressively and unleash a barrage of flames.
Ad
Meanwhile, the Sticky Oil Mythic Gear slows down her enemies by 10% when they step on one of her inflammable puddles. This makes them vulnerable and allows Amber to hit maximum flames to destroy them.

Is Amber a good Brawler in Brawl Stars?
Amber is classified as an A-tier Brawler, excelling in close-quarter combat. Her high damage potential helps her team to succeed in clearing out enemies in bush-filled maps.
What is Amber’s best game mode?
The Gem Grab and Hot Zone are the best game modes for Amber in Brawl Stars. In Gem Grab, she can control the center area of the map from which gems are spawned to help the team collect 20 gems and achieve victory. Meanwhile, in Hot Zone, Amber can remove her opponent from the main zone by defeating and capturing it to achieve victory.
Amber can also be pretty useful in a bush-filled Heist map as she can move toward the Heist Safe with ease and destroy it with several flames.

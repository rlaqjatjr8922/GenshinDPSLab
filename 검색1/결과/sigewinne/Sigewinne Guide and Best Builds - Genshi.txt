Sigewinne's Strengths & Weaknesses
Strengths
Healer support tailored around Furina being able to buff her damage and generate Fanfare stacks easily.
Comfortable Team-wide healing using her Elemental Skill.
Can be used as a flexible healer in a range of team compositions.
Weaknesses
While being a tailor-made support for Furina, she is never her best support until C2.
Low
Hydro
application.
Elemental Burst is rarely worth doing.

Sigewinne is very limited in terms of weapons which can be effectively used in her build,
Sigewinne's best weapon is her signature weapon
Silvershower Heartstrings
.
That being said, this weapon is incredibly niche and only suited
to her but an amazing option due to how easily she can activate the weapons'
effects. More general build options for Sigewinne include other HP bows like
Sequence of Solitude
and
Recurve Bow
,
Aqua Simulacra
which gives some HP
and
Favonius Warbow
to assist with Energy generation.
Sigewinne has a wide range of viable artifact options to choose for your build.
The main 3 are
Song of Days Past
with Sigewinne easily being able to cap the
effect to increase the DMG of your Main DPS,
Ocean-Hued Clam
is instead better for increasing Sigewinne's personal damage.
2p HP sets can be used if you simply want to hit the 65k HP required
for her
Requires Appropriate Rest
passive.
With regards to stat priorities, Sigewinne wants to be
built solely as a healer before C4 and therefore you would be focusing
exclusively on HP % in this build.
In most teams, Sigewinne will not be using her Elemental Burst meaning she
requires no Energy Recharge. In teams that she does however, Sigewinne
wants around 160-200%.
3.
Sigewinne Team Comps
Sigewinne's strength comes from her amazing healing potential.
Due to having low Hydro application her focus is not on
reaction-focused teams. She does however have good healing as well as
great supportive utility making her
an amazing healer especially for
Furina
.

Sigewinne has some incredibly powerful Constellations,

(C1) provides a
significant damage boost to her personal DMG and
"Can the Most Merciful of Spirits Defeat Its Foes?"
(C2) brings
great utility which can increase her DMG a little but is mainly for other
Hydro
characters such as
Furina
.
"Can the Most Radiant of Spirits Pray For Me?"
(C6) provides
an incredibly massive 20% Crit Rate and 110% Crit DMG which is huge
for her Elemental Burst DMG.

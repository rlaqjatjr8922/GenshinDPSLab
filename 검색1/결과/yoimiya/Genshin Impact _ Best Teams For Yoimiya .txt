Synergy Of Machine Gun Yoimiya Team
Character
Detail
Fischl
At C6, Oz releases an additional projectile whenever Yoimiya performs a normal ATK. With Thundering Fury x4, triggering Electro-related reactions reduces Skill's CD which is great for sustaining Oz's presence on the field.
Yelan
An excellent off-field Hydro DPS who is recommended to pair with a DPS character that utilizes normal ATK. Also, Elegy for the End provides additional buffs that are beneficial for the team.
Zhongli
A great shielder who can decrease the enemies' resistance and provide ATK buff to the team with Tenacity of the Millelith x4.
The objective of this team is to enable coordinated attacks with Yoimiya. This team highlights the coordinated attacks in quick succession.
Echoes Of An Offering Vs Shimenawa's Reminiscence
Artifact
Detail
Echoes of an Offering
- Can potentially increase each Normal ATK
- Inconsistent damage output
- Can freely use Burst which is advantageous for additional damage, especially if Yoimiya is C1 or above
Shimenawa's Reminiscence
- Improved Normal ATK by consuming energy
- Consistent damage output
- Restricts the free use of Burst
It is true that the 4-piece effect of both artifacts is beneficial for Yoimiya. Use the artifact with better sub-stats and the one you can confidently use.
Pros & Cons Of Machine Gun Yoimiya Team
Pros
- Powerful against single targets, especially if the target is a single boss
- Can inflict damage & trigger elemental reaction simultaneously thru Yoimiya's normal ATK
Cons
- Yelan needs to have decent ER value for consistent Burst usage
- When affected by Cryo, Yoimiya's normal ATK speed is slowed which also affects the frequency & performance of coordinated attacks
- Difficult to utilize if you are facing an enemy with a debuff that can ignore shield such as corrosion
Rotation Of Machine Gun Yoimiya Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Zhongli's Elemental Skill for shield
2
Switch to Yelan
3
Use Yelan's Elemental Skill to inflict Hydro DMG and produce Hydro particles
4
Use Yelan's Elemental Burst for the coordinated attack
5
Switch to Fischl
6
Use Fischl's Elemental Skill to inflict initial Electro DMG and summon Oz
7
Switch to Yoimiya
8
Use Yoimiya's Elemental Skill and utilize it until the duration ends
9
Repeat from step 1
Occasionally, switch to Zhongli to refresh the shield before using Yoimiya's Elemental Skill. In addition, you can summon Oz thru Fischl's Burst so you can use it in case her Skill is still in cooldown.
When To Use Yoimiya's Burst
Use Yoimiya's Burst
freely if you are using Echoes of an Offering x4
. However, you need to have at least 30 energy to activate the 4-piece effect buff of Shimenawa so you
can only use her Burst with Shimenawa's Reminiscence x4 if you can accumulate 30 energy while her Skill is down
.

Synergy Of Hypercarry Yoimiya Team
Character
Detail
Mona
The Omen buff from her Burst can temporarily improve Yoimiya's damage output, especially if Yoimiya hits an enemy that is affected by a bubble.
Yun Jin
An excellent support to pair with a DPS that utilizes normal ATK. She can also provide a little energy when the effect of Favonius Lance has been triggered.
Bennett
A great healer who can significantly increase Yoimiya's ATK thru Burst, Noblesse Oblige x4, & Pyro resonance.
The objective of this team is to funnel all buffs to Yoimiya. This team highlights the various multipliers that can drastically improve Yoimiya's normal ATK damage output.

Pros

- Can effectively deal damage to a single target
Cons
- Reliant to Yoimiya's Skill
- Needs to sustain all buffs
- Can be interrupted during rotation unless protected by a shield from crystallize reaction produced by Yun Jin's Skill & Burst

Synergy Details
Synergy Of Vape-Burgeon Yoimiya Team
Character
Detail
Nahida
Her Dendro application is activated by attacking enemies that are marked with her Skill. She can also provide an EM bonus thru Burst.
Yelan
An excellent off-field Hydro DPS who is recommended to pair with a DPS character that utilizes normal ATK. Also, Elegy for the End provides additional buffs that are beneficial for the team.
Bennett
A great healer who can significantly increase Yoimiya's ATK thru Burst, Noblesse Oblige x4, & Pyro resonance. With Sapwood Blade, a seed for EM buff is also produced when triggering Pyro-Dendro reactions.
The objective of this team is to consistently trigger vaporize, burning, bloom, & burgeon reactions simultaneously. This team highlights the various EM & ATK multipliers that can effectively increase the damage output of Yoimiya's attack & elemental reactions.
Pros & Cons Of Vape-Burgeon Yoimiya Team
Pros
- Collective damage is advantageous, especially when there are numerous enemies
- Helpful for aiding Yoimiya's single-target attacks
Cons
- Can be interrupted during rotation
Rotation Of Vape-Burgeon Yoimiya Team
No.
Procedure
1
Use Nahida's Burst for EM buff
2
Use Nahida's Skill for mark
3
Switch to Bennett
4
Use Bennett's Skill for Pyro particle & seed for EM buff
5
Use Bennett's Burst for ATK buff
6
Switch to Yelan
7
Hold Yelan's Elemental Skill for Hydro particle
8
Use Yelan's Elemental Burst for coordinated attacks
9
Switch to Yoimiya
10
Use Yoimiya's Elemental Burst for periodic Pyro DMG
11
Use Yoimiya's Elemental Skill and utilize it until the duration ends
12
Repeat from step 1
Yoimiya can be switched out as soon as the Pyro infusion ends so you can produce Hydro particles for Yelan's Burst & refresh Nahida's mark.
